Horse score (lites 300 to special) is advanced by hitting a Rodeo Target. The horse score functions as a outhole bonus and is affected by a center multiplier x1 to x4. The spinning target is decorative only, as it is not connected to any switch thus scores nothing for the player. Playfield glass and its metal frame raise up to service the playfield. Drop-down cabinet. The three drop targets are known as Rodeo Targets on this game and they drop and later \"jump up\" automatically, under control of the CPU. They do not drop when hit by the ball.
