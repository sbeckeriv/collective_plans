# 🚧 Red & Ted's Road Show - Strategy Guide

*Williams, 1994 — Designed by Pat Lawlor, Dwight Sullivan & Ted Estes*

> **"Feed the heads. Blow stuff up. Drive to the next city."**

Road Show is a WPC widebody with four flippers, two talking animatronic construction-worker heads (Red and Ted), a road trip progression through 22 US cities, multiball, and a shaker motor that rattles the cabinet during explosions. The game rewards players who feed Red and Ted consistently while advancing the road trip. Our goal: **500M–2B** by reaching the later cities (where jackpots explode) and stacking Construction Multiball.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Feed Red (left head)** by shooting the left scoop — he advances your city and awards points
- [ ] **Feed Ted (right head)** by shooting the right scoop — he advances TNT and multiball qualification
- [ ] **Light TNT** by hitting the TNT bumpers/targets, then trigger an explosion for massive scoring
- [ ] **Complete the city map** progressively — later cities (past #10) have 5–10× higher jackpots
- [ ] **Start Construction Multiball** by locking balls in Ted's mouth
- [ ] **Use both plungers** — each one has its own skill shot value

## 🛣️ Core Mechanics

### Two Plungers (Dual Skill Shots)

Road Show has two manual plungers, one per side:
- **Left plunger:** Launches toward Red's side — controllable arc toward upper playfield
- **Right plunger:** Launches toward Ted's side — feeds lower playfield

Each plunger has a skill shot: nail the lit target immediately after plunging for a city advance bonus.

### City Progression (22 Cities)

The road trip advances through 22 US cities, from Nashville to Las Vegas:

| City Range | City Bonus Multiplier |
|---|---|
| Cities 1–5 (Nashville–Dallas) | 1× base |
| Cities 6–10 (Denver–Phoenix) | 2–3× |
| Cities 11–15 (LA–Portland) | 4–6× |
| Cities 16–22 (Vegas area) | 7–10× |

Each city completion awards a city bonus (scaled by city number) and advances jackpot values.

**Advance cities by:** Feeding Red or Ted, completing TNT explosions, and making the loop shots.

### Red (Left Head) — "Feed Me!"

- Shoot the left scoop to feed Red a ball
- Red chews, awards city progress, and ejects the ball
- Extended city bonuses if you feed Red multiple times in a row

### Ted (Right Head) — "Gimme More TNT!"

- Shoot the right scoop to feed Ted
- Ted advances TNT qualification and ball lock
- Lock 2 balls in Ted for Construction Multiball start

### TNT System

Hit the TNT targets (standup targets mid-playfield) to arm TNT, then make the trigger shot:
- **Explosion:** Destroys city obstacles, awards city bonus, advances map progress
- Multiple TNT explosions in a ball compound each other for increasing payouts
- Later cities have more obstacles requiring multiple explosions

## 🎳 Multiball

### Construction Multiball

**How to start:** Lock 2 balls in Ted's mouth (right scoop, lit after specific city + TNT progression).

**Jackpot structure:**
- 3-ball multiball
- Jackpots come from feeding Red AND Ted during multiball
- **Super Jackpot:** Feed both heads in sequence during multiball — the TNT explosion triggers a super jackpot worth 2–5× regular jackpots
- City multiplier applies to all multiball jackpots — this is why reaching city 15+ before multiball is so valuable

> 🏆 **The core strategy:** Advance to city 12+ before triggering multiball. A super jackpot at city 15 is worth 5–6× a super jackpot at city 5.

- **Cities compound:** Each city passed permanently increases jackpot values for the rest of the game
- **TNT rearms** after each explosion — you can chain multiple per ball
- **Extra ball** lit at specific city milestones (typically city 10 and city 20)

## 🧙 Wizard Mode

### Vegas (City 22 — Final City)

**Requirement:** Complete all 22 cities.

**Gameplay:** A massive scoring sequence with all shots lit at 10× values. The TNT super jackpot here can hit 100M+ in a single explosion. This is where elite games are built.

> Reaching Vegas requires an expert ball-conserving road trip — but even reaching cities 18–20 yields enormous per-shot value.

## 📊 Shot Analysis

| Shot | Value | Risk |
|---|---|---|
| Left scoop (Red) | City advance + points | Low |
| Right scoop (Ted) | City advance + ball lock | Medium |
| TNT targets | Explosion setup | Low |
| Left ramp | City road progress | Low |
| Right loop | City road progress | Low |
| Upper playfield | TNT trigger access | Medium |

### Dangerous Shots

- **Right scoop** — can rattle out toward right drain if not centered; play from left flipper
- **Upper playfield shots** — four-flipper widebody means upper-to-lower transfers need attention
- **Post-explosion shake** — the shaker motor actually jiggles the ball; be ready for unpredictable exits

### Ball Control on a Widebody

- Trap on left flipper between scoop sequences
- Don't rush — Road Show rewards methodical city building over frantic shooting

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Balls 1–2 (Build The Road Trip)
1. Skill shot on plunge (city advance)
2. Feed Red and Ted to reach city 8–10
3. Multiple TNT explosions mid-ball
4. Lock one ball in Ted

### Ball 3 (Multiball Window)
1. Lock second ball → start Construction Multiball
2. Feed both heads during multiball for Super Jackpot
3. TNT explosion during multiball = massive score
4. Post-multiball: continue city advancement

### Score Targets

| Performance | Score |

| Solid | 200M–500M |
| Strong (City 15+) | 600M–1.5B |

## 💡 Pro Tips

> **City number is everything.** Don't rush multiball — every 5 cities you advance before triggering it doubles your jackpot ceiling.

> **TNT is free money.** The targets are low-risk standup shots; arm TNT at every opportunity and trigger the explosion when heading toward a head shot.

> **The shaker surprises new players.** When TNT explodes, the whole machine vibrates and the ball moves. Anticipate it; don't panic-flip.
