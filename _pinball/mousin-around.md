# 🐭 Mousin' Around! Pinball Machine - Strategy Guide
## *A Cat-and-Mouse Classic from Bally*

> **"Steal the Cheese, Trap the Mice, Score the Jackpot!"**

A late-80s Bally widebody with playful rodent-themed gameplay, featuring innovative mouse-trap style drop targets and ramp-based shot progression. Master the cheese-and-trap cycle to reach the 1-3 million point range <kcite ref="1"/>.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Skill shot:** Plunge to flashing lane for 100K+ and jackpot advance
- [ ] **Spell CHEESE:** Hit ramps or right target to spell out C-H-E-E-S-E
- [ ] **Spell MOUSE TRAP:** Hit slingshots/inlanes to light ball locks
- [ ] **Lock 2 balls:** Send balls into left and right trap targets (they snap shut!)
- [ ] **Start 3-Ball Multiball:** Third ball in play triggers multiball mode
- [ ] **Relock during multiball:** Get 2 balls locked again in the traps
- [ ] **Shoot mouse hole:** Right ramp shot collects jackpot (grows with CHEESE letters)
- [ ] **Build bonus:** Maximize end-of-ball bonus via ramp shots and letters

---

## 🎱 Skill Shots & Opening Strategy

### Initial Plunge
| Element         | Value       | Effect                                   |
| --------------- | ----------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| **Plunge**      | 100,000+    | Ball advances to flashing lane           |
| **Skill Lane**  | 100,000+    | No lane-change allowed; fixed direction  |
| **Bonus Award** | Jackpot +1  | Increases mouse hole jackpot on backglass|

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** You cannot lane change the skill shot, so master plunge control for consistent access <kcite ref="2"/>.

### Early Ball Strategy
1. Complete **CHEESE** spelling first (easier than building locks)
2. Hit ramps or the right target to advance each letter
3. Each CHEESE letter also advances the base jackpot value
4. Complete CHEESE to unlock bonus multiplier window (double scoring for ~15 seconds)

## 📍 Core Mechanics

### Spelling CHEESE (Jackpot Builder)

| Shot Type       | Targets/Ramps           | Scoring      |
| --------------- | ----------------------- | ------------ |
| **Left Ramp**   | ZIP ramp (advanced)     | Spell letter |
| **Center Ramp** | Via drop target bank    | Spell letter |
| **Right Ramp**  | Direct shot (MOUSE HOLE)| Spell letter |
| **Right Target**| Standup on right side   | Spell letter |

**Completing C-H-E-E-S-E:**
- Lights jackpot collection window
- Activates **double-scoring bonus multiplier** for ~15 seconds (all shots worth 2×)
- Advances mouse hole jackpot value on backglass
- Bonus multiplier resets after time expires or next drain

> ⚠️ **Note:** A center drop target bank must be lowered to access the center ramp; this takes 2-3 shots to drop and scores 100,000 each time hit afterward.

---

### Spelling MOUSE TRAP (Ball Locks)

| Method                | Targets                          | Effect          |
| --------------------- | -------------------------------- | --------------- |
| **Left Slingshot**    | Bumpers near left side           | Spot 1 letter   |
| **Right Slingshot**   | Bumpers near right side          | Spot 1 letter   |
| **Lit Inlanes**       | Left/right outlane return lanes  | Spot 1 letter   |
| **Pop Bumpers**       | Three bumpers on playfield       | Spot 1 letter   |

**Completing M-O-U-S-E-T-R-A-P:**
- Lights ball locks in left and right trap targets
- Trap targets are drop-target style with audible snapping sound
- Each lock is a "giant mousetrap" that captures the ball
- Trap targets stay locked until balls are physically removed for multiball start

## 🔒 Multiball Mode

### Qualifying Multiball

**Setup Phase:**
1. Spell MOUSE TRAP (light both left and right locks)
2. Send first ball into left trap target — ball snaps into position
3. Send second ball into right trap target — ball snaps into position
4. Plunge third ball or receive new ball in normal play

**Multiball Start:**
- All three balls now in play
- Left and right traps reset and become ready for relocking
- Center ramp and mouse hole (right ramp) light for scoring

### Multiball Scoring

| Action                    | Value                   | Notes                           |
| ------------------------- | ----------------------- | ------------------------------- |
| **Mouse Hole (Right Ramp)**| Base Jackpot (1M+)      | Grows per CHEESE letter        |
| **Relock 2nd ball (left)** | 500,000 - 1,000,000     | Ball re-enters trap            |
| **Relock 3rd ball (right)**| 500,000 - 1,000,000     | Ball re-enters trap            |
| **Jackpot Multiplier**    | 2× all shots (15 sec)   | If CHEESE completed            |
| **Bumper hits**           | 10,000-50,000 each      | Keep balls alive in multiball   |

**Advanced Multiball:**
- After relocking 2 balls, you can loop between mouse hole and ramps
- Each mouse hole hit during multiball collects the jackpot and resets it
- Ball that drains from play ends multiball (if no extra balls available)

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Build CHEESE completion before starting multiball — the 2× scoring multiplier active during the window you get after completing CHEESE will apply to your first multiball jackpot shots.

## 💰 Jackpot Value & Progression

### Base Jackpot Growth

| Letters Completed (CHEESE) | Mouse Hole Base Value | Notes                  |
| -------------------------- | --------------------- | ---------------------- |
| 0-1 letters                | 1,000,000 (1M)        | Starting value         |
| 2-3 letters                | 1,250,000 (1.25M)     | Incremental increase   |
| 4-5 letters                | 1,500,000 (1.5M)      | Mid-game range         |
| 6 letters (Full CHEESE)    | 2,000,000+ (2M+)      | Highest without stacks |

**Each CHEESE completion advances value by +250K or more** <kcite ref="2"/>.

### Bonus Multiplier Window
- Lasts approximately 15 seconds after CHEESE completion
- All shots score 2× value during window
- Multiball jackpot during multiplier window = **4M+** on full CHEESE
- Window resets on next drain

## 🎁 Bonus & End-of-Ball Scoring

### Bonus Multiplier
- **Active window:** 15 seconds after completing CHEESE
- **Value:** All scoring doubled (2×)
- **End-of-ball bonus:** Collected automatically at drain
- Bonus = sum of all CHEESE and MOUSE TRAP progress toward letters

### Extra Ball Opportunities

| Method                        | Conditions                      |
| ----------------------------- | ------------------------------- |
| **Mouse Hole during bonus**   | Collect BONUS during bonus shot |
| **100K+ End Bonus**           | Reach high end-of-ball bonus    |
| **Special Completion**        | Complete all letters + multiball |

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Conservative play in early balls builds bonus safely; aggressive multiball attempts in Ball 3 maximize final score <kcite ref="2"/>.

## 🎯 Shot Analysis & Ball Control

### Key Shot Targets

| Shot                    | Difficulty | Purpose                      | Safety           |
| ----------------------- | ----------- | ---------------------------- | ---------------- |
| **Left Ramp (ZIP)**     | Medium      | Spell CHEESE, loop play      | Feeds left flipper|
| **Center Ramp**         | Medium-Hard | Spell CHEESE, jackpot shots  | Needs trap       |
| **Right Ramp (Hole)**   | Easy        | Jackpot collection, bonus    | Safe return      |
| **Right Target**        | Easy        | Spell CHEESE backup          | Flips to left    |
| **Slingshots**          | Auto        | Letter spotting, feed bumpers | Chaotic bounces  |
| **Pop Bumpers (3)**     | Auto        | Keep ball alive, spot letters | Can SDTM risk    |

### Dangerous Plays
- **Center ramp miss** → fast return to flipper, SDTM risk
- **Left ramp during tight shot** → can bounce toward outlane
- **Bumper during lock setup** → uncontrolled trap target hit

### Safe Positioning
- **Right flipper trap:** Best after left ramp shot
- **Left flipper trap:** Hold after right ramp/bumper returns
- **Post passes:** Transfer via right flipper when needed

## 📈 Path to 1+ Million Points

Based on confirmed playfield mechanics and typical late-80s Bally scoring scales <kcite ref="1"/>:

### Phase 1: Skill Development (Ball 1)
1. ✅ Land skill shot consistently (100K+)
2. ✅ Spell CHEESE at least halfway (3 letters = 500K+ bonus progress)
3. ✅ Hit left ramp or right target 3-4 times
4. **Estimated:** 500K-1M

### Phase 2: Multiball Entry (Ball 2)
1. ✅ Complete MOUSE TRAP spelling
2. ✅ Lock 2 balls in trap targets
3. ✅ Start 3-ball multiball
4. ✅ Hit mouse hole 1-2 times during multiball (1-2M each)
5. **Estimated:** 2-4M

### Phase 3: Bonus Exploitation (Ball 3+)
1. ✅ Spell CHEESE again to reactivate bonus multiplier (2×)
2. ✅ Enter multiball during active multiplier window
3. ✅ Hit mouse hole with bonus active (2-4M shot value)
4. ✅ Maximize end-of-ball bonus collection
5. **Estimated:** 3-6M total game

**Realistic Target:** Most players achieve **1-3 million** with solid multiball execution and full CHEESE spellings <kcite ref="1"/>.

## 🎰 Bonus Calculation

**Formula:** (CHEESE letters completed × letter bonus + MOUSE TRAP progress × progress bonus) × end multiplier

| Game Phase            | Bonus Structure                    |
| --------------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| **Early (Balls 1-2)** | 50,000 per CHEESE letter           |
| **Mid (Ball 3-4)**    | 75,000 per CHEESE letter           |
| **Late (Extra Balls)**| 100,000 per CHEESE + full TRAP EB  |

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Double-flip at end of ball to skip bonus animation and keep ball alive in flipper trap <kcite ref="2"/>.

## 🔍 What This Game Does NOT Have

Based on research, the following features confirmed **absent**:
- ❌ **Wizard Mode:** No final endgame sequence confirmed
- ❌ **Complex Progression System:** Mechanics are straightforward CHEESE → TRAP → MULTIBALL
- ❌ **Mode Stacking:** Modes do not layer or compound
- ❌ **Ball-Saver Mode:** No ball-save mode beyond trap locks
- ❌ **Letter Carry-Over:** Progress resets on drain (except bonus multiplier window)

This is a **light, accessible machine** designed for fast-paced, ramp-focused play rather than deep rules complexity.
