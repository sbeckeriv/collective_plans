## 🏎️ Core Mechanics: Position & Laps

### Position System

You start at **Position 28** (back of the field). Advance toward **Position 1** (pole position) through completed modes and shots. Reaching the front unlocks **Victory Lap**, the game's primary wizard-style mode.

**Position Advancement:**
- Complete a Speedway mode
- Shoot bonus multiplier shots
- Hit targeted lit lanes

**Bonus Calculation Example:**
Each position nets you additional bonus: `10,000 × laps + (50,000 × (27 - position)) + 200,000 × multiplier` <kcite ref="1"/>

### Laps Counter

Accumulate laps through:
- Right Ramp shots
- Successful mode completions
- Pit Stop sequences
- Target hits during lit modes

**200 Laps = Victory Lap Qualification** <kcite ref="1"/>

## 🎪 Pit Stop Sequence

**Pit Stop** is the gateway to 2-ball multiball and is a critical skill shot.

### Starting Pit Stop

1. Hit all three **PIT rollover lanes** on the left side of the playfield
2. When all three light, the Right Ramp becomes **Pit Stop Ramp**
3. Shoot the Right Ramp to lock the ball in a car diverter

### Pit Stop Window

- **You have 25 seconds** to make another qualifying shot (typically a ramp)
- Exit via the ramp, and 2-ball multiball begins
- Faster times light **more jackpot cars** during multiball
- Miss or timeout = ball drains from diverter, ending the sequence

### Strategy

Complete Pit Stop sequences regularly—they feed consistent 2-ball multiballs with manageable jackpots (20–50 million base) <kcite ref="1"/>.

## 🔧 Turbo Boost & Left Ramp Mechanics

### Turbo Boost Multiball Mode

When you start **Turbo Boost** as a Speedway mode, you enter a dedicated 2-ball multiball:

- **Base Jackpot:** 10,000,000
- **Jackpot Max:** 90,000,000
- **Jackpot Growth:** Each Left Ramp shot adds 10,000,000 to jackpot value
- **Collection:** Shoot the Right Orbit to collect lit jackpot

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Prioritize Left Ramp shots during Turbo Boost to escalate jackpot value before collecting <kcite ref="1"/>.

### Lock Mechanism

- Two balls can be locked via the **Left Orbit** and **Turbo Lock** diverter
- After two locks, a **3-ball regular multiball** begins automatically

## 🎯 Regular Multiball

### Qualification & Start

1. Lock two balls (Left Orbit → Turbo Lock)
2. A third ball auto-plunges
3. 3-ball multiball commences with lit jackpot shots

### Scoring

- **Base Jackpot:** 20,000,000 (upgradeable to 50,000,000 via mode stacking)
- **Jackpot Shots:** Ramps and orbits
- **Escalation:** Each collected jackpot increases next jackpot value by 5,000,000

### Strategy During Multiball

- Alternate between Left and Right Ramps for clean feeds
- Collect 2–3 jackpots before drains end the multiball
- Stack 3X Playfield mode during multiball for 3x jackpot values

## 🏆 Victory Lap & Wizard Mode

### Qualification Requirements

- **Position 1** (advance from Position 28 through completed modes)
- **200+ Laps** (accumulate via Right Ramps, pit stops, and target hits)

### Victory Lap Scoring

Once qualified, **Victory Lap** becomes the primary scoring engine:

- **Base Award:** 100,000,000 to 200,000,000 per lap collected
- **3X Playfield Multiplier:** Triples this award (300–600 million per lap)
- **Stacking:** Multiple Victory Lap awards stack before collection

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Light 3X Playfield, then trigger Victory Lap. Collect multiple stacked laps for 300+ million in a single shot <kcite ref="1"/>.

### Reaching the Front

Progressive shots and mode completions move you forward. Dueling Drivers, Go For Pole, and Checkered Flag modes contribute significantly to position advancement.
