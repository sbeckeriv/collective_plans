# 🎩 Theatre of Magic - Complete Strategy Guide
## Goal: 1 Billion Points & Beyond

*Become a Master Magician by completing all illusions and reaching the Grand Finale!*

---

## 📋 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Plunge for Skill Shot** → Aim for the Trunk hole
- [ ] **Shoot Center Ramp** → Spells MAGIC to light locks
- [ ] **Hit Hocus Pocus targets** → Activates outlane ball savers (15 sec)
- [ ] **Shoot Left Loop** → Spells THEATRE letters
- [ ] **Avoid direct trunk shots from left flipper** → High drain risk!
- [ ] **Open Trap Door** → Hit 4 targets, then center ramp for basement awards
- [ ] **Work toward Grand Finale** → Complete all 4 objectives

## 🎯 Core Mechanics

### Skill Shots

| Plunge                 | Target       | Awards                                                                  |
| ---------------------- | ------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **First Plunge**       | Trunk Hole   | 10M OR 20M, 2x Bonus, Advance Clock (2 hrs), OR Begin Illusion/2x Combo |
| **Subsequent Plunges** | Captive Ball | Advance Clock (1 hour)                                                  |

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Use flippers before launch to cycle through which Illusion is lit. Choose strategically! <kcite ref="31"/>

### The 8 Illusions (Modes)

Illusions are advanced by slingshots and bumpers. Start them via Skill Shot, Basement Award, or by shooting the Trunk 3 times to light it, then shooting the hole <kcite ref="31"/>.

| #   | Illusion             | Target Shot       | Scoring                    | Tips                          |
| --- | -------------------- | ----------------- | -------------------------- | ----------------------------- |
| 1   | 🐯 **Tiger Saw**      | Captive Ball      | 15M per shot (increasing)  | Safe shot, good points        |
| 2   | 👩 **Levitate Woman** | Center Ramp       | 11M, 12M, 13M...           | Easy from left flipper        |
| 3   | 📦 **Trunk Escape**   | Trunk (4 hits)    | 17M per shot               | ⚠️ Risky - trunk can drain     |
| 4   | 🃏 **Spirit Cards**   | Spinner           | 800K, 1.1M, 1.4M per spin  | Use right loop if lock is lit |
| 5   | 🔐 **Safe Escape**    | Either Inner Loop | 20M + 20M + 30M = 60M      | 3 shots total                 |
| 6   | 🦋 **Metamorphosis**  | Right Ramp        | 12M, 16M, 20M...           | Spirit Ring activates!        |
| 7   | 🎭 **Strait Jacket**  | Left/Right Orbit  | 6M per bumper (60M for 10) | Ball goes to bumpers          |
| 8   | 🎩 **Hat Magic**      | Trunk Hole        | 15M per shot               | ⚠️ Risky trunk shots           |

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** You only need to **START** all 8 illusions for Grand Finale credit - you don't need to complete them! <kcite ref="31"/>

**⚠️ Dangerous Illusions:** Trunk Escape and Hat Magic require trunk shots which can drain SDTM (straight down the middle) <kcite ref="53"/>.

**✅ Safe Illusions:** Tiger Saw, Levitate Woman, Spirit Cards, Safe Escape, Metamorphosis <kcite ref="52"/>.

### MAGIC Letters

| How to Spell                                                         | Purpose                                          |
| -------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------ |
| Shoot Center Ramp (1st-2nd multiball) or Right Ramp (3rd+ multiball) | Lights LOCK on Inner Loops for Theatre Multiball |

Each letter = 1M × Bonus Multiplier at end of ball <kcite ref="31"/>.

### THEATRE Letters

| How to Spell                            | Purpose                                                             |
| --------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Shoot Left Loop (single-ball play only) | Lights THEATRE Hurry-Up at Trunk (50M+), qualifies for Grand Finale |

Each letter = 500K × Bonus Multiplier at end of ball <kcite ref="31"/>.

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** The left loop is one of the safest shots on the table. Spam it for THEATRE letters and combos! <kcite ref="54"/>

### Combos

Consecutive shots to ramps/orbits score combo points <kcite ref="41"/>:
- **2 in a row:** 2M
- **3 in a row:** 4M
- **4 in a row:** 6M
- **Maximum:** ~12M per combo shot

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** You can combo the same shot repeatedly! Center ramp from left flipper is ideal <kcite ref="52"/>.

## 🎱 Multiballs

### Theatre Multiball (4-Ball)

| Step | Action                               |
| ---- | ------------------------------------ |
| 1    | Spell MAGIC on ramps                 |
| 2    | Shoot Inner Loop to lock ball (20M)  |
| 3    | Lock second ball → Multiball starts! |

**Jackpot Structure:**
- **Immediate Jackpot:** Shoot Trunk hole during countdown = **100M** <kcite ref="31"/>
- **Standard Jackpot:** If missed, Trunk lights for 50M base
- **Building Jackpot:** Left Lane, Inner Loops, Right Ramp, Right Orbit add 5M each (no max - can reach 115M+)
- **Relighting:** Hit Trunk multiple times (increases each jackpot)

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** The 100M countdown jackpot is huge - prioritize it immediately when multiball starts!

### Tiger Saw Multiball (2-Ball)

| Step | Action                                                    |
| ---- | --------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1    | Hit Captive Ball 3 times (increases after each multiball) |
| 2    | "VANISH" lights (40M award)                               |
| 3    | Shoot Right Orbit to start multiball                      |

**Jackpot Structure:**
- 1st shot: 30M
- 2nd shot: 40M
- 3rd shot: 50M
- Maximum: 70M per shot <kcite ref="31"/>

> ⚠️ **Note:** Tiger Saw Multiball does NOT count toward Grand Finale! <kcite ref="25"/>

## 📈 Progression Systems

### The Clock

| Method                    | Advancement |
| ------------------------- | ----------- |
| Skill Shot (Captive Ball) | +1 hour     |
| Skill Shot (Trunk)        | +2 hours    |
| Right Orbit (single-ball) | +1 hour     |
| Basement Award            | Variable    |

**Milestones:**
- **6:00** → Extra Ball lit at Left Lane
- **12:00 (Midnight)** → Midnight Madness starts + Grand Finale credit <kcite ref="31"/>

### Midnight Madness

When clock hits midnight, the Trunk spins to reveal bulls-eye target. Shots score 2× current hour (minimum 10M). Clock rewinds 1 hour every 5-7 seconds <kcite ref="31"/>.

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** During Midnight Madness, let ball fall to left flipper, hold it, let it bounce to right flipper, then shoot trunk. Repeat for 100M+ <kcite ref="56"/>!

### Grand Finale Requirements

| Objective               | How to Complete                                |
| ----------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| 🎭 **THEATRE**           | Spell all 7 letters via Left Loop              |
| 🎱 **Theatre Multiball** | Play Theatre Multiball once                    |
| 🕛 **Midnight**          | Advance clock to 12:00                         |
| ✨ **All Illusions**     | Start all 8 illusions (don't need to complete) |

Progress carries across balls but resets each game <kcite ref="31"/>.

## 🔀 Stacking & Combos

### ✅ What Stacks Well

| Combination                      | Benefit                                                                     |
| -------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Illusions + Trunk hits           | Trunk hits during illusions score 2M, 3M, 4M... (carries between illusions) |
| Left Loop + Combos               | Build THEATRE while scoring combo points                                    |
| Hocus Pocus + Risky shots        | Activate ball savers before trunk shots                                     |
| Basement Awards + Multiball prep | Get "Spell MAGIC" award to speed up locks                                   |

### ❌ What NOT to Stack / Limitations

| During Multiball    | Blocked Features                                                           |
| ------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Theatre Multiball   | Cannot advance THEATRE, Clock, Vanish Lock, or Trap Door <kcite ref="31"/> |
| Tiger Saw Multiball | Can advance Clock but limited other progress                               |

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Complete THEATRE letters and advance the clock BEFORE starting Theatre Multiball!

### Optimal Timing

1. **Early Ball:** Activate Hocus Pocus ball savers, work on THEATRE letters
2. **Mid Ball:** Spell MAGIC, open Trap Door for awards
3. **Before Multiball:** Ensure THEATRE is spelled and clock is advanced
4. **During Multiball:** Focus purely on jackpots

## 🏆 Wizard Mode: Grand Finale

### Requirements
Complete all 4 objectives (in any order):
1. Spell THEATRE
2. Play Theatre Multiball
3. Reach Midnight
4. Start all 8 Illusions

### Starting Grand Finale
Shoot either Inner Loop when "Start Finale" is lit <kcite ref="31"/>.

### Gameplay
- **Time Limit:** 60 seconds
- **Objective:** Spell "MAGIC THEATRE" (12 letters)
- **Shots:** Any ramps, orbits, or trunk
- **Scoring:** 50M per letter
- **Super Jackpot:** Complete all 12 letters = **500M bonus** <kcite ref="29"/>

**Total Potential:** 600M + 500M = **1.1 BILLION** from Grand Finale alone!

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** The left loop is fast and reliable - use it to rack up letters quickly during Grand Finale <kcite ref="31"/>!

## 🎯 Shot Analysis

### ⚠️ Dangerous Shots

| Shot                | Risk                             | Mitigation                                                                   |
| ------------------- | -------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Trunk (direct)**  | SDTM drain, unpredictable bounce | Hit edge/corner, use backhand, or carrom off other targets <kcite ref="64"/> |
| **Trap Door exit**  | Can drain SDTM                   | Be ready to nudge                                                            |
| **Weak ramp shots** | Roll back to drain               | Commit to full power                                                         |

### ✅ Safe Shots

| Shot             | From Flipper | Notes                                                         |
| ---------------- | ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Center Ramp**  | Left         | Go-to shot, advances MAGIC, opens trap door <kcite ref="52"/> |
| **Left Loop**    | Left         | Spells THEATRE, great for combos                              |
| **Right Ramp**   | Right        | Advances MAGIC, Spirit Ring during Metamorphosis              |
| **Captive Ball** | Right        | Advances clock, builds Vanish                                 |

### Ball Control Positions

| Position               | Use For                                          |
| ---------------------- | ------------------------------------------------ |
| **Left Flipper Hold**  | Center ramp, left loop, backhand to trunk corner |
| **Right Flipper Hold** | Right ramp, captive ball, right orbit            |
| **Post Pass**          | Transfer ball between flippers safely            |

### Hocus Pocus Ball Savers

Hit either Hocus Pocus target (left edge of playfield) to activate magnetic ball savers in BOTH outlanes for 15 seconds <kcite ref="67"/>.

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Always activate Hocus Pocus before attempting risky trunk shots!

### End-of-Ball Bonus

Your bonus is calculated from <kcite ref="31"/>:
- Magicianship (Grand Finale tasks completed)
- MAGIC letters (1M each)
- THEATRE letters (500K each)
- Clock Bonus (1M × current hour)
- Illusions played (4M each)
- Skill Shots made (1M each)
- **All × Bonus Multiplier (up to 8×)**

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** With 8× multiplier and full progress, end-of-ball bonus can exceed 100M!

### Does Completing Modes Change Gameplay?

- **Illusions:** Once started, they count toward Grand Finale permanently (per game)
- **Theatre Multiball:** Only needs to be played once for Grand Finale credit
- **THEATRE/Clock:** Progress persists across balls
- **After Grand Finale:** Game continues normally; you can attempt Grand Finale again
