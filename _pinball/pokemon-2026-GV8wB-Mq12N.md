# 🎮 POKÉMON (Pro) Pinball Machine - Complete Strategy Guide
## *Build Your Perfect Team & Master the Multiball*

> **"Gotta Catch 'Em All!"**

Stern Pinball's 2026 Pokémon machine celebrates 30 years of the franchise with a fresh take on pinball basics. This guide will help you master catching, battling, and climbing toward million-point milestones <kcite ref="1"/>.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Ball 1 Plunge:** Hit top lanes to spawn Pokémon early
- [ ] **Complete Story Modes:** Hit three of your Pokémon's shot each to unlock story modes
- [ ] **Catch Pokémon:** Hit bumpers → captive ball CATCH hurry-up → purple CATCH! targets → left ramp capture
- [ ] **Spell BATTLE:** Hit center targets to lower the Meowth balloon (progress toward multiball)
- [ ] **Start Rival Battles:** Light scoop by capturing Pokémon, then battle trainers
- [ ] **Unlock Team Rocket Multiball:** Hit Meowth balloon 3 times after spelling BATTLE
- [ ] **Complete All Four Story Modes:** Unlock Pikachu vs. Charizard mini-wizard
- [ ] **Type Advantage Swaps:** Match lit colors to scoop to use type advantages in battles

## 🎱 Skill Shots & Plunge Strategy

### Top Lane Skill Shot
- **Objective:** Plunge into lit top lane
- **Award:** Spawn a wild Pokémon for potential capture
- **Extended Value:** Multiple top lane hits build Pokémon diversity for bonus

### Left Flipper Plunge (Soft Plunge)
- **Hold left flipper during plunge** to reduce momentum
- **Precision landing:** Direct ball to weak ramps or controlled areas
- **Best for:** Starting specific story mode shots without accidentally triggering others

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** The top lane skill shot is one of the best ways to stock your Pokédex early in the ball. Make it your priority during plunge! <kcite ref="2"/>

## 📖 Story Modes (Core Progression)

Each starter Pokémon has a unique story mode started by **hitting their designated shot 3 times** during single-ball play <kcite ref="2"/>.

### 🌱 Bulbasaur Mode
- **Shot:** Left orbit
- **Mechanic:** Shoot orbit/ramp shots to build pollen value
- **Completion:** Hit center berry target 6 times to relight shots and complete
- **Scoring:** Base 2.5M per completion; increases with each subsequent play
- **Perk:** Unlocks part of Pikachu vs. Charizard multiball

### 🐢 Squirtle Mode
- **Shot:** Left ramp
- **Mechanic:** Hit left ramp 3 times; other shots increase multiplier on ramps
- **Completion:** Complete the whirlpool sequence
- **Scoring:** Ramp shots worth escalating multiplier (1x → 2x → 3x)
- **Perk:** Unlocks part of Pikachu vs. Charizard multiball

### ⚡ Pikachu Mode
- **Shot:** Right ramp
- **Mechanic:** 2-ball multiball with all shots lit for jackpots
- **Completion:** Captive ball scores super jackpot
- **Scoring:** Jackpots escalate; super jackpot worth sum of previous jackpots
- **Perk:** Unlocks part of Pikachu vs. Charizard multiball

### 🔥 Charmander Mode
- **Shot:** Right orbit (spinner)
- **Mechanic:** Rack up 100+ spinner shots with multipliers from other major shots
- **Completion:** Reach spinner shot target
- **Scoring:** Each spinner worth escalating values (base 50k per spin, up to 250k+)
- **Perk:** Unlocks part of Pikachu vs. Charizard multiball

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Completing all four story modes is required to access the Pikachu vs. Charizard wizard mode! Prioritize finishing all four on each ball for maximum scoring potential. <kcite ref="2"/><kcite ref="3"/>

## 🎣 Catching Pokémon & CATCH! Hurry-Up

### Discovery & Scanning
1. **Bump Spawning:** Shoot right orbit to enter bumper area
2. **Spawn Pokémon:** Bumpers randomly spawn wild Pokémon (audio callout: "Wild Pokémon appeared!")
3. **Scanning:** Hit captive ball when a Pokémon is active
4. **CATCH! Hurry-Up Begins:** Purple targets light for 2M, decreasing with each flip or target hit

### Capture Sequence
- **Spell CATCH!:** Hit 5 purple targets in sequence (C → A → T → C → H!)
- **Left Ramp Shot:** After spelling CATCH!, shoot left ramp to capture
- **Scoop Lights:** Successful capture lights town scoop (far right) for Rival Battle or mystery award
- **Pokedex Entry:** Capture counts toward end-of-ball bonus (500k per Pokémon caught) <kcite ref="2"/>

### Capture Difficulty Scaling
Each subsequent capture in a single ball requires **one additional hit** to the playfield before attempting the hurry-up. Early captures are easiest!

> ⚠️ **Warning:** The catching mechanic can be addictive—players often abandon other objectives when hearing the discovery callout! Focus on strategy, not just catches. <kcite ref="4"/>

## ⚔️ Rival Battles

### Activation
- **Light Scoop:** Complete a Pokémon capture, or hit town scoop during certain awards
- **Shoot Town Scoop:** Initiates a 1v1 battle with an opponent trainer
- **Type Matchup Display:** Opponent's Pokémon type appears (color-coded: water=blue, electric=yellow, grass=green, fire=red)

### Battle Mechanics
- **Type Advantage Strategy:** Swap your active Pokémon by matching the lit scoop color to gain type advantage
  - Water beats Fire
  - Fire beats Grass
  - Grass beats Water
  - Electric beats Water
  - Normal types lack advantage
- **Shot Sequence:** Flashing shots represent attacks; hit them to damage opponent
- **Arena Bonus:** Center lane scores bonus when hit during active battle (escalating with consecutive hits)
- **Victory:** Defeat opponent by hitting all required shots; earn points based on type advantage <kcite ref="4"/>

### Strategic Team Composition
Your starter team covers all types:
- **Charmander** (Fire) → Bulbasaur (Grass)
- **Squirtle** (Water) → Pikachu (Electric)

Consider type matchups when swapping! <kcite ref="4"/>

## 🎰 Team Rocket Multiball

### Activation Sequence

| Step | Action                           | Effect                      |
| ---- | -------------------------------- | --------------------------- |
| 1    | Hit center targets              | Spell BATTLE (B-A-T-T-L-E) |
| 2    | Lower Meowth Balloon            | Meowth swoops down to arena |
| 3    | Hit Meowth balloon 3 times      | Light Team Rocket Multiball |
| 4    | Shoot lit shot to start          | 2-ball multiball begins     |

<kcite ref="2"/><kcite ref="3"/>

### Multiball Play

| Phase | Action                    | Points                         |
| ----- | ------------------------- | ------------------------------ |
| Setup | Hit ramps & balloon       | Jackpots light (base 5M each)  |
| Main  | Collect jackpots at shots | Escalating (6M, 7M, 8M...)    |
| Super | After 6 jackpots          | Center lane lights super jackpot |

**Super Jackpot Value:** Sum of all 6 jackpots collected (30M+ typical) <kcite ref="2"/>

**Add-a-Ball:** Hit specific targets while lit green (up to 3 adds during multiball window)

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** During Team Rocket Multiball, prioritize collecting all 6 jackpots before attempting the super jackpot. Build value systematically! <kcite ref="3"/>

## 🔮 Pikachu vs. Charizard (Mini-Wizard Mode)

### Activation
- **Requirement:** Complete all 4 story modes (Bulbasaur, Squirtle, Pikachu, Charmander)
- **Start:** Light and shoot right ramp after completing 4th story mode
- **Ball Count:** 2-6 balls depending on how many story modes were completed successfully

### Battle Structure

| Phase | Action                                    | Scoring                  |
| ----- | ----------------------------------------- | ------------------------ |
| 1     | Shoot any orbit/ramp to start fight       | Phase 1 base value       |
| 2     | Hit all 4 shots, then captive ball (add-a-ball) | Move to Phase 2          |
| 3     | Repeat for Phases 3 & 4                   | Values escalate per phase |
| Final | Hit center lane after 4 phases complete   | Super jackpot hurry-up   |

**Super Jackpot Hurry-Up:** Starts at 100M and counts down; hit center lane to collect <kcite ref="3"/>

**Scoring Formula:** 10% of total score from each respective story mode = phase base value <kcite ref="2"/><kcite ref="3"/>

> 🏆 **This is the only true wizard mode currently available**, though inserts for Lucario and Mewtwo suggest future content updates may expand wizard modes. <kcite ref="3"/>

## 📊 Scoring Systems & Multipliers

### Bonus Multiplier (End-of-Ball)
- **Source:** Complete top lane
- **Max:** 20x multiplier
- **Bonus Calculation:** (500k × Pokémon caught) + (25k × berry targets) + (12.5k × Poké Balls collected) × multiplier
- **Example:** 50 Pokémon caught, 40 berries, 30 Poké Balls at 5x multiplier = (25M + 1M + 375k) × 5 = **131.875M** <kcite ref="2"/>

### Shot Multipliers
- **Qualify:** Complete a story mode or score first super jackpot in Team Rocket Multiball
- **Effect:** Next shot made becomes permanently 2x for entire ball
- **Strategy:** Apply to high-value left ramp first <kcite ref="2"/>

### 2x Playfield (Short Duration)
- **Activation:** Hit left berry + right berry (each twice), then center berry when flashing
- **Duration:** 20 seconds
- **Extension:** Each additional hit adds 5 seconds (max 20 seconds total active)
- **Scope:** All playfield shots during window get 2x boost <kcite ref="2"/>

## 🎁 Secondary Features & Scoring

### Poké Balls (Combo Awards)
- **Earn:** Each combo shot awards 1 Poké Ball
- **Multi-way Combos:** Ending at captive ball awards balls = combo length (3-shot combo = 3 balls)
- **Milestone:** 50 collected Poké Balls lights extra ball
- **End-of-Ball:** Multiplied in bonus (12.5k per ball × multiplier) <kcite ref="2"/>

### Eevee Evolution Combos (Secret Sequences)
- **Count:** 8 unique secret shot sequences
- **Value Per Combo:** 2.5M + 500k per unique combo made
- **All 8 Complete:** Award 10M and reset availability
- **Total Available:** Up to 52.5M from full Eevee combo set <kcite ref="2"/>

### Berry Target Hits
- **Left Playfield Targets:** Three standup targets boost story mode values
- **Center Berry:** Relight shots during modes
- **End-of-Ball:** Counted in bonus (25k per berry × multiplier)

### Outlane Ball Saves
- **Activate:** Complete town targets to light alternating outlane saves
- **Progressive:** Each subsequent save requires +1 additional town target completion
- **Storage:** Multiple saves can queue up for later use <kcite ref="2"/>

## 🎯 Shot Analysis & Ball Control

### ⚠️ Dangerous Shots

| Shot                    | Risk                                    | Mitigation                          |
| ----------------------- | --------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------- |
| **Right Orbit (spinner)**| Fast spinner can feed to outlanes       | Trap after hit; control speed      |
| **Center Lanes**        | Quick return, SDTM risk if uncontrolled | Aim for orbit feeds instead         |
| **Bumper Area Entry**   | Chaotic bounces; unpredictable exits   | Use weak plunge or soft ramps      |
| **Town Scoop (far right)**| Aggressive return; can SDTM            | Post-pass or controlled catch      |

### ✅ Safe & Consistent Shots

| Shot           | Benefit                              |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------ |
| **Left Orbit**| Feeds directly to left flipper       |
| **Left Ramp** | Predictable return; essential for captures |
| **Captive Ball**| Ball held; time to plan next move    |
| **Scoop**     | Ball held; full control for next shot |

### 🎮 Ball Control Positions

- **Left Flipper Trap:** Best position after right ramp/orbit shots
- **Right Flipper Trap:** Best after left ramp or center shots
- **Post Pass:** Transfer between flippers safely; use right-post pass to set up left ramp
- **Live Catch:** Clean catches after orbit returns
