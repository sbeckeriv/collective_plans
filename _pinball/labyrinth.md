## 🚪 Modes

### How to Start Modes

1. **Spell LABYRINTH** by hitting white arrow shots
2. **Hit the U-Turn** (either side) or Wiseman scoop when lit

**Mode difficulty ramps up:**
- 1st mode: 1 letter needed
- 2nd mode: 2 letters
- 3rd mode: 3 letters
- ...and so on <kcite ref="31"/>

### Changing Modes

- Hit the **Change Mode target** to cycle within current row
- Spell **HELP** (inlanes/outlanes) → shoot Helping Hands scoop to switch rows

---

### 🔨 The Knockers (Top Row)

**Objective**: Hit yellow shots (ramps or horseshoe for 2x)

**How to Complete**:
1. Make 5 yellow shots
2. Wait for "Knock, and the door will open"
3. Hit the drop target between horseshoe shots
4. Quickly shoot through the doorway

**Orb**: Complete the mode <kcite ref="31"/>

---

### 🛡️ The Four Guards (Top Row)

**Objective**: Match all shot colors to the correct color

**How to Complete**:
1. Ramp/orbit shots toggle between blue and red
2. **Ask the question** (upper loop or center ramp) to learn correct color
3. The guards lie! Opposite of what they say is correct
4. Match all 4 shots to correct color
5. Hit the right horseshoe lane

**Orb**: Choose correct door on FIRST try <kcite ref="31"/>

> ⚠️ **Warning**: If you don't ask the question first, you WILL be wrong!

---

### 🔥 The Fireys (Top Row)

**Objective**: Pop Firey heads and collect jackpots

**How to Complete**:
1. Mode starts with auto-plunged ball to trap
2. Hit trapped captive ball to "pop head" = Add-a-Ball
3. Hit red shot to relight trap
4. Maximum 5 add-a-balls
5. After 5th head pop, roving super jackpot appears

**Orb**: Pop 5 Firey heads total (cumulative) <kcite ref="31"/>

---

### 🧹 The Cleaners (Bottom Row)

**Objective**: Build hurry-up value and collect

**How to Complete**:
1. Spin the left orbit spinner to build value
2. Shoot right orbit to collect
3. Complete 3 collects before clock hits 13

**Orb**: Complete mode with 5M+ total score <kcite ref="31"/>

---

### 🚪 The Oubliette (Bottom Row)

**Objective**: Complete shot pairs

**How to Complete**:
1. Shoot any lit shot to activate it
2. Shoot the SAME shot again to complete
3. Complete all 5 doors

**Orb**: Complete 5 doors total (cumulative) <kcite ref="31"/>

> 💡 **Tip**: If you hit a different shot, that becomes your new active shot!

---

### 💨 The Bog of Eternal Stench (Bottom Row)

**Objective**: Hit orange shots, avoid green shots

**Shot Sequence** (always the same):
1. Left ramp
2. Right ramp  
3. Left orbit
4. Right horseshoe
5. Right orbit

**Orb**: Complete 5 orange shots total (cumulative) <kcite ref="31"/>

> ⚠️ **Warning**: Hit a green shot and you'll **STINK FOREVER** - your high score will be displayed in green! <kcite ref="31"/>

## 🎱 Multiballs

### Brick Keeper Multiball

**How to Start**:
1. Complete green targets to light locks
2. Lock 1: Left orbit
3. Lock 2: Center ramp
4. Lock 3/Start: Right orbit

**Scoring**:
- Red arrows = Jackpots
- Collect jackpots to light Super Jackpot (left orbit)
- Captive ball spinner increases Super value

**Orb**: Collect 3 Super Jackpots (cumulative) <kcite ref="31"/>

> 💡 **Tip**: On Ball 3, if not started yet, all lock shots are automatically lit!

---

### Friend Multiball

**How to Start**:
- Collect a jackpot for each friend (Hoggle, Ludo, Sir Didymus)
- Left scoop flashes green when ready
- Hold flipper to cancel if you want higher friend levels first

**Scoring**:
- Random shots lit blue/yellow/red
- Collect all 3 colors → shoot left scoop for jackpot

**Orb**: Collect at least one Friends jackpot <kcite ref="31"/>

---

### Tea Time Multiball

**How to Start**: Mystery award only (Ello/Worm shot when purple)

**Scoring**:
- Left ramp: Collect value
- Orbits: Increase value
- U-turn: Multiply value
- After 3 jackpots: Victory laps begin <kcite ref="31"/>

### Wiseman Multiball

**How to Start**: Complete your 4th Wiseman Quest

## 👥 Friends System

Friends are found and leveled by hitting colored shots.

### How Friends Work

1. **Find** the friend (combo shots when flashing)
2. **Collect items** (hit solid lit shots)
3. **Collect jackpot** at Helping Hands scoop
4. Friend levels up, returns to "not found" state

> 💡 **Tip**: Collect 10 friend items total to light Extra Ball!

---

### Finding Friends - Combo Requirements

When a friend is **not found**, their circle inserts will be **flashing** in their color. You must complete a combo to find them.

| Level       | Combo Required                |
| ----------- | ----------------------------- |
| **Level 1** | Single shot (no combo needed) |
| **Level 2** | 2-shot timed combo            |
| **Level 3** | 3-shot timed combo            |
| **Level 4** | 4-shot timed combo            |

**How Combos Work**:
- Hit consecutive shots of that friend's color within the combo timer
- Miss a shot = restart the combo from scratch
- Once found, inserts change from **flashing → solid**
- Solid shots now **collect items** instead of finding

> 💡 **Tip**: Ramps and orbits often flow into each other - plan combos around natural shot sequences!

## 🏰 Wizard Modes

### Poisoned Peach (Mini-Wizard)

**Requirement**: Complete one row of modes (top 3 OR bottom 3)

**Gameplay**:
- Timed mode with full ball save
- Hit drop targets to break the spell
- Ramps increase value (combos = more)
- Break spell before clock hits 13 for time bonus <kcite ref="31"/>

---

### Goblin City Multiball (Wizard Mode)

**Requirement**: Complete all 6 modes

**Phase 1 - Gate Golem Battle**:
- Single ball, alternate ramps and captive ball
- Defeat golem, shoot left ramp to enter city

**Phase 2 - Run & Hide**:
- 3-ball multiball
- Shoot 4 blue shots
- Lock balls in designated areas

**Phase 3 - Attack (Here Come the Rocks!)**:
- 6-BALL MULTIBALL!
- All shots = jackpots
- Wiseman scoop = Super Jackpot
- **Orb**: Collect 5 jackpots

**After Goblin City**: Mode progress resets - play all modes again! <kcite ref="31"/>

---

### Battle Jareth (Ultimate Wizard)

**Requirement**: Collect ALL 13 Orbs

**Phase 1 - Remember the Words**:
- Complete yellow shots, then red shot
- Sets increase: 1 yellow → 2 yellow → etc.
- Complete 6 red shots total

**Phase 2 - Say the Words**:
- Repeat the 6 red shots in order
- Complete to escape the Labyrinth!

**Scoring**: Based on remaining orbs - lose orbs if clock hits 13 or you drain <kcite ref="31"/>

> ⚠️ **Note**: Game ends after facing Jareth, win or lose!

## 📊 Feature Stacking

### What Stacks Well

✅ **Modes + Multiballs**: Run a mode during Brick Keeper MB for massive points

✅ **Ludo Level 1 + Modes**: 50% bonus applies to all mode scoring

✅ **Orb Multipliers + Everything**: 3x on a key shot multiplies ALL scoring on that shot

✅ **Friendzy + Friend MB**: Delay Friend MB start to level up friends first <kcite ref="83"/>

### What Doesn't Stack Well

❌ **Multiple Multiballs**: Generally can't stack different multiballs

❌ **Friendzy during Friend MB**: Friendzy delays until MB ends <kcite ref="103"/>

> 💡 **Strategy**: "Rules require a more cerebral approach as stacking of multiballs and modes work well with some and is horrible for others" <kcite ref="83"/>

## ⚠️ Dangerous vs Safe Shots

### 🔴 Dangerous Shots

| Shot                    | Risk                                                           |
| ----------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Outlanes**            | "Very brutal outlanes" - most drains go here <kcite ref="77"/> |
| **Upper flipper shots** | Often reject and roll back down <kcite ref="93"/>              |
| **Wiseman scoop**       | "Insanely tight" <kcite ref="93"/>                             |
| **Left spinner/fork**   | Can be tough to hit consistently <kcite ref="87"/>             |
| **Bricked ramps**       | Missed shots often = outlane drain <kcite ref="118"/>          |

### 🟢 Safer Shots

| Shot                             | Notes                                                      |
| -------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Ramps**                        | When properly dialed in, very satisfying <kcite ref="73"/> |
| **Shots from "correct" flipper** | Each shot has an optimal flipper <kcite ref="83"/>         |
| **U-Turn**                       | Central location, feeds back to flippers                   |
| **Center ramp**                  | Good flow when made cleanly                                |

### Shot Angle Matters!

"This game is reliant on shot angle for them to be made, it does require precise ball to flipper placement... if you use the 'correct' flipper to make the shot, it goes very smoothly." <kcite ref="83"/>

## ❓ Does Completing Modes Change Gameplay?

**YES!**

1. **After completing one row (3 modes)**: Poisoned Peach starts automatically

2. **After completing all 6 modes**: 
   - Goblin City Multiball qualifies
   - After Goblin City ends, **all mode progress resets**
   - You can play all modes again! <kcite ref="31"/>

3. **Helping Hands row change**: No longer available after Poisoned Peach

4. **Orb requirements**: Some are cumulative across attempts (Fireys heads, Bog shots, Oubliette doors)

## 💡 Additional Tips

### Setup Matters

- **Pitch**: 7.0-7.5 degrees recommended <kcite ref="83"/>
- **Level**: Must be perfectly level or drains increase <kcite ref="92"/>
- **Flipper power**: Can be adjusted in service menu <kcite ref="101"/>

### Extra Balls

Light Extra Ball by:
- Collecting 10 friend items total
- Completing 2 Wiseman Quests
- 3rd Mystery award (if under limit) <kcite ref="31"/>

### Wiseman Quests

- Knock down drop target → shoot scoop
- Quest types: Ramps, Spins, Targets, Orbits, Loops
- Odd quests (1, 3, 5, 7+) start Friendzy
- Quest 4 starts Wiseman Multiball
- 6 quests = Orb <kcite ref="31"/>

### Mystery Awards

- Light by hitting targets near Wiseman scoop
- Collect under upper right flipper
- Possible awards: Friend item, complete quest, Extra Ball, 750k points, Tea Time MB, Four Guards color reveal <kcite ref="31"/>

### Didymus Defense

- Post above left outlane
- Default: 1 free use per ball
- Sir Didymus Level 1: Reactivates via spelling HELP <kcite ref="31"/>

### Challenge Mode

Hold RIGHT flipper for 3 seconds in attract mode to access:
- One-shot Goblin City attempt
- One-shot Battle Jareth attempt <kcite ref="103"/>
