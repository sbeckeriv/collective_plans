# 🥊 Champion Pub - Strategy Guide

*Bally, 1998 — WPC-95 Era*

> **"Train your boxer. Win the title. Collect the jackpot."**

Champion Pub (Bally, 1998) is a WPC-95 machine with a boxing theme featuring an interactive boxer toy that physically punches. The game has training mini-modes for Speed Bag, Jump Rope, Sparring, and Heavy Bag, each building your boxer's skill level before the Championship Fight multiball. Our goal: **300M–700M** by maxing your fighter's stats, triggering the Championship Fight, and collecting the title jackpot.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Complete all four training modes** to build your fighter's stats
- [ ] **Speed Bag:** Hit the speed bag toy (left side target) rapidly for combo bonuses
- [ ] **Jump Rope:** Shoot ramps in rhythm with the rope animation on DMD
- [ ] **Sparring:** Hit the center standup targets in sequence
- [ ] **Heavy Bag:** Left loop repeatedly for heavy bag training combos
- [ ] **Lock balls** after training completion to start Championship Fight multiball

## 🥊 Core Mechanics

### Skill Shot

| Skill Shot | How | Reward |
|---|---|---|
| **Speed bag** | Short plunge to hit the speed bag target | Speed training advance + points |
| **Standard** | Full plunge | Ball to upper area |

### Training Modes (4)

Each training mode builds one of four boxer stats:

**Speed Bag (Toy — Left):**
- Hit the physical speed bag toy repeatedly
- Each hit combo-chains (1M → 2M → 3M per consecutive hit)
- Complete the speed threshold → Speed stat maxed

**Jump Rope (DMD + Ramps):**
- The DMD shows a jump rope animation with a rhythm beat
- Shoot the left or right ramp in sync with the "jump" cue
- Perfect timing = 3M; early/late = 1M; miss = 0
- Complete the sequence → Endurance stat maxed

**Sparring (Center Standup Targets):**
- Targets light in sequence (jab, jab, cross, hook pattern)
- Hit them in the illuminated order for combo bonuses
- Complete the pattern → Power stat maxed

**Heavy Bag (Left Loop Combos):**
- Shoot the left loop repeatedly for combo chains
- Each consecutive left loop within a time window adds to the combo
- Complete the combo threshold → Stamina stat maxed

### Boxer Stat System

All four training modes contribute to your boxer's stats. Each maxed stat:
- Increases Championship Fight jackpot values
- Unlocks higher training round bonuses
- Adds a "round" to the Championship Fight (higher stats = more rounds = more jackpots)

### Championship Fight Multiball

**How to start:** Complete at least 2 training modes → lock balls in the corner lock areas.

**Jackpot structure:**
- 3-ball multiball (the fight!)
- Each "round" (major shot sequence) represents one fight round
- **KO Jackpot:** Complete all fight rounds → center scoop → 50M–200M+ (scales with all 4 stats maxed)
- Speed Bag toy activates during multiball for bonus mid-fight scoring

## 💡 Pro Tips

> **Complete all 4 training modes before locking.** The difference between a 2-stat fight and a 4-stat fight is 3–4× the jackpot ceiling. Don't rush to multiball.

> **Jump Rope requires listening.** This is the only training mode where audio matters. Turn up the volume and feel the rhythm — the ramp shot timing is audio-cued.

> **Speed bag combos compound.** On the speed bag, each consecutive hit within 1 second of the last doubles in value. Don't rush; strike rhythmically.
