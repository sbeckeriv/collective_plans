# ☠️ Pirates of the Caribbean - Strategy Guide

*Stern Pinball, 2006 — Designed by John Borg*

> **"Why is the rum always gone? Because you shot the ship."**

Pirates of the Caribbean is a Stern Spike-era licensed machine based on the first two films. It features a rotating ship toy (the Black Pearl), ramp shots, an interactive Davy Jones Locker, and three multiballs with a deep mode progression tied to scenes from the films. Our goal: **300M–600M** by stacking multiballs with active modes and farming Tortuga jackpots.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Shoot the right ramp** to advance through movie scenes (your main progression shot)
- [ ] **Hit the ship (Black Pearl toy)** to qualify and start Ship Multiball
- [ ] **Collect the Tortuga saucer** when lit — it awards valuable mystery prizes
- [ ] **Lock balls in Davy Jones' Locker** for Locker Multiball
- [ ] **Complete LOOT standup targets** to light Loot Multiball
- [ ] **Stack Ship Multiball with an active mode** for maximum jackpot values

---

## 🏴‍☠️ Core Mechanics

### Skill Shot

| Skill Shot | How | Reward |
|---|---|---|
| **Standard** | Full plunge to right lane | Points + mode start progress |
| **Super skill shot** | Short plunge to hit spinner, then make the lit shot | Bonus + bonus multiplier |

### Scene Modes (Film Progression)

Shoot the right ramp to advance through scenes from the movies:

| Scenes | Notable Features |
|---|---|
| Cursed Gold, Tortuga, etc. | Timed shot sequences, each with escalating jackpot values |
| Dead Man's Chest scenes | Introduce Kraken and higher-value shots |

Each completed scene awards a scene bonus and advances toward the wizard mode. Completing all scenes unlocks **Davy Jones' Locker** wizard mode.

### Tortuga Saucer

The center saucer awards mystery prizes when lit. Prizes include:
- Points bonuses
- Extra ball lighting
- Mode advancement
- Jackpot value boosts

Shoot it whenever lit — it's the safest center shot on the machine.

### LOOT Standup Targets

Four standup targets spell LOOT. Completion lights **Loot Multiball** (2-ball) and adds to jackpot base values.

---

## 🎳 Multiballs

### Ship Multiball (Primary)

**How to start:** Hit the Black Pearl ship toy repeatedly to qualify (3 hits typically), then shoot the ship one final time to lock and release.

**Jackpot structure:**
- Shoot the ship during multiball for jackpots (~5M–15M each, escalating)
- **Super Jackpot:** Shoot the locker after collecting all regular jackpots — worth 30M–50M+
- Ships can be hit multiple times per multiball for repeated jackpots

> 🏆 **Stack it:** Start a scene mode before triggering Ship Multiball. Mode shots during multiball count AND score jackpot multipliers.

### Locker Multiball

**How to start:** Lock 2 balls in Davy Jones' Locker (left side scoop, lit after scene progression).

**Gameplay:** 3-ball multiball, shoot lit shots for jackpots. The Locker itself is the super jackpot target.

### Loot Multiball

**How to start:** Complete LOOT standup targets.

**Gameplay:** 2-ball multiball, less lucrative than the others but fast to start.

---

## 🔄 Progression Systems

- **Scene completion** → unlocks scenes for repeat play at higher values
- **Loot multiball** → each trigger during the game increases base jackpot values
- **Ship hits** → accumulate between multiballs; more hits = faster qualification on next attempt

---

## 🔀 Stacking & Combos

| Stack | Effect |
|---|---|
| Active scene mode + Ship Multiball | All mode shots score AND collect jackpots |
| Loot Multiball + Ship Multiball | Both multiballs overlap; ship jackpots apply to both ball counts |
| Tortuga mystery during mode | Can award instant mode completions |

**Avoid:** Starting Locker Multiball on its own without a scene mode active — the jackpots alone don't justify the drain risk without mode multipliers.

---

## 🧙 Wizard Mode

### Davy Jones' Locker (Wizard)

**Requirement:** Complete all scenes from both films.

**Gameplay:** All shots lit simultaneously, giant jackpot values available. A 4–6 minute session with skilled play yields the machine's peak scores.

## 📊 Shot Analysis

| Shot | Value | Risk |
|---|---|---|
| Right ramp | Scene advance (primary) | Low |
| Black Pearl ship | Multiball qualification | Medium — toy-based |
| Tortuga saucer | Mystery + points | Low |
| Left ramp | Scene advance (secondary) | Low |
| LOOT targets | Multiball start | Low |
| Davy Jones Locker | Ball lock / super jackpot | Medium |

### Dangerous Shots

- **Ship toy hit** — the ball can kick sideways off the ship at bad angles; play right flipper
- **Left outlane** — fast exit from left ramp if underpowered

---

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Game
1. Start a scene mode via right ramp (3–4 shots)
2. Hit the ship 3 times to qualify multiball
3. Collect Tortuga mystery mid-approach

### Mid Game
1. Trigger Ship Multiball with an active scene — jackpots immediately
2. Complete LOOT targets for Loot Multiball availability
3. Lock second ball for Locker Multiball prep

### Late Game
1. Stack Locker + scene mode
2. Chase wizard mode if 14+ scenes complete
3. Collect end-of-ball bonus via bonus multiplier shots

### Score Targets

| Performance | Score |
|---|---|
| Beginner | 50M–150M |
| Solid | 150M–350M |
| Strong | 350M–600M |
| Elite | 600M+ |

---

## 💡 Pro Tips

> **Right ramp is your engine.** Every good ball starts with right ramp shots advancing scenes. Don't deviate until a mode is running.

> **The ship is a trap for new players.** It's tempting to bash the ship constantly, but qualifying multiball without an active mode running wastes the stack opportunity.

> **Tortuga is free money.** Never pass up a lit saucer — the mystery can award game-changing prizes.
