# 🎳 300 Strategy Guide
## Gottlieb 1975 | Bowl a Perfect Game

---

> ⚠️ **IMPORTANT NOTE FOR MODERN PINBALL PLAYERS**
>
> 300 is a **1975 electromechanical (EM) pinball machine**. Many features of modern pinball do not exist here:
> - ❌ No Video Modes
> - ❌ No Wizard Modes
> - ❌ No Stacking or Combos
> - ❌ No Digital Displays or DMD
>
> **The title refers to a perfect bowling score.** Completing the serial rollover lanes drives the mechanical backbox animation, adding bowling balls to simulate frames. This is one of the most charming EM themes of the 1970s.
>
> 300 is the 4-player companion to **King Pin** (model 387), which is the 2-player version of the same design.

### 🎱 Plunge Strategy

EM plunging is about ball placement, not skill shot timing:

- **Soft Plunge**: Routes ball toward the pop bumpers for early point accumulation
- **Medium Plunge**: Aims for the star rollover to build rollover lane progress
- **Hard Plunge**: Sends ball toward serial rollover lanes for maximum frame credit

> 💡 **Pro Tip**: The serial rollover lanes are your primary scoring feature. Plunge to feed them whenever possible.

---

## 🎳 Serial Rollover Lanes — The Heart of the Game

The two parallel lanes of serial rollovers are what make 300 unique. Each rollover collected in sequence advances the backbox bowling animation:

| Rollovers Completed | Backbox Animation |
| ------------------- | ----------------- |
| Sequence progressing | Bowling balls added |
| Full sequence       | Frame complete — bonus lit |

**Strategy:**
- Route the ball through the lanes consistently for bonus and animation progress
- Both lanes score separately — covering both lanes maximizes frame advancement
- The kick-out holes are positioned to feed back toward the rollover lanes when the ball exits

> 💡 **Pro Tip**: The parallel lane layout means both left and right flippers have a clear shot at the respective lanes. Learn which flipper feeds which lane.

## 🎯 Standup Targets

Four standup targets are scattered across the playfield:

| Target Action       | Points    | Additional Effect           |
| ------------------- | --------- | --------------------------- |
| Single target hit   | 500 pts   | +1 bonus unit               |
| Completing the set  | Bonus lit | Opens path to kick-out hole |

**Target Strategy:**
- Hit standup targets whenever the ball passes near them — each builds bonus
- Targets are secondary to rollover lanes but shouldn't be ignored
- A deflection off a standup target can feed the pop bumper area

## 🕳️ Kick-out Holes

Two kick-out holes provide bonus awards and help drive the bowling animation:

| Kick-out Condition | Award           |
| ------------------ | --------------- |
| Base               | Points + bonus  |
| After completing rollovers | Higher award |

- Kick-out holes eject the ball back into active playfield territory — be ready
- The holes are positioned so the ejected ball has a chance to roll through rollover lanes again

## 🌀 Spinning Target

The center-stage spinning target adds rotational scoring:

- Each full rotation scores points
- Best hit with a clean center-flipper shot
- Works well in combination with the pop bumper zone above it

---

## ⛳ Right Outlane Ball Return Gate

One of 300's helpful features — the right outlane has a gate that returns the ball to the shooter lane rather than draining it:

- **Not always active** — gate must be lit to function
- Completing certain rollover sequences lights the gate
- A saved drain from this gate is a major swing in a competitive game

> 💡 **Pro Tip**: Know whether the right outlane gate is lit before shooting toward the right side. A lit gate makes aggressive right-side play much safer.

## 📈 Scoring Strategy

### Early Game (Ball 1–2)
1. 🎳 Work the serial rollover lanes consistently
2. 🎯 Hit standup targets when convenient to build bonus
3. 🌀 Spinner shots for quick points
4. ⛳ Identify if right outlane gate is lit for safety

### Mid Game (Ball 2–3)
1. 🎳 Keep driving rollover lane progress
2. 🕳️ Kick-out hole shots when bonus is high
3. 📈 Monitor bonus meter heading toward max
4. ⛳ Use right outlane gate aggressively if lit

### Final Ball
1. 📈 Maximize bonus before draining
2. 🎳 Complete as many rollover sequences as possible
3. 🎯 Clear remaining standup targets
