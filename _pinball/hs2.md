# 🏎️ The Getaway: High Speed II - Strategy Guide

*Williams, 1992 — Designed by Steve Ritchie*

> **"Floor it. Hit the supercharger. Drive through the jackpot."**

The Getaway: High Speed II (Williams, 1992) is a Steve Ritchie WPC masterpiece — the sequel to High Speed (1986). It features a supercharger loop (the single fastest shot in 1990s pinball), a freeway on-ramp, a helicopter, RPM meter that advances as you hit the supercharger, and a gear shift system where the ball shifts through 6 speeds. Our goal: **300M–1B** by hitting the supercharger to red-line the RPM meter and triggering Supercharger Multiball at maximum speed.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Hit the supercharger** (left loop) repeatedly — every consecutive hit advances the RPM meter
- [ ] **Red-line the RPM** (6th gear) to light Supercharger Multiball
- [ ] **Complete the freeway on-ramp** (right ramp) to advance gear shifts
- [ ] **Collect the helicopter** (right scoop) when lit for mode bonuses
- [ ] **Use the left kickback** when lit to save drain balls
- [ ] **Light the left kickback** via left loop completions at the start of every ball

## 🚗 Core Mechanics

### Skill Shot

| Skill Shot | How | Reward |
|---|---|---|
| **Supercharger** | Full plunge → ball feeds supercharger | RPM advance + 1st gear |
| **Controlled** | Short plunge → left side | Manual approach to supercharger |

### Supercharger (Left Loop) — The Central Shot

The supercharger is a tight left loop that the ball races through at speed. It's the fastest moving ball in WPC-era pinball:
- Each supercharger pass advances the RPM meter by 1 step
- **6 steps = Red Line** → Supercharger Multiball lit
- Consecutive passes (without the ball going elsewhere) chain together for increasing RPM bonus

**RPM Meter:**
| Gear | RPM | What's Lit |
|---|---|---|
| 1st | 1,000 | Supercharger 1M |
| 2nd | 2,000 | Supercharger 2M |
| 3rd | 3,000 | Freeway mode |
| 4th | 4,000 | Kickback relight |
| 5th | 5,000 | Extra ball |
| 6th (Red Line) | 6,000+ | Supercharger Multiball |

### Freeway On-Ramp (Right Ramp)

The right ramp is the "freeway" entry:
- Advances gear progression (contributes to RPM)
- Lights the helicopter award
- Chains with the supercharger for combo bonuses

### Helicopter (Right Scoop)

The helicopter scoop when lit awards:
- Mode activation (Freeway mode, Police Chase mode)
- Mid-ball bonus points
- Supercharger RPM credit

### Left Kickback

The left kickback is critical:
- Light it early each ball via the left loop
- Each left loop lit = one kickback charge
- Kickback fires from the left outlane back into play

### Gear Shift System

The DMD shows a dashboard with a gear indicator. Each gear:
- Increases the value of subsequent supercharger hits
- 6th gear (Red Line) triggers multiball

## 🎳 Multiball

### Supercharger Multiball

**How to start:** Red-line the RPM meter (6th gear via supercharger loops).

**Jackpot structure:**
- 3-ball multiball at maximum speed
- **Supercharger jackpot:** Shoot the supercharger during multiball — jackpot starts at 10M and escalates to 50M+ per hit
- **Super Jackpot (Red Line again):** Red-line the RPM DURING multiball → 100M–300M+
- All three balls in the supercharger simultaneously is the elite play — all jackpots trigger on the same loop

> 🏆 **The supercharger in multiball is your entire scoring arc.** Keep feeding it. With all three balls in the loop at once, you can score 3 jackpots per second.

## 🔄 Progression Systems

- **RPM meter** resets on drain (not saved between balls) — red-line every ball
- **Extra ball** lit at 5th gear on first qualifying
- **Kickback charges** replenish via left loop hits

### Dangerous Areas

- **Right outlane** — fast after freeway ramp; no right kickback
- **Post-supercharger exit** — the ball exits the loop fast and goes to the right flipper; be ready immediately
- **3-ball multiball chaos** — the supercharger at 3-ball speed is genuinely difficult to control; let the machine's geometry work for you

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Ball 1
1. Light kickback (left loop)
2. Supercharger × 4 (4th gear, kickback relit)
3. Freeway ramp for gear advance
4. Red-line attempt (6th gear) → Supercharger Multiball

### Ball 2
1. Kickback immediately
2. Supercharger to Red Line again
3. During multiball: supercharger jackpot × 5+
4. Red Line during multiball → Super Jackpot

### Ball 3
1. Second Red Line (kickback saves extend this ball)
2. Full multiball Super Jackpot
3. Post-multiball: gear advancement for bonus

## 💡 Pro Tips

> **The supercharger is everything.** Every other shot exists to support the supercharger loop. Master the loop first; everything else is secondary.

> **Red-line every ball.** 6 consecutive supercharger passes is achievable in 15–20 seconds with consistent aim. Don't waste time on other shots before reaching Red Line.

> **During multiball, feed all three balls into the supercharger.** This is the machine's designed peak state. It's chaotic but the scoring is unmatched.
