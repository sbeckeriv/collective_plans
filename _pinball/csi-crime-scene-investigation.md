# 🔬 CSI: Crime Scene Investigation - Strategy Guide

*Stern Pinball, 2008 — Designed by Pat Lawlor*

> **"The evidence doesn't lie."**

CSI is a multiball-heavy Pat Lawlor design built around three deep, distinct multiball systems — Skull, Centrifuge, and the sprawling Microscope Multiball — plus a trio of timed crime-scene modes. Accurate shooting and multiball success are everything here. Our goal: qualify and complete all three multiballs to light the **50-million-point wizard shot**.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Take the skill shot at the Morgue sink hole** — starts at 500K, +100K per subsequent lit attempt
- [ ] **Light evidence early** (red, green, or blue) at the Microscope — this is your longest-lead multiball, so start building it from Ball 1
- [ ] **Spell C-S-I via the inlane/rollover switches** for 1x bonus + 100K, then stack more multipliers via the Lab award and right orbit shots
- [ ] **Stack Skull and Centrifuge multiballs whenever possible** — accurate shooting matters more than memorizing every stage
- [ ] **Complete all three multiballs** (Skull, Centrifuge, Microscope) to light the 50M wizard shot

---

## 🔍 Core Mechanics

### Skill Shot

The **Morgue sink hole** is your skill shot entrance — starts at 500K, increasing by 100K per subsequent attempt when lit. The Morgue also grants access to the **AV Lab**, which can award extra balls and light Specials.

### Skull Multiball (4 Levels)

1. Hit the **skull ramp** to light locks, then shoot again to trap balls in the skull's eye sockets
2. **Level 1:** Six yellow arrow shots, 250K each
3. **Level 2:** All bullet targets, 500K each
4. **Level 3:** Dual skull hits within a 5-second window, 750K each
5. **Level 4:** 1 million points plus a completion bonus

> 🎯 **Pro Tip:** Level 3's dual-hit timing window is the hardest part of Skull Multiball — practice quick back-to-back skull shots before relying on this in a real game.

### Centrifuge Multiball

Hitting the ball into the **Centrifuge** spins it on a disc for a short countdown. Build jackpots via switch hits, flipper pumping ("Big Wow"), or an immediate multiball start. **Collect triple jackpots to complete the mode.**

### Microscope Multiball ("The Mother of All Multiball Modes")

1. **Qualify** by collecting **5 evidence pieces of one color** (red, green, or blue) at the Microscope
2. Shoot the Microscope again to start — this is a **6-stage** mode requiring a minimum of **23 multiball shots**
3. Stages escalate: **single jackpot (275K) → double → triple → quadruple jackpot (1.375M) → final Mega Jackpot (5.5M)**

> 💎 **Pro Tip:** Microscope Multiball has the longest setup but the biggest single payoff (the 5.5M Mega Jackpot) — start collecting evidence of one color as early in the game as possible since it takes real time to qualify.

## 📊 C-S-I Lanes & Bonus Multiplier

- **Spell C-S-I** via the 3 inlane/rollover switches for **1x bonus + 100K**
- Collecting the **Lab award** adds another **1x multiplier**
- Subsequent **right orbit shots** add **3x bonus** each

## 🏆 Wizard Mode

**Requirement:** Complete all three multiballs (Skull, Centrifuge, Microscope).

**Reward:** A **50-million-point wizard shot** lights up — a rare achievement that most players never see in practice, since fully completing all three deep multiball systems in a single game is a serious undertaking.

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **General high drain risk** | This game is widely noted as a significant drain risk overall — prioritize accuracy over power on every shot |
| **Skull Level 3 dual-hit timing** | Forcing a rushed second hit to beat the 5-second window risks a wild shot | Only attempt when you have clean control of the ball |
| **Microscope Multiball's high shot requirement (23+)** | Long multiballs mean more exposure to drain over time | Play patiently — this mode rewards endurance, not speed |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Game (Ball 1)

1. Take the skill shot at the Morgue
2. Start collecting evidence of one color toward Microscope Multiball
3. Spell C-S-I for your first bonus multiplier

**Target Score:** 5M–15M

### Mid Game (Ball 2)

1. Qualify and run Skull Multiball, pushing through as many of its 4 levels as you can
2. Trigger Centrifuge Multiball and chase the triple-jackpot completion
3. Run a crime scene mode (Suspect Shakedown, Fingerprint Frenzy, or Ballistics) for supplemental scoring

**Target Score:** 15M–40M

### Late Game (Ball 3+)

1. Cash in Microscope Multiball if you've collected enough evidence — its Mega Jackpot is your biggest single payout
2. If all three multiballs are complete, shoot for the 50M wizard shot
3. Stack bonus multipliers (C-S-I + Lab + right orbit) for a strong end-of-ball bonus

**Target Score per ball:** 20M–60M+
