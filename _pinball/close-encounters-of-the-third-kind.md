# 🛸 Close Encounters of the Third Kind - Strategy Guide

*Gottlieb, 1978 — Designed by Ed Krynski*

> **"Light the spinner. Watch for the star."**

Close Encounters of the Third Kind pairs Gottlieb's signature roto-target with a clean ABC rollover/drop-target loop. Once you understand the simple priority rule — spin unless a star roto-target appears — this game plays itself into a strong, repeatable score. Our goal: max the bonus multiplier at 5X (solid-state version) and light both Special and Extra Ball.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Shoot the left standup target first** to light the spinner
- [ ] **Default to spinning from the right flipper** and hitting drop targets from the left — this is the core, repeatable loop
- [ ] **If one of the visible roto-targets is a star, shoot that instead** — stars are worth more and advance your bonus multiplier
- [ ] **Hit A-B-C rollovers** to increase the indicated roto-target's value, and complete all three to light **Extra Ball** and **Special**
- [ ] **Complete the 5-bank drop targets** for bonus multiplier advances, +5 bonuses, Special/Extra Ball lighting, and 5,000 points — then the bank resets for another run
- [ ] **Remember bonus resets after each ball** — there's no point banking strategy across balls here, just maximize within each one

## 👽 Core Mechanics

### Roto-Targets & A-B-C Rollovers

- Hitting **A-B-C rollovers** increases the value of the **indicated roto-target**
- Completing all three A-B-C rollovers lights **Extra Ball** and **Special**
- The **star roto-target** is worth **500 points** and **increases your bonus multiplier** when hit

> ⭐ **Pro Tip — Core Strategy:** Default to spinning the spinner from the right flipper and hitting drop targets from the left flipper. The *only* time you should deviate from this loop is when one of the visible roto-targets shows a **star** — shoot that instead, since it's both higher value and advances your multiplier.

### Drop Targets (5-Bank)

| Target Color | Value |
|---|---|
| Black | 500 |
| Yellow | 5,000 |

**Completing the full bank** rewards: a bonus multiplier increase, **+5 bonuses**, lighting Special and Extra Ball, **5,000 points**, and a full **reset** of the bank for another run.

### Spinner

Lit via the left standup target, the spinner is your primary repeatable scoring shot from the right flipper — safe, steady, and the backbone of the entire scoring strategy.

### Bonus System

- **Bonus resets after each ball** — there's no carryover, so maximize fully within each individual ball
- **Solid-state version:** bonus multiplier goes up to **5X**
- **EM version:** bonus multiplier caps at **3X**

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Ball

1. Shoot the left standup target to light the spinner
2. Start the default loop: spin from the right flipper, drop targets from the left
3. Watch for a star roto-target and prioritize it whenever it appears

### Mid Ball

1. Work A-B-C rollovers to boost roto-target values and progress toward Extra Ball/Special
2. Complete the 5-bank drop target run for the multiplier bump and reset
3. Keep defaulting to the spin/drop-target loop between special opportunities

### Late Ball

1. Push your bonus multiplier toward the maximum (5X on solid-state, 3X on EM)
2. Take any Extra Ball or Special opportunities you've lit
3. Maximize final scoring within this ball, since bonus doesn't carry to the next
