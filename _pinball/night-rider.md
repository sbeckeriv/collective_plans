# 🚚 Night Rider - Strategy Guide

*Bally, 1976 — Designed by Greg Kmiec*

> **"Hit what's lit for its highest value. Skip the drop targets."**

Night Rider is a trucking-themed Bally classic built around a single, central kickout saucer whose value rotates through a sequence, and a Double Bonus that's lit at a specific point in that rotation. The drop targets exist but are widely considered a drain trap not worth the risk. Our goal: chase the saucer's highest lit values, especially Double Bonus, and largely ignore the drop target banks.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Focus on the top-center kickout saucer** — landing in it scores whatever value is currently lit, rotating through **1,000 → 50 → 3,000 → 50 → 5,000** based on switch hits (including the spinners)
- [ ] **The saucer also advances your end-of-ball bonus** with every landing
- [ ] **Double Bonus is lit whenever the 1,000-point saucer award is lit** — this is your best moment to shoot the saucer
- [ ] **Follow the core strategy: "hit what's lit for its highest value"** — track the rotation and time your saucer shot accordingly
- [ ] **Largely ignore the drop targets** — while a completed bank scores 3,000 and a special, veteran players consider them a drain risk not worth the trade-off

## 🚛 Core Mechanics

### Kickout Saucer (Your Main Objective)

The saucer's lit value **rotates through a sequence**: **1,000 → 50 → 3,000 → 50 → 5,000**, advanced by any 10 or 100-point switch hit (including spinner hits). Landing in the saucer:
- Scores the **currently lit value**
- **Advances your end-of-ball bonus**

### Double Bonus

**Double Bonus is lit whenever the 1,000-point saucer award is specifically lit** — this is the moment to prioritize the saucer shot above all else, since it converts an otherwise modest 1,000-point hit into a doubled bonus payout.

> 🎯 **Pro Tip — Core Strategy:** "Hit what's lit for its highest value." Pay attention to the saucer's current rotation state and time your shots to catch it at 5,000 or during the Double Bonus window, rather than shooting reflexively whenever you're near it.

### Drop Targets (Avoid By Default)

Two 5-bank drop target sets exist. Completing either bank scores **3,000 points**, awards a **Special**, and **resets both banks**. Despite this payout, **veteran players recommend largely ignoring the drop targets** — they're considered a genuine drain risk that isn't worth the trade-off compared to the safer saucer-focused strategy.

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **Drop targets** | Widely flagged as a drain trap relative to their payout | Default to ignoring them in favor of the saucer strategy |
| **Pop bumper unpredictability** (3 bumpers) | Standard mid-70s bumper chaos | Treat as incidental scoring |
| **Spinner shot timing** | Spinners affect the saucer's rotation state, so mistimed spins can leave you shooting into a low-value window | Track the rotation before committing to the saucer shot |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Ball

1. Start tracking the saucer's rotation state via spinner and switch hits
2. Shoot the saucer when it's at a high value (3,000 or 5,000) or during Double Bonus
3. Avoid the drop targets by default

### Mid Ball

1. Continue the saucer-focused loop, watching for the Double Bonus window
2. Build end-of-ball bonus passively through saucer landings
3. Only consider drop targets if you have genuinely safe control and want the Special

### Late Ball

1. Maximize saucer shots at their highest lit values
2. Bank your accumulated bonus, boosted by any Double Bonus collections
3. Avoid unnecessary risk-taking on drop targets this late in the ball

## 📋 Quick Reference: Strategic Priorities

**Throughout the game:** Track the kickout saucer's rotating value, prioritize shots during high-value and Double Bonus windows, avoid the drop targets by default.

**Target Outcome:** Consistently catching the saucer during its 5,000-point and Double Bonus windows, while avoiding unnecessary drop target risk, represents a strong, disciplined game on this trucking-themed classic.
