# 🍺 Family Guy - Strategy Guide

*Stern Pinball, 2007 — Designed by Pat Lawlor*

> **"Giggity giggity goo."**

Family Guy layers Fart Multiball, five TV Modes, Stewie's dual-pinball-table mini-playfield, and a six-goal final wizard mode ("Sperm Attack") into one of Pat Lawlor's most chaotic rulesheets. Our goal: complete TV Wizard, win Stewie Multiball's Super Jackpot, and qualify the full six-goal checklist for Sperm Attack.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Spell F-A-R-T (3 targets, repeatable) to start Fart Multiball** — the required completions increase by 1 each time, up to a max of 6
- [ ] **Spell PINBALL** to enable **Stewie's mini pinball machine** on the upper playfield
- [ ] **Complete all 5 TV Modes** to unlock the first sub-wizard, **TV Wizard**
- [ ] **Get Stewie to capture all the other family members** in his mini pinball game to start **Stewie Multiball**
- [ ] **First Fart Multiball starts as 2-ball with a very short ball saver** — subsequent ones start as 3-ball, so don't expect much grace on your first attempt
- [ ] **Track all six Sperm Attack goals** from early in the game — it's a long-term checklist, not a last-minute scramble

---

## 💨 Core Mechanics

### Fart Multiball

1. Complete the **F-A-R-T** targets (3 hits) to start
2. Each subsequent Fart Multiball requires **+1 more completion**, up to a maximum of **6**
3. **First Fart Multiball:** 2-ball, with a **very short ball saver** — be ready to play tight immediately
4. **Each additional Fart Multiball:** starts with **3 balls**

### Stewie's Mini Pinball Machine

- **Spell PINBALL** to enable Stewie's own miniature pinball table on the upper playfield
- Use it to **capture family members** one at a time
- **Capturing all of them** starts **Stewie Multiball**

### TV Modes & TV Wizard (Sub-Wizard #1)

- Complete all **5 TV Modes** to unlock **TV Wizard**
- In TV Wizard, hit the different **Jackpot shots before the Jackpot runs out** to score its current value — a race against a depleting timer rather than a fixed-value collection

### Stewie Multiball (Sub-Wizard #2)

- **Starts after Stewie captures all other family members** in his mini pinball game
- Both the **main playfield and Stewie's mini table play simultaneously**
- **Collect each character on both tables** for a chance at the **Super Jackpot**

## 🏆 Wizard Mode: Sperm Attack

**Requirement — complete all six goals:**

| Goal | Target |
|---|---|
| Evil Monkey jackpots | At least **5** |
| Crazy Chris shots | At least **10** |
| Fart Multiball jackpots | At least **15** |
| Beer Can awards | **All of them** |
| Stewie Multiball | Get the **Super Jackpot** |
| TV Wizard score | **25 million or more** |

**Gameplay:** Starts on Stewie's mini pinball table, then **migrates to the main playfield** with **4 balls in play** — shoot as many jackpot shots as possible during the chaos.

> 🎯 **Pro Tip:** Sperm Attack is a full-game checklist, not a single achievable combo — track your progress across all six goals from Ball 1, since some (like 15 Fart Multiball jackpots or all Beer Can awards) take sustained play across multiple balls to complete.

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **First Fart Multiball's short ball saver** | Minimal grace period means an early mistake can end the multiball almost immediately | Be in position and ready the instant the multiball starts |
| **Captive balls / Newton balls** | Can produce unusual rebound physics | Approach with controlled shots rather than assuming standard rebound behavior |
| **4-ball Sperm Attack chaos** | Extremely difficult to track and control | Prioritize making any lit jackpot shot over precision — volume matters more here |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Game (Ball 1)

1. Start spelling F-A-R-T toward your first Fart Multiball
2. Work toward spelling PINBALL to unlock Stewie's mini table
3. Begin chipping away at the 5 TV Modes

**Target Score:** 10M–30M

### Mid Game (Ball 2)

1. Run additional Fart Multiballs as you re-qualify them (remember the increasing target count)
2. Use Stewie's mini table to start capturing family members
3. Complete TV Modes toward TV Wizard, aiming for 25M+ in that mode for the Sperm Attack goal

**Target Score:** 30M–80M

### Late Game (Ball 3+)

1. Complete Stewie Multiball and chase its Super Jackpot
2. Check your Sperm Attack checklist — push toward whichever goals are still incomplete (Evil Monkey, Crazy Chris, Beer Cans, Fart jackpot count)
3. If all six goals are met, dive into Sperm Attack for the game's biggest, most chaotic scoring opportunity

**Target Score per ball:** 30M–100M+

## 📋 Quick Reference: Strategic Priorities

**Ball 1:** Start Fart Multiball, spell PINBALL, chip away at TV Modes.
**Ball 2:** Re-qualify Fart Multiball, use Stewie's table, push toward TV Wizard.
**Ball 3+:** Win Stewie Multiball's Super Jackpot, complete the Sperm Attack checklist, dive into the finale.

**Target High-Score Ballpark:** **80–200 million points** is a strong, competitive game; reaching Sperm Attack requires sustained, full-game progress across all six tracked goals.
