# 🎰 High Roller Casino - Strategy Guide

*Stern Pinball, 2001 — Designed by Keith P. Johnson & Jon Norris*

> **"Win all six casino games. Beat the house."**

High Roller Casino brings the full Vegas experience to a pinball table: six distinct casino games (21, Craps, Hi-Lo, Poker, Roulette, Slots) to win, a slot-machine-locked multiball, and three separate wizard modes. Our goal: collect all six casino games, run the slot machine multiball's escalating Super Jackpot, and reach one of the three wizard modes.

> ⚠️ **Tournament rule:** The **first time** you play a given casino game, you **lose** it (unless cheat game is enabled). The **second time**, you **win and collect** it. **Collecting all 6** is the prerequisite for starting a wizard mode.

### Slot Machine Multiball

1. Shoot the **slot machine** to light locks
2. **3 locks** starts a **4-ball multiball**
3. During multiball, shoot **all lit shots** for **single, double, and triple jackpots**
4. Shoot the **slot machine** for the **Super Jackpot** when lit
5. **The Super Jackpot's value depends on how many single, double, and triple jackpots you've already collected** — so building up jackpot count before going for the Super Jackpot pays off

> 💰 **Pro Tip:** Since the Super Jackpot scales with your prior collections, don't rush straight to the slot machine the moment it lights — collect as many of the single/double/triple jackpots as you reasonably can first.

### The Roulette Magnet

A **magnet under the roulette** can **stop the ball from completing the loop shot** — a built-in mechanical quirk specific to this machine. Don't assume every loop attempt will pass through; the magnet is part of the intended design, not a malfunction.

### Elevating Ramp

The ramp in front of the slot machine has **three positions of elevation**, adding mechanical variety to how that shot plays depending on game state.

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Game (Ball 1)

1. Start playing through the casino games, accepting the first-attempt "loss" on tournament settings
2. Begin locking balls at the slot machine toward your first multiball
3. Watch for the roulette magnet's effect on loop shots

**Target Score:** Build steadily through casino game completions

### Mid Game (Ball 2)

1. Win and collect casino games on your second attempts
2. Run slot machine multiball, collecting single/double/triple jackpots before going for the Super Jackpot
3. Track progress toward whichever wizard mode path looks most achievable

1. Finish collecting all 6 casino games if not already done
2. Cash in a well-built Super Jackpot during multiball
3. Push for your chosen wizard mode (Casino Frenzy, Break the Bank, or Super Surprise)
