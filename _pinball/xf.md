# 👽 The X Files - Strategy Guide

*Sega Pinball, 1997*

> **"The truth is out there. So is the jackpot."**

The X Files (Sega, 1997) is a licensed machine based on the hit TV series featuring Mulder and Scully investigating paranormal cases. The game has case-based progression (episodes), an alien abduction feature, multiball, and a UFO toy on the playfield. Our goal: **200M–600M** by solving cases, triggering Alien Abduction Multiball, and collecting the Conspiracy Jackpot.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Shoot the right ramp** to advance case files (primary progression)
- [ ] **Hit the UFO toy** (center) when lit for alien interaction bonuses
- [ ] **Complete case shots** during timed episode modes
- [ ] **Lock balls** in the alien abduction zone for Alien Multiball
- [ ] **Collect the Conspiracy Jackpot** via the left scoop when all cases are solved
- [ ] **Follow the Cigarette Smoking Man's instructions** (DMD prompts) for mode advances

## 👾 Core Mechanics

### Skill Shot

| Skill Shot | How | Reward |
|---|---|---|
| **Case file** | Time plunge to lit lane | Case advance + episode start |
| **Standard** | Full plunge | Ball to pop bumpers |

### Case Files (Episode Modes)

The game progresses through X Files cases (episodes from the show). Each case is a timed shot mode:
- **Squeeze (Eugene Tooms):** Hit the standup targets in sequence
- **Ice:** Left ramp + right orbit combo
- **Duane Barry:** Right ramp + center UFO
- **Little Green Men:** Pop bumper accumulation + left scoop

Each solved case: 15M–60M + advances the conspiracy.

### UFO Toy (Center)

The UFO hovers over the center playfield:
- When lit, hit the UFO for alien interaction points (5M–25M)
- During Alien Abduction mode, the UFO glows and the ball is launched into the abduction zone

### Left Scoop (Conspiracy/Mulder's Office)

The left scoop represents Mulder's office:
- When lit, collects conspiracy jackpots
- When not lit, contributes to case file advancement
- After solving all cases, the Conspiracy Jackpot becomes available here

---

## 🎳 Multiball

### Alien Abduction Multiball

**How to start:** Solve 2 cases → qualify the abduction zone via the center VUK → 3-ball multiball.

**Jackpot structure:**
- 3-ball multiball with UFO toy activated
- Jackpots via right ramp + UFO toy
- **Super Jackpot (Conspiracy):** Complete all jackpot shots → left scoop → 50M–200M+
- The UFO light show during multiball is the machine's visual peak

### Black Oil Mini-Multiball

Triggered by completing Black Oil target sequences (right standup bank). Brief 2-ball multiball with elevated standup values.
