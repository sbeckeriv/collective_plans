# 🧞 Genie - Strategy Guide

*Gottlieb, 1979 — Designed by Ed Krynski*

> **"Don't collect below 4X."**

Genie is a widebody System 1 classic where the entire game boils down to one disciplined rule: build your bonus multiplier as high as possible before you ever collect. With a 145,000-point maximum bonus, patience at the collect saucer is the single biggest factor separating a good score from a great one. Our goal: hit 4X or 5X before collecting, and avoid wasting collections at low multiplier.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Most drop targets, rollovers, and spot targets add 1,000 to your Bonus Value**, up to a maximum of **29,000**
- [ ] **Hit the 4-bank of yellow drop targets** (center playfield) when "BONUS LIT" is on to advance your **Bonus Multiplier**, up to a max of **5X**
- [ ] **Maximum possible bonus: 145,000** (29,000 × 5X)
- [ ] **When Bonus Value reaches 20,000, the "SCORE BONUS" hole lights** — slotting the ball there scores your bonus and resets the counter
- [ ] **The critical rule: don't collect bonus unless you're at 4X or 5X** — below that, keep working the multiplier instead
- [ ] **The 4-bank drop targets above the right flipper also raise your multiplier when that bank is lit** — a key secondary target

## 🧞‍♂️ Core Mechanics

### Bonus Value (Base)

Most drop targets, rollovers, and spot targets each add **1,000 points** to your Bonus Value, climbing to a maximum of **29,000**.

### Bonus Multiplier (The Real Strategic Decision)

- Starts at **1X**, advances up to a maximum of **5X**
- Advanced by hitting one of the **four yellow drop targets** in the center of the playfield **when "BONUS LIT" is on**
- The **4-bank drop targets above the right flipper** also raise your multiplier when that bank is lit

### Score Bonus Hole

Once your Bonus Value reaches **20,000**, the **"SCORE BONUS" hole lights**. Slotting the ball there **scores your current bonus (Value × Multiplier) and resets the Value counter** back to zero — but your multiplier may or may not reset depending on game state, so timing this collection matters enormously.

> 🎯 **Pro Tip — The Entire Strategy in One Rule:** **Don't collect your bonus unless you're at 4X or 5X.** If your multiplier is below that threshold when the Score Bonus hole lights, resist the temptation to cash in — instead, keep working the yellow drop target bank to push your multiplier higher first. A bonus collected at 1X or 2X is a fraction of what the same Value would be worth at 5X.

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Ball

1. Start building Bonus Value via drop targets, rollovers, and spot targets
2. Prioritize the yellow drop target bank (when BONUS LIT) to begin raising your multiplier
3. Don't rush toward the Score Bonus hole yet — multiplier comes first

### Mid Ball

1. Continue pushing your multiplier toward 4X or 5X
2. Keep building Bonus Value toward its 29,000 cap in parallel
3. Resist collecting if you're still below 4X, even once the hole is lit

### Late Ball

1. Once at 4X or 5X, collect via the Score Bonus hole for maximum value
2. If time/balls remain, rebuild and repeat the cycle
3. Bank your highest-multiplier collection before the game ends
