# 🍫 Willy Wonka & The Chocolate Factory Pinball - Complete Strategy Guide
## *Enter the Factory*

> **"We are the music makers, and we are the dreamers of dreams."**

Based on Roald Dahl's beloved 1971 film adaptation, this Pat Lawlor masterpiece brings Wonka's factory to life with five Golden Tickets to find, four multiballs to master, and a wizard mode finale worthy of Pure Imagination itself <kcite ref="1"/>.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Master the plunge:** Learn the four skill shots to start every ball strong
- [ ] **Collect Wonka Bars:** Build your end-of-ball bonus via ramp shots
- [ ] **Understand Factory Tour:** Three 4-insert sections = foundation of progression
- [ ] **Stack the multiballs:** Slugworth, Gobstopper, Kid, and Wonkavator can overlap
- [ ] **Hunt Golden Tickets:** Five tickets unlock wizard modes
- [ ] **Qualify Computer Awards:** Mystery bonuses support multiball setup
- [ ] **Complete a Factory Tour path:** Required for each Golden Ticket
- [ ] **Reach Pure Imagination:** The ultimate endgame mode

## 🎱 Skill Shots

The plunge offers four distinct opportunities to start strong. Select via right flipper before releasing.

| Shot                      | Difficulty | Awards                              |
| ------------------------- | ---------- | ----------------------------------- |
| **Standard Plunge**       | Easy       | Points + 1 WONKA Letter + Candy +1  |
| **Upper Flipper Scoop**   | Medium     | Points + Light Computer Award + +5  |
| **Super Skill (Center)**  | Hard       | +1 Factory Tour + +10 Candy + +1x   |
| **Super Secret (All)**    | Expert     | +2 Computer Award + +3 Factory + +x |

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** The Super Skill Shot (upper flipper to center ramp to saucer) is repeatable—master it to build Candy Jar and bonus multipliers consistently. <kcite ref="2"/>

## 🍬 Wonka Bars & Bonus System

Wonka Bars are your primary scoring currency. Collected via ramp shots, they:
- Accumulate as **end-of-ball bonus** (500 points per bar)
- Unlock **Computer Awards** when thresholds are met
- Directly feed scoring in special modes like Wonka Bar Craze

### Bonus Multiplier Breakpoints

| Multiplier | Method                    |
| ---------- | ------------------------- |
| 1x         | Base (default)            |
| 2x         | Hit Gobstopper target     |
| 3x+        | Super Skill Shot (+1 per) |
| 5x         | Complete Scrumdiddlyumptious combo |

**Bonus Formula:** Total Wonka Bars × 500 × Multiplier

> ⚠️ **Important:** Bonus carries between balls once you reach checkpoints (10, 25, 50, 100, 200, 300, and 400 bars hold the bonus). <kcite ref="2"/>

## 🎪 Factory Tour Modes

The heart of Wonka's ruleset: **12 modes across 3 four-insert sections.** Each section plays differently.

### Blue Section (Timed Modes)
- **The Candy Man:** Spinner value matches Candy Jar. Hit spinner to collect points rapidly.
- **Wonka Bar Craze:** Points per ramp shot = Wonka Bars collected × 10,000
- **Rainbow of Colors:** Hit colored shots in sequence

### Purple Section (Super X Multipliers)
- **Ticket Frenzy:** All ramps pay 2x normal value
- **Fizzy Lifting Drinks:** Bumper hits score escalating points (10K, 20K, 30K...)
- **Scrumdiddlyumptious:** Complete combo: Left ramp → Right ramp → Center ramp. Extends with Gobstopper locks.

### Orange Section (Captive Ball Features)
- **Most Secret Machine:** 60-second unlimited ball on Gobstopper hole for escalating values
- **Pure Imagination Warmup:** Builds toward final wizard mode
- Other character-themed modes

> 💡 **Stacking Strategy:** Start Blue modes early (quick scoring). Save Purple modes for when multiballs are active (the Super X multiplies multiball jackpots). Orange modes feed into Golden Ticket collection. <kcite ref="2"/>

## 🍫 Candy Jar & Computer Awards

**Candy Jar** accumulates via:
- Skill shots (+1 standard, +5 upper flipper, +10 Super Skill)
- Inlane rollovers
- Certain mode completions

### Effects of Candy Jar Level
- **Spinner value** scales with Candy level (The Candy Man mode affected)
- **Computer Award scoring** increases
- **Visual feedback** shows current Candy accumulation

### Computer Awards (15 Mystery Options)
Common awards include:
- Light super lock (critical for Wonkavator Multiball)
- Advance factory tour section
- Collect bonus
- Extra ball
- Add-a-ball to active multiball
- Light special

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** If you see "Light Super Lock (all 3!)" as a Computer Award, grab it—this instantly qualifies Wonkavator Multiball, a major scoring opportunity. <kcite ref="2"/>

## 🎪 Multiballs

Wonka features four distinct multiballs. Nearly all can be stacked together for exponential scoring.

### 🟡 Slugworth Multiball (2-Ball)

**Qualification:** Hit Slugworth standup target (progressive hits needed)

**Mechanics:**
- Two balls in play
- Orange-lit shots are jackpots
- Hurry-up value starts high and decreases
- Move jackpot targets around playfield using ramps/shots

**Scoring:** Base 5M jackpot, increases with successive hits

> ⚠️ **Warning:** Slugworth is the easiest multiball to qualify. Use it early to build confidence and start building score momentum.

---

### 🟤 Gobstopper Multiball (3-Ball)

**Qualification:**
1. Hit Gobstopper blocking target repeatedly (counter displays on LCD)
2. Lock 3 balls in designated hole
3. Shoot "Most Secret Machine" to start

**Mechanics:**
- Three balls in play
- Shoot ramps to open/access jackpot hole
- Most Secret Machine super jackpot collects all ramp values
- Escalating shots build value

**Scoring Path:**
- Collect jackpots × 5, then super jackpot for massive payout
- Ramp shots during mode build the super value

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Stack a Purple section mode (Super X multiplier) with Gobstopper to multiply the jackpot values astronomically. This is a core strategy element. <kcite ref="2"/>

---

### 🎠 Kid Multiball (Variable Balls)

**Qualification:** Qualify and collect five characters—Charlie, Augustus, Veruca, Violet, Mike—through various tasks

**Character Jackpots:**
- Each character has a dedicated lane/shot
- Collect all five during multiball for special bonus
- Ball count increases with harvesters destroyed (up to 5 balls)

**Mechanics:**
- Collect character jackpots in sequence
- Each character collected = +1 ramp shot toward bonus
- Final super jackpot awards all accumulated values

---

### 🛗 Wonkavator Multiball (3-Ball, Multi-Wave)

**Qualification:**
1. Collect three **Super Locks** via:
   - Computer Awards ("Light Super Lock" awards)
   - Factory Tour completions
   - Mystery awards
2. Lock balls in the elevator (Wonkavator)
3. Shoot to start

**Wave Structure:**
- **Wave 1:** Collect 3 jackpots (standard difficulty)
- **Wave 2:** Harder shot sequence, escalating values
- **Wave 3:** Maximum difficulty, 5+ jackpots for super multiball finale

**Scoring:** Exponential—each wave's multiplier increases. Combine with active Super X modes for devastating scores.

> 🏆 **Strategy:** Wonkavator is worth planning for. Save Computer Awards and Factory Tour progress to accumulate three locks, then activate when you have a Purple section mode running. <kcite ref="2"/>

## 🎫 Golden Tickets & Wizard Mode Progression

**Five Golden Tickets** must be collected to reach the final wizard modes. Each ticket is earned by completing major objectives:

### Golden Ticket Sources

| Method                          | Ticket # |
| ------------------------------- | -------- |
| **Complete a Factory Tour (any)** | 1        |
| **Gobstopper Multiball + collect 5 jackpots** | 2        |
| **Kid Multiball + all 5 characters** | 3        |
| **Wonkavator Multiball (any wave)** | 4        |
| **Gumball Machine random** | 5        |

> ⚠️ **Important:** The 5th ticket is **hidden in the Gumball Machine**—a random award mechanism. You cannot control its timing, so plan for unexpected ticket arrivals late in the game. <kcite ref="2"/>

## 👑 Wizard Modes (Post-5 Golden Tickets)

Once you've collected all five Golden Tickets, wizard modes unlock in order:

### 1️⃣ Oompa-Loompa Odyssey (1 Ticket Required)

**Objective:** Collect all green-lit shots before draining a ball

**Mechanics:**
- 90-second, 2-ball unlimited multiball
- Backbox display shows a live camera feed of the player
- Green shots (flashing) must all be hit
- One drain = mode ends (unlike most multiballs)

**Scoring:** Escalating shot values for collected greens; bonus for completing all

**Strategy:** This is the entry-level wizard mode. Use it to warm up and build confidence for later modes. The camera feed is a unique feature—fun, but doesn't affect scoring.

---

### 2️⃣ Wonka's Office (2 Tickets Required)

**Objective:** Follow alternating left-right shot sequences

**Mechanics:**
- Standard 2-ball multiball (drains don't auto-end)
- Shoot left ramp, then right ramp, alternating
- Each completion = progression
- Gobstopper locks extend the mode and increase final jackpot

**Scoring Path:**
- Base jackpot for sequence completion
- +5M per Gobstopper lock added
- Super jackpot if you complete extended sequence

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** If you've saved Gobstopper locks (from Computer Awards), activate them during Wonka's Office to maximize the jackpot value before collecting. <kcite ref="2"/>

---

### 3️⃣ Candy Stash (3 Tickets Required)

**Objective:** Award yourself massive bonuses

**Mechanics:**
- **Instant award:** +100 Wonka Bars + 50 Candy Jar
- No active shooting required—this mode awards instantly upon entry

**Scoring:** Immediate payout: 100 bars × 500 × multiplier = 50K–250K straight bonus

**Strategy:** Less exciting than others, but these bars fuel your end-of-ball bonus. Essential for reaching 100M+.

---

### 4️⃣ The Most Secret Machine (4 Tickets Required)

**Objective:** Bash the Gobstopper hole for 60 seconds, building escalating value

**Mechanics:**
- 60-second hurry-up mode
- Bash captive ball in Gobstopper hole
- Each hit = escalating value (starts 10M, increases 10M per hit)
- Difficulty: Ball becomes harder to control mid-way
- Collect at spinner for 2x if entering from ramp

**Scoring Peak:** Skilled players can reach 20+ hits in 60 seconds = 200M+ escalating payout

> 🏆 **High Skill Mode:** This requires strong ball control and rhythm. Practice the Gobstopper hole bash to master it. <kcite ref="2"/>

---

### 5️⃣ Pure Imagination (5 Tickets Required - Final Wizard)

**Objective:** 4-ball unlimited multiball; no timer

**Mechanics:**
- Up to 4 balls in simultaneous play (unheard of in most machines!)
- Switch hits during multiball build jackpot values
- Completing "levels" (all 4 balls hitting targets) increases switch payouts
- No drain immediately = game end; play until balls naturally drain

**Scoring:** Cumulative; skilled play extends ball time indefinitely

> 🏆 **Achievement:** Reaching Pure Imagination is the ultimate goal. The unlimited multiball with escalating payouts offers the highest scoring potential on the machine. <kcite ref="2"/>

## 🔧 Stacking & Combos

### ✅ Highly Stackable

| Combination                  | Benefit                                |
| ---------------------------- | -------------------------------------- |
| **Purple Mode + Multiball**  | Super X multiplier on all jackpots     |
| **Multiple Multiballs**      | Simultaneous 4+ balls = chaos scoring  |
| **Wonka Bars + Bonus Multiplier** | Exponential end-of-ball totals  |
| **Computer Awards + Multiball** | Add-a-ball extends play time    |

### ❌ Avoid Stacking

| Conflict                     | Reason                            |
| ---------------------------- | --------------------------------- |
| **Two Factory Tours active** | Can't shoot different ramps (shot blockers clash) |
| **Five multiballs at once**  | Ball control becomes impossible  |

### ⏰ Stacking Strategy

1. **Early Ball (1-2):** Start Slugworth (easy 2-ball), qualify Gobstopper locks
2. **Mid-Ball (2-3):** Activate Purple mode, then Gobstopper multiball → super jackpot
3. **Late Ball (3+):** Stack remaining multiballs; build Golden Tickets
4. **Endgame:** Activate wizard modes in sequence with active multipliers

## 🎯 Shot Analysis

### ⚠️ Dangerous Shots

| Shot                 | Risk                                 | Mitigation                 |
| -------------------- | ------------------------------------ | -------------------------- |
| **Center Ramp**      | Fast return to flippers, SDTM hazard | Trap up after shot        |
| **Left Ramp Deflect**| Can ricochet to right outlane        | Aim straight, trap after  |
| **Gobstopper Hole**  | Chaotic ball control when multiple   | Bash with rhythm, patience |
| **Bumper Cluster**   | Unpredictable bounces toward drain  | Avoid during critical play |

### ✅ Safe, Controlled Shots

| Shot            | Benefit                          |
| --------------- | -------------------------------- |
| **Right Ramp**  | Predictable return to left trap |
| **Scoop (TV)**  | Ball held, setup time            |
| **Slingshots**  | Predictable deflection patterns  |

### 🎮 Ball Control Positions

- **Left Flipper Trap:** Ideal after center/right ramp shots; allows setup for left ramp
- **Right Flipper Trap:** Best for orbit shots; prepares right ramp or scoop
- **Post Pass:** Transfer ball between flippers for shot variety

## 📊 Bonus Calculation & End-of-Ball

Your final bonus is calculated each ball:

**Total Bonus = Wonka Bars Collected × 500 × Multiplier**

Example:
- 200 Wonka Bars × 500 × 3x multiplier = **300,000 points**

Over three balls with 150, 175, 200 bars:
- Ball 1: 75,000 (150 × 500 × 1x)
- Ball 2: 175,000 (175 × 500 × 2x after Gobstopper)
- Ball 3: 300,000 (200 × 500 × 3x after Scrumdiddlyumptious)
- **Total bonus = 550,000 points alone!**

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Double-flip to skip the bonus display animation. <kcite ref="2"/>
