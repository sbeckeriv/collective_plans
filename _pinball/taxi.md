# 🚕 Taxi - Strategy Guide

*Williams, 1988 — Designed by Mark Ritchie, Software by Ed Boon*

> **"Pick up the passengers. Hit the Spin-Out. Drive away with the jackpot."**

Taxi (Williams, 1988) is a System 11B machine with a left-side catapult that launches the ball airborne into a habitrail, a Spin-Out whirlpool skill shot worth up to 100,000 points, two kick-out holes, and two 3-bank drop target sets. The theme features collecting celebrity passengers (Lola, Pinbot, Gorbie, etc.) and racing to multiball. Our goal: **10M–30M** (System 11 scoring) by collecting all passengers and triggering multiball.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Nail the Spin-Out skill shot** — plunge with authority into the whirlpool for up to 100K points (1M-equivalent in feel)
- [ ] **Pick up passengers** by shooting the designated passenger shots (lit inserts)
- [ ] **Use the catapult** (left side) to send the ball airborne into the habitrail — it's a key multiball lock mechanism
- [ ] **Complete both 3-bank drop target sets** for bonus advance and mode lights
- [ ] **Lock balls via the kick-out holes** to start Multiball
- [ ] **Collect all 6 passengers** to light the jackpot insert

---

## 🚖 Core Mechanics

### Spin-Out Skill Shot

The plunger skill shot is the defining early-game moment:
- **Full-power plunge** → ball enters the Spin-Out whirlpool at top → spins around for up to 100,000 points
- **Slow plunge** → ball doesn't reach the whirlpool → lower skill shot award
- The whirlpool spins visually — the longer the ball circles, the higher the score

> 🎯 **Always go for it.** A successful Spin-Out skill shot is worth the entire first ball of casual play. Plunge with full authority every ball.

### Passenger Collection (6 Passengers)

Pick up six celebrity passengers by hitting their lit shots:
- **Lola** — left scoop
- **Pinbot** — right kick-out
- **Gorbie** — pop bumper cluster shot
- **Claude** — right orbit
- **Phyllis** — left drop targets (complete bank)
- **Marilyn** — catapult shot into habitrail

Each passenger collected: 100K–500K points + bonus step. Collecting all 6 lights the jackpot and starts the multiball sequence.

### Left-Side Catapult

The catapult on the left side launches the ball airborne into a habitrail wireform:
- The ball travels through the air and lands in the habitrail
- The habitrail feeds to the lock area or returns to the right flipper
- Collecting Marilyn (a passenger) requires a successful catapult shot
- During multiball setup, the catapult feeds the lock

### Drop Targets (Two 3-Banks)

Two 3-bank drop target sets on left and right sides:
- Complete the left bank → Phyllis picked up (passenger)
- Complete the right bank → bonus advance + feature light
- Both banks reset after completion

### Kick-Out Holes (2)

Left and right kick-out holes:
- Right kick-out: collects Pinbot (passenger) when lit
- Both kick-outs: ball lock locations for multiball when passengers are collected

---

## 🎳 Multiball

### Taxi Multiball

**How to start:** Collect all 6 passengers → two ball locks (kick-out holes and catapult).

**Jackpot structure:**
- 3-ball multiball
- Jackpot shot = right orbit (highest value, 500K–2M+)
- **Jackpot collect:** Shoot the right orbit during multiball for the lit jackpot — escalates with each collection
- Passenger collection during multiball counts toward the next jackpot qualification

> 🏆 **Right orbit is the money shot.** In multiball, keep shooting the right orbit. It's the fastest route to the jackpot and the safest repeatable multiball shot.

---

## 🔄 Progression Systems

- **Passengers:** Collecting the same passengers in a second game (if the machine keeps memory) doesn't reset — but within a single game, each collection is once-per-game
- **Extra ball:** Lit after collecting 4+ passengers
- **Bonus multiplier:** Advanced by right drop target bank completions

## 📊 Shot Analysis

| Shot | Value | Risk |
|---|---|---|
| Spin-Out skill (plunge) | Up to 100K | Skill-based |
| Right orbit (jackpot) | 500K–2M+ | Low |
| Catapult (left) | Marilyn + lock | Low once learned |
| Left drop targets | Phyllis + bonus | Low |
| Right drop targets | Bonus advance | Low |
| Kick-out holes | Passengers + lock | Low |
| Pop bumpers | Gorbie + passive | None |

### Dangerous Areas

- **Right outlane** — fast exit from right orbit if ball doesn't fully complete; no kickback on right side
- **Left outlane** — typically has a kickback on some configurations; verify on your machine
- **Catapult habitrail exit** — the ball lands in a predictable but fast location; be ready on the right flipper

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Ball 1
1. Spin-Out skill shot (full plunge)
2. Lola (left scoop) + Pinbot (right kick-out) = 2 passengers
3. Left drop target completion → Phyllis
4. Pop bumper access → Gorbie

### Ball 2
1. Right orbit → Claude
2. Catapult → Marilyn
3. All 6 passengers collected → multiball lock
4. Multiball → right orbit jackpot

### Ball 3
1. Second multiball attempt
2. Extra ball if lit (4+ passengers before multiball)
3. Right drop target bank for bonus multiplier

| Performance | Score |

## 💡 Pro Tips

> **The Spin-Out is the best single shot in the game relative to its timing.** It takes 2 seconds on the plunge and scores 100K. That's 50K/sec — nothing else comes close.

> **Get Gorbie from the pop bumpers early.** Route the ball through the bumpers in the first 30 seconds of each ball — passive Gorbie collection without sacrificing shots.

> **Catapult takes practice.** The airborne shot is intimidating but very consistent once you've done it 5–10 times. It's essential for Marilyn and multiball.
