# 🎢 RollerCoaster Tycoon - Strategy Guide

*Stern Pinball, 2002 — Designed by Pat Lawlor*

> **"Build the park. Ride the rides. Don't let the guests leave."**

RollerCoaster Tycoon (Stern, 2002) is a Whitestar widebody designed by Pat Lawlor featuring four flippers, three colored ramps, a captive ball, and a park-building theme where you construct rollercoasters to score massive jackpots. The game's secondary DMD on the playfield displays your park status. Our goal: **200M–500M** through efficient coaster construction, multiball, and stacking completed rides.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Shoot the three colored ramps** to build coaster sections (each ramp = one track segment)
- [ ] **Complete a full coaster** (all 3 ramp colors) to open it and score the coaster bonus
- [ ] **Lock balls** via the looping ramp to start Park Multiball
- [ ] **Hit the captive ball** to advance your park's guest count and bonus multiplier
- [ ] **Watch the playfield DMD** — it shows your park layout and which coasters are open
- [ ] **Use upper flippers** to direct balls to the upper ramp entries

## 🎡 Core Mechanics

### Skill Shot

| Skill Shot | How | Reward |
|---|---|---|
| **Ramp skill shot** | Time the plunge to hit a specific colored ramp | Coaster segment + bonus |
| **Standard** | Full plunge | Ball to pops area |

### Coaster Building (Three Ramps)

| Ramp Color | Location | Coaster Segment |
|---|---|---|
| 🔵 Blue | Left side | Start segment |
| 🔴 Red | Center | Middle segment |
| 🟡 Yellow | Right side | End segment + bonus |

Complete all three segments → coaster opens → collect coaster score bonus. Each open coaster increases jackpot values for multiball.

**Building multiple coasters** across balls cumulatively raises the park's value — a park with 3+ open coasters in multiball is extremely lucrative.

### Captive Ball (Guest Counter)

The captive ball advances your park's guest count. More guests = higher base bonus at ball end. Hit it frequently; it's very low risk.

### 3-Bank Drop Targets

Completing the drop target bank awards park-themed bonuses and resets. Also contributes to lighting extra ball.

### Park DMD (Secondary Display)

The small dot matrix display on the playfield shows:
- Which coasters are open/under construction
- Current guest count
- Jackpot values for multiball

Use it to prioritize — if the blue ramp coaster is close to completion, shoot blue.

## 🎳 Multiball

### Park Multiball

**How to start:** Lock 2 balls via the looping ramp (which arcs over the flipper area — lit after completing 1+ coasters).

**Jackpot structure:**
- 3-ball multiball using 4 flippers
- Each jackpot = shoot a coaster ramp (scores that coaster's open value)
- **Super Jackpot:** Complete all three coaster ramps in multiball — triggers a combined park super jackpot (sum of all open coasters × multiplier)
- More open coasters at multiball start = dramatically higher jackpot ceiling

> 🏆 **Strategy:** Open at least 2 coasters before locking for multiball. A 3-coaster park with multiball running is your primary billion-point path.

- **Guest milestones:** Hitting guest count thresholds awards extra balls and bonus multiplier steps
- **Drop target bank:** Complete once per ball for bonus, repeat for extra ball lighting

## 📊 Shot Analysis

| Shot | Value | Risk |
|---|---|---|
| Blue left ramp | Coaster segment | Low |
| Red center ramp | Coaster segment | Low |
| Yellow right ramp | Coaster segment + bonus | Low |
| Looping ramp (lock) | Ball lock | Medium — steep entry |
| Captive ball | Guest count | Very low |
| Drop target bank | Bonus + extra ball progress | Low |

### Dangerous Shots

- **Looping ramp** — the ball arcs over the lower flipper area; a weak shot can come back and drain between flippers
- **Right outlane** — fast exit from right ramp if ball comes off angle

### Ball Control (4 Flippers, Widebody)

- Upper flippers are primarily for redirecting pop bumper exits toward ramp entries
- Trap on left lower flipper between upper sequences
- Don't rush shots — widebody pace is slower than it looks

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Ball 1
1. Blue ramp × 2 + Red ramp × 1 = coaster 1 open
2. Captive ball × 3 hits
3. Drop target completion

### Ball 2
1. Complete coasters 2 (all three colors again)
2. Lock first ball (looping ramp)
3. Guest count milestone for extra ball

### Ball 3

2. Three coasters open → harvest super jackpot

## 💡 Pro Tips

> **Open coasters before locking.** The single biggest mistake RCT players make is locking balls immediately when the lock lights — don't. Open at least 2 coasters first.

> **The playfield DMD is your guide.** Pat Lawlor designed it to be informative — glance at it between shots to confirm coaster progress without guessing.

> **Upper flippers aren't decoration.** Use them to redirect bumper exits toward the blue ramp entry — it's faster than letting the ball fall to the lower flippers.
