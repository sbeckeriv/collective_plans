# 🤖 Bride of Pinbot 2.0 - Strategy Guide

*Dutch Pinball, 2014 — Conversion Kit*

> **"The Bride returns. Upgraded. Even more billions."**

Bride of Pinbot 2.0 (Dutch Pinball, 2014) is a full conversion kit that replaces the original Bride of Pinbot's playfield, electronics, and ruleset while keeping the same cabinet. It modernizes the rules with a new code base, updated scoring, additional modes, and a deeper progression system — while preserving the iconic Big Wheel and BRIDE letter mechanic. Our goal: **1B–5B** by completing BRIDE multiple times, exploiting the updated multiball rules, and reaching the Human Face wizard completion.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Shoot the Big Wheel (left ramp)** to collect BRIDE letters — same as the original but with enhanced scoring
- [ ] **Complete B-R-I-D-E** to light the Jackpot shots (now multiple jackpot targets, not just one)
- [ ] **Start multiball** via the right side lock (enhanced multiball with 3 balls in BOP 2.0)
- [ ] **Collect the mini-playfield bonuses** for multiplier steps (bagatelle retained from original)
- [ ] **Advance the Bride's face** through all stages for escalating bonus payouts
- [ ] **Use the updated action button** (if equipped) for bonus abilities

## 💎 Core Mechanics

### BRIDE Letters (Big Wheel — Left Ramp)

Same fundamental mechanic as the original — shoot the left ramp to spin the Big Wheel and collect BRIDE letters. Key differences in BOP 2.0:
- The wheel has weighted sections calibrated for more consistent letter distribution
- Completing BRIDE now lights **multiple jackpot shots** (not just one)
- Each subsequent BRIDE completion scales the jackpot values higher

### Multiple Jackpot Shots

After BRIDE completion, jackpot shots light across the playfield:
- Left loop, right ramp, and center ramp each can be lit simultaneously
- Collecting all three jackpot shots in one BRIDE completion = Super Jackpot (500M–2B+)
- This replaces the original single 1 Billion shot with a more dynamic jackpot structure

### Updated Multiball (3-Ball)

BOP 2.0 upgrades the original 2-ball multiball to 3-ball:
- Lock 2 balls (right side lock × 2) for 3-ball multiball
- All jackpot shots active simultaneously during multiball
- **Super Jackpot during multiball:** All three balls active + complete all lit jackpots → center scoop → 1B–3B+

### Bride Face Progression

Same skull → robot → human progression but with additional intermediate stages in BOP 2.0:
- Each face change now awards an escalating face bonus (vs. flat bonus in original)
- The fourth stage (beyond Human) was added in 2.0 — a "Goddess" final state

### Mini-Playfield Bagatelle

Retained from the original. The bagatelle awards bonus multiplier steps (up to 10× in BOP 2.0 vs. 5× in original).

## 🎳 Multiball

### 3-Ball Bride Multiball

**How to start:** Lock 2 balls in the right side lock → 3-ball multiball.

**Jackpot structure:**
- 3-ball multiball with all jackpot shots lit simultaneously
- Each jackpot shot: 25M–200M (scales with BRIDE completion count)
- **Super Jackpot:** Complete all lit shots → center scoop → 500M–3B depending on current multiplier

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Ball 1
1. Left ramp × 5 → BRIDE completion
2. Collect left loop jackpot + right ramp jackpot
3. Lock first ball

### Ball 2
1. Second BRIDE completion (higher jackpot values)
2. Lock second ball → 3-ball multiball
3. During multiball: all three jackpot shots → Super Jackpot

### Ball 3
1. Third BRIDE completion → Goddess face stage
2. Maximized Super Jackpot during second multiball attempt
3. Bagatelle multiplier collection at ball end

## 💡 Pro Tips

> **BOP 2.0 rewards multiple BRIDE completions** in a way the original doesn't. Aim for 3 completions across the game — jackpot ceiling scales dramatically.

> **All three jackpot shots at once during multiball is the game's peak moment.** With 3 balls running, you can collect all three jackpots in rapid succession.

> **The bagatelle multiplier goes to 10×.** Aggressively pursue mini-playfield access early — 10× bonus at ball end on a large base is 500M+ by itself.
