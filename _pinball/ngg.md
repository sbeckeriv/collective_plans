# ⛳ No Good Gofers - Strategy Guide

*Williams, 1997 — Designed by Bill Pfutzenreuter & Python Anghelo*

> **"Play golf. Destroy the course. Score insane jackpots."**

No Good Gofers is a golf-themed WPC-95 machine starring Buzz and Bud, two unhinged caddies sabotaging your round. The game is structured around playing 18 holes via shots around the playfield, with multiball, skill shots, and a deep bonus system layered on top. Our goal: **500M–1B points** by stacking Gofer Mania with hole completions and milking jackpots.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Take the skill shot** — slow plunge to drop the ball in the target hole for big points + hole completion
- [ ] **Complete the GOLF lanes** at the top to advance your bonus multiplier
- [ ] **Shoot the ramps and loops** to advance through the 18 holes
- [ ] **Lock balls in the Gofer Head** to start Gofer Mania multiball
- [ ] **Shoot lit shots during multiball** — jackpots stack with hole completions
- [ ] **Collect the Hole-in-One award** by hitting the skill shot hole during multiball

## 🏌️ Core Mechanics

### Skill Shot

| Skill Shot | How | Reward |
|---|---|---|
| **Hole-in-One** | Plunge softly to land in the top hole | Completes current hole + 25M points |
| **Lane select** | Hold flipper at launch to choose lit lane | Bonus multiplier advance |
| **Full plunge** | Standard plunge | Ball into pops |

The Hole-in-One skill shot is exceptionally valuable — it bypasses all shots required for the current hole and gives a massive points bonus.

### 18 Holes System

Each hole is a specific shot (or sequence) around the playfield:

| Holes | Key Shots |
|---|---|
| 1–6 | Left ramp, right ramp, loop combos |
| 7–12 | Center shots, Gofer head, saucer |
| 13–18 | Multi-shot combos, ramp + loop sequences |

Completing all 18 holes starts **Back Nine** (the mini-wizard mode) and awards a massive bonus.

### GOLF Lanes (Top)

Roll through G-O-L-F to advance bonus multiplier. Each complete set = +1×. Maximum: **10×**.

## 🎳 Multiballs

### Gofer Mania (Main Multiball)

**How to start:** Lock 2 balls in the Gofer Head (center scoop), then lock a 3rd ball on the playfield lock.

**Jackpot structure:**
- Shoot lit shots to collect jackpots (starts ~5M, increases with each hit)
- **Super Jackpot:** Shoot the Gofer Head after lighting it via all jackpot shots — worth 2–3× a regular jackpot
- Multiball continues until 1 ball remains

> 🏆 **Pro Tip:** Gofer Mania stacks with active hole progress — if you trigger multiball mid-hole, shots during multiball still count toward hole completion AND collect jackpots.

### Bud's Bumper Bash (Bumper Multiball)

**How to start:** Hit the pop bumpers repeatedly until lit (count varies by operator settings).

**Gameplay:** 2-ball multiball with elevated bumper values. Less lucrative than Gofer Mania but easier to start.

## 🔄 Progression Systems

### Hole Completion Bonuses

| Achievement | Bonus |
|---|---|
| Complete holes 1–9 | Front Nine award (points + extra ball) |
| Complete holes 10–18 | Back Nine wizard mode starts |
| Complete all 18 twice | Ultimate Gofer Champion award |

### Ball Save / Extra Balls

- **Hole-in-One** during normal play: extra ball lit
- **Front Nine completion:** extra ball awarded
- Operator may limit extra balls per game

---

## 🔀 Stacking & Combos

| Stack | Result |
|---|---|
| Gofer Mania + active hole | All jackpot shots count toward hole AND score jackpots |
| High bonus multiplier (8–10×) + large bonus base | Ball-end bonus can rival multiball scoring |
| Skill shot hole-in-one on ball 1 | Jump ahead in hole sequence immediately |

**Don't:** Try to start Bud's Bumper Bash during Gofer Mania — it interrupts multiball flow and the bumper multiball is worth much less.

## 🧙 Wizard Modes

### Back Nine

**Requirement:** Complete all 18 holes.

**Gameplay:** All shots lit simultaneously, each worth escalating jackpots. A frantic timed sequence where every shot scores big. **This is where the B+ scores happen.**

## 📊 Shot Analysis

| Shot | Value | Risk |
|---|---|---|
| Left ramp | Hole advance + moderate points | Low |
| Right ramp | Hole advance + moderate points | Low |
| Loop (full orbit) | Hole advance + combo bonus | Medium |
| Gofer Head (center) | Ball lock / jackpot / super jackpot | Medium — can reject |
| Skill shot hole | Hole-in-one | Low — on plunge only |
| Pop bumpers | Bumper bash progress | None |

### Dangerous Shots

- **Right outlane** — fast after loop exit; nudge to save
- **Gofer Head reject** — ball can kick sideways to drain; be ready

### Safe Positions

- Left flipper trap after pop bumper action
- Right ramp is the safest repeatable shot for hole progress

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Game (Balls 1–2)
1. Hole-in-One skill shot if confident
2. Build GOLF multiplier to 5× minimum
3. Lock first ball in Gofer Head
4. Advance holes via ramps

### Mid Game
1. Complete Gofer Mania multiball — aim for Super Jackpot
2. Push to Front Nine completion for extra ball
3. Keep GOLF multiplier maxed

### Late Game (Ball 3)
1. Start Gofer Mania if not already done
2. Chase Back Nine if close to 18 holes
3. Collect high bonus via end-of-ball collect shot

### Score Targets

| Performance | Score |
|---|---|
| Beginner | 50M–150M |
| Solid | 200M–500M |
| Strong | 500M–800M |
| Elite (Back Nine) | 1B+ |

## 💡 Pro Tips

> **The skill shot hole is the highest value shot per second in the game.** On ball 1 especially, a successful Hole-in-One sets you up a hole ahead with a huge points bonus — always attempt it.

> **Lock the first ball early.** Gofer Mania is your primary scoring engine. The sooner you start it, the more bites you get.

> **Protect your bonus multiplier.** GOLF lanes are passive — route the ball through them after pop bumper sequences. 10× bonus on a large base hits harder than most single jackpots.
