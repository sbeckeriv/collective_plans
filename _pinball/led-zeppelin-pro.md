# 🎸 Led Zeppelin - Strategy Guide

*Stern Pinball, 2020/2021 — Designed by Steve Ritchie*

> **"One of the most punishing layouts Stern has released this century."**

Led Zeppelin is notoriously left-heavy — every shot except the right ramp and right orbit sits on the left half of the playfield, making horizontal motion "exceptionally deadly." Underneath that punishing layout is a deep, consistent ruleset: 10 song modes (all sharing the same scoring mechanics), 4 tours building toward World Tour Multiball, and two distinct wizard modes. Our goal: turn 5 songs platinum to unlock Mothership Multiball, then push all 10 platinum for Top of the Charts or Celebration Day.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Take the skill shot** by timing your aim at the blinking L-E-D target (it changes automatically, but flippers can adjust it) for a brief ball save and points
- [ ] **Select a song before each ball** — all 10 songs work identically mechanically, so pick based on shot patterns you're comfortable with rather than worrying about hidden differences
- [ ] **Earn "record sales" by making lit shots** — 5 gold records completes a song, 10 gold records makes it **platinum** (permanently locking that song)
- [ ] **5 platinum songs unlock Mothership Multiball**; **all 10 platinum songs unlock both Top of the Charts and Celebration Day**
- [ ] **Respect the layout's left-heavy danger** — horizontal motion is exceptionally deadly here, and the plunge frequently feeds toward the right outlane
- [ ] **Build your Icarus X multiplier** by clearing the four target banks (Ball Save, LED, ZEP, ROCK) — each starts at 1.0x and climbs 0.1x per combo shot

## 🎤 Core Mechanics

### Song Modes (10 Total, All Mechanically Identical)

- Choose a song at the start of every ball
- Lit shots **change throughout the song's structure** (verse, chorus, solo), but the **underlying scoring formula is the same across all 10 songs**
- **The center ramp becomes especially valuable during guitar solos**
- Making shots earns **record sales**: **5 gold records** completes the song; **10 gold records** advances it to **platinum**, permanently locking it

### Icarus X Multiplier System

- Built by clearing **four target banks**: Ball Save, LED, ZEP, ROCK
- Each bank starts at **1.0x** and increases **0.1x per shot in a combo that ends into that bank**
- **Final multiplier = all four banks multiplied together**

> 🎯 **Pro Tip:** Since the final multiplier is a product of all four banks, balanced progress across all four pays off far more than maxing one and ignoring the others.

### The Four Tours

Accessed by shooting the **left eject**, each tour requires collecting **locks** via specific features:

| Tour | Format |
|---|---|
| Tour 1 | 2-ball multiball |
| Tour 2 | 3-ball multiball (more locks required) |
| Tour 3 | 4-ball multiball |
| Tour 4 | 5-ball multiball |

**Completing all four tours lights World Tour Multiball.**

---

## 🎁 Multiball Modes

### Zeppelin Multiball (Main Mode)

- **Lock 3 balls via the left ramp**
- Jackpots on **flashing shots**, with **Super Jackpots available at the left eject**

### Electric Magic Multiball (Premium/LE only)

- Completed by finishing the **LED ZEP bank multiple times**
- A **spinner-based mode** featuring magnetized ball mechanics and timed jackpot multipliers

### Electric Magic Frenzy (Premium/LE only)

- A lucrative **switch-frenzy multiball**, activated similarly to Electric Magic, but offering **immediate 4-ball play**

## 🏆 Wizard Modes

| Wizard Mode | Requirement |
|---|---|
| **Mothership Multiball** | 5 platinum songs |
| **Top of the Charts** | All 10 songs platinum |
| **Celebration Day** | All 10 songs platinum |

Which of Top of the Charts or Celebration Day you reach depends on **how well you've performed** across the rest of your game — both represent the pinnacle of the ruleset.

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **Horizontal ball motion** | Described as "exceptionally deadly" on this layout | Play extra conservatively on any cross-playfield shot or rebound |
| **The plunge feeding the right outlane** | A known, frequent failure mode on this specific machine | Be ready on the flippers immediately after every plunge |
| **The left-heavy shot concentration** | Nearly everything except the right ramp/orbit is on the left half | Develop strong left-side flipper control since you'll rely on it disproportionately |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Game (Ball 1)

1. Take the skill shot for the ball save
2. Select a song you're comfortable with and start earning record sales
3. Begin clearing the four Icarus X target banks for multiplier growth

**Target Score:** 15M–40M

### Mid Game (Ball 2)

1. Push toward your first platinum songs
2. Work toward Zeppelin Multiball via left ramp locks
3. Start a tour via the left eject if convenient

**Target Score:** 40M–100M

### Late Game (Ball 3+)

1. Push for 5 platinum songs to unlock Mothership Multiball
2. Complete all four tours for World Tour Multiball if you're close
3. If at or near 10 platinum songs, chase Top of the Charts or Celebration Day

**Target Score per ball:** 60M–200M+

| Setting | Recommendation | Reason |

| **Plunge strength calibration** | Verify carefully given the known right-outlane feed issue | A poorly calibrated plunge compounds this layout's already significant difficulty |
| **Electric gate mechanism** | Check smooth, reliable operation | Unique feature affecting several shot routes |

**Ball 1:** Skill shot, pick a comfortable song, start clearing Icarus X banks.

**Ball 3+:** Chase 5 platinum for Mothership, complete tours for World Tour, push for the final wizard modes.
