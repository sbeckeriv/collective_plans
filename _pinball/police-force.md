# 🚔 Police Force Pinball - Ultimate Strategy Guide
## *The Path to Maximum Score*

> **Goal:** Reach the highest possible score through mastering the center ramp for Unlimited Millions and executing the devastating "Take Highest Score" feature on Ball 3.

---

## 📋 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] Learn the center ramp shot - this is your bread and butter
- [ ] Understand skill shot timing (aim for 75K or 100K)
- [ ] Know how to jail all 4 criminals for Jackpot
- [ ] Practice the right ramp shot for Ball 3's "Take Highest Score"
- [ ] Note: There's a **CENTER POST** between the flippers! <kcite ref="122"/>

---

## 🎯 Core Mechanics

### Skill Shot (Firing Range)

When plunging, the ball passes through a spinner that determines your skill shot value <kcite ref="75"/><kcite ref="123"/>:

| Spinner Spins | Base Value          | Ball 1 | Ball 2 | Ball 3 |
| ------------- | ------------------- | ------ | ------ | ------ |
| 1 spin        | 10K                 | 10K    | 20K    | 30K    |
| 2 spins       | 25K                 | 25K    | 50K    | 75K    |
| 3 spins       | 50K                 | 50K    | 100K   | 150K   |
| 4 spins       | 75K + POLICE letter | 75K    | 150K   | 225K   |
| 5+ spins      | 100K                | 100K   | 200K   | 300K   |

> **💡 Pro Tip:** Adjust your plunge strength to consistently hit 4-5 spins for the 75K (with bonus POLICE letter) or 100K values <kcite ref="43"/>.

If the ball doesn't reach the spinner, you receive 10K × Ball Number as consolation <kcite ref="75"/>.

---

### Criminal Targets 🦈🐊🐀🦡

Jail all four criminals to light the **Jackpot** <kcite ref="77"/><kcite ref="125"/>:

| Criminal               | How to Jail                        | Location                                   |
| ---------------------- | ---------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| 🦈 **Loan Shark**       | Hit all 3 Loan Shark targets       | Left side of playfield <kcite ref="117"/>  |
| 🐊 **Machine Gun Croc** | Shoot the Croc Kickout (Hot Sheet) | Upper left area <kcite ref="115"/>         |
| 🐀 **Drug Rat**         | Drop all 3 Drug Rat targets        | Center, near right ramp <kcite ref="121"/> |
| 🦡 **Diamond Weasel**   | Drop all 3 Diamond Weasel targets  | Right side of playfield <kcite ref="110"/> |

Jailed criminals are indicated by lights in the upper left playfield above the Croc Kickout <kcite ref="125"/>.

---

### P-O-L-I-C-E Letters

Spell POLICE to light the **TOP COP BONUS** worth **3,000,000 points** <kcite ref="82"/><kcite ref="127"/>!

**Ways to spot letters:**
- Shoot the right ramp when lock is NOT lit <kcite ref="127"/>
- 75K Skill Shot (4 spins) spots a letter <kcite ref="127"/>
- Hot Sheet "Spot P-O-L-I-C-E" award <kcite ref="124"/>
- During multiball, shooting the right ramp <kcite ref="82"/>

> **💡 Pro Tip:** TOP COP stays lit until collected or end of ball - don't forget to collect it via the half-orbit shot to the upper left! <kcite ref="127"/>

---

### G-U-N Rollovers & Bonus Multiplier

Three lanes at the top spell G-U-N <kcite ref="76"/><kcite ref="112"/>:

| Multiplier | Carry Over?    | Special                                             |
| ---------- | -------------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
| 1X         | Default        | -                                                   |
| 2X         | ✅ Yes          | -                                                   |
| 3X         | ✅ Yes          | -                                                   |
| 4X         | ❌ Resets to 1X | -                                                   |
| 5X         | ❌ Resets to 1X | -                                                   |
| 6X         | ❌ Resets to 1X | Spell GUN again = **SPECIAL** lit <kcite ref="76"/> |

**Key benefit:** When multiplier increases, the **Jackpot value increases** from all target hits for ~10 seconds <kcite ref="76"/><kcite ref="128"/>!

> **💡 Pro Tip:** Use flipper buttons to change which G-U-N lane is lit (left flipper = shift left, right flipper = shift right) <kcite ref="112"/>.

## 🎱 Multiballs

### How to Start Multiball

1. **Lock is lit** at game start (or after jailing a criminal on subsequent attempts) <kcite ref="81"/><kcite ref="126"/>
2. Shoot the **right ramp** to lock Ball 1
3. Shoot the right ramp again (or hit Croc Kickout) to lock Ball 2 and start Multiball <kcite ref="81"/>

Green lights at the bottom of the ramp indicate lock status:
- Solid "Lock n" = ball is locked
- Blinking "Lock n" = next ball to be locked <kcite ref="126"/>

### Multiball Features

| Feature       | Details                                                                        |
| ------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Balls in Play | 2 balls <kcite ref="126"/>                                                     |
| Scoring       | **All playfield values DOUBLED** <kcite ref="126"/>                            |
| Ball Release  | Police car gizmo moves down, releasing balls to left inlane <kcite ref="116"/> |
| Center Ramp   | Hitting targets doesn't reset the ramp progression! <kcite ref="129"/>         |

### DOUBLECROSS Jackpot 🎯

During multiball, hit **both the center ramp AND right ramp simultaneously** for the **DOUBLECROSS** award worth **1M+** points! <kcite ref="45"/>

> **💡 Pro Tip:** This is the highest-value multiball scoring opportunity. Try to time shots so both balls are heading up ramps at once.

## 💰 Jackpot Structure

### Standard Jackpot

**Unique to Police Force:** Jackpot does NOT require multiball! <kcite ref="80"/><kcite ref="125"/>

| Requirement      | Details                                                      |
| ---------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| How to Light     | Jail all 4 criminals <kcite ref="125"/>                      |
| How to Collect   | Shoot the right ramp <kcite ref="125"/>                      |
| Base Value       | 1,000,000 points <kcite ref="80"/>                           |
| Maximum Value    | 4,000,000 points <kcite ref="80"/>                           |
| Time Limit       | **15 seconds** after jailing last criminal <kcite ref="80"/> |
| After Collection | Resets to 1M, criminals released <kcite ref="80"/>           |

### Building Jackpot Value

- Spell G-U-N to enable jackpot building for ~10 seconds <kcite ref="76"/>
- During this window, **all target hits increase jackpot value** <kcite ref="76"/>
- Stack this with multiball for doubled target values!

## 📈 Progression Systems

### Criminal Capture Cycle

```
Jail 4 Criminals → Jackpot Lit (15 sec) → Collect or Miss → Criminals Released → Repeat
```

Progress resets each time you collect the jackpot or the timer expires <kcite ref="77"/>.

### Center Ramp Progression (Unlimited Millions)

This is the **primary scoring mechanism** in Police Force <kcite ref="83"/><kcite ref="129"/>:

| Shot # | Value              | Notes                                 |
| ------ | ------------------ | ------------------------------------- |
| 1      | 50K                | Usually pre-lit at game start         |
| 2      | 75K                | Must hit within time limit            |
| 3      | 100K               | No other targets between shots        |
| 4      | 150K               | Keep the streak going!                |
| 5+     | **1,000,000 each** | UNLIMITED MILLIONS! <kcite ref="83"/> |

**Rules:**
- Must make consecutive shots without hitting other targets <kcite ref="129"/>
- Must complete within time limit
- During multiball, target hits DON'T break the streak <kcite ref="129"/>
- When Unlimited Millions is reached and you miss, **Extra Ball lights at Top Cop target** <kcite ref="83"/><kcite ref="129"/>
- After missing, ramp resets to 5K → 10K → 25K → then normal progression <kcite ref="129"/>

> **💡 Pro Tip:** Designer Mark Ritchie stated that for good players, the center ramp is "the only shot on the table they would aim for" <kcite ref="83"/>.

### Bonus Multiplier Carry-Over

| End of Ball Multiplier | Next Ball Starts At                          |
| ---------------------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| 2X or 3X               | Same value (carries over) <kcite ref="128"/> |
| 4X, 5X, or 6X          | Resets to 1X <kcite ref="128"/>              |

## 🔄 Stacking & Combos

### ✅ What Stacks Well

| Combination                           | Why It's Good                                                               |
| ------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Multiball + Center Ramp**           | Doubled values + no target penalty = massive millions <kcite ref="129"/>    |
| **Multiball + Jackpot**               | Doubled jackpot value (up to 8M effective)                                  |
| **GUN completion + Jackpot building** | 10-second window to pump up jackpot value <kcite ref="76"/>                 |
| **DOUBLECROSS during Multiball**      | 1M+ for simultaneous ramp shots <kcite ref="45"/>                           |
| **Playfield X + Multiball**           | Top rollovers light inlane for timed playfield multiplier <kcite ref="46"/> |

### ❌ What NOT to Stack

| Avoid                                        | Reason                                                                           |
| -------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Random target hits during center ramp streak | Breaks your Unlimited Millions progression (single ball only) <kcite ref="129"/> |
| Collecting jackpot too early                 | Wait until value is built up via GUN completion                                  |
| Starting multiball with no criminals jailed  | Wastes the doubled scoring potential                                             |

### Optimal Timing

1. **Early Ball:** Build up center ramp to Unlimited Millions
2. **Mid-Ball:** Jail criminals while maintaining ramp streak if possible
3. **Multiball:** Focus on DOUBLECROSS and center ramp (no target penalty)
4. **Ball 3:** Set up for Take Highest Score

## 🏆 Wizard Mode: Take Highest Score

This is Police Force's ultimate scoring feature - and its most controversial <kcite ref="84"/>!

### Requirements
- Must be on your **LAST BALL** (Ball 3 or final extra ball) <kcite ref="84"/>
- Must shoot the **right ramp TWICE in a row** <kcite ref="84"/>

### How It Works
- Successfully shooting the right ramp twice adds the **highest score achieved in that game** to your score <kcite ref="84"/>
- If you're the only player (or in the lead), this **DOUBLES your score** <kcite ref="84"/>
- You only get **ONE CHANCE** - hitting any other target after the first ramp shot counts as a miss <kcite ref="84"/>
- If you earn an extra ball, you get another attempt <kcite ref="84"/>

### Richie Tournament ROM Variant
On the competition ROM, shooting the right ramp twice on Ball 3 instead maxes the jackpot value at **9.9M** (instead of Take Highest Score) <kcite ref="47"/>.

> **⚠️ Designer's Note:** Mark Ritchie later called this feature "the largest flaw in the game, making competition play moot" <kcite ref="84"/>.

## 🎯 Shot Analysis

### Playfield Layout

```
        [G-U-N Rollovers]
    [EB/Top Cop]  [Pop Bumpers]
         \          /
    [Croc Kickout]  [Spinner/Skill Shot]
           \        |
    [Loan Shark]   [Police Car Lock]
      Targets      [Right Ramp →]
         \         /
    [Center Ramp ↑]  [Weasel Targets]
         \         /
    [Drug Rat Targets]
         \       /
    [LEFT FLIPPER] ═══ [CENTER POST] ═══ [RIGHT FLIPPER]
```

### 🟢 Safe Shots

| Shot             | Risk Level | Notes                                              |
| ---------------- | ---------- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| **Center Ramp**  | LOW        | Primary scoring shot, returns to left flipper area |
| **Right Ramp**   | LOW-MEDIUM | Returns to left inlane when lock not active        |
| **Croc Kickout** | LOW        | Controlled kickout, awards Hot Sheet               |

### 🔴 Dangerous Shots

| Shot                    | Risk Level  | Drain Path                  |
| ----------------------- | ----------- | --------------------------- |
| **Loan Shark Targets**  | MEDIUM-HIGH | Can deflect to left outlane |
| **Weasel Drop Targets** | MEDIUM      | Can bounce unpredictably    |
| **Drug Rat Targets**    | MEDIUM      | Center drain risk           |
| **Pop Bumpers**         | VARIABLE    | Chaotic ball movement       |

### Flipper Selection

| Target             | Recommended Flipper                           |
| ------------------ | --------------------------------------------- |
| Center Ramp        | **Right flipper** - backhand or straight shot |
| Right Ramp         | **Right flipper** - direct shot               |
| Croc Kickout       | **Left flipper** - cross shot                 |
| Loan Shark Targets | **Left flipper** - direct                     |
| Top Cop/EB Target  | **Left flipper** - orbit shot                 |

> **💡 Pro Tip:** The center post between flippers provides extra drain protection - use it! <kcite ref="122"/>

## 📊 Scoring Strategy

### Path to Maximum Score

#### Phase 1: Early Game (Ball 1)
1. **Skill Shot:** Aim for 75K (spots POLICE letter) or 100K <kcite ref="43"/>
2. **Center Ramp Focus:** Build to Unlimited Millions immediately
3. **Collect millions** until you miss or drain
4. **Jail criminals** opportunistically (don't break ramp streak)
5. **Build GUN multiplier** to 2X or 3X (carries over) <kcite ref="128"/>

#### Phase 2: Mid Game (Ball 2)
1. **Continue center ramp** strategy
2. **Start Multiball** when ready (jail a criminal first if needed)
3. **During Multiball:**
   - Center ramp for doubled millions (no target penalty!) <kcite ref="129"/>
   - Attempt DOUBLECROSS for 1M+ <kcite ref="45"/>
   - Collect Jackpot if all criminals jailed
4. **Spell POLICE** and collect Top Cop bonus (3M) <kcite ref="127"/>

#### Phase 3: End Game (Ball 3)
1. **Build score as high as possible** via center ramp
2. **When ready for Take Highest Score:**
   - Cradle the ball
   - Take a breath
   - Shoot right ramp
   - **Immediately** shoot right ramp again
   - **DOUBLE YOUR SCORE** <kcite ref="84"/>

### Scoring Hierarchy

| Feature            | Points            | Priority                 |
| ------------------ | ----------------- | ------------------------ |
| Take Highest Score | **DOUBLES TOTAL** | 🥇 Ball 3 only            |
| Unlimited Millions | 1M per shot       | 🥇 Primary strategy       |
| DOUBLECROSS        | 1M+               | 🥈 Multiball only         |
| Jackpot            | 1-4M              | 🥈 After criminals jailed |
| Top Cop Bonus      | 3M                | 🥉 After POLICE spelled   |
| Skill Shot (max)   | 300K (Ball 3)     | 🥉 Free points            |

### Realistic Score Targets

| Skill Level  | Target Score | Strategy                                                            |
| ------------ | ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Beginner     | 5-10M        | Focus on center ramp basics                                         |
| Intermediate | 20-50M       | Consistent Unlimited Millions + Jackpots                            |
| Advanced     | 50-100M      | Multiball stacking + Take Highest Score                             |
| Expert       | 100M+        | Perfect center ramp control + doubled score on B3 <kcite ref="98"/> |

## 🎁 Additional Information

### Extra Ball Opportunities

| Source               | How to Light                                                                     |
| -------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Top Cop Target**   | Reach Unlimited Millions, then miss the ramp <kcite ref="83"/><kcite ref="129"/> |
| **Hot Sheet**        | "Hot Extra Ball" random award <kcite ref="124"/>                                 |
| **Outlanes/Inlanes** | "Pity EB" - lit on last ball when game feels sorry for you <kcite ref="118"/>    |

### Mystery Awards (Hot Sheet)

Shoot the Croc Kickout to receive one of five awards <kcite ref="79"/><kcite ref="124"/>:

| Award            | Frequency | Notes                                            |
| ---------------- | --------- | ------------------------------------------------ |
| Hot Score        | Common    | Mystery point value                              |
| Hot Extra Ball   | Uncommon  | Free ball!                                       |
| Spot P-O-L-I-C-E | Uncommon  | Completes POLICE instantly                       |
| Hot Multi-Ball   | Rare      | Only if 1 ball already locked <kcite ref="124"/> |
| 5 Free Games     | Very Rare | Least common award <kcite ref="124"/>            |

### Setup Recommendations

| Setting          | Recommendation            | Reason                             |
| ---------------- | ------------------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| Pitch            | 6.5° - 7.0°               | Standard for System 11 games       |
| Level            | Ensure side-to-side level | Prevents unfair drains             |
| Flipper Strength | Factory default           | Center ramp shot calibration       |
| Lock at Start    | ON (default)              | Allows immediate multiball attempt |

### Competition ROM (LX-5) Changes

If playing on the Richie Tournament ROM <kcite ref="47"/>:
- Center ramp **never** lights for Unlimited Millions <kcite ref="48"/>
- Center ramp maxes at 100K but advances jackpot by 250K per shot
- Ball 3 right ramp x2 = **9.9M Jackpot** (not Take Highest Score) <kcite ref="47"/>
- More balanced for tournament play

### Easter Eggs & Trivia

- 🦇 Originally designed as a **Batman** game before Data East secured the license <kcite ref="72"/>
- 🚗 The Batmobile became the Police Car, the Batcave became the Jail <kcite ref="72"/>
- 🚕 The taxi cab in the backglass is from Mark Ritchie's previous game, *Taxi* <kcite ref="73"/>
- 🐌 A snail representing the golden ratio crosses the street in the backglass art <kcite ref="73"/>
- 🎮 Available on Atari Lynx in *Pinball Jam* (1992) alongside *Elvira and the Party Monsters* <kcite ref="86"/>
