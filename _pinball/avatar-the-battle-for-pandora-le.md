# 🌿 Avatar: The Battle for Pandora - Strategy Guide

*Jersey Jack Pinball, 2024*

> **"I see you."**

Avatar is a sprawling, ramp-heavy modern machine with six flippers (three full-size, three mini), two lower playfields, and a deep PANDORA-letter progression system feeding into a true wizard mode. Our goal: spell **PANDORA**, survive **Eywa'Eveng**, and chase the 10-milestone super wizard mode **Oel Ngati Kameie**.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Choose a skill shot via the action button** at the start of each ball — locking toward multiball, lighting Mystery at the Home Tree VUK, or spotting bond combos are all strong picks
- [ ] **Prioritize Sivako modes early** — they're the most consistent scoring source
- [ ] **Mash the action button during 4x multiplier windows** to maintain your arrow/multiplier streak
- [ ] **Use the left flipper for scoop and Tree of Souls shots** — it's the more reliable angle for these
- [ ] **Extra ball lights at the Home Tree VUK after your first 3 PANDORA letters** — go get it
- [ ] **Don't ignore the lower playfields** — the dive shot ("Scramp") feeds two distinct lower playfields worth real points, including the crab-suit battle

## 🪐 Core Mechanics

### Skill Shot (Action Button Menu)

At the start of each ball, choose from several skill shot options:
- **Lock a ball toward multiball**
- **Light Mystery at the Home Tree VUK**
- **Spot bond combos at the rainforest ramp**

> 🎯 **Pro Tip:** There's no single "best" skill shot — pick whichever sets up the strategy you're leaning into for that ball (multiball progress vs. bond combo progress vs. a guaranteed bonus).

### PANDORA Letters

Earn letters toward spelling **P-A-N-D-O-R-A** by:
- Completing **Sivako modes**
- Finishing **Songcord modes**
- Scoring **Eclipse jackpots**
- Collecting **Bond Combo jackpots**
- Winning **multiballs**

**Extra ball** lights at the Home Tree VUK once your first 3 letters are scored. Spelling all of PANDORA lights the scoop for **Eywa'Eveng**.

### Bond Combos

Eight specific shots build "bond combo" progress. Once all 8 have been used in a combo sequence, the **Bond Combo Jackpot** becomes collectible at the scoop.

### Eclipse Jackpot

Builds from accumulated switch hits across the playfield. **Crucially, it factors in points scored during any modes running alongside it** — so starting Eclipse while a mode is active and stacking them together maximizes its value. Collect at the right ramp's spinner; it resets after collection.

## 🎁 Multiballs

### Battle of the Hallelujah Mountains

1. Hit the **center arrow standup targets** to light lock
2. Lock balls in the **Infinity Mirror** via the upper-right flipper
3. Lock 3 balls, then shoot the lit orbits to start

### Battle of Awa'Atlu

1. Make the **dive shot** ("Scramp") into the lower playfield
2. Complete the lower-playfield targets
3. Defeat the **RDA's crab suit** using the lower playfield's mini-flippers (a multistage battle)

### Shared Multiball Jackpot Sequence

Both multiballs share the same jackpot shot sequence: **center targets → loopback ramp → scoop → VUK → spider target**. An **Add-A-Ball** is earned every 10 award shots collected during either multiball.

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Shot | Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| **Ramp-heavy layout in general** | Six ramps weaving across floating rock sculpts demand precise aim; mis-hits can deflect unpredictably | Practice each ramp's specific angle individually before attempting them under multiball pressure |
| **Dive shot ("Scramp") misses** | The hybrid scoop/ramp mechanism has multiple positions (neutral, raised, jump-ramp) — timing matters | Confirm the Scramp's current position before committing to the shot |
| **Lower playfield crab-suit battle** | Controlled by mini-flippers, which are less forgiving than full-size flippers | Take it slow; mini-flipper control requires more deliberate, smaller shots |
| **Losing your 4x multiplier streak** | Letting the arrow timer expire resets accumulated multiplier progress | Mash the action button during the window if the game supports streak maintenance this way |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Game (Ball 1)

1. Pick a skill shot that sets up your early game (Mystery or bond combo spotting are safe choices)
2. Prioritize Sivako modes for consistent, reliable scoring
3. Start working toward your first 3 PANDORA letters to unlock the extra ball

**Target Score:** 20M–50M

### Mid Game (Ball 2)

1. Build toward a multiball (Hallelujah Mountains or Awa'Atlu) via locks or the dive shot
2. Start stacking modes with Eclipse to maximize its jackpot value
3. Keep collecting PANDORA letters through Songcord modes and bond combos

**Target Score:** 50M–120M

### Late Game (Ball 3+)

1. If PANDORA is spelled, dive into **Eywa'Eveng** — a tough 3-ball multiball worth roughly 100M if you can make enough shots
2. Stack Add-A-Balls during multiball to extend playtime
3. If you've hit all 10 milestones, push for **Oel Ngati Kameie**, the true super wizard mode

**Target Score per ball:** 50M–150M+

## 🏆 Wizard Modes

### Eywa'Eveng (Primary Wizard Mode)

- **Requirement:** Spell PANDORA
- **Gameplay:** 3-ball multiball — re-spell PANDORA by completing sets of lit shots shown on the display
- **Difficulty:** Very tough to finish; a strong run scores around **100M**

### Oel Ngati Kameie — "I See You" (Super Wizard Mode)

- **Requirement:** Complete all **10 milestones** across a game (or across games, depending on settings)
- **Note:** The true end-game achievement on this machine — reaching it requires sustained, multi-session mastery of every major mode and multiball

## ⚙️ Setup Recommendations

| Setting | Recommendation | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| **Playfield pitch** | 6.5°–7.0° | JJP factory standard for modern wide-body ramp layouts |
| **Scramp calibration** | Verify both raised and neutral positions feed cleanly | Misaligned Scramp positioning disrupts both lower-playfield access and the jump-ramp shot |
| **Mini-flipper strength** | Factory default, check regularly | Crab-suit battle relies on precise mini-flipper control |

## 📋 Quick Reference: Strategic Priorities

**Ball 1:** Pick a setup skill shot, grind Sivako modes, bank your first 3 PANDORA letters.
**Ball 2:** Build multiball locks, stack modes into Eclipse, keep collecting letters.
**Ball 3+:** Cash in Eywa'Eveng if PANDORA is spelled, chase milestones toward Oel Ngati Kameie.

**Target High-Score Ballpark:** **150–300 million points** is a strong, competitive game; reaching Eywa'Eveng and eventually Oel Ngati Kameie marks true mastery of this deep ruleset.
