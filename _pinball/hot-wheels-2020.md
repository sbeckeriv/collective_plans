# 🏎️ Hot Wheels - Strategy Guide

*American Pinball, 2020 — Designed by Joe Balcer*

> **"Rev the tach. Collect the cars. Stack everything."**

Hot Wheels races toward billion-point scores through five collectible Hero Cars, four Battle modes against Draven's creatures, four EPIC features, and four stackable multiballs — all multiplied by a constantly-decaying tachometer value. Our goal: collect all 5 Hero Cars to light Legend Multiball, while keeping your tach value high and stacking as many modes together as possible.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Take the skill shot** by softly plunging over the numbered rollovers (1, 2, 3) to build tachometer wedges
- [ ] **Try the advanced skill shot**: hold the left flipper to rev the engine, release at tach value **"10"** (without exceeding it) for a launch-point multiplier
- [ ] **Keep your tachometer value high** — it multiplies nearly all scoring, and **decays over time** (faster at higher gear levels), so don't let it idle
- [ ] **Collecting 2 Hero Cars lights an extra ball** on the left saucer shot
- [ ] **Stack everything** — all four multiballs stack with active Battle and EPIC modes, and Loop Crash + Victory Lap can run together as a 5-ball multiball
- [ ] **Activate turbo boost via pop bumper hits at high RPMs** for maximum scoring impact, ideally after you've already stacked multiple modes

---

## 🏁 Core Mechanics

### Tachometer (Multiplies Everything)

Your tach value multiplies nearly all scoring in the game — but it **decreases over time while the ball is in play**, and **drops faster at higher gear levels**. Managing this decay is the single most important ongoing decision in the game.

> 🎯 **Pro Tip — Core Strategy:** Don't let the tach idle down. Keep making shots that maintain or boost it, and save your **turbo boost** (via pop bumper hits at high RPM) for moments when you've already stacked multiple modes — the multiplier applies to everything happening simultaneously.

### Hero Cars (5 Total — Your Main Collection Goal)

| Hero Car | How to Collect |
|---|---|
| **Twin Mill** | Finish **Car Chaos Multiball** |
| **Roger Dodger** | Complete the **Draven battle mode** |
| **Sharkruiser** | Finish **Victory Lap, Loop Crash, AND Track Builder Unlimited** multiballs |
| **Bone Shaker** | Complete **Ultimate Epic** |
| **Rip Rod** | Finish **Red Line Mania** once |

**Collecting 2 Hero Cars lights an extra ball** at the left saucer shot.

### Battle Modes (4 + Draven Finale)

Qualify by completing all **BATTLE** standup targets. The four modes — **Gorilla, T-Rex, Cobra, Triceratops** — each have progressive waves. **Draven** is the final battle, available only after completing the other four; **completing Draven awards the Roger Dodger Hero Car** if not already lit.

### EPIC Features (4 + Ultimate)

Collect **E-P-I-C** letters via inlane/outlane rollovers. Each letter requires hitting specific shot counts: **Cars: 8, Loops: 5, Pops: 20, Ramps: 5**. Completing the **Ultimate Epic** feature grants the **Bone Shaker Hero Car**.

## 🎁 The Four Multiballs

| Multiball | Qualification | Format |
|---|---|---|
| **Victory Lap** | Progress through 12 race positions via designated ramps/orbits, finish with the checkered flag | 4-ball, progressive jackpots |
| **Loop Crash** | Hit 6 lit loop shots | 3-ball, "crashes" awarded for loop/orbit/bumper hits |
| **Track Builder Unlimited** | Hit the drop target or left saucer 4 times | 2-ball, expandable to 3, jackpot arrows on orbits/loops |
| **Red Line Mania** | Starts automatically when tachometer maxes out | 4 balls auto-launched, 45 seconds, complete all lit shots for Super Jackpots, increases gear up to 5th |

> 🔗 **Stacking:** **All four multiball modes stack with active Battle and EPIC modes.** Loop Crash and Victory Lap specifically can run together as a combined **5-ball multiball** — the single biggest stacking opportunity in the game.

## 🏆 Legend Multiball (Wizard Mode)

**Requirement:** Collect all 5 Hero Cars.

- A **2-minute timed multiball** with a **ball save active for the entire duration**
- Eventually reaches **6 balls in play**
- **Hit all shots** before time runs out for the **Legend Jackpot**

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **Pop bumper area exits** | Can drain straight down without any nudging opportunity | Stay alert any time the ball enters the pop bumper field |
| **Left and right outlanes without ball save** | Outside of Legend Multiball's guaranteed ball save, outlanes drain normally | Don't assume protection unless you're specifically in a ball-saved mode |
| **Letting the tach decay during a stacked mode** | Reduces the value of everything happening simultaneously | Keep feeding tach-building shots even while focused on mode objectives |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Game (Ball 1)

1. Take the skill shot, ideally landing the tach-10 advanced version
2. Start working toward your first Hero Car via whichever path is closest
3. Begin collecting EPIC letters and Battle mode targets

**Target Score:** 50M–150M

### Mid Game (Ball 2)

1. Push toward 2 Hero Cars for the extra ball
2. Start stacking multiballs — Loop Crash + Victory Lap is your biggest combo
3. Keep tach value high through consistent shot-making

**Target Score:** 150M–500M

### Late Game (Ball 3+)

1. Finish collecting remaining Hero Cars
2. Once all 5 are collected, light and run Legend Multiball
3. Use turbo boost during your biggest stacks for maximum scoring

**Target Score per ball:** 300M–2B+ (this game's scoring can reach into the billions with strong stacking)

## 📋 Quick Reference: Strategic Priorities

**Ball 1:** Skill shot, start a Hero Car path, collect EPIC/Battle progress.
**Ball 2:** Push to 2 Hero Cars for extra ball, stack Loop Crash + Victory Lap, manage tach decay.
**Ball 3+:** Finish all 5 Hero Cars, run Legend Multiball, use turbo boost during big stacks.

**Target High-Score Ballpark:** Hot Wheels is explicitly designed to reach **billions of points** with strong mode-stacking — don't be surprised by scores far larger than typical modern Stern/JJP titles.
