### 🎱 Plunge Strategy

Unlike modern machines with skill shots, EM plunging is about **ball control**:

- **Soft Plunge**: Keeps ball in upper playfield longer for bumper action
- **Medium Plunge**: Aims to roll over star rollovers on the way down
- **Hard Plunge**: Sends ball directly toward drop targets

> 💡 **Pro Tip**: A medium-strength plunge that sends the ball over the star rollovers toward the drop targets is ideal for building both saucer value AND bonus simultaneously <kcite ref="21"/>

## ⭐ Star Rollovers - The Key to Big Scores

The 5 star rollovers are **central to your strategy** <kcite ref="22"/>:

| Rollovers Collected | Saucer Value |
| ------------------- | ------------ |
| 0                   | 1,000 pts    |
| 1                   | 2,000 pts    |
| 2                   | 3,000 pts    |
| 3                   | 4,000 pts    |
| 4                   | 5,000 pts    |
| 5 (all)             | 6,000 pts    |

<kcite ref="22"/><kcite ref="58"/>

**Additional Benefits of Completing All 5 Rollovers:**
- Lights the side lane rollovers for **Double Bonus** <kcite ref="22"/>
- Enables path to **Triple Bonus** on last ball

> ⚠️ **Warning**: The second rollover from the right is the riskiest shot - balls continue into the right bumper and can rebound dangerously! <kcite ref="22"/>

## 🎯 Drop Targets

The 5-bank drop targets are your primary scoring objective:

| Action             | Points        | Bonus         |
| ------------------ | ------------- | ------------- |
| Each target hit    | 500 pts       | +1 bonus unit |
| All 5 targets down | Targets reset | -             |

<kcite ref="50"/><kcite ref="86"/>

**Drop Target Strategy:**
- Targets are positioned behind the pop bumpers, making them challenging shots <kcite ref="47"/>
- Ball deflections from targets often send the ball into pop bumpers
- Complete all 5 rollovers BEFORE the drop targets reset for maximum efficiency <kcite ref="66"/>

## 📈 Scoring Strategy

### 🌅 Early Game (Ball 1-2)

**Priority: Build Foundation**

1. ⭐ Collect all 5 star rollovers to max saucer value
2. 🎯 Hit drop targets for 500 pts each + bonus
3. 🕳️ Shoot saucer repeatedly at 6,000 pts
4. 🔓 Light side lanes for bonus multiplier opportunity

### 🌊 Mid Game (Ball 3-4 in 5-ball / Ball 2 in 3-ball)

**Priority: Accumulate Points**

1. 🕳️ Saucer farming at max value (6,000 pts per shot)
2. 🎯 Continue drop target completion
3. 📈 Build bonus toward 15,000 maximum
4. 2️⃣ Achieve Double Bonus if possible

### 🏆 Late Game (Final Ball)

**Priority: Maximize Bonus**

1. 3️⃣ **CRITICAL**: Light Triple Bonus! <kcite ref="52"/>
2. 📈 Max out bonus to 15,000
3. 🕳️ Continue saucer shots
4. 💰 Collect 45,000 point bonus at ball end

## 🎁 Extra Ball & Special

### Extra Ball

- **How to Light**: Complete all 5 drop targets AND all 5 star rollovers <kcite ref="78"/>
- **How to Collect**: Drain through the **left outlane** when lit <kcite ref="66"/><kcite ref="75"/>
- **Note**: "Extra Ball can only be obtained by losing the current ball" <kcite ref="66"/>

### Special

- **How to Light**: Same as Extra Ball (complete drops + rollovers)
- **How to Collect**: Drain through the **right outlane** when lit
- **Award**: Free game (or points if set by operator)

> ⚠️ **Unique Mechanic**: You must drain to collect these awards - they're consolation prizes for losing your ball through the outlanes!

## 🏆 Competition Tips

1. **Practice the saucer shot** - This is your bread and butter
2. **Learn to nudge** - Essential for saving balls from bumper chaos
3. **Prioritize rollovers early** - 6,000 pt saucer >> 1,000 pt saucer
4. **Don't neglect bonus** - 45,000 points is nearly half a great game
