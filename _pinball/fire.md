# 🔥 Fire! - Strategy Guide

*Williams, 1987 — Designed by Barry Oursler*

> **"Put it out fast, or watch the multiplier burn down."**

Fire! is a speed game disguised as a firefighting theme: a Fire Multiplier starts every ball at its maximum 10x and counts down — 10x → 7x → 5x → 3x → 2x — every time you fail to extinguish a fire within its timed window. Our goal: extinguish every fire as fast as possible, lock balls for multiball, and put out all 5 multiball fires for the 1M rescue bonus.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Understand the Fire Multiplier starts at 10x and only goes down** — every ball begins at maximum value, so speed (not building up a multiplier) is the entire strategic challenge
- [ ] **Put out fires immediately** by completing the relevant target bank or making the lit shot — the longer you take, the multiplier drops: 10x → 7x → 5x → 3x → 2x
- [ ] **Hit targets to lower the ramps at the horseshoe lanes** to start ball locks toward multiball
- [ ] **The small horseshoe lane above the upper ramp** advances a separate bonus multiplier (2x-3x-4x-5x-10x), shown in the storefront windows
- [ ] **During multiball, put out all 5 fires and make the rescue shot for a flat 1,000,000 points**
- [ ] **"Save the baby"** — rescue-themed objectives also decay if you're too slow, so treat every timed objective with urgency

## 🚒 Core Mechanics

### The Fire Multiplier (The Whole Game)

A series of playfield inserts displays your current multiplier, starting at **10x** for every new ball:

| Multiplier | Trigger |
|---|---|
| **10x** | Start of every ball (maximum) |
| 7x | After 1 fire missed within its time window |
| 5x | After 2 fires missed |
| 3x | After 3 fires missed |
| 2x | After 4+ fires missed |

**Putting out a fire** (by completing its target bank or making its lit shot) **scores points multiplied by your current Fire Multiplier**. Since the multiplier only ever goes down from its starting maximum, your entire strategic goal each ball is simple: **extinguish fires as fast as possible** to keep cashing in at the highest multiplier you can.

> 🔥 **Pro Tip — Core Strategy:** There's no benefit to waiting or building up to this multiplier — it's already maxed at the start of every ball. Speed is the only lever you control; treat every appearing fire as urgent.

### Horseshoe Lanes & Ball Locks

Two large horseshoe lanes are each protected by **moving ramps** that raise and lower:
- **Hit targets to lower the ramps**, which then **deliver the ball to ball locks**
- Locking balls progresses you toward multiball

### Upper Playfield Ramp & Bonus Multiplier

A separate ramp on the upper playfield raises and lowers, allowing entry to an **elevated opening for multiball**. A **small horseshoe lane above this ramp** advances a distinct **bonus multiplier (2x → 3x → 4x → 5x → 10x)**, displayed in the lower playfield's storefront windows — this is separate from the Fire Multiplier and affects your end-of-ball bonus instead.

### Multiball: The 5-Fire Rescue

1. Lock balls via the horseshoe ramps to start the **3-ball multiball**
2. During multiball, work to **put out all 5 fires**
3. **Successfully extinguishing all 5 and making the rescue shot awards a flat 1,000,000 points**

> 💰 **Pro Tip:** The 1M multiball rescue bonus is a flat award independent of your Fire Multiplier — it's one of the single biggest guaranteed point swings in the game, so prioritize completing the full 5-fire sequence over cashing in partial progress.

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **No pop bumpers to absorb stray shots** | This layout lacks the forgiving chaos of pop bumpers, so misses are more consequential | Play deliberate, controlled shots rather than relying on incidental bumper scoring |
| **Moving ramp timing** | Horseshoe ramps raise/lower during play — a mistimed shot can be wasted if the ramp isn't in the right position | Watch ramp position before committing to a horseshoe shot |
| **Panicking under the multiplier countdown** | Rushing shots to "beat the clock" can lead to careless misses | Prioritize accuracy over speed on individual shots — losing one multiplier tier is better than draining |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Ball

1. Extinguish your first fires quickly to bank points at the full 10x multiplier
2. Start working horseshoe targets to lower ramps toward ball locks
3. Use the small upper horseshoe lane to begin building your separate bonus multiplier

### Mid Ball

1. Keep extinguishing fires promptly — every tier you preserve matters
2. Lock balls toward the 3-ball multiball
3. Continue building the bonus multiplier (2x-10x) via the small horseshoe lane

### Late Ball

1. Once in multiball, focus on completing the full 5-fire sequence for the 1M rescue bonus
2. Bank your accumulated bonus, now multiplied by whatever you built via the small horseshoe lane
3. Take any remaining fire-extinguishing opportunities before the ball ends

## 📋 Quick Reference: Strategic Priorities

**Early:** Extinguish fires fast at full 10x, start lowering horseshoe ramps.
**Mid:** Keep preserving multiplier tiers, lock balls toward multiball, build bonus multiplier.
**Late:** Complete the full 5-fire multiball rescue for 1M, bank your multiplied bonus.

**Target High-Score Ballpark:** Consistently extinguishing fires at high multiplier tiers, combined with at least one completed 5-fire multiball rescue, represents a strong, well-played game on this speed-focused firefighting classic.
