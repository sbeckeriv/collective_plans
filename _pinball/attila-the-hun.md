# ⚔️ Attila the Hun Pinball Machine - Strategy Guide

*Game Plan Inc., 1984 — Designed by John Trudeau*

> **"Conquer the grid, collect the bonus."**

Attila the Hun is a tightly-designed early-80s solid-state game built around a central 4×3 grid: drop targets, top lanes, and a captive ball all feed into completing rows and columns. With only 500 units made, this is a rare machine — but its rules are refreshingly straightforward once you understand the grid. Our goal: a strong **40K–80K** game, built almost entirely on bonus accumulation rather than in-the-moment scoring.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Think in rows and columns, not individual shots** — the central grid rewards completing lines, not random hits
- [ ] **Spread your shots around early** — drop targets, top lanes (H-U-N, T-H-E), and the captive ball all need attention
- [ ] **Don't chase one objective repeatedly** — most scoring comes from end-of-ball bonus, not immediate points
- [ ] **Complete H-U-N or T-H-E three times** to light Special
- [ ] **Complete H-U-N or T-H-E plus all drop targets** to light the extra ball
- [ ] **Watch captive ball and drop target rebounds** — these are the main drain risks on this layout

## ⚔️ Core Mechanics

### The Central Grid

The heart of the game is a 4×3 grid filled in by three shot types:

| Shot Type | Grid Row/Column Contribution |
|---|---|
| **A-T-T-I-L-A drop targets** (two 3-banks) | Fill grid positions |
| **Top lanes (H-U-N, T-H-E)** | Fill grid positions |
| **Captive ball hits** (3 strikes) | Fill grid positions |

**Completing a line scores more than individual hits:**

| Completion | Value |
|---|---|
| **Row** (e.g., A-T-T or A-I-T-H) | 25K |
| **Column** | 50K |

> 🎯 **Pro Tip:** Look at the grid display and plan toward completing a column over a row whenever you have a choice — columns pay double.

### Lane & Captive Ball Multiplier Triggers

| Action | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Captive ball hit completing T-H-E** | Advances bonus multiplier |
| **Completing the top H-U-N lanes** | Advances bonus multiplier |
| **H-U-N or T-H-E completed 3 times** | Lights **Special** |
| **H-U-N or T-H-E + all drop targets completed** | Lights **Extra Ball** |

### No Shortcuts

There's no "quickie" route to a big score here — you genuinely need to work through **all six drop targets, all three top lanes, and three captive ball hits** across the ball to maximize your scoring chances. This is a game of thoroughness, not repetition.

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Shot | Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| **Captive ball rebounds** | Can kick back unpredictably toward the outlanes | Approach with a controlled, centered shot rather than a full-power slap |
| **Drop target rebounds** | Similar unpredictable kickback risk | Track which bank you're shooting and adjust angle based on prior rebounds |
| **Spinner** | Relatively safe — use this as a low-risk filler shot | Lean on it when you need a breather between grid attempts |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Ball (Ball 1)

1. Spread shots across drop targets, top lanes, and the captive ball — don't fixate on one
2. Prioritize completing a column if the grid display shows you're close
3. Work toward your first H-U-N or T-H-E completion for an early multiplier bump

**Target Score:** 10K–20K

### Mid Ball (Ball 2)

1. Continue balanced shot variety — by now you should have multiple grid lines filled
2. Chase a second H-U-N/T-H-E completion toward lighting Special
3. Watch your drop target completion count — getting all six plus a lane completion lights the extra ball

**Target Score:** 20K–35K

### Late Ball (Ball 3+)

1. Finish remaining grid positions for any uncompleted rows/columns
2. Cash in on your accumulated bonus — this is where most of your final score comes from
3. If extra ball is lit, take it before risking a drain on a marginal grid shot

**Target Score per ball:** 15K–30K

## ⚙️ Setup Recommendations

| Setting | Recommendation | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| **Playfield pitch** | 6.0°–6.5° | Standard for early-80s Game Plan solid-state machines |
| **Flipper strength** | Balanced (no dominant flipper) | The game doesn't favor one flipper over the other — both see regular action |
| **Captive ball lane condition** | Keep clean and well-lubricated | A sticky captive ball lane changes rebound behavior significantly |

---

## 📋 Quick Reference: Strategic Priorities

**Ball 1:** Spread shots evenly across drop targets, lanes, and captive ball.
**Ball 2:** Chase H-U-N/T-H-E completions for multiplier and Special.
**Ball 3+:** Finish remaining grid lines, light and take the extra ball, bank your bonus.

**Target High-Score Ballpark:** **40K–80K** is a strong, thorough game on Attila the Hun — remember, a good bonus trumps any single flashy shot on this machine.
