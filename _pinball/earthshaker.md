# 🌋 Earthshaker! Ultimate Strategy Guide
## Williams, 1989 | Target: 50-100 Million Points

> **Reality Check:** Earthshaker is a System 11 game where 1 billion points is not achievable. The highest recorded scores on Pinside are around **90 million points** <kcite ref="107"/>. This guide targets the **50-100 million range** as an elite goal.

---

## 📋 Quick-Start Checklist

- [ ] Medium plunge for 100K skill shot
- [ ] Complete lit zones to enable lock
- [ ] Lock balls via Fault (side ramp) or Shelter
- [ ] During Multiball: Center ramp → Fault for Jackpot
- [ ] Spam center ramp when safe (builds to 200K/shot)
- [ ] Always collect Match-Ups at Shelter
- [ ] Hit drop targets to increase Jackpot value

---

## 🎯 Core Mechanics

### Skill Shot Options

| Plunge Strength   | Exit Point      | Award        | Notes                                     |
| ----------------- | --------------- | ------------ | ----------------------------------------- |
| Very Soft         | First hole      | 10K          | Rarely useful                             |
| Soft              | Second hole     | 25K          | Safe option                               |
| Medium            | Third hole      | 50K          | Good balance                              |
| **Medium-Strong** | **Fourth hole** | **100K**     | **Optimal choice**                        |
| Hard (Overpass)   | Left inlane     | 25K + 1 mile | Sets up 50K center ramp <kcite ref="66"/> |

> **💡 Pro Tip:** The medium plunge to 100K is the standard skill shot. However, the overpass (hard plunge) gives you 25K + 1 mile AND feeds the left flipper for an immediate center ramp shot worth 50K if hit quickly <kcite ref="66"/>.

---

### The 9 Zones

Complete zones to light ball locks for Multiball <kcite ref="67"/>:

| Zone | Location             | How to Hit             |
| ---- | -------------------- | ---------------------- |
| 1    | Left stand-up target | Upper left flipper     |
| 2    | Upper right stand-up | Right flipper backhand |
| 3    | Lower right stand-up | Right flipper          |
| 4    | Center stand-up      | Right of center ramp   |
| 5    | Eject hole           | Upper left flipper     |
| 6    | Loop                 | Upper left flipper     |
| 7    | Right return lane    | Ball return            |
| 8    | Left return lane     | Ball return            |
| 9    | Captive ball         | Right flipper          |

**Zone Order Requirements:**
- **1st Multiball:** Any order <kcite ref="67"/>
- **2nd Multiball:** Row-by-row (1-2-3, then 4-5-6, then 7-8-9)
- **3rd+ Multiball:** Strict numerical order (1→2→3→4→5→6→7→8→9) <kcite ref="26"/>

> **💡 Pro Tip:** Zones can be spotted by: plunge, captive ball hits, and fault trips <kcite ref="24"/>. The fault trip is especially valuable as it spots the lowest uncompleted zone automatically <kcite ref="52"/>.

---

### Center Ramp Scoring

The center ramp is your bread-and-butter shot <kcite ref="57"/>:

| Shot #         | Value                 | Miles |
| -------------- | --------------------- | ----- |
| 1              | 25K (or 50K if quick) | 1-2   |
| 2              | 50K                   | 1-2   |
| 3+             | +10K per shot         | 1-2   |
| Max            | 100K                  | 1-2   |
| After 99 miles | **200K**              | 1-2   |

- **2 Miles Timer:** Hit center ramp within ~5 seconds of passing left inlane for 2 miles instead of 1 <kcite ref="57"/>
- **Side Ramp (Fault):** Awards 3 miles per trip <kcite ref="22"/>

> **⚠️ Tournament Note:** The "Soren ROM" heavily nerfs the center ramp spam strategy. Expect this in major tournaments <kcite ref="88"/>.

## 🎱 Multiballs

### Regular Multiball (3-Ball)

**How to Qualify:**
1. Complete all predicted zones for the current building <kcite ref="64"/>
2. Lock Ball 1 at Shelter scoop OR Fault ramp
3. Complete zones for second prediction
4. Lock Ball 2 at Fault ramp only
5. Plunge Ball 3 to start Multiball <kcite ref="41"/>

**Jackpot Structure:**

| Jackpot Level | Value             | How to Increase           |
| ------------- | ----------------- | ------------------------- |
| 1             | 500K + Special    | Starting value            |
| 2             | 1M                | Complete drop targets     |
| 3             | 1.25M             | Complete drop targets     |
| 4             | 1.5M              | Complete drop targets     |
| 5             | 1.5M + Extra Ball | Complete drop targets     |
| 6             | 2M                | Complete drop targets     |
| 7             | **2.5M**          | Maximum <kcite ref="53"/> |

**Collecting the Jackpot:**
1. **Enable:** Shoot center ramp first <kcite ref="25"/>
2. **Collect:** Shoot Fault (side ramp) <kcite ref="53"/>
3. **Note:** Jackpot cannot be relit after collection! <kcite ref="25"/>

> **💡 Pro Tip:** If Jackpot is not enabled or already collected, Fault trips award 250K during Multiball <kcite ref="52"/>.

---

### Quick Multiball (2-Ball)

**How to Start:**
- Hit captive ball when lit for 250K <kcite ref="59"/>
- OR lock ball in eject hole (Zone 5) when enabled <kcite ref="58"/>
- OR receive "Quick Multiball Lit" from Match-Up award <kcite ref="29"/>

**Building Captive Ball Value:**

| Value    | How to Light                                            |
| -------- | ------------------------------------------------------- |
| 25K      | Default                                                 |
| 50K      | 1 center ramp shot                                      |
| 100K     | 2 center ramp shots                                     |
| 150K     | 3 center ramp shots                                     |
| **250K** | 4 center ramp shots (lights Quick MB) <kcite ref="59"/> |

> **💡 Pro Tip:** Entering the Shelter raises the minimum captive ball value to 50K or 100K, meaning you need fewer center ramp shots to reach 250K <kcite ref="59"/>.

---

### Aftershock (2-Ball Bonus Multiball)

**How to Trigger:**
- Enter the Shelter within **15 seconds** after regular Multiball ends <kcite ref="56"/>

**Scoring:**
- Jackpot can be enabled and collected same as regular Multiball
- This is essentially a free bonus multiball—always go for it!

## 📈 Progression Systems

### Miles Accumulation

Miles are cumulative throughout the game and affect:
- **Bonus:** 1K × miles × multiplier at end of ball <kcite ref="63"/>
- **Center Ramp Value:** 200K per shot after 99 miles <kcite ref="57"/>
- **Extra Ball:** Lit at 12 miles and 60 miles (factory settings) <kcite ref="54"/>

**Miles Sources:**

| Source      | Miles Awarded                  |
| ----------- | ------------------------------ |
| Center ramp | 1-2 miles                      |
| Fault trip  | 3 miles <kcite ref="52"/>      |
| Match-Up    | 2 or 5 miles <kcite ref="29"/> |

---

### Bonus Multiplier

Advances after completing each building <kcite ref="55"/>:

| Round         | Multipliers Available             |
| ------------- | --------------------------------- |
| 1st Multiball | 2X, 3X (white)                    |
| 2nd Multiball | 4X, 5X (yellow)                   |
| 3rd Multiball | 6X + Light EB, 6X + Special (red) |

**Bonus Formula:** `(1K × total miles × multiplier) + (20K × fault trips)` <kcite ref="63"/>

---

### Drop Targets (Billboard)

| Completion # | Award      | Effect                              |
| ------------ | ---------- | ----------------------------------- |
| 1            | 50K        | Increases Jackpot                   |
| 2            | 60K        | Increases Jackpot                   |
| 3            | 70K        | Increases Jackpot                   |
| 4            | 80K        | Increases Jackpot                   |
| 5            | 90K        | Increases Jackpot                   |
| 6+           | 100K (max) | Increases Jackpot <kcite ref="60"/> |

## 🔄 Stacking & Combos

### ✅ What Stacks Well

| Combination                     | Why It Works                                 |
| ------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| High miles + Multiball          | Bonus multiplier maximizes end-of-ball bonus |
| Max Jackpot + Multiball         | 2.5M Jackpot is huge                         |
| Aftershock after Multiball      | Free 2-ball multiball with Jackpot potential |
| Center ramp spam + Captive ball | Builds captive value while scoring           |

### ❌ What NOT to Stack

| Avoid                        | Reason                                       |
| ---------------------------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| Quick MB before Regular MB   | Wastes multiball opportunity; save for after |
| Collecting Jackpot too early | Build Jackpot value with drop targets first  |
| Ignoring Aftershock window   | 15-second window is short—prioritize Shelter |

### ⏱️ Optimal Timing

1. **Early Game:** Complete zones, build Jackpot value via drop targets
2. **Pre-Multiball:** Max out Jackpot (2.5M) before locking balls
3. **During Multiball:** Center ramp → Fault immediately for Jackpot
4. **Post-Multiball:** Rush to Shelter for Aftershock within 15 seconds

## 🏆 Wizard Modes

> **Note:** Earthshaker is a System 11 game from 1989 and does not have formal wizard modes like modern machines. The closest equivalent is achieving multiple Multiballs with maximum Jackpots.

**Progression Goals:**
- Complete 5 buildings → Lights Extra Ball <kcite ref="54"/>
- Reach 6X multiplier → Lights Extra Ball AND Special
- Accumulate 99+ miles → Center ramp worth 200K

## 🎯 Shot Analysis

### ⚠️ Dangerous Shots

| Shot                         | Risk                     | Mitigation                          |
| ---------------------------- | ------------------------ | ----------------------------------- |
| Stand-up targets (Zones 1-4) | Unpredictable rebounds   | Shoot from controlled position      |
| Captive ball                 | Can bounce to outlane    | Soft shot, be ready                 |
| Loop from lower flipper      | Weak shot = center drain | Use upper flipper when possible     |
| Any shot during shaker motor | Disrupts aim             | Anticipate timing <kcite ref="76"/> |

### ✅ Safe Shots

| Shot                    | Why Safe                    | Best Flipper                    |
| ----------------------- | --------------------------- | ------------------------------- |
| **Center ramp**         | Returns to left flipper     | Left flipper                    |
| **Fault (side ramp)**   | Controlled upper playfield  | Left or upper left              |
| **Eject hole (Zone 5)** | Slow eject to upper flipper | Right flipper <kcite ref="58"/> |
| **Shelter scoop**       | Controlled kickout          | Right flipper                   |

### 🎮 Flipper Selection

| Target           | Recommended Flipper    |
| ---------------- | ---------------------- |
| Center ramp      | Left flipper (primary) |
| Fault/Side ramp  | Upper left flipper     |
| Captive ball     | Right flipper          |
| Loop             | Upper left flipper     |
| Eject hole       | Right flipper          |
| Stand-up targets | Varies—use caution     |

> **💡 Pro Tip:** The eject hole (Zone 5) shot from the right flipper is excellent—it ejects slowly past the upper left flipper for a controlled Fault attempt <kcite ref="32"/>.

### Path to 50+ Million

1. **Skill shots:** 100K × 3-5 balls = 300K-500K
2. **Center ramp spam:** 100+ shots × 100K avg = 10M+
3. **Jackpots:** 3-4 Multiballs × 2M avg = 6-8M
4. **Fault trips:** 50+ × 50K = 2.5M
5. **Bonus:** High miles × 6X multiplier = 2-5M
6. **Miscellaneous:** Zones, bumpers, targets = 5-10M

**Total potential:** 30-50M+ per game with good play

## 🏁 Two Main Strategies

### Strategy 1: Multiball Hunter 🎱

**Focus:** Complete zones → Lock balls → Collect Jackpots

**Pros:**
- Higher single-shot value (2.5M Jackpot)
- More exciting gameplay
- Works on tournament ROMs

**Cons:**
- Zone shots can be dangerous <kcite ref="76"/>
- Harder to qualify subsequent Multiballs
- Requires precise shooting

---

### Strategy 2: Center Ramp Spam 🔄

**Focus:** Control ball on left flipper → Spam center ramp

**Pros:**
- Very safe shot
- Consistent scoring (100K-200K per shot)
- Builds miles for bonus

**Cons:**
- Can feel repetitive <kcite ref="30"/>
- Nerfed on Soren ROM
- Lower ceiling than Jackpot hunting

---

### Hybrid Approach (Recommended) 🎯

1. **Spam center ramp** while zones are being spotted naturally
2. **Go for Multiball** when zones are nearly complete
3. **Collect Jackpot** immediately in Multiball
4. **Return to ramp spam** after Multiball/Aftershock
5. **Repeat** with increasing difficulty
