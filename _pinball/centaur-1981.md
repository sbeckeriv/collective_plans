# 🐎 Centaur - Strategy Guide

*Bally, 1981 — Designed by Jim Patla*

> **"Spell O-R-B-S. Lock without limit."**

Centaur is famous for arguably the most fun multiball mechanic of its era — there's effectively no cap on how many balls you can virtually lock and release, making this one of the few early-80s machines where multiball scoring scales without a hard ceiling. Our goal: chain ORBS completions into a massive multiball and max your bonus at 395K.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Hit O-R-B-S drop targets in sequence** — this is the main strategy on this machine, since it both starts a 2-ball multiball immediately and adds a virtual lock
- [ ] **Out-of-order ORBS hits still grant a lock**, but you'll need to restart the sequence — so try to keep your shot order clean
- [ ] **You can virtually lock up to 4 balls**, shown via amber lights above the flippers — keep feeding ORBS for bigger and bigger multiball
- [ ] **Use the safer alternative multiball start**: light all four return/outlane lanes via the flipper lane-change buttons for a 2-ball multiball without risking the ORBS shots
- [ ] **Shoot through the two left arc star rollovers before hitting the captive ball** to double its point value
- [ ] **Watch side-to-side ball action near the center gap** — this is the machine's primary drain risk

## 🐴 Core Mechanics

### ORBS Drop Targets & Multiball (The Main Strategy)

1. Hit the **O-R-B-S** targets **in sequence** to start a **2-ball multiball immediately** and add **1 ball to your virtual lock count**
2. Hitting them **out of order still grants a lock**, but the sequence resets and you'll need to start over for the next one
3. **Up to 4 balls** can be virtually locked, displayed via amber lights above the flippers
4. Repeat ORBS completions during multiball to keep adding more balls — there's effectively **no limit** to how many balls you can stack into play

> 🐎 **Pro Tip:** This is widely considered one of the most fun multiball systems in classic pinball specifically because of its lack of a hard cap — hitting ORBS repeatedly during an active multiball is the single best thing you can do on this machine.

### 1-2-3-4 Drop Targets

Completing this second bank provides three benefits:
1. Lights the **far upper-right target** for bonus collection
2. Scores points in escalating sequence: **10K → 20K → 40K → 80K**
3. Activates the **release target**, unlocking all held balls for full multiball

**Completing them in order maximizes scoring; any order still lights the bonus collect shot.**

### Captive Ball Shots

Knocking down the targets behind the captive ball scores progressively: **10K → 20K → 30K → 40K → 50K**, each with escalating bonus advances.

> 💰 **Pro Tip:** Route your shot through the **two left arc star rollovers** before hitting the captive ball to **double** its point value — a small detour for a big payoff.

### Alternative (Safer) Multiball Start

Light all **four return and outlane lanes** via the flipper lane-change buttons to trigger a 2-ball multiball **without** needing to risk the ORBS drop target shots — a good fallback if you're not confident in your drop-target accuracy yet.

### Bonus System

- **Base bonus** reaches **79K**, held at checkpoints of **20K, 40K, and 60K** (excess beyond a checkpoint isn't banked until you reach the next one)
- **Top three lanes** advance your bonus multiplier up to **5X**, for a maximum overall bonus of **395K**
- **Both flipper buttons control the top lane lights**, moving them in opposite directions — useful for precise lane targeting

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **Side-to-side ball action near the center gap** | The machine's primary drain risk — more dangerous than up-down movement | Stay especially alert during pop bumper exits and drop target rebounds near center |
| **Forcing ORBS out of order** | Resets your sequence progress, costing time | Plan your shot order rather than taking whatever's available |
| **Tilt mechanism (if 2-tilt option enabled)** | First tilt is a free warning, but the second ends the game | Nudge conservatively once you've already triggered one warning |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Ball

1. Start working the ORBS sequence immediately — this is your priority above everything else
2. Use the lane-change alternative multiball start as a backup if ORBS isn't coming together
3. Build toward your first bonus checkpoint (20K)

### Mid Ball

1. Once in multiball, keep feeding ORBS to stack additional balls without limit
2. Work the 1-2-3-4 bank in sequence for escalating points and the release target
3. Route captive ball shots through the left arc rollovers for double value

### Late Ball

1. Maximize your bonus multiplier via the top three lanes before the ball ends
2. Keep chasing ORBS completions for as long as you can sustain multiball
3. Bank your bonus at the highest checkpoint you can reach (60K × up to 5X)

## 📋 Quick Reference: Strategic Priorities

**Early:** Start the ORBS sequence, use lane-change as backup, build toward first bonus checkpoint.
**Mid:** Stack balls into multiball via repeated ORBS, work the 1-2-3-4 bank, double captive ball shots.
**Late:** Maximize bonus multiplier, keep chasing ORBS for sustained multiball, bank your score.

**Target Outcome:** A multiball with 3-4 balls stacked via repeated ORBS completions, combined with a maxed 5X bonus multiplier, represents a strong, well-played game on this beloved machine.
