### Skill Shots

### Monster Battles (Modes)

**How to Start a Mode:**
1. Shoot any of the three center shots (Flail, Center Target, Shield) to qualify the ramp
2. Shoot the ramp to raise the Black Knight's shield
3. Shoot behind the shield to start the battle

| Mode                | Color  | Objective                                                              | Completion       | KNIGHT Letter  |
| ------------------- | ------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------- | -------------- |
| **🟢 Mud Bog**       | Green  | Defeat the Hydra - 5 shots lit, shoot to lock, shoot again to cut head | Cut 4 heads      | After 2nd head |
| **🟠 Molten Fire**   | Orange | Harm the Magma Beast - all 7 shots lit, complete 8 total               | 8 shots          | After 3 shots  |
| **🟡 Burning Sands** | Yellow | Hit the Sandworm - 3 roving shots move left to right                   | 6 shots (2 sets) | After 3 shots  |
| **🔴 Wicked Cavern** | Red    | Defeat Hell Hand - alternate left/right sides                          | 8 shots          | After 3 shots  |
| **🔵 Deep Freeze**   | Blue   | Freeze then defeat Lich Lords - shoot blue to freeze, red to kill      | 4 Lich kills     | After 2nd Lich |

**Mode Timer:** Starts at 65 seconds. Add time by hitting the left standup targets (~3 sec per target, 10 sec per completion).

**Mode Value Increases:**
- +200k base value per completed mode
- +950k once a full lap of modes is completed
- Hit the spinning flail when mode is lit for small boost

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Modes carry over from ball to ball! If you drain with a mode running, time is automatically added if under a certain threshold.

### ⚔️ Sword of Rage (POWER)

The sword meter on the right side of the display fills via:
- Right drop target hits (small bump ~1%)
- Drop target bank completions (~20%)
- Center target shots (~20%)

**When POWER is collected during a battle:**
- 2x Scoring for the entire battle
- Mode becomes easier to complete

| Mode          | POWER Effect                                       |
| ------------- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| Mud Bog       | Every shot both seals AND cuts a head              |
| Molten Fire   | Incomplete shots become permanent - loop one shot! |
| Burning Sands | Auto-lights the three center shots for damage      |
| Wicked Cavern | Auto-lights all four shots                         |
| Deep Freeze   | Enables one-shot Lich kills                        |

**Meter Starting Values:**
- 1st activation: 70% full
- 2nd activation: 40% full
- 3rd+ activation: 0% (must fill completely)

> ⚠️ **Warning:** Don't burn POWER on a mode that's about to end - it expires when the mode ends!

## 🎱 Multiballs

### Triple Knights Challenge

**How to Start:**
1. Shoot the "light lock" target to light lock at the shield saucer
2. Lock a ball (re-lights the target)
3. Lock 3 balls total (subsequent MBs require multiple target hits per lock)

**Phase 1:**
- Left spinner lane, ramp, and shield saucer lit for jackpots
- Complete all 3 to light ramp for hurry-up

**Phase 2:**
- Hurry-up at ramp (starts at 2M, +1M per playthrough)
- Flail spins rapidly - make a strong ramp shot through it
- Hurry-up value sets jackpot value for next phase

**Phase 3:**
- All shots lit for jackpots (based on hurry-up value)
- Complete all shots to light ramp for **Super Jackpot** (3x jackpot value + KNIGHT letter)

**Add-A-Ball:** Collect enough spins at left spinner, then shoot left spinner lane again. On Premium/LE, the drop target serves this function.

---

### 🏰 Catapult Multiball (Premium/LE Exclusive)

**How to Start:**
1. Hit the standup next to the catapult to light locks (first MB has all 3 lit)
2. Lock 3 balls in the upper playfield catapult

**Scoring:**
- All major shots: 250k (+25k per jackpot)
- Catapult Super Jackpot: 300k base + total jackpots since last super
- Standup lights +1x multiplier for next super (up to 4x)

**Add-A-Ball:** Hit left drop target to light, collect there as well.

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** On Premium/LE, you can progress toward BOTH Triple Knights and Catapult Multiball simultaneously!

---

### 👑 KNIGHT Multiballs

**Collecting KNIGHT Letters:**
- Doing well during Monster Battles (1 letter per mode)
- Completing WAR Hurry-Ups
- Collecting Super Jackpot during Triple Knights Challenge

**Default:** K and N are spotted at game start (need 4 letters first time, 6 letters after)

**KNIGHT Multiball (1st completion):**
- 3-ball multiball with playfield multiplier (2x with 2 balls, 3x with 3 balls!)
- Original Black Knight style with BK2000 music
- No progression toward other features except Super Modes
- Super Mode values lock in when MB starts

**The King's Ransom (2nd completion):**
- 60-second 3-ball multiball with UNLIMITED ball saver!
- +5x bonus multiplier added
- Extra ball lit at shield saucer
- Jackpots at: Flail, both orbits, WAR lane completions
- After timer expires, new ball sent to plunger

## 🔗 Stacking & Combos

### ✅ What Stacks Well

| Combination                  | Why It's Good                                      |
| ---------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| **Mode + Triple Knights MB** | Mode shots score during MB, extra balls for safety |
| **Mode + Catapult MB**       | Same benefits, Premium/LE exclusive                |
| **Super Modes + KNIGHT MB**  | 3x playfield multiplier on Super Mode values!      |
| **Black Castle + Multiball** | Extra balls, pop bumpers pause timer               |
| **Multiple Super Modes**     | Values stack, one big cashout                      |

### ❌ What NOT to Stack

| Combination                    | Why It's Bad                                         |
| ------------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------- |
| **KNIGHT MB + Any Timed Mode** | Starting KNIGHT MB ends all timed modes immediately! |
| **Mode near timeout + POWER**  | POWER expires when mode ends - wasted                |

### 🎯 Optimal Timing

1. **Before KNIGHT MB:** Max out Super Mode levels by shooting left spinner
2. **During KNIGHT MB:** Focus on Super Spinner - it's the biggest scorer
3. **For Black Castle:** Have a multiball ready to stack for safety

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** The left spinner lane VUK cashes out ALL active Super Modes. Build them up, then collect during 3x KNIGHT Multiball for massive scores!

## 🏆 Wizard Modes

### ⚫ Black Castle (Mini-Wizard Mode)

**Requirements:** Complete all 5 Monster Battles

**How to Start:** Same as regular modes - qualify ramp, shoot ramp, shoot behind shield

**Gameplay (200 seconds):**
1. Three center shots lit (Flail, Center Target, Shield)
2. Hit all three to light four outer loop shots
3. Make an outer loop shot
4. Center shots relight (flail faster, shield more violent)
5. Repeat until all 4 outer loops completed
6. Shoot shield saucer to finish off the Black Knight

**Ball Saves:** At mode start + after each outer loop shot

**Completion Bonus:** Based on remaining time

---

### 🔥 Rage Multiball (Main Wizard Mode)

**Requirements:** Complete Black Castle

**Gameplay:**
- 6-ball multiball frenzy!
- Center target always lit for frenzy bonus
- Starts at 1M per hit, increases linearly
- Increment amount tied to remaining Black Castle time

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Stack a multiball with Black Castle for extra balls and safety. Pop bumpers pause the Black Castle timer!

## 🎯 Shot Analysis

### ⚠️ Dangerous Shots

| Shot                     | Danger Level | Common Drain Path           |
| ------------------------ | ------------ | --------------------------- |
| **Knight/Center Target** | 🔴 EXTREME    | SDTM or deflect to outlane  |
| **Shield Saucer**        | 🔴 HIGH       | Violent kickback to outlane |
| **Flail**                | 🟠 HIGH       | Unpredictable deflections   |
| **Light Lock Target**    | 🟠 MEDIUM     | Right side drains           |

### ✅ Safe Shots

| Shot                  | Safety Level | Notes                                      |
| --------------------- | ------------ | ------------------------------------------ |
| **Left Orbit**        | 🟢 SAFE       | Good for U-Turns, always backhand          |
| **Right Orbit**       | 🟢 SAFE       | Only shot harder to backhand than forehand |
| **Ramp**              | 🟡 MEDIUM     | Safe when flail is open                    |
| **Left Spinner Lane** | 🟢 SAFE       | Feeds to VUK, great for Super Modes        |

### 🎮 Flipper Selection

| Shot                 | Best Flipper     | Technique                          |
| -------------------- | ---------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| Left Orbit           | Left (backhand)  | Always backhand                    |
| Shield Saucer        | Right (backhand) | Critical - forehand is dangerous   |
| Light Lock Target    | Right (backhand) | Safer from right side              |
| Knight/Center Target | Left             | Safest option, still dangerous     |
| Right Orbit          | Either           | Only shot where forehand is easier |
| Ramp                 | Either           | Can backhand from rolling ball     |

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** The name of the game is **BACKHAND, BACKHAND, BACKHAND**. All shots except the right orbit are safer with a backhand due to the nasty black post rubbers.

### 🛡️ Ball Control Positions

- **Right Flipper Cradle:** Light lock, lock shot, mode shots (backhand)
- **Left Flipper Cradle:** Light mode start, mode shots
- **Post Pass:** Standard Spike 2 placement - use to transfer sides

### 🆘 Slap Save Technique

When the ball is screaming SDTM:
1. Give a short, sharp slap to the side of the cabinet
2. Simultaneously raise the flipper on the same side
3. Time it as the ball passes the Knight's symbol in the center
4. Goal: Tick the ball off the flipper tip

> ⚠️ Large cabinet movements trigger tilt warnings. Short, sharp blows are safer.

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Path to 1 Billion Points

#### Early Game (0-100M)
1. ✅ Collect skill shots consistently
2. ✅ Light and complete modes - don't just start them
3. ✅ Work toward first KNIGHT completion
4. ✅ Keep Magna-Save lit (complete drop targets)
5. ✅ Stack modes with Triple Knights Challenge

#### Mid Game (100M-500M)
1. ✅ Complete all 5 modes for Black Castle access
2. ✅ Build Super Mode levels by shooting left spinner
3. ✅ Start KNIGHT Multiball with Super Modes ready
4. ✅ During KNIGHT MB: Focus on Super Spinner with 3x scoring
5. ✅ Cash out Super Modes via left spinner VUK

#### Late Game (500M-1B)
1. ✅ Second KNIGHT completion for King's Ransom
2. ✅ Black Castle + stacked multiball
3. ✅ Rage Multiball for frenzy scoring
4. ✅ Repeat KNIGHT MB with maxed Super Modes

### 🏆 Highest Value Opportunities

| Opportunity                           | Potential Value    | Difficulty |
| ------------------------------------- | ------------------ | ---------- |
| **Super Spinner during 3x KNIGHT MB** | 100-300M+          | High       |
| **King's Ransom**                     | 50-100M+           | Medium     |
| **Rage Multiball**                    | 50-100M+           | High       |
| **Black Castle Completion**           | 20-50M             | High       |
| **Stacked Mode + Multiball**          | 20-50M             | Medium     |
| **WAR Hurry-Up (maxed)**              | Up to 75M (3x 25M) | Medium     |

> 💡 **The Secret to 1 Billion:** Super Spinner during KNIGHT Multiball with 3x playfield scoring. At Level 4, you're scoring ~240k per spin × 3 = 720k per spin. A good spinner rip can net 100-300M in a single collect!

## ✅ Quick-Start Checklist

### 🆕 New Player Checklist

- [ ] **Backhand everything** - Safer than forehand shots
- [ ] **Slow down** - Speed kills on this table
- [ ] **Keep Magna-Save lit** - Complete drop targets after each use
- [ ] **Light ball save** - Complete left "add time" targets
- [ ] **Start modes** - Hit center shots → ramp → shield saucer
- [ ] **Lock balls** - Hit light lock target → shield saucer
- [ ] **Collect KNIGHT letters** - Complete modes and WAR hurry-ups

### 🎯 Every Ball Priorities

1. 🛡️ Ensure Magna-Save is lit
2. 🎯 Work toward mode start or lock
3. ⚔️ Complete active mode if running
4. 🔤 Progress KNIGHT letters
5. 💫 Build Super Mode levels when possible

### 🏆 Scoring Priority Order

1. **KNIGHT MB + Super Spinner** (3x scoring = massive points)
2. **King's Ransom** (unlimited ball save + 5x bonus)
3. **Black Castle → Rage Multiball** (wizard mode payoff)
4. **Stacked Mode + Multiball** (safe, consistent scoring)
