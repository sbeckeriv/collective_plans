# 🐒 CONGO Pinball Machine - Complete Strategy Guide
## *Diamond Hunting Through the Jungle*

> **"Come On Everybody, Play That Congo!"**

A mid-90s Williams gem built around diamond collection and multiball depth, Congo rewards consistent play and strategic lock building over pure high-value shot chasing <kcite ref="1"/>.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Ball 1:** Learn the left ramp (M-A-P) and center saucer (Z-I-N-J)
- [ ] **Standard Skill Shot:** Hit the lit kickback target on inlanes
- [ ] **Super Skill Shot:** Soft plunge left outlane for 25M
- [ ] **Spell A-M-Y** on lanes → increases bonus multiplier
- [ ] **Spell Z-I-N-J** on targets → light Mystery saucer
- [ ] **Collect 4 diamonds** → lock ball for multiball
- [ ] **Lock 3 balls total** → start standard 3-ball multiball
- [ ] **Reach 100 diamonds** → Super Multiball on lower playfield

## 🎪 Core Modes

### 🎵 A-M-Y Mode (Multiplier Builder)

**Start:** Complete A-M-Y letters via lane rollovers

**Mechanics:**
- Timed countdown starting at **25 million**
- Scoring via upper orbit grants **double value**
- Primary purpose: **increase bonus multiplier**
- Timer decreases rapidly

**Strategy:** Prioritize A-M-Y early in the ball for multiplier; don't start if you need to focus on locks <kcite ref="1"/>

---

### 💪 GRAY Attack (Lower Playfield Gorilla Mode)

**Start:** Spell G-R-A-Y via targets or mode selection

**Mechanics:**
- Lower playfield activated
- Control gorilla batting at C-O-N-G-O targets
- **5 targets to complete**
- Difficulty escalates with each completion

**Rewards (per completion level):**
- 1st completion: diamonds + points
- 2nd completion: multiball access
- 3rd completion: extra ball consideration
- 4th completion: **Extra Ball awarded**

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Gray Attack rewards accumulate—completing it multiple times is a legitimate EB farm strategy. <kcite ref="1"/>

---

### 🔥 Skill Fire (Most Lucrative Mode)

**Start:** Via M-A-P award saucer or mystery

**Mechanics:**
- **Flippers disabled for 20 seconds**
- Unlimited ball saves within time limit
- Skill shot values escalate dramatically
- Regular shot: starts 10M, increases each attempt
- Super shot: starts 20M, increases each attempt

**Escalation:** Each successful skill shot increases both regular and super values—perfect execution can exceed **one billion points in this mode alone** <kcite ref="1"/>

> ⚠️ **Warning:** High risk/high reward; one missed shot and ball drains with no recovery.

---

### 🎬 Mine Shaft Video Mode

**Start:** Upper orbit selection or mystery

**Mechanics:**
- Navigates randomized path avoiding obstacles
- **5 diamonds, snake bite, bear mauling, or N × 10M** outcomes
- Path remains constant in multiplayer until completed

**Strategy:** Mid-game distraction mode; useful if needing quick diamonds but not essential <kcite ref="1"/>

## 🎰 Multiball Mechanics

### Standard 3-Ball Multiball

**Qualification:** Lock 3 balls (at 4, 8, 12 diamond thresholds)

**Mechanics:**
- Left and right ramps lit for jackpots throughout multiball
- **Jackpot base value: 16.5M to 28M+** (scales with diamond count)
- Jackpots remain lit for entire multiball
- No super jackpot (unlike some multiballs)

| Diamonds at Start | Approx. Jackpot Base |
| ----------------- | -------------------- |
| 0-5               | 16.5M                |
| 6-10              | 18M                  |
| 11-20             | 20M+                 |
| 30+               | 28M+                 |

<kcite ref="1"/>

### Diamond Hunt Multiball (2-Ball)

**Qualification:** Spell Z-I-N-J, then shoot mystery saucer for this award

**Mechanics:**
- **Every shot is lit for 2 diamonds each**
- Continues until 1 ball remains
- High-value diamond farming

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** In tournament mode, you can strategically start the game, light Mystery, and begin Diamond Hunt early for aggressive diamond building. <kcite ref="1"/>

---

### Ghost Tribe Multiball

**Qualification:** Successful Jungle Jackpot completion

**Mechanics:**
- Countdown from 20M visible
- Re-shooting MAP saucer collects Jungle Jackpot
- Either MAP or Mystery saucer scores the jackpot
- **Super Jackpot: 40M** for hitting both saucers simultaneously

<kcite ref="1"/>

---

### Super Multiball (4-Ball, Lower Playfield)

**Qualification:** Collect 100 diamonds

**Mechanics:**
- All 4 balls ejected to lower playfield
- Jackpots at ramps and lower saucers: **10M each**
- **Super Jackpot: 50M** for defeating gorilla on lower playfield
- **CONGO letters only stay lit ~5 seconds** after hitting (tight timing)
- Repeatable: every additional 100 diamonds awards another Super Multiball

**Strategy:** Super Multiball is the game's highest-value event; prioritize building to 100 diamonds on later balls <kcite ref="1"/>

## 📍 M-A-P Combo System

Shooting the left ramp progressively collects M-A-P letters.

| Letter | Award                         | Notes                        |
| ------ | ----------------------------- | ---------------------------- |
| **M**  | 1,001,000 points              | First left ramp hit          |
| **A**  | 1,001,000 points              | Second left ramp hit         |
| **P**  | Ball returns to left flipper   | Third left ramp hit          |

### M-A-P Completion Routes

After completing M-A-P, ball enters saucer with choice of three routes:

| Route               | Award                               | Notes                           |
| ------------------- | ----------------------------------- | ------------------------------- |
| **Route 1**         | 25M points                          | Standard cash-out               |
| **Route 2**         | Diamond Hunt Multiball start        | 2-diamond-per-shot mode         |
| **Route 3**         | 50 diamonds (?mystery award)        | "?" at volcano on map           |

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Three consecutive left ramp shots (M-A-P completion) award **additional 20 million points** on top of base awards. <kcite ref="1"/>

## 🏆 Additional Info

### Video Mode Accessibility

Mine Shaft and other video modes are accessed through:
- Upper orbit selection screens
- Mystery saucer awards
- Mode completion paths

### Lower Playfield (Gray Attack & Super Multiball)

The lower, backward-sloping playfield is used exclusively during Gray Attack modes and Super Multiball (100-diamond qualification). Mastering lower playfield ball control is essential for high scoring.

### Known Software Quirks (Version 2.1)

- Extra ball awarded during Amy Mode may prevent Amy Mode completion reward in same ball
- One M-A-P route displays "Multiball" label but awards Jungle Jackpot instead
- Ball save can occasionally remain active longer than programmed

### Game Accessibility

Congo has "no point at which absolutely nothing is ready to go for some big scoring action"—continuous lit shots and mode availability ensure consistent engagement <kcite ref="3"/>
