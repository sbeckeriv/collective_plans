# 🤘 Metallica - Strategy Guide

*Stern Pinball, 2013 — Designed by John Borg*

> **"Crank it up. Four times. Then ride to the End of the Line."**

Metallica centers on collecting Electric Chairs, Snakes, Grave Markers, and Coffins (12 of each) to repeatedly trigger the Crank It Up mini-wizard mode, with a 5th completion unlocking the final wizard mode, **End of the Line**. Stacking all four multiballs together with completed Pick targets is the core scoring engine. Our goal: stack multiballs, complete Pick targets for double scoring, and run Crank It Up four times before chasing End of the Line.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Collect 12 each of Electric Chairs, Snakes, Grave Markers, and Coffins** to light Crank It Up at the scoop
- [ ] **Use the Spider mechanism** to light additional items at previously-hit shots — this significantly accelerates collection
- [ ] **Hit all four Pick standup targets** (around the left ramp) to light the Snake for 20 seconds of double scoring plus an Add-A-Ball — progress **carries over ball to ball** if not fully collected
- [ ] **Stack multiballs together** — all four can run simultaneously, and Pick targets can still be completed during multiball play
- [ ] **Watch the left inlane/outlane area** — it's a genuine high-drain-risk zone on this layout
- [ ] **Avoid the FUEL targets on the far left** unless you're confident — they're described as a "suicidal shot"

## 🤘 Core Mechanics

### Four Primary Multiballs

| Multiball | Format | How to Start |
|---|---|---|
| **Electric Chair** | 3-ball | Repeatedly hit the center feature (Sparky) |
| **Grave Marker** | 2-ball | Clear inline drop targets, then hit the grave marker shot |
| **Snake** | 2-ball | Repeated shots to the snake captive ball area |
| **Coffin** | 3-ball | 25+ hits to the coffin captive ball (rarely reached in a single ball) |

### Pick Targets & Double Scoring

- Complete all **4 Pick standup targets** (around the left ramp) to light the **Snake for 20 seconds of double scoring** plus an **Add-A-Ball**
- **Progress carries over ball to ball** if you don't complete all four in one ball
- **Pick targets remain completable during any active multiball**, making them a key stacking tool

> 🎯 **Pro Tip — Core Strategy:** Since all four multiballs can stack together and Pick targets work during any of them, the highest-scoring approach is starting one multiball, then working toward triggering a second (or third) while it's still running, while picking off Pick targets along the way for the double-scoring bonus.

### Crank It Up Songs (4 Modes)

| Song | Mechanic |
|---|---|
| **For Whom the Bell Tolls** | Lights the 5 major shots for jackpots; ramp-focused |
| **Fade to Black** | Switch-hitting mode; GI lights gradually dim as the song progresses |
| **Battery** | Centers on repeatedly shooting Sparky (the Electric Chair) |
| **Enter Sandman** | Randomly flashing inserts among the 5 major shots |

### Crank It Up (Mini-Wizard Mode)

- **Requirement:** Collect 12 each of Electric Chairs, Snakes, Grave Markers, Coffins
- Can be played **up to 4 times**

---

## 🏆 End of the Line (Final Wizard Mode)

**Requirement:** Complete Crank It Up a **5th** time.

- A **2-ball multiball** with **escalating Super Jackpots**
- Value formula: **12x your 1st Crank It Up + 10x your 2nd + 8x your 3rd + 6x your 4th**

> 💰 **Pro Tip:** Since End of the Line's value is built directly from your *prior* Crank It Up performances, playing each Crank It Up run as strongly as possible compounds directly into your eventual End of the Line payout.

---

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **Left inlane/outlane area** | Genuine high-drain-risk zone; balls bounce off the rail between inlanes into the top of the left sling and into the outlane | Play extra carefully around this specific area |
| **FUEL targets (far left)** | Explicitly called a "suicidal shot" | Avoid unless you have a specific, confident reason to attempt it |
| **Center funnel toward Sparky** | Can produce unpredictable ball behavior | Approach with controlled shots |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Game (Ball 1)

1. Start collecting toward your first multiball (Electric Chair or Snake are typically easiest)
2. Begin working Pick targets for the double-scoring bonus
3. Avoid the FUEL targets and play carefully around the inlane/outlane zone

**Target Score:** 10M–30M

### Mid Game (Ball 2)

1. Stack multiple multiballs together as you qualify them
2. Complete Pick targets during multiball for compounded scoring
3. Run a Crank It Up song if you've hit 12 of any item type

**Target Score:** 30M–100M (multiball stacking can exceed 100M)

### Late Game (Ball 3+)

1. Push toward completing Crank It Up multiple times
2. Keep stacking Pick target double-scoring into every multiball
3. If you've run Crank It Up 4 times, push for your 5th and End of the Line

**Target Score per ball:** 50M–200M+

## ⚙️ Setup Recommendations

| Setting | Recommendation | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| **Playfield pitch** | 6.5°–7.0° | Stern factory standard |
| **Coffin captive ball mechanism** | Verify smooth, reliable registration | The 25+ hit requirement makes consistent registration essential |
| **Left sling/inlane geometry** | Check standard alignment | This area is a known high-risk zone even when properly calibrated |

---

## 📋 Quick Reference: Strategic Priorities

**Ball 1:** Start an easy multiball, begin Pick target collection, avoid FUEL targets.
**Ball 2:** Stack multiballs together, complete Pick targets during multiball, run a Crank It Up song.
**Ball 3+:** Push toward multiple Crank It Up completions, chase End of the Line at your 5th.

**Target High-Score Ballpark:** **100–250+ million points** with strong multiball stacking; reaching End of the Line caps off a truly excellent game.
