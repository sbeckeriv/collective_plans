# 🐢 Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles Pro - Complete Strategy Guide

**Goal: 1 Billion Points (or Grand Champion)**

This guide covers everything you need to dominate TMNT Pro and reach that coveted billion-point score. The game features 8 episode modes, 2 main multiballs, 3 wizard modes, and deep progression systems that reward skilled play.

---

## 📋 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] Choose **Michelangelo** for ball save + fast pizza contest access
- [ ] Make your skill shot (Pizza Parlor for Mike) to light Weapon
- [ ] Shoot left ramp + right ramp to light Start Episode
- [ ] Lock 3 balls at Pizza Parlor for Ninja Pizza Multiball
- [ ] During multiball: defeat 6 Foot Soldiers → hit upper loop for Mondo Jackpot
- [ ] Complete 4 episodes to unlock Team-Up Multiball
- [ ] Keep 2X Playfield running as much as possible

## 🎯 Core Mechanics

### Skill Shots

At the start of each ball, your chosen turtle determines which shot is the skill shot <kcite ref="32"/>:

| Turtle         | Skill Shot                 | Reward        |
| -------------- | -------------------------- | ------------- |
| 🔵 Leonardo     | Lair Shot (behind flipper) | Lights Weapon |
| 🔴 Raphael      | Left Ramp                  | Lights Weapon |
| 🟠 Michelangelo | Pizza Parlor               | Lights Weapon |
| 🟣 Donatello    | Side Ramp                  | Lights Weapon |

**Pro Only - Top Lane Skill Shot:** Plunge to the top left lane for extra points and bonus multipliers <kcite ref="31"/>.

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** The skill shot timer is generous and can be extended by hitting the "more time" target below the side ramp.

### Weapon Hurry-Up

Making the skill shot lights a return lane for Weapon <kcite ref="32"/>:
1. Roll through the lit return lane (cycle with flippers)
2. Shoot the flashing orbit (opposite side)
3. Shoot the side ramp for **2X the first value**

### Foot 1-2-3 Combo

Lit at the start of each ball when the ball enters the "Foot" lane <kcite ref="38"/>:
1. Hit the **right ramp** (backhand from right flipper is safer)
2. Hit either **upper loop → side ramp** OR **side ramp → left ramp**
3. Score increases with spinner hits

> ⚠️ **Note:** This combo can only be completed **once per game** - make it count!

### Krang Kombo

Build value by shooting the upper loop repeatedly <kcite ref="38"/>:
- Each upper loop shot increases the combo value
- **3 shots** maxes out at **500K**
- Collect at the side ramp before the timer expires
- Value resets if you miss the side ramp

## 🎬 Episode Modes

### Starting Episodes

Shoot the **left ramp** and **right ramp** (one each) to light Start Episode at the Pizza Parlor <kcite ref="33"/>. Change the lit episode by shooting the "X" targets near the left ramp.

### Episode Breakdown

| #   | Episode                              | Objective                                                       | Tips                                  |
| --- | ------------------------------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------- |
| 1   | 🍕 **Pizza-O-Meter**                  | Hit 3 yellow shots                                              | Easiest mode - great for beginners    |
| 2   | 🐗 **Window Shopping**                | Hit 3 shots to left OR right orbit                              | Focus on one orbit                    |
| 3   | 🧠 **I Want a Body**                  | Alternate ramps/orbits, 4 shots total                           | Unlocks "Wrath of Krang"              |
| 4   | 🐭 **Night of the Mousers**           | Hit 6 yellow shots before 10 time out                           | Fast-paced, stay focused              |
| 5   | ⚡ **See You on the Other Side Ray**  | Shoot ramp → hit LAIR target, repeat 3x                         | Requires precision                    |
| 6   | ⚔️ **Welcome to the Concrete Jungle** | Defeat Slash (yellow + left ramp), escape Dimension X (3 shots) | Complex, save for multiball           |
| 7   | 🪰 **Super Fly**                      | Right shot → left shot → upper flipper shot                     | *Unlocked after Night of the Mousers* |
| 8   | 👾 **The Wrath of Krang**             | Hit cycling red shots 3x                                        | *Unlocked after I Want a Body*        |

**Completion:** After objectives, shoot any colored turtle shot to complete the episode <kcite ref="33"/>.

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Episode scoring increases with each one completed. Completed episodes average 2-3M at base value.

## 🎱 Multiballs

### Ninja Pizza Multiball (NPMB) 🍕

**The primary scoring multiball - prioritize this!**

**How to Start:** Lock 3 balls at the Pizza Parlor <kcite ref="34"/>

**Scoring Structure:**
1. Defeat **6 Foot Soldiers** by shooting lit shots:
   - Left Ramp: 3 Foot
   - Krang Loop: 2 Foot
   - Center Ramp: 2 Foot
   - Pizza Parlor: 1 Foot
   - Right Ramp: 1 Foot

2. **Mondo Jackpot** lights at upper loop: **500K base (+250K per jackpot)** <kcite ref="44"/>
3. Loop repeatedly for **2X** then **3X Mondo Jackpots**
4. **Super Jackpot** lights at Lair: **3M + 500K per Mondo since last Super** <kcite ref="44"/>

**Pizza Toppings** affect multiball rules <kcite ref="47"/>:

| Topping         | Effect                            | Rating |
| --------------- | --------------------------------- | ------ |
| 🍦 Ice Cream     | Jackpot starts lit                | ⭐⭐⭐    |
| 🌶️ Chili Pepper  | Super Jackpot starts lit          | ⭐⭐⭐⭐   |
| 🍬 Marshmallows  | Unlimited Triple Mondo Jackpots   | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐  |
| 🍬 Gummy Bears   | +1 Ball                           | ⭐⭐⭐⭐   |
| 🥜 Peanut Butter | +1 Pizza Slices                   | ⭐⭐     |
| 🐙 Octopus       | Pizza Frenzy (all switches score) | ⭐⭐     |
| 🍖 Pepperoni     | +250K Jackpot Value               | ⭐⭐⭐    |
| 🍫 Fudge         | +1 Foot Kills per shot            | ⭐⭐⭐    |
| 🌭 Sausage       | +50K per Foot Kill                | ⭐⭐     |
| 🍍 Pineapple     | Parlor spots +1 Foot              | ⭐⭐     |
| 🐟 Anchovies     | +10K Slice Value                  | ⭐      |

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Marshmallows + Chili Pepper is the dream combo for massive scoring!

**Restart Requirement:** Play 4 episodes to relight NPMB <kcite ref="34"/>.

---

### Turtle Power Multiball (TPMB) 💪

**How to Start:** Shoot the right ramp **4 times** (Donatello starts with it lit!) <kcite ref="35"/>

**Scoring Structure:**
1. Shoot **green shots** for jackpots: **100K base (+25K each, max 250K)** <kcite ref="46"/>
2. Collect **4 jackpots** to light **Mondo Jackpot** at upper loop: **350K base (+50K)** <kcite ref="44"/>
3. Loop for **2X** and **3X Mondo**
4. **Super Jackpot** at Lair: **500K (+125K) × number of Supers (max 3X)** <kcite ref="44"/>

**Add-A-Ball:** Complete all 4 turtle shots → shoot right ramp <kcite ref="46"/>

> ⚠️ **Bug Note:** First TPMB currently enjoys 2X Mondo values (700K base) due to a bug <kcite ref="44"/>.

**Restart Requirement:** Play 4 episodes to relight TPMB <kcite ref="35"/>.

## 📈 Progression Systems

### Training & Turtle Levels

**Lighting Training:** Spell LAIR by hitting the left bar target 4 times quickly <kcite ref="36"/>

**Training Mode:** Started at the Lair shot when lit. Hit flashing white shots (right ramp is always first). Completing training increases your turtle's level, adding **+10% to ALL future scores** <kcite ref="36"/>.

### Turtle Perks by Level

| Level | Leonardo               | Raphael                 | Michelangelo              | Donatello           |
| ----- | ---------------------- | ----------------------- | ------------------------- | ------------------- |
| **1** | Training Lit           | Episodes Lit            | Battle Again Lit +5 Pizza | Turtle Power MB Lit |
| **2** | +10 Seconds All Timers | 2X Krang Kombo          | 2X April Hurry-Up         | 2X 1-2-3 Foot       |
| **3** | 2X Weapon Hurry-Up     | 3X Playfield Multiplier | 2X Pizza Eating Contest   | +10 Sec Ball Saves  |
| **4** | 2X Team-Up MB          | 3X Episode Scoring      | 3X Ninja Pizza MB         | 3X Turtle Power MB  |

<kcite ref="48"/>

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Raphael's Level 3 (3X Playfield) + Level 4 (3X Episodes) are **multiplicative** = 9X episode scoring! With training boost, that's **10.7X**! <kcite ref="48"/>

### Neutrino Pizza Party

Unlocked after completing first training level <kcite ref="36"/>:
1. Hit the two "X" targets to light left ramp
2. One blue shot lit at a time for big points
3. Red shots increase the blue shot value
4. Timer resets when you make the blue shot

> ⚠️ **Warning:** Single-ball mode - very risky but potentially massive points!

## 🔗 Stacking & Combos

### ✅ What Stacks Well

| Combination                        | Why It Works                                                    |
| ---------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Episode + Multiball**            | Complete episodes during multiball for safety <kcite ref="45"/> |
| **2X Playfield + Everything**      | Doubles ALL scoring - keep it running! <kcite ref="45"/>        |
| **NPMB + Episode**                 | Use ball save to complete difficult episodes                    |
| **April Hurry-Up + Stacked Modes** | Value increases with more modes running <kcite ref="37"/>       |

### ❌ What NOT to Stack

| Avoid                              | Reason                             |
| ---------------------------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| **Training during Multiball**      | Single-ball mode, wastes multiball |
| **Neutrino Pizza Party during MB** | Single-ball, high risk             |
| **1-2-3 Foot during TPMB**         | Right ramp advances TPMB instead   |

### Optimal Timing

1. **Start of Ball:** Make skill shot → Weapon Hurry-Up
2. **Early Game:** Lock balls for NPMB while lighting episodes
3. **During NPMB:** Complete an episode if possible
4. **After NPMB:** Play 4 episodes to relight multiballs
5. **Mid-Game:** Get 2X Playfield running, stack with modes
6. **Late Game:** Push for Team-Up or Final Battle with multipliers active

## 🏆 Wizard Modes

### Team-Up Multiball (Mini-Wizard)

**Requirement:** Play any **4 episodes** <kcite ref="41"/>

**Start:** Shoot the Left Ramp

**Your Partner** depends on your turtle <kcite ref="31"/>:
- Leonardo → Splinter
- Donatello → Metalhead
- Raphael → Casey Jones
- Michelangelo → The Neutrinos

**Phase 1 - Rescue Phase:**
- 6 shots lit for "Bad Guys" (orbits for Bebop/Rocksteady, ramps for Foot Soldiers)
- Defeat all 6 → Rescue Hurry-Up lights at turtle shot (500K start)
- Collect 3 Rescue Hurry-Ups → Shredder Hurry-Up at right ramp
- Collect Shredder → Final Rescue Hurry-Up <kcite ref="41"/>

**Phase 2 - Pizza Party (Victory Laps):**
- Yellow shots collect "next award" (starts 150K, +25K each, max 1M)
- 2X shots available after hitting yellow <kcite ref="41"/>

**Episode Perks in Team-Up:**

| Completed Episode          | Perk                                         |
| -------------------------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| Pizza-O-Meter              | 2X Pizza Party scoring                       |
| Window Shopping            | Either orbit completes both Bebop/Rocksteady |
| I Want a Body              | Rescue any turtle                            |
| Night of the Mousers       | Lit return lane spots Bad Guy                |
| See You on the Other Side  | 2X Rescue Hurry-Up value                     |
| Welcome to Concrete Jungle | +250K per Bad Guy                            |
| Super Fly                  | 2X Shredder Hurry-Up                         |
| Wrath of Krang             | One Foot shot defeats all Foot               |

<kcite ref="41"/>

> 💡 **HUGE SCORING:** Leonardo at Level 4 with 2X Playfield + Pizza-O-Meter perk = **10.4X scoring** during Pizza Party! Red/yellow shots can reach **20.8X**! <kcite ref="41"/>

---

### Final Battle (Major Wizard)

**Requirement:** Complete all **8 episodes** <kcite ref="42"/>

**Start:** Shoot the Pizza Parlor

**Starting Bonus:** Points awarded based on completed episodes <kcite ref="42"/>:
- Failed episodes: 250K each
- Completed episodes: 500K → 1.25M → 2M → 2.75M → 3.5M → 4.25M → 5M → 5.75M
- **All 8 completed = 25M starting bonus!**

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** With Raphael's 3X Playfield perk, this becomes **75M**! <kcite ref="49"/>

**Phase 1 (Multiball):** Shoot 3 of 4 lit shots (ramps + side-loop) → X targets → left ramp

**Phase 2 (Single Ball):** 8 shots lit (4 red Bebop, 4 blue Rocksteady) → collect both hurry-ups → left ramp

**Phase 3 (Multiball):** Defeat Slash (left ramp) → Krang (side-loop) → Shredder (right ramp) → left ramp to WIN! <kcite ref="42"/>

---

### Cowabunga Multiball (Ultimate Wizard)

**Requirements** <kcite ref="43"/>:
- [ ] Collect Max Krang Kombo (500K)
- [ ] Collect Jackpoto Grande (either multiball)
- [ ] Collect Triple Mondo Jackpot (either multiball)
- [ ] Collect Weapon Hurry-Up
- [ ] Collect 5 April Hurry-Ups
- [ ] Complete 1-2-3 Foot
- [ ] Play Final Battle

> ⚠️ This is the most challenging mode to reach in the game <kcite ref="31"/>.

## 🎯 Shot Analysis

### ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Paths

| Danger Zone             | Issue                                           | Mitigation                                            |
| ----------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------- |
| **Left Outlane**        | Nearly 2X drains vs right <kcite ref="80"/>     | Add Siamese post or cupboard bumper <kcite ref="83"/> |
| **Spinner**             | Throws ball SDTM <kcite ref="80"/>              | Nudge immediately after                               |
| **Rejected Ramp Shots** | Go straight down the middle <kcite ref="82"/>   | Don't shoot weak ramps                                |
| **Slingshots**          | Chain into outlane drains <kcite ref="81"/>     | Reduce sling power <kcite ref="83"/>                  |
| **Upper Left Flipper**  | Feeds very fast <kcite ref="82"/>               | Be ready immediately                                  |
| **LAIR Shot**           | Ball can bounce out and drain <kcite ref="81"/> | Remove outlane pin rubbers                            |

### ✅ Safe Shots & Ball Control

| Shot                        | Notes                                                                                     |
| --------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Right Ramp (Backhand)**   | Safer than forehand - missed shots hit left flipper tip for dead bounce <kcite ref="40"/> |
| **Pizza Parlor**            | Relatively safe, feeds upper flipper                                                      |
| **Left Ramp**               | Good feed to right flipper                                                                |
| **Cradle on Right Flipper** | Best control position for most shots                                                      |

### Flipper Selection

| Target               | Best Flipper     | Notes                                              |
| -------------------- | ---------------- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| Right Ramp           | Right (backhand) | Safer than left flipper forehand <kcite ref="40"/> |
| Left Ramp            | Left             | Standard forehand                                  |
| Pizza Parlor         | Left or Right    | Both work                                          |
| Upper Loop           | Upper Left       | Stage flip from lower flippers                     |
| Lair (Super Jackpot) | Upper Left       | Raise left flipper, shoot behind                   |
| Side Ramp            | Right            | Forehand                                           |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Most Valuable Shots (Ranked) <kcite ref="96"/>

1. **Final Battle Start** - Up to 25M (75M with 3X PF!)
2. **NPMB Super Jackpot** - Starts ~3M
3. **NPMB Mondo Jackpot** - ~1M, repeatable
4. **Episode Finals after Team-Up** - 2-4M each
5. **Neutrino Pizza Party** - Potentially massive but risky
6. **Team-Up Rescue Shots** - ~1M each
7. **TPMB Super Jackpot** - Starts ~1M
8. **First 4 Episode Completions** - ~1M each
9. **April Hurry-Up** - 300K-2M depending on stacking
10. **TPMB Mondo Jackpot** - ~500K

### Path to 1 Billion Points

#### Early Game (0-100M)
- [ ] Choose **Michelangelo** (ball save + pizza contest) or **Raphael** (multipliers)
- [ ] Make skill shot every ball
- [ ] Lock balls for NPMB immediately
- [ ] During NPMB: chain Mondo → Super Jackpots
- [ ] Complete 2-3 easy episodes (Pizza-O-Meter, Night of the Mousers)

#### Mid Game (100-400M)
- [ ] Complete 4 episodes → Start Team-Up Multiball
- [ ] Keep 2X Playfield running constantly <kcite ref="45"/>
- [ ] Train your turtle to Level 3-4 for multiplier perks
- [ ] Stack episodes with multiballs
- [ ] Collect April Hurry-Ups during stacked modes

#### Late Game (400M-1B)
- [ ] Complete all 8 episodes
- [ ] Start Final Battle with 2X or 3X Playfield active
- [ ] If playing Raphael at Level 4: 3X PF × 3X Episodes = **9X scoring**
- [ ] Push for Cowabunga requirements
- [ ] Exploit Team-Up Pizza Party with Leonardo's 2X perk

### Character Recommendations by Strategy

| Goal                    | Best Turtle  | Why                                    |
| ----------------------- | ------------ | -------------------------------------- |
| **Pure Points**         | Michelangelo | 3X NPMB, fast pizza contest, ball save |
| **Multiplier Stacking** | Raphael      | 3X PF + 3X Episodes = 9X               |
| **Team-Up Scoring**     | Leonardo     | 2X Team-Up + 2X Pizza Party            |
| **Multiball Focus**     | Donatello    | Instant TPMB, 3X TPMB at Level 4       |
| **Beginners**           | Michelangelo | Ball save forgives mistakes            |

## 📝 Additional Information

### Extra Ball Opportunities

- Completing episodes <kcite ref="31"/>
- Pizza Eating Contest (40 slices in ~10 seconds) <kcite ref="39"/>
- Eating enough pizza slices

### Mystery Awards

The Pict-O-Pops (pop bumpers) change picture pieces. Match all 3 for the displayed award <kcite ref="31"/>.

### Pizza Eating Contest

Lights after collecting **7 pizza slices** (only 2 for Michelangelo!) <kcite ref="39"/>:
- Mash the action button as fast as possible
- Goal: 40 slices in ~10 seconds
- Awards 2-3M and often lights Extra Ball

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Make sure the ball is controlled before starting - you can't flip while mashing!

### Setup Recommendations

| Setting                 | Recommendation             | Why                                                      |
| ----------------------- | -------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Pitch**               | 6.5° or lower              | Reduces brutal drains <kcite ref="83"/><kcite ref="84"/> |
| **Slingshot Power**     | Reduced                    | Prevents chain drains <kcite ref="83"/>                  |
| **Outlane Posts**       | Add Siamese post or bumper | Left outlane is brutal <kcite ref="83"/>                 |
| **Outlane Pin Rubbers** | Remove                     | Eliminates LAIR bounce-out drains <kcite ref="81"/>      |

### Gameplay Modes

Hold both flipper buttons during attract mode <kcite ref="31"/>:
- **Standard** - Normal competitive play
- **Cooperative** - Share progress and scores with all players
- **Competitive** - Randomness removed (tournament mode)
- **Co-op + Competitive** - Combined
- **Team Play** - 2v2, 2v1, or 3v1

### Co-op "Coolest Rule Ever"

In Co-op mode, if you make your skill shot, ALL players can follow that ball to have Weapon lit for them too. Player 4 could potentially have **4 pairs of cascading Weapon Hurry-Ups**! <kcite ref="31"/>

### Easter Eggs & Notes
