# 👑 King of Diamonds - Complete Strategy Guide
## Gottlieb 1967 | Electro-Mechanical Classic

---

## ⚠️ Important: Understanding This Machine
> 💡 **Pro Tip:** In the EM era, the real goal was winning **replays** (free games) by collecting Specials, not just accumulating points!

---

## 🎯 Core Mechanics

### The Card Collection System

King of Diamonds features a unique playing card theme where you collect all 13 diamond cards (2 through Ace) <kcite ref="55"/>.

| Card Values | Location             | How to Collect          |
| ----------- | -------------------- | ----------------------- |
| 2️⃣           | Right outlane        | Roll through            |
| 3️⃣           | Left outlane         | Roll through            |
| 4️⃣           | Right side lane      | Roll through            |
| 5️⃣           | Left side lane       | Roll through            |
| 6️⃣           | Top right rollover   | Roll through            |
| 7️⃣           | Top right rollover   | Roll through            |
| 8️⃣           | Top left rollover    | Roll through            |
| 9️⃣           | Top left rollover    | Roll through            |
| 🔟           | Right standup target | Hit target              |
| J           | Left standup target  | Hit target              |
| Q           | Upper right standup  | Hit target (via bumper) |
| K           | Upper left standup   | Hit target (via bumper) |
| A           | Top center standup   | Hit target (via bumper) |

### 🎰 The Roto-Target (Center Drum)

The signature feature of King of Diamonds is the **rotating drum target** in the center of the playfield <kcite ref="57"/>.

- **16 faces** total, **5 visible** at any time, **3 shootable**
- Each face displays a card value
- **Hitting a face** collects that card (if not already collected)
- **Any rollover lane** spins the drum to show different cards

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** If the three visible cards are ones you already have, shoot a rollover lane to spin the drum and reveal new cards! <kcite ref="23"/>

## 🎱 Skill Shot (Plunge Options)

The manual plunger sends the ball to the top arch where it can land in various lanes <kcite ref="22"/>.

| Plunge Target      | Value  | Strategy Notes                                        |
| ------------------ | ------ | ----------------------------------------------------- |
| 9 lane (top left)  | 50 pts | ⭐ **Best choice** - ball likely bounces into K target |
| 6 lane (top right) | 50 pts | ⭐ **Best choice** - ball likely bounces into Q target |
| 8 lane             | 50 pts | Good option                                           |
| 7 lane             | 50 pts | Good option                                           |
| Center drop        | 5 pts  | ❌ Worst outcome - may hit Ace if lucky                |

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Aim for the 9 or 6 lanes - after rolling through, the ball often bounces between the pop bumper and the K or Q standup target multiple times! <kcite ref="22"/>

## 🏆 Special Awards (The "Wizard Mode")

Collecting all 13 cards is the ultimate achievement and lights **Special** for the rest of the game <kcite ref="59"/>.

### How Special Works:

1. **Lit inlane** now awards Special (instead of 100 pts)
2. **One of 10-J-Q-K-A targets** is lit for Special (rotates with 1-pt switch hits)
3. Specials can be collected from standup targets OR the roto-target faces
4. **Special = Free Game (Replay)**

### End-of-Ball Bonus <kcite ref="23"/>

- **10 points per card** collected during the ball
- Completing all 13 cards **resets them** for another bonus set
- **Maximum bonus:** 2 complete sets + 12 cards = 380 points

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** If you're close to completing all 13 cards, prioritize finishing the set! Once complete, all 13 relight and you can quickly collect many more <kcite ref="23"/>.

## ⚠️ Shot Analysis

### 🔴 Dangerous Shots & Drain Paths <kcite ref="25"/>

| Danger Zone                 | Risk Level | What Happens                             |
| --------------------------- | ---------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| Direct roto-target shot     | 🔴 HIGH     | Rebounds straight down the middle (SDTM) |
| Jack/10 standup targets     | 🔴 HIGH     | Rebounds into opposite slingshot → drain |
| Slow rolls off upper mounts | 🔴 HIGH     | Rolls down middle or into outlanes       |
| Six exposed rubber posts    | 🟡 MEDIUM   | Unpredictable bounces on missed shots    |

### 🟢 Safe Shots & Ball Control

| Safe Zone                | Why It's Good                      |
| ------------------------ | ---------------------------------- |
| Top rollover lanes (6-9) | Ball feeds to bumpers, good action |
| Side lanes (4, 5)        | Spins roto, controlled return      |
| Roto-target SIDE hits    | Less dangerous than center hits    |
| Pop bumper area          | Can score K/Q/A targets repeatedly |

### Flipper Selection Tips

- **2-inch flippers** are close together - good gap coverage
- **Left flipper:** Best for right side lane, roto-target right side
- **Right flipper:** Best for left side lane, roto-target left side
- **Both flippers:** Can trap and aim for top lanes

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Watch for balls coming out of the #4 and #5 side lanes - try to guide them through rather than letting them roll toward center <kcite ref="24"/>.

## 📈 Scoring Strategy

### Early Game (Ball 1-2)

1. ✅ Plunge for 9 or 6 lane
2. ✅ Let bumpers work - collect K, Q, A targets
3. ✅ Focus on easy rollover lanes (6, 7, 8, 9)
4. ✅ Avoid direct roto-target shots
5. ✅ Collect cards from roto when favorable

### Mid Game (Ball 3-4)

1. ✅ Target missing cards specifically
2. ✅ Use roto-target for hard-to-get cards (J, 10)
3. ✅ Spin roto via lanes until needed cards appear
4. ✅ Build toward completing all 13

### Late Game (Ball 5 / Final Ball)

1. ✅ Complete the 13-card set if close
2. ✅ Collect Specials from lit targets
3. ✅ Maximize end-of-ball bonus
4. ✅ Play for replays!

### Highest Value Opportunities

| Opportunity             | Value          | Priority |
| ----------------------- | -------------- | -------- |
| Complete all 13 cards   | Lights Special | ⭐⭐⭐      |
| Collect Special         | Free game      | ⭐⭐⭐      |
| K/Q bouncing in bumpers | 50+ pts/hit    | ⭐⭐       |
| Lit inlane              | 100 pts        | ⭐⭐       |
| End-of-ball bonus       | Up to 380 pts  | ⭐⭐       |

## 🔧 Setup Recommendations

### Pitch & Level

| Setting            | Recommendation            |
| ------------------ | ------------------------- |
| Playfield pitch    | 6.0° - 6.5° (standard EM) |
| Side-to-side level | Perfectly level           |
| Leg levelers       | All four adjusted evenly  |

### Operator Settings

- **Replay scores:** Adjustable via backbox
- **Special awards:** Can be set to replay, extra ball, or points
- **Match feature:** Standard end-of-game match for free game

### Maintenance Tips

- Keep roto-target mechanism clean and lubricated
- Check pop bumper responsiveness (key to scoring K/Q/A)
- Ensure all rollover switches register properly
- Clean playfield for consistent ball speed

## ✅ Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

### Before You Play
- [ ] Check credits or insert coin
- [ ] Note which cards are already lit (all should be lit at game start)
- [ ] Observe roto-target position

### During Play
- [ ] Plunge for 9 or 6 lane
- [ ] Let ball bounce in bumper area
- [ ] Collect cards from rollovers first (easier)
- [ ] Use roto-target for 10, J, Q, K, A cards
- [ ] Spin roto via any lane when needed cards aren't showing
- [ ] Avoid direct center shots at roto-target
- [ ] Watch for lit inlane (100 pts or Special)

### Goals

- [ ] Collect Special = Free Game!
- [ ] Target score: 2,000+ points
- [ ] Champion score: 3,000+ points

## 🎲 Additional Information

### Add-A-Ball Versions <kcite ref="61"/>

Two add-a-ball variants exist:
- **Diamond Jack** (domestic)
- **Solitaire** (Italy export)

These versions award extra balls instead of replays:
- Complete 2-3-4-5 = 1 extra ball
- Complete 6-7-8-9 = 1 extra ball  
- Complete 10-J-Q-K-A = 1 extra ball

### Historical Notes <kcite ref="72"/>

- **Production run:** 3,200 units
- **Last Gottlieb game** to use silkscreened '1' for thousands digit
- Spanish version: **Rey de Diamantes** (Petaco, 1967)
- **Retro Pinball LLC** produced a modern remake in 2010

### Easter Egg

The backglass artwork shows a card player being thrown out of the saloon on the right side - a subtle warning about the perils of gambling! <kcite ref="90"/>
