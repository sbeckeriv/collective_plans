## 🎮 Core Mechanics

### Skill Shots

| Skill Shot                     | How to Execute                                 | Value                          | Notes                        |
| ------------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------ | ---------------------------- |
| 🎁 **Crate Skill Shot**         | Plunge into right VUK without hitting switches | 1.5M (clean) / 750K (1 switch) | Starts a Job immediately     |
| 🔴 **VIP Skill Shot**           | Plunge into Red Circle backdoor                | 650K base                      | Advances Excommunicado MB    |
| 🚗 **Car Skill Shot** (LE only) | Plunge behind the car                          | 850K base                      | Spots progress toward Car MB |

> **💡 Pro Tip:** The Red Circle skill shot is the most valuable long-term choice—it advances toward Excommunicado Multiball, which is a major scoring opportunity.

### Jobs (Main Modes)

Jobs are the core single-ball modes. Shoot the **Weapons Crate** to advance the blue lights, then shoot the **right VUK** to start a Job.

**How to Select Your Job:**
- The shot you make *before* starting determines which faction's job you play
- Each faction has a unique perk awarded upon completion

| Shot        | Faction            | Color    | Job Name            | Objective                                      | Completion Perk                       |
| ----------- | ------------------ | -------- | ------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------- |
| Left Eject  | Bowery             | 🟣 Purple | **Night Watch**     | Make all 7 lit shots                           | +5 sec to all ball savers             |
| Left Orbit  | NY Continental     | 🟢 Green  | **Assassination**   | Shoot lit shots → captive ball kill shots (5x) | Marker ball save lights BOTH outlanes |
| Left Ramp   | Osaka Continental  | 🔴 Red    | **Security Detail** | Shoot moving lit shot (7 shots)                | **Multiball Extend** perk             |
| Red Circle  | High Table         | ⚪ White  | **Escort the VIP**  | Chase blinking VIP shot (6 hurry-ups)          | Bonus multiplier starts at 2x         |
| Center Ramp | Ruska Roma         | 🟡 Yellow | **Heist**           | Hit standups to light shots (6 shots)          | **Motion Sensors** during Lights Out  |
| Right Orbit | Marquis de Gramont | 🔵 Blue   | **Gather Intel**    | Combo left/right ramps, collect at center      | +20 sec to Lights Out timer           |
| Right Ramp  | Tarasov Family     | 🟠 Orange | **Task a Crew**     | Rip the spinner (4 times)                      | **Add-a-Ball** perk                   |

**Job Mechanics:**
- ⏱️ Base timer: 60 seconds (+3 sec per shot, +30 sec every 4th job)
- 🔄 Timer boost: Complete crate targets + VUK for +30 sec (once per job)
- 💰 Cashout: Shoot left eject for 20% of job score + faction perk
- 👥 Enemies act as shot multipliers during jobs (up to 4x per shot)

> **💡 Pro Tip:** Prioritize **Security Detail** (multiball extend) and **Task a Crew** (add-a-ball) early—these perks are game-changers for multiball scoring.

### Adversary Battles

Defeat **10 enemies** during single-ball play to light the left eject for an Adversary Battle. These are high-value single-ball modes with a 40-second ball save.

| Adversary   | Objective                                                  | Blood Marker (Escape) | "Be Seeing You" |
| ----------- | ---------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------- | --------------- |
| **Viggo**   | Hit car/captive ball to light gold shots                   | 6 lit shots           | 12 lit shots    |
| **Kirill**  | Shoot alternating left/right shots → Red Circle collects   | 6 lit shots           | 12 lit shots    |
| **Cassian** | Switch frenzy—everything scores, chase moving jackpot      | 3 jackpots            | 6 jackpots      |
| **Ares**    | All shots lit white, find the red shot (Ares location)     | 4 red shots           | 8 red shots     |
| **Zero**    | Combo mode—shoot combos, collect at car/captive/left eject | 3 combos              | 6 combos        |

**Battle Mechanics:**
- 🎯 Make lit shots → hit Blood Marker → light left eject for escape
- 💰 **Continue option:** Don't cash out to increase multiplier (+1x, max 4x)
- 🏆 **"Be Seeing You" bonus:** 25M instant + 10M end-of-ball bonus + Extra Ball (first time only)

> **💡 Pro Tip:** If you have an unused ball save before starting a battle, you get +30 seconds added to the battle timer!

## 🎱 Multiballs

There are **6 multiballs** total. Playing all 6 lights a wizard mode.

### Car Chase Multiballs (4 variants)

**How to Qualify:** Bash the car to light the left orbit for Car Multiball.
- First MB: 2 car hits (LE)
- Subsequent MBs: +1 hit each time (max 8 hits)

**Car Jackpot Building:**
- Base: 2M
- Increases via: car hits (+20%), award shots (+5K), jackpots (+50K), super jackpots (+250K)

| #   | Multiball              | Balls | Objective                                       | Super Jackpot                           |
| --- | ---------------------- | ----- | ----------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------- |
| 1   | **Helipad Showdown**   | 3     | Red shots → light car for 1-4x jackpot          | 5x at car, 2x super at left orbit       |
| 2   | **Taxicab Chase**      | 3     | 15-sec hurry-up → chase awards → jackpots       | 5x at left orbit (bash car to increase) |
| 3   | **Motorcycle Pursuit** | 3     | Left/right shots for awards, car switches sides | Super at Continental (car blocks it!)   |
| 4   | **Bagarre à l'Étoile** | 4     | Ramps light car, orbits increase multiplier     | 4 super jackpots at car                 |

> **💡 Pro Tip:** Hit the car before starting to cycle through multiball types. **Helipad Showdown** and **Bagarre à l'Étoile** are the highest-value options.

---

### Excommunicado Multiball

**How to Qualify:** Shoot the Red Circle bumper and surrounding targets to max the dance floor level, then shoot Red Circle again.

**Gameplay:**
1. **Build Phase (15 sec):** Single ball—hit shots to increase jackpot value (200K/shot, 125K/target). Orange targets add +5 sec.
2. **Multiball Phase:** 2-ball—jackpots pan outward from center ramps to outer shots
3. **Super Jackpot:** Lights at Red Circle after 6 jackpots

**Super Jackpot Value:** 5M + (2.5K × enemies this game) + (25K × enemies this ball) + (150K × enemies this mode)

> **💡 Pro Tip:** On LE, short-plunge during build phase to lock balls at Red Circle and increase super jackpot value!

---

### Deconsecrated Multiball

**How to Qualify:** Shoot the captive ball to spell **WINSTON**, then lock 3 balls at center ramp.

**Gameplay:**
- Green shots score jackpots: 500K + (75K × enemies this mode) + (5K × enemies this ball)
- Captive ball increases super jackpot by 200K
- After 7 jackpots (14 for subsequent phases): captive ball lights for **15-second unlimited super jackpots!**

**Super Jackpot Value:** 2.5M + (2.5K × enemies this game) + (25K × enemies this ball) + (250K × enemies this mode)

> **💡 Pro Tip:** This is your bread-and-butter multiball. Stack it with jobs for massive scoring!

## 📈 Progression Systems

### Enemies

Enemies are the **blue circles** in front of each major shot. They serve two critical functions:

1. **Shot Multipliers:** Each enemy = +1x multiplier (max 3 enemies = 4x)
2. **Battle Qualification:** Defeat 10 enemies in single-ball play to light Adversary Battle

**Enemy Spawning:**
- Ball start: Spawns equal to ball number (Ball 2 = 2 enemies)
- During play: Spawns every 20-60 seconds (phone ring sound)
- Spawn rate increases with jobs/multiballs played

**Armored Enemies:** When 3 enemies are at one shot, additional spawns add **armor** (solid light instead of blinking). Armored enemies require an extra hit.

> **💡 Pro Tip:** Enemies "block" shot arrows—clear them to progress other features!

---

### Allies

Allies assist during Jobs by manipulating enemies.

**How to Qualify:**
1. Hit gold coin targets (when not in a mode)
2. Shoot the ally's ramp to activate for next job

| Ally       | Ramp        | Effect                                                     |
| ---------- | ----------- | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Akira**  | Left Ramp   | Rotates enemies one shot to the right                      |
| **Charon** | Center Ramp | Kills enemies and replenishes them (more scoring chances!) |
| **Katia**  | Right Ramp  | Moves enemies toward other shots, prioritizing combos      |

> **💡 Pro Tip:** **Charon** is the most valuable ally—enables massive enemy defeat chains during jobs!

---

### Lights Out (2x Scoring)

**How to Qualify:** Spell **YAGA** at inlanes/outlanes (only collects where BABA is already lit)

**Effect:** 
- All scoring **2x** for 30 seconds
- Playfield lighting dims (can't see lit shots!)
- **Motion Sensors** (from Heist perk): Press action button to briefly see shots

**Timer Extensions:**
- Gather Intel perk: +20 seconds to base timer

> **💡 Pro Tip:** Save Lights Out for multiball stacks or right before cashing out an Adversary Battle!

---

### Marker Ball Save

**How to Qualify:** Hit left standup targets 4 times (increases by 2 per ball save lit)

**Mechanics:**
- Lights one outlane for ball save
- Flip to change which outlane is lit
- **Assassination perk:** Lights BOTH outlanes

---

### Bonus Multiplier

- Spell **BABA** at inlanes to increase (max 5x)
- **Escort the VIP perk:** Starts each ball at 2x

**End-of-Ball Bonus:**
- 1M per job started
- 2M per adversary battle started
- 10M per "Be Seeing You" scored

## 🔗 Stacking & Combos

### ✅ What Stacks Well

| Combination                        | Why It Works                                      |
| ---------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------- |
| **Job + Car Multiball**            | Enemies multiply both job and MB shots            |
| **Job + Deconsecrated MB**         | Green jackpots + job shots = double progress      |
| **Job + Excommunicado MB**         | Ruska Roma (Heist) + EC MB is especially powerful |
| **Lights Out + Any Multiball**     | 2x scoring on all jackpots                        |
| **Lights Out + Adversary Cashout** | Double your escape jackpot!                       |
| **Charon + Gather Intel**          | Enemy combos + ramp jackpots = massive scores     |

### ❌ What Does NOT Stack

| Feature               | Restriction                                            |
| --------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Adversary Battles** | Mutually exclusive—nothing else can run during battles |
| **Jobs**              | Can only be started in single-ball play                |
| **Wizard Modes**      | Lights Out cannot be used during wizard modes          |

> **💡 Pro Tip:** Start a job, THEN start a multiball. You can't do it the other way around!

---

### Optimal Stacking Sequence

1. Qualify a job (hit crate targets)
2. Select your faction (shoot desired shot)
3. Start the job (right VUK)
4. Immediately start a multiball (left orbit for car, center ramp for Deconsecrated)
5. Activate Lights Out during multiball
6. Profit! 💰

## 🧙 Wizard Modes

Light wizard modes by completing ANY of these:
- ✅ Play all 7 Jobs
- ✅ Play all 5 Adversary Battles
- ✅ Play all 6 Multiballs

### The Staircase (Mini-Wizard Mode 1)

**A three-level ascent with a final multiball phase!**

| Level | Shots Lit                                      | Enemies Each | Completion Bonus |
| ----- | ---------------------------------------------- | ------------ | ---------------- |
| 1     | Left Ramp, Right Ramp                          | 1            | 20M base         |
| 2     | Left Orbit, Left Ramp, Right Orbit, Right Ramp | 2            | 20M base         |
| 3     | All 6 major shots (except center ramp)         | 3            | 40M base         |

**Scoring:**
- 5M per enemy shot
- White arrows light after defeating enemies
- Collect white arrows for +1x completion bonus multiplier

**Maximum Completion Bonuses:**
- Level 1: 20M × 3 = **60M**
- Level 2: 20M × 5 = **100M**
- Level 3: 40M × 7 = **280M**

**Final Phase:** After Level 3, 2-ball multiball begins—fight back down the stairs!

---

### The Duel (Mini-Wizard Mode 2)

**A 2-ball hurry-up mode—keep both balls alive for 2x scoring!**

**Sequence (repeat 3 times):**
1. Red Circle (5M hurry-up)
2. Left Ramp + Center Ramp (10M each)
3. Left Orbit + Right Ramp (10M each)
4. Lock both balls at Left Eject + Crate within 10 seconds (15M each)

**Final Shots:** Left Eject, Red Circle, Right Orbit (25M each), Crate (50M + mode complete)

**Key:** Keep both balls in play for **2x all scores!**

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Game Priorities (Ball 1)

1. **🎯 Go for Car Skill Shot** (LE) or Crate Skill Shot
2. **🔨 Bash the car** during ball save to advance Car MB
3. **📦 Open the crate** and start a job (preferably Security Detail or Task a Crew)
4. **🎱 Start Car Multiball** at left orbit
5. **🔵 Spell WINSTON** at captive ball when possible

### Mid Game Priorities (Ball 2-3)

1. **Stack jobs with multiballs** every ball
2. **Defeat enemies** to qualify Adversary Battles
3. **Cash out Adversary Battles** with multiplier buildup
4. **Collect faction perks** (especially multiball extend + add-a-ball)
5. **Build Lights Out** for 2x scoring opportunities

### Late Game Priorities

1. **Complete wizard mode qualifications** (7 jobs, 5 battles, or 6 MBs)
2. **Stack Lights Out with multiball** for maximum scoring
3. **Play wizard modes** for massive point totals
4. **Continue Adversary Battles** to 4x multiplier before cashing out
