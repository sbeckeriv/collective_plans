# 🐎 Mustang - Strategy Guide

*Stern Pinball, 2014 — Designed by John Trudeau*

> **"Race through the Challenge Ladder. Stampede to the finish."**

Mustang builds toward the **Silverball Stampede** wizard mode through a Challenge Ladder of race-themed modes, a Gears-based instant-multiball mechanic, and escalating Burnout/Mustang multiballs. Our goal: spell M-U-S-T-A-N-G via race completions, build Gears multiball up to 4 balls before cashing in, and complete the full ladder with a maxed Mustang collection.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Snipe the alternating gears target columns** — hitting the lit "A" for +2 gears and 1M points is basically an instant multiball
- [ ] **Cancel out of Gears Multiball until you have at least 4 balls** — the jackpots are significantly better at higher ball counts, so don't cash in early
- [ ] **Complete races to advance the M-U-S-T-A-N-G letter sequence** — the next letter flashes at race completion and is collected at the scoop, always in order
- [ ] **Consider starting with Cruisin' or Road Race modes** rather than the bottom row — some players find this produces higher final scores
- [ ] **Save Drag Race for later** in the game, specifically for when you need an easy multiball
- [ ] **Reach Silverball Stampede** by advancing through all Challenge Ladder challenges with a complete Mustang collection and fully-upgraded rides

---

## 🏁 Core Mechanics

### M-U-S-T-A-N-G Letter Collection

- Letters advance **in order**, always
- The next letter **flashes upon completing a race**
- **Collected at the scoop**
- (Note: the spinner has little to no direct effect on letter progression, despite some community confusion about this)

### Gears Multiball (Your Instant-Multiball Engine)

- **Snipe the alternating gears target columns**; hitting the lit **"A"** target for **+2 gears and 1M points** is close to a guaranteed multiball trigger
- **Don't cash in immediately** — cancel out and keep building gears until you reach **at least 4 balls**, since jackpot value scales meaningfully with ball count

### Upgrade Scoring & Roving Shots

- The top **FORD** inserts feature roving/sweeping shot mechanics
- After completing a level of **Upgrade Scoring**, two inserts become a **"wide roving" shot**
- After **Upgrade Multiball**, a **single insert "roving" shot** remains
- These contribute to the broader upgrade system feeding into your overall Mustang collection

### Burnout Multiball

- During its **second phase**, each whirlpool spin **adds another letter to BURNOUT**
- Completing the word **begins the final phase**, lighting the **Super Jackpots**

### Mustang Multiball

A separate multiball mode within the broader Challenge Ladder progression, contributing toward your overall Mustang collection alongside Burnout and Gears.

---

## 🏆 Silverball Stampede (Wizard Mode)

**Requirements:**
- Advance through **all challenges** in the Challenge Ladder
- Complete your **Mustang collection**
- **Fully upgrade your rides**

This is the culmination of the entire ruleset, themed around the (then-upcoming) **2015 Mustang model**.

---

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **Cashing in Gears Multiball too early** | Forfeits the significantly better jackpots available at 4+ balls | Be patient and cancel out repeatedly until you're at the higher ball count |
| **Captive ball / whirlpool mechanics** | Unique features that can produce unexpected ball behavior | Approach with controlled shots until you understand their specific physics |
| **Pop bumper unpredictability** (4 bumpers) | Standard chaos zone | Treat as incidental scoring |

---

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Game (Ball 1)

1. Start with Cruisin' or Road Race rather than the bottom-row challenges
2. Begin sniping gears targets to build toward Gears Multiball
3. Complete your first race to start the M-U-S-T-A-N-G letter sequence

**Target Score:** 15M–40M

### Mid Game (Ball 2)

1. Build Gears Multiball up to 4+ balls before cashing in
2. Save Drag Race for when you specifically need an easy multiball later
3. Continue collecting M-U-S-T-A-N-G letters and working Upgrade Scoring

**Target Score:** 40M–100M

### Late Game (Ball 3+)

1. Push through Burnout Multiball's phases toward the Super Jackpots
2. Complete remaining Challenge Ladder modes and finish your Mustang collection
3. If rides are fully upgraded and the ladder is complete, push for Silverball Stampede

**Target Score per ball:** 50M–150M+

---

## ⚙️ Setup Recommendations

| Setting | Recommendation | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| **Playfield pitch** | 6.5°–7.0° | Stern factory standard |
| **Shaker motor** | Consider an after-market install if not already present | Stern provided wiring but not the motor itself on this title |
| **Captive ball / whirlpool mechanism** | Verify smooth, predictable operation | Central to Burnout Multiball's letter-spelling phase |

---

## 📋 Quick Reference: Strategic Priorities

**Ball 1:** Start with Cruisin'/Road Race, begin sniping gears, complete your first race.
**Ball 2:** Build Gears Multiball to 4+ balls, save Drag Race for later, keep collecting letters.
**Ball 3+:** Push through Burnout phases, finish the Challenge Ladder, chase Silverball Stampede.

**Target High-Score Ballpark:** **80–200 million points** is a strong, competitive game; reaching Silverball Stampede represents full mastery of the Challenge Ladder.
