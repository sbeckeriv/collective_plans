# 🦾 Iron Man Pinball Strategy Guide
## The Path to 1 Billion Points

> **Target Score:** 1,000,000,000 points  
> **Grand Champion Default:** 75,000,000  
> **Difficulty:** Reaching 1B requires exceptional play, stacking, and likely multiple wizard modes

## 📋 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

1. ✅ Plunge ball, tap flipper to move skill shot to correct lane
2. ✅ Shoot spinners (orbits/center) to light pop bumpers and spell M-O-N-G-E-R
3. ✅ Hit I-R-O-N-M-A-N targets to start Fast Scoring
4. ✅ Collect 8 drone targets to light War Machine Multiball
5. ✅ Complete all 6 S.H.I.E.L.D. lanes for mystery award
6. ✅ Build both ramps to 400K each to start Bogey mode
7. ✅ Stack Double Scoring with multiballs for maximum points

## 🎯 Core Mechanics

### Skill Shots

| Skill Shot              | How to Execute                                                     | Value                                                      |
| ----------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Basic Skill Shot**    | Plunge to top lanes, tap flipper to move lit insert to ball's lane | 250K (first), +25K each subsequent <kcite ref="31"/>       |
| **Enhanced Skill Shot** | Hold LEFT flipper while plunging to top lanes                      | 1M + 250K for each additional skill shot <kcite ref="73"/> |

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** The enhanced skill shot keeps the left orbit up-post DOWN, allowing the ball to travel around. This is the superior choice for experienced players.

### S.H.I.E.L.D. Lanes (6 Total)

The 6 S.H.I.E.L.D. inserts include:
- 2 top rollover lanes
- 2 inlanes  
- 2 outlanes

**Completing all 6 awards:** <kcite ref="31"/>
1. Bonus multiplier increase (up to 25x max)
2. Mystery award lit at War Machine
3. All inserts reset for another completion
4. **First completion only:** Lights a Mark on the ladder

**Mystery Award Possibilities:**
- 200K points
- Light Extra Ball
- Light Special
- Bonus multiplier boost
- 100K added to pop bumper jackpot
- Add time to current mode
- Add-a-ball during multiball

### Iron Man Modes (I-R-O-N-M-A-N Targets)

Three sequential scoring modes activated by completing the 7 standup targets (4 left, 3 right): <kcite ref="31"/>

| Mode                 | Difficulty                              | Duration | Scoring                                                            |
| -------------------- | --------------------------------------- | -------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Fast Scoring**     | Easy (any target in bank lights letter) | 40 sec   | All targets score 10K minimum, +1K per I-R-O-N-M-A-N hit (max 50K) |
| **Double Scoring**   | Medium (adjacent targets also work)     | 40 sec   | ALL scoring doubled                                                |
| **Iron Man Scoring** | Hard (exact target required)            | ~17 sec  | Hurry-up: 750K→400K, collect at any I-R-O-N-M-A-N target           |

> 🔥 **Critical Strategy:** Double Scoring is the most valuable mode in the game. Time it to start just before a multiball or wizard mode to DOUBLE all jackpots for 40 seconds! <kcite ref="31"/>

**Completing all 3 modes** causes the Iron Man insert to FLASH (important for Do or Die Multiball).

---

### Pop Bumpers & Super Pops

- Each pop hit adds 5K to pop bumper jackpot (starts at 500K)
- Lit pop bumpers add 7.5K instead
- Light bumpers by shooting the 3 spinner lanes (left orbit, center, right orbit)

**Super Pops:** When all 3 bumpers are lit: <kcite ref="31"/>
- Next 25 hits collect 1/25th of jackpot each
- Spinner shots add 600 points per spin to super pop value
- Lit drone targets add +5 to hit count
- Relighting all 3 bumpers resets count to 25

### Bogey Mode ("We Got A Bogey")

**Qualification:** Build both ramps to 400K (4 shots each on Easy, 8 total on Hard) <kcite ref="32"/>

| Setting    | Requirement                    |
| ---------- | ------------------------------ |
| Easy Bogey | Any 4 ramp shots total         |
| Hard Bogey | 4 shots to EACH ramp (8 total) |

**Bogey Scoring:** <kcite ref="31"/>
- 25 seconds (default)
- All arrow shots lit
- Starting value: 500K
- Each shot: +100K (max 1M per shot)
- Ramps are preferred (return ball to flippers)

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Alternate ramps rapidly during Bogey for maximum scoring. Mystery award often gives extra time.

## 🎱 Multiballs

### War Machine Multiball

**Qualification:** Destroy 8 drones by hitting lit drone targets <kcite ref="31"/>

| Round | Lit Drones at Once  | Notes                                    |
| ----- | ------------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| 1st   | 3 (after first hit) | War Machine shot relights disabled drone |
| 2nd   | 2                   | Harder to complete                       |
| 3rd+  | 1                   | Very difficult                           |

**Starting:** Shoot War Machine kicker (250K + starts multiball)

**Multiball Structure (2-ball start):** <kcite ref="31"/>

| Stage         | Shots           | Value (v1.6)                            | Notes                                             |
| ------------- | --------------- | --------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------- |
| Stage 1       | 4 drone targets | 150K each (1st round), 250K (2nd round) | Complete in 20 sec = add-a-ball <kcite ref="35"/> |
| Stage 2       | 5 arrow shots   | Double jackpot (2x stage 1 value)       |                                                   |
| Super Jackpot | War Machine     | Sum of all jackpots collected           | Flashes War Machine insert                        |

> 🔥 **Scoring Tip:** With Double Scoring active, all jackpots are doubled AND those doubled values count toward the super jackpot!

### Iron Monger Multiball

**Qualification:** Spell M-O-N-G-E-R via spinner shots <kcite ref="31"/>

| Difficulty       | Letters Spotted | Spinner Hits Needed            |
| ---------------- | --------------- | ------------------------------ |
| Easy             | 2               | 4                              |
| Medium (default) | 0               | 6                              |
| Hard             | 0               | 10 (spell I-R-O-N-M-O-N-G-E-R) |

**Phase 1 (Single Ball):** <kcite ref="31"/>
- Iron Monger rises from playfield
- Hit him 6 times to spell M-O-N-G-E-R again
- Each hit: 100K base + spinner value (7.5K per spin)
- Magnet throws ball unpredictably

**Phase 2 (3-Ball Multiball):**
- Shoot any spinner lane to raise Iron Monger
- Jackpots: 250K per Iron Monger hit
- After 6 hits: Super Jackpot at center lane = **3,000,000** <kcite ref="31"/>
- Collecting super jackpot flashes Iron Monger insert

> ⚠️ **Warning:** Iron Monger multiball DISABLES War Machine and Whiplash multiballs. Start Iron Monger LAST if stacking!

## 🔄 Stacking & Combos

### ✅ What Stacks Well

| Combination                               | Why It's Powerful                               |
| ----------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| **Double Scoring + Any Multiball**        | All jackpots doubled for 40 seconds             |
| **Double Scoring + Wizard Mode**          | Massive point multiplier on already huge awards |
| **Double Scoring + Bogey**                | 2M per ramp shot instead of 1M                  |
| **Fast Scoring + Pop Bumpers**            | Rapid 50K hits during super pops                |
| **War Machine MB + S.H.I.E.L.D. Mystery** | High chance of add-a-ball                       |

> 🔥 **Golden Stack:** Qualify Double Scoring, then immediately start a multiball. 40 seconds of doubled jackpots can yield 20M+ easily.

### ❌ What NOT to Stack

| Avoid                                          | Reason                                                          |
| ---------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Iron Monger MB + Other MBs                     | Iron Monger disables War Machine and Whiplash <kcite ref="31"/> |
| Iron Monger MB during Bogey                    | Iron Monger blocks left ramp when raised <kcite ref="34"/>      |
| Starting modes during single-ball wizard modes | Wastes valuable time                                            |

### Optimal Timing

1. **Early Ball:** Build toward Double Scoring qualification
2. **Mid Ball:** Qualify a multiball while Double Scoring is ready
3. **Start Double Scoring** → Immediately start multiball
4. **Collect jackpots** during 40-second window

## 🧙 Wizard Modes

### Do or Die (Mini-Wizard Mode)

**Requirement:** Light all 5 characters on the ladder <kcite ref="31"/>

**Version 1: Hurry-Up** (characters lit solid)
- Shoot center lane
- Countdown: 50M → 15M <kcite ref="31"/>
- Collecting awards **permanent Double Scoring** for rest of ball
- Characters reset (except flashing ones)

**Version 2: Multiball** (ALL characters flashing)
- 4-ball timed multiball
- Jackpots at both ramps
- Other targets raise jackpot value
- Let 1-2 balls drain for easier ramp shots <kcite ref="31"/>

> 💡 **Strategy:** Getting all 5 characters flashing is extremely difficult. Focus on super jackpots in each multiball.

---

### Jericho Missile Mayhem (Main Wizard Mode)

**Requirement:** Complete Mark ladder (all 6 marks in one ball on default) <kcite ref="31"/>

**Starting:** Shoot flashing center lane (1M instant award)

**Objectives (Single Ball Mode):**

| Feature            | Requirement                         | Base Value      |
| ------------------ | ----------------------------------- | --------------- |
| Iron Man Targets   | Spell I-R-O-N-M-A-N (exact targets) | 250K per letter |
| S.H.I.E.L.D. Lanes | Light all 6                         | 250K per lane   |
| Iron Monger        | Hit 6 times                         | 250K per hit    |
| War Machine        | Hit 5 times                         | 250K per hit    |
| Whiplash           | Hit 6 times                         | 250K per hit    |
| Bogey Ramps        | 4 shots each ramp                   | 250K per shot   |

**Scoring Bonuses:** <kcite ref="31"/>
- Pop bumper hits and spinner spins increase Jericho value (+1K each, max 500K)
- Completing features awards escalating bonuses: 1M → 2M → 3M → 4M → 5M → 6M
- **Final award at center lane: 50,000,000** <kcite ref="31"/>

## 💰 Scoring Strategy: Path to 1 Billion

### Understanding the Scoring Landscape

| Jericho             | 50-100M       | 150M+                      |

### Phase-by-Phase Strategy

#### 🟢 Early Game (0-50M)
1. Collect skill shot (enhanced if confident)
2. Complete S.H.I.E.L.D. lanes for mystery + Mark 1
3. Spell I-R-O-N-M-A-N for Mark 2
4. Start Fast Scoring (lights Iron Man character)
5. Build ramps toward Bogey

#### 🟡 Mid Game (50-200M)
1. **Qualify Double Scoring** (complete I-R-O-N-M-A-N again)
2. Collect 8 drones (lights Drones character)
3. Spell M-O-N-G-E-R via spinners
4. **Stack Double Scoring + War Machine MB** for doubled jackpots
5. Aim for War Machine Super Jackpot (flashes insert)
6. Complete Bogey mode

#### 🔴 Late Game (200M-1B)
1. **Stack Double Scoring + Iron Monger MB**
2. Collect Iron Monger Super Jackpot (3M × 2 = 6M)
3. **Stack Double Scoring + Whiplash MB**
4. Collect Whiplash Super Jackpot (5M × 2 = 10M)
5. Complete all 3 Iron Man modes (flashes Iron Man insert)
6. **Reach Do or Die** (all characters lit)
7. If characters are flashing: Do or Die Multiball (100M+ potential)
8. **Complete Mark ladder for Jericho** (50M+ guaranteed)
9. Repeat wizard modes with Double Scoring stacks

### The 1 Billion Formula

To reach 1 billion, you need approximately:
- **3-4 complete Jericho runs** (~150M each with stacking)
- **Multiple Do or Die completions** (~50M each)
- **Consistent multiball super jackpots** with Double Scoring
- **Extended ball times** through extra balls and ball saves

> 🎯 **Reality Check:** 1 billion is an exceptional score. The default Grand Champion is 75M. Reaching 1B requires near-perfect play, excellent stacking, and likely 15+ minute ball times. Focus on consistent 100M+ games first.
