## ✅ Quick-Start Checklist

- [ ] Pick your turtle wisely (see Turtle Select below)
- [ ] Make your skill shot (+250k + light Weapon HU)
- [ ] Start shooting toward the **Pizza Parlor** lock (NPMB is your #1 priority)

- [ ] Always **backhand the Right Ramp** to be safer
- [ ] Watch for **2x Playfield** to activate — time your multiball start with it
- [ ] Collect **pizza slices** toward the Extra Ball

## 📺 Core Modes — Episodes

There are **8 episodes** to complete. Each has a unique set of shots and objectives.

### How to Start an Episode

1. Shoot **Left Ramp** (lights yellow) AND **Right Ramp** (lights yellow)
2. Shoot **Pizza Parlor** to start the selected episode
3. Use **Dimension X standup targets** (left = up, right = down on display) to change which episode starts next

> 📌 **Note:** Starting an episode re-enables the LAIR and APRIL targets if they were locked out.

## 🎳 Multiballs

TMNT is fundamentally a **multiball-centric** game. Two main multiballs define your score.

---

### 🍕 Ninja Pizza Multiball (NPMB) — The Big One

> **"Many players think NPMB is so overpowered that everything else is secondary."**

**How to start:**
1. Shoot the **Pizza Parlor** 3 times to lock balls
2. Before each lock, select a pizza **topping** (hit the Pizza Parlor while the topping is shown)
3. Three balls release from the spinning disc — multiball begins!

**Restarting NPMB:** Available again after playing **4 more episodes** from when it last started.

**During NPMB — Defeat Foot Soldiers:**

Hit the **blue lit shots** to kill Foot Soldiers:

| Shot         | Foot Soldiers Killed |
| ------------ | -------------------- |
| Left Ramp    | 3                    |
| Krang Loop   | 2                    |
| Center Ramp  | 2                    |
| Pizza Parlor | 1                    |
| Right Ramp   | 1                    |

Defeat **6 Foot Soldiers** → lights **Mondo Jackpot** at Krang Loop

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Focus on the Krang Loop during NPMB — it lights Mondo AND it's where you collect it. Use the auto-launch to send a ball to the upper flipper, then stage-flip to set up the loop shot.

---

### ⚡ Turtle Power Multiball (TPMB)

**How to start:**
- Shoot the **Right Ramp 4 times** during single-ball play
- OR choose **Donatello** — his L1 perk lights it on the **first right ramp shot**

**During TPMB:**
- Shoot **green lit shots** for jackpots: starts at **100k, +25k per shot, max 250k**
- Collect **4 jackpots** → lights **Mondo Jackpot** at Krang Loop

> 💡 **TPMB vs NPMB:** TPMB jackpots are roughly **half the value** of NPMB. Still worthwhile, but treat it as practice or a lower-priority multiball. Mondo starts ~500k in TPMB vs ~1M+ in NPMB.

---

### 🤝 Team-Up Multiball (Mini-Wizard Mode)

**How to qualify:** Complete **4 episodes**

**How to start:** Shoot the **Left Ramp** when lit

**Gameplay:**
1. **Rescue Phase:** Hit 6 shots to defeat the bad guys (Bebop, Rocksteady, and friends). Values per rescue shot ~1M. Perks from completed episodes modify this phase.
2. **Rescue Hurry-Up:** After rescue, collect a hurry-up shot

**Episode Perks apply here** — Night of the Mousers and Window Shopping perks are especially powerful in this mode.

## ⚙️ Other Features & Scoring

### 🏠 LAIR Targets (L-A-I-R)

Completing all 4 LAIR targets alternates between two rewards:
- **Odd completions → Training:** Scoring bonus +10% per completion (stacks to +30%)
- **Even completions → 2× Playfield:** 30 seconds of 2× scoring on all shots

After 2× expires, it relights with **4 more LAIR shots** as long as Training is already lit.

> 💡 **Key Insight:** You need **8 LAIR shots** to get the first 2× playfield. These are risky shots (the Lair mono-target is a tight shot). Leonardo starts with 4 spotted!

### ✖️ 2× Playfield

One of the most important features in the game.

- Active for **30 seconds**
- Multiplies **all** scoring including jackpots, modes, and hurry-ups
- Re-lightable from even LAIR completions while Training is lit
- **Always try to have 2× running when starting NPMB or Final Battle**

### 🎓 Training & Neutrino Pizza Party

- Complete Training (full sequence) → triggers **Neutrino Pizza Party**
- Neutrino Pizza Party is a random mystery-style award
- Training also stacks bonus multipliers (+10%, +20%, +30%) on all scoring

### 🗡️ Weapon Hurry-Up

1. Make the skill shot → lights Weapon inlane insert
2. Roll over the lit inlane → starts hurry-up (base **500k**) at an orbit
3. Base value can be raised by Mystery Awards — can reach **1M–2M+** in late game!

> 💡 **Early game:** Avoid actively chasing this (too low value). Late game with 2× and raised base value, it's worth going for.

### 💕 April Hurry-Up

- Triggered by completing **APRIL** targets
- Base value is low (~300k) but Mystery Awards raise it cumulatively
- In late game with 2× running: can be worth **750k–2M+** per collect
- Michelangelo L2 perk → **2× April HU** value

### 🦶 1-2-3 Foot

- Activated by the **2nd left inlane** rollover
- Shoot 3 shots (right ramp = first shot typically)
- Final shot value starts ~300k; grows as foot spinner builds through the game
- Disables TPMB progress while active — plan accordingly

### 🌀 Krang Kombo

1. Hit the **Upper Loop** repeatedly
2. Each loop builds the combo
3. Cash out by shooting the **Center Ramp** (max = 500k)
- Raphael L2 perk = **2× Krang Kombo**
- **Max Krang Kombo** is one of the 7 requirements for Cowabunga!

### 🍕 Pizza Eating Contest

- Start by shooting **Pizza Targets** after collecting **7 pizza slices**
- Pizza slices collected from: Pizza Parlor, Michelangelo's L1 perk (+5), toppings (Peanut Butter), and general play
- Contest = timed mode to hit pizza shots

### 🎪 Glider *(Premium/LE Only)*

- Accessed via the upper flipper area
- Unique shots/awards not available on the Pro
- Adds another layer of shot variety and some exclusive scoring

## 🧙 Wizard Modes

**Phase 1 — Rescue Phase:**

### ⚔️ Final Battle (Main Wizard — 8 Episodes)

**Requirements:** Complete all **8 episodes**, then shoot **Left Ramp**

**Immediate Bonus on Start:** A bonus equal to all accumulated episode scores is awarded. With 2× playfield running, this can be **30–60M+ in one shot!**

> 🔥 **Critical Tip:** If you're about to start Final Battle, light 2× Playfield first — even spending shots to do it is worth the risk. One player scored a 60M bonus because they had 2× running when they hit the left ramp!

**Final Battle has multiple phases** involving multiball segments and single-ball tasks against Shredder.

## 🎯 Shot Analysis

### ⚠️ Dangerous Shots

| Shot                | Risk Level    | Notes                                                       |
| ------------------- | ------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------- |
| Left Orbit          | 🔴 High        | Fast return; unpredictable bounces; left outlane drain risk |
| Right Orbit         | 🔴 High        | Same as left — tight shot                                   |
| Lair (mono-target)  | 🔴 High        | Tight, center-field target; miss = center drain             |
| Center Ramp         | 🟠 Medium-High | Missed shots can come back center or feed the lair area     |
| Dimension X Targets | 🟠 Medium-High | Left inlane area; missed shots risky                        |
| Left Ramp (fast)    | 🟡 Medium      | Can be backhanded safely                                    |

### ✅ Safer Shots

| Shot                    | Safety Notes                                                                                 |
| ----------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Right Ramp (backhanded) | Hit from the **left flipper** — misses tend to return to left flipper tip or cradle on right |
| Left Ramp (backhanded)  | Consider backhanding from right flipper when possible                                        |
| Pizza Parlor            | Relatively forgiving — fed cleanly off both flippers                                         |
| Pop Bumpers             | No real drain risk from deliberate bumper feeding                                            |

### 🏏 Flipper & Ball Control Tips

- **Backhand the right ramp always** — it's the single most important technique on this machine. Missed forehand shots often hit a post and can drain.
- **Stage flipping** to the upper flipper → aims the ball at the Krang Loop for Mondo Jackpots
- **Dead bounce / catch** on missed right ramp returns — they tend to come to the left flipper tip, allowing a right-flipper cradle
- **Post passes** are hard on TMNT — even successful ones often result in a live ball, so be prepared to play on the fly
- Left outlane is **brutal** — watch for fast inlane/outlane bounces, especially after orbit shots

### 🎮 Early Game (Ball 1)

**Priority:** Get locked in on your multiball runway

1. Make your **skill shot**
2. Shoot toward LAIR targets (4–8 shots) for Training → 2× Playfield
3. Start one episode (especially Wrath of Krang, Pizza-O-Meter, or Night of the Mousers)
4. Begin **Pizza Parlor lock progress** (shoot parlor whenever safe)
5. Advance **Right Ramp** toward TPMB (Donnie player: 1 shot does it)

### 🔥 Mid Game (Ball 2)

**Priority:** NPMB + stacked 2× = game-defining ball

1. If 2× is close, finish LAIR targets before starting NPMB
2. **Lock 3 balls with good toppings** — prioritise Ice Cream, Chili Peppers, Fudge, Marshmallows
3. During NPMB: hit blue shots to defeat Foot Soldiers; aim for Mondo → Grande chain
4. After NPMB, complete **2nd and 3rd episodes** (Extra Ball at 2!)
5. Progress toward Team-Up Multiball (4 episodes)

### 🏆 Late Game (Ball 3)

**Priority:** Complete all 8 episodes + Final Battle, Cowabunga tasks

1. Play Team-Up Multiball (4 episodes done)
2. Work toward all 8 episodes → Final Battle
3. **Have 2× running when you start Final Battle** for a massive instant bonus (30-60M+)
4. Complete Cowabunga tasks as they come naturally
5. If you reach NPMB again (after 4 more episodes), stack it with everything

### 💰 Score Breakdown to 1 Billion

**Realistic point distribution across 3+ balls:**

- **NPMB with 2×:** 50-150M (Grande chains + Mondo loops)
- **Final Battle bonus with 2×:** 30-60M
- **Team-Up Multiball:** 10-20M
- **Episodes (4-8 completions):** 40-80M cumulative
- **TPMB + jackpots:** 20-40M
- **Misc (hurry-ups, combos, training):** 50-100M

**Total:** 200M-450M is achievable; 1B requires multiple NPMBs, extended play, and optimal stacking.
