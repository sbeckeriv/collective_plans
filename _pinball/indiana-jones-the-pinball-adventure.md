# 🗿 Indiana Jones: The Pinball Adventure - Strategy Guide

*Williams, 1993 — Designed by Mark Ritchie & Doug Watson*

> **"Choose wisely. Then go for the billion."**

This Williams widebody is one of the deepest 90s rulesheets ever made — 12 modes across all three original films, a liberal extra-ball system, and a 6-ball Eternal Life wizard mode where hitting every switch scores **1 billion points**. Our goal: complete all 12 modes, build your INDY multiplier to 8x, and run the full jackpot/super-jackpot ladder before chasing Eternal Life.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Shoot the Mode Saucer** to access the 12 modes — it's notoriously hard ("harder than Twilight Zone's Slot Machine"), but it's your gateway to nearly everything
- [ ] **Complete the I-N-D-Y rollovers** to build your bonus multiplier: 2x → 4x → 6x → 8x; after 8x, each subsequent INDY scores a flat **10M Maximum Bonus** award instead
- [ ] **At 8x multiplier, Extra Ball is lit at the Mode Saucer** — a major incentive to push the multiplier early
- [ ] **This is one of the most liberal games ever made for Extra Balls** — there are 5+ distinct sources, so play aggressively for them
- [ ] **Shoot the E-N-T drop targets** to open the sinkhole and light Lock — 3 locked balls triggers multiball
- [ ] **Complete all 12 modes to light Eternal Life Multiball**, the wizard mode where every switch scores 10M and clearing them all (including Path of Adventure) scores **1 billion points**

## 🎬 The 12 Modes (Three Films)

### Raiders of the Lost Ark
| Mode | How to Complete |
|---|---|
| **Castle Grunewald** | Hit drop target, then strike captive ball 3 times (5M/10M/15M) to escape |
| **Tank Chase** | Loop the orbits as Indy chases a tank; loops count down from 15M to 5M; enough loops boards the tank for 30M |
| **Three Challenges** | Right ramp to raised playfield; hit all lit rollovers for 10M/20M/30M |
| **Choose Wisely** | Select the correct chalice while shuffled; 5M penalty or 20M reward |

### Temple of Doom
| Mode | How to Complete |
|---|---|
| **Monkey Brains** | Ramps and orbits score 8M each |
| **Steal the Stones** | Collect yellow flashing lights from Path of Adventure; 5M/stone, 20M bonus for all 8 |
| **Escape in the Cart** | Video mode: 25 correct flipper decisions; 5M + 1M per correct turn |
| **Survive the Rope Bridge** | Timed: 4 ramp shots to cross; 5th shot cuts the rope; lights Extra Ball |

### Last Crusade
| Mode | How to Complete |
|---|---|
| **Get the Idol** | Shoot sinkhole while E-N-T targets reset; 5M/10M/20M for targets down, 20M for collecting Idol and locking ball |
| **Streets of Cairo** | Find Marion under boxes (10M/attempt); correct box triggers a 20M shot |
| **Well of Souls** | Hectic **6-ball multiball**; lock sinkhole shots worth 2M × balls-in-play |
| **Get the Medallion** | Video mode: aim and shoot bad guys (1M each); 15M bonus for the Medallion |

**Completing all 12 modes lights Eternal Life Multiball.**

## 🎯 I-N-D-Y Rollovers & Bonus Multiplier

| Completions | Multiplier |
|---|---|
| 1st | 2x |
| 2nd | 4x |
| 3rd | 6x |
| 4th | **8x (max)** |
| 5th+ | Flat **10M Maximum Bonus** award each time |

Each INDY completion also lights one of **Indy's Friends** (flashing lights between the slingshots) — relevant to the Loop Jackpot combo below.

---

## 🎁 Multiball & Jackpot Structure

1. Shoot **E-N-T** drop targets to open the sinkhole and light **Lock**
2. **3 locked balls** triggers the standard multiball
3. **Three named jackpots**: The Ark (20M base), The Stones (30M base), The Grail (40M base)
4. **Jackpot formula:** `(base + raise jackpot) × (balls in Idol + 1)`
5. Shoot the **sinkhole to multiply**: 1 ball = 2x + lights jackpot; 2 balls = 3x
6. **Collect via the right ramp**
7. **Super Jackpots** (after all 3 regular jackpots collected): hit the **captive ball** for **80M (Ark), 90M (Stones), or 100M (Grail)**

> 💰 **Pro Tip — Loop Jackpot Combo:** Light all of Indy's Friends via the INDY rollovers, shoot lit loops to solidify each Friend, then shoot the right ramp to collect — scoring is the total of all lit loops collected, **up to a maximum of 200M**. This is one of the biggest single combos in the game.

---

## 🏆 Eternal Life Multiball (Wizard Mode)

**Requirement:** Complete all 12 modes.

- A **6-ball multiball**
- **Every switch (except outlanes) is worth 10M each**
- **Collecting ALL switches, including the Path of Adventure ones, scores 1 BILLION points**

---

## 🎟️ Extra Balls (5+ Sources — Extremely Liberal)

1. **Path of Adventure**: first rollover set completion lights the EB hole
2. **I-N-D-Y rollovers**: completing 4 times (8x) lights Extra Ball at the Mode Saucer
3. **Hand of Fate**: random chance when collected at the Mode Saucer
4. **Survive the Rope Bridge**: completion lights EB at the Mode Saucer
5. **Raven Bar Video Mode**: after eliminating enough bad guys

> 🎯 This is genuinely one of the most generous pinball machines ever made for extra balls — play aggressively toward any of these five paths.

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **Mode Saucer** | Notoriously among the hardest shots in pinball history | Don't force it under pressure; it's worth the wait for a clean attempt |
| **Sinkhole lock** | Also a genuinely difficult shot | Approach deliberately rather than rushing |
| **Player-controlled tilting upper playfield** | Unique mechanism requiring its own learning curve | Practice tilt control before relying on it for precision shots |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Game (Ball 1)

1. Start working I-N-D-Y rollovers toward your multiplier
2. Attempt the Mode Saucer to begin unlocking modes
3. Build toward your first E-N-T lock

**Target Score:** 50M–150M

### Mid Game (Ball 2)

1. Push INDY to 8x for the Extra Ball light
2. Run standard multiball, building jackpot multiplier via the sinkhole before collecting
3. Chase the Loop Jackpot combo (up to 200M) once Friends are lit

**Target Score:** 150M–400M

### Late Game (Ball 3+)

1. Complete remaining modes toward the full set of 12
2. Cash Super Jackpots (80M/90M/100M) once all three regular jackpots are collected
3. If all 12 modes are done, run Eternal Life Multiball and chase the billion-point clear

**Target Score per ball:** 200M–1B+

## ⚙️ Setup Recommendations

| Setting | Recommendation | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| **Playfield pitch** | 6.5°–7.0° | Standard for early-90s Williams widebody machines |
| **Tilting upper playfield mechanism** | Verify smooth, predictable tilt response | Unique mechanical feature core to several mode shots |
| **Mode Saucer & sinkhole** | Check consistent registration despite difficulty | Both are intentionally hard but should register reliably when hit correctly |

---

## 📋 Quick Reference: Strategic Priorities

**Ball 1:** Build INDY multiplier, attempt the Mode Saucer, start E-N-T locks.
**Ball 2:** Push to 8x for Extra Ball, run multiball with built jackpot multiplier, chase Loop Jackpot.
**Ball 3+:** Complete all 12 modes, cash Super Jackpots, run Eternal Life for the billion-point clear.

**Target High-Score Ballpark:** **300M–1 billion+** is achievable on this legendarily deep and generous machine — reaching Eternal Life Multiball and clearing all switches for the literal billion-point award is the ultimate goal.
