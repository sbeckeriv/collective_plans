# 🦖 Godzilla (Sega, 1998) - Strategy Guide

*Sega Pinball, 1998 — Designed by Joe Balcer*

> **"Stack all five. Save New York."**

This is the original 1998 Sega Godzilla — based on the American remake film of the same year, and a genuine rarity at only 510 units produced. Don't confuse it with the 2021 Stern Godzilla remake; this older machine has its own distinct ruleset built around **five stackable multiballs** that all feed into the final **Save New York** mode. Our goal: qualify and stack as many of the five multiballs as possible before starting, then complete all five to unlock Save New York.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Take the skill shot**: hold the left flipper, plunge, and hit the right ramp
- [ ] **All 5 multiballs can be stacked together** — the more you qualify before starting one, the higher the scoring across the board
- [ ] **Focus on the safer multiballs first**: Godzilla MB and Baby MB are built on safe orbit/ramp shots — light these as your foundation
- [ ] **Bring in the riskier three (Taxi, Sonar, Helicopter) opportunistically** once you're already in a multiball, since you can stack additional MBs in after starting one (at slightly reduced scoring)
- [ ] **After qualifying any multiball, hit the left inner loop to start it**
- [ ] **After playing all 5 multiballs, hit the inner loop again to start Save New York** — spell NEW YORK, then hit the inner loop for the Super Jackpot

## 🦖 Core Mechanics

### The Five Stackable Multiballs

| Multiball | Qualification |
|---|---|
| **Godzilla MB** | Hit 4 ramps |
| **Baby MB** | Hit 4 left orbits |
| **Taxi MB** | Hit the TAXI standups (easiest option in a pinch) |
| **Sonar MB** | Hit the captive ball 6 times |
| **Helicopter MB** | Combo from an inlane into the side standup targets, 5 times |

**All five can be stacked together** — qualifying more before you start increases the scoring of all of them simultaneously. **Hit the left inner loop to start** whichever multiball(s) you've qualified.

> 🎯 **Pro Tip — Core Strategy:** Godzilla MB and Baby MB are built on the **safest shots** (ramps and orbits) — use them as your foundation. Taxi, Sonar, and Helicopter are riskier to qualify directly, so bring them in by stacking additional multiballs **after** you've already started one — you take a slightly lower scoring hit, but it's much safer than chasing their qualification shots cold.

### Save New York (Final Mode)

1. **Complete all 5 multiballs** at least once
2. Hit the **inner loop** again to start **Save New York**
3. **All shots are worth big points** during this mode
4. **Spell NEW YORK**, then hit the **inner loop** for the **Super Jackpot**

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **Taxi/Sonar/Helicopter qualification shots** | Riskier than the ramp/orbit shots used for Godzilla/Baby MB | Qualify these via multiball-stacking rather than chasing them directly when not already in a multiball |
| **Captive ball (Sonar MB)** | Can produce unpredictable rebounds | Approach with controlled, deliberate shots |
| **Combo shot requirement (Helicopter MB)** | Inlane-to-standup combos require precision | Only attempt when you have a clean, controlled setup |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Game (Ball 1)

1. Take the skill shot toward the right ramp
2. Start building toward Godzilla MB (4 ramps) and Baby MB (4 orbits) — your safest qualification paths
3. Pick up incidental Taxi/Sonar/Helicopter progress along the way

**Target Score:** Build steadily through safe shots

### Mid Game (Ball 2)

1. Qualify as many multiballs as possible before starting any of them
2. Start your stacked multiball via the left inner loop
3. Bring in additional multiballs mid-multiball for extra (if slightly reduced) scoring

### Late Game (Ball 3+)

1. Work through any remaining un-played multiballs to complete the full set of 5
2. Once all 5 are done, start Save New York via the inner loop
3. Spell NEW YORK and hit the inner loop for the Super Jackpot
