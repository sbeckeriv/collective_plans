## 🎯 Core Mechanics

### Skill Shots

| Skill Shot             | How to Execute                                | Reward                                                                         |
| ---------------------- | --------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Dead End Lanes**     | Medium plunge to land in either Dead End lane | 250K (+25K each success) + Bonus X +5 <kcite ref="21"/>                        |
| **Back Door** (Secret) | Soft plunge to land behind the mansion        | 1M (+250K each, max 2.5M) + Instantly qualifies/starts Haunt <kcite ref="21"/> |

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** The Back Door skill shot is your best friend. During Haunts, it spots the next mode shot. Always attempt this plunge!

### 🏚️ Haunts (Main Modes)

Haunts are the core gameplay loop. Shoot the House Entrance when flashing purple to start. Use flipper buttons to select which room (Haunt) to enter via the house windows. <kcite ref="23"/>

**General Haunt Rules:**
- Base shot value: 500K (+increments per shot)
- Final shot value: 2M (builds during mode)
- Timer: 45 seconds (pauses in bumpers)
- Making shots with <15 seconds resets timer to 15 seconds
- **Draining or timing out locks the Haunt until "They Came From Space" wizard mode** <kcite ref="23"/>

#### Main Haunts (No Key Required)

| Haunt                           | Location             | Stages   | Key Strategy                                                                                                   |
| ------------------------------- | -------------------- | -------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Night of the Living Dead**    | Basement (right)     | 3 stages | Shoot ramps before House, destroy 4+ ghouls, finish at House <kcite ref="23"/>                                 |
| **Teenagers from Outer Space**  | Attic (left)         | 2 stages | Make 3+ shots, then spam right spinner for massive final value <kcite ref="23"/>                               |
| **The Brain That Wouldn't Die** | Left garage          | 4 stages | Find the flashing white shot, complete 4+ shots, finish both ramps <kcite ref="23"/>                           |
| **The Manster**                 | Right garage         | 4 stages | All switches score! Make 5 shots, clear all 6, shoot House twice, both ramps <kcite ref="23"/>                 |
| **Manos: The Hands of Fate**    | Middle floor (right) | 4 stages | Left-right-house pattern, find solid red shot, 3 shots to Cellar <kcite ref="23"/>                             |
| **The Werewolf of Washington**  | Attic (right)        | 2 stages | Transform via orbits, combo flashing shots (quadratic scoring!), 3 transformations to finish <kcite ref="23"/> |

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Werewolf has the highest scoring potential. The combo value increases quadratically: Nth shot = base + 125K × (N² - N). A 10-shot combo is worth massive points! <kcite ref="23"/>

#### Bonus Haunts (Skeleton Key Required)

Unlock one per Skeleton Key collected. Order is random (except Dracula first on Halloween, Santa first in December). <kcite ref="24"/>

| Haunt                                 | Stages   | Notes                                                                    |
| ------------------------------------- | -------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Santa Claus Conquers the Martians** | 2 stages | Ramps→Garage/Crypt→Orbits, then toggle green/red shots <kcite ref="24"/> |
| **The Giant Gila Monster**            | 2 stages | Pop bumper mode! 20 bumper hits to light final shot <kcite ref="24"/>    |
| **The Satanic Rites of Dracula**      | TBD      | (Rules incomplete in documentation)                                      |
| **Eegah**                             | 3 stages | All shots→Cellar, 2 ramps, 6 shots to finish <kcite ref="24"/>           |

### 🎬 Trailer Trash (Post-Haunt Bonus)

After completing any Haunt, the House lights for a hurry-up worth **50% of your Haunt score**! <kcite ref="25"/>

| Ramps Hit Before Collecting | Bonus Percentage |
| --------------------------- | ---------------- |
| 0 ramps                     | 50%              |
| 1 ramp                      | 75%              |
| 2 ramps                     | **100%**         |

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Always hit both ramps before collecting Trailer Trash. Doubling a 10M Haunt score is huge!

## 🎱 Multiballs

### Garage Multiballs

Lock 3 balls in the Garage to start. Hit the drop target to light locks. <kcite ref="31"/>

| Multiball              | Lock Requirements                  | Jackpot Structure                                                                          |
| ---------------------- | ---------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Wild Women** (1st)   | 1 drop target hit lights all locks | All shots = 350K (+25K), Super at House = 1M, then orbits = 800K+ <kcite ref="31"/>        |
| **Add-a-Zombie** (2nd) | 1 hit per lock                     | 4 shots lit = 500K (+25K), Garage adds random jackpot <kcite ref="31"/>                    |
| **Attic Attack** (3rd) | 2 hits per lock                    | All shots = 500K (+25K), Super at House every 5 jackpots = jackpot total <kcite ref="31"/> |

### Trunk Multiball

2-ball multiball from locking balls in the Trunk. <kcite ref="33"/>

- Two random jackpot shots lit
- Trunk targets change jackpot locations
- Complete targets → Left ramp = Super Jackpot + 1-3 random Junk pieces

### Phone-A-Fiend Multiball

**Requirement:** Collect at least one of each Junk item, then lock 2 balls in Trunk <kcite ref="34"/>

- Trunk targets → Left ramp = Jackpot + lights all ramps/orbits for 20 seconds
- Each jackpot = 500K more than previous
- **This is a wizard mode requirement!**

### Gappa Angry! Multiball

**Requirement:** Fill the Freak Fryer (1000 cumulative switch hits), then shoot House <kcite ref="29"/>

| Stage | Shots Required                   | Lock Location                         |
| ----- | -------------------------------- | ------------------------------------- |
| 1     | House Entrance                   | House Lock                            |
| 2     | Left + Right Ramps               | Garage                                |
| 3     | Both Orbits + House              | Crypt                                 |
| 4     | Both Ramps + House + Crypt       | Trunk                                 |
| 5     | Both Orbits + Both Ramps + House | Garage                                |
| 6     | Cellar                           | **6-Ball Multiball + Super Jackpot!** |

**Consolation Modes (if you drain during Gappa Angry):** <kcite ref="29"/>

| Drain During Stage | Mode              | Balls                            |
| ------------------ | ----------------- | -------------------------------- |
| 2                  | Pool Party        | Background mode (bumpers = 250K) |
| 3                  | Dance Fever       | 2-ball                           |
| 4                  | Make-Out Mayhem   | 3-ball                           |
| 5                  | Scream Test       | 4-ball (find the jackpot shot!)  |
| 6                  | Run For Your Life | 5-ball                           |

### Gar-Goils Gone Wild

Complete Gar-Goil targets 2-4 times to fill gauge, then once more to start. <kcite ref="30"/>

- Target hits = 50K (+10K increment)
- Complete all 4 = 150K (+25K)
- Orbits = 150K (+150K per shot)
- Super Jackpot available (~20M)

## 📈 Progression Systems

### Freak Fryer (Switch Counter)

Every switch hit counts toward the Freak Fryer, displayed on the playfield. <kcite ref="29"/>

| Level | Hits Required | Cumulative | Award                |
| ----- | ------------- | ---------- | -------------------- |
| 1     | 150           | 150        | 1M                   |
| 2     | 175           | 325        | 1.5M                 |
| 3     | 200           | 525        | 2M                   |
| 4     | 225           | 750        | 2.5M                 |
| 5     | 250           | 1000       | 3M + Gappa Angry lit |

### B-Restorator Rank

Displayed above flippers. Advances by completing Haunts. <kcite ref="27"/>

- Each rank = 1M (+500K per additional rank)
- **Rank 4 = Director's Cut lit at House**

### Junk in the Trunk

Hit Trunk targets (36-24-36) to lock in a Junk item, complete targets, shoot left ramp to collect. <kcite ref="32"/>

| Junk Item             | Effect                                     |
| --------------------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| 🔑 **Skeleton Key**    | Unlocks one bonus Haunt                    |
| 🧪 **Lab Test**        | Increases jackpot values                   |
| 🗡️ **Elvira's Dagger** | Adds bonus to non-multiball Haunts         |
| 🔫 **Atomic Raygun**   | Action button spots a shot in current mode |

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** The Atomic Raygun cannot be used on Trailer Trash, but it's invaluable for completing difficult Haunt shots! <kcite ref="25"/>

### Deadhead Family Crypt

Hit Crypt entrance to lower it, shoot the skull target, finish in Crypt. <kcite ref="28"/>

| Deadheads Killed | Reward                                        |
| ---------------- | --------------------------------------------- |
| 1                | Right outlane ball save (Return)              |
| 2                | Extra Ball lit                                |
| 3                | Super Head Shot lit (wizard mode requirement) |

## 🔄 Stacking & Combos

### ✅ What Stacks Well

| Combination                   | Why It's Good                                                            |
| ----------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Haunt + Garage Multiball**  | Extra balls protect you while completing Haunt shots <kcite ref="43"/>   |
| **Haunt + Trunk Multiball**   | Trunk MB can be brought into any mode <kcite ref="33"/>                  |
| **Double Trouble + Any Mode** | 2x/4x scoring multiplies everything! <kcite ref="105"/>                  |
| **Haunt + Trailer Trash**     | Trailer Trash runs during multiball if Haunt completes <kcite ref="25"/> |
| **Playfield X + Bonus X**     | Multiplicative scoring at end of ball <kcite ref="44"/>                  |

### ❌ What NOT to Stack

| Combination                          | Why to Avoid                                |
| ------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------- |
| **Un-Happy Hour + Drive Me Crazy**   | Cannot run simultaneously <kcite ref="35"/> |
| **Side Modes + Gar-Goils Gone Wild** | Cannot stack <kcite ref="35"/>              |
| **Multiple Haunts**                  | Only one Haunt at a time                    |

### Optimal Stack Strategy

1. Qualify a Haunt (don't start yet)
2. Complete target banks for Double Trouble
3. Lock 2 balls in Garage
4. Start Haunt
5. Start Garage Multiball
6. Activate Double Trouble via inlane
7. Complete Haunt during 2x/4x multiball scoring

## 🧙 Wizard Modes

### Mini-Wizard: House Party

**Requirement:** Play 3 Haunts <kcite ref="26"/>

- 4-ball multiball (like AFM's Total Annihilation)
- All shots = Jackpots worth 25K × shots made in previous 3 Haunts
- House = Super Party Jackpot (re-collects all jackpots)
- **Bonus:** If turret eyeball faces you when shooting House, add-a-ball (once per House Party)

### Mini-Wizard: They Came From Space

**Requirement:** Play 6 Haunts (or 5 main + 1 bonus) <kcite ref="26"/>

- 6-ball multiball
- 6 phases based on previously played Haunts
- Each phase: 5 jackpots → House for Super Jackpot
- Complete all 6 phases = **100M bonus!**
- **Required for final wizard mode**

### Director's Cut

**Requirement:** Complete 4 Haunts (B-Restorator Rank 4) <kcite ref="27"/>

- Extended Trailer Trash mode
- Re-collect values from each completed Haunt
- Shoot lit blue shots → Cellar to cash out each film
- Complete 4 times = **100M bonus!**
- **Required for final wizard mode**

### 🏆 Final Wizard Mode: Wild Market Value

**Requirements (all 7 must be completed):** <kcite ref="41"/>

| Task                      | How to Complete                     |
| ------------------------- | ----------------------------------- |
| ✅ Start Director's Cut    | Complete 4 Haunts                   |
| ✅ Super Head Shot         | Kill 3 Deadheads                    |
| ✅ Phone-A-Fiend Multiball | Collect all 4 Junk types            |
| ✅ Gappa Angry             | Fill Freak Fryer (1000 switches)    |
| ✅ Gar-Goils Gone Wild     | Complete Gar-Goil targets 3-5 times |
| ✅ Attic Attack Multiball  | Lock 3 balls in Garage (3rd time)   |
| ✅ They Came From Space    | Play 6 Haunts                       |

**Gameplay:**
- Start at Cellar
- 30-second hurry-up to hit Wild Market Value target
- Sell the house for accumulated value!

## 🎯 Shot Analysis

### ⚠️ Dangerous Shots

| Shot                | Risk                                                     | Mitigation                                    |
| ------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------- |
| **Crypt VUK**       | Can miss and send ball to left outlane <kcite ref="57"/> | Be ready for erratic kickout                  |
| **Right Orbit**     | Fast return, hard to control                             | Avoid during single ball unless necessary     |
| **Standup Targets** | Unpredictable rebounds                                   | Only shoot during multiball <kcite ref="43"/> |
| **Weak Left Orbit** | Can fall into Back Door unexpectedly                     | Actually beneficial during Haunts!            |

### ✅ Safe Shots

| Shot                   | Why It's Safe               | Best Use                              |
| ---------------------- | --------------------------- | ------------------------------------- |
| **Left Ramp**          | Returns to right flipper    | Primary scoring shot, Haunt qualifier |
| **Right Ramp**         | Returns to left flipper     | Trunk progress, Trailer Trash boost   |
| **House Entrance**     | Controlled feed             | Starting Haunts, collecting bonuses   |
| **Backhand Left Ramp** | Reliable from right flipper | Combo building                        |

### Flipper Selection Guide

| Target         | Flipper          | Notes             |
| -------------- | ---------------- | ----------------- |
| Left Ramp      | Right (forehand) | Most reliable     |
| Right Ramp     | Left (forehand)  | Standard shot     |
| House Entrance | Either           | Center shot       |
| Left Orbit     | Right (backhand) | Risky but doable  |
| Right Orbit    | Left (forehand)  | Fast return       |
| Crypt          | Right (forehand) | Aim carefully     |
| Garage         | Left (backhand)  | Drop target first |
| Cellar         | Right (forehand) | Lower playfield   |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy: Path to 1 Billion

Based on player reports, scores of **1.5-2 billion** are achievable with optimal play, extra balls, and good multiplier stacking. <kcite ref="99"/>

### Early Game (0-100M)

1. **Always attempt Back Door skill shot** (1M+ and Haunt progress)
2. **Start and complete your first Haunt** (5-15M with Trailer Trash)
3. **Work Trunk targets** for Junk collection
4. **Complete top lanes** for Bonus X
5. **Kill first Deadhead** for right outlane save

### Mid Game (100M-500M)

1. **Stack Haunts with Garage Multiball** for protected scoring
2. **Activate Double Trouble** (2x scoring) during modes
3. **Complete 3 Haunts** → House Party wizard mode
4. **Collect all 4 Junk types** → Phone-A-Fiend ready
5. **Build Freak Fryer** toward Gappa Angry

### Late Game (500M-1B+)

1. **Stack Double Trouble (4x) with multiball + Haunt**
2. **Complete They Came From Space** (100M bonus)
3. **Complete Director's Cut** (100M bonus)
4. **Reach Wild Market Value** for ultimate payout
5. **Maximize Bonus X + Double Bone-Us** at end of ball

### Highest Value Opportunities

| Opportunity                     | Potential Value                 |
| ------------------------------- | ------------------------------- |
| They Came From Space completion | 100M+                           |
| Director's Cut completion       | 100M+                           |
| Gappa Angry 6-ball Multiball    | 50M+                            |
| Werewolf combo chains           | 20M+ per transformation         |
| 4x Double Trouble + Multiball   | Multiplies everything           |
| Wild Market Value               | Variable (game total dependent) |

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** The key to 1 billion is **multiplier stacking**. A 4x Double Trouble during a stacked Haunt + Multiball can generate 50M+ in under a minute. <kcite ref="105"/>

### Hand of Fate (Mystery Awards) <kcite ref="37"/>

Complete right target bank to light left outlane. When ball drains left, press action button within 6 seconds to spin wheel.

**First Wheel Awards:**
- Ball Save
- Extend Garage Multiball
- Extend Trunk Multiball
- 5 Million
- 2 Million
- Double Bone-Us (2x entire bonus!)

**After collecting all 6:** Manos Wheel starts with point values (250K-5M) collectible at targets.

### Side Modes <kcite ref="35"/>

| Mode               | Requirement                  | Reward                          |
| ------------------ | ---------------------------- | ------------------------------- |
| **Un-Happy Hour**  | 5 ramp shots (+2 each time)  | Ramps = 350K (+25K) for 30 sec  |
| **Drive Me Crazy** | 5 orbit shots (+2 each time) | Orbits = 300K (+25K) for 30 sec |

### Setup Recommendations

| Setting              | Recommended        | Notes                                                                   |
| -------------------- | ------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Pitch**            | 6.5° - 7.0°        | Standard is 6.5°, steeper = faster <kcite ref="114"/><kcite ref="116"/> |
| **Level**            | Side-to-side level | Critical for consistent shots                                           |
| **Flipper Strength** | Factory default    | Adjust if ramps are difficult                                           |

### Easter Eggs & Special Features

- **Halloween (Oct 31):** Dracula bonus Haunt unlocks first <kcite ref="24"/>
- **December:** Santa Claus Conquers the Martians unlocks first <kcite ref="24"/>
- **Blood Red Kiss Exclusive:** Red sparkle aesthetic on all components <kcite ref="83"/>

### Does Completing Modes Change Gameplay?

Yes! <kcite ref="23"/>
- **Failed Haunts** are locked until They Came From Space
- **Completed Haunts** increase difficulty of subsequent Haunts (+1 shot requirements)
- **B-Restorator rank** persists and unlocks Director's Cut
- **Junk collection** is cumulative across balls
