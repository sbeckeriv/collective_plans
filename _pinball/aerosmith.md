# 🎸 Aerosmith Pinball Machine - Strategy Guide

*Stern Pinball, 2017 — Designed by John Borg*

> **"Dream on" — but bring your A-game to the Toy Box.**

Aerosmith puts a 60-second timed song mode at the center of every shot decision, feeding two separate multiballs (Toybox and Elevator) on the way to a 24-city Final Tour wizard mode. Our goal: **80–150 million points**, with elite "380M+" runs requiring deep multiball stacking and full song completion.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Take the Super Skill Shot** (short plunge to the right flipper, then hit a lit shot) over the Hidden Skill Shot when you can — double the points and Smart Missiles
- [ ] **Start 3 songs early** — this lights an extra ball
- [ ] **Build AEROSMITH targets** for shot multipliers before diving deep into modes
- [ ] **Lock balls in the Toy Box** rather than starting Toybox Multiball immediately — more locks (up to 6) means a bigger multiball
- [ ] **Don't panic in 6-ball multiball** — bail out and cradle if you're overwhelmed rather than airmailing a ball
- [ ] **Finish all 9 songs** to unlock Medley Multiball and the Final Tour wizard mode

## 🎶 Core Mechanics

### Skill Shots (Plunge)

| Skill Shot | How | Reward |
|---|---|---|
| **Hidden** | Gentle plunge directly into the lock saucer | 500K (+500K per repeat attempt) + 1 Smart Missile |
| **Super** | Short plunge to the right flipper, then hit any lit shot | 1M (+1M per repeat attempt) + 2 Smart Missiles |

### Song Modes (7 Timed Modes, 60 Seconds Each)

Each song lights a specific shot pattern; completing lit shots advances progress, and combos earn coins that increase the song's "super" feature value.

| Song | Shots Required | Super Feature |
|---|---|---|
| **Rats in the Cellar** | 8 shots, escalating pattern | Super spinner (100 spins) |
| **Dude Looks Like A Lady** | 10 shots | Super lanes (10 inlanes) |
| **Back in the Saddle** | 8 shots | Super pops (100 bumper hits) |
| **Sweet Emotion** | 12 shots | Super scoring (60s all-switch multiplier) |
| **Walk This Way** | 10 shots | Super ramps (10 ramp shots) |
| **Same Old Song & Dance** | 10 random shots | Super targets (20 hits) |
| **Last Child** | 8 shots | Super loops (10 loop shots) |

> 🎤 **Pro Tip:** Use **Crank It Up** at the left scoop mid-song to raise difficulty in exchange for bigger rewards — only do this once you're confident you can finish the harder shot count.

**Starting 3 songs lights an extra ball** — prioritize sampling songs early over grinding one to completion.

### AEROSMITH Targets & Multipliers

Complete the AEROSMITH standup targets to light shot multipliers (2x) at the major shots, then roving 3x once all letters are lit. Completing the bank also lights a Mystery award at the left eject.

## 🎁 Toybox Multiball

1. Shoot the **Toy Box** (upper playfield) to light Lock at the lock saucer
2. **Lock up to 6 balls** before collecting — the more locked, the bigger the eventual multiball
3. Multiball plays as 3, 4, 5, or 6 balls depending on locks banked
4. Complete all **8 shots, twice**, for jackpots (500K base + 50K per shot); the 8th jackpot each round is a **Super Jackpot**
5. Re-lock 1–2 balls mid-multiball for a **2x or 3x scoring bonus** on the remainder

> ⚠️ **Reality check:** A 6-ball multiball is genuinely hard to control — most players end up bailing on triple-ball cradles rather than risk an airmail. Don't be afraid to play it safe and let balls drain rather than forcing a risky save.

---

## 🛗 Elevator Multiball

1. Shoot **both orbits** to light locks at the Elevator
2. Collect **3 locks** to start Elevator Multiball
3. Once one is locked, only the **Elevator scoop** is lit for jackpots — alternate between major shots and the scoop
4. **Super Jackpot** value scales with how many attempts it took: **100%, 75%, or 50%** of total collected, so a clean fast run pays the most

> 🎯 **Pro Tip:** Lock 4 balls in the Toy Box *before* starting Elevator Multiball — stacking sets up a massive combined 6-ball Elevator round.

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Shot | Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| **6-ball multiball chaos** | Cradling 3+ balls on one flipper is extremely difficult | Bail out deliberately rather than attempting a risky save |
| **Toy Box shot under pressure** | Upper playfield access can deflect awkwardly | Only attempt with a controlled, trapped ball |
| **Forcing Crank It Up too early** | Raises difficulty before you're ready, risking a wasted song attempt | Only crank up once you're consistently completing the base shot count |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Game (Ball 1)

1. Take the Super Skill Shot if your plunge timing is reliable
2. Start 3 different songs quickly to light the extra ball
3. Build AEROSMITH targets for multipliers before committing to deep mode play
4. Begin banking locks in the Toy Box

**Target Score:** 10M–25M

### Mid Game (Ball 2)

1. Cash in Toybox Multiball with at least 3–4 locks banked
2. Work toward Elevator Multiball qualification via the orbits
3. Save ramp-shot songs for when shot multipliers are active to maximize coin value
4. Keep cycling through remaining songs toward full completion

**Target Score:** 25M–60M

### Late Game (Ball 3+)

1. Stack Toybox locks (4+) before triggering Elevator Multiball for a combined 6-ball round
2. Finish remaining songs — completing all 9 lights **Medley Multiball**
3. In Medley Multiball, replay incomplete song shot sets; finishing all songs during it lights the **Medley Super Jackpot** at the left scoop (worth 10M × songs completed beforehand)
4. If all 9 songs are done, push into **Final Tour** for the biggest scoring window in the game

**Target Score per ball:** 30M–100M+

---

## 🏆 Wizard Modes

### Medley Multiball (Mini-Wizard)

- **Qualify:** Start all songs (don't need to complete them first)
- **Gameplay:** Replay shot sets from any songs not yet completed
- **Super Jackpot:** Completing all 9 songs during Medley lights the left scoop for the Super Jackpot, bonus-scaled at 10M × songs already completed before Medley began

### Final Tour (Main Wizard Mode)

- **Qualify:** Complete all 9 songs
- **Gameplay:** Advance through **24 Cities** by completing sets of 8 shots; values escalate **10M → 20M → 30M** per city tier
- **Final Super Jackpot:** 100M
- **Note:** All shot multipliers remain active throughout, so this is the single highest-value scoring phase in the game

## 📋 Quick Reference: Strategic Priorities

**Ball 1:** Super Skill Shot, start 3 songs, build multipliers, begin Toy Box locks.
**Ball 2:** Cash Toybox Multiball, qualify Elevator, keep cycling songs.
**Ball 3+:** Stack locks into a 6-ball Elevator round, finish songs, chase Medley → Final Tour.

**Target High-Score Ballpark:** **80–150 million points** is a strong, achievable goal; serious runs into the **300M+** range require full song completion and disciplined multiball stacking.
