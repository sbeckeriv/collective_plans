# 🐇 Playboy - Strategy Guide

*Stern Pinball, 2002 — Designed by Dwight Sullivan & George Gomez*

> **"Collect the centerfolds. Unlock the mansion. Score big."**

Playboy (Stern, 2002) is a Whitestar-era licensed machine with ramps, a center spinner, Playmate collection modes, a mansion progression system, and 3-ball multiball. The game rewards players who methodically collect Playmates via ramp and orbit shots while keeping the center spinner spinning for multiplier value. Our goal: **200M–400M** through efficient Playmate collection, Hot Tub Party multiball, and mansion modes.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Shoot the spinner** early and often — it's your fastest points-per-second shot
- [ ] **Complete left and right ramps** to collect Playmates and advance the mansion
- [ ] **Lock balls in the Grotto** to start Hot Tub Party multiball
- [ ] **Collect all 12 Playmates** to light Mansion Party wizard mode
- [ ] **Hit the center saucer** when lit for mystery awards and mansion progression
- [ ] **Watch your mansion floor** — each completion step lights more valuable shots

## 🐰 Core Mechanics

### Skill Shot

| Skill Shot | How | Reward |
|---|---|---|
| **Spinner skill shot** | Plunge with authority to hit the center spinner | Points + spinner multiplier |
| **Lane skill shot** | Controlled short plunge to lit lane | Bonus multiplier step |

### Spinner (Center)

The center spinner is the best repeatable low-risk shot on the machine. Each spin scores points, and hitting it while the multiplier is lit (activated by ramp shots) increases its value dramatically. In multiball, the spinner can be the highest single-ball scoring shot.

### Playmate Collection

There are 12 Playmates to collect, each tied to a specific shot or orbit:

| Playmate Group | How to Collect |
|---|---|
| Left ramp Playmates | Left ramp completions |
| Right ramp Playmates | Right ramp completions |
| Orbit Playmates | Full orbit shots (left or right) |
| Saucer Playmates | Center saucer when lit |

Each collection awards points and advances the mansion.

### Mansion Progression

Collecting Playmates fills mansion floors. Each floor completion:
- Lights additional features
- Increases jackpot values for multiball
- Moves you closer to Mansion Party wizard mode

### Grotto (Ball Lock)

The Grotto is the ball lock area. Lock two balls here (lit after ramp progression) to start Hot Tub Party Multiball.

---

## 🎳 Multiball

### Hot Tub Party (3-Ball Multiball)

**How to start:** Lock 2 balls in the Grotto (requires left ramp progression to light).

**Jackpot structure:**
- Shoot ramps for jackpots (5M–20M each, escalating)
- **Super Jackpot:** Shoot the Grotto after collecting all major jackpots — 30M–60M+
- Spinner scores elevated during multiball — exploit it between ramp shots

> 🏆 **Optimal stack:** Have 2 Playmates remaining to collect when you start multiball — each ramp shot during multiball collects a Playmate AND a jackpot.

### Mansion Multiball (Bonus Multiball)

Triggered by specific mansion floor completions. 2-ball brief multiball with mansion-themed jackpots.

## 🔄 Progression Systems

- Each Playmate collected adds to your mansion and bonus base
- Repeating Playmate shots after all 12 are collected awards bonus points instead
- Mansion floors unlock increasingly valuable features; floor 4+ makes jackpots significantly larger

## 🧙 Wizard Mode

### Mansion Party

**Requirement:** Collect all 12 Playmates.

**Gameplay:** All shots lit, elevated values across the board, timed sequence. The Grotto during Mansion Party awards the final mega-jackpot.

## 📊 Shot Analysis

| Shot | Value | Risk |
|---|---|---|
| Center spinner | Fast points + multiplier | Low |
| Left ramp | Playmate + jackpot | Low |
| Right ramp | Playmate + jackpot | Low |
| Full orbit | Playmate + spinner feed | Low |
| Grotto | Ball lock / super jackpot | Medium |
| Center saucer | Mystery + Playmate | Low |

### Dangerous Shots

- **Grotto approach** — steep angle; weak shot can miss and hit slingshot, kicking to drain
- **Right outlane** — fast exit from right orbit if ball slips past flipper

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Balls 1–2
1. Spinner for immediate points
2. Left ramp × 3 (light Grotto)
3. Lock ball 1, collect Playmates via right ramp
4. Lock ball 2, start Hot Tub Party

### Ball 3
1. Exploit multiball — alternate ramps for jackpots
2. Spin the spinner aggressively during multiball
3. Post-multiball: chase Mansion Party if 8+ Playmates collected

| Solid | 100M–200M |

## 💡 Pro Tips

> **The spinner is your metronome.** Hit it every time you pass — a well-spun spinner during multiplier adds 10M+ passively over a full ball.

> **Don't skip the orbit shots.** Orbit Playmates are often missed by players obsessed with ramps — two orbit shots = two Playmates = faster Mansion Party access.

> **Trap on the left flipper between ramp attempts.** Playboy's right side drains fast if you panic-flip — compose yourself between shots.
