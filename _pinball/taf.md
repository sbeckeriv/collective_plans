# 🖤 The Addams Family - Strategy Guide

*Bally, 1992 — Designed by Pat Lawlor*

> **"You rang?"**

The Addams Family (TAF, Bally 1992) is the best-selling pinball machine of all time — a WPC masterpiece from Pat Lawlor with Thing (a disembodied hand that grabs the ball), a swamp kick-out, the Electric Chair, the vault, the bear kick-targets, the train, and the infamous electric chair multiball. TAF has deep, layered rules with 13 mansion rooms, three multiballs, and a wizard mode that has never been reached in competition without exceptional play. Our goal: **500M–2B** by completing mansion rooms, stacking multiballs, and reaching the Showdown.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Shoot the left ramp** (Train) to advance the mansion lights (Thing Flips feature)
- [ ] **Collect the Swamp Kickout** when lit for mid-ball bonuses
- [ ] **Hit the center scoop** (Thing Seat) to advance mansion rooms and start modes
- [ ] **Complete the GREED targets** (left standups) for instant mode advances
- [ ] **Light and start Electric Chair** multiball via the left outlane kickback sequence
- [ ] **Stack Electric Chair with an active mansion room** for peak jackpot values

## 🏚️ Core Mechanics

### Skill Shot

| Skill Shot | How | Reward |
|---|---|---|
| **Thing Skill Shot** | Plunge to the lit rollover lane | Mansion room advance + points |
| **Swamp** | Short soft plunge | Swamp kickout access |

### 13 Mansion Rooms

The core of TAF — thirteen rooms must be lit and completed for the Showdown wizard mode:

| Room | Shot |
|---|---|
| Festers Filibuster | Swamp kickout |
| Cousin Its Hidden Camera | Left loop |
| Mamushka | Right loop combo |
| Pugsleys Playroom | Bear kick left |
| Wednesday's Playroom | Bear kick right |
| Séance | Thing seat (center scoop) |
| Morticia's Garden | Right ramp |
| Thing Bucks | Train (left ramp) |
| Granny's Gunpowder | GREED targets |
| The Vault | Vault shot (center) |
| Lite Extra Ball | Left loop × 2 |
| Super Game | Thing seat × 2 |
| Showdown | All 12 rooms complete |

Light each room's insert by hitting the required shot, then collect via Thing Seat (center scoop) or the specified collect shot.

### Thing (The Hand)

Thing sits in a box on the right side. When called (by completing certain shots), Thing reaches out and grabs the ball:
- Scoring depends on what Thing grabs mid-flight (the ball type, game state)
- **Thing Flips:** After certain modes, Thing physically flips the ball back into play
- Always shoot toward Thing when the hand indicator is lit

### GREED Targets (Left Standup Bank)

G-R-E-E-D: Five standup targets on the left side. Completing them:
- Instantly completes a lit mansion room (Granny's Gunpowder)
- Awards GREED bonus points
- Resets for a second completion (higher value)

The GREED targets are your "skip the hard shot" tool — use them to shortcut difficult room completions.

### Electric Chair (Left Side)

The Electric Chair is accessed by the left side target. When it's active:
- Ball is caged in the electric chair
- DMD shows Lurch flipping the switch
- Awards randomized prizes (extra ball, mansion rooms, multiball start)

### Swamp Kickout

The swamp is a center-left kickout hole. When lit:
- Collects the Fester's Filibuster mansion room
- Awards mid-ball bonus
- Safe shot with predictable eject direction

### Bear Kicks (Left and Right)

Two bear kick-out targets on the sides. Completing the left and right bears:
- Lights Pugsley's and Wednesday's rooms
- Awards bear bonus points

## 🎳 Multiballs

### Electric Chair Multiball

**How to start:** Advance the left kickback via left outlane balls (or specific mansion progression), then activate the Electric Chair to trigger multiball.

**Jackpot structure:**
- 3-ball multiball
- Jackpots via the vault + right ramp
- **Super Jackpot:** Complete all three jackpot shots → Thing Seat → 50M–200M+
- Electric Chair activating during multiball adds bonus to all active jackpots

### Mamushka Multiball (Mode)

**How to start:** Complete the Mamushka sequence (right loop → right loop → thing seat in succession).

**Gameplay:** 3-ball frenzy with elevated loop values. Less structured than Electric Chair multiball but very fast to trigger.

### Showdown (Ultimate)

**Requirement:** All 13 mansion rooms complete.

This is the rarest wizard mode in competitive pinball history. No standard scoring — it's a special sequence with game-ending jackpot potential.

## 🔄 Progression Systems

- **Mansion rooms:** Permanent within game; each completion reduces Showdown distance
- **GREED targets:** Reset after each completion; repeat for escalating GREED bonus
- **Bonus multiplier:** Advanced by left loop completions
- **Extra balls:** Multiple opportunities — Lite Extra Ball room, Electric Chair prize, Mamushka

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Ball 1
1. Skill shot → mansion room 1
2. Left ramp × 2 (Thing Bucks)
3. GREED targets × 5 (Granny's room)
4. Thing seat → collect 2 rooms

### Ball 2
1. Mamushka sequence (right loop × 2 → seat)
2. Electric Chair → multiball

4. Complete 4 more rooms

### Ball 3

## 💡 Pro Tips

> **GREED is your escape valve.** When you're stuck on a difficult room, GREED targets are available and easy — they skip the room completely. Use them freely.

> **Mamushka starts fast.** Two right loop shots and a seat — three shots total — starts a multiball. It's the fastest multiball in the game and worth pursuing on every ball.

> **The left kickback is essential.** TAF's left outlane is a common drain path. The kickback saves a ball that would otherwise be catastrophic. Relight it aggressively.
