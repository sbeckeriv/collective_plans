# 💼 Pulp Fiction - Strategy Guide

*Chicago Gaming Company, 2023 — Premium/SE Edition*

> **"Zed's dead, baby. Now stack those jackpots."**

Pulp Fiction (CGC, 2023) translates Tarantino's interconnected anthology structure directly into pinball: three stories (Vincent & Jules, The Boxer, The Bonnie Situation) each with their own shot sequences, characters, and jackpot payouts. The Premium/SE edition adds a spinning briefcase toy and a moving shot mechanism on the playfield. Our goal: **500M–1.5B** by completing all three stories and triggering Royale With Cheese wizard mode.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Shoot the left ramp** to advance the Vincent & Jules story (your primary progression)
- [ ] **Hit the briefcase** to collect mystery awards and advance story interlocks
- [ ] **Shoot Butch's Boxer modes** via the center shots to earn big jackpots
- [ ] **Complete The Bonnie Situation** via right ramp sequence
- [ ] **Stack all three active stories** before triggering the story completion bonus
- [ ] **Go for Royale With Cheese** by completing all stories — the wizard mode jackpots are enormous

---

## 🎬 Core Mechanics

### Skill Shot

| Skill Shot | How | Reward |
|---|---|---|
| **Briefcase skill shot** | Timed plunge to hit the spinning briefcase | Briefcase bonus + mystery award |
| **Lane skill shot** | Hold flipper to select lit lane | Bonus multiplier advance |

### Three Stories

**Story 1 — Vincent & Jules:**
- Primary shots: Left ramp repeatedly
- Key moments: Marvin in the car, the diner holdup, the BMF wallet
- Jackpots tied to left ramp value, escalates with each loop

**Story 2 — The Boxer (Butch):**
- Primary shots: Center shots (pops area feed, center scoop)
- Key moments: The watch, the fight, the motorcycle escape
- Elevated jackpots mid-story for fight sequences

**Story 3 — The Bonnie Situation (Winston Wolfe):**
- Primary shots: Right ramp + scoop combination
- Key moments: Cleaning the car, the diner resolution
- Highest base jackpot values of the three stories

> 🎯 **Pro Tip:** The three stories can be **active simultaneously**. Triggering story 3 while 1 and 2 are mid-progression means every shot counts toward multiple stories.

### Briefcase (Toy)

The spinning briefcase in the center lights randomly (after sequences) and awards:
- Mystery prizes (extra ball, score bonuses, mode boosts)
- Story advancement for whichever is active
- Briefcase jackpot (escalating, collected mid-story)

## 🎳 Multiball

### Gimp Multiball

**How to start:** Lock balls via the left side lock lane (lit by specific story progression).

**Jackpot structure:**
- 3-ball multiball, all shots at elevated values
- **Super Jackpot:** Shoot the briefcase after completing the jackpot loop — 50M–120M+
- Stacks fully with active story modes

### Zed's Dead Multiball

**How to start:** Complete specific Bonnie Situation story beats.

**Gameplay:** 2-ball intense multiball with time-limited super jackpot available immediately.

---

## 🔄 Progression Systems

- **Story completion** → each completed story permanently increases jackpot base values for remaining stories
- **Briefcase hits** → accumulate a "glowing briefcase" value that pays out at wizard mode
- **Combo system** → consecutive ramp shots in one story multiply the next jackpot

## 🔀 Stacking & Combos

| Stack | Effect |
|---|---|
| All 3 stories active + Gimp Multiball | Every shot scores across all three stories AND multiball jackpots |
| Right ramp combo during Bonnie Situation | Each consecutive right ramp 2× the story jackpot |
| Briefcase mystery during multiball | Can award instant Super Jackpot lighting |

## 🧙 Wizard Mode

### Royale With Cheese

**Requirement:** Complete all three stories.

**Gameplay:** Timed frenzy mode, all shots lit at massive values. The briefcase shot at the climax awards the stored briefcase jackpot (cumulative from the entire game). This is where 1B+ games are built.

## 📊 Shot Analysis

| Shot | Value | Risk |
|---|---|---|
| Left ramp | Story 1 advance (primary) | Low |
| Right ramp | Story 3 advance | Low |
| Center scoop | Story 2 advance | Medium |
| Briefcase toy | Mystery + story boost | Low (toy-assisted) |
| Left lock lane | Ball lock | Medium |
| Pop bumper area | Passive scoring | None |

### Dangerous Shots

- **Center scoop** — if underpowered, ball can rattle out toward right drain
- **Briefcase approach** — spinning toy can deflect ball unpredictably; right flipper preferred

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Ball 1
1. Briefcase skill shot
2. Left ramp × 4 to advance Story 1 to the BMF Wallet
3. Trigger Story 2 via center
4. Lock ball for Gimp Multiball

### Ball 2
1. Trigger multiball if ready
2. During multiball: left ramp loops for jackpots + story continuation
3. Start Story 3 (right ramp × 3)

### Ball 3
1. Complete all stories if close
2. Chase Royale With Cheese
3. Collect briefcase jackpot at wizard mode climax

### Score Targets

| Performance | Score |
|---|---|
| Beginner | 80M–200M |
| Solid | 250M–600M |
| Strong | 600M–1.2B |
| Elite (Royale) | 1.5B+ |

## 💡 Pro Tips

> **All three stories can run simultaneously** — don't wait for Story 1 to fully complete before starting 2 and 3. The interlock is intentional.

> **The briefcase is a mood booster, not your main scoring engine.** Shoot it when convenient, but don't detour from ramp loops to chase it.

> **Gimp Multiball stacked with all three stories is your peak moment.** Set it up: advance all three stories to mid-progression, then start Gimp. Every single shot pays out three ways.
