# 🌀 Out of Sight - Strategy Guide

*Gottlieb, 1974 — Designed by Ed Krynski*

> **"Get protected first. Then chase the twice-lit target."**

Out of Sight (also known as Far Out) is a psychedelic-themed Gottlieb EM with a clean, disciplined strategy: secure your outlane protection first, then hunt for the single drop target that's lit for double the multiplier. Since drop targets don't reset once completed, every hit counts permanently toward your final bonus. Our goal: light both side lanes for protection, then methodically chase the twice-lit 5,000-point target across both banks.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Aim at the top lanes first** until you have both side lanes lit — this is your safety setup before taking any risks
- [ ] **Left side lane lights the left kickback; right side lane lights the right-hand gate** — both are outlane protection features
- [ ] **Once protected, shoot for the drop targets**
- [ ] **One bank is always lit for 10x** (base value 500); within that bank, **one specific target is lit for an additional 10x factor**, making it worth **5,000** — always shoot for this twice-lit target
- [ ] **The red star rollover in the center** changes which target is the twice-lit one — track this before committing to a shot
- [ ] **Remember drop targets don't reset once completed** — every hit is permanent progress, so there's no rush to clear a bank before working the other

---

## 🌀 Core Mechanics

### Protection First

| Lane | Effect |
|---|---|
| Left side lane | Lights the **left kickback** |
| Right side lane | Lights the **right-hand gate** |

> 🎯 **Pro Tip — Core Strategy:** Aim at the top lanes until both side lanes are lit before taking any meaningful risks elsewhere. Once "protected," you can shoot the drop targets with much less fear of an immediate drain.

### The Twice-Lit Target System

- **One bank of 5 drop targets is always lit for 10x** (base value 500 per target)
- **Within that bank, one specific target carries a second 10x factor**, making it worth **5,000** when hit
- The **red star rollover** in the center of the playfield **advances which target is the twice-lit one**, one step at a time

> 💰 **Pro Tip:** Always prioritize the twice-lit target over any other drop target — it's worth 10x more than a standard hit in the same bank.

### Bonus System

- **Targets, top lanes, and (when lit) bottom return lanes** all increase your end-of-ball bonus
- **Completing all targets on both banks lights Double Bonus**
- **Your last ball (normally Ball 5) automatically gets Double Bonus**, regardless of target completion

### Drop Targets Don't Reset

Unlike many machines, **completed drop targets stay down for the rest of the game** — there's no resetting bank to re-clear. This means your progress is cumulative and permanent; work toward completing both banks over the course of the whole game rather than worrying about "wasting" a bank on an early ball.

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **Shooting drop targets before securing side-lane protection** | Higher drain risk without the kickback/gate active | Always light both side lanes first |
| **Pop bumper unpredictability** (3 bumpers) | Standard mid-70s bumper chaos | Treat as incidental scoring |
| **Losing track of which target is twice-lit** | Wastes shots on lower-value targets | Watch the red star rollover state before committing to a drop target shot |

---

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Ball

1. Work the top lanes to light both side lanes for protection
2. Once protected, start identifying and chasing the twice-lit target
3. Pick up bottom return lane value when lit

### Mid Ball

1. Continue working both drop target banks, prioritizing the twice-lit target in each
2. Remember progress is permanent — there's no penalty for splitting attention between both banks
3. Track the red star rollover to keep finding the current twice-lit target

### Late Ball

1. Push to complete both drop target banks for Double Bonus
2. Remember your final ball gets Double Bonus automatically regardless
3. Bank your accumulated bonus at game end

| **Red star rollover** | Check smooth, accurate target-cycling | Central to identifying the current twice-lit target |
