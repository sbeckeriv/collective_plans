# 🎸 DOLLY PARTON Pinball Machine - Strategy Guide

*Bally, 1978 — Classic Electromechanical Simplicity*

> **Spell the Queen of Country for your fortune.**

Dolly Parton is a straightforward, letter-chasing early solid-state machine that rewards disciplined target shooting and skillful use of limited multipliers. With its classic drop-target bank and spinner mechanic, it builds steadily across balls rather than exploding in single modes. Our goal: reach **150,000–250,000 points**, a realistic competitive score on a well-maintained machine.

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Start with drop targets:** Get all four inline drop targets completed on Ball 1 to light 5X multiplier
- [ ] **Hit DOLLY targets first:** Five standup targets in upper left; hit each once to spell D-O-L-L-Y
- [ ] **Collect PARTON letters:** Top saucer (skillshot) or via drop target completion; build second set
- [ ] **Use the spinner:** Once lit by first drop target, it's your best money shot late game
- [ ] **Collect bonus at ball end:** Multiplied bonus carries forward; accumulates across balls
- [ ] **Secure extra balls early:** Available only from drop target completion—earn on Balls 1 & 2
- [ ] **Avoid center drain:** Pop bumpers bounce wildly; play for controlled flipper shots

## 🎱 Core Mechanics

### Inline Drop Targets (4 Targets)

| Target | Impact | Points |
|--------|--------|--------|
| **1st (Lit spinner)** | Lights the spinner to 1,000/spin | 200 pts |
| **2nd (2X multiplier)** | Lights 2X bonus multiplier | 400 pts |
| **3rd (3X multiplier)** | Lights 3X bonus multiplier | 600 pts |
| **4th (5X multiplier & EB)** | Lights 5X multiplier + Extra Ball + Special | 800 pts |

**Critical Strategy:** Complete all four targets early (ideally Ball 1) to lock in 5X multiplier for the entire game. Once completed, hitting any drop target again scores **20,000 points**—your second-best shot behind the spinner <kcite ref="1"/>.

### DOLLY Targets (Upper Left Bank)

Five standup targets spell D-O-L-L-Y. Each hits 400 points initially; after completion, each additional hit = **1,000 points**. These targets are partially blocked by the three pop bumpers, making direct flipper shots essential <kcite ref="2"/>.

### PARTON Sequence (Top Saucer + Drop Targets)

Letters P-A-R-T-O-N collected via:
- **Top saucer (skillshot):** 5,000 points + advances PARTON sequence. Notoriously difficult to make—over half of plunge attempts miss <kcite ref="3"/>.
- **Drop target completion:** Each drop target automatically spots the next PARTON letter when completed <kcite ref="2"/>.

### The Spinner

- **Unlit:** 100 points per spin
- **Lit (after 1st drop target):** **1,000 points per spin**
- Best strategy: once lit and you've completed drop targets, camp the spinner on your upper flipper and fire repeatedly

**Pro tip:** If the spinner registers many spins per shot (15+, ideally 20+), spinning all day becomes viable <kcite ref="1"/>.

### Thumper Bumpers (3 Bumpers)

Located in the upper-middle playfield. Score **100 points each**, always lit. They block direct shots at DOLLY targets but provide occasional bonus points and nudge opportunities <kcite ref="2"/>.

### Bonus System

**Base Bonus:** 2,000 points per letter in DOLLY PARTON collected <kcite ref="1"/>.

**Bonus Multipliers:** Built via drop targets:
- Complete 2nd drop = 2X
- Complete 3rd drop = 3X  
- Complete 4th drop = 5X

**Locked Bonuses:**
- One complete DOLLY PARTON set = 22,000 bonus points
- Two complete sets = 44,000 bonus points

**Theoretical Maximum:** (44K + 10×2K) × 5X = **320,000 points end-of-ball bonus** <kcite ref="1"/>.

**Carryover:** Accumulated bonus and multipliers carry from ball to ball—this is the engine of your final score <kcite ref="2"/>.

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

### High-Risk Areas

| Shot | Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------|-----------|
| **Center plunge** | Pop bumpers create chaotic bounces toward center drain | Aim for upper flippers; soft, controlled plunge |
| **Missed left slingshot** | Can deflect directly to right outlane | Use flipper control to trap upper flipper first |
| **Pop bumper area** | Blocks DOLLY shots; unpredictable bounces to outlanes | Avoid unless building bonus only |
| **DOLLY standup targets** | Behind bumpers; aggressive shots risky | Use gentle flipper angle or wait for bumper rebound |

### Safe Control Positions

- **Upper flipper cradle:** Best for planning next controlled shot
- **Lower flipper trap:** Setup for slingshot or gentle nudge toward upper targets
- **Slingshot feed:** Right slingshot feeds predictably to lower flipper

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Ball 1 (Foundation)

1. **Make the drop targets** (all four if safe, at least first two for spinner)
   - Completes PARTON letters automatically
   - Locks in multiplier escalation
   
2. **Hit DOLLY targets** once each (5 shots)
   - Aim around bumpers using nudge and flipper finesse
   - Each = 400 pts, plus letter progression

3. **Build bonus base** via easy shots (spins, bumpers, dolly reruns)
   - Goal: collect at least 4–5 letters total for 8K–10K base bonus

4. **Don't chase high-risk shots** on Ball 1
   - Preserve multiplier progression; losing this ball early hurts

**Target Score:** 25K–50K

### Ball 2 (Acceleration)

1. **Spinner spam** if lit and working well (register 15+ spins)
   - Each = 1,000 pts
   - Build bonus + points simultaneously

2. **Work toward second DOLLY PARTON set** if close
   - Replay drop targets (now 20K each) and standup targets (1,000 each)
   
3. **Collect end-of-ball bonus**
   - Even at 2X or 3X, this is now substantial

4. **Earn extra balls** if available (drop target EB opportunity)
   - Only one extra ball per ball in play <kcite ref="2"/>

**Target Score:** 50K–100K

### Ball 3+ (Multiplication Phase)

1. **Maximize spinner spins** on every ball
   - With 5X multiplier approaching bonus, spinner value climbs

2. **Complete second letter set** if not done
   - +22,000 locked bonus for next ball

3. **End-of-ball bonus collection** is now your primary scoring engine
   - At 5X, each letter = 10K end-of-ball (2K × 5X)
   - Collecting 8 letters at 5X = 80K per ball

4. **Extra balls extend your window**
   - Each extra = one more bonus collection

**Target Score per ball:** 40K–80K (accumulate across balls)

### Final Push (Ball 4+)

- Play conservatively for ball control
- Collect every end-of-ball bonus
- Aim for **150,000–250,000+ total game score** <kcite ref="3"/>

## 🎯 Shot Analysis

### The Spinner (Your Money Shot)

**Why it matters:** After the first drop target is completed, each spin = 1,000 points. With 5X multiplier pending, spinning 20+ times on one shot generates 20,000+ points in seconds.

**How to execute:**
- Complete the first drop target early
- Trap ball on upper flipper
- Angle lower flipper toward spinner lane
- Fire and hold flipper; watch the reel count your spins
- Execute multiple times per ball once you have control

### Drop Targets (20K Each After Completion)

**Why they matter:** Completing all four builds your 5X multiplier and spots PARTON letters. Re-hitting any target = 20,000 points.

**How to execute:**
- Early game: shoot carefully to complete the sequence
- Late game: re-hit any drop target (especially easier shots) for 20K bonus value
- Faster than trying to complete letter sets again

### DOLLY Targets (1,000 Points Each After First Hit)

**Why they matter:** After spelling DOLLY once, each standup target = 1,000 points—your third-best money shot.

**How to execute:**
- Use nudging to bounce ball toward targets
- Aim around bumpers using flipper angle control
- On late balls with 5X, these are valuable secondary targets

### Top Saucer Skillshot (5,000 Points + Letter)

**Why it matters:** Advances PARTON sequence; difficult but rewarding.

**How to execute:**
- Plunge ball smoothly up center lane
- Over 50% of plunges miss; improve hit rate through practice
- Worth attempting on Ball 1 for letter progression; less critical if drop targets already spot letters

## 🏆 Extra Ball Opportunities

| Condition | Award |
|-----------|-------|
| **4th drop target completion** | Extra Ball (lights on target 4 only) |
| **Only one EB per ball in play** | Cannot stack EB lights; 4th target EB resets each ball |

**Strategy:** Earn your first extra ball on Ball 1 by completing all drop targets. Use Ball 2 to attempt a second extra ball if possible (some machines allow EB from Special). Extra balls extend your bonus-collection window significantly.

## ⚙️ Setup Recommendations

| Setting | Recommendation | Reason |
|---------|---|---|
| **Playfield pitch** | 6.0°–6.5° | Standard for early solid-state; prevents tilt-forward drain |
| **Flipper strength** | Medium (default) | Balanced for precision; high strength creates wild bounces |
| **Bumper energy** | Medium to firm | Bumpers should provide a fair challenge, not brutal deflection |
| **Slingshot tension** | Standard | Should feed predictably to lower flipper |
| **Ball speed (nudge sensitivity)** | Moderate | Early machines punish nudge abuse; play with restraint |

> 💡 **Spinner Quality Matters:** Check if your machine's spinner is "juicy" (high spin count per shot). If it registers 20+ spins consistently, spinner strategy becomes dominant. If low (5–10 spins), prioritize drop targets and standup targets instead <kcite ref="1"/>.

## 📖 Strategic Progression Summary

**Multiplier-Focused Play:**
1. Ball 1: Complete all four drop targets → Lock 5X multiplier
2. Ball 2: Spam spinner; build bonus letters
3. Ball 3+: End-of-ball bonus becomes massive (2K × letters × 5X)

**Conservative Play (if drop targets risky):**
1. Ball 1: Complete first two drop targets → Light spinner at minimum
2. Ball 2: Spinner spins + careful DOLLY shots
3. Ball 3: Build multiplier slowly across balls; accept 2X–3X target

**Spinner-Heavy Play (if spinner registers 15+ spins):**
1. Complete first drop target to light spinner
2. Earn second-set letters via standup targets and careful drop target replays
3. Spam spinner every ball once multiplier is active; ignore other shots

## 💡 Key Learnings

- **Multiplier is everything:** Unlike modern games, your 5X multiplier is a one-time unlock. Preserve it by completing all four drop targets early.
- **Letters accumulate slowly:** Spelling DOLLY PARTON twice within a game is realistic but requires discipline across 3–4 balls.
- **Spinner can dominate:** A quality spinner (20+ spins per shot) can outpace all other scoring once lit.
- **End-of-ball bonus is your engine:** This is not a "multiball game"—it's a bonus accumulation game. Plan your shots to maximize letters collected by end of ball.
- **Skill shot is optional:** While the top saucer is flashy, reliable drop target completion is more valuable than chasing a 50%-miss saucer.
