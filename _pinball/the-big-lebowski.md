### 🎱 Skill Shots

**Three skill shot options available at ball launch** <kcite ref="21"/>:

| Skill Shot             | How To Execute                                  | Reward                                                                                        |
| ---------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Standard**           | Normal plunge → hit lit rollover in MAUDE lanes | Use flippers to move blinking letter before ball arrives                                      |
| **Rug Mode** 🔥         | Hold LEFT flipper + plunge                      | Ball feeds to flippers via left loop. Shoot rug within 5 seconds to open rug hole immediately |
| **Let's Go Bowling** ⭐ | Hold RIGHT flipper + plunge                     | Ball goes to upper playfield. Hit bowling loop to instantly light "Let's Go Bowling"          |

> **💡 Pro Tip:** The Super Skill Shot (bowling) is generally the best choice - it's easy to hit the loop and instantly qualifies bowling, giving you immediate progress toward wizard mode <kcite ref="80"/>.

---

### 🎬 Rug Modes

**How to Start:** Hit the rug multiple times to roll it back. When fully rolled, shoot the revealed hole to start a mode <kcite ref="21"/>.

| Mode                    | Objective                  | Tips                                  |
| ----------------------- | -------------------------- | ------------------------------------- |
| **Where is the Money?** | Hit the rug multiple times | Keep bashing!                         |
| **Ransom Note**         | Hit any standup targets    | Easy mode - any target works          |
| **Female Form**         | Hit pop bumpers or spinner | Shoot Maude/Donny loops to reach pops |
| **Ringer Drop Off**     | Shoot both ramps           | Left and right ramps                  |
| **Bash the Car**        | Hit car on upper playfield | Shoot Jesus/Walter to get up there    |
| **Honor Donny**         | Shoot Donny or Maude loops | Blue inserts will blink               |

**Mode Selection:** Pop bumpers cycle through which mode is selected <kcite ref="21"/>.

**Timer:** All modes have 60-second countdown. Fill progress bar, then shoot rug hole again to complete <kcite ref="21"/>.

> **⚠️ Warning:** The rug is a dangerous shot with unpredictable ball returns. Only commit when the rug has already rolled back partially <kcite ref="80"/>.

### 🚗 Car Modes

**How to Qualify:** Hit the two blue parking standup targets on the upper playfield to light all 4 parking inserts <kcite ref="21"/>.

**How to Start:** Shoot the upper playfield scoop when Car Mode is lit.

| Mode                | Objective                                | Notes                                    |
| ------------------- | ---------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| **Kill Dude's Car** | Bash the car repeatedly                  | Upper flipper stays active               |
| **Car Chase**       | Shoot the loop repeatedly, DON'T hit car | Hitting car loses speed                  |
| **Car Impound**     | Bash the car to free it                  | Upper flipper deactivates after each hit |

**Progress Carries Over:** Car mode progress transfers between balls <kcite ref="21"/>.

---

### 🥃 White Russian Multipliers

**How to Collect:** Hit the 4 standup targets on the right side of the playfield <kcite ref="21"/>.

| Step | Action                                                    |
| ---- | --------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1    | Complete all 4 White Russian targets                      |
| 2    | A White Russian starts blinking at character shots        |
| 3    | First shot you make lights that White Russian permanently |
| 4    | That shot now has a multiplier active                     |

**Important Details:**
- Each White Russian lasts for **4 shots** (like 4 sips of a drink) <kcite ref="21"/>
- After 4 shots, the multiplier ends
- **Free Refills:** Completing targets again can refill partially-used White Russians
- Multipliers transfer between balls

> **💡 Pro Tip:** Stack White Russian multipliers with Bowling Ball multipliers for maximum scoring!

---

### ⭕ Mark It Zero

**How to Qualify:** Light Z-E-R-O using the in/outlane rollovers. Use flipper buttons to move unlit inserts <kcite ref="21"/>.

**How to Collect:** Shoot the left saucer when Mark It Zero is lit.

**Important:** This is a TIMED award - it will expire if not collected quickly!

**Walter's Bowling Ball Perk:** If you have Walter's bowling ball, Mark It Zero becomes untimed and carries over between balls <kcite ref="21"/>.

> **💡 Pro Tip:** If Mark It Zero is lit when you accidentally hit the saucer for Character Multiball, you can CANCEL the multiball by pressing the Launch button immediately <kcite ref="21"/>.

## 🎱 Multiballs

### Main Multiball (3-Ball)

**How to Qualify** <kcite ref="21"/>:

| Step | Action                                  |
| ---- | --------------------------------------- |
| 1    | Hit LIGHT standup (left of left ramp)   |
| 2    | Hit LOCK standup (right of left ramp)   |
| 3    | Shoot left ramp (The Dude) to lock ball |
| 4    | Repeat 3 times to start multiball       |

**Jackpot Structure:**
- All 5 character shots + rug are lit red for jackpots
- Complete all 6 shots to light **Super Jackpot** at left ramp
- After Super Jackpot, all shots relight
- Subsequent Super Jackpots can be collected at the upper playfield loop

**Add-a-Ball:** Shoot the scoop 3 times during multiball <kcite ref="21"/>.

**Difficulty Scaling:** First multiball requires 1 hit per lock target. Second multiball requires 2 hits, etc. <kcite ref="21"/>

> **💡 Pro Tip:** Don't aim directly for lock standups - they're dangerous shots. Let random caroms light them, then lock immediately when lit <kcite ref="80"/>.

---

### Character Multiball (2-5 Ball)

**How to Qualify:** Spell each character's name by making their shot repeatedly <kcite ref="21"/>.

**How to Start:** Shoot the left saucer when Character Multiball is lit.

| Characters Lit | Balls in Play |
| -------------- | ------------- |
| 1              | 2 balls       |
| 2              | 3 balls       |
| 3              | 4 balls       |
| 4              | 5 balls       |
| 5              | 5 balls       |

**Jackpot Structure:**
- Character shots light in their color for jackpots
- After shooting all active character jackpots, they relight
- Shooting the upper playfield scoop relights all jackpots OR adds 25K if already lit

**Side Character Jackpots** (during Character Multiball) <kcite ref="21"/>:

| Character        | How to Collect           |
| ---------------- | ------------------------ |
| Brandt           | 2 Saucer hits            |
| Bunny            | 8 Right slingshot hits   |
| Jackie Treehorn  | 2 Trampoline target hits |
| Knox Harrington  | 10 Jet bumper hits       |
| Marty            | 4 Lock target hits       |
| Nihilists        | 4 Parking target hits    |
| The Big Lebowski | 8 Left slingshot hits    |
| The Stranger     | 2 Scoop hits             |
| Thugs            | 2 Rug hits               |

> **💡 Pro Tip:** Start Character Multiball with as many characters lit as possible - it increases both ball count AND jackpot values. Characters that start together multiply each other's jackpots <kcite ref="21"/>.

---

### Nihilist Showdown

**How to Qualify:** Hit the 3 Nihilist standup targets (left/right of rug, left of Walter ramp) multiple times <kcite ref="21"/>.

**How to Start:** Shoot the scoop when Nihilist symbol is flashing.

**Gameplay:**
- Wave 1: Hit each Nihilist target once
- Wave 2: Hit each target twice
- Wave 3+: Continues escalating

**Scoring:**
- Wave Jackpots: 200K, 400K, 600K, etc.
- Nihilist targets: 50K each
- Arrow shots during waves: +50K to Wave Jackpot value
- Completing a wave lights Add-a-Ball at scoop

**Progress Saves:** Starting Nihilist Showdown again resumes at your previous wave <kcite ref="21"/>.

### Multiball Rules (All Multiballs)

These rules apply during any multiball <kcite ref="21"/>:

- ✅ MAUDE rollovers still increase bonus multiplier
- ✅ Z-E-R-O lanes still work for Mark It Zero
- ✅ Spinner still progresses toward Mystery
- ✅ Upper playfield ramp gives 1.5X-5X multiplier (decreases over time)
- ✅ 2.5 second grace period when down to last ball
- ⚠️ Newly plunged balls go around left loop (not through rollovers)

## 🎳 Bowling Alley

### How to Qualify

Shoot the bowling loop on the upper playfield to spell B-O-W-L-I-N-G <kcite ref="21"/>.

### How to Start

Shoot the upper playfield scoop OR left saucer when "Let's Go Bowling" is lit.

### Bowling Challenges

| Challenge Type          | Objective                                                 |
| ----------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Spare or Strike**     | Knock down all 10 pins (1 ball = strike, 2 balls = spare) |
| **Hit the Roaming Pin** | Hit the moving pin                                        |
| **Hit the Gap**         | Roll ball through moving gap WITHOUT hitting pins         |
| **Complete the Split**  | Pick up the split                                         |

### Bowling Ball Perks

Each character's bowling ball grants a permanent perk until wizard mode <kcite ref="21"/>:

| Character      | Perk                                                                    |
| -------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 🔵 **Donny**    | Easier Car Modes - fewer parking hits needed, modes start with progress |
| 🟠 **The Dude** | Easier Rug Modes - fewer rug hits needed, modes start with progress     |
| 🟡 **Maude**    | Super Jackpot Pre-Lit - Super Jackpot lit at multiball start            |
| 🩷 **Jesus**    | Easier BOWLING spelling - fewer loop hits required                      |
| 🟢 **Walter**   | Untimed Mark It Zero - never expires, carries between balls             |

### Bowling Scoring

- Full 10-frame bowling game with official scoring
- Bowling Super Jackpot after 10 frames = Bowling Score × 25K
- **Maximum:** 300 × 25K = **7,500,000 points** <kcite ref="21"/>

> **🎉 Easter Egg:** Play bowling on Saturday (Shabbos) and select Walter - he will complain! <kcite ref="21"/>

## 📈 Progression Systems

### 6 Wizard Goals

Progress toward "The Dude Abides" wizard mode by completing <kcite ref="21"/>:

| Goal                        | Requirement                                 |
| --------------------------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| 1️⃣ **Start Characters**      | Play all 5 Character Multiballs             |
| 2️⃣ **Collect Bowling Balls** | Win all 5 bowling balls                     |
| 3️⃣ **Super Jackpot**         | Collect Super Jackpot in main Multiball     |
| 4️⃣ **Car Modes**             | Start (or complete on Hard) all 3 car modes |
| 5️⃣ **Rug Modes**             | Complete rug modes                          |
| 6️⃣ **Mark It Zero**          | Collect multiple Mark It Zeros              |

Progress is shown at the bottom of the playfield <kcite ref="21"/>.

### Trophies System

Player profiles can track collected trophies across games <kcite ref="21"/>. Categories include achievements for modes, multiballs, bowling, and more.

### Multiplier Stacking

**Two types of multipliers can stack** <kcite ref="21"/>:

1. **Bowling Ball Multipliers** - Permanent for that character's shot
2. **White Russian Multipliers** - Temporary (4 shots)

Combine both on the same shot for maximum value!

## 🔄 Stacking & Combos

### ✅ What Stacks Well

| Combination                                  | Why It Works                                   |
| -------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| Multiple Characters → Character Multiball    | More balls, higher jackpots, multiplied values |
| Bowling Balls + White Russians               | Double multiplier on character shots           |
| Mark It Zero + Character Multiball ready     | Allows canceling accidental multiball start    |
| Let's Go Bowling + Character Multiball ready | Same cancel option                             |
| Rug Modes during single ball                 | Safe, controlled scoring                       |

### ❌ What NOT to Stack

| Avoid                                              | Reason                                      |
| -------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| Starting Character Multiball with only 1 character | Wastes potential - wait for more characters |
| Rug modes during multiball                         | Too chaotic, rug is dangerous               |
| Car modes when not ready                           | Upper playfield can drain quickly           |

### Optimal Timing

1. **Early Game:** Focus on qualifying all 5 characters before starting Character Multiball <kcite ref="80"/>
2. **Before Multiball:** Collect bowling balls for the characters you'll use
3. **During Multiball:** Focus on jackpots, not side objectives
4. **Late Game:** Stack multipliers for wizard mode push

## 🏆 Wizard Mode: The Dude Abides

### Requirements

Complete all 6 Wizard Goals (shown at bottom of playfield) <kcite ref="21"/>.

### Stage 1 - Single Ball

| Step | Action                                                            |
| ---- | ----------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1    | Shoot the lit Character Shot (opens the rug)                      |
| 2    | **Optional:** Repeat character shot to go bowling (Launch button) |

**Note:** You can drain and continue on next ball during Stage 1 <kcite ref="21"/>.

> **💡 Pro Tip:** Bowling Jackpots in Stage 1 add that bowling ball's multiplier to Stage 2!

### Stage 2 - Multiball

| Jackpot               | How to Collect                 |
| --------------------- | ------------------------------ |
| Character Jackpot     | Main character shots           |
| Bowling Jackpot       | Repeat character shot          |

| Car Jackpot           | Bash the car                   |
| Nihilist Jackpot      | Complete 3 Nihilist targets    |
| White Russian Jackpot | Complete White Russian targets |
| Mark It Zero Jackpot  | Complete Z-E-R-O lanes         |

### The Drugged White Russian Effect

When down to one ball in Stage 2, flippers become progressively more delayed - simulating The Dude's drugged state from the movie! <kcite ref="21"/>

## 📝 Additional Information

### Extra Ball Opportunities

Extra Ball lights at the scoop when <kcite ref="21"/>:
- ✅ Complete 3 Rug Modes, OR
- ✅ Complete 3 Mark It Zeros

**Ball Save:** Completing Mark It Zero with an outlane rollover returns your ball (if it would drain) <kcite ref="21"/>.

### Kickback

- Located in left outlane
- Relight by hitting the Trampoline standup target <kcite ref="21"/>
- Often hit naturally via slingshot caroms

### Mystery Awards

- Qualified by spinner shots (30 hits for first, +10 for each subsequent)
- Collected at the scoop when lit <kcite ref="21"/>
- If nothing else is lit at scoop, you get spinner progress toward Mystery

### Setup Recommendations

| Setting              | Recommendation                                                   |
| -------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Pitch**            | 6.5°-6.8° (7.3° is too steep - like Pachinko!) <kcite ref="81"/> |
| **Level**            | Ensure side-to-side is perfectly level                           |
| **Flipper Response** | Check coil sleeves if flippers feel sluggish                     |

### Known Issues

- Rug sensitivity can be inconsistent <kcite ref="78"/>
- Car bash toy may not register reliably
- Upper playfield can drain unexpectedly
- Bowling pins visibility limited for tall players <kcite ref="81"/>

## ✅ Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

### Before Your First Ball
- [ ] Understand the 5 character shots and their colors
- [ ] Know where the saucer (left) and scoop (center) are
- [ ] Plan to take the Super Skill Shot (hold right flipper)

### Ball 1 Goals
- [ ] Hit bowling loop for instant bowling qualification
- [ ] Make character shots to spell names
- [ ] Don't start Character Multiball until multiple characters are lit
- [ ] Lock balls when lock is lit (don't aim for standups)

### Key Things to Remember
- [ ] Rug is dangerous - only shoot when partially open
- [ ] Keep Mark It Zero or Let's Go Bowling lit for multiball cancel option
- [ ] White Russians = temporary multipliers (4 shots)
- [ ] Bowling Balls = permanent multipliers + perks
- [ ] Upper playfield is valuable - stay up there when possible

### Scoring Priorities
1. 🥇 5-Character Multiball with stacked multipliers
2. 🥈 Super Jackpot in main Multiball
3. 🥉 Wizard Mode (The Dude Abides)
