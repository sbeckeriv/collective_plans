# 🎪 TOY STORY 4 Pinball Machine - Complete Strategy Guide
## *Path to 500+ Million Points*

> **"To infinity and beyond!"**

Jersey Jack Pinball's 2022 Toy Story 4 machine brings the beloved Pixar film to the arcade in spectacular fashion, with carnival games, multiball chaos, and a wizard mode finale at the spinning carousel <kcite ref="1"/>. This guide will help you master Andy's toy universe and break through 500 million points.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Ball 1:** Collect the skill shot at the Woody scoop (50,000 pts)
- [ ] **Light kickback:** Spell F-O-R-K-Y at standup targets for ball save
- [ ] **Start Road Trip Multiball:** Knock down left drop target, lock 3 balls
- [ ] **Earn carnival tickets:** Hit ramps to build ticket value
- [ ] **Complete carnival games:** Win games to light locks for Carnival Multiball
- [ ] **Start Buzz Quick Multiball:** Shoot the Inner Voice saucer repeatedly
- [ ] **Unlock scenes:** Complete Rescue Forky, Gabby Gabby Frenzy, and others
- [ ] **Reach the carousel:** Qualify all 7 scenes for the wizard mode

## 🎱 Skill Shots

### Primary Skill Shot: Woody Scoop
- **Target:** Woody saucer (center of playfield)
- **Points:** 50,000 pts + Bonus Multiplier +5x
- **Method:** Controlled plunge or ramp feed from upper flipper
- **Tip:** Aim for a clean arc; momentum helps reach the scoop

### Skill Shot Progression
Successfully making consecutive skill shots builds value:
- 1st Shot: 50,000 pts
- 2nd Shot: 100,000 pts  
- 3rd Shot: 150,000 pts

**Extra Ball Alert:** Make all 3 skill shots in a game to light an extra ball! <kcite ref="2"/>

### Secret Skill Shots (Direct from Plunge)

| Shot Target            | Points      | Bonus Effect                    |
| ---------------------- | ----------- | ------------------------------- |
| **Duke Caboom Jump**   | 75,000      | Bumper value increase next ball |
| **Jet Bumpers Hit**    | 25,000 each | Build toward Super Jackpot      |
| **Bo Peep Loops**      | 50,000      | Progress toward Super Loops     |

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Control your plunge to reach different playfield areas and maximize your opening shot! <kcite ref="2"/>

## 🎪 Carnival Games & Tickets

Carnival tickets are the currency of Andy's world. Accumulate them to play digital carnival games and unlock multiball opportunities.

### Ticket Collection
- **Left Ramp:** Standard ticket award (starting value: 10 tickets)
- **Right Ramp:** Standard ticket award (starting value: 10 tickets)
- **Jet Bumpers:** 1 ticket per hit
- **Value Increases:** Ticket values escalate with each ramp shot

### Carnival Game Modes
When locks are lit via winning carnival games, hit the left ramp when glowing to start a carnival game. Each game is timed (approximately 40 seconds).

| Game          | Objective                                     | Reward           |
| ------------- | --------------------------------------------- | ---------------- |
| **Star Adventurer** | Hit all lit shots in sequence              | Character Perk   |
| **Dragon Zone**     | Complete combo sequences                   | Character Perk   |
| **Squirrel Derby**  | Bash targets to cross finish line           | Character Perk   |
| **Flipball**        | Mini-pinball game on 10" display            | Character Perk   |

**Perks Awarded:** Character toys grant scoring bonuses during Carnival Multiball <kcite ref="3"/>

## 🎁 The Seven Scenes (Main Modes)

Your primary objective is to qualify or complete all seven story scenes, unlocking the final wizard mode.

### Scene 1: Road Trip Multiball

**Qualification:**
1. Hit the **left drop target** to light lock mode
2. Lock three balls behind the left drop target into the pit
3. Start multiball mode

**Scoring Phases:**

| Phase | Action                                  | Points                    |
| ----- | --------------------------------------- | ------------------------- |
| 1     | Jackpot shots during 3-ball play        | 1,000,000 base            |
| 2     | Pit wall hits (pre-start)               | +650,000 per hit          |
| 3     | Super Jackpot                           | Sum of all jackpots ×2    |
| 4     | Mega Jackpot (hurry-up)                 | Starts 1,000,000 / decays |

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Build jackpot value in the pit before starting the multiball! Pit wall hits and spinner shots increase your super jackpot significantly. <kcite ref="2"/>

**Completion:** Awards Scene 1 completion mark toward wizard mode

---

### Scene 2: Buzz Quick Multiball

**Qualification:**
1. Shoot the **Inner Voice saucer** (pop bumper area) repeatedly
2. Hit it enough times to light multiball (typically 4-6 hits)
3. Start mode at scoop

**Scoring:**

| Action                          | Points              |
| ------------------------------- | ------------------- |
| Jackpot shot (ramps/bumpers)    | 2,000,000 base      |
| Double jackpot bonus            | ×2 during hurry-up  |
| Super Jackpot (scoop)           | Accumulated total   |
| Intergalactic Jackpot (hurry-up)| 2,000,000 + decay   |

**Strategy:** The jet bumpers are your friend—blasting through them during multiball builds jackpot value and feeds shots. <kcite ref="2"/>

**Completion:** Awards Scene 2 completion mark

---

### Scene 3: Rescue Forky

**Qualification:**
1. Spell **F-O-R-K-Y** at standup targets
2. Hit each letter target once
3. Lights Rescue Forky mode when complete

**Mode Mechanics:**
- Execute **left ramp → Duke Caboom Jump → Curio Cabinet** combo sequences
- Each successful combo: 250,000 pts (increases per consecutive combo)
- Lights **kickback** at left outlane when Forky mode is fully completed

**Combo Chain:**
- 1st combo: 250,000
- 2nd combo: 500,000
- 3rd combo: 750,000
- 4th+ combo: 1,000,000+ pts

> ⚠️ **Warning:** The Duke Caboom jump is unpredictable—it rises mid-shot, so timing is critical! <kcite ref="2"/>

**Completion:** Awards Scene 3 completion + Kickback ball save

---

### Scene 4: Gabby Gabby Frenzy

**Qualification:**
1. Hit the **Gabby Gabby pop-up mech** on left playfield
2. Activate frenzy mode by hitting it enough times
3. Pop-up raises and becomes target area

**Frenzy Mechanic:**
- All playfield shots are lit simultaneously
- Make shots rapidly within time limit (30-45 seconds)
- Each shot: 500,000 → 750,000 → 1,000,000 pts (escalating)
- **Super Jackpot:** Complete all shots for 5,000,000 bonus

**Strategy:** Go for ramps and loops during frenzy—they're typically the highest value and easiest to land. <kcite ref="3"/>

**Completion:** Awards Scene 4 completion mark

---

### Scene 5: Take A Chance Wheel

**Qualification:**
1. Spell **A-L-I-E-N** at the captive ball
2. Hit the spinning wheel when lit
3. Wheel awards a random prize

**Award Wheel Options:**
- Extra Ball
- Super Spinners (activate for high-value spinner shots)
- Tiki Party (mini mode)
- Jackpot multiplier increase
- Character perk unlock
- Multiplier advance

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** The wheel sometimes spins automatically mid-play—watch for it and position your shot accordingly! <kcite ref="3"/>

**Completion:** Awards Scene 5 completion + Wheel award

---

### Scene 6: Super Spinners

**Qualification:**
1. Complete the **Woody Spinner** (left side)
2. Complete the **Bo Peep Loops** (right side) 3 times
3. Or land Take A Chance Wheel "Super Spinners" award

**Super Spinner Scoring:**
- Spinner value builds during this phase
- Base value: 100,000 per spin
- Escalates per shot: 150,000 → 200,000 → 300,000+
- Complete all spinners: 2,000,000 bonus

**Strategy:** Trap up after a loop and execute back-to-back spinner shots for continuous scoring. <kcite ref="2"/>

**Completion:** Awards Scene 6 completion mark

---

### Scene 7: Bo Peep's Super Loops

**Qualification:**
1. Shoot the **upper loop** (right ramp area) repeatedly
2. Make 3 successful loops in a row
3. Lights final scene qualification

**Loop Combo Scoring:**
- 1st loop: 250,000 pts
- 2nd loop combo: 500,000 pts
- 3rd loop combo: 1,000,000 pts
- Continue combo: +500,000 per additional loop

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** After a loop shot, use the right flipper trap position to control your return and reset for another loop shot! <kcite ref="2"/>

**Completion:** Awards Scene 7 completion mark + Final scene unlock

## 🎠 Carnival Multiball

**Qualification:**
1. Complete carnival games (win at least 3 games) to light locks
2. Lock 3 balls in the **left lane** via scoop
3. Lock 3 more balls in the **scoop area**
4. Start at scoop

**3-Ball Play Scoring:**

| Action                       | Points           |
| ---------------------------- | ---------------- |
| Jackpot shot (any ramp/loop) | 2,500,000 base   |
| Bumper hits during MB        | 100,000 each     |
| Character perk bonus         | ×1.5 multiplier  |
| Super Jackpot (pit)          | Sum of all JPs   |

**Add-A-Ball Feature:** Shoot the scoop when lit (green) to add extra balls (up to 3 additional, with 12-second timeout per add)

**Strategy:** Use perks from carnival games to increase your multiball scoring. Multiple perks stack for 2x, 3x, or even 4x multipliers. <kcite ref="3"/>

## 🎡 "Meet Me at the Carousel" - Wizard Mode

After completing all seven scenes at least once, you unlock the final wizard mode.

### Wizard Mode Structure

**Setup:**
- Two variants: **Woody's Route** and **Buzz's Route**
- Playing through both unlocks access to both versions on subsequent plays
- Scene completion multiplier: +1x for every scene completed before starting (+7x max)

### Mode Phases

| Phase | Action                                    | Base Points       |
| ----- | ----------------------------------------- | ----------------- |
| 1     | Complete opening objective                | 5,000,000         |
| 2     | Spin the Take A Chance Wheel             | Award-dependent   |
| 3     | Shoot all lit carousel shots             | 2,000,000 each    |
| 4     | Final Super Jackpot (hurry-up)           | 10,000,000 + ×    |

**Scoring Multiplier:** Each scene you completed = +1 multiplier applied to all wizard mode shots

- **Minimum (1 scene):** 1x multiplier
- **Maximum (7 scenes):** 8x multiplier (7x bonus + 1x base)

**Wizard Mode Completion:** Can award 50,000,000+ points with full multiplier <kcite ref="1"/>

## 🎰 Additional Features & Modes

### Duke Caboom Jump
- **Location:** Right orbit leading to jump ramp
- **Mechanic:** Ramp raises mid-shot, creating a jump
- **Skill Requirement:** High (timing is crucial)
- **Reward:** 500,000 pts + feed to upper flipper
- **Bonus:** Completing Duke Caboom jumps advances carousel progress

### Take A Chance Wheel (Standalone)
- Spins automatically and can act as a playfield obstacle
- Hit when spinning for random awards
- Common awards: Extra Ball, Multiplier Boost, Jackpot Increase
- Rare award: Instant ball saver

### Tiki Party (Mini Mode)
- Triggered from Take A Chance Wheel or special shot
- 20-second frenzy of all playfield shots lit
- Scoring: 500,000 → 750,000 → 1,000,000 pts per shot
- Completion bonus: 3,000,000 pts

### Kickback Ball Save
- Awarded by spelling F-O-R-K-Y and completing Rescue Forky
- Active at left outlane only
- One-time use per ball
- **Pro Tip:** Let it save you strategically—don't waste it early in the game! <kcite ref="2"/>

## 📈 Stacking & Combos

### ✅ What Stacks Well

| Combination                      | Benefit                                  |
| -------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| **Carnival Perks + Multiball**   | Multiplier boost during high-value shots |
| **Scene Multipliers + Wizard**   | 8x multiplier on all wizard mode scoring |
| **Kickback + Risky Shots**       | Take calculated risks with ball save     |
| **Buzz Multiball + Bumpers**     | Bumper hits build jackpot value fast     |
| **Road Trip + Pit Shots**        | Pre-multiball value building strategy    |

### ❌ What NOT to Stack

| Avoid                                  | Reason                          |
| -------------------------------------- | ------------------------------- |
| **Completing scenes in wrong order**   | Wastes opportunity for multiplier |
| **Missing kickback save early**        | Reduces your safety margin      |
| **Ignoring carnival tickets**          | Locks become harder to achieve   |

### ⏰ Optimal Timing

1. **Build early:** Collect tickets and complete carnival games
2. **Scene sequence:** Complete scenes in any order, but plan for wizard mode
3. **Multiball stacking:** Time multiballs when perks are active
4. **Endgame push:** Save your best scenes (highest multiplier potential) for last to maximize wizard mode scoring

## 🎯 Shot Analysis

### ⚠️ Dangerous Shots

| Shot                    | Risk                                       | Mitigation                        |
| ----------------------- | ------------------------------------------ | --------------------------------- |
| **Duke Caboom Jump**    | Unpredictable ramp angle, difficult timing | Aim center, use soft touch        |
| **Bo Peep Loops**       | Fast return path, SDTM risk                | Trap after each loop              |
| **Jet Bumpers**         | Chaotic bounces toward outlanes             | Accept risk during multiballs     |
| **Left Ramp (contested)**| Can interact with Gabby Gabby pop-up      | Check pop-up position before shot |

### ✅ Safe Shots

| Shot              | Benefit                               |
| ----------------- | ------------------------------------- |
| **Right Ramp**    | Clean feed to upper flipper           |
| **Spinners**      | Predictable return, reliable scoring  |
| **Scoop**         | Ball held, time to prepare next shot  |
| **Pop Bumpers**   | Energy-giving, time to reset position |

### 🎮 Ball Control Positions

- **Left Flipper Trap:** Best for right ramp control, loop shots
- **Right Flipper Trap:** Best for left ramp, spinner shots
- **Post Pass:** Transfer between flippers safely after loops
- **Live Catch:** Grab balls from jet bumper area for clean trap
