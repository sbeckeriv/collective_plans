# 🎩 Houdini: Master of Mystery - Strategy Guide

*American Pinball, 2017 — Designed by Joe Balcer, Software by Josh Kugler*

> **"Escape every trick. Collect every Houdini letter."**

American Pinball's debut title is an extraordinarily deep ruleset: 10 magic trick "Stage Modes," 5 multiballs, a Magic Shop economy, and a seven-part checklist gating the **Master Magician** wizard mode. Our goal: escape as many tricks as possible, collect Houdini letters from every available source, and complete the full Master Magician requirement list.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Take the skill shot carefully**: the opening plunge targets 3 rotating ESC targets with random awards (points, outlane modes, Houdini letters, multipliers, Magic Shop access, ball locks) — or **soft-plunge the left Magic target ("Super") to collect all three simultaneously**
- [ ] **Spin the spinner to light the Magic Shop** at the Jail hole — collecting **8 items** earns a Houdini letter toward Master Magician Mode
- [ ] **Use the Milkcan loop → ramp combo** to increase your playfield multiplier — a key repeatable scoring technique
- [ ] **Stage Alley shots provide automatic 2X mode value** — prioritize routing through Stage Alley during any active mode
- [ ] **Extra balls light at the Magic Shop, after 3 Secret Missions, or at a 7X bonus multiplier threshold** (default setting)
- [ ] **Outlane modes (Return from Beyond, Escape Death) provide ball saves** — a built-in safety net worth understanding

---

## 🎪 Core Mechanics

### Skill Shot

The opening plunge targets **3 rotating ESC targets** with random awards. Alternatively, a **soft plunge to the left Magic target ("Super")** collects **all three awards simultaneously** — generally the stronger choice when you can execute it.

### Magic Shop (Yellow)

1. **Spin the spinner** to light the mystery award at the **Jail hole**
2. Awards include bonus multipliers, ball saves, points, trunk locks, extra balls, and outlane modes
3. **Collecting 8 items** earns a **Houdini letter** toward Master Magician Mode

## 🎩 Stage Modes (Magic Tricks)

| Trick | How to Complete |
|---|---|
| **Chinese Water Torture** | Shoot Key Lane → either orbit → Stage |
| **Indian Needle Trick** | All switches score X; Magic and Key standups increase switch value |
| **Milkcan Escape** | Shoot the lower-left loop **3 times** |
| **Vanishing Elephant** | Hit the ramp 3 times, then Stage |
| **Walk Through Walls** | Sequence: right shot → middle shot → left shot through various lanes |
| **Handcuff King** | Hit 3 pops to shed a handcuff; shed **6 handcuffs** total |
| **Metamorphosis** | Trunk → orbit → stage/trunk sequence |
| **King of Cards** (video mode) | 45-second timed card-throw mini-game, 5 cards per hand |

There are **10 Stage Modes total** — completing all of them is one of the seven Master Magician requirements.

---

## 🎁 Five Multiball Modes

| Multiball | Mechanics |
|---|---|
| **Trunk Multiball** (Turquoise) | Lock 3 balls via the inner loop catapult into the trunk; score jackpots at 3 lit shots, then the trunk for Super Jackpot; collect 3 jackpots for a Houdini letter |
| **Seance Multiball** (Teal) | Spell S-E-A-N-C-E at the scoop to start; spelling it in order scores the (very hard) Super Jackpot; collect 2 jackpots for a Houdini letter |
| **Straight Jacket Escape** | Instant 3-ball multiball; jackpots at orbits, Stage Alley, Key Lane, ramp; choose reversed flippers (standard value) or reversed+inverted (3X value) |
| **Bullet Catch** | 2-ball, 3-phase: load via inner loop → fire the roving hurry-up shot → multiball with extra jackpots |
| **Man From Beyond** (Movie Mode) | Add-a-ball multiball: free Houdini from ice, then restraints, using spotlight-based shot recognition |

> 💰 **Pro Tip — Core Combo:** The **Milkcan loop immediately followed by the ramp** increases your playfield multiplier — this is the single most repeatable high-value technique in the game. Combine it with **Stage Alley's automatic 2X mode value** for compounding scoring during active modes.

## 🏆 Master Magician (Wizard Mode) — Full Requirement Checklist

| Requirement | Target |
|---|---|
| Stage Modes completed | **10** |
| Movie Modes completed | **5** |
| Magic Shop items collected | **8** |
| Jail Escape Hurry-Ups completed | **5** |
| Secret Mission Combos completed | **5** |
| Trunk Multiball jackpots collected | **3** |
| Seance Multiball jackpots collected | **2** |

> 🎯 This is a genuinely comprehensive checklist — reaching Master Magician requires sustained, full-game progress across nearly every system in the ruleset, not a single lucky run.

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **Straight Jacket Escape's reversed/inverted flippers** | Genuinely disorienting control scheme | Practice reversed-flipper control before relying on the 3X inverted mode for serious scoring |
| **Spotlight-based shot recognition (Man From Beyond)** | Requires the spotlight mechanism to track correctly | Verify spotlight calibration if shots aren't registering as expected |
| **Forcing Seance's in-order Super Jackpot** | Explicitly described as "very hard" | Don't force it under pressure; take the standard jackpots if the in-order sequence isn't lining up |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Game (Ball 1)

1. Take the skill shot (soft-plunge "Super" if you can execute it reliably)
2. Start spinning toward your first Magic Shop item
3. Begin working an easier Stage Mode (Milkcan Escape or Vanishing Elephant)

**Target Score:** 20M–50M

### Mid Game (Ball 2)

1. Run the Milkcan loop → ramp combo repeatedly for multiplier growth
2. Start qualifying multiballs — Trunk and Seance are strong early targets for Houdini letters
3. Continue collecting Magic Shop items toward 8

**Target Score:** 50M–150M

### Late Game (Ball 3+)

1. Push through remaining Stage Modes and Movie Modes
2. Cash in Trunk and Seance jackpots toward their Master Magician thresholds
3. If close to the full 7-item checklist, push for Master Magician

**Target Score per ball:** 50M–250M+

## ⚙️ Setup Recommendations

| Setting | Recommendation | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| **Playfield pitch** | 6.5°–7.0° | American Pinball factory standard |
| **Catapult mechanisms (2)** | Verify smooth, reliable launches | Central to Trunk Multiball ball-locking |
| **Theater spotlight calibration** | Check accurate shot tracking | Core to Man From Beyond's spotlight-based recognition system |

---

## 📋 Quick Reference: Strategic Priorities

**Ball 1:** Skill shot, start Magic Shop collection, work an easy Stage Mode.
**Ball 2:** Grind the Milkcan/ramp multiplier combo, start Trunk/Seance multiballs.
**Ball 3+:** Complete remaining Stage/Movie Modes, push the full Master Magician checklist.

**Target High-Score Ballpark:** **150–400 million points** is a strong, competitive game; reaching Master Magician represents genuine mastery of one of pinball's deepest modern rulesheets.
