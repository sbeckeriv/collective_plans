# 🌌 Tri Zone (Williams, 1979) - Strategy Guide

*Williams Electronics, 1979 — Designed by Tony Kraemer*

> **"Three zones, four drop targets, one spinner — outer space made simple."**

Tri Zone (Williams, 1979) is an early solid-state machine with a clean outer-space theme and straightforward mechanics: 4 drop targets driving the bonus multiplier, a single kick-out hole for bonus collection, a spinner for rapid scoring, and 3 pop bumpers. No multiball, no wizard mode — just clean SS fundamentals. Our goal: **100K–400K** by completing the drop targets efficiently, building a high multiplier, and farming the spinner.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Complete the 4 drop targets** to advance the bonus multiplier
- [ ] **Shoot the kick-out hole** when lit for bonus collection
- [ ] **Farm the spinner** between target resets for rapid point accumulation
- [ ] **Feed pop bumpers** passively for bonus building in the upper playfield
- [ ] **Watch for the kick-out return** — it ejects toward the right flipper; be ready

---

## 🎮 Core Mechanics

### Skill Shot

| Skill Shot | How | Reward |
|---|---|---|
| **Spinner feed** | Partial plunge to spinner lane | Spinner chain + bonus advance |
| **Standard** | Full plunge | Ball to pop bumpers |

### 4 Drop Targets

Four individual drop targets are the primary multiplier advancement mechanism:
- Each target: 500–1,000 points on hit
- **Completing all 4:** Advances bonus multiplier one step AND lights the kick-out hole
- Targets reset immediately after a complete bank; each cycle raises the multiplier
- The 4-target spread is manageable — typically 2 left-side, 2 right-side

### Spinner (Center Lane)

The spinner scores per-revolution on a pass-through lane:
- Each revolution: 100–1,000 points (depending on what's lit)
- A hard spinner shot = 20–50 revolutions = fast point accumulation
- **Between drop target resets:** Spinner passes keep the score building
- Spinner value is occasionally lit higher by specific bonus sequences

### Kick-Out Hole (Primary Collection)

The single kick-out hole collects the bonus when lit:
- Lit by completing the 4-bank drop targets
- Collecting: pays current bonus × current multiplier, then resets bonus counter
- Ejects ball toward right flipper — be ready to catch
- **Collect before draining.** Uncollected bonus is lost at drain.

### 3 Pop Bumpers

Three pop bumpers in the upper playfield:
- Passive point and bonus accumulation
- 100 points each hit
- High traffic area — new players will spend significant ball time here early

## 📊 Shot Analysis

| Shot | Value | Risk |
|---|---|---|
| Drop targets (4) | Multiplier advance | Low-Medium |
| Spinner | Per-revolution points | Low |
| Kick-out hole | Bonus × multiplier collect | Low |
| Pop bumpers (3) | Passive | None |

### Dangerous Areas

- **Right outlane** — after kick-out eject or spinner exit; ball moves fast
- **Post-multiplier** — the temptation to chase another bank before collecting is real; drain ends the bonus

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Ball 1
1. Controlled plunge → spinner hit
2. Drop target completions × 2 (advance to 2–3× multiplier)
3. Kick-out collection at 3× bonus

### Ball 2
1. 4 targets complete → kick-out collect
2. Spinner × 3 between completions
3. Aim for 4–5× multiplier

### Ball 3
1. Max multiplier (5×): 3–4 bank completions
2. Spinner farm at high multiplier
3. Final kick-out collection before drain

### Score Targets

| Performance | Score |
|---|---|
| Beginner | 30K–80K |
| Solid | 100K–200K |
| Strong (max mult + spinner) | 250K–400K |
| Elite | 400K+ |

---

## 💡 Pro Tips

> **Spinner and drop targets alternate cleanly.** After completing the 4 targets, collect the kick-out, then shoot the spinner while the targets reset — you never have dead time.

> **3× multiplier is the efficiency threshold.** Getting to 3× in Ball 1 means Balls 2 and 3 each have meaningful bonus collection. Getting to 5× in Ball 2 means Ball 3 is pure farming.

> **Catch the kick-out eject.** It comes toward the right flipper at medium speed — trap it with a still flipper rather than swinging, then continue the target sequence.
