# 🌑 Blackout Pinball Machine - Strategy Guide

*Williams, 1980 — Designed by Claude Fernandez*

> **"Hit the bank. Kill the lights."**

Blackout was the first Williams machine to feature a computer-controlled general illumination "lights out" effect — but the gameplay underneath that gimmick is a clean, classic three-bank target game. Complete green, red, and yellow banks to light the Blackout saucer, max your bonus multiplier, and grind lit spinners for steady points. Our goal: a strong **120K–180K** game.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Work the green standup bank from the right flipper** — each completion step (5K → 10K → 15K → 20K) raises the left orbit's value, eventually lighting an extra ball
- [ ] **Complete the yellow drop target bank twice** to light both spinners for 3,000 per spin — your best repeatable scoring shot
- [ ] **Approach the red drop targets carefully** — they carry the most rebound risk on the table
- [ ] **Complete all three color banks** to light the Blackout saucer for 30,000 points and the signature lights-out effect
- [ ] **Hit all three top lanes** to advance your bonus multiplier toward the 5X maximum (145,000 overall)

---

## 🎯 Core Mechanics

### The Three Target Banks

| Bank | Location | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| **Green standups** (5 targets) | Left side | Advances left orbit value progressively: 5K → 10K → 15K → 20K, then lights an **Extra Ball** |
| **Red drop targets** (3 targets) | Center | High-power scoring; contributes toward "Condition Red" and the Blackout feature, but carries significant rebound risk |
| **Yellow-orange drop targets** (3 targets) | Upper right | Complete **twice** to light both spinners for 3,000/spin |

### Blackout Feature

**Completing all three color banks** lights the **Blackout saucer** for **30,000 points**, accompanied by the game's signature lights-out-plus-sound effect — the novelty that gave the machine its name.

### Spinners (Your Main Scoring Engine)

Once lit (via the yellow bank completed twice), spinners are worth **3,000 per spin**. The **top spinner is the cleanest, safest shot** to repeatedly target once lit.

> 🎯 **Pro Tip — Core Strategy:** Finish the yellow targets twice, then UTAD (shoot up the right flipper, down the left, or vice versa) via the top right spinner repeatedly. This single loop is both safe and lucrative, and also helps push your bonus multiplier toward maximum.

### Bonus System

- **Base bonus** climbs to **29,000**
- **Multiplier** advances from 1X to a maximum of **5X** (145,000 overall) by completing all three top lanes
- Lane change is active, so you can reposition which lane is lit for your shot

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Shot | Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| **Red drop targets** | The riskiest shot on the table — significant rebound chance | Approach with controlled, deliberate shots rather than full-power slaps |
| **Pop bumper chaos** | General bumper action can send the ball unpredictably | Treat bumper time as incidental scoring, not a shot you actively chase |
| **Left orbit whip** | Can whip across to a center drain coming from the right side | Be ready on the flippers immediately after any left orbit shot |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Ball (Ball 1)

1. Work the green standup bank from the right flipper to build left orbit value
2. Begin the yellow drop target bank — your first completion lights the "Blackout" indicator
3. Hit top lanes opportunistically to start building bonus multiplier

**Target Score:** 20K–40K

### Mid Ball (Ball 2)

1. Complete the yellow bank a second time to light both spinners
2. Settle into the top-spinner UTAD loop for steady, safe scoring
3. Push bonus multiplier toward 5X via top lanes

**Target Score:** 40K–80K

### Late Ball (Ball 3+)

1. Finish the red bank carefully for "Condition Red" progress, despite the rebound risk
2. Complete all three banks for the 30,000-point Blackout saucer award
3. Keep grinding the lit spinner loop — it remains your safest, most reliable point source throughout

**Target Score per ball:** 30K–60K

## ⚙️ Setup Recommendations

| Setting | Recommendation | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| **Playfield pitch** | 6.0°–6.5° | Standard for early-80s Williams solid-state machines |
| **Spinner mechanism** | Verify smooth, free spinning | Core to the safest scoring loop in the game |
| **Drop target reset** | Check for clean, reliable resets | Especially important for the red bank given its higher rebound tendency |

---

## 📋 Quick Reference: Strategic Priorities

**Ball 1:** Work the green bank, start the yellow bank.
**Ball 2:** Light spinners via yellow bank completion, settle into the UTAD loop.
**Ball 3+:** Tackle the red bank for Condition Red/Blackout, keep spinning.

**Target High-Score Ballpark:** **120K–180K** is a strong, competitive score on Blackout, driven mostly by disciplined spinner looping and a maxed bonus multiplier.
