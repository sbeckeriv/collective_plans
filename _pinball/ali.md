# 🥊 Ali Pinball Machine - Strategy Guide

*Stern Electronics, 1980 — Designed by Harry Williams*

> **"Float like a butterfly, sting like a bee."**

Ali is a classic early solid-state machine built almost entirely around one decision: how fast can you max your bonus multiplier, and how many times can you cash it in? With two 3-bank drop target sets, spelling lanes for ALI and GREATEST, and a single bonus-collect saucer, this is a game of disciplined repetition rather than complex modes. Our goal: a strong **150K–250K** game, since this 4-digit-era scoring layout rewards consistent multiplier collection over flashy single shots.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Hit the upper-left 3-bank first, every ball** — this is your bonus multiplier, and it carries over between balls
- [ ] **Push for 5X as early as possible**, ideally Ball 1 — the earlier you're maxed, the more total collections you get
- [ ] **Spell A-L-I** to boost the center-top eject pocket value
- [ ] **Spell A-L-I & G-R-E-A-T-E-S-T** to light an extra ball at the top turnaround
- [ ] **Camp the right bonus-collect saucer** once your multiplier is high — repetition beats exploration here
- [ ] **Watch the right outlane** — pop bumper rebounds feed it often

---

## 🥊 Core Mechanics

### Upper Left 3-Bank (Bonus Multiplier)

This is the most important target bank on the table. Completing it advances your bonus multiplier:

| Multiplier | How to Reach |
|---|---|
| 1X → 2X → 3X → 4X → 5X | Each full completion of the upper-left 3-bank advances one step |
| **5X (max)** | Carries over ball to ball — once earned, it's yours for the rest of the game |

> 🏆 **Pro Tip:** Because the multiplier carries over between balls, a 5X earned on Ball 1 pays off for the entire game. Prioritize this bank above everything else early on.

### Spelling Lanes: ALI & GREATEST

| Spell | Effect |
|---|---|
| **A-L-I** | Increases the value of the center-top eject pocket |
| **A-L-I & G-R-E-A-T-E-S-T** (both completed) | Lights the top turnaround for an **extra ball** |
| **A-L-I & G-R-E-A-T-E-S-T (completed twice)** | Lights **Special** |

### Side Drop Targets & Side Stars

The left-side 3-bank advances your "side stars" — completing this bank scales in value with how many GREATEST letters and star rollovers you've already collected, worth up to **90K** at maximum star value. Star rollovers (hit incidentally during normal play) also advance bonus passively, so a long ball naturally builds value even without deliberate target chasing.

### Pop Bumpers & Spinner

- **Pop bumpers:** 100 points each — minor, but contribute to wear-down play and incidental star advances
- **Horseshoe spinner:** 2K per spin when the feed is reliable — a safe, repeatable bonus-builder when you're not in position for the drop banks

### Bonus Collection (Right Saucer)

The **right eject/kick-out saucer** is your bonus-collect shot. Banking your current bonus × multiplier here is the core scoring engine of the game.

> 💰 **Pro Tip:** Once at 5X, repeatedly feeding this saucer is the single highest-value loop in the game — far more valuable than chasing fresh drop-target completions.

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Shot | Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| **Pop bumper rebounds** | Frequently kick the ball toward the right outlane | Play a controlled, centered plunge to minimize chaotic pop bumper time |
| **Unreliable saucer feed** | Some machines kick the ball awkwardly instead of returning it cleanly to the flipper | Learn your specific machine's kickout angle and adjust flipper position accordingly |
| **Side drop target overshoot** | Can skip past the bank into the outlane | Use soft, controlled flipper shots rather than full-power slaps |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Ball (Ball 1)

1. **Attack the upper-left 3-bank immediately** — getting to 5X on Ball 1 is the single biggest swing in the game
2. Pick up ALI letters opportunistically along the way
3. Don't worry about GREATEST yet if it means risking the ball — multiplier comes first

**Target Score:** 20K–50K

### Mid Ball (Ball 2)

1. If not yet at 5X, keep working the upper-left bank
2. Once maxed, shift focus to the right saucer for repeated bonus collection
3. Work on GREATEST letters for the extra ball if convenient

**Target Score:** 50K–100K

### Late Ball (Ball 3+)

1. Camp the right saucer for collections — this is now your main scoring loop
2. Chase the extra ball via ALI + GREATEST completion if you haven't already
3. Use the horseshoe spinner as a safe filler shot between saucer collections

**Target Score per ball:** 30K–80K

| Feature | Requirement |
|---|---|
| **Extra Ball** | Spell A-L-I and G-R-E-A-T-E-S-T (both completed) and hit the lit top turnaround |
| **Special** | Complete A-L-I & G-R-E-A-T-E-S-T a second time |

## ⚙️ Setup Recommendations

| Setting | Recommendation | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| **Playfield pitch** | 6.0°–6.5° | Standard for early Stern M-200 solid-state machines |
| **Saucer kickout direction** | Check and adjust per machine | Feed reliability to the flipper directly affects how often you can camp the bonus collect |
| **Flipper strength** | Default | Game doesn't demand unusual power; precision matters more |

---

## 📋 Quick Reference: Strategic Priorities

**Ball 1:** Max the bonus multiplier (5X) as fast as possible.
**Ball 2:** Lock in 5X if not done, start camping the bonus saucer.
**Ball 3+:** Repeat bonus collections, chase the extra ball via ALI/GREATEST.

**Target High-Score Ballpark:** **150K–250K** is a strong, competitive score on Ali; the game's ceiling is largely set by how early you reach 5X and how reliably you can repeat-feed the bonus saucer.
