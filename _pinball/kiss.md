# 🎸 KISS (Bally, 1979) - Strategy Guide

*Bally, 1978/79 — Designed by Jim Patla*

> **"Spell it four times. Chase the Colossal Bonus."**

This is the original Bally KISS — a pure bonus-building game centered on a grid of letters spelling K-I-S-S four times. Don't confuse it with the 2015 Stern remake; this classic has its own distinct grid/bonus system topped by a genuinely huge "Colossal Bonus." Our goal: complete the KISS grid multiple times, push toward Colossal Bonus, and use the A-B-C-D bank for double bonus along the way.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Spell K-I-S-S** across the center grid — each letter is worth **1,000** in bonus, and a **complete line (horizontal or vertical) adds 3,000**
- [ ] **Complete the left bank or roll through the top center lane** to spot rows in the grid — your main route to filling letters
- [ ] **Build toward at least 44,000 bonus** by finishing one full set of four K-I-S-S letters and lighting the **44K insert**
- [ ] **Complete A-B-C-D twice for an extra ball; three times for a Special**
- [ ] **Complete all 4 rows a second time for Super Bonus; a third time for Colossal Bonus**
- [ ] **Remember bonus carries over ball to ball** — and advances on Ball 1 are worth more than the same advances on Ball 3, so front-load your bonus-building effort

---

## 💋 Core Mechanics

### The KISS Letter Grid (Spelled 4 Times)

- A grid of letters in the center of the table spells out **K-I-S-S, four separate times**
- Each letter scored: **1,000 points in bonus**
- Each **complete line (horizontal or vertical)**: an additional **3,000 in bonus**
- **Complete the left bank, or roll through the top center lane**, to spot rows in the grid

### A-B-C-D Standup Bank

| Completions | Award |
|---|---|
| 2x | Extra Ball |
| 3x | Special |

> 🎯 **Pro Tip:** Once your bonus is high enough — specifically once you have **at least four complete sets of letters** to light the **40K Super Bonus** — go for the **A-B-C-D bank** to get your **double bonus**. Sequencing matters: build the letter grid first, then cash in via A-B-C-D.

### Bonus Tiers

| Grid Completions | Tier |
|---|---|
| 1st full set | Standard bonus, light the 44K insert |
| 2nd full set | **Super Bonus** |
| 3rd full set | **Colossal Bonus** |

### Bonus Carryover (Critical Rule)

**All bonus carries over from ball to ball** — meaning early progress is never wasted. Crucially, **bonus advances made on Ball 1 are worth more than the same advances made on Ball 3**, so prioritize aggressive grid-building as early in the game as possible.

> 💰 **Pro Tip — Core Strategy:** Front-load your effort. Since Ball 1 advances are more valuable and everything carries forward, treat your first ball as the most important scoring opportunity in the entire game, not just a warm-up.

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **Right outlane detour gate** | A specific drain-risk feature on this layout | Be aware of its current state before committing to risky right-side shots |
| **Pop bumper unpredictability** (4 bumpers) | Standard late-70s bumper chaos | Treat as incidental scoring |
| **Tilt penalty applies to ball in play** | Aggressive nudging during a high-bonus ball risks losing that ball's progress | Nudge conservatively, especially when you have substantial bonus banked for the current ball |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Ball (Ball 1 — Your Most Valuable Ball)

1. Prioritize grid-filling shots above all else, since Ball 1 advances are worth the most
2. Work toward your first full K-I-S-S set and the 44K insert
3. Build toward A-B-C-D completions for extra ball/Special

### Mid Ball

1. Continue building additional KISS grid sets toward Super Bonus
2. Cash in A-B-C-D bank completions once you have 4 complete letter sets, for double bonus
3. Keep tracking your bonus carryover — nothing here is wasted

### Late Ball

1. Push for a third full grid completion and Colossal Bonus if you're close
2. Take any remaining extra ball/Special opportunities from A-B-C-D
3. Bank your accumulated, carried-over bonus

## 📋 Quick Reference: Strategic Priorities

**Ball 1 (most valuable):** Aggressively fill the KISS grid, build toward your first 44K bonus tier.
**Ball 2:** Continue grid sets toward Super Bonus, cash A-B-C-D for double bonus.
**Ball 3+:** Push for Colossal Bonus, take remaining A-B-C-D awards, bank everything.

**Target Outcome:** Reaching Colossal Bonus (third full grid completion) represents a strong, well-played, disciplined game on this classic rock-licensed machine.
