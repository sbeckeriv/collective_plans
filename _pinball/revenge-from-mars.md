# 🛸 Revenge from Mars - Complete Strategy Guide

> **Goal: 1 Billion Points** | Record: 2.57B <kcite ref="58"/>
> 
> A worthy score is 300M+, excellent is 500M+, and 1B+ puts you among the elite players.

---

## 📋 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] Learn the **Super Skill Shot** (hold left flipper, plunge, shoot left ramp/orbit)
- [ ] Focus on the **Lock Shot** (capture lane) - it's the safest shot on the playfield
- [ ] Complete mode groups to unlock **Bonus Wave Multiballs**
- [ ] Collect **40 items** in Bonus Wave MBs to reach **Mothership Multiball**
- [ ] Light all **9 Saucer Lights** to unlock **Attack Mars**
- [ ] Use **Action Buttons** to aim during video modes

## 🎯 Core Mechanics

### Skill Shots

| Type                  | How to Execute                                                             | Award                                   | Notes                                 |
| --------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------- |
| **Normal Skill Shot** | Plunge into rollover lanes                                                 | 500,000 pts (+100K each subsequent)     | Easy and consistent                   |
| **Super Skill Shot**  | Hold LEFT flipper while plunging, then shoot left ramp/orbit within 10 sec | 1,000,000 pts (+200K each) + 3 Missiles | Much more valuable! <kcite ref="42"/> |

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** For the Super Skill Shot, the ball bypasses the rollovers and circles the left orbit. Do a loop pass to the right flipper, then shoot the left ramp for maximum control. <kcite ref="42"/>

### Super Skill Shot Bonus Trick

Start the game, select **Alien Abduction** but DON'T plunge yet. Wait until the mode animation starts, then use the left action button to select the highest point value item. NOW do the Super Skill Shot - you'll get credit for destroying the left saucer! <kcite ref="43"/>

## 🎮 Mode System

### How Modes Work

1. **Select a mode** at game start using Action Buttons
2. **Complete objectives** during the mode
3. **Shoot the center mini-ramp** when it raises to finish
4. Achieve **"Flawless"** rating for a **Saucer Light** bonus

### The 9 Main Modes (3 Groups)

#### 🔋 FUEL GROUP

| Mode                   | Objective                                      | Flawless Requirement            | Tips                                                                                |
| ---------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Alien Abduction**    | Save objects from UFO, destroy 7 small saucers | Destroy all saucers + mini ramp | Tough for Flawless with ball control - focus on Lock shot instead <kcite ref="43"/> |
| **Martian Happy Hour** | Destroy 5 martians at bar, orbits trigger kegs | Destroy all 5 martians          | Center ramp destroys center keg                                                     |
| **Secret Weapon**      | Fight giant martian with ABE mech              | Deplete life bar                | Easy Flawless - repeat left ramp shots <kcite ref="43"/>                            |

#### 🛸 SAUCER GROUP

| Mode                    | Objective                           | Flawless Requirement   | Tips                                                        |
| ----------------------- | ----------------------------------- | ---------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Tower Struggle**      | Tug-of-war at Leaning Tower of Pisa | Destroy all 4 martians | Ramps destroy saucers, center targets damage tower          |
| **Mystery Mode (?)**    | Random mini-mode                    | Varies                 | Use this time to lock balls for Multiball <kcite ref="45"/> |
| **Drive-In Demolition** | Destroy martians blocking screen    | Complete damage meter  | All shots inflict damage                                    |

**Mystery Mode Options:**
- **Martian Aerobics** - Space Invaders tribute, destroy 24 martians with missiles
- **Martian Bowling** - Use Action buttons to aim
- **Martian Autopsy** - Hurry-up on center shot (starts 3M), collect 10 objects
- **Martian Tank** - Destroy 2 martians on moving tank (difficult!)

#### 🔫 WEAPONS GROUP

| **Big-O-Beam**        | Shrink enlarged farm animals                       | Shrink all 4 animals OR deplete saucer | High-point mode! <kcite ref="41"/>                                           |
| **Mars Kneads Women** | Tune into 6 TV screens                             | View all 6 stations                    | Not worth Flawless - just hit 2 center monitors and finish <kcite ref="43"/> |

## 🎱 Multiballs

### Regular Multiball (3-Ball)

| Aspect            | Details                                                                 |
| ----------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **How to Start**  | Lock 3 balls in Capture Lane OR Stroke of Luck award                    |
| **Jackpots**      | Shoot 10 saucers to reveal bigger saucer                                |
| **Super Jackpot** | Hit bigger saucer 5 times = Super Jackpot + 1 Saucer Light              |
| **Strategy**      | Aim for Lock shot (safe) and left ramp for big points <kcite ref="45"/> |

> ⚠️ **Cannot run simultaneously with Martian Multiball**

### Martian Multiball (2-Ball)

| Aspect            | Details                                                                  |
| ----------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **How to Start**  | Complete Martian Attack mode (kill 2 rubber martians + giant on display) |
| **Scoring**       | Every shot destroys a martian = 1M points                                |
| **Saucer Lights** | 10 martians = 1 Saucer Light, 30 martians = another Saucer Light         |

### Bonus Wave Multiballs (4-Ball) ⭐ KEY TO HIGH SCORES

**Triggered after completing each mode group (Fuel, Saucer, Weapons)**

| Aspect          | Details                                                   |
| --------------- | --------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Goal**        | Collect weapons/fuel items                                |
| **Multiplier**  | Use Action Buttons to move 2X multiplier, builds to 5X    |
| **Shot Values** | Center: 100K+100K, Lock: varies, Ramps/Orbits: increasing |

**Item Collection Rewards (Rev 1.4):** <kcite ref="41"/>

| Items | Reward                   |
| ----- | ------------------------ |
| 5     | Jackpot                  |
| 10    | Jackpot                  |
| 15    | Saucer Light             |
| 20    | Jackpot + Extra Ball     |
| 25    | Jackpot                  |
| 30    | Extra Ball               |
| 35    | Jackpot                  |
| 40    | **MOTHERSHIP MULTIBALL** |

> 💡 **Pro Strategy:** Move multiplier to center shots first. During ball save, hit both center X shots. When ball save ends, trap on right flipper. Focus on Lock shot (safe) and ramp X shots. When down to 1 ball, DON'T shoot the center ramp immediately - keep collecting items via ramps! <kcite ref="43"/>

### Mothership Multiball (4-Ball) ⭐ THE BIG MONEY

| Aspect           | Details                                                   |
| ---------------- | --------------------------------------------------------- |
| **How to Start** | Collect 40 items in Bonus Wave MBs (50 on Hard)           |
| **Objective**    | Destroy Mothership by hitting shots, then center ramp     |
| **Progression**  | Mothership → Grandmother Ship → Mother of All Motherships |
| **Scoring**      | A good MSMB = 200-300M points! <kcite ref="44"/>          |

> 💡 **Pro Strategy:** During ball save, bat at center to destroy mothership. When Grandmother Ship appears (ball save off), trap on right flipper and shoot Lock shot repeatedly - it's safest and does decent damage. ALL shots should target Lock. <kcite ref="44"/>

### Hypno Multiball

| Aspect           | Details                                               |
| ---------------- | ----------------------------------------------------- |
| **How to Start** | Stroke of Luck award                                  |
| **Objective**    | Lower "Beam Integrity" to 0% (~15 center target hits) |
| **Rewards**      | 1st completion: Extra Ball, 2nd: 15M                  |
| **Special**      | Hitting spirals adds balls + ball saver               |

## 📈 Progression Systems

### Saucer Lights (9 Total Required for Attack Mars)

**Ways to Earn Saucer Lights:**

| Method                   | Lights Earned |
| ------------------------ | ------------- |
| Flawless mode completion | 1             |
| Regular MB Super Jackpot | 1             |
| Martian MB (10 kills)    | 1             |
| Martian MB (30 kills)    | 1             |
| Bonus Wave (15 items)    | 1             |
| Stroke of Luck award     | 1             |

### Missiles

- Earned via orbits/ramps and Super Skill Shot (3 missiles)
- Used during modes for attacks
- **20 missiles** = Extra Ball lights at Stroke of Luck <kcite ref="65"/>
- **30 missiles** = 5M points
- **50 missiles** = Extra Ball lights again

### Bonus Multiplier

- Advanced via rollover lanes (upper left + inlanes/outlanes)
- Can be held between balls via Stroke of Luck

1. **Early game:** Build captures while completing modes
2. **Mid game:** Complete mode groups → Bonus Wave MBs
3. **Late game:** Mothership MB and Attack Mars

## 🧙 Wizard Modes

### Attack Mars (Mini-Wizard Mode)

**Requirements:** Light all 9 Saucer Lights + complete all modes

**Three Stages:**

| Stage             | Objective                                           | Tips                                                                                |
| ----------------- | --------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **1. Radar**      | Destroy 3 ships using Action Buttons to aim         | Line up shots carefully                                                             |
| **2. Satellites** | Destroy 4 targets using main shots + Action Buttons | 4 different shots = 2X bonus, 5 different = 4X bonus <kcite ref="46"/>              |
| **3. Corn City**  | Shoot cornhead until energy = 0, then center ramp   | Add balls via martian targets, ramps/loops add 10M to stage bonus <kcite ref="46"/> |

**Final Shot Award:** 100M+ for destroying the Big Brain martian!

### Mothership Multiball (Ultimate Wizard Mode)

This IS the ultimate goal - see Multiballs section above. Destroying multiple ships in succession leads to the "Mother of All Motherships" for enormous points.

## 🎯 Shot Analysis

### 🟢 Safe Shots

| Shot                  | Why It's Safe                               | Best Use                                          |
| --------------------- | ------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------- |
| **Lock/Capture Lane** | Ball returns predictably, advances captures | Primary target during multiball <kcite ref="44"/> |
| **Left Ramp**         | Clean feed back to flipper with ramp pass   | Mode completion, item collection                  |
| **Right Ramp**        | Similar to left ramp                        | Alternate when left is blocked                    |

### 🔴 Dangerous Shots

| Shot                | Risk                                        | Mitigation                                                                               |
| ------------------- | ------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Center Targets**  | Ball can bounce unpredictably toward drains | Only shoot during ball save or when necessary                                            |
| **Orbits**          | Fast ball return, can catch you off-guard   | Hold flipper for controlled catch                                                        |
| **Jet Bumper Exit** | Ball comes down fast to left flipper        | Let ball hit left flipper - it should bounce to right flipper for trap <kcite ref="42"/> |

### Ball Control Tips

| Situation                  | Technique                                                                                        |
| -------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Jet exit**               | Let ball hit left flipper without flipping - bounces to right flipper for trap <kcite ref="42"/> |
| **Stroke of Luck kickout** | Don't flip - ball bounces to right flipper for trap <kcite ref="42"/>                            |
| **Ramp returns**           | Hold corresponding flipper to pass back to original flipper <kcite ref="42"/>                    |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy: Path to 1 Billion

### Phase 1: Early Game (0-100M)

- [ ] Execute Super Skill Shots every ball
- [ ] Complete modes efficiently (don't obsess over Flawless)
- [ ] Lock balls when possible
- [ ] Collect missiles (20 = Extra Ball opportunity)

### Phase 2: Mid Game (100-400M)

- [ ] Complete all 3 mode groups
- [ ] Maximize Bonus Wave Multiballs:
  - Move multiplier to center first
  - Collect 40+ items per Bonus Wave
  - DON'T end Bonus Wave early - keep shooting ramps!
- [ ] Earn Saucer Lights via Super Jackpots

### Phase 3: Late Game (400M-1B+)

- [ ] Reach Mothership Multiball (40 items in Bonus Wave)
- [ ] Destroy multiple Motherships (200-300M per good MSMB) <kcite ref="44"/>
- [ ] Complete Attack Mars for 100M+ finale
- [ ] Repeat Bonus Wave → Mothership cycle

## 🥚 Easter Eggs & Hidden Features

### Cows & Corn

Look for hidden cows and corn throughout the game - they appear in various modes and on the backglass! <kcite ref="41"/>

### Software Update

RFM supports software updates via serial cable connection. Latest version is 1.4. <kcite ref="41"/>

### Exploit: Ramp Switch Trick

> ⚠️ **Use at your own risk - may trigger tilt**
> 
> When ball drains with center ramp up (waiting to finish mode), a quick jolt (bangback-style) can trigger the ramp switch due to the trough weight, fooling the game into thinking you made the shot. <kcite ref="47"/>

### Exploit: Extended Mothership MB

During Mothership MB with one ball trapped and one draining, shoot the ramp just as the last ball enters the drain. This destroys the current ship and brings out the next - you're now playing 1-ball MSMB! Downside: no more modes that ball. Upside: enormous points after 6-7 ships. <kcite ref="47"/>

## 🏆 Summary: The 1 Billion Point Formula

1. **Super Skill Shot** every ball (+1M + 3 missiles)
2. **Complete mode groups** efficiently (not perfectly)
3. **Maximize Bonus Wave MBs** - collect 40+ items, use multiplier
4. **Reach Mothership Multiball** - this is where games are won
5. **Destroy multiple Motherships** - 200-300M per good run
6. **Complete Attack Mars** - 100M+ finale
7. **Repeat** - multiple Mothership runs = billion point games

> 🎯 **The key insight:** Your 300M game becomes a 600M game in Attack Mars. Your 600M game becomes a billion in Mothership Multiball. Focus on reaching these wizard modes! <kcite ref="41"/>
