# ⚖️ JUDGE DREDD Pinball Machine - Complete Strategy Guide
## *Path to 500 Million Points*

> **"I Am The Law!"**

Based on the Judge Dredd comic license, this 1993 Bally widebody machine delivers a complex rule set featuring nine Chain Link modes, progressive multiballs, and the Ultimate Challenge wizard mode. Master the crime scenes and reach the coveted **500 million point** milestone <kcite ref="1"/>.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Ball 1:** Learn the ramp shots; focus on completing Crime Scenes
- [ ] **Advance Crime Levels** via the Advance target to build multipliers
- [ ] **Spell JUDGE** with drop targets to light lock
- [ ] **Complete Chain Links** sequentially (modes last ~20 seconds each)
- [ ] **Collect all four jackpots** during Deadworld Multiball
- [ ] **Start Block War Multiball** after completing every 4 Crime Scenes
- [ ] **Build toward Ultimate Challenge** by completing all 9 modes
- [ ] **Use Super Game to accelerate** progression after first multiball

## 🔴 Crime Scenes & Levels

The playfield has **five Crime Scene locations** spread across multiple shot types. Each advances independently through four levels:

| Level             | Color  | Points Per Scene | Description                          |
| ----------------- | ------ | ---------------- | ------------------------------------ |
| **Warning**       | Green  | 1M               | First level; easily achieved         |
| **Misdemeanor**   | Yellow | 2M               | Second level                         |
| **Felony**        | Red    | 3M               | Third level                          |
| **Class X Felony**| White  | 4M               | Maximum level; hardest to reach      |

### Crime Scene Locations:
- **Left Orbit**
- **Small Loop** (Underground orbit)
- **Sniper Tower** (Ball popper)
- **Large Loop**
- **Right Ramp** (Stake Out)

### Advance Crime Level Target

Hit the **Advance target** (blue stand-up '?') to:
- Award **1M points**
- Advance ALL lit Crime Scenes one level up
- Build toward Extra Balls at 7 scenes, 22 scenes, then every 15 thereafter

> ⚠️ **Warning:** Crime Level advancement is crucial for scoring exponentially. Target the Advance button repeatedly after each mode <kcite ref="1"/>.

## 🔓 Playfield Features & Key Shots

### Left Ramp (Lock Ramp)
- **Function:** Starts Chain Link modes when lit; principal lock mechanism
- **Default feed:** Right inlane (clean trap position)
- **Scoring:** 5-6M base value; increases when modes are active

### Right Ramp (Stake Out Crime Scene)
- **Function:** Crime Scene shot; mode requirement
- **Default feed:** Left inlane
- **Scoring:** 3M unlit, increases with Crime Level

### Centre Ramp
- **Function:** Difficult high-angled shot; used in modes
- **Feeding:** Feeds to left orbit entrance
- **Risk:** Fast return to upper flipper; SDTM threat

### Large Loop (Skill Shot Route)
- **Function:** Primary skill shot
- **Value:** 5M, 10M, 15M+ for consecutive shots
- **Safety:** Best shot from right flipper

### Small Loop (Underground Crime Scene)
- **Function:** Crime Scene; slower rolling shot
- **Scoring:** 1-4M per Crime Level; +3M consecutive shots
- **Combo:** Part of two-way and three-way combos

### Sniper Tower
- **Function:** Pops ball up; used in Sniper mode
- **Auto-feed:** Returns to right inlane
- **Scoring:** 3M in modes; can be mode requirement

### JUDGE Drop Targets
- **Count:** Five targets spelling J-U-D-G-E
- **Function:** Complete the word to light lock
- **Scoring:** +1M per target hit (adds to Deadworld Multiball jackpot)

### Advance Crime Level Target (Blue '?')
- **Function:** Critical target for progression
- **Scoring:** 1M per hit
- **Effect:** Advances ALL lit Crime Scenes one level

### Subway Scoop
- **Function:** Ball holder; jackpot collection in multiball
- **Used in:** Safe Cracker mode, multiball, post-mode awards
- **Safety:** Controlled exit allows setup

### Reactors (3 Captive Balls)
- **Function:** Melt Down mode requirement
- **Bonus Effect:** Third hit doubles end-of-ball bonus that ball
- **Scoring:** 10M per hit in mode

## 🎰 Deadworld Multiball

### Lighting & Starting

**Requirement:** Spell **JUDGE** with the five drop targets
- Targets track individually
- Multiple players can progress toward lock simultaneously
- Once complete, next JUDGE letter shot locks the ball

**Lock Sequence:**
1. **First Lock:** Shoot left ramp; ball parks on rotating planet (Dead World)
2. **Second Lock:** Shoot left ramp again after subsequent play
3. **Third Lock:** Third JUDGE completion triggers multiball with auto-plunge of 3 additional balls (total 4 balls)

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** In multiplayer, lock targets are accessible to all players. Plan your JUDGE progression carefully on later balls <kcite ref="1"/>.

### Jackpot Building

| Action                         | Jackpot Increase |
| ------------------------------ | ---------------- |
| Hit drop target in any context | +1M              |
| Base at start                  | 20-25M           |
| Each jackpot collected         | Resets to base   |

### Four Sequential Jackpots

During Deadworld Multiball, four jackpot stages unlock:

| Jackpot | Value              | Notes                          |
| ------- | ------------------ | ------------------------------ |
| Mortis  | 20-25M + accrual   | First jackpot collected        |
| Fire    | Mortis + 5M        | Second jackpot; progressive    |
| Fear    | Fire + 5M          | Third jackpot                  |
| Death   | Fear + 5M          | Final jackpot; triggers reload |

**Collecting Jackpots:** Shoot left ramp to light; hit Subway scoop to collect <kcite ref="1"/>.

**Post-Death Effect:** After Death Jackpot collected, any previously drained balls during multiball are relaunched, extending the multiball.

**Multiball End:** Ends when fewer than two balls remain in play.

## 💫 Drain Shield & Safety Features

**Activation:** Automatic after first major shot or 4-5 switch hits

**Duration:** Factory default 5 seconds (operator-configurable)

**Effect:** Ball caught at drain lane without loss. Audio cue when deactivated. Provides crucial protection early in ball.

## 🎁 End-of-Ball Bonus

### Base Bonus Calculation

Accumulated through play based on:
- Chain Links started (3M each)
- Crime Scenes advanced
- Drop targets hit
- Mode progression

### Reactor Doubler

**Critical Mechanic:** Hitting the third captive ball in **Melt Down mode** doubles your end-of-ball bonus for that entire ball.
