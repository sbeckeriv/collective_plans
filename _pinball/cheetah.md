# 🐆 CHEETAH Pinball Machine - Strategy Guide

*Stern Electronics, 1980 — Early Solid-State Widebody*

> **Speed and precision on a widebody classic.**

Cheetah is a pure, early solid-state pinball experience from the golden age of mechanical design. With its four drop-target banks, responsive flipper control demands, and straightforward but challenging bonus system, it rewards focus and ball control. Our goal: reach **7–8 million points**, a realistic competitive milestone on well-maintained machines.

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Understand the collect bonus lane:** This narrow left orbit is your money shot once you've built bonus
- [ ] **Complete the numbered 5-bank first:** Hit targets 1–5 in order to light bonus collection
- [ ] **Build multipliers:** Complete the top 3-bank drop targets to increase bonus multiplier
- [ ] **Preserve center shots:** Use flipper control to avoid chaotic center play
- [ ] **Chase extra balls:** Light the inner return lanes and horseshoe for EB opportunities
- [ ] **Master ball control:** Cradle trapping and smooth shots are essential on this widebody

## 🎱 Core Mechanics

### Drop Target Banks (4 Total)

| Location | Target Count | Purpose | Base Value |
|----------|--------------|---------|------------|
| **Mid-upper left** | 5-bank | Numbered sequence (1–5) | 5K (lit), 500 (unlit) |
| **Top/bonus multiplier** | 3-bank | Multiplier advancement | 10K → 15K → 20K → 25K |
| **Right side** | 3-bank | Bonus advance | Adds 10K per hit |
| **Front horseshoe** | 3-bank | Extra ball setup | Contributes to EB lights |

**Numbered 5-Bank Rule:** You must hit the targets in numeric order (1, 2, 3, 4, 5). If you hit an out-of-sequence target, the bank resets and you must complete the full sequence again to light bonus collection.

### Bonus System

**Base Bonus:** Starts at 10K. Each hit to the right 3-bank or outer return lane advances by 10K. Side drains (outer lanes) triple this advancement (30K per drain). Maximum bonus: **100K** <kcite ref="1"/>.

**Bonus Multiplier:** Earned by completing the top 3-bank targets. Multiplier increases with each completion (1x, 2x, 3x, etc.).

**Collection:** Once the numbered 5-bank is completed, bonus becomes collectible via the narrow left orbit shot. Bonus held at ball end = points awarded next ball.

**Variable Rules:** Some machines allow repeated bonus collection per ball; others allow only one collection. **Check your machine's ROM settings before play.** If repeated collection is enabled, camp the collect lane once you have a solid multiplier. If only one collection per ball, build aggressively elsewhere and time your collection for maximum value.

### Spinners

Three spinners on the playfield:
- **Lit spinner:** 3K per spin
- **Unlit spinner:** 1K per spin
- Star targets light spinners for bonus advancement and general playfield activity

### Right Chute & Kickback Lane

The middle-right kickback lane scores **25K** and spots the next number in the numbered 5-bank. This is a valuable automatic playfield feature that adds points and progress simultaneously.

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

### High-Risk Areas

| Shot | Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------|-----------|
| **Direct center plunge** | Pop bumper chaos, unpredictable; often ends in drain | Aim for upper flippers or right chute via smooth plunges |
| **Missed left ramp/orbit** | Can deflect to outlanes | Trap on upper flipper; execute smooth shots only when controlled |
| **Aggressive pop bumper play** | Bounces to center/outlanes; ball lacks predictability | Use pop bumpers as bonus-builders only, not primary targets |
| **Horseshoe overshot** | Ball can skip past and drain right if shot too hard | Soft touch required; use controlled flipper shots |

### Safe Control Positions

- **Upper flipper cradle:** Best position for planning next shot
- **Lower flipper trap:** Allows setup for right chute or left ramp
- **Post pass/live catch:** Transfer between flippers safely on orbit returns

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Ball (Ball 1)

1. **Complete the numbered 5-bank** (even without multiplier) — this lights bonus collection for future balls
2. **Build bonus base** by hitting right 3-bank and outer lanes (10K per hit)
3. **Start multiplier work** — begin completing top 3-bank if safe
4. **Avoid drain risk** — play conservatively; losing Ball 1 early hurts your multiplier progress

**Target Score:** 500K–2M

### Mid Ball (Ball 2)

1. **Advance multiplier** if you didn't finish top 3-bank on Ball 1
2. **Build bonus aggressively** — hit right 3-bank and outer lanes repeatedly (each outer lane = 30K bonus advance)
3. **Collect bonus** via left orbit if your multiplier is solid (2x or higher)
4. **Chase extra balls** — if inner return lanes or horseshoe become lit, aim for them

**Target Score:** 2M–4M

### Late Ball (Ball 3+)

1. **Maximize bonus multiplier** — if you have control, complete top 3-bank one more time for higher multiplier
2. **Collect bonus repeatedly** (if ROM allows) on every ball; prioritize the left orbit
3. **Hit right chute** to spot numbers and rack up 25K bonus value
4. **Play for positioning** — extra balls extend your game and scoring window

**Target Score per ball:** 1M–3M

### Final Push (Ball 4, or After Extra Balls)

- Go for broke: attack the left collect lane and spinners relentlessly
- If you have 2+ multiplier, each bonus collection yields substantial points
- Extra ball lights (inner lanes, horseshoe) provide one final scoring push

## 🎯 Shot Analysis

### The Left Collect Bonus Lane (Your Primary Target)

**Why it matters:** Once bonus is built and multiplier is active, this narrow left orbit is your best money shot, worth multiples of 10K base bonus × multiplier. A 100K bonus at 3x multiplier = 300K on one shot.

**How to execute:**
- Trap ball on upper flipper
- Aim for smooth, controlled left orbit shot
- Time it after building bonus via other targets
- Repeat as often as ROM allows

### The Numbered 5-Bank (Progression)

**Why it matters:** This is your gate to bonus collection. Without it, all bonus sits unused.

**How to execute:**
- Hit targets 1 → 2 → 3 → 4 → 5 in order
- If you miss or hit out of sequence, you reset and start over
- Work on this early in each ball
- Once lit, the right chute helps you spot missing numbers

### The Top Multiplier 3-Bank

**Why it matters:** Each completion increases your bonus multiplier (1x → 2x → 3x, etc.), directly multiplying your collected bonus value.

**How to execute:**
- Upper flipper shots toward the top-right area
- Requires precise ball control
- Best done when you're not under drain pressure
- Completion values escalate (10K, 15K, 20K, 25K)

### Spinners (Secondary Bonus Builders)

**Why they matter:** Easy hits that advance bonus by 1K + light star targets.

**How to execute:**
- Use them to build bonus safely when bonus is low
- Not worth chasing if you're already at 100K bonus
- Good choice when you're playing defensively

### Right Chute (Automatic Progress)

**Why it matters:** 25K points + spots the next number in your numbered 5-bank. Automatic kickback feature makes this a free bonus builder if the ball drains left.

**How to execute:**
- Aim gentle right ramps or right-side shots toward the chute opening
- Happens automatically if ball drifts left toward the middle

## 🎲 Match & Bonus Multiplier Growth

**Bonus multiplier resets at each new ball**, so preserve your earned multiplier bonuses via high collection values. The growth pattern:

- **After 1st top 3-bank completion:** 2x multiplier
- **After 2nd completion:** 3x multiplier
- **After 3rd+ completions:** 4x, 5x, etc. (game-specific, may cap at 5x or 10x)

Each subsequent multiplier completion after the first side 3-bank hit also advances multiplier and increases in value, making late-game strategy tied to high multiplier accumulation.

## 📋 Key Setup Notes

Cheetah is a **widebody** with a large playfield. Ball control and positioning are paramount. Practice:
- **Cradle trapping** on the upper flipper to plan your next shot
- **Controlled flipper shots** to avoid wild bounces off the 4 drop-target banks
- **Orbit feeds** that lead safely to flipper positions rather than direct-to-drain paths
- **Gentle right-side ramps** that funnel toward the right chute (25K + number spot)

The machine rewards **precision over speed**. Patience in building bonus and multiplier beats aggressive, chaotic play.
