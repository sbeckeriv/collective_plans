# 🧊 Demolition Man - Strategy Guide

*Williams, 1994 — Designed by Dennis Nordman*

> **"Send a maniac to catch a maniac."**

Demolition Man is a widebody SuperPin built around the Cryo-Claw — a player-controlled magnetic claw that drops the ball into 5 distinct award lanes — feeding into four named multiballs (Fortress, Museum, Wasteland, Cryo-Prison) and a final combined **Demolition Jackpot**. Combos are the scoring backbone throughout. Our goal: cycle all 5 Claw modes, run all four multiballs, and cash the Demolition Jackpot.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Build combos relentlessly** — they start at 5M and escalate (6M, 7M...) with no apparent ceiling; this is the scoring backbone of the entire game
- [ ] **Shoot the right ramp when lit to access the Cryo-Claw**, then steer it with the gun triggers into one of 5 award lanes
- [ ] **Hit 10 combos for a Computer award**, 12 combos for your first extra ball, and 50 combos for a second
- [ ] **Build yellow targets toward a 5x bonus multiplier** — also lights an extra ball on the left loop
- [ ] **Watch the outlanes carefully** — this game's physics are notoriously unpredictable and the outlane dividers are genuinely dangerous
- [ ] **Complete all 5 Claw modes** to light Demolition Time, the game's wizard mode

---

## 🦾 The Cryo-Claw (5 Award Lanes)

Access the Claw via the **right ramp when lit**, then steer the magnetic claw with the gun triggers into one of five lanes:

| Lane | Award |
|---|---|
| **Start AcMag** | Ball returns to left inlane; center ramp shots award escalating 5M→12.5M, then 6M→13.5M (+1M per success) |
| **Lock Freeze** | Locks flashing Freeze lamps, or starts multiball directly if required locks are already frozen |
| **Prison Break** | Ball drops above upper-left flipper for 15M base; reflex shot to Underground doubles it (30M); side ramp triples it (45M); lights Car Chase (+5M per ramp) |
| **Super Jets** | Jet bumpers lit for 25 hits at 1M each; yellow targets extend the hit count |
| **Capture Simon** | Ball served via tunnel to the right ramp; 7 major shots light for 5M each (3 needed) = 40M total |

> 🎯 **Pro Tip:** All Claw awards increase in value on repeat visits — don't treat the Claw as a one-time bonus, cycle back to it throughout the game.

---

## 🎁 The Four Multiballs

| Multiball | Requirement | Jackpot Structure |
|---|---|---|
| **Fortress** | 1 frozen ball | Center Ramp/Underground/Left Ramp = jackpots; Right Ramp = Super (3x value); base 20M–35M + 2M/yellow target |
| **Museum** | 2 frozen balls | All shots lit for jackpots; Right Ramp becomes Super once all collected; base 25M min + 1M/jet bumper |
| **Wasteland** | More freezes | Mirrors Fortress structure but with larger jackpots and multiple simultaneous lit jackpots |
| **Cryo-Prison** | Most freezes | All jackpot shots feed into a single Super Jackpot (lit at Right Ramp); regular jackpots don't score independently |

**Lock balls** via the left ramp or through the Claw's Lock Freeze mode. **Completing Cryo-Prison resets the sequence back to Fortress Multiball** for another full cycle.

---

## 💰 The Demolition Jackpot

After completing **all four multiball types**, a special shot appears on the **Right Ramp**. Its value is calculated as:

```
(Fortress jackpots + 1) × 15M
+ (Museum jackpots + 1) × 20M
+ (Wasteland jackpots + 1) × 25M
+ (Cryo-Prison jackpots + 1) × 30M
```

**Minimum possible value: 90M**, even with zero jackpots collected in any category — meaning simply completing all four multiball types guarantees a substantial payout.

> ⚠️ **Starting another multiball cancels a lit Demolition Jackpot** — collect it before chasing anything else once it's available.

---

## 🏆 Demolition Time (Wizard Mode)

**Requirement:** Complete all 5 Claw modes, then shoot the **Underground** shot.

- All major playfield shots stay lit at **15M** throughout
- Completing **5 yellow targets adds a ball** and **increases the jackpot by 5M**; losing a ball decreases it
- Starts with an immediate **50M bonus** plus dedicated sound/animation
- Tracked separately on the final score table as **"Demolition Time Total"**

---

## 🔗 Combos & Secondary Scoring

- **Combos** are the backbone of scoring: first combo = 5M, escalating with each successive combo (6M, 7M...) with no apparent ceiling
- **Skill shots** boost the base combo value by +1M
- **Explode shots** (unlit Underground, followed by ramp hits) = 10M each
- **Computer award** (every 10 combos) grants random bonuses, including "Light Explodes"
- **Retina Scan**: triggers an Explode Hurry-Up at escalating intervals (first at 1 hit, then every 4 hits); 3 hits also lights an extra ball
- **Car Crash targets**: 3M, 6M, 10M for three hits — doubles if the top car impacts the target
- **MTL rollovers**: advance bonus multiplier (2x–5x); completing the sequence lights Claw access

---

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **Outlanes** | Genuinely dangerous — the ball tends to have unpredictable spin near the inlane/outlane dividers, and the game shipped without outlane post rubber on some units | Play conservatively near the outlanes; don't assume standard rubber behavior |
| **"Weird physics" overall** | The game is known for uncontrolled air-clearing trajectories and spin-induced ricochets | Stay alert on every shot rather than assuming standard ball behavior |
| **Car Crash target deflection** | Can send the ball straight down the middle | Built-in ball-saver logic compensates somewhat, but don't rely on it exclusively |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Game (Ball 1)

1. Start building combos immediately — this is your main scoring engine all game
2. Access the Cryo-Claw via the right ramp and try a couple of different award lanes
3. Build yellow targets toward your bonus multiplier and Quick Freeze

**Target Score:** 30M–80M

### Mid Game (Ball 2)

1. Lock balls and start Fortress Multiball, working toward Museum and Wasteland
2. Push combos toward 12 for your first extra ball
3. Keep cycling the Claw for escalating repeat-visit values

**Target Score:** 80M–200M

### Late Game (Ball 3+)

1. Complete all four multiball types to light the Demolition Jackpot (minimum 90M) — collect it before starting anything else
2. If all 5 Claw modes are done, shoot Underground for Demolition Time
3. Push combos toward 50 for your second extra ball

**Target Score per ball:** 100M–400M+

## ⚙️ Setup Recommendations

| Setting | Recommendation | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| **Playfield pitch** | 6.5°–7.0° | Standard for mid-90s Williams widebody machines |
| **Outlane post rubber** | Verify installed and in good condition | Some units shipped without it — a significant difficulty/safety difference |
| **Cryo-Claw mechanism** | Check smooth magnetic pickup and gun-trigger steering | Central to the entire game's award structure |

---

## 📋 Quick Reference: Strategic Priorities

**Ball 1:** Build combos, sample the Claw, work yellow targets.
**Ball 2:** Start Fortress Multiball, push combos toward 12, keep cycling the Claw.
**Ball 3+:** Complete all four multiballs for the Demolition Jackpot, finish all 5 Claw modes for Demolition Time.

**Target High-Score Ballpark:** **200–500 million points** is a strong, competitive game — collecting the Demolition Jackpot and reaching Demolition Time both represent real mastery of this deep ruleset.
