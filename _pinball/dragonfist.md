## 🎱 Core Mechanics

### Drop Target Banks

The machine features **two 3-bank drop target arrays** in unusual side-hitting positions (left and right orbit lanes). The key distinction:

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Lit Bank** | Travels around the playfield (similar to Seawitch) |
| **Progression** | Complete the lit 3-bank to advance **Bonus X** |
| **Reset** | Once a bank is down, hitting it again resets it |
| **Side-Hit Design** | Allows balls to approach from orbit lanes |

**Bonus X Mechanic:** Each time you complete the traveling lit drop target bank, Bonus X advances one level. Bonus X directly increases the point value of the **right spinner**. This is the core loop of Dragonfist scoring.

### Standup Targets (Yellow)

Nine yellow standup targets dot the playfield:
- **Primary Function:** Advance the **left lane value**
- **Lit Targets:** Shoot them whenever lit to build lane payout
- **Strategy:** If bonus X is low or unreliable, hit yellow standups instead to build left lane value, then aim for the left orbit

### The Right Spinner

The right spinner is the machine's **hardest and highest-value shot**:
- **Base Value:** Multiplied by **Bonus X** level
- **Difficulty:** Notoriously hard to line up due to its positioning
- **Mechanics:** Each spin counts; the backglass tracks total spins
- **Milestone Bonuses:** Special bonus awards trigger at 20, 40, 60, and 80 total spins during a game

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** If you can land the right spinner consistently and have built Bonus X to 3–4, the spinner becomes your primary money shot. Otherwise, prioritize the left lane or standup targets. <kcite ref="1"/>

### Star Rollover & Left Lane

The machine has one star rollover that contributes to lane lighting. The **left lane (orbit)** is where you cash out your built-up value after hitting yellow standups or completing the drop target banks.

> ⚠️ **WARNING:** The left orbit feeds directly to a **center drain** path. This is the machine's most dangerous shot. Aim carefully and be prepared to nudge if the ball heads center. <kcite ref="2"/>

### Pop Bumpers & Slingshots

Two pop bumpers and two slingshots provide:
- General playfield activity and bonus advancement
- Chaotic bounces; not primary scoring targets
- Useful for defensive play when under pressure

## 🎯 Shot Analysis

### The Left Orbit (Your Money Shot)

**Why it matters:** Once you've built yellow standup targets, the left orbit is your cash-out, worth a large payout multiplied by any advance you've made via standups.

**How to execute:**
- Trap on left flipper
- Ensure you've built yellow target value (ideally 3+ standups lit)
- Aim for the left lane with smooth flipper control
- **Be ready to nudge if the ball heads center** — this is critical

**Risk/Reward:** High reward if positioned correctly; high risk if you're not prepared for center-drain threat.

### The Right Spinner (Advanced Play)

**Why it matters:** With high Bonus X (3+), the right spinner becomes your best-case money shot, worth potentially 30K–50K+ per spin.

**How to execute:**
- Build Bonus X to at least 2 before attempting
- Approach from right flipper
- Hit the spinner smoothly and let it spin multiple times
- Play for spinner milestone bonuses (20, 40, 60, 80 spins)

**Difficulty:** Hardest shot on the machine. Many players skip this and focus on left lane + yellow standups instead.

### Yellow Standup Targets (Reliable Scoring)

**Why they matter:** Direct path to building left lane value. Each yellow target lit increases the payout of the left orbit.

**How to execute:**
- Shoot them whenever you have the opportunity
- No sequence required; hit them in any order
- Build up 3–5 standups, then execute the left orbit shot
- Repeat this cycle

**Risk/Reward:** Low risk, steady progression. Your most reliable strategy.

### Right Drop Target Bank (Bonus X Builder)

**Why it matters:** Completing the lit bank advances Bonus X, which multiplies right spinner value and overall scoring.

**How to execute:**
- Hit all three targets in the lit bank
- Position matters because the bank side-hits from the orbit
- Once complete, the lit bank moves to another location
- Attempt again if the new position is safe

**Difficulty:** Moderate; requires good positioning but not as hard as the right spinner.

### Spinner Spin Milestones

**Why they matter:** Track your total spinner revolutions. At 20, 40, 60, and 80 spins, you earn special bonus awards.

**How to execute:**
- Hit the right spinner repeatedly
- The backglass keeps a running count
- Shoot the spinner intentionally to reach milestone thresholds
- Plan your ball to accumulate spins for milestone payouts
