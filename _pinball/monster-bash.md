# 🧟 Monster Bash - Strategy Guide

*Williams, 1998 — Designed by George Gomez*

> **"Start two things rather than finish one."**

Monster Bash is a beloved George Gomez design where six Universal Monsters each have a mode and an instrument to collect, feeding into the Monsters of Rock final wizard mode. The core philosophy, straight from veteran players: it's almost always better to light and stack multiple modes than to grind one to completion alone. Our goal: stack modes before triggering multiball (which freezes all mode timers), collect all six instruments, and reach Monsters of Rock.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Take the top lane skill shot** for a Smart Missile
- [ ] **Light modes without starting them whenever possible**, then start 2-4 at once before triggering multiball
- [ ] **During any multiball, all mode timers stop** — this is the single most important rule in the game; stacking a hurry-up mode (Creature Feature, Full Moon Fever) into multiball locks in maximum value
- [ ] **Priority order for starting modes**: Creature Feature first (easiest, overlaps with other shots), then Mummy Mayhem progress (bumper hits), then Ball & Chain, then Frankenstein (usually lights by accident — don't chase it without stacking), then Dracula last (easiest to finish during multiball anyway)
- [ ] **Avoid the center spinner unless you mean it** — it's aligned perfectly with the center drain and will instantly kill you on a miss
- [ ] **The Phantom Flip feature is widely disliked** — it auto-flips the ball via game control and frequently causes drains; many competitive settings disable it

## 🎸 The Six Monster Modes

| Mode | How to Start | Objective | Instrument |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Creature Feature** | Shoot the creature saucer 4x | Hit all 5 major shots; 1st awards a hurry-up (1M→500k), later shots multiply it | Saxophone |
| **Ball & Chain** | Shoot both non-Frankenstein ramps 3x each | 30-sec timed mode; ramps award double spinner value, victory laps at triple | Microphone |
| **Frankenstein Multiball** | Collect 7 body parts via standup hits (lowers target, exposes ramp) | 3-ball multiball; all shots 400k+ (escalating), super 1M+ | Keyboard |
| **Mummy Mayhem** | Hit 60 bumpers (hardest mode to start) | 45-sec mode; all switches 50k, major shots 500k jackpot; reach 7.5M | Bass |
| **Full Moon Fever** | Shoot either orbit 4x | Collect 4 hurry-ups (1M/2M/3M/4M) at orbits | Drums |
| **Drac Attack** | Spell D-R-A-C-U-L-A on red standups | 30-sec mode; hit the flying Dracula toy 5x (doubled hits worth more) | Guitar |

> 🎯 **Pro Tip — Stacking Philosophy:** "Broadly speaking, it's generally better to start two things than it is to finish one." Light modes passively, start several together, then dive into multiball — since mode timers freeze during multiball, this is how you bank maximum hurry-up value safely.

## 🎁 Multiball Systems

### Frankenstein Multiball (3-Ball)

Started by collecting all 7 body parts (see table above). This is the most lucrative individual mode — but **don't play it without stacking other modes first**, since it usually lights somewhat accidentally during normal play.

### Mosh Pit Multiball (2-Ball, Alternative)

- Light at the scoop after **6 center spinner shots** during single-ball play
- All shots worth **750k**; spinner increases jackpot value
- Hit the Frankenstein ramp **10 times** for the Super Jackpot
- ⚠️ **Genuinely dangerous:** "Six shots that are liable to instantly kill you if you're anything less than perfect" — the center spinner drains straight down the middle on a miss. High value, but optional; not required to reach the endgame.

---

## 🏆 Wizard Modes

### Monster Bash (Intermediate Wizard)

- **Light at the scoop** after activating all 6 monster characters (modes continue running)
- **4-ball multiball**, all shots worth **2M**
- Typical value: **50M on a bad day, 100M+ on a good one**

### Monsters of Rock (Final Wizard)

- **Light at the scoop** after collecting **all 6 instrument rewards** — supersedes Monster Bash if both are lit simultaneously
- **Startup bonus:** 5M per instrument collected + 25M per prior Monsters of Rock completion
- **4-ball multiball**, monsters worth 4M each; jackpots multiplied **5-10X** based on your collection count
- The spinner spots an uncollected monster if you miss shots
- Roughly **twice as valuable** as Monster Bash — estimated **55M+ minimum**

### Lyman's Lament (Secret/Novelty Mode)

Shoot the scoop **44 times** to hear all venue callouts; a fun Easter egg with increased switch values and bonus multiplier, but less valuable than Monsters of Rock — primarily for entertainment.

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **Center spinner** | Aligned perfectly with the center drain — an instant kill on a miss | Only shoot it deliberately for Mosh Pit qualification, never casually |
| **Right ramp miss** | Usually sends the ball straight down the middle | Only attempt with a controlled, trapped-ball setup |
| **Red standups (right side)** | Genuinely annoying to hit safely | Many players prefer hitting these during multiball, when drain consequences are lower |
| **Phantom Flip feature** | Game-controlled auto-flip that frequently causes unwanted drains | Often disabled in competitive settings; be cautious with lit inlanes if it's active |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Game (Ball 1)

1. Take the skill shot for a Smart Missile
2. Start Creature Feature (easiest mode to begin)
3. Build toward Mummy Mayhem passively via bumper hits

**Target Score:** 10M–30M

### Mid Game (Ball 2)

1. Stack 2-4 modes before diving into multiball
2. Run Frankenstein Multiball once you have other modes stacked alongside it
3. Continue collecting instruments toward the full set of 6

**Target Score:** 30M–80M

### Late Game (Ball 3+)

1. Activate all 6 monsters for Monster Bash (50-100M+)
2. Push to collect all 6 instruments for the more valuable Monsters of Rock (55M+ minimum)
3. Consider a Mosh Pit Multiball attempt only if you're confident in your center spinner accuracy

**Target Score per ball:** 50M–150M+

| Setting | Recommendation | Reason |
|---|---|---|

## 📋 Quick Reference: Strategic Priorities

**Ball 1:** Skill shot, start Creature Feature, build Mummy Mayhem passively.
**Ball 2:** Stack multiple modes, run Frankenstein Multiball with stacks active, collect instruments.
**Ball 3+:** Activate all 6 monsters for Monster Bash, push for all 6 instruments and Monsters of Rock.

**Target High-Score Ballpark:** **80–200 million points** is a strong, competitive game; reaching Monsters of Rock represents the true pinnacle of this beloved ruleset.
