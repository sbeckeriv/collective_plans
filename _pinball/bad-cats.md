# 🐱 Bad Cats Pinball Machine - Strategy Guide

*Williams, 1989 — Designed by Barry Oursler*

> **"Meow, meow, meow, meow."**

Bad Cats has no multiball — and doesn't need one. Between the Tiger Ramp's escalating "Unlimited Million" award, a five-step Fish Bone-Us linear target, and a literal spinning wheel of prizes, this is a game about milking a small number of very lucrative repeatable shots. Our goal: a strong **15–30 million point** game, built on Tiger Ramp grinding and Fish Bone-Us stacking.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Take the skill shot** for 2X bonus + 120K + the barbecue light
- [ ] **Grind the Tiger Ramp** with consecutive right-flipper shots — values escalate fast and cap at Unlimited Million
- [ ] **Complete drop target banks** (Bird 5-bank or Milk Bottles 3-bank) to spell BAD CATS — all 7 letters lights the Fishbowl Ramp Jackpot
- [ ] **Hit the Fish target within its 20-second window** before its value decays back to the 25K minimum
- [ ] **Secure the right inlane for the 10X multiplier** before cashing in the Garbage Can / Fish Bone-Us — it's worth up to 5 million with it active
- [ ] **Watch your outlanes** — they deduct 30K in bonus and trigger the "Bad kitty!" penalty

---

## 🐾 Core Mechanics

### Skill Shot

Successfully complete the skill shot for **2X bonus + 120K points** and it also lights the barbecue light.

### Spelling BAD CATS

Complete either the **5-target Bird bank** or the **3-target Milk Bottles bank** to earn one letter of BAD CATS. Letters **carry over across all three balls** — completing the full word is widely considered the hardest challenge in the game, since it takes sustained drop-target work across a whole game. **Completing all 7 letters lights the Fishbowl Ramp for the Jackpot.**

### Fish Bone-Us (Linear Target)

The signature gadget of the game — a Gottlieb-style vari-target that advances through five steps based on hit force:

| Step | Value |
|---|---|
| 1 | 25,000 |
| 2 | 50,000 |
| 3 | 75,000 |
| 4 | 100,000 |
| 5 | 500,000 |

⚠️ **The value decays one step every 20 seconds** back down to the 25K minimum — don't let it sit. Collect via the **Garbage Can kickout saucer**, which is worth up to **5 million** when the **10X multiplier** (lit via the right inlane) is active.

> 🎯 **Pro Tip:** Hit the Fish target hard and immediately, then cash in at the Garbage Can while the 10X multiplier is lit — this single combo is the biggest scoring play in the game.

### Tiger Ramp

Far-left ramp with escalating consecutive-shot values:

| Consecutive Shots | Value |
|---|---|
| 1st | 50,000 |
| 2nd | 100,000 |
| 3rd | 200,000 |
| 4th+ | **Unlimited Million** |

**Shots must be made without hitting any other switch in between, or the value resets to the start.** On the final ball only, a one-time **20 million** bonus is available after enough consecutive shots.

> 💰 **Pro Tip:** This is your primary, most reliable scoring engine for the entire game — once you're chaining ramp shots, every additional clean shot is a million points or more.

### Fishbowl Ramp

Center ramp progression: **30K → 50K → 100K → Extra Ball**, each shot needing to land within a 7-second window of the previous one. Completing the BAD CATS letters also lights this ramp for the **Progressive Jackpot** (starts at 1M, grows ~10% per player's score, caps around 20M).

### Seafood Table Wheel

A literal spinning prize wheel, triggered by shooting the lit **Doghouse/Mouse Hole** scoop (or automatically via the "Curiosity Spin" on the final ball). Eight possible prizes:

| Prize |
|---|
| BAD CATS letter (25K) |
| 10K points |
| Special |
| 50K points |
| Jackpot light |
| Spin Again (100K) |
| Extra Ball |
| 250K points |

### Doghouse / Mouse Hole

Light it via the lit left inlane, or shoot it unlit for a small 5K consolation (the dog barks). When lit for its 20-second window, shooting it scores 10K **and** spins the Seafood Table.

### Bonus & Multiplier

Top rollover lanes spell **T-O-Y**, building your bonus multiplier up to **7X**. Base bonus starts at 1,000 and maxes at 999,000 — multiplied bonus at game end can be a large chunk of your final score.

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Shot | Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| **Outlane drains** | Deduct 30K in bonus and trigger the "Bad kitty!" penalty animation | Play controlled, centered shots; don't force risky ramp attempts under pressure |
| **Tilting** | Forfeits all accumulated bonus | Nudge gently — this game punishes aggressive nudging hard |
| **Forcing a missed Tiger Ramp shot** | Resets your entire consecutive-shot progression back to 50K | Only attempt the ramp when you have a clean, controlled trapped-ball setup |
| **Letting Fish Bone-Us decay** | Loses value every 20 seconds it sits unclaimed | Cash in promptly rather than chasing other shots first |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Ball (Ball 1)

1. Take the skill shot for the early bonus and barbecue light
2. Start chaining Tiger Ramp shots — this is your bread and butter all game
3. Work on one drop target bank toward your first BAD CATS letters

**Target Score:** 2M–5M

### Mid Ball (Ball 2)

1. Build toward the 10X multiplier via the right inlane, then cash in Fish Bone-Us at the Garbage Can
2. Continue Tiger Ramp grinding — by now you should be hitting Unlimited Million regularly
3. Spin the Seafood Table whenever the Doghouse is lit for bonus prize variety

**Target Score:** 5M–12M

### Late Ball (Ball 3, Final Ball)

1. Chase the one-time 20M Tiger Ramp bonus if you're deep into consecutive shots
2. Finish remaining BAD CATS letters if you're close to all 7 — the Jackpot can be worth up to 20M
3. Take advantage of the Curiosity Spin on the final ball for a potential extra ball

**Target Score per ball:** 5M–15M+

## ⚙️ Setup Recommendations

| Setting | Recommendation | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| **Playfield cleanliness** | Keep clean and waxed | Tiger Ramp consecutive shots require very consistent ball speed and control |
| **Center post** | Verify it's installed and functioning | Aids ball recovery down the middle, reducing cheap drains |
| **Slingshot/inlane behavior** | Learn your machine's toggle pattern | Inlanes only alternate when the *same-side* slingshot is hit as the currently-lit inlane — understanding this helps you control the 10X multiplier lighting |

---

## 📋 Quick Reference: Strategic Priorities

**Ball 1:** Skill shot, start Tiger Ramp chaining, begin a drop target bank.
**Ball 2:** Land the 10X multiplier + Fish Bone-Us combo, keep ramping Tiger Ramp.
**Ball 3+:** Chase the 20M Tiger Ramp bonus, finish BAD CATS for the Jackpot, use Curiosity Spin.

**Target High-Score Ballpark:** **15–30 million points** is a strong, competitive game — almost entirely earned through disciplined Tiger Ramp grinding and well-timed Fish Bone-Us collections.
