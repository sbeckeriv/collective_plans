# 🧙‍♀️ SEAWITCH Pinball Machine - Strategy Guide

*Stern Electronics, 1980 — Early Solid-State*

> **Drop targets and multipliers: the witch's spell.**

Seawitch is a straightforward early solid-state machine. No modes, no wizard modes, no multiball—just clean, mechanical progression through drop targets and orbits. Multiply your bonus, light extra balls, and control your ball. Our target: **3–5 million points**, a realistic competitive score on well-maintained machines.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Understand the multiplier cycle:** Center → Left → Top → repeats
- [ ] **Complete all three drop banks on one ball** to light extra ball
- [ ] **Build bonus to 39K max:** Hit drop targets and center shots repeatedly
- [ ] **Hit the four star targets** to increase spinner value (up to 1K/spin)
- [ ] **Avoid pop bumpers:** Chaotic bounces, high drain risk
- [ ] **Execute orbits cleanly:** Once you've built bonus and multiplier, loop shots are your money shots

## 🎱 Core Mechanics

### Drop Target Banks (3 Total)

Seawitch features three distinct drop-target banks. The **lit X** designates which bank multiplies your bonus when completed.

| Bank Order | Location | Lit Value | Effect |
|-----------|----------|-----------|--------|
| **1st (Center)** | Center playfield | 2X | Bonus multiplier becomes 2X |
| **2nd (Left)** | Left side | 3X | Bonus multiplier becomes 3X |
| **3rd (Top)** | Top-right area | 4X | Bonus multiplier becomes 4X |
| **Repeat (Center)** | Center playfield | 5X | Bonus multiplier becomes 5X |
| **Repeat (Left)** | Left side | 6X | Bonus multiplier becomes 6X |
| **Repeat (Top)** | Top-right area | 7X | Bonus multiplier becomes 7X (max) |

**Key Rule:** Completing the bank with the lit X advances your multiplier permanently. The multiplier carries over from ball to ball until you reach 7X, at which point it resets to 3X on your next ball <kcite ref="1"/>.

**Bonus Completion:** Each individual target hit advances your bonus by 1,000 points. Maximum bonus: **39,000 points** <kcite ref="1"/>.

### Loop (Orbit) Scoring

The orbit loop starts at **1,000 points** and increases by 1K for each drop target completed, maxing at ~10–12K per orbit shot.

**When you complete all three drop banks on the same ball, double value activates:** your loop shot becomes **20,000 points** until the next ball <kcite ref="1"/>.

### Four Star Targets (Upper Playfield)

These four standup targets each increase spinner value by 200 points.
- **1–3 stars lit:** Spinner is unlit (minimal value)
- **All 4 stars lit:** Spinner is worth **1,000 points per spin** plus your current loop value <kcite ref="1"/>

### Extra Ball

**Condition:** Complete all three drop-target banks on a single ball.

When you accomplish this, you light an extra ball (if enabled in machine settings). The 1-2-3 lights above the left slingshot track your progress toward the complete set <kcite ref="1"/>.

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

### High-Risk Areas

| Shot | Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------|-----------|
| **Center drops from upper left flipper** | Fast rebound to center drain if shot at wrong speed | Aim carefully; soft touch or full power, not in-between |
| **Direct center plunge** | Ball can tumble into bumper area and center drain | Use soft plunge to right flipper, then sweep targets |
| **Pop bumpers** | Chaotic bounces, unpredictable; often leads to center drain or outlane | Avoid intentionally; use only for bonus build in safe situations |
| **Aggressive ball control** | Nudge/flipper combos can trigger early drains | Play with restraint; early SS machines punish excess |

### Safe Control Positions

- **Upper left flipper trap:** Best for planning drop-target shots
- **Upper right flipper trap:** Allows controlled sweeps of center/left banks
- **Soft plunge to right flipper:** Feeds safely into target banks
- **Post passes:** Transfer between flippers on orbit returns

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Ball (Ball 1)

1. **Soft plunge to right flipper** (or full plunge and flip early to upper left)
2. **Sweep the lit X bank** to advance multiplier (aim for center on first ball)
3. **Build bonus** by hitting any drop targets, spinners, and orbits
4. **Complete all three banks if safe** to light extra ball
5. **Avoid risky center shots** from upper left flipper—premature drains cost multiplier progress

**Target Score:** 500K–1.5M

### Mid Ball (Ball 2)

1. **Advance multiplier** (should be at 3X or 4X if Ball 1 went well)
2. **Build bonus aggressively:** Hit drop targets, spinners, use safe orbits
3. **Complete all three banks again** if you haven't yet—this lights another extra ball
4. **Cash in orbit shots:** With 3X+ multiplier and high bonus, loop shots worth 30K–60K each

**Target Score:** 1.5M–3M

### Late Ball (Ball 3+)

1. **Focus on high-multiplier play:** If you have 5X+ multiplier, each orbit is worth 50K–100K
2. **Complete drop banks to reach 7X** if not there yet
3. **Loop shots become your primary score source** once multiplier is maxed
4. **Light and collect extra balls** to extend game

**Target Score per ball:** 1M–2.5M

### Final Push (Ball 4, or After Extra Balls)

- **Camp the orbit** if you have 7X multiplier and high bonus (20K loop × 7X = 140K per shot!)
- **If EB lights remain:** Complete all three banks one final time for the last EB opportunity
- **Maximize scoring:** Each subsequent completion after 7X pays bonus (39K × 7X = 273K unmultiplied, but you stay at 7X)

## 🎯 Shot Analysis

### The Loop/Orbit (Your Primary Target)

**Why it matters:** Once multiplier is 5X or higher and bonus is near 39K, the orbit becomes your money shot. A 20K loop at 7X multiplier = **140K points**.

**How to execute:**
- Trap on upper flipper
- Aim for clean, controlled orbit shot
- Repeat as ball physics allow
- Each orbit advances gameplay safely

### The Drop-Target Banks (Progression Shots)

**Why they matter:** Each completion advances your multiplier and builds bonus. Completing all three on one ball lights extra ball and activates loop doubling.

**How to execute from upper left flipper:**
- **Center bank:** Straight shot, low risk if aimed with medium power
- **Left bank:** Angle shot; requires precision flipper timing
- **Top bank:** Often hit from upper right flipper; backhand from upper left is risky

**How to execute from upper right flipper:**
- **Center bank:** Can be hit directly if ball is in right cradle
- **Left bank:** Backhand shot (harder but possible)
- **Top bank:** Most natural shot from right flipper

### The Spinner (Secondary Scoring)

**Why it matters:** Safe bonus builder, and with all four star targets lit, it's worth 1K + loop value per spin (~12K at max loop).

**How to execute:**
- Right-side shots toward the spinner opening
- Repeat spins if safe
- Minimal drain risk compared to center shots

## 🎲 Bonus & Multiplier Carryover

**Bonus:** Resets to 0 at start of each new ball; maximum 39,000 points (built by hitting targets).

**Multiplier:** Carries over from ball to ball until reaching 7X, then resets to 3X on the next ball <kcite ref="1"/>.

| Multiplier | Bonus at Max (39K) | Notes |
|-----------|----------|-------|
| 1X | 39K | Starting multiplier |
| 2X | 78K | After completing center bank |
| 3X | 117K | After completing left bank |
| 4X | 156K | After completing top bank |
| 5X | 195K | 2nd center bank completion |
| 6X | 234K | 2nd left bank completion |
| 7X | 273K | 2nd top bank completion (max) |

Once at 7X, completing another bank keeps you at 7X but scores the unadvanced bonus (273K) plus orbit points with 7X multiplier.

| **Flipper strength** | Medium (factory default) | Balanced for precision and safe orbit shots |

## 📋 Key Setup Notes

Seawitch rewards **ball control and target focus** over aggressive play. Practice:
- **Soft plunges** to the right flipper to set up safe drop-target sweeps
- **Controlled flipper shots** that reach intended banks without wild rebounds
- **Orbit timing** to avoid center-drain rebounds
- **Bumper avoidance** in critical moments (bonus building, multiplier advancement)

The machine is **not a bumper banger**—it's a target-shooter. Precision beats chaos.

## 📈 Realistic High-Score Potential

Based on research, one ball can reach **~2 million points** through sustained play, targets, and orbits <kcite ref="1"/>. Across 4–5 balls with extra balls earned, a **3–5 million** game is competitive.

**Path to 5M:**
1. Ball 1: Complete center bank (2X), build to 1.5M
2. Ball 2: Complete left & top banks (reach 4X), build to 2.5M, light EB
3. Ball 3 (extra): Reach 7X multiplier, loop scoring at 100K+ per shot, build to 3.5M
4. Ball 4+: Maximize 7X orbits, aim for final 1.5M

**Actual high-score data is sparse in public sources**, but the theoretical maximum approaches **8–10 million** with perfect play and multiple extra balls. However, **3–5 million is the realistic competitive range** on well-maintained machines.
