# 👻 Ghostbusters - Strategy Guide

*Stern Pinball, 2016 — Designed by John Trudeau*

> **"Who you gonna call?"**

Ghostbusters is widely regarded as one of Stern's most difficult modern releases — a wide flipper gap, unpredictable shots, and a notoriously hard right ramp. But the scoring path is clear: collect ghosts toward Storage Facility Multiball, stack your playfield multiplier to 6x, and work through nine Scenes toward the final "Are You a God?" wizard mode. Our goal: hold 6x playfield, complete mode sequences in order, and push past 100 ghosts.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Take the skill shot** by flipping to select either a top lane or major shot — successful completion scores 1M + 5% of your current score
- [ ] **Hit ghost logo inserts to collect ghosts** — 10 ghosts lights your first Storage Facility Multiball lock, then every 20 more (30, 50, 70...)
- [ ] **Use the center captive ball** as the easiest, most reliable way to collect additional ghosts
- [ ] **Light 2X and 3X playfield multipliers** via the two targets beside the left ramp — running both together gives **6X playfield**, described as crucial for high scores
- [ ] **Avoid the notoriously difficult right ramp and center GHOST target** — loop the safer, easier left ramp instead
- [ ] **Complete mode sequences in order** rather than jumping between the three Scene tracks

---

## 👻 Core Mechanics

### Ghost Collection & Storage Facility Multiball

- Hit scattered **ghost logo inserts** to collect ghosts
- **10 ghosts** lights your first lock for **Storage Facility Multiball**; subsequent locks come every **20 more** (30, 50, 70, etc.)
- The **center captive ball is the easiest method** to rack up additional ghosts
- In multiball: **all lit ghosts are jackpots**; collecting all of them lights **three Super Jackpots** and relights the ghosts
- You can **stack locks and add balls** via captive ball hits

### Playfield Multiplier (Your Top Priority)

- Two targets beside the **left ramp** light **2X or 3X** multipliers, each lasting **~30 seconds**
- Running **both simultaneously creates 6X playfield** — described as **crucial for high scores**
- **Gear Combos** (left ramp + captive ball) are an efficient way to light multipliers quickly

> 🎯 **Pro Tip — Core Strategy:** Prioritize building and holding 6X playfield over chasing Super Jackpots (which range from a minimum 5M up to 30M). The multiplier affects everything you score while it's active, making it the higher-leverage investment of your shots.

### PKE Lanes & Bonus

Completing the **P-K-E top lanes** advances your bonus multiplier. Bumper hits award mystery prizes and increase your **PKE Frenzy** value.

### Scenes (9 Modes, 3 Sequences)

Scenes are organized into **three sequences** — left scoop, left ramp, right orbit — and **must be played sequentially within each sequence**. Most scenes involve shooting a pattern of blinking shots. Key scenes include **Slimer encounters** and the multi-phase **Gozer**/**Stay Puft Marshmallow Man** battles.

- Completing **Gozer The Gozerian** lights **Stay Puft Marshmallow Man** at the right loop
- Stay Puft is another multi-part scene — make lit shots to weaken him until he's lit red, then shoot the right loop to finish him off
- Completing **an entire sequence** triggers **"We Came, We Saw, We Kicked Its..."** multiball, with ball count equal to the number of modes completed in that sequence
- After Stay Puft, the **left ramp lights for the mini-wizard mode** of the same name

### Mass Hysteria Multiball

Reached at **100 ghosts collected**. This mode **reverses your flipper controls** (left button operates the right flipper!) — hitting the **captive ball temporarily restores normal controls**, making it a critical safety valve during this mode.

---

## 🏆 Wizard Modes

### "We're Ready to Believe You!"

A **60-second, 4-ball frenzy** requiring continued ghost collection.

### "Are You a God?" (Final Wizard Mode)

Unlocked at **100 ghosts**. Reportedly features **billion-point jackpots** and a victory multiball — the ultimate goal of the entire ruleset.

---

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **The right ramp** | Notoriously difficult and inconsistent | Avoid relying on it; route your strategy around the safer left ramp instead |
| **Center GHOST target** | Also notoriously hard to hit cleanly | Don't force it; use the captive ball for ghost collection instead |
| **Wide flipper gap & unpredictable inlanes** | Most shots don't return to the flippers predictably on this layout | Play conservatively and expect more drains than on a typical modern Stern |
| **Mass Hysteria's reversed controls** | Easy to panic and hit the wrong flipper | Hit the captive ball promptly to restore normal control if things go wrong |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Game (Ball 1)

1. Take the skill shot toward a Scene start if possible
2. Begin collecting ghosts via the captive ball
3. Light your first playfield multiplier via the left ramp targets

**Target Score:** 10M–30M

### Mid Game (Ball 2)

1. Push for 6X playfield by lighting both multiplier targets
2. Lock balls toward Storage Facility Multiball (10, 30, 50 ghosts)
3. Work through one Scene sequence at a time, completing it before switching

**Target Score:** 30M–80M

### Late Game (Ball 3+)

1. Cash in Storage Facility Multiball jackpots and Super Jackpots while holding 6X
2. Push toward 100 ghosts for Mass Hysteria and the final wizard mode
3. Complete Gozer and Stay Puft if you haven't already, working toward "We Came, We Saw, We Kicked Its..."

**Target Score per ball:** 40M–150M+

| Setting | Recommendation | Reason |
|---|---|---|

| **Captive ball mechanisms** (6 total) | Verify smooth, reliable operation | Central to ghost collection and multiplier-building shortcuts |
| **Right ramp calibration** | Check carefully — known difficulty area | Even with good calibration, expect this to remain the hardest shot on the table |

## 📋 Quick Reference: Strategic Priorities

**Ball 1:** Skill shot, start collecting ghosts via captive ball, light your first multiplier.
**Ball 2:** Build to 6X playfield, lock toward Storage Facility Multiball, work one Scene sequence.
**Ball 3+:** Cash jackpots at 6X, push toward 100 ghosts, complete Gozer/Stay Puft for the mini-wizard.

**Target High-Score Ballpark:** **80–200 million points** is a strong, competitive game on this genuinely difficult machine — reaching "Are You a God?" at 100 ghosts represents true mastery.
