# 🐉 Dragon - Strategy Guide

*Gottlieb, 1978 — Designed by Ed Krynski*

> **"Max your bonus first. The vari-targets reward you after."**

Dragon has a deliberately simple layout — two pop bumpers, two vari-targets, and a clever spinning dragon/fire target — but its bonus rules are genuinely unusual: the vari-targets score net-zero until you've already maxed your bonus. Understanding this one quirk is the entire strategic key to the game. Our goal: max bonus to 19K with the 5X multiplier, then milk the vari-targets for repeated rebuild-and-collect cycles.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Build your bonus multiplier through the top 4 lanes cumulatively**: A alone = 2X, A+B = 3X, A+B+C = 4X, all four = 5X
- [ ] **Understand the vari-target quirk**: they score **net-zero until your bonus is already maxed out** — don't expect them to be a primary scoring source early on
- [ ] **Once bonus is maxed, hit the vari-targets to collect 1K, 2K, or 3K** (depending on how far back you push the target) **and apply your multiplier**
- [ ] **After collecting, your bonus drops back down** — rebuild it and repeat the cycle for more multiplied collections
- [ ] **Watch the spinning dragon/fire target** — it's mostly a visual gimmick (fire appears to come from the dragon's mouth) rather than a major scoring shot, but it's part of the simple shot rotation

---

## 🐲 Core Mechanics

### Bonus Multiplier (Top 4 Lanes)

The multiplier builds cumulatively as you light more of the top lanes:

| Lanes Lit | Multiplier |
|---|---|
| A | 2X |
| A + B | 3X |
| A + B + C | 4X |
| A + B + C + D (all four) | **5X (max)** |

### Vari-Targets (The Game's Key Quirk)

The two vari-targets are mini bonus-collect features:
- Hitting them scores **1K, 2K, or 3K** depending on how far back you push the target
- This **also lets you "drop down" to lower bonus values** — useful for relighting extra balls or Special at a lower threshold
- **Bonus collection applies your current multiplier**, if any

> ⚠️ **The critical rule:** Vari-targets score **net-zero until you've already maxed out your bonus** (19K base). Before that point, hitting them just trades current bonus for an equivalent collected amount — no net gain. **Once maxed, however, every vari-target hit is pure profit on top of your already-banked maximum bonus.**

> 🎯 **Pro Tip — Core Strategy:** Don't bother chasing vari-targets early in the ball. Focus entirely on maxing your base bonus (19K) and lighting the 5X multiplier first. Only once you're maxed should you start hitting vari-targets — at that point, each hit is a genuine multiplied bonus collection, and your bonus then rebuilds for another cycle.

### Spinning Dragon/Fire Target

A clever visual gimmick — the target shows a dragon image on one side and a fire image on the other, so spinning it creates the illusion of fire coming from the dragon's mouth. It's a fun centerpiece but not a major standalone scoring feature; treat it as part of your normal shot rotation.

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **Chasing vari-targets before maxing bonus** | Nets zero actual gain — wasted shots and risk for no benefit | Wait until bonus is maxed (19K) before prioritizing vari-target hits |
| **Pop bumper unpredictability** | Standard late-70s bumper chaos with only 2 bumpers on this simple layout | Treat bumper time as incidental, not a shot to actively chase |
| **Slingshot rebounds** | Can send the ball toward outlanes unpredictably | Play controlled, deliberate shots near the single slingshot |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Ball

1. Focus entirely on lighting the top 4 lanes (A, B, C, D) to build your multiplier
2. Build base bonus toward the 19K maximum — this is your priority above all else
3. Avoid the vari-targets for now; they offer no net benefit until bonus is maxed

### Mid Ball

1. Continue pushing base bonus toward 19K if not yet maxed
2. Once maxed, start hitting vari-targets for multiplied 1K/2K/3K collections
3. Let bonus rebuild after each vari-target collection

### Late Ball

1. Cycle between collecting via vari-targets and rebuilding bonus for repeated multiplied collections
2. Keep your 5X multiplier active throughout for maximum value per cycle
3. Use vari-target "drop down" flexibility if you need to relight an extra ball or Special

## 📋 Quick Reference: Strategic Priorities

**Early:** Light all 4 top lanes for the 5X multiplier, build base bonus toward 19K, ignore vari-targets.
**Mid:** Finish maxing bonus, then start cycling vari-target collections.
**Late:** Repeat the collect-and-rebuild cycle for maximum multiplied scoring.

**Target Outcome:** Maxing your bonus to 19K with the full 5X multiplier, then running several vari-target collect-and-rebuild cycles, represents a strong, well-played game on this deceptively simple machine.
