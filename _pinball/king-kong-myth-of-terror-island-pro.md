# 🦍 King Kong: Myth of Terror Island - Strategy Guide

*Stern Pinball, 2025 — Designed by Keith Elwin*

> **"Climb the island. Defeat the T-Rex. Crash the Gate."**

Keith Elwin's 2025 King Kong design layers skill shots, KING KONG letter spelling, five island scenes, three multiball systems, and a T-Rex Boss Battle mini-wizard mode into the path toward the final wizard mode, **Crash the Gate**. Our goal: collect all six map segments, defeat both T-Rex encounters, and unlock the exponentially-multiplying Super Jackpots.

## 🦍 Core Mechanics

### Skill Shots & KING KONG Letters

Three skill shot routes: **left inlanes**, **spider pit targets**, or the **river lane**. Each successful skill shot:
- Awards a **KING KONG letter** at the gong
- Adds a **ball save**

Letters can also come from sufficient **cliffs ramp shots** or accumulated switch hits.

> 🎯 **Pro Tip:** Practice and memorize the exact plunger strength for each skill shot — reliability here directly translates to faster letter collection and more ball saves banked.

### Deadeye Mode

Once **KING** is spelled, **Deadeye unlocks at the Kong Cave VUK** — a **30-second mode** where your flippers are disabled and **only skill shots work**. A unique, high-pressure mode that rewards your skill shot precision specifically.

### Completing KONG → T-Rex Battle Access

Spelling the **full KING KONG** sequence lights the **gong for T-Rex Battle**, but only when **no other modes are currently running** — plan your timing accordingly.

### Island Scenes & Locks (5 Scenes, 6 Map Segments)

## 🎁 Multiball Systems

### King Kong Multiball

1. Light **3 locks** via **drop target completion at the center ramp**
2. During multiball: **complete drop targets** to light jackpots at the **punchback target and cliffs ramp**, or hit **green shots** to score awards and light the **center ramp for relock + 2x scoring**

### Spider Pit Multiball

- Shoot the pit **4 times initially** to start; subsequent multiballs require **progressively more hits**
- **Three variants** exist, with **Giant Spider** offering the highest scoring potential via switch-hit collection and ramp-based multipliers

### T-Rex Battle (Mini-Wizard Mode)

- A **4-ball multiball** requiring you to **defeat two separate T-Rex encounters**
- Each T-Rex requires completing **color-specific shot sets** (3 sets per T-Rex)
- **Mash the action button after pit shots** to deal damage — each mash is worth an **increasing value**

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Game (Ball 1)

1. Practice skill shots toward your first KING KONG letters
2. Start an early island scene without locks for safer initial progress
3. Begin building toward King Kong Multiball via center ramp drop targets

**Target Score:** Build toward the competitive average of 100M+

### Mid Game (Ball 2)

1. Run King Kong Multiball and Spider Pit Multiball as they qualify
2. Continue collecting KING KONG letters and map segments
3. Make strategic NYC event perk selections and advance the biplane attack via side ramp shots

### Late Game (Ball 3+)

1. Push toward T-Rex Battle if KONG is fully spelled and no other mode is running
2. Defeat both T-Rex encounters for map segment progress
3. If all 6 segments are collected and both T-Rex defeated, run Crash the Gate for the exponential Super Jackpot finale
