# ⚽ World Cup Soccer Pinball Machine - Complete Strategy Guide
## *Path to 500 Million Points*

> **"A meter goles!"** — Striker the World Cup Pup

John Popadiuk's first production pinball design, World Cup Soccer captures the spirit of the 1994 FIFA World Cup hosted in the USA. Face 15 international soccer teams in multiball showdowns, navigate nine U.S. cities with special modes, and compete in the Final Match against Germany for a massive wizard-mode jackpot. This mid-90s Bally solid-state game emphasizes reliable goal-shooting over deep stacking, making it accessible to intermediate players while rewarding consistency <kcite ref="61"/>.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Ball 1:** Complete the skill shot for easy progression
- [ ] **Light the Goal** via rollovers or shots; score it at least 5 times
- [ ] **Activate an Ultra Mode** (Ramps, Spinner, Bumpers, or Goalie) by scoring goals
- [ ] **Collect Tickets** by shooting both ramps to build cities
- [ ] **Lock balls** for Multiball at the left ramp, then shoot the TV scoop to start
- [ ] **Advance through cities** by reaching Los Angeles to qualify Final Match
- [ ] **Use Magna-Save and kickback** to recover balls when in danger
- [ ] **During Multiball:** Shoot the Goal (now a jackpot) to defeat teams #16–#1

## ⚽ Core Mechanics & Goal Scoring

### The Goal
The centerpiece of World Cup Soccer is the **Goal** at the top-left of the playfield, defended by an articulating moving goalie. Shooting the Goal:

- **Base Goal (unlit):** 10,000,000 points
- **Lit Goal:** 20,000,000 points (lit by completing all four center rollover buttons)
- **During Multiball:** Becomes a jackpot (20M–250M depending on opponent)

Every Goal scored activates one of four **Ultra Modes** (determined at random or by shot sequence) that grant escalating bonus points at end of ball.

### Ultra Modes (Activated After Scoring Goals)

| Mode             | Mechanic                    | Per-Hit Value |
| ---------------- | --------------------------- | ------------- |
| **Ultra Ramps**  | Shoot ramps for bonus       | 5M per ramp  |
| **Ultra Spinner** | Hit spinner for bonus       | 1M per spin  |
| **Ultra Bumpers** | Pop bumpers award big value  | 2M per bump  |
| **Ultra Goalie** | Hit the Goalie target again | 10M per hit  |

> ⚠️ **Important:** Ultra Mode points are **delayed until end-of-ball bonus** — avoid tilting after scoring goals! <kcite ref="61"/>

### Rolling the Kickback & Center Rollover Buttons

- **Left outlane kickback:** Relit by hitting the left inlane after a right ramp shot
- **Center rollovers (4 buttons):** Complete all four to light the Goal for 20M
- **Left spiral:** Collecting this counts toward city progression

## 🏙️ Cities & City Modes

World Cup Soccer features nine playable U.S. cities. Progress through them by earning tickets (shooting both ramps or completing rollover sequences) or via skill shots and awards. Each city reached = 5,000,000 points bonus.

### Key Cities with Special Modes

| City            | Qualification      | Mode                | Award |
| --------------- | ------------------ | ------------------- | ----- |
| **Boston**      | 1st city           | Boston Tea Party    | 30M (countdown) |
| **Washington**  | Mid-progression    | Extra Ball Round    | Lights Extra Ball |
| **Los Angeles** | All 9 cities       | **FINAL MATCH** (Wizard Mode) | 500M (45 sec, outscore Germany) |

> 💡 **Strategy Note:** Reaching Los Angeles qualifies Final Match, the primary wizard mode. Focus on consistent city accumulation early in the game. <kcite ref="61"/>

## 🎪 Ramps & Shot Layout

World Cup Soccer features **two ramps** (left spiral and right ramp):

### Ramp Mechanics

| Ramp      | Primary Effect                    | Secondary Effect       |
| --------- | --------------------------------- | ---------------------- |
| **Left**  | Feeds right flipper; lights cities | Combo with right ramp |
| **Right** | Feeds left flipper; locks for MB | Diverter redirect    |

**Two Ramp Combos:**
- Shooting left then right (or vice versa) within a short window: **Diverter Close** — ball redirects to opposite flipper for easier control and bonus
- Locks ball at ramp during multiball qualification phase

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Ramp shots are the primary method for collecting tickets and lighting the Goal. Alternate ramps to build your city progress. <kcite ref="61"/>

## 🎰 Multiball: The Final Draw

### Qualifying Multiball

To qualify multiball, **complete the soccer ball panel** by shooting designated "BUILD" shots:

1. **Shoot left spiral** (once)
2. **Shoot right ramp** (once)  
3. **Shoot left ramp** (once)
4. **Shoot Striker's saucer / TV scoop** (once)

Once the panel is complete, multiball is lit at the TV scoop.

### Starting Multiball

1. **Lock Phase:** Shoot the left ramp to lock a ball at the mini-playfield (Magna-Lock). Return to main playfield.
2. **Start:** Shoot the **TV scoop (Final Draw)** to launch multiball.

### Multiball Active Play

During multiball, face **15 teams** ranked from easiest (#16 USA) to hardest (#1 Germany):

| Team Rank | Jackpot Value | Notes |
| --------- | ------------- | ----- |
| #16–#12   | 20M–60M       | Early opponents |
| #11–#6    | 80M–150M      | Mid-tier teams |
| #5–#2     | 170M–220M     | Difficult opponents |
| **#1**    | **250M**      | Germany (final jackpot) |

**During Multiball:**
- Shooting the **Goal** collects the current jackpot (advances to next team)
- Shooting **either ramp** relights the Goal for another attempt
- **3-ball multiball**: Lock and start ball, plus 1 locked ball from earlier
- **If you drain before hitting a jackpot**, shooting a ramp or the Final Draw scoop restarts multiball where you left off

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Multiball is the most consistent high-scoring mode. Focus on executing Goal shots reliably rather than chasing difficult ramp combinations. <kcite ref="61"/>

## 🥅 Final Match (Wizard Mode)

### Qualification

Reach **Los Angeles** (the 9th city) to qualify Final Match.

### Mechanics

- **Duration:** 45 seconds  
- **Objective:** Shoot the Goal as many times as possible to outscore the German national team's simulated goal count
- **Reward:** 500,000,000 points if you outscore Germany (or reach a threshold goal count)
- **Ball Save:** Active during Final Match for safety

> 🏆 **Strategic Note:** This is your primary path to 500M+ totals. Reaching this mode consistently is more important than maximizing individual multiball runs. <kcite ref="61"/>

## 📈 Bonus Calculation

End-of-ball bonus is calculated as:

**Bonus = (5M per Goal × Number of Goals Scored) + (5M per City × Number of Cities) + Ultra Mode Points + TV Award Points**

| Achievement | Bonus Points |
| ----------- | ------------ |
| Per Goal scored | 5M |
| Per City reached | 5M |
| Ultra Ramps (first) | 5M–10M |
| Ultra Spinner (first) | 1M–5M |
| Ultra Bumpers (first) | 2M–8M |
| Ultra Goalie (first) | 10M–20M |

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Double-flip to skip the bonus animation and get to the next ball quickly. <kcite ref="61"/>

## 🎯 Shot Analysis

### ⚠️ Dangerous Shots

| Shot             | Risk                              | Mitigation                 |
| ---------------- | --------------------------------- | -------------------------- |
| **Center shots** | Fast return; SDTM risk            | Trap up after shot         |
| **Pop bumpers**  | Chaotic bounces to outlanes       | Avoid during low-ball play |
| **Unlit ramps**  | Can feed outlane if missed        | Aim for center of ramp     |
| **Goalie target** | Unpredictable rebound            | Plan trap position         |

### ✅ Safe Shots

| Shot          | Benefit                           |
| ------------- | --------------------------------- |
| **TV scoop**  | Ball held; time to prepare        |
| **Lit Goal**  | Predictable return to flipper     |
| **Left spiral** | Feeds right flipper cleanly      |
| **Right ramp** | Feeds left flipper after lock    |

### 🎮 Ball Control Positions

- **Left flipper trap:** Optimal after right ramp and left spiral shots
- **Right flipper trap:** Useful after Goal shots and center shots
- **Live catch:** Essential after ramp combos to prevent SDTM

## 📋 Additional Features & Options

### Mystery Awards & TV Rounds

Random TV awards appear during play, including:

- **Where's Striker:** Hit Striker targets in sequence for escalating points
- **Hit the Goalie!:** Score points per Goalie hit (10M base, increases by 10M per Goal)
- **Big Goal Round:** Score three goals for 15M, 15M, and 30M bonuses
- **Extra Ball Round:** Qualify extra ball (lights at Washington D.C.)

### Game Modifiers

- **Kickback Relighting:** Enabled by right inlane after ramp shots
- **Magna-Save Activation:** Press left flipper side button to activate magnetic recovery above left flipper
- **Diverter Mechanic:** Two consecutive ramp shots in short window redirect ball to opposite flipper
