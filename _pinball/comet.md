# 🎡 Comet - Strategy Guide

*Williams, 1985 — Designed by Barry Oursler*

> **"Hit the gallery. Ride the corkscrew."**

Comet brings a carnival shooting-gallery theme to a clean, accessible Williams layout: two target banks (Rabbits and Ducks) build separate bonus tracks, each collected via a different ramp shot. Simple rules, real challenge in the ramp accuracy. Our goal: max both bonus tracks at 63K with 5X multipliers, then ride the center ramp's escalating timer.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Work both Shooting Gallery target banks** (Rabbits and Ducks) — each advances its own separate bonus counter, up to 63,000 each
- [ ] **"Dunk the dummy"** (hit the drop target, then the standup target behind it) to raise your bonus multiplier, up to **5X** on both tracks
- [ ] **Complete the left bank of standups, then shoot the corkscrew ramp** to collect that bonus
- [ ] **Complete the right bank, then shoot the funhouse** to collect that bonus
- [ ] **Complete both banks** to light the center ramp for an escalating timed value: **30K → 50K → 100K → 100K**
- [ ] **Qualify the Cycle Jump ramp** (upper right) for a flat **1 million points**, and the Corkscrew ramp for an **extra ball and/or Special**

## 🎪 Core Mechanics

### Shooting Gallery Target Banks (Rabbits & Ducks)

Two separate target banks let you build two independent bonus tracks:

| Bank | Bonus Track | Maximum |
|---|---|---|
| Rabbits | Rabbit bonus | 63,000 |
| Ducks | Duck bonus | 63,000 |

Both tracks can be multiplied up to **5X** independently.

### Bonus Multiplier ("Dunk the Dummy")

1. Hit the **drop target** (which protects a stationary target behind it)
2. Hit the **standup target** now exposed behind the drop target
3. This raises your bonus multiplier on **both** the rabbit and duck tracks, up to **5X**

### Bonus Collection (Two Different Ramps)

| Bank Completed | Collect Via |
|---|---|
| Left bank of standups | **Corkscrew ramp** (upper left) |
| Right bank of standups | **Funhouse** |

> 🎯 **Pro Tip:** Since each bank routes to a *different* collection shot, plan which bank you're completing based on which ramp you have better control over in the moment.

### Center Ramp (Unlocked by Completing Both Banks)

Completing **both** target banks lights the **center ramp** for an escalating timed value: **30K → 50K → 100K → 100K**. Hit it as early in the timer window as you can for repeat shots at the higher values.

### Corkscrew Ramp & Cycle Jump Ramp

- **Corkscrew ramp** (upper left): when qualified, awards an **extra ball and/or Special**
- **Cycle Jump ramp** (upper right): when qualified, awards a flat **1,000,000 points**

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **Ramp shot misses** | Both the corkscrew and Cycle Jump ramps require real accuracy | Practice each ramp's specific angle individually before relying on them under pressure |
| **Pop bumper/slingshot time** | Standard mid-80s unpredictability | Treat as incidental scoring rather than something to chase |
| **Left crossover return lane** | Can feed the ball in an unexpected direction | Stay ready on the flippers after any crossover feed |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Ball

1. Start working both target banks (Rabbits and Ducks) to build separate bonus tracks
2. "Dunk the dummy" early and often to start raising your multiplier
3. Complete one bank and collect via its corresponding ramp (corkscrew or funhouse)

### Mid Ball

1. Complete the second bank to light the center ramp's escalating timer
2. Push for 5X on both bonus tracks via repeated dunk-the-dummy completions
3. Qualify the Cycle Jump ramp for a flat 1M shot when available

### Late Ball

1. Hit the center ramp as early in its timer as possible for the higher escalating values
2. Take the extra ball/Special from the corkscrew ramp if qualified
3. Bank both maxed bonus tracks (63K × 5X each) before the ball ends

## 📋 Quick Reference: Strategic Priorities

**Early:** Work both target banks, start dunking the dummy for multiplier.
**Mid:** Complete both banks for the center ramp timer, push toward 5X on both tracks.
**Late:** Hit the center ramp early in its timer, take any qualified extra ball/Special/Cycle Jump shots.

**Target Outcome:** Maxing both the rabbit and duck bonus tracks (63K each) at 5X, plus a few center ramp collections and a Cycle Jump 1M shot, represents a strong, well-played game on this accessible carnival-themed machine.
