# 👮‍♀️ Charlie's Angels - Strategy Guide

*Gottlieb, 1978 — Designed by Allen Edwall*

> **"Spell CHIC. Climb the multiplier. Collect at the bullseye."**

Charlie's Angels is a late-70s Gottlieb classic where the bonus multiplier climb is the entire game: a chain of escalating shots (drop target banks, the bullseye target, individual red and white drop targets) each unlock the next stage of the multiplier, topping out at a 95K maximum bonus. Our goal: climb all the way to 5X and collect a maxed bonus, plus the extra ball along the way.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Complete the red 5-bank of drop targets (top left) or spell C-H-I-C** via the four lanes to advance your multiplier to **2X**
- [ ] **From the left flipper, go up top via the right arc** to collect CHIC letters as part of your core shot routine
- [ ] **At 2X, hit the bullseye target** (above the right slingshot) to advance to **3X**
- [ ] **At 3X, hit the left-most red drop target** to advance to **4X**
- [ ] **At 4X, hit the top white drop target** to reach the **5X maximum**
- [ ] **Once base bonus is 11,000+, collect it by hitting the bullseye standup target**
- [ ] **Hit "H" and all 3 white drop targets (with bonus at 11,000+)** to score an extra ball

---

## 🕵️‍♀️ Core Mechanics

### Bonus Multiplier Climb (The Whole Game)

This machine's defining feature is a **shifting target** for advancing your multiplier — each stage requires hitting a *different* specific shot than the last:

| Current Multiplier | Shot to Advance to Next Stage | Resulting Multiplier |
|---|---|---|
| 1X | Complete red 5-bank (top left) **or** spell C-H-I-C | 2X |
| 2X | Hit the **bullseye target** (above right slingshot) | 3X |
| 3X | Hit the **left-most red drop target** | 4X |
| 4X | Hit the **top white drop target** | 5X (max) |

> 🎯 **Pro Tip:** Memorize this chain — the shot that advances your multiplier changes at every stage, so you need to know which shot is "live" for advancement at any given moment rather than just hitting whatever's convenient.

### Spelling CHIC

Complete all four **C-H-I-C** lanes (reachable via the right arc from the left flipper) as an alternative route to your first multiplier advance. This is the core shot pattern veteran players default to.

### Bonus Collection

- **Base bonus** advances from all lanes, targets, and lit star rollovers, up to **19,000**
- Once base bonus reaches **at least 11,000**, hit the **bullseye standup target** to collect it
- **Maximum overall bonus** (19K base × 5X multiplier) is **95,000**

### Extra Ball

Hit **"H"** and all **3 white drop targets** while bonus is **11,000 or higher** to score an extra ball.

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **Chasing the wrong multiplier-advance shot** | Wastes time and shots since only the *current* stage's specific target counts | Track which multiplier stage you're on before committing to a shot |
| **Pop bumper/slingshot chaos** | Standard late-70s unpredictability | Treat as incidental scoring, not a primary objective |
| **Bullseye target overcommitment** | It serves double duty (multiplier advance at 2X, bonus collection at 11K+) — don't assume it always does the same thing | Know your current game state before each bullseye attempt |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Ball

1. Work toward your first multiplier advance via the red 5-bank or CHIC lanes
2. Build base bonus toward the 11,000 collection threshold
3. Use the left-flipper-to-right-arc CHIC routing as your default shot pattern

### Mid Ball

1. Climb the multiplier chain: bullseye (→3X), left red target (→4X), top white target (→5X)
2. Collect bonus at the bullseye once you're past 11,000 base
3. Work toward the extra ball via H + 3 white drop targets

### Late Ball

1. Push for the full 5X multiplier if you haven't reached it yet
2. Maximize base bonus toward 19,000 before your final collection
3. Take the extra ball if available — it's a meaningful boost given the otherwise hard ball-by-ball scoring

## 📋 Quick Reference: Strategic Priorities

**Early:** Advance to 2X via red 5-bank or CHIC, build base bonus toward 11,000.
**Mid:** Climb through 3X (bullseye), 4X (left red target), 5X (top white target); collect bonus.
**Late:** Max base bonus toward 19,000, take the extra ball if available, bank your 5X bonus.

**Target Outcome:** Reaching the full 5X multiplier with a maxed 19K base bonus (95,000 total) plus securing the extra ball represents a strong, well-played game on this machine.
