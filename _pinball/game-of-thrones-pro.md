# ⚔️ GAME OF THRONES (Pro) Pinball Machine - Complete Strategy Guide
## *Path to 500 Million - 1 Billion Points*

> **"Winter is Coming... But You Must Master the Houses First"**

Based on the HBO/fantasy license, this guide will help you master the seven houses of Westeros and reach the coveted **500 million to 1 billion point** milestone <kcite ref="1"/>.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Choose your house** - Select Martell or Tyrell for new players (add-a-ball or combo multiplier)
- [ ] **Execute the skill shot** - Hard plunge to light one bonus X and 500K × Ball #
- [ ] **Light house modes** - Hit same shot 3 times to light each house mode
- [ ] **Start a house mode** - Shoot center ramp to begin gameplay
- [ ] **Collect gold** - Feed shots repeatedly for increasing multipliers (capped at 5x or 6x)
- [ ] **Qualify Winter is Coming** - Complete three of any iced-over shots to start multiball
- [ ] **Lock balls for Blackwater** - Get three ball locks at center ramp for major multiball
- [ ] **Build 4+ house modes** - Unlock Hand of the King mini-wizard mode
- [ ] **Complete 7 houses** - Unlock Iron Throne final wizard mode
- [ ] **Stack multipliers** - Use playfield + combo multipliers to boost jackpots by 10-20x

## 🏰 House Selection - The Foundation of Your Game

Your house choice provides persistent benefits throughout the game and dramatically affects strategy. Choose wisely! <kcite ref="2"/>

### ⭐ Best Houses for Maximum Scoring

| House     | Passive Benefit              | Button Ability                       | Best For              |
| --------- | ---------------------------- | ------------------------------------ | --------------------- |
| **Tyrell** | One inlane always lit for +1X combo multiplier | Cash combo multipliers for points | **Top scoring potential** |
| **Lannister** | 1.5x gold multiplier          | Buy playfield multipliers (8 uses max) | Flexible multiplier building |
| **Martell** | None                         | Add-a-ball once per multiball       | Ball save safety       |
| **Targaryen** | None                         | Freeze timers for 15 seconds        | Reaching wizard modes  |

### 👑 Alternative Houses

| House      | Passive Benefit                       | Button Ability                      | Best For                      |
| ---------- | ------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------- | ----------------------------- |
| **Baratheon** | 2 Wall Multiball advances             | Instant outlane ball save           | Intermediate safety play      |
| **Stark** | +10M to Winter is Coming hurry-ups    | Complete current house mode         | Lower difficulty play         |
| **Greyjoy** | Gains powers from defeated houses     | Use most recently defeated house's ability | Advanced/expert players |

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** For your first 500M+ score, choose **Tyrell** (highest ceiling) or **Martell** (safer with add-a-ball). Tyrell's combo multiplier can boost a single shot from 20M to 120M+ with proper stacking <kcite ref="2"/>.

## 🎪 Skill Shots & Initial Ball Setup

### Skill Shot (Right Flipper Selection)

| Plunge Type | Points        | Bonus Award     |
| ----------- | ------------- | --------------- |
| **Hard Plunge (Lit Lane)** | 500K × Ball # | +1 Bonus X     |
| **Weak Plunge (Unlit)** | 100K-200K | Varies          |

> ⚠️ **Critical:** Always hard plunge on Ball 1. The bonus X multiplier applies to your final bonus, making skill shot one of the highest-value shots in the game if you collect bonus X multipliers <kcite ref="3"/>.

## 🏛️ The Seven House Modes & Mode Selection

To start a house mode, you must first **light it by completing its three shots**, then **shoot the center ramp**. Each house provides unique gameplay but follows the same core pattern: make shots to progress, hit jackpots, and earn gold/multipliers.

### House Shot Locations

| House     | Shot Location        | Qualifier |
| --------- | -------------------- | --------- |
| Baratheon | Left bank (3-drop)   | 3 hits    |
| Greyjoy   | Left orbit            | 3 hits    |
| Targaryen | Dragon (center)      | 3 hits    |
| Lannister | Center ramp          | 3 hits    |
| Stark     | Right ramp           | 3 hits    |
| Martell   | Right orbit          | 3 hits    |
| Tyrell    | Right bank (2-drop)  | 3 hits    |

### House Mode Rewards

Each house mode awards **gold** (multiplier fuel) and progresses you toward wizard modes:
- **Complete 3 house modes:** Extra Ball lit
- **Complete 4 house modes:** Hand of the King (mini-wizard) lit
- **Complete 7 house modes:** Iron Throne (final wizard) lit

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Don't complete all 7 houses in a single ball. Spread them across multiple balls to rack up extra balls and keep the game alive longer <kcite ref="2"/>.

## 💰 Gold & Multipliers - The Scoring Engine

Game of Thrones is fundamentally about **multiplying points, not accumulating them**. Gold is your multiplier fuel.

### Gold Collection System

| Action | Gold Gained |
| ------ | ----------- |
| Complete house mode | 1 Gold (Lannister: 1.5x) |
| Collect specific shots | 1-2 Gold |
| Blackwater jackpot | Variable (based on multiplier) |

### Multiplier Stacking (Critical to High Scores!)

Two types of multipliers stack **multiplicatively** (not additively):

1. **Playfield Multiplier** - Starts at 1x, built via:
   - Lannister button presses (costs 1 gold per use, max 8)
   - Tyrell inlane hits (via combo multiplier spillover)
   - Default cap: 3x
   - Expanded cap: 5x (with Tyrell) or 6x (with specific code versions)

2. **Combo Multiplier** (Tyrell house only) - Starts at 1x, built by:
   - Routing ball through lit inlane
   - Each pass: +1x (capped at 5x or 6x)
   - Button press: Cash all accumulated points at current multiplier
   - Reset: Multiplier resets if you don't hit inlane for ~8 seconds

### Example Multiplier Stacking

A shot worth 20M with:
- Playfield 3x = 60M
- Combo 5x on top = **300M from one shot!**

> ⚠️ **Critical:** This is how players reach 500M+ in a single ball. Stack multipliers aggressively before cashing major jackpots <kcite ref="1"/>.

## 🎰 Multiballs

### ❄️ Winter is Coming (3-Ball Multiball)

**Qualification:** Complete any three iced-over shots across the playfield

**Mechanics:**
1. Four hurry-up values light after three shot completions
2. Collect all four hurry-ups to start Winter Has Come (4-ball)
3. Alternates between **Horde Phase** (regular shots) and **Lieutenant Phase** (left flipper freeze on specific shots)

**Scoring:**
- Each hurry-up: 20M-50M depending on progression
- Four-ball mode: Exponentially higher jackpot values

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Winter is Coming is easier to qualify than Blackwater; use it early to fund multiplier builds <kcite ref="3"/>.

---

### ⚔️ Blackwater (4-Ball Multiball) - THE MONEY MODE

**Qualification:** Lock three balls at center ramp (via Tyrell targets)

**Phase 1: Collect Green Jackpots**
- All major shots light green jackpots
- Collect 5 green jackpots = lights Super Jackpot frenzy

**Phase 2: Super Jackpot Frenzy (Timed)**
- Battering Ram lights up with timer
- Repeated hits accumulate value: 25M, 50M, 75M, 100M, 150M, etc.
- **Key:** Hit Battering Ram with stacked multipliers to reach 200M-500M per hit!

**Scoring Potential:**
- Single Super Jackpot with 3x playfield + 5x combo = 500M+
- Multiple Supers in one Blackwater = 1B+

> 🏆 **This is how top scores are made!** One or two monster Blackwater jackpots account for majority of 500M-1B scores <kcite ref="1"/>.

---

### 🏰 Wall/Battle Modes

- Qualification varies (see code version; typically drop target / shot completions)
- Less valuable than Blackwater for top scores
- Use strategically when Blackwater not immediately available

## 🧙 Wizard Modes

### 👑 Hand of the King (Mini-Wizard)

**Qualification:** Complete 4 house modes

**Objective:** Make sets of shots with escalating multipliers

**Scoring:** Based on houses brought in; combo multiplier boosts make this mode valuable (50M-200M range possible)

### 🏁 Iron Throne (Final Wizard Mode)

**Qualification:** Complete all 7 house modes

**Mechanics:**
1. Two-ball multiball starts
2. Each major shot and target bank available for "castle siege" shots
3. Hit Battering Ram to complete siege and add a ball
4. Conquer all seven houses
5. Victory lap phase with escalating shot values

**Scoring Potential:** 500M-1B+ depending on multiplier stack and efficiency

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Iron Throne continues even after multiball ends. If you drain and get a new ball, you can resume Iron Throne progress! <kcite ref="3"/>.

## 🎯 Shot Analysis

### ⚠️ Dangerous Shots to Avoid

| Shot | Risk | Mitigation |
| ---- | ---- | ---------- |
| **Battering Ram (during non-multiball)** | Can drain immediately (SDTM) | Only hit during multiball |
| **Pop Bumper Area** | Chaotic drains | Avoid when ball is lively |
| **Right Loop (during certain modes)** | Unpredictable return angle | Ensure clean trap after |

### ✅ Safe / High-Value Shots

| Shot | Benefit | When to Use |
| ---- | ------- | ----------- |
| **Center Ramp** | Starts modes / Locks balls | Whenever it's lit |
| **Left Ramp** | Consistent playfield | Control/setup shot |
| **Orbits** | Feeds to flipper | Trap setup |

### 🎮 Ball Control Positions

- **Left Flipper Trap:** Default position after most shots
- **Right Flipper Trap:** For inlane combo setup (Tyrell)
- **Cradle Hold:** Keep ball controlled between shots
- **Post Pass:** Transfer safely between flippers

## 📈 Stacking & Combos

### ✅ What Stacks Well

| Combination | Benefit | Value |
| ----------- | ------- | ----- |
| **Playfield 3x + Combo 5x** | Multiplicative boost | 15x total multiplier |
| **High Gold + Playfield Multipliers + Blackwater** | Monster jackpots | 200M-500M+ per shot |
| **Tyrell + Lannister (via Greyjoy)** | Double multiplier powers | Unlimited stacking potential |
| **4+ Houses Completed + Multiballs** | Access to wizard modes | Exponential scoring |

### ❌ What NOT to Stack

| Avoid | Reason |
| ----- | ------ |
| **Wasting gold on multipliers without Blackwater queued** | Gold better spent during actual multiball |
| **Draining during setup phase** | Ball dies before reaching scoring modes |
| **Too many weak houses early** | Delays access to wizard modes |

### ⏰ Optimal Progression

1. **Ball 1:** Qualify 2-3 house modes, execute skill shot, build some gold
2. **Ball 2:** Complete 4 houses (unlock Hand of the King), start one Blackwater if possible
3. **Ball 3+:** Stack multipliers, crush Blackwater with full multiplier setup, work toward Iron Throne

## 🎮 House-Specific Advanced Tips

### Tyrell Strategy
- Route ball through inlane constantly to build combo multiplier
- Button press AFTER major shots to cash at peak multiplier
- Coordinate with Blackwater start to maximize combo×playfield stacking
- Weakness: Requires precision flipper control

### Lannister Strategy
- Use button presses during early modes to build playfield multiplier
- Each button use: 1 gold spent, +1 playfield multiplier (up to 8 uses)
- More forgiving than Tyrell; simpler execution
- Weakness: Limited to 8 multiplier purchases per game

### Martell Strategy
- Focus on reaching multiball with minimal setup
- Use add-a-ball button strategically during drain situations
- Less scoring ceiling than Tyrell/Lannister but more reliable
- Best for learning the game safely
