# 🏒 Bobby Orr's Power Play - Strategy Guide

*Bally, 1977 — Designed by Greg Kmiec*

> **"Drop the targets. Max the multiplier. Skate the orbits."**

Bobby Orr's Power Play is a classic late-70s EM-style solid-state machine built on a clean two-part loop: a progressive top saucer that lights orbit rollovers for bonus advancement, and twin drop-target banks that push your bonus multiplier all the way to 5X before rewarding extra ball and special. Our goal: a strong **100K–145K** game — the practical ceiling given the 29K base bonus cap.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Shoot the top saucer first** — it climbs 3K → 6K → 9K → 12K → 15K, and specific values light orbit rollovers along the way
- [ ] **The 6K saucer shot lights the left orbit rollovers** for bonus advancement; **the 12K shot lights the right orbit rollovers**
- [ ] **Use the upper mini-flippers to complete drop target banks** — don't flip too late or you'll feed the outlanes
- [ ] **Complete either drop target bank (not both)** to advance your bonus multiplier: 2x → 3x → 5x, then extra ball/special
- [ ] **Don't rely on the center up-post for defense** — it's inconsistent and shouldn't be treated as a safety net
- [ ] **Once you hit 5X bonus, pivot back to orbit shots** to maximize your final bonus value before the ball ends

---

## 🏒 Core Mechanics

### Top Saucer (Progressive Value)

| Shot | Value | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| 1st | 3,000 | — |
| 2nd | 6,000 | Lights **left orbit rollovers** for bonus advancement |
| 3rd | 9,000 | — |
| 4th | 12,000 | Lights **right orbit rollovers** for bonus advancement |
| 5th (max) | 15,000 | — |

> 🎯 **Pro Tip:** Prioritize orbit shots early to light both rollover sets, since they feed your bonus passively for the rest of the ball once lit.

### Drop Target Banks (Bonus Multiplier)

Completing **either** drop target bank (you don't need both) advances your bonus multiplier progressively:

| Stage | Multiplier |
|---|---|
| 1st completion | 2x |
| 2nd completion | 3x |
| 3rd completion | **5x (max)** |
| Beyond max | Extra ball or Special |

**Use the upper mini-flippers** to line up clean shots into the drop target banks — this is the recommended technique over trying to hit them from the main flippers.

> ⚠️ **Timing matters:** flip the upper mini-flippers too late and you risk feeding the ball straight into the outlanes. Practice the timing before relying on this in competitive play.

### Star Rollovers & Center Post

Ten star rollovers around the playfield contribute to bonus advancement passively. The **center up-post** between the flippers raises via the top star rollover and center standup, and lowers via the two lower star rollovers and inlanes — but it's **inconsistent enough that veteran players don't treat it as a reliable defensive feature**.

### Bonus System

- **Base bonus** maxes at **29,000**
- **Multiplier** maxes at **5X**, for a theoretical overall maximum of **145,000** from bonus alone

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Shot | Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| **Late upper mini-flipper timing** | Feeds directly into the outlanes if mistimed | Practice the specific timing window before relying on it under pressure |
| **Relying on the center post** | Inconsistent raise/lower behavior means it can't be trusted as a safety net | Play as if the post isn't there — control the ball actively instead |
| **Pop bumper time** | Can send the ball unpredictably toward the outlanes | Treat bumpers as incidental scoring rather than a shot to actively chase |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Ball (Ball 1)

1. Shoot orbits first to light both rollover sets (via the 6K and 12K saucer milestones)
2. Begin working one drop target bank with the upper mini-flippers
3. Pick up incidental star rollover value along the way

**Target Score:** 20K–40K

### Mid Ball (Ball 2)

1. Push your drop target bank completions toward the 5X multiplier
2. Keep collecting top saucer value as it climbs toward 15K
3. Continue feeding orbit rollovers now that both sets should be lit

**Target Score:** 40K–80K

### Late Ball (Ball 3+)

1. Once at 5X multiplier, pivot back to orbit shots to maximize your final bonus value
2. Chase the extra ball/special if you've pushed the drop targets past max multiplier
3. Bank as much bonus as possible before the ball drains — this is where most of your final score comes from

**Target Score per ball:** 30K–60K

## ⚙️ Setup Recommendations

| Setting | Recommendation | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| **Playfield pitch** | 6.0°–6.5° | Standard for late-70s Bally solid-state machines |
| **Upper mini-flipper timing** | Practice and calibrate before competitive play | Central to safely completing drop target banks |
| **Center post mechanism** | Verify behavior, but don't depend on it | Known to be inconsistent even when working as designed |

---

## 📋 Quick Reference: Strategic Priorities

**Ball 1:** Light orbit rollovers via the saucer, start a drop target bank.
**Ball 2:** Push toward 5X bonus multiplier, keep saucer value climbing.
**Ball 3+:** Pivot to orbits once maxed, bank bonus before draining.

**Target High-Score Ballpark:** **100K–145K** is a strong, near-maximal game on Bobby Orr's Power Play, given the hard 145K ceiling from base bonus × multiplier.
