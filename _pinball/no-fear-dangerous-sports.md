# 🏍️ No Fear: Dangerous Sports - Strategy Guide

*Williams, 1995 — Designed by Steve Ritchie*

> **"No limits. No fear. Meet Your Maker."**

No Fear packs five extreme-sports athlete modes, four escalating multiballs, and a final wizard mode where every jump is worth 200 million points. Spelling N-O-F-E-A-R through mode completions is the thread connecting No Limits, Fear Fest, and ultimately Meet Your Maker. Our goal: complete athlete challenges to spell NOFEAR, stack toward Fear Fest, and chase the four-ball Meet Your Maker finale.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Cycle the skill shot options at the plunger**: Start Challenge, Super Cross Level, Advance Raceway, Ball Lock, or Flipper Skill Shot — making the Jump Ramp scores **50M**
- [ ] **Lock 3 balls to start regular Multiball** — hit the Skydive drop target once, then shoot Skydive or Skull to lock
- [ ] **During multiball, jackpots progress Skull (50M) → Skydive (100M) → Jump Ramp (200M)**, repeating until fewer than 2 balls remain
- [ ] **Spell N-O-F-E-A-R through mode completions** to build toward No Limits → Fear Fest → Meet Your Maker
- [ ] **Trap on the left flipper and abuse the Jump Ramp repeatedly during multiball** — it's one of the highest-value repeatable shots in the game
- [ ] **5 consecutive Raceway shots light Extra Ball at Skull** — a reliable, repeatable EB source

## 🏔️ Five Athlete Challenge Modes

| Mode | Mechanics |
|---|---|
| **Dirt Challenge** | 30-sec ramp-shooting mode; values escalate 5M per shot (max 20M) |
| **Asphalt Challenge** | 3 Track orbits worth 50M/75M/100M within 30 seconds |
| **Water Challenge** | 30-sec timer; Tube Saucer shots add time and increase shot values |
| **Snow Challenge** | 30-sec mode requiring Hairpin Ramp completion; magnets redirect Jump Ramp attempts during this mode |
| **Air Challenge** | Skydive countdown from 25M; complete lit shots within 30 sec for a 75M bonus |

## 🎁 Multiball Progression

### Regular Multiball (3-Ball)

1. Lock **3 balls** (hit Skydive drop target once, then Skydive or Skull to lock)
2. Jackpot sequence: **Skull (50M) → Skydive (100M) → Jump Ramp (200M)**, repeating
3. Ends when **fewer than 2 balls** remain in play
4. Subsequent multiballs require **hitting the drop target again for each lock**

### Over the Edge (2-Ball)

- **Qualify** by achieving 1st Place **three times**, or via a random **Tube Saucer award**
- **Jump Ramp and Hairpin Ramp** each worth **25M base + 10M per ramp shot**

### No Limits (3-Ball, Major Challenge)

- **Qualify** by spelling **N-O-F-E-A-R** through mode completions
- All major shots lit for **20M**, each collection **adds 1M** to that shot's value (no cap)

### Fear Fest (3-Ball, Major Challenge)

- **Qualify** by spelling **N-O-F-E-A-R again**, after No Limits completion
- **Skull or Skydive** jackpots start at **80M**, increasing **20M per major shot**

---

## 🏆 Meet Your Maker (Wizard Mode)

**Requirement:** Final challenge after completing the N-O-F-E-A-R sequence through No Limits and Fear Fest.

- **4-ball multiball**
- All switches worth **250K**
- **50M jackpot at Skull**, **200M at Jump Ramp**
- **Extra Ball lit**
- **"Payback Time" active**
- **20-second ball saver**

> 💎 This is the ultimate payoff of the entire NOFEAR-spelling chain — every jump during this mode is worth 200 million points.

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **Jump Ramp under normal play** | Despite being a loop-style ramp, it features **magnets that redirect weak shots back toward the left inlane** — making it safer than it looks, but still requires real commitment | Commit fully to the shot rather than taking a half-hearted attempt |
| **No pop bumpers** | This layout lacks the forgiving chaos of pop bumpers, making misses more consequential | Play deliberate, controlled shots |
| **Snow Challenge's magnet redirection** | A unique mode-specific mechanic affecting Jump Ramp attempts | Be aware the Jump Ramp behaves differently during this specific mode |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Game (Ball 1)

1. Take the skill shot, ideally scoring 50M via the Jump Ramp
2. Start an athlete challenge mode
3. Work toward your first regular multiball lock

**Target Score:** 50M–150M

### Mid Game (Ball 2)

1. Run regular multiball, trapping the left flipper and abusing the Jump Ramp for repeated 200M jackpots
2. Continue completing athlete modes to spell N-O-F-E-A-R
3. Chase 5 consecutive Raceway shots for an Extra Ball

**Target Score:** 150M–400M

### Late Game (Ball 3+)

1. Qualify and run No Limits, then Fear Fest as NOFEAR completes
2. Push for Meet Your Maker if you've finished the full chain
3. Maximize Jump Ramp abuse during any active multiball for 200M-per-shot value

**Target Score per ball:** 200M–800M+
