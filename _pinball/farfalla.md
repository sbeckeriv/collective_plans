# 🦋 Farfalla - Strategy Guide

*Zaccaria, 1983*

> **"Butterfly" — six flippers, one timer, endless drop targets.**

Farfalla ("Butterfly" in Italian) is a distinctive Italian widebody with **six flippers** — two on the main playfield, two on an upper transparent playfield, and two dedicated outlane-save flippers — plus a unique timed "bonus ball" feature on your last ball. Our goal: keep the ball alive as long as possible, collect Multi Special, and use your last-ball timer wisely.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Shoot yellow, blue, and red targets** to collect toward **Special** and eventually **Multi Special**
- [ ] **Yellow targets are the most lucrative** — prioritize them when available
- [ ] **After 21 drop targets hit, Special Bonus lights** and must be **collected before the ramp can be lowered again**
- [ ] **Orange targets have their own separate Special**
- [ ] **Light LOVE for a multiplier** on your Special Bonus
- [ ] **Use the React Feature** on outlane drains — a split-second window to use a smaller flipper and knock the ball back into play
- [ ] **On your last ball, a timer counts up while you play** — after you drain, you can keep playing until that timer counts back down to zero, so build it as high as you can

---

## 🦋 Core Mechanics

### Target Colors & Special Collection

| Target Color | Function |
|---|---|
| **Yellow** | Most lucrative — collect toward Special/Multi Special |
| **Blue** | Collect toward Special/Multi Special |
| **Red** | Collect toward Special/Multi Special |
| **Orange** | Separate, standalone Special |

**Standup targets** lower the ramp to the **upper transparent playfield** — your route to the secondary flipper set.

### Special Bonus & the 21-Target Threshold

After **21 drop targets** have been hit across the game, **Special Bonus lights** — and importantly, **it must be collected before the ramp can be lowered again**. Don't ignore a lit Special Bonus while chasing the upper playfield ramp, or you'll find yourself locked out until you go back and collect it.

### LOVE Multiplier

Lighting **LOVE** applies a multiplier to your Special Bonus value — a meaningful boost worth prioritizing once you're close to a Special collection.

### Six Flippers & the React Feature

This widebody features:
- **2 main playfield flippers**
- **2 upper transparent playfield flippers**
- **2 dedicated outlane-save flippers**

When the ball heads down an outlane, the **React Feature** gives you a **split-second window** to use the smaller outlane flipper and knock the ball back into play — a genuine second chance, but one that requires fast reflexes.

### Last-Ball Timer ("Bonus Ball")

On your **final ball**, a timer **increments while you play**. Once that ball drains, **you can keep playing until the timer counts back down to zero** — meaning a long, skillful final ball directly extends your total play time even after the "real" ball ends.

> 🎯 **Pro Tip — Core Strategy:** Since the entire game is fundamentally about *keeping the ball in play*, prioritize safe, sustainable scoring over risky high-value attempts — especially on your last ball, where survival time directly banks you bonus playing time.

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **Outlane drains without using the React Feature** | A wasted save opportunity since the window is brief but real | Stay alert and ready to react the instant the ball heads toward an outlane |
| **Lowering the ramp with Special Bonus still lit** | You won't be able to lower it again until you collect | Always collect a lit Special Bonus before chasing the upper playfield |
| **Six-flipper complexity** | More flippers to track and time correctly than most machines | Practice each flipper set's role individually before relying on fast transitions between them |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Ball

1. Start working yellow targets for their high value
2. Build toward your first Special collection
3. Get familiar with the upper transparent playfield via the standup-target ramp

### Mid Ball

1. Track your drop target count toward the 21-target Special Bonus threshold
2. Light LOVE for a multiplier before collecting Special Bonus
3. Use the React Feature on any outlane drains

### Late Ball (especially your final ball)

1. Play for survival and timer accumulation rather than risky high-value shots
2. Collect any lit Special Bonus before it blocks ramp access
3. After draining your last ball, keep playing until the bonus timer counts down

| **React Feature flippers** | Verify quick, reliable response | Central to the outlane-save mechanic that defines this machine's safety net |
| **Drop target reset** | Check for clean, reliable resets | Affects your progress toward the 21-target Special Bonus threshold |

## 📋 Quick Reference: Strategic Priorities

**Early:** Work yellow targets, build toward Special, explore the upper playfield.
**Mid:** Track drop targets toward 21 for Special Bonus, light LOVE for multiplier.
**Late:** Play for survival on your last ball, maximize your post-drain timer.

**Target Outcome:** Collecting Multi Special with the LOVE multiplier active, combined with effective use of the React Feature and a long final-ball timer, represents a strong, well-played game on this distinctive six-flipper Italian classic.
