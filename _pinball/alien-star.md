# 👽 ALIEN STAR Pinball Machine - Strategy Guide

*Gottlieb, 1984 — Early Solid-State Sci-Fi Classic*

> **Light the A-L-I-E-N, chase the 5X multiball, and hit that spinner hard.**

Alien Star is a lean, fast 1984 Gottlieb that cuts straight to the point: complete standup targets, lock a ball, and capitalize on a two-ball multiball with a playfield multiplier that maxes at 5X. With no modes, progression systems, or wizard modes—just pure mechanical pinball with skill-shot urgency—it rewards quick decisions and solid spinner control. Our goal: hit **800,000–1.4 million points**, a realistic benchmark for well-tuned machines.

## 🎱 Core Playfield Features

### A-L-I-E-N Standup Targets

Located on the right side of the playfield. Completing all five (A, L, I, E, N) in sequence lights the ball lock on the left.

**Strategic note:** The "N" target is notoriously difficult to hit cleanly due to the right slingshot's proximity. Plan to hit it carefully or accept resets.

| Target | Location | Notes |
|--------|----------|-------|
| A | Upper right | Easiest entry |
| L | Mid right | Standard shots |
| I | Mid right | Adjacent to L |
| E | Lower right | Accessible from flipper |
| N | Lower right near sling | Most difficult; close to slingshot |

Once all five are complete, the lock lights and an award lamp rotates among the top lanes.

### The Left Lock Saucer

Activated after completing A-L-I-E-N. The saucer holds a **playfield multiplier** that cycles between **2X, 3X, and 5X**. Each spinner hit advances the multiplier value.

**Critical strategy:** Before locking, use the left spinner to cycle the multiplier to 5X, then shoot the lock. This multiplier applies to all shots during the subsequent two-ball multiball.

### Top Lanes (Four Lanes)

Awards rotate among four options:
- **Hold Bonus** — Bonus is held at ball end
- **Collect Bonus** — Bonus is collected immediately
- **Advance Bonus Multiplier** — Increases bonus multiplier (up to 5X)
- **Extra Ball** — Awards extra ball

The active lane cycles; track which one is lit and plan your top lane shots accordingly.

### The Left Spinner

Primary scoring shot and the engine of multiball scoring. Advances the lock multiplier and generates massive points when shot during multiball, especially with the 10X bonus that triggers after the right return lane is hit.

| Condition | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Normal play | Base value |
| After right return lane, during multiball | 10X value (potentially 50X with multiplier) |
| Max single shot | 1M+ (with optimal timing and multiplier) |

### Pop Bumpers (3)

Scattered on the playfield. They're bonus builders but inherently chaotic; avoid relying on them for critical shots. Use them defensively when ball control is poor.

### Slingshots (2)

Left and right slingshots provide standard pinball hazards. They drain points back into play but can create unpredictable bounces, especially near the "N" target.

## 🎱 Bonus System

**Base Bonus:** Starts at a low value. Each top lane hit advances bonus by small increments. Maximum base bonus: **29K points**.

**Bonus Multiplier:** Earned by hitting the "Advance Bonus Multiplier" top lane award. Multiplier increases up to **5X**.

**Collection:** Bonus is collected at ball end (if "Hold Bonus" is not lit) or immediately (if "Collect Bonus" is lit). With a 5X multiplier at max 29K base, collected bonus can reach **145K per ball**.

**Carry-over:** Unused bonus multiplier carries to the next ball if held.

## 🎱 Multiball Mechanics

Once A-L-I-E-N is complete and the lock is shot, the game ejects a second ball into play. Both balls remain in play until one drains (total time: ~30–60 seconds depending on play).

**Multiplier active:** The 2X, 3X, or 5X playfield multiplier you cycled into the lock applies to **all shots** during multiball, making multiball the primary scoring window.

**Ball lock feeds:** The saucer ejects the locked ball into play with a moderate feed toward the right flipper. Ball control from this feed is critical—a clean trap allows setup for the high-value spinner shot.

**Jackpot:** Hitting the spinner immediately after the right return lane switch activates a 10X value bonus on the spinner shot. With a 5X playfield multiplier active, this can yield over 1 million points on a single shot.

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Ball (Ball 1)

1. **Complete A-L-I-E-N targets** — This is your primary objective. Work methodically on the right side.
2. **Build spinner value** — Each spinner hit advances the lock multiplier; aim for it to cycle to 5X.
3. **Top lane hits** — If you can safely reach them, grab "Advance Bonus Multiplier" and "Extra Ball" awards.
4. **Avoid aggressive play** — Early drains hurt your late-game multiplier setup.

**Target Score:** 200K–500K

### Mid Ball (Ball 2)

1. **Lock the ball at 5X multiplier** — If you achieved it on Ball 1, this is your move. Lock and go to multiball.
2. **During multiball, control the second ball** — Trap it cleanly after the saucer ejects.
3. **Hit the spinner** after the right return lane activates the 10X bonus.
4. **Ride the multiplier** — All other shots are also multiplied; hit whatever you can safely reach.

**Target Score:** 500K–1M (including multiball)

### Late Ball (Ball 3+)

1. **Repeat A-L-I-E-N** if the targets have reset, or exploit any remaining lit lanes.
2. **Collect bonus** repeatedly from top lanes.
3. **Reach multiball again** if possible.
4. **Play for extra balls** — They extend your game window and scoring opportunity.

**Target Score:** 300K–600K per ball

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Shot | Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------|-----------|
| **Right slingshot area** | "N" target is close to slingshot; miss and drain is likely | Aim deliberately; accept resets if needed |
| **Center shots to pop bumpers** | Chaotic bounces to outlanes during bumper chaos | Avoid unless building bonus desperately |
| **Aggressive spinner plays** | Right flipper control is needed; weak shots drift left | Use short, controlled flips |
| **Lock saucer feed during multiball** | Ball exits with speed toward right; control is critical | Cradle trap on right flipper immediately |

### Safe Control Positions

- **Right flipper trap:** Best position after lock feed or right return lane
- **Left flipper cradle:** Plan next shot; aim at left spinner or top lanes
- **Post pass:** Transfer between flippers to set up spinner shots

## 🎯 Shot Analysis

### The Spinner (Your Money Shot)

**Why it matters:** The spinner is your primary scoring engine. During multiball, especially when the 10X bonus is active, a well-timed spinner shot is worth over 1 million points.

**How to execute:**
- Trap ball on right flipper after lock feed or right return lane
- Aim for the left spinner
- Hold the shot just long enough for the ball to register multiple spins
- Repeat if ball returns

### The A-L-I-E-N Targets (Your Gate)

**Why they matter:** Completing them lights the lock and activates multiball, the primary scoring mode.

**How to execute:**
- Work the right side methodically (A → L → I → E → N)
- Use right flipper shots toward standup targets
- Accept that "N" is difficult; reset and retry if blocked
- On subsequent balls, repeating the sequence relights the lock

### Top Lanes (Bonus & Extra Balls)

**Why they matter:** Awards rotate among hold/collect/multiplier/extra ball. Grabbing multiplier lanes stacks bonus value.

**How to execute:**
- Aim upper flipper shots toward the rotating lane
- Prioritize "Advance Bonus Multiplier" and "Extra Ball" awards
- Track the active lane via callouts or lighting

### Right Return Lane (The Setup Shot)

**Why it matters:** Hitting the right return lane activates the 10X spinner bonus for a few seconds, turning the next spinner shot into a potential 1M+ point opportunity.

**How to execute:**
- Let ball drain to right outlane, catch on right flipper
- Immediately shoot the spinner before the 10X expires
- Clean trap and smooth shot maximize this combo
