# 🧛 Dracula - Strategy Guide

*Stern Electronics, 1979 — Designed by Harry Williams*

> **"Max your bonus. Earn the permanent spinner."**

Dracula is a build-and-collect bonus game at heart: nearly every shot exists to push your end-of-ball bonus higher, and the spinner's value is tied directly to specific bonus milestones rather than a separate skill shot. Our goal: max bonus to 20,000, permanently light the high-value spinner, and double it with the 2X multiplier.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Light the 2X bonus multiplier** via either 3 "star" loop shots around the left circle (past the saucer) or completing the left 3-bank drop targets twice
- [ ] **Understand the spinner isn't lit by skill** — it's lit automatically at specific bonus milestones: **2,000, 8,000, 14,000, and 20,000 (max)**
- [ ] **Max your bonus to permanently light the spinner** — this is the only way to keep it lit for good
- [ ] **Prioritize the top bumper** (1,000 points + bonus advance) over the other bumpers (only 100 points each)
- [ ] **Generally ignore the right-side X-Y-Z standup targets**, except the 4th standup (above the X) — it registers a star loop just like the left circle shot

## 🦇 Core Mechanics

### Bonus Multiplier (2X)

Light it via either:
- **3 "star" loop shots** around the left circle (past the saucer), **or**
- Completing the **left 3-bank drop targets twice**

### The Spinner (Lit by Bonus Milestones, Not Skill)

This is the game's signature quirk — the spinner doesn't light from a skillful shot. Instead, it lights automatically whenever your **end-of-ball base bonus** hits specific values:

| Bonus Milestone | Spinner Status |
|---|---|
| 2,000 | Lit |
| 8,000 | Lit |
| 14,000 | Lit |
| **20,000 (max)** | **Lit permanently** |

When lit, the spinner pays **1,000 per spin** instead of the normal 100 — a 10x difference. **The only way to keep it lit for the rest of the ball is to max out your bonus to 20,000.**

> 💰 **Pro Tip — Core Strategy:** Since the spinner's value is entirely tied to your bonus level, treat bonus-building shots (drop targets, star loops, the top bumper) as your real priority. The spinner reward follows automatically once bonus is high enough — you don't need to "earn" it separately.

### Bumper Priority

| Bumper | Value |
|---|---|
| **Top bumper** | 1,000 points + bonus advance |
| All other bumpers | 100 points each |

> 🎯 **Pro Tip:** The top bumper is worth 10x the others *and* advances your bonus — if you have any control over where the ball spends its bumper time, favor the top bumper.

### Standup Targets (Mostly Skippable)

The right-side **X-Y-Z** standup targets are generally not worth detouring for. The exception is the **4th standup target** (positioned above the X) — it registers a **star loop** completion, identical to shooting the left circle, making it a useful alternative path toward your 2X multiplier.

| **Horseshoe lane misses** | Can deflect unpredictably | Approach with controlled, deliberate shots |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Ball

1. Work the left 3-bank drop targets or star loop shots toward your first 2X multiplier
2. Let the top bumper passively build bonus toward your first spinner milestone (2,000)
3. Use the 4th standup target as an alternative star-loop path if convenient

### Mid Ball

1. Keep pushing bonus toward 8,000 and 14,000 milestones, unlocking the spinner each time
2. Grind the spinner at 1,000/spin whenever it's lit
3. Avoid wasting shots on the low-value X-Y-Z standups

### Late Ball

1. Push bonus all the way to the 20,000 maximum to permanently light the spinner
2. Grind the now-permanent 1,000/spin spinner for the rest of the ball
3. Double everything with your 2X multiplier if you haven't already lit it

**Early:** Build toward 2X multiplier via drop targets or star loops, let the top bumper build bonus.
**Mid:** Push bonus through the 8,000 and 14,000 spinner milestones, grind the lit spinner.
**Late:** Max bonus to 20,000 for a permanent spinner, grind it for the rest of the ball.
