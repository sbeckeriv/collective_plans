### Portal Locks & Victory Laps

**Portal Locks enable multiball during Gem Quests!** Prior to starting a Gem Quest, up to 2 Portal Locks can be qualified and collected at the disc.

**How to Qualify & Collect Portal Locks:**

| Method               | Requirement                   |
| -------------------- | ----------------------------- |
| Combos               | 4, 10, 20, 35, 45, 55+ combos |
| Secret Skill Shot #1 | Medium plunge to mini loop    |
| Hawkeye Challenge    | Complete Level 1              |
| Computer Grid        | Bingo award                   |

Once qualified, shoot the disc to collect and bank the Portal Lock.

**Starting a Gem Quest with Portal Locks:**
- 1 Portal Lock banked = **2-ball multiball** during quest = **2x scoring**
- 2 Portal Locks banked = **3-ball multiball** during quest = **3x scoring**
- **Keep the balls in play!** Scoring multiplier only applies while all balls are active
- Pop bumper lane and upper loop score 2x during Portal Lock multiball

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Portal Locks can be hoarded! You can have locks in reserve for future quests <kcite ref="60"/>. Always bank 2 Portal Locks before starting high-value quests like Reality Gem.

**Victory Laps:** After completing a Gem Quest with extra balls still in play, all major shots light for hurry-up scoring. Spin disc to raise value, multiplied by balls in play.

1. Spell I-R-O-N at return lanes (disc changes letters, NOT flippers!)
2. Lock 3 balls at Avengers Tower (1 lock for subsequent MBs)

**Jackpot Structure:**

| Phase | Jackpots At                | Then...                     |

| 1     | Left ramp + Right ramp     | Spell STRANGE at disc       |
| 2     | Left orbit + Right orbit   | Spell STRANGE at disc       |
| 3     | Pops lane + Captain Marvel | Spell STRANGE at disc       |
| 4     | Tower + Mini orbit         | Light Super Jackpot at disc |

**Super Jackpot:** 250K per disc spin for 22 seconds <kcite ref="21"/>

**Tower 2x Super:** Get two balls into Tower simultaneously for double Super Jackpot!

**Add-a-Ball:** Spell I-R-O-N during multiball → shoot Sanctum target (once per MB)

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Level 2 Iron Man doubles ALL Iron Man Multiball scoring <kcite ref="22"/>.

### ⚡ Thor Multiball

**How to Start:**
- Spell T-H-O-R at captive ball (T & H spotted at game start on Easy)
- 1st MB: 3 shots | 2nd MB: Spell THOR 2x | 3rd+: Spell THOR 3x

**Jackpot Structure:**
1. All shots lit for jackpots (200K base + 2.5K per disc spin)
2. Collect X jackpots → Captive ball lights for Mjolnir Attack
3. Mjolnir Attack = Jackpot × THOR letters collected (1-4x)
4. Repeat sequence (2 JPs → captive, 3 JPs → captive, etc.)
5. Spell THOR (4 sequences) → **Super Jackpot at Tower** (20x jackpot)

**Double Jackpots:** Pop lane and upper loop score 2x and boost Mjolnir Attack 2x <kcite ref="21"/>

**Add-a-Ball:** Spell STRANGE at disc → shoot Sanctum target

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Level 2 Thor doubles all captive ball scoring. Level 1 Black Widow lights captive ball for 2x THOR letters after left inlane <kcite ref="21"/>.

### Gem Placement & Perks

**What is Gem Placement?** After you complete a Gem Quest, that Infinity Gem is automatically placed on one of the playfield shots (Avengers, Drop Targets, or Captain Marvel). Each gem provides different bonuses depending on where it's placed and what level that shot is at.

**How to Control Placement:**
1. **Initial placement** is automatic when you complete the Gem Quest
2. **Light "Change Gems"** through Computer Grid bingos or Mystery awards
3. When lit, shoot any Avenger shot to **move that gem** to a different location
4. **Alternative:** Use the Space Gem's ability - shoot the Space Gem shot, then press the Action Button within 3 seconds to rotate ALL gems one position (1-3 uses depending on Space Gem level)

**Strategic Placement:** The same gem provides different benefits depending on where you place it. For example, the Soul Gem on a Level 2 Avenger gives you a 150% jackpot, but only 50% on other locations. This creates deep strategy around optimizing gem positioning for your playstyle.

After collecting a gem, place it on any shot for unique perks:

## 🏆 Wizard Modes

### Mini-Wizard Modes (Avengers Assemble)

Collect all 6 Avengers → Shoot right ramp to start (in order):

#### 💚 Soul Gem Quest

| Setting            | Flips                   | Points per Shot |
| ------------------ | ----------------------- | --------------- |
| Normal             | 30 start, +8 per shot   | Base            |
| Hard (hold Action) | 20 start, less per shot | **10x scoring** |

**Shot Order:**
1. Right orbit → 2. Left ramp → 3. Right ramp → 4. Left orbit → 5. Captive ball → 6. Drop targets → 7. Captain Marvel → 8. Tower

- Drain = -5 flips
- Flip counter hits 0 = Mode failed
- Mystery/Flank Attack spots current shot (not Tower)
- **Completion awards Soul Gem** <kcite ref="21"/>

#### 🔴 Black Order Multiball

3-ball multiball. Light Avenger jackpots by shooting their shots:

| Avenger         | Requirement      |
| --------------- | ---------------- |
| Hulk            | 50 spinner spins |
| Iron Man        | Complete I-R-O-N |
| Captain America | 20 pop hits      |
| Black Widow     | 3 left ramps     |
| Thor            | Spell THOR       |
| Black Panther   | 3 right orbits   |

- More Avengers lit = higher jackpot multiplier
- Captain Marvel adds +1x to jackpot multiplier
- **Jackpot at upper loop** → Spell STRANGE → **Super Jackpot at pop lane**
- Super JP = 5M + all jackpots since last Super + adds ball + returns Soul Gem if lost
- **Soul Gem Extension:** Hold Action button to risk Soul Gem for multiball restart <kcite ref="22"/>

#### ⚔️ Black Order Battle Royale

6 lives (Avengers). Defeat all Black Order members.

- Each Avenger has health bar + flip counter
- Health depletes from: flip counter reaching 0, timer reaching 0, draining
- Shoot lit RGB arrows to damage Black Order and recharge flips
- Clear all arrows = defeat member, reset timer/flips

**Support Characters:**
- Drops = Vision adds 8 flips
- Disc = STRANGE lights 3x/4x power-up shot
- Captain Marvel = Freeze timer
- Upper loop = Hawkeye lights 3 left shots
- Ant-Man = 1st hit = damage, 2nd hit = Wasp adds ball (2x damage/score)

**Completion Bonus:** 20M per remaining Avenger + 1M per flip + 1M per second <kcite ref="22"/>

### Thanos Modes

#### ⚔️ Thanos Attacks!
**Requirement:** 2+ Gems + Soul Gem collected

Defend each gem you have:
1. Shoot gem shot to select
2. Shoot left orbit, left ramp, right orbit (+ 1 more if Thanos has Power Gem)
3. Shoot right ramp to defend
4. 45 seconds per gem (+5 sec per lit shot, +15 sec for drops)

- Fail = Thanos takes that gem
- **Success (all gems defended):** Win back lost gem OR start Super Victory Laps <kcite ref="21"/>

#### 👊 Battle Thanos (Final Wizard Mode)
**Requirement:** All 6 Gem Quests attempted + All 3 mini-wizard modes played

4-ball multiball:
- Blue shots (Span 1) + Red shots (Span 2) deal damage
- Disc rotates shot positions
- Overlapping blue+red = 4x jackpot, 2x damage
- Spell THOR = add ball
- Reduce Thanos health to 0 → **Final Blow at Tower**

> 🎉 **1 BILLION POINT BONUS:** Start Battle Thanos with ALL 6 GEMS collected = automatic victory + 1,000,000,000 points! <kcite ref="21"/>

#### 🌟 Super Victory Laps
- 3-ball (after Thanos Attacks) or 6-ball (after Battle Thanos)
- All shots lit for 500K base (vs 250K normal)
- Multiplied by balls in play
- Only most recent shot unlights <kcite ref="22"/>

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Path to 1 Billion Points

#### 🟢 Early Game (Ball 1)
1. **Soft plunge** for skill shot (+3 sec ball save)
2. **Make 4 combos** (left ramp → Captain Marvel is easiest) to light Portal Lock
3. **Collect Portal Lock** at disc
4. **Work toward Thor MB** (3 captive ball hits)
5. **Spell STRANGE** while building Thor
6. **Start Reality Gem Quest** with Portal Lock active

#### 🟡 Mid Game (Balls 1-2)
1. **Stack Thor MB into Gem Quest** for massive scoring
2. **Keep spinning disc** during Reality Quest for 7x multiplier
3. **Complete quest while in multiball** for Victory Laps
4. **Place gems strategically:**
   - Soul Gem on highest level Avenger
   - Power Gem on drops
   - Reality Gem on Hulk
5. **Collect Soul Gem Jackpot** (150% at Level 2!)
6. **Work toward 10 combos** for second Portal Lock

#### 🔴 Late Game (Ball 3+)
1. **Stack 2 Portal Locks** before each Gem Quest (3x scoring)
2. **Complete all 6 Gem Quests** (aim for 30M+ each for Gold trophies)
3. **Collect all Avengers 3x** for all mini-wizard modes
4. **Reach Battle Thanos with all 6 gems** = **1 BILLION BONUS**

### Recommended Gem Collection Order

Complete gems in this strategic sequence to maximize scoring and progression:

#### 1️⃣ Reality Gem (Ebony Maw) - FIRST
- **Why:** Easiest to stack for massive scoring (100M+ with 2 Portal Locks)
- **Strategy:** Keep spinning disc for 7x multiplier throughout quest
- **Placement:** Hulk spinner for boosted scoring

#### 2️⃣ Power Gem (Black Dwarf) - SECOND  
- **Why:** Critical for long-term progression
- **Benefits:** Spots extra grid awards (3x), keeps Avenger levels through drains
- **Placement:** Drop targets immediately

#### 3️⃣ Mind Gem (Supergiant) - THIRD
- **Why:** Straightforward quest, easier with Power Gem accelerating progress
- **Strategy:** Free all 4 Avengers before spinning disc for 5x multiplier
- **Placement:** Black Panther to spot shots during modes

#### 4️⃣ Space Gem (Corvus Glaive) - FOURTH
- **Why:** Moderate difficulty, unlocks gem rotation ability
- **Strategy:** Left ramp → Right ramp → Mini loop = 4x attack value
- **Placement:** Captain America for easy recharge via pops

#### 5️⃣ Time Gem (Proxima Midnight) - FIFTH
- **Why:** Complete AFTER Power/Reality gems on drops for easier completion
- **Strategy:** Use gem placement to help with drop target cycling
- **Placement:** Black Widow for easy timer boost access

#### 6️⃣ Soul Gem (Mini-Wizard) - LAST
- **Why:** Requires all 6 Avengers collected first
- **Strategy:** 30 flips to complete flip-limited challenge
- **Placement:** Highest Level 2 Avenger for 150% jackpot bonus

> 💡 **Quick Path:** Reality → Power → Mind → Space → Time → Soul maximizes early scoring while building infrastructure for late-game dominance.
