# ⭐ STARS Pinball Machine - Strategy Guide

*Stern Electronics, 1978 — Foundational Solid-State Design*

> **Five stars, two banks, one goal: light the special and cash in.**

Stars is one of Stern's earliest solid-state pinballs, released in March 1978. With its simple but elegant five-star standup target layout, two drop target banks, dual spinners, and a scoring system that scales with player progress, it remains a beautiful and strategic machine. This guide targets a realistic competitive score of **200K–300K**, based on period-appropriate play and documented ruleset variation <kcite ref="1"/>.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Complete all five stars** to light the special (lit red star)
- [ ] **Shoot the lit red special** repeatedly — it awards 100K each time
- [ ] **Work the right spinner:** It increases 200 points per star (up to 1K/spin at 5 stars)
- [ ] **Hit drop targets strategically:** Complete one 3-bank for 2X bonus, both banks for 3X
- [ ] **Build bonus via return lanes:** Lit lanes award triple bonus advances (use them!)
- [ ] **Monitor drain paths:** Bumper area feeds dangerously right; control flipper shots carefully

## 🎱 Core Mechanics

### Star Stand-Up Targets (5 Total)

| Target   | Points | Effect |
|----------|--------|--------|
| **Each star** | 500 | Advances one bonus point; lights right spinner value |
| **All 5 collected** | N/A | **Lights red special** at one of the five stars |
| **Red special (lit)** | 100,000 | Repeatable each time shot; resets at drain |

**Strategy:** Hit stars in any order. Once all five are collected, one star becomes red and is lit as the special. You may shoot the special multiple times before draining, depending on machine settings (some allow unlimited 100K awards per ball, others cap it at one per ball).

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** On machines with unlimited specials, light all stars ASAP, then camp the lit red star and shoot it as many times as you can before tilting or draining. Each shot is 100K. <kcite ref="1"/>

### Drop Target Banks (2 × 3-bank)

| Completion | Bonus Multiplier | Points Awarded |
|------------|------------------|-----------------|
| **One 3-bank completed** | 2X | 3,000 (reset) |
| **Both banks completed** | 3X | 3,000 (reset) |

**Mechanics:** Each drop target is worth 500 points. Completing either 3-bank increments your end-of-ball bonus multiplier from 1X → 2X. Completing both banks advances from 2X → 3X. After both are completed, subsequent completions reset both banks and award 3,000 points plus the ability to build multiplier again.

> ⚠️ **Important:** Multiplier maxes at 3X on most settings, though some ROM versions allow higher progressions. Check your machine.

### Spinners (2 Total)

| Spinner | Starting Value | Scaling |
|---------|-----------------|---------|
| **Right spinner** | 200 points/spin | +200 per star (1K/spin at all 5) |
| **Left spinner** | 200 points/spin | Advances with drop target completion |

**Strategy:** The right spinner becomes your high-value spinner once you've collected all five stars. At that point, each spin is worth 1,000 points. Spin it liberally if drop targets are risky. The left spinner sits between the drop targets and is lower-value early game.

### Bonus System

**Base Bonus:** Starts at 1K per ball and advances via:
- Star targets: +1 bonus point per hit
- Return lanes (unlit): +1 bonus point
- Return lanes (lit): +3 bonus points (triple advance!)
- Button rollovers (center and top): +1 bonus point

**Base Maximum:** 19K (without multiplier)

**With Multiplier:**
- 1X multiplier: 19K
- 2X multiplier: 38K
- 3X multiplier: 57K

**Timing:** Bonus is awarded at end of ball, then resets to 1K for the next ball.

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Prioritize lit return lanes early — they triple your bonus advance rate and are relatively safe shots. Build to 10K+ bonus before attempting risky drop target work. <kcite ref="1"/><kcite ref="2"/>

### Pop Bumper

| Feature | Value |
|---------|-------|
| **Pop bumper hit** | 1,000 points |
| **Always lit** | No progression needed |

**Strategy:** The bumper is a defensive score builder but also dangerous — its bounces can send the ball toward outlanes. Use it as a bonus advance tool when other shots are unavailable, not as your primary target.

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

### High-Risk Areas

| Hazard | Location | Mitigation |
|--------|----------|-----------|
| **Bumper area rebounds** | Center-upper | Avoid prolonged bumper play; let it settle before next shot |
| **Right outslide feed** | Right side of bumper | Spinner and drop target rebounds can deflect right — control shots carefully |
| **Center post uncertainty** | Between flippers | Center shots are unpredictable; trap after orbit returns instead |
| **Left outlane (unlit)** | Left side | Safe only if you have lit return lane active |

### Safe Ball Control Positions

- **Upper flipper cradle:** Safest trap position for planning next shot
- **Lower flipper hold:** Allows setup for controlled star or spinner shot
- **Live catch after orbit:** Minimizes bumper exposure on return

## 💰 Scoring Strategy by Ball

### Ball 1 (Foundation)

**Goal:** Build bonus and complete first star sequence

1. **Plunge smoothly** to avoid center bumper chaos
2. **Hit 3–4 return lanes** (especially lit ones) to build bonus base to 5K+
3. **Start collecting stars** — any order, goal is all five by end of ball
4. **Avoid risky drop target work** — only attempt if you're controlling well and bonus is already building
5. **Drain safely:** Accept end of ball with 10K+ bonus, even at 2–3 stars

**Target:** 15K–50K total score, bonus multiplier at 1X

### Ball 2 (Aggressive Build)

**Goal:** Complete stars, light special, start multiplier work

1. **Resume star collection** if not finished — this is priority one
2. **Once 5 stars collected:** Shoot the lit red special immediately to earn 100K
3. **Work one 3-bank of drop targets** to light 2X multiplier (if not already)
4. **Build bonus via return lanes and spinners** to 10K+
5. **Attempt second special** if machine allows repeats

**Target:** 100K–200K cumulative score, multiplier at 2X or higher

### Ball 3+ (Multiplier Payoff)

**Goal:** Maximize bonus value with earned multiplier

1. **Complete drop targets if multiplier isn't maxed** — push for 3X if reachable
2. **Focus on bonus builders:** Return lanes, button rollovers, stars (if reset)
3. **Shoot lit special every chance** (if repeatable)
4. **Hit right spinner** for 1K/spin value once stars are all lit
5. **Time final bonus** — bank your 57K (at 3X) by end of ball

**Target:** 150K–300K per ball, cumulative 200K–500K+

## 🎱 Shot Analysis

### The Star Targets (Your Primary Gate)

**Why it matters:** Lighting the special requires all five stars. Special is your fastest 100K per shot.

**How to execute:**
- Shoot controlled shots toward the five standup targets (distributed across playfield)
- Upper-left, center, and right targets are most accessible from flippers
- Once all five are down, one lights red
- Shoot that red star repeatedly before draining

**Scoring impact:** 500 pts per star × 5 = 2,500 direct points + ~100K per special = **102.5K** per ball if specials are unlimited per ball

### The Right Spinner (Late-Game Workhorse)

**Why it matters:** Once all stars are lit, this spinner becomes 1,000 per spin — your safest repeatable shot for building scores late game.

**How to execute:**
- Approach from right flipper with controlled shot
- Gentle orbit feed to right spinner
- Spin multiple times in a row if ball stays in spinner lane
- Repeat as often as playfield allows

**Scoring impact:** 20 spins × 1,000 = 20K (plus bonus advancement)

### The Drop Target Banks (Multiplier Gates)

**Why they matter:** Required for 2X and 3X bonus multiplier, which scales your end-of-ball payout by 2–3×.

**How to execute:**
- Work one 3-bank first (easier access from one flipper)
- Complete all three targets in that bank
- This advances multiplier to 2X
- Return to the other 3-bank to push to 3X
- If both are completed, you can reset and repeat for additional 3K awards

**Scoring impact:** 3X multiplier on 57K bonus = 57K vs. 19K = **38K extra per ball**

### Return Lanes (The Bonus Engine)

**Why they matter:** Lit lanes award triple bonus advances (3 points vs. 1). Building bonus via safe shots is core strategy.

**How to execute:**
- Identify which return lane is lit (usually alternates or rotates)
- Feed ball to left or right flipper
- Execute smooth controlled shot to lit lane
- Each lit lane hit = +3 bonus points
- Repeat on different orbits to push bonus toward 15K+

**Scoring impact:** 10 lit lane hits × 3 = 30 bonus points toward max

## 🏆 Extra Ball & Replay Opportunities

| Feature | Condition | Rarity |
|---------|-----------|--------|
| **WOW award** | Complete all six drop targets (3+3) | Once per ball initially; repeatable after reset |
| **Replay score** | Match or exceed previous high score | Game dependent (check machine settings) |

**Strategy:** The WOW is your main extra ball path. Completing both drop target banks once per ball unlocks it. Subsequent completions award 100K specials (on some machines) instead. Prioritize the WOW on Ball 2 or 3 to extend play.

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** If your machine allows repeated specials and WOW awards, focus on completing both banks multiple times per ball for stacked 100K awards. <kcite ref="2"/>

## 🎯 Bonus Calculation & End-of-Ball

**Formula:** Base Bonus (1K–19K) × Multiplier (1X, 2X, or 3X)

| Scenario | Bonus Value |
|----------|------------|
| **No work, 1X mult** | 1K |
| **Light play, 2X mult** | 20K–38K |
| **Full play, 3X mult** | 40K–57K |

**Timing:** Bonus is awarded at end of ball automatically. You cannot collect or spend bonus mid-ball; it is always held until drain.

## ⚙️ Setup Recommendations

| Setting | Recommendation | Reason |
|---------|---|---|
| **Playfield pitch** | 6.0°–6.5° | Standard for 1978 SS; avoid steep slope-induced drains to center |
| **Special replay** | **Check ROM** | Game varies: some allow unlimited 100K per ball, others once per ball only |
| **Extra Ball source** | WOW completion | Primary EB path; some settings allow replay-score EB |
| **Bumper sensitivity** | Conservative | Early SS bumpers are unpredictable; avoid prolonged play there |
| **Spinner tightness** | Medium | Too loose = endless spinner; too tight = ball exits early |

## 📋 Configurable Rules Variance

Stars came in ROM variations that significantly affect strategy:

| Rule Variant | Impact |
|--------------|--------|
| **Special limit (1 vs. unlimited per ball)** | Changes whether you spam red star or focus elsewhere |
| **Multiplier progression (2X, 3X, or higher max)** | Affects drop target work priority |
| **WOW repeatability (one-time vs. repeating)** | Shapes extra ball availability mid-ball |
| **Bonus carryover** | Some machines preserve bonus into next ball; most reset it |

**Before playing:** Check your machine's attract mode or operator manual for these settings. Early ROM sets (1978–1980) often have looser rules than later house versions.

## 📈 Realistic High Score Target

Based on documented Pinside community scores and period ruleset data, a realistic competitive score for well-maintained Stars is **200K–300K** <kcite ref="1"/><kcite ref="3"/>. This assumes:

- 4 balls played
- Multiplier advanced to 2X or 3X by Ball 2
- 2–4 special shots scored
- Bonus collected at 20K–57K per ball
- 1–2 extra balls earned via WOW or replay

**Milestone Breakdown:**
- **50K:** First multiplier earned, some specials shot
- **100K:** Both multipliers working, bonus collections adding up
- **200K:** Sustained multiplier play, 3+ specials, good bonus management
- **300K:** Excellent multiplier uptime, multiple extra balls, strong bonus chains
