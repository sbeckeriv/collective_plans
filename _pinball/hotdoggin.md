# ⛷️ Hotdoggin' - Strategy Guide

*Bally, 1979/80 — Designed by Gary Gayton*

> **"Spell it twice. Nudge it into the disappearing kicker."**

Hotdoggin' is one of only 5 widebody games Bally ever produced, and the rarest of them, with a distinctive skiing theme. The game is built around two parallel systems: spelling HOTDOGGIN (twice, for a 30K bonus light) and maxing your bonus multiplier via the upper-left inline targets — then banking it all through a genuinely unique disappearing kicker. Our goal: spell HOTDOGGIN twice, hit 5X multiplier, and time your kicker collection for maximum value.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Knock down drop targets to spell H-O-T-D-O-G-G-I-N** — each letter is worth 2K in bonus
- [ ] **HOTDOGGIN letters carry over ball to ball** — but your bonus multiplier does NOT, so don't expect free multiplier progress on a fresh ball
- [ ] **Completing the full word lights the 30K bonus light** and lets you start a **second set of letters**
- [ ] **Shoot the bonus multiplier inline targets** (upper left) to push toward 5X, then go for the extra ball/special saucer to their right
- [ ] **Watch for the disappearing kicker** — when lit, nudge the ball into it (especially with a large bonus built up) to collect, since it turns off once triggered and must be relit via the K lane at the top
- [ ] **Don't sit on a big bonus too long** — get it into the kicker while it's lit, since the kicker won't stay available indefinitely

## ⛷️ Core Mechanics

### Spelling HOTDOGGIN (Twice)

- Knock down drop targets to spell out **H-O-T-D-O-G-G-I-N**
- Each letter is worth **2K** in bonus
- **Letters carry over from ball to ball** — unfinished progress isn't lost
- **Completing the full word lights the 30K bonus light** and **starts a second set of letters** to spell again

### Bonus Multiplier (Resets Each Ball)

- Shoot the **inline targets in the upper left** to advance your multiplier
- Unlike the HOTDOGGIN letters, **the multiplier does NOT carry over between balls** — you're starting fresh each time
- Push toward **5X** before chasing the **extra ball/special saucer** just to the right of the multiplier targets

> 🎯 **Pro Tip — Core Strategy:** Since the multiplier resets each ball but the letters don't, prioritize multiplier-building early in each new ball, while letting HOTDOGGIN progress accumulate naturally across the whole game.

### The Disappearing Kicker (Signature Feature)

The **right side disappearing kicker** is this game's standout mechanic:
- When **lit**, you can **nudge the ball into it** to collect your bonus
- **Especially valuable when you've built up a large bonus** — this is your premier collection point
- **Once triggered, the kicker turns off** and must be **relit by hitting the K lane at the top** again

> ⚠️ **Don't sit on a big bonus too long** — the kicker isn't permanently available, so collect promptly once it's lit and you have meaningful bonus banked.

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **Pop bumper unpredictability** (4 bumpers) | Standard late-70s bumper chaos, slightly more aggressive with 4 on this widebody | Treat as incidental scoring |
| **Nudging too aggressively for the kicker** | Risky nudges to direct the ball can tip into a tilt | Use controlled, measured nudges rather than aggressive shoves |
| **Drop target rebounds across 3 different banks** | Multiple drop target banks (4-bank, 3-bank, 3-in-line) increase overall rebound exposure | Approach each bank with deliberate, controlled shots |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Ball

1. Prioritize the inline multiplier targets first, since this resets every ball
2. Work on HOTDOGGIN letters opportunistically — progress carries over regardless of timing
3. Relight the K lane if needed to enable the kicker

### Mid Ball

1. Push your multiplier toward 5X
2. Continue building HOTDOGGIN progress, aiming for your first full completion (and the 30K light)
3. Nudge the ball into the kicker once it's lit and you have solid bonus banked

### Late Ball

1. If you've completed HOTDOGGIN once, work on the second set of letters
2. Maximize multiplier before your final kicker collection
3. Don't leave bonus uncollected at the end of the ball — use the kicker proactively

## 📋 Quick Reference: Strategic Priorities

**Early:** Build multiplier (resets each ball), work HOTDOGGIN letters (carries over).
**Mid:** Push toward 5X, relight and use the kicker when bonus is substantial.
**Late:** Complete HOTDOGGIN's second set if possible, collect via kicker before the ball ends.

**Target Outcome:** Spelling HOTDOGGIN twice for the 30K bonus light, combined with reaching 5X multiplier and reliable kicker collections, represents a strong, well-played game on this genuinely rare widebody classic.
