- **Hard Plunge**: Sends ball toward the top kick-out holes

## 🎭 Drop Targets — The 5-Bank

The 5-bank of drop targets is Cinema's centerpiece scoring feature:

| Action              | Points   | Bonus Effect       |
| ------------------- | -------- | ------------------ |
| Single target hit   | 500 pts  | +1 bonus unit      |
| All 5 targets down  | Targets reset | Bonus lit / kick-out lit |

**Drop Target Strategy:**
- Clearing the bank lights or increases kick-out hole values
- After the bank resets, repeat — each clear yields another full scoring cycle
- Approach from the flipper side with a controlled upper shot

> 💡 **Pro Tip**: The 5-bank clear triggers are Cinema's best reliable scoring burst. Target the drop bank first before farming the kick-out holes.

## 🎪 Kick-out Holes — Four Screens

Cinema's four kick-out holes are its most distinctive feature. Think of them as four separate "cinema screens" lighting up for points:

| Kick-out Condition           | Award              |
| ---------------------------- | ------------------ |
| Base (any hole)              | Points + bonus     |
| After drop target bank clear | Increased award    |
| Multiple holes lit           | Multiplied payouts |

**Kick-out Strategy:**
- With four holes scattered across the playfield, the ball is frequently captured and returned
- After a bank clear, identify which holes are now lit for higher values
- Kick-out ejections tend to feed back toward the drop targets — use this loop

> 💡 **Pro Tip**: The kick-out holes create a natural circuit: hit drops → clear bank → holes light → collect holes → ball feeds drops again. This loop is Cinema's core scoring engine.
