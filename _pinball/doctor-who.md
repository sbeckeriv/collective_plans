# 📞 Doctor Who - Strategy Guide

*Bally, 1992 — Designed by Barry Oursler & Bill Pfutzenreuter*

> **"Exterminate the Daleks. Maximize Davros."**

Doctor Who lets you pick from the first seven Doctors, each granting a different playfield bonus, while the signature **Time Expander** mini-playfield raises and lowers through three levels to set up the game's Dalek-tiered multiball. Our goal: build a strong playfield multiplier via Doctor selection, then run multiball all the way to the Davros Jackpots.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Choose Doctor 6 for the playfield multiplier** — it's especially valuable during multiball, multiplying jackpots by up to 4x
- [ ] **Shoot the Loop Ramp before going for the gates** — build your Playfield X *before* starting multiball, since jackpots scale with it
- [ ] **Use the Time Expander's three levels deliberately**: Level 1 (lowest) opens the kick-out holes, Level 2 (middle) opens 5 round white standups, Level 3 (upper) opens 3 flyaway targets
- [ ] **Shoot a ball through each of the three gates** to start multiball
- [ ] **To reach the Davros Jackpots (the wizard mode), score all other multiball jackpots first, then hit all Time Expander targets**

---

## 🕰️ Core Mechanics

### Choosing Your Doctor

The game lets you select from the first seven Doctors, each tied to a different playfield scoring feature. **Doctor 6** is the standout strategic choice — it builds your **Playfield X multiplier**, which is especially valuable during multiball since it multiplies jackpots by **1.5x up to 4x**.

### The Time Expander (Three Levels)

This mini-playfield mechanically raises and lowers during play:

| Level | Position | Accessible Feature |
|---|---|---|
| **1** | Lowest | Kick-out holes |
| **2** | Middle | 5 round white standup targets |
| **3** | Upper | 3 flyaway targets |

> 🎯 **Pro Tip:** Pay attention to which level the Time Expander is currently in before committing to a shot — aiming for a feature that isn't currently accessible wastes the attempt.

### Starting Multiball

Shoot a ball through **each of the three gates** to start multiball.

### Dalek Jackpot Tiers

Jackpots scale with the rank of Dalek you're facing:

| Dalek Rank | Jackpot Value |
|---|---|
| Lowest tier | 5,000,000 |
| ... escalating ... | ... |
| **Emperor Dalek** (highest) | **50,000,000** |

> 💰 **Pro Tip — Core Strategy:** Build your Playfield X via the Loop Ramp (and Doctor 6 selection) *before* starting multiball — since jackpots are multiplied by this value, a high Playfield X turns an already-large Dalek jackpot into something far bigger.

---

## 🏆 Davros Jackpots (Wizard Mode)

The three **Davros Jackpots** during multiball represent the game's wizard-mode-equivalent achievement:

1. **Start multiball**
2. **Score all other available jackpots** first
3. **Hit all the Time Expander targets** across its three levels to enable each Davros Jackpot

This is the highest-value sequence in the entire game — treat reaching the Davros Jackpots as your primary end-game target once multiball is running well.

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **Shorter flippers** | Williams intentionally shortened the bottom flippers on this game (to shorten game time), making shots feel less forgiving than standard-size flippers | Adjust your timing expectations — don't assume standard flipper reach |
| **Mistiming Time Expander shots** | Aiming for a feature not currently accessible at the mechanism's current level wastes the shot | Track the Time Expander's position before committing |
| **Rushing into multiball with low Playfield X** | Caps your jackpot values for the entire multiball | Build the Loop Ramp and Doctor 6 multiplier first |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Game (Ball 1)

1. Select Doctor 6 if available for the playfield multiplier benefit
2. Work the Loop Ramp to start building Playfield X
3. Get familiar with the Time Expander's three levels and their associated targets

**Target Score:** 10M–30M

### Mid Game (Ball 2)

1. Continue building Playfield X before committing to the three gates
2. Start multiball once your multiplier is solid
3. Score available jackpots as Dalek tiers escalate

**Target Score:** 30M–80M

### Late Game (Ball 3+)

1. Push through all available jackpots during multiball
2. Hit every Time Expander target across all three levels to unlock the Davros Jackpots
3. Cash in the Davros Jackpots as your biggest scoring opportunity in the game

**Target Score per ball:** 50M–150M+

## ⚙️ Setup Recommendations

| Setting | Recommendation | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| **Playfield pitch** | 6.5°–7.0° | Standard for early-90s Bally/Williams solid-state machines |
| **Time Expander mechanism** | Verify smooth raise/lower action across all 3 levels | Central to the entire target-accessibility system |
| **Flipper timing practice** | Account for the shorter-than-standard flippers | Shots that feel automatic on other machines require adjustment here |

---

## 📋 Quick Reference: Strategic Priorities

**Ball 1:** Pick Doctor 6, build Playfield X via Loop Ramp, learn the Time Expander levels.
**Ball 2:** Keep building multiplier, start multiball through the three gates, score Dalek jackpots.
**Ball 3+:** Push through all jackpots, hit every Time Expander target, cash the Davros Jackpots.

**Target High-Score Ballpark:** **80–200 million points** is a strong, competitive game — reaching the Davros Jackpots with a high Playfield X multiplier marks true mastery of this Whovian classic.
