# 🕷️ Scared Stiff - Strategy Guide

*Bally, 1996 — Designed by Dennis Nordman & Mark Weyna*

> **"Elvira's back. The spider's spinning. Time to get scared."**

Scared Stiff is a Bally WPC machine starring Elvira with a horror-comedy theme, a mechanical spider in the backbox that spins when the ball drops into the playfield trap (and stops when you press a flipper to award a feature), 3 pop bumpers, and a surprisingly deep ruleset built around the Crate multiball system and Elvira's Scared Stiff bonuses. Our goal: **200M–500M** through the Crate multiball, Stiff-O-Meter maximization, and the Scared Stiff sequence completion.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Shoot the left ramp** to advance the Stiff-O-Meter (your primary progression)
- [ ] **Hit the Crate targets** (right side) to unlock and start Crate Multiball
- [ ] **When the ball drops into the playfield trap**, press a flipper to stop the backbox spider and collect the indicated feature
- [ ] **Complete the Scared Stiff modes** by shooting the right orbit
- [ ] **Collect "Dead Heads"** (skull inserts) via pop bumpers and left orbit
- [ ] **Use the Stiff-O-Meter** to track your progression toward wizard mode

## 👻 Core Mechanics

### Skill Shot

| Skill Shot | How | Reward |
|---|---|---|
| **Standard** | Full plunge to top lanes | Lane completion + bonus |
| **Controlled** | Short plunge to lit lane | Feature advance |

### Stiff-O-Meter (Main Progression)

The Stiff-O-Meter tracks your progress through 5 stages:
1. **Scarecrow** — shoot left ramp 2× to complete
2. **Bride of Frankenstein** — shoot left ramp + left orbit
3. **The Mummy** — left ramp + right orbit
4. **The Creature** — all major shots once
5. **Dead Head** — left ramp + Crate + pop bumper sequence

Completing each stage advances toward the wizard mode and awards escalating bonuses (25M–100M per stage).

### Backbox Spider Mechanic

When the ball falls into the playfield scoop/trap:
1. The mechanical spider in the backbox starts spinning rapidly
2. Press either flipper to **stop the spider** — it points to a prize:
   - Extra time bonus
   - Score multiplier
   - Extra ball
   - Crate advance
   - Mystery award

> 🎯 **Timing:** Watch the spider pointer, not just mash the button. Experienced players time their press to land on the high-value segments (extra ball and multiplier).

### Crate Targets

Three standup targets on the right side. Completing them:
- Advances the Crate lock sequence
- After two completions, lights the lock
- Lock 2 balls to start Crate Multiball

### Dead Heads (Skull Collectibles)

Collect Dead Heads by hitting the pop bumpers (each bumper hit = fraction of a Dead Head), left orbit completions, and spider awards. Collecting a full set of Dead Heads lights the right orbit for a major bonus.

### Right Orbit (Scared Stiff Modes)

Shooting the right orbit starts/advances a series of brief modes themed around classic horror movie types. Each mode completion awards 25M–50M.

---

## 🎳 Multiball

### Crate Multiball

**How to start:** Complete Crate targets twice (6 total hits), then lock 2 balls in the right scoop.

**Jackpot structure:**
- 3-ball multiball
- Jackpots via left ramp + right orbit
- **Super Jackpot:** Complete the Crate target set during multiball → shoot the spider scoop for the super jackpot (50M–150M)
- Spider awards still apply during multiball — press the flipper to collect while balls are running

> 🏆 **Spider timing in multiball:** A well-timed extra ball award from the spider during Crate Multiball can dramatically extend your scoring window.

---

## 🔄 Progression Systems

- **Stiff-O-Meter** gates the wizard mode — every stage completed is permanent within the game
- **Dead Head collection** resets each ball but contributes to cumulative bonus at ball end
- **Mode completions** (right orbit horror modes) increase base jackpot values

---

## 🧙 Wizard Mode

### Elvira's House of Horrors (Final Mode)

**Requirement:** Complete all 5 Stiff-O-Meter stages.

**Gameplay:** All shots lit at maximum values, spider award randomized for mega-jackpot, 90-second frenzy. Reaching this mode in a single game requires expert play — but completing 3–4 stages is achievable for intermediate players and yields enormous bonuses.

## 📊 Shot Analysis

| Shot | Value | Risk |
|---|---|---|
| Left ramp | Stiff-O-Meter advance | Low |
| Right orbit | Horror modes | Low |
| Crate targets (right) | Multiball qualification | Low |
| Spider scoop | Spider award | Medium — precise angle |
| Left orbit | Dead Head progress | Low |
| Pop bumpers | Dead Head progress | None |

### Dangerous Areas

- **Left outlane** — fast after left orbit; nudge gently to save
- **Spider scoop exit** — the scoop eject angle can vary; be ready for side kicks

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Ball 1
1. Complete Stiff-O-Meter stage 1 (left ramp × 2)
2. Crate target bank × 2 (light lock)
3. Spider scoop — time for extra ball

### Ball 2
1. Continue Stiff-O-Meter to stage 3
2. Lock first ball, then second → Crate Multiball
3. During multiball: left ramp + super jackpot via spider

### Ball 3
1. Complete remaining Stiff-O-Meter stages
2. Second Crate Multiball if possible
3. Collect Dead Head set for right orbit bonus

### Score Targets

| Performance | Score |
|---|---|
| Beginner | 30M–80M |
| Solid | 100M–250M |
| Strong (3+ stages + MB) | 300M–500M |
| Elite (House of Horrors) | 500M+ |

## 💡 Pro Tips

> **Watch the spider, don't mash.** The difference between an extra ball and a 5M award is pure timing. Every spider activation deserves your full attention.

> **Left ramp is your engine.** Most Stiff-O-Meter stages require it. If you can only make one shot consistently, make it the left ramp.

> **Crate targets are free progress.** They're low-risk standups that you'll naturally brush on your way to the right side — always complete the sequence when within reach.
