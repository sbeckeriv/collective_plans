# 🛹 SKATEBALL Pinball Machine - Strategy Guide

*Bally, 1980 — Early Solid-State Playfield*

> **Build your bonus, multiply your rewards, manage risk.**

Skateball is a focused, straightforward solid-state machine that rewards deliberate play. With its reversing lane mechanics and saucer progression, it demands careful shot selection and patience. Our goal: reach **2–3 million points**, a realistic competitive score on well-maintained machines.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Hit S-K-A-T-E targets twice** to build saucer value to 100K (then avoid a risky third set)
- [ ] **Build base bonus** via pop bumpers and lanes to reach 60K max
- [ ] **Advance multiplier** using top lanes or arrow targets (goal: 4x–5x)
- [ ] **Collect bonus at saucer** mid-ball without resetting multiplier
- [ ] **Avoid center drop targets** — they create dangerous rebounds
- [ ] **Manage the reverse lanes** on the left; the right outlane is positioned unusually
- [ ] **Track extra ball conditions** after third S-K-A-T-E completion

## 🎱 Core Mechanics

### S-K-A-T-E Drop Target Bank (Saucer Progression)

The 5-bank drop targets on the left are your primary objective. Each complete set advances the right saucer's value:

| Completion | Saucer Value | Notes |
|------------|--------------|-------|
| 1st set | 20K | Advance bonus, score 20K at saucer |
| 2nd set | 50K | Advance bonus, score 50K at saucer |
| 3rd set | **Special/Extra Ball** | Resets saucer; **avoid unless you intend EB** |

**Strategy:** Complete the targets twice to build saucer value, then focus on collecting at 100K via the saucer before attempting a dangerous third set <kcite ref="1"/>.

### Bonus System

**Base Bonus:** Starts at 20K. Maximum of **60K** held between balls.

**How to advance:**
- Hit pop bumpers (+2K each)
- Lane completions (top lanes, spinner area)
- Each S-K-A-T-E completion (+10K bonus advance)

**Collection:** Bonus is **held at ball end** and collected at the start of the next ball. You can collect mid-ball at the saucer **without resetting** your multiplier—critical for high scoring <kcite ref="1"/>.

### Bonus Multiplier

Starts at **1x**. Advances via:

1. **Top 3 lanes** (unusual reverse lane-change; advance to 2x, 3x, 4x, 5x)
2. **Arrow drop targets** (upper left, when green "X" light is lit; same progression)

After reaching 5x, the next completion scores **Special** <kcite ref="1"/>.

**Multiplier is held between balls**, so each ball's multiplier value compounds with your base bonus.

### Saucer & Kickout Management

The right-side saucer is your money shot. When you shoot it:
- Scores saucer value × multiplier
- Returns ball to right flipper area (or upper flipper, depending on machine wear)

**Kickout decision:** You have options for the saucer return:
- Let it roll uninterrupted down the right lane
- Hold upper flipper up (risk: risky lane reversal)
- Hold and drop technique (requires practice per machine)
- Flip away toward S-K-A-T-E targets

Experiment on your machine to find the safest recovery <kcite ref="2"/>.

### Pop Bumpers & Spinners

- **3 pop bumpers** advance bonus (+2K each) but create chaotic bounces; use them for safe bonus building only
- **Spinning target** contributes to bonus advance and general playfield activity

### Extra Ball Conditions

- **After 3rd S-K-A-T-E completion:** Shooting the saucer lights the "BALL" slot
- **Multiple EB lights** activate after several bonus advances (exact count varies by ROM)
- **Replay score** may award EB if enabled on your machine

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

### High-Risk Areas

| Shot/Area | Risk | Mitigation |
|-----------|------|-----------|
| **Center drop targets** | Severe rebounds toward outlanes and drains | Avoid entirely; focus on S-K-A-T-E instead |
| **Direct left lane shot** (reverse lane) | Unpredictable rollover; can feed to outlane | Trap on upper flipper first; execute smooth shots only |
| **Right outlane** (unusual positioning) | Easy drain if saucer return flows wrong way | Know your machine's saucer return pattern |
| **Top gap between lanes** | Ball can lodge or bounce chaotically | Keep controlled flipper shots; avoid aggressive angles |

### Safe Control Positions

- **Upper flipper cradle:** Plan next shot; preferred trap position
- **Right flipper trap:** Prepares for S-K-A-T-E or lane shots
- **Post pass:** Safely transfer between flippers on lane returns
- **Live catch:** After saucer return for immediate control

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Ball (Ball 1)

1. **Hit S-K-A-T-E targets once** — advance saucer value to 20K
2. **Build base bonus** via pop bumpers to 30K–40K
3. **Start multiplier advancement** on top lanes (goal: 2x)
4. **Play conservatively** — preserve ball for multiplier development

**Target Score:** 300K–500K

### Mid Ball (Ball 2)

1. **Complete S-K-A-T-E targets a second time** — saucer now worth 50K
2. **Advance multiplier to 3x–4x** via top lanes or arrow targets
3. **Build bonus aggressively** to 50K–60K via pop bumpers and lanes
4. **Collect bonus at saucer** once multiplier is solid (2x+)
5. **Monitor EB lights** on right side

**Target Score:** 800K–1.5M

### Late Ball (Ball 3+)

1. **Decide: Take EB or avoid 3rd S-K-A-T-E set?**
   - If bonus/multiplier is strong, avoid; preserve current state
   - If struggling, a risky 3rd set can light EB
2. **Collect bonus repeatedly** at saucer (multiplier is held)
3. **Chase any lit EB lanes** if multiple EB lights are active
4. **Maximize saucer collections** while multiplier remains high

**Target Score per ball:** 600K–1.2M

## 🎯 Shot Analysis

### S-K-A-T-E Drop Targets (Primary Objective)

**Why:** Progressive saucer value. Each set adds +10K bonus and advances saucer 20K → 50K → Special.

**How to execute:**
- Shoot from right flipper with smooth, controlled trajectory
- Both shots should be on the left side of the playfield
- Time them before attempting risky saucer shots

**Progression strategy:**
- 1st set: Safe, builds foundation
- 2nd set: Execute cleanly; saucer now worth 50K
- 3rd set: Only if you need EB; will reset saucer value

### The Saucer (Money Shot)

**Why:** Multiplied score. 20K → 50K → 100K based on S-K-A-T-E progress.

**How to execute:**
- Shoot from right flipper after building bonus/multiplier
- Account for unusual return lane positioning (varies by machine)
- Only shoot when multiplier is 2x+; otherwise, build bonus first
- Bonus is held mid-ball, so collect early if saucer is lit

### Top Lanes (Multiplier Builders)

**Why:** Advance bonus multiplier (1x → 2x → 3x → 4x → 5x).

**How to execute:**
- Upper flipper shots, right-to-left crossover
- Note: Lanes use **reverse lane change** — unexpected direction
- Safest play: multiple lane completions on Ball 2–3 when you have flipper control

### Arrow Drop Targets (Alternative Multiplier Path)

**Why:** Advance multiplier when green "X" light is lit (same progression as lanes).

**How to execute:**
- Upper-left target bank
- Only shoot when light is active
- Lower priority than S-K-A-T-E targets

### Pop Bumpers (Bonus Advance)

**Why:** Safe bonus building (+2K each), but chaotic bounces.

**How to execute:**
- Use early in ball when safety is priority over saucer preparation
- Not a primary scoring shot; supplementary only
- Avoid if you already have 50K+ bonus built

## 📝 Key Rules Notes

**Lane Reversal:** The left return lane and outlane are reversed from typical pinball machines. The right outlane is also positioned unusually. Spend time on your machine learning how saucer returns flow <kcite ref="2"/>.

**Bonus Carryover:** Unlike many early SS machines, Skateball **holds multiplier and base bonus between balls**. This is your primary advantage for high scoring.

**Mid-Ball Collection:** You can collect bonus via the saucer without resetting multiplier, allowing you to cash in mid-ball and continue building <kcite ref="1"/>.

**Tournament Modification:** Some tournament machines snip the right return lane wire to prevent infinite cradle abuse (two bonus advances with minimal flipper activity) <kcite ref="1"/>.
