# 🎸 KISS (Stern, 2015) - Strategy Guide

*Stern Pinball, 2015 — Designed by John Borg*

> **"Light the band. Burn through Rock City."**

This modern KISS, a spiritual successor to the 1979 Bally original, is built around 7 songs/modes, four band-member inserts, two distinct multiballs (Demon and Love Gun), and a three-tier wizard mode chain culminating in **Rock City**. Our goal: light all four band member inserts for KISS Army, complete the center grid for Heaven's On Fire, and solidly light every insert for the final Rock City showdown.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Work the Demon (Gene Simmons) shot** — it's a good shot to build around, since both it and other major shots enable multiballs
- [ ] **Play through the 7 songs**: Lick It Up, Hotter Than Hell, Black Diamond, Deuce, Detroit Rock City, Shout It Out Loud, and Rock and Roll All Nite
- [ ] **Collect all four band members' inserts** to unlock **KISS Army**, the first sub-wizard mode
- [ ] **Complete the center playfield grid** to unlock **Heaven's On Fire**, the second sub-wizard mode
- [ ] **Get all inserts solidly lit** (not just collected once) to access the final wizard mode, **Rock City**
- [ ] **Watch for Love Gun Multiball's captive ball mechanic** if started in the Starchild area — shooting the captive ball collects a hurry-up award and adds a ball

---

## 🎤 Core Mechanics

### Songs (7 Modes)

Play through: **Lick It Up, Hotter Than Hell, Black Diamond, Deuce, Detroit Rock City, Shout It Out Loud, Rock and Roll All Nite**. Each song-mode contributes toward your overall progression and insert-lighting.

### Demon Multiball

The **Demon (Gene Simmons) shot** is a strong, repeatable target — both it and other major shots can enable multiball starts, making it a good default shot when you're not sure what else to prioritize.

### Love Gun Multiball

- Starts as a **2-ball multiball**, with **1 ball held captive** if started in the **Starchild area**
- Shooting the **captive Starchild ball** collects a **Love Gun Hurry-up award** and **adds an additional ball into play**

---

## 🏆 Wizard Mode Chain (Three Tiers)

### 1. KISS Army (Sub-Wizard Mode)

- **Requirement:** Collect all four band members' inserts
- **Gameplay:** Multiball with most shots lit — make all lit shots, then hit the **Demon** for the Jackpot

### 2. Heaven's On Fire (Sub-Wizard Mode)

- **Requirement:** Complete the center playfield grid
- **Gameplay:** **4-ball multiball** — all major shots relight part of the grid; completing the grid lights the **Super Jackpot**

### 3. Rock City (Final Wizard Mode)

- **Requirement:** Get **all inserts solidly lit** (a higher bar than just collecting them once)
- **Gameplay:** All shots are initially lit; hitting them **raises the Super Jackpot value**, but you must make **each shot enough times to fully enable the jackpot for that specific shot**

> 🎯 **Pro Tip — Core Strategy:** Notice the escalating requirements: KISS Army needs inserts *collected*, Rock City needs them *solidly lit*. Treat insert-lighting as an ongoing, full-game project rather than something you finish quickly — Rock City specifically rewards sustained, repeated shot-making across many balls.

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **4 pop bumpers + 4 slingshots** | A genuinely bumper/slingshot-heavy layout that can produce chaotic ball movement | Treat bumper/slingshot time as incidental, not something to force |
| **Forcing Demon shots under pressure** | Repeated attempts can lead to careless misses | Take the Demon shot when comfortable, not as a forced default every time |
| **Love Gun's captive ball timing** | Mistimed captive ball shots waste the hurry-up opportunity | Approach the Starchild captive ball deliberately rather than rushing |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Game (Ball 1)

1. Start working through songs and the Demon shot
2. Begin collecting band member inserts
3. Build toward Demon and Love Gun multiball qualifications

**Target Score:** 20M–50M

### Mid Game (Ball 2)

1. Complete all four band member inserts for KISS Army
2. Work the center grid toward Heaven's On Fire
3. Run Love Gun Multiball, using the Starchild captive ball for extra balls

**Target Score:** 50M–150M

### Late Game (Ball 3+)

1. Complete the center grid for Heaven's On Fire if not already done
2. Push toward solidly lighting all inserts for Rock City
3. If Rock City is lit, work through each shot's repeat-hit requirement for the escalating Super Jackpot

**Target Score per ball:** 80M–250M+

## 📋 Quick Reference: Strategic Priorities

**Ball 1:** Play songs, work the Demon shot, start collecting band inserts.
**Ball 2:** Complete inserts for KISS Army, work the grid toward Heaven's On Fire.
**Ball 3+:** Push inserts to "solidly lit" for Rock City, grind repeat shots for the Super Jackpot.

**Target High-Score Ballpark:** **80–250 million points** is a strong, competitive game; reaching Rock City represents full mastery of this modern KISS reimagining.
