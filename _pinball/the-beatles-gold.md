# 🎸 The Beatles Beatlemania Strategy Guide
## *Path to 40 Million Points*

> **"All You Need Is Love... And Good Shot Placement"**

The Beatles Beatlemania is a 1960s-themed pinball machine by Stern (2018) celebrating The Beatles' first conquest of America. Designed by George Gomez with code by Dean Grover, this game blends classic playfield mechanics with five engaging modes, two wizard modes, and strategic multiball opportunities <kcite ref="1"/>.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Ball 1 Priority:** Collect a skill shot; build drop target bonus multiplier early
- [ ] **Activate modes:** Start with "It Won't Be Long" (pop bumpers—safest first mode)
- [ ] **Complete at least 2 modes** to light Beatlemania (Level 2+ in all 5 required)
- [ ] **Collect FAB drop target bank** to advance loop value and bonus multiplier
- [ ] **Hit right spinner** when lit to build value
- [ ] **Spell BEATLEMANIA** via upper loop or right orbit mystery awards
- [ ] **Reach Beatlemania Wizard Mode** on Ball 2/3 for massive scoring
- [ ] **Attempt Taxman Multiball** if you level all modes to 5

## 🎱 Skill Shots

The plunge offers four distinct skill shot opportunities. Earlier attempts are easier but lower-value; repeated successes increase multipliers on subsequent balls.

| Shot                        | Base Points | Advancement | Strategy                      |
| --------------------------- | ----------- | ----------- | ----------------------------- |
| **Short Plunge to Star**    | 10,000      | Safe        | Reliable opening shot         |
| **Short Plunge to Loop**    | 25,000      | Safe        | Build toward loop value       |
| **Magnet Hole via Bumper**  | 100,000     | Medium      | Requires precise aim          |
| **Left Inlane Secret Shot** | 50,000      | Risky       | Light via right flipper       |

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** The magnet hole skill shot is worth the risk—100k is substantial early in the ball. Aim for the bumper lane to trap the ball in the magnet hole. <kcite ref="2"/>

## 🎵 Five Core Modes (60-Second Timer)

Each mode has five progressive levels. Completing the mode objective advances the level; reaching Level 2+ in all five modes lights **Beatlemania Wizard Mode**.

### 1️⃣ **It Won't Be Long** — Pop Bumper Frenzy

**Objective:** Hit 10 pop bumper hits to advance level

| Level | Difficulty | Points per Hit | Strategy                           |
| ----- | ---------- | -------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| 1-2   | Easy       | 500            | Safe early-ball mode               |
| 3-4   | Moderate   | 1,000          | Bumpers harder to control          |
| 5     | Hard       | 2,000          | Multiball territory                |

> ⚠️ **Warning:** Once in bumpers, ball motion becomes chaotic. Trap early and avoid excessive flipping. <kcite ref="3"/>

**Perk:** Completing bumper modes unlocks bonus timing extensions.

---

### 2️⃣ **Drive My Car** — Loop Shots

**Objective:** Complete 1 loop orbit to advance level

| Level | Loop Value | Bonus             | Notes                 |
| ----- | ---------- | ----------------- | --------------------- |
| 1     | 25,000     | +10,000 base      | Easiest mode          |
| 2     | 50,000     | +20,000           | Orbit control matters |
| 3     | 75,000     | +30,000           | Requires consistency  |
| 4     | 100,000    | +40,000           | Skip if risky?        |
| 5     | 125,000+   | +50,000 + Taxman  | Only with Taxman goal |

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Drive My Car completion grants **2× spinner value** during mystery awards. If you spell BEATLEMANIA early, start with Drive My Car to boost spinner scoring. <kcite ref="3"/>

---

### 3️⃣ **I Should Have Known Better** — Drop Target Frenzy

**Objective:** Complete all drop target bank hits in sequence

| Phase | Target Bank | Points per Hit | Multiplier |
| ----- | ----------- | -------------- | ---------- |
| 1     | 4-bank FAB  | 7,500 × Level  | Up to 5x   |
| 2     | 4-bank FOUR | 7,500 × Level  | Up to 5x   |
| 3     | 3-bank 1964 | 7,500 × Level  | Up to 5x   |

**Bonus Advance:** Each bank completion increases bonus multiplier by **+1x** (max 7x). This bonus carries ball-to-ball! <kcite ref="2"/>

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Completing all three drop target banks lights **Extra Ball** at left inlane. This is critical for extending your game. <kcite ref="2"/>

---

### 4️⃣ **Ticket to Ride** — Spinner Accumulation

**Objective:** Collect 30 combined spins on right spinner to advance level

| Level | Spins Needed | Value per Spin | Advance Method                      |
| ----- | ------------ | -------------- | ----------------------------------- |
| 1-2   | 30 total     | 500            | Right spinner only                  |
| 3     | 30 total     | 1,000          | Orbit shots light spinner value     |
| 4     | 30 total     | 2,000          | BEATLEMANIA 2× spinners active      |
| 5     | 30 total     | 3,000+         | Required for Taxman Multiball       |

**Spinner Value Escalation:** Right spinner advances through lit inserts on standup targets. Each insert completion increases spinner value. Value resets at end-of-ball. <kcite ref="2"/>

---

### 5️⃣ **A Hard Day's Night** — All My Loving (2-Ball Multiball)

**Objective:** Collect 3 jackpots during 2-ball multiball to advance level

| Phase           | Points per Jackpot | Multiplier | Notes                                |
| --------------- | ------------------ | ---------- | ------------------------------------ |
| Start           | 25,000             | All scoring 2×  | Difficulty: High                 |
| Mystery Award   | +25,000 per extra  | +Add-a-ball    | Keeps multiball alive           |
| Completion      | 75,000 × Level     | —              | Auto-advances when 3 jackpots hit |

> ⚠️ **Multiball Risk:** Drain loss during multiball drops level back to 1. But completing it (3 jackpots) guarantees advancement. <kcite ref="3"/>

## 🎪 Drop Target Banks & Bonus System

The three drop target banks—FAB, FOUR, and 1964—are the backbone of your scoring strategy:

| Bank  | Hits to Complete | Bonus Advance | Super Jackpot Multiplier |
| ----- | ---------------- | ------------- | ------------------------ |
| FAB   | 4 targets        | +1x (max 7x)  | Yes (loop value up)      |
| FOUR  | 4 targets        | +1x (max 7x)  | Yes (loop value up)      |
| 1964  | 3 targets        | +1x (max 7x)  | Yes (loop value up)      |

**Loop Value Scaling:** Each completed bank advances "loop value" (2,000 points per lit loop insert minimum). Multiple uncollected Super Jackpots stack multipliers on the next collection. <kcite ref="2"/>

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Hold loop shots if possible. Uncollected Super Jackpots multiply the final payout. <kcite ref="2"/>

## 🎸 BEATLEMANIA Mystery Awards

Two "Light Mystery" standup targets activate the F-A-B mystery at the drop target area. Priority awards include:

| Priority | Award              | Effect                              |
| -------- | ------------------ | ----------------------------------- |
| 1        | Add-a-Ball (MB)    | Extends active multiball            |
| 2        | Add Time (Modes)   | +30 seconds on current mode         |
| 3        | 2× Spinners        | Right spinner value ×2 rest of ball |
| 4        | 5× Loops           | Loop shots ×5 value boost           |
| 5        | Collect Bonus      | Immediate end-of-ball bonus payout  |
| 6-10     | 10× Loops / Hold   | Escalating awards in competition    |

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** The 2× Spinners + 5× Loops combo is devastating if you've leveled Ticket to Ride and Drive My Car modes. <kcite ref="3"/>

## 🏆 Wizard Modes

### 1️⃣ **Beatlemania / Hard Day's Night Wizard Mode**

**Qualification:** Reach **Level 2 or higher** in all five modes

**Setup:**
- 30-second single-ball round
- All modes go offline
- Focus: Complete drop target banks to raise wizard jackpot value
- 4-ball multiball begins when time expires (or all banks hit)

| Phase | Objective             | Points per Bank | Bonus                        |
| ----- | --------------------- | --------------- | ---------------------------- |
| 1     | FAB bank              | 500,000 base    | +value for next bank         |
| 2     | FOUR bank             | 500,000 base    | +value for next bank         |
| 3     | 1964 bank             | 500,000 base    | Triggers 4-ball multiball    |
| 4     | Jackpot collection    | 1.5M - 4M+      | Depends on banks collected   |

> ⚠️ **Strategy Alert:** If you're on your fifth mode before Beatlemania and it's risky, intentionally time it out (do nothing). Beatlemania is worth far more than attempting a dangerous final mode. <kcite ref="3"/>

---

### 2️⃣ **Taxman Multiball (Final Wizard Mode)**

**Qualification:** Reach **Level 5 in all modes** (All My Loving requires 3 jackpots collected during its multiball)

**Setup:**
- 4-ball multiball
- All four single-ball modes (Drive My Car, I Should Have Known Better, Ticket to Ride, It Won't Be Long) are **ACTIVE SIMULTANEOUSLY**
- Completing standup targets adds balls (up to 6 total)

| Active System       | Multiplier | Points per Hit |
| ------------------- | ---------- | -------------- |
| Loop Mania          | 5×         | 125,000        |
| Drop Target Frenzy  | —          | 7,500 per hit  |
| Super Spinners      | 2×         | 3,000 base     |
| Super Pop Bumpers   | —          | 1,000 per hit  |

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** The standup target add-a-ball mechanic is your lifeline. Target those aggressively to extend the multiball and stack up points. <kcite ref="1"/>

**Taxman Scoring Potential:** With all systems active and 4+ balls in play, Taxman can reach 5M+ per cycle. This is your highest-scoring phase. <kcite ref="3"/>

## 🎰 Multiballs

### **All My Loving (2-Ball Multiball)**

**Start:** Automatically qualifies on Ball 3 if not played earlier. Can be manually started by spelling ALL MY LOVING via mystery awards or mode completion.

**Mechanics:**
- All scoring is **doubled** during multiball
- Mystery awards grant add-a-balls
- Complete 3 jackpots to advance corresponding mode level

**Scoring:** 25k base per jackpot, doubled = 50k minimum. Add mysteries for extra value. <kcite ref="2"/>

## 📈 Stacking & Combos

### ✅ What Stacks Well

| Combination                      | Benefit                                       |
| -------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------- |
| **Drive My Car + 2× Spinners**   | Spinner value becomes 1-6k per spin           |
| **5× Loops Mystery + Loop Shot** | 125k+ per orbit (massive!)                    |
| **High Bonus Mult + Taxman**     | Bonus payout at end = 250k-500k               |
| **All Modes Level 3+ at Wizard** | Jackpot escalation during Beatlemania         |
| **Multiple EB + Long Game**      | Extend to Ball 5+ for Taxman attempts         |

### ❌ What NOT to Stack

| Avoid                            | Reason                                        |
| -------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------- |
| **Bumper mode + low trapping**   | Ball becomes uncontrollable                   |
| **Mode attempt + risky 5th mode**| Drain risk outweighs reward pre-Beatlemania  |
| **Multiball in late game**       | Risk of losing extra balls (configurable)     |

### ⏰ Optimal Progression

1. **Build drop target bonus** before attempting risky modes
2. **Complete 2-3 safe modes first** (It Won't Be Long, Drive My Car, Ticket to Ride)
3. **Target Beatlemania qualification** (Level 2+ all modes)
4. **Beatlemania → dump remaining modes** to Level 5 if confidence is high
5. **Taxman Multiball** is the endgame goal: requires all 5 at Level 5 <kcite ref="3"/>

## 🏅 Path to 40 Million Points

Based on verified high scores reaching **40.9 million** (Pinside record) <kcite ref="4"/>, here's the proven path:

### Phase 1: Foundation (Target: 5M)

2. ✅ Progress **Ticket to Ride** to Level 3

1. ✅ Complete Beatlemania 4-ball multiball (1.5-4M payout)
2. ✅ Build remaining modes to Level 4
3. ✅ Land drop target banks during Beatlemania
4. ✅ Extend game with extra balls

### Phase 4: Final Push (Target: 40M+)
1. ✅ Attempt **Taxman Multiball** qualification (all modes Level 5)
2. ✅ Hit standup targets to add balls (aim for 5+ balls)
3. ✅ Maximize end-of-ball bonus (7× multiplier = 500k+)

## 🥚 Easter Eggs & Hidden Features

### "Classic Mode" Alternative
Hidden within settings is **Classic Mode**, which converts the game to resemble the 1979 Stern machine **Sea Witch**:
- All modern features disabled
- No multiballs
- Separate high score table
- Pure mechanical scoring

Access: Game settings menu <kcite ref="1"/>

### Commentary & Callouts
- **Ed Sullivan introductions** featured at ball start
- **Cousin Brucie** (legendary disc jockey from Beatles' Shea Stadium 1965 intro) provides custom commentary throughout <kcite ref="5"/>
- Nine Beatles songs from the touring era: "A Hard Day's Night," "Can't Buy Me Love," "Help!," "Taxman," and others <kcite ref="5"/>
