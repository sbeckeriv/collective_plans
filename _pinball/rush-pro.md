# 🎸 Rush (Pro) - Strategy Guide

*Stern Pinball, 2022 — Designed by John Borg*

> **"Pick your song. Fly by night. Score the jackpot."**

Rush (Stern, 2022) lets you choose from 16 Rush songs at the start of every ball, each tied to specific shot sequences and escalating jackpot values. The Pro edition features a bidirectional thrust magnet, a subway ball-view window, dual right inlanes, and 3 flippers. Like AC/DC, song selection is your primary strategic lever. Our goal: **300M–800M** by picking songs that match your shot strengths and stacking YYZ Multiball with active song modes.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Pick a song you can shoot** — each song has a key shot; choose based on which shot you're most accurate with
- [ ] **Complete the song's key shot** repeatedly to build the jackpot value
- [ ] **Hit the left scoop** to advance the album (career progression)
- [ ] **Shoot the spinner** for fast bonus and combo potential
- [ ] **Lock balls via the right scoop** to start YYZ Multiball
- [ ] **Use the action button** on the lockdown bar for song-specific abilities

## 🥁 Core Mechanics

### Song Selection (Each Ball)

At the start of every ball, you select from 16 Rush songs. Each song has:
- A **key shot** (the primary scoring shot for that song)
- A **tempo multiplier** (faster songs = faster jackpot escalation, harder to execute)
- A **completion reward** (finishing the song sequence awards a major bonus + album advance)

**Beginner picks:** Tom Sawyer (left ramp), The Spirit of Radio (spinner/orbit) — high-value shots, manageable tempo

**Advanced picks:** Cygnus X-1, YYZ — intense tempo, massive jackpots when executed

### Key Shots by Song Category

| Song Type | Primary Shot | Tempo |
|---|---|---|
| Classic (Tom Sawyer, Spirit of Radio) | Left ramp / spinner | Moderate |
| Progressive (YYZ, 2112) | Right ramp / subway combo | Fast |
| Ballad (Closer to the Heart) | Left scoop / orbit | Slow — good for learning |

### Bidirectional Thrust Magnet

The center magnet fires the ball either left or right depending on the song and game state:
- Song-specific: certain songs use the magnet as a jackpot delivery mechanism
- During multiball, the magnet redirects balls for safety or jackpot assist
- The action button can sometimes control magnet direction

### Subway Ball View

The transparent window on the lower playfield lets you watch balls traveling through the subway. It's cosmetic but useful for tracking ball position during multiball chaos.

### Dual Right Inlanes

Two right inlanes (narrow and wide) each feed slightly different positions. The narrow inner inlane is safer for right flipper holds; the outer feeds directly to the right flipper tip.

### Action Button

Press during active modes for:
- Song-specific power moves (varies by song)
- Extra ball lighting (specific timing windows)
- Magnet direction select during certain modes

## 🎳 Multiball

### YYZ Multiball

**How to start:** Lock 2 balls in the right scoop (lit by completing song sequences or album advancement).

**Jackpot structure:**
- 3-ball multiball
- Jackpots via the song's key shot + left ramp combo
- **Super Jackpot:** Complete the full YYZ drum pattern sequence (hit all lit shots in order) — 50M–150M+
- Stacks with active song mode; song jackpots + YYZ jackpots apply simultaneously

> 🏆 **Best stack:** Start YYZ Multiball during Tom Sawyer or Spirit of Radio song — high-value song jackpots + YYZ jackpots = double income per shot.

### Subdivisions Frenzy (Bonus Multiball)

Triggered by completing the pop bumper sequence during specific album stages. Brief 2-ball frenzy with elevated bumper values.

## 🔄 Progression Systems

- **Album completion:** Each completed song advances the album. Complete all songs on an album = major bonus + new album unlocked with higher jackpot ceiling
- **Career mode:** Albums chain; completing all available albums triggers the Rush Tour wizard mode
- **Drop target bank:** Complete for 2112 mode qualifier — a mini-mode with elevated jackpots

## 🔀 Stacking & Combos

| Stack | Effect |
|---|---|
| Song mode + YYZ Multiball | Every key shot scores song jackpot AND multiball jackpot |
| Spinner combo | Consecutive spinner passes multiply each other; great for tempo-building |
| Action button at song climax | Triggers max jackpot window in some songs |

## 🧙 Wizard Mode

### Rush Tour (Career Wizard Mode)

**Requirement:** Complete all available albums (typically 4–5 across a single game).

**Gameplay:** All shots lit at 10× values, final YYZ mega-jackpot available. Elite play territory — reaching this requires exceptional ball control.

## 📊 Shot Analysis

| Shot | Value | Risk |
|---|---|---|
| Left ramp | Song key shot (many songs) | Low |
| Right ramp | Song key shot (progressive songs) | Low |
| Spinner | Fast base points + combo | Low |
| Left scoop | Album advance + jackpot | Low |
| Right scoop | Ball lock / jackpot | Medium |
| Pop bumpers | Frenzy progress | None |
| Drop target bank | 2112 mode qualifier | Low |

### Dangerous Areas

- **Right outlane** — dual inlane layout can be confusing; the outer inlane feeds right flipper but is narrower
- **Post-magnet redirect** — magnet can send the ball anywhere; watch where it's aimed before flipper commitment

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Ball 1
1. Select Tom Sawyer or Spirit of Radio
2. Complete song (left ramp × 4–5)
3. Advance album + lock one ball

### Ball 2
1. Select a different song type
2. Complete album page 2
3. Lock second ball → YYZ Multiball
4. During multiball: left ramp loops for stacked jackpots

### Ball 3
1. Select highest-jackpot unlocked song
2. YYZ Multiball again if possible
3. Push for album completion and Rush Tour

### Score Targets

| Performance | Score |
|---|---|
| Beginner | 50M–150M |
| Solid | 200M–500M |
| Strong (YYZ + song stack) | 500M–900M |
| Elite (Rush Tour) | 1B+ |

## 💡 Pro Tips

> **Song selection is your biggest decision each ball.** Don't just pick your favorite song — pick the song whose key shot you can reliably hit.

> **Spinner combos are addictive but don't overdo it.** Two or three passes through the spinner is great; chasing it for 10+ passes burns time you should spend advancing the album.

> **YYZ Multiball with a song running is the game's scoring peak.** Sacrifice ball 2 entirely to setting it up if needed.
