# 🌊 Creature from the Black Lagoon - Strategy Guide

*Bally, 1992 — Designed by John Trudeau*

> **"Find the Creature. Rescue the girl."**

Set at a 1950s drive-in theater rather than literally in the lagoon, this John Trudeau design centers on spelling F-I-L-M to unlock the **Rescue the Girl** wizard mode, with a holographic Creature animated beneath the playfield throughout. Our goal: complete FILM, find the Creature, rescue the girl, and collect the Super Jackpot.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Work all four FILM letters** — each comes from a different shot, so spread your attention across the playfield rather than fixating on one
- [ ] **F = complete "KISS"** (shoot the left sink 4 times, or via the Skill Shot)
- [ ] **I = shoot all four Snackbar targets**
- [ ] **L = complete "PAID"** (top 4 rollover targets, or via the Skill Shot)
- [ ] **M = shoot the Slide** (right hole) once
- [ ] **Cash in the "Move Your Car" hurry-up** by hitting the center shot — it counts down from 8M to 2M, so the earlier you hit it, the bigger the payout
- [ ] **Once FILM is lit, find the Creature, then hit the Snack Bar twice** (once to rescue the girl, once more to collect the Jackpot) — then hit the jet bumpers the required number of times to light the Super Jackpot

## 🎬 Core Mechanics

### Spelling F-I-L-M

| Letter | How to Light |
|---|---|
| **F** | Complete "KISS" — shoot the left sink **4 times**, or via the Skill Shot |
| **I** | Shoot all **4 Snackbar targets** |
| **L** | Complete "PAID" — hit the **top 4 rollover targets**, or via the Skill Shot |
| **M** | Shoot the **Slide** (right hole) once |

> 🎯 **Pro Tip:** The Skill Shot can contribute toward both F (KISS) and L (PAID) depending on which shot you land — factor this into your plunge strategy early in the ball.

### "Move Your Car" Hurry-Up

Making the **center shot** awards points and starts a **15-second timer**, with the hurry-up value counting down from **8 million to 2 million**. The faster you can return to the center shot, the more it's worth.

### Creature Multiball / Rescue Sequence

Once FILM is complete, the chase begins:
1. Make the **correct shot to find the Creature**
2. Hit the **Snack Bar** to **rescue the girl**
3. Hit the **Snack Bar again** to **collect the Jackpot**
4. Hit the **jet bumpers a specified number of times** to light the **Super Jackpot**

> 🐊 **Pro Tip:** This whole sequence is your wizard mode payoff — don't rush the Creature-finding shot under pressure, since a clean hit keeps the rescue sequence flowing toward the Super Jackpot.

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **Forcing the "Move Your Car" center shot under the 15-second timer** | A rushed shot under time pressure risks a drain | Only chase the full hurry-up value if you have a clean, controlled shot lined up |
| **Whirlpool ball funnel unpredictability** | Funnel mechanisms can return the ball at unexpected angles | Stay ready on the flippers immediately after any funnel feed |
| **Ramp shot misses while chasing FILM letters** | Missing under pressure wastes a letter-progress opportunity | Spread attempts across all four letters rather than over-focusing on one difficult shot |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Ball

1. Take the Skill Shot, aiming toward KISS or PAID progress if possible
2. Start working all four FILM letters in parallel rather than focusing on just one
3. Hit the center shot for "Move Your Car" hurry-up value when it's freshly lit (biggest payout)

### Mid Ball

1. Continue building FILM letter progress across the snack bar targets, sink, rollovers, and slide
2. Cash in additional "Move Your Car" hurry-ups whenever re-lit
3. Watch for the Skill Shot opportunities on subsequent plunges (after locks, etc.) to fast-track letters

### Late Ball

1. Once FILM is complete, commit fully to finding the Creature and running the rescue sequence
2. Hit the Snack Bar twice in sequence (rescue, then Jackpot)
3. Work the jet bumpers toward the Super Jackpot requirement
