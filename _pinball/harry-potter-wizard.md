## 📋 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Ball 1:** Plunge for skill shot → Start a Term → Complete 2-3 lessons
- [ ] **Ball 2:** Spell HARRY at inlanes → Work on Golden Trio locks → Hit Quidditch upper playfield
- [ ] **Ball 3:** Start a multiball → Focus on jackpots → Build toward wizard modes

## 🎯 Core Mechanics

### Skill Shots

The skill shot system has **three progressive levels** - completing a Level 1 shot unlocks Level 2, and so on. Press the **action button** to change the lit skill shot <kcite ref="24"/>.

| Level       | Shot Options              | Reward                                |
| ----------- | ------------------------- | ------------------------------------- |
| **Level 1** | RON targets               | Lights corresponding Golden Trio lock |
|             | HARRY lanes               | Lights corresponding Golden Trio lock |
|             | HERMIONE targets          | Lights corresponding Golden Trio lock |
| **Level 2** | Left ramp                 | Starts Quidditch match                |
|             | Center loop               | Lights Diagon Alley                   |
|             | Center ramp               | Standard award                        |
|             | Right staircase           | Standard award                        |
| **Level 3** | *Unlocks as you progress* | *Advanced awards*                     |

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** The skill shots change and open up the more you collect throughout the game - mastering these is key to fast starts <kcite ref="19"/>.

---

### School Year Modes (Lessons/Terms)

Harry, Ron, and Hermione attend Hogwarts for **6 years**. Each year consists of <kcite ref="25"/>:

- **Fall Term:** 5 lessons
- **Spring Term:** 5 lessons  
- **Final Exam:** 6 lessons (after completing 10 lessons)

**How to Start:**
1. Shoot the **time-turner right spinner** to light the scoop
2. Shoot the **scoop** to start a term
3. Use flipper buttons and action button to select desired year

**Lesson Rewards:**
- Time bonus: (seconds remaining × 25k) + (25k × consecutive lessons completed)
- Random elements awarded at 2, 3, and 4 lessons played
- **Complete 7 lessons** → Lights scoop for **Deathly Hallows** mini-wizard mode

| Year       | Theme                | Notable Lessons                                        |
| ---------- | -------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Year 1** | Sorcerer's Stone     | Privet Drive, Sorting Hat, Fluffy, Mirror of Erised    |
| **Year 2** | Chamber of Secrets   | Flying Car, Polyjuice Potion, The Basilisk             |
| **Year 3** | Prisoner of Azkaban  | Knight Bus, Boggart, Time Turner                       |
| **Year 4** | Goblet of Fire       | Triwizard Tournament, Three Tasks, Duel                |
| **Year 5** | Order of the Phoenix | Dumbledore's Army, Occlumency, Department of Mysteries |
| **Year 6** | Half-Blood Prince    | Horace Slughorn, Sectumsempra, The Cave                |

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Use **Bombtastic Bombs** from Weasley's Wizard Wheezes to instantly complete difficult lessons by pressing the action button <kcite ref="26"/>.

### Diagon Alley Shops

Shoot the **center loop** to spell D-I-A-G-O-N (3 shots initially, +1 per shop visited, max 6). Change the lit shop with the **Hagrid target** or **action button** <kcite ref="26"/>.

| Shop                           | Effect                                                                                                    |
| ------------------------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 🪄 **Ollivander's Wand Shop**   | 30-second timed mode - shoot lit double shots to boost Golden Trio multiball jackpot value                |
| 📚 **Flourish & Blotts**        | Video mode - memorize spell sequences (flipper/action button inputs). Completing awards **Diary horcrux** |
| 🦉 **Owl Post**                 | Lights random hurry-up shot starting at 25M. VUK sends ball to best flipper for the shot                  |
| 🎪 **Weasley's Wizard Wheezes** | Awards 10M + 2 Bombtastic Bombs                                                                           |
| 🏚️ **Borgin & Burkes**          | Only available after building Knockturn Alley value through multiballs. Awards accumulated jackpot        |

### Allies System

Shoot the **allies target** followed by either the **inner loop** (Dumbledore's Army) or **right orbit** (Order of the Phoenix) to join a faction and work toward **super modes** <kcite ref="52"/>.

- Both ally factions have **two super modes** each
- Collect **10 total allies** → Awards **Locket horcrux** <kcite ref="28"/>

---

### Wizard Travel & Playfield Multiplier

Complete **combos** to light the scoop to advance wizard travel <kcite ref="53"/>:

- **Odd-numbered advances:** Start timed "Geminio" **playfield multiplier**
- **Even-numbered advances:** Other awards

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Stack playfield X with multiballs for massive scoring!

---

### Potions (Cauldron)

Land the ball in the **cauldron shot** to browse potions. Shoot the **left ramp**, **center ramp**, or **right orbit** twice to collect the listed award <kcite ref="54"/>.

## 🎱 Multiballs

### Explore Hogwarts Multiball

**How to Qualify:**
1. Shoot lit **center ramp** and **right staircase ramp** shots to discover staircase paths
2. First Explore Hogwarts only requires **2 lit ramp shots**
3. Once enough paths discovered, **right ramp** starts multiball <kcite ref="27"/>

**Jackpot Structure:**
- All shots lit to send ball to 4 playfield areas for jackpots
- After scoring a jackpot, another shot required to relight that area
- **4 jackpots** → Lights roving shot (left to right) for **Super Jackpot**
- Super jackpot value increases with additional jackpots
- Super jackpot remains lit even after multiball ends if not collected

**Horcrux:** Collect Super Jackpot → Awards **Ring horcrux**

---

### Golden Trio Multiball

**How to Qualify:**
1. Complete spellouts to light wand locks at right orbit/upper loop <kcite ref="27"/>:
   - **HARRY:** Complete return lanes (inlanes)
   - **RON:** Complete left standup targets
   - **HERMIONE:** Complete targets near upper loop
2. Each spellout lights **one lock**
3. Lock **3 balls** at the wand to start multiball

**Jackpot Structure:**
- Three jackpot types: 🔵 Blue (left), 🔴 Red (center), 🟡 Yellow (right)
- Collecting any jackpot requires RON/HARRY/HERMIONE shots to relight
- **3 of any jackpot type** → Lights right orbit/upper loop for **Wand Jackpot**
- Wand Jackpot multiplied by +1x for every jackpot type scored
- **First Wand Jackpot** awards **add-a-ball**
- Process resets with more jackpots required each round

**Horcrux:** Collect full value Super Jackpot → Awards **Cup horcrux** <kcite ref="119"/>

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Visit Ollivander's before starting Golden Trio to boost jackpot values!

---

### Death Eater Multiball

**How to Qualify:**
1. Shoot **left drop target** to light Death Eater battle
2. During battle: Shoot hole behind drop target **2 times in 20 seconds** to defeat Death Eater (awards 20M)
3. **First Death Eater defeated** → Lights **Extra Ball**
4. **Second Death Eater defeated** → Lights hole to start multiball <kcite ref="27"/>

**Multiball Structure (4 Stages):**
1. **Hurry-up:** Value starts at 1M. At 800k and 400k thresholds, required Death Eater kills decrease by 1
2. **Jackpots:** Green shots score locked hurry-up value, decrease Death Eater health by 1/2
3. **Super Jackpots:** Shoot any right shot (scoop, right spinner, behind flipper) for 5M × Death Eaters killed
4. **Frenzy:** All red shots score points

**Horcrux:** Collect frenzy jackpot during Death Eater Multiball → Awards **Nagini horcrux** <kcite ref="119"/>

> ⚠️ **Warning:** All other game rules are **disabled** during Death Eater Multiball! The flippers are killed at start.

---

### Quidditch World Cup Multiball

**How to Qualify:**
1. **First left ramp shot** of game starts **Quidditch Tryouts** (one-time only)
2. Hit upper playfield shots twice to advance position: Beater → Chaser → Keeper → **Seeker** (best)
3. Hit upper playfield shots to start **Quidditch matches**
4. During matches: Shoot spinner/ramp on upper playfield to qualify goal post captive balls
5. **Alternative:** Catch the roving "Golden Snitch" (white insert) to instantly complete match
6. Complete **3 matches** (one per rival house)
7. Next **left ramp** starts Quidditch World Cup Multiball <kcite ref="27"/>

**Jackpot Structure:**
- Green shots score jackpots
- Left ramp scores larger "Bulgaria region" jackpots
- After enough jackpots (4 first time, 6 second, etc.), all shots build **Super Jackpot** value
- Super Jackpot scored with repeated upper playfield ramp shots (multiplier increases each hit)

**Horcrux:** Collect Super Jackpot → Awards **Diadem horcrux**

## 🔀 Stacking & Combos

### ✅ What Stacks Well

| Combination                       | Why It Works                                           |
| --------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Playfield X + Any Multiball**   | Geminio multiplier applies to all jackpots             |
| **Lessons + Staircase Paths**     | Lit shots during lessons send ball toward best flipper |
| **Potter Frenzy + Multiball**     | All switches scoring during multiball chaos            |
| **Ollivander's → Golden Trio MB** | Boosted jackpot values carry over                      |
| **Multiple Horcrux Progress**     | Work toward several simultaneously                     |

### ❌ What NOT to Stack

| Avoid                                  | Reason                                          |
| -------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| **Death Eater MB + Anything**          | All other rules disabled during this mode       |
| **Starting new Term during Multiball** | Wastes multiball time on single-ball objectives |
| **Borgin & Burkes without buildup**    | Knockturn value needs multiball play first      |

### Optimal Timing

1. **Build Ollivander's jackpot boost** before Golden Trio
2. **Activate Playfield X** just before starting multiball
3. **Save Potter Frenzy** for multiball situations
4. **Complete Quidditch position** early (first left ramp) for better end-game bonus

## 🏆 Wizard Modes

### The Deathly Hallows (Mini-Wizard Mode)

**Requirements:** Collect all 3 Deathly Hallows, then shoot scoop <kcite ref="29"/>

**Gameplay:**
- Three sets of colored shots: 🔵 Blue (Cloak), 🟣 Purple (Stone), 🔴 Red (Wand)
- Complete **5 shots** to finish each color group
- Collect corresponding Deathly Hallow for each completed group
- **Complete all three** → Finish mode and collect **Harry horcrux**

---

### The Battle of Hogwarts (Mini-Wizard Mode)

**Requirements:** Play all **4 different multiballs** <kcite ref="29"/>

**Gameplay:**
- Multi-stage multiball mode
- Achieve defined number of playfield feature shots (spinners, targets, ramps, jets, orbits) per stage
- 5 on-screen badges count down as you hit shots
- Complete 2/3/4/5 features per stage depending on difficulty
- **Stage 1:** Starts with multiple balls
- **Stages 2-4:** Add-a-ball/ball save adds 3, then 2, then 1 additional balls
- **Drain to single ball** → Mode ends

---

### The Boy Who Lived (Main Wizard Mode)

**Requirements:** Collect all **7 horcruxes** <kcite ref="29"/>

**How to Start:** Shoot center lane

**Gameplay:**
Destroy all 7 horcruxes **in movie order**, then defeat Voldemort:

| Horcrux  | Shots Required            |
| -------- | ------------------------- |
| 📔 Diary  | 2 orange shots            |
| 💍 Ring   | 3 purple shots            |
| 🔗 Locket | 4 pink shots              |
| 🏆 Cup    | 3 white shots (multiball) |
| 👑 Diadem | 3 blue shots              |
| ⚡ Harry  | 3 red shots               |
| 🐍 Nagini | *Details TBD*             |

**Final Battle:** Destroying the 7th horcrux begins the battle against Voldemort.

## 🎯 Shot Analysis

### ⚠️ Dangerous Shots

| Shot                          | Risk                                                                       | Mitigation                                  |
| ----------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| **Death Eater (drop target)** | "Fastest shot in all of pinball" - ball rips back at you <kcite ref="66"/> | Be ready for quick return, keep flippers up |
| **Upper Playfield**           | Fast action, multiple drain paths                                          | Focus on controlled shots                   |
| **Right Orbit**               | Can SDTM on weak hits                                                      | Full power shots only                       |
| **Center Loop**               | Rebounds unpredictably                                                     | Watch ball path carefully                   |

### ✅ Safe Shots

| Shot               | Why Safe                                      |
| ------------------ | --------------------------------------------- |
| **Left Ramp**      | Controlled return to right flipper            |
| **Center Ramp**    | Feeds back to flippers predictably            |
| **Staircase Ramp** | During lessons, sends ball to optimal flipper |
| **Inlanes**        | Build HARRY safely while ball returns         |

### Ball Control Positions

- **Cradle on left flipper:** Best for center loop, left ramp, Death Eater
- **Cradle on right flipper:** Best for right orbit, staircase, upper playfield access
- **Post pass:** Use to transfer ball between flippers safely
- **Live catch:** Essential for controlling fast returns from Death Eater shot

## 💰 Scoring Strategy: Path to 1 Billion

### Early Game (0-100M)

1. **Nail skill shots** - progress through all 3 levels
2. **Establish Quidditch position** (first left ramp) - aim for **Seeker** for 300k multiplier
3. **Start first Term** - complete 2-4 lessons for element awards
4. **Begin HARRY/RON/HERMIONE spellouts** for Golden Trio locks
5. **Hit Diagon Alley** - visit Ollivander's to boost jackpots

### Mid Game (100M-500M)

1. **Start multiballs** - prioritize Golden Trio and Explore Hogwarts
2. **Stack Playfield X** with multiballs when possible
3. **Work toward horcruxes** - focus on easier ones first (Diary, Locket)
4. **Activate Potter Frenzy** during multiball (spell HARRY 4 times) <kcite ref="88"/><kcite ref="89"/>
5. **Complete Deathly Hallows requirements** in parallel

### Late Game (500M-1B+)

1. **Battle of Hogwarts** - play all 4 multiballs to qualify
2. **The Boy Who Lived** - collect remaining horcruxes
3. **Stack everything possible** for wizard modes
4. **Maximize Quidditch Bonus** - 150 pts/Snitch, 10 pts/goal × position multiplier <kcite ref="28"/>

### Highest Value Opportunities

| Opportunity                         | Potential Value              |
| ----------------------------------- | ---------------------------- |
| **The Boy Who Lived wizard mode**   | Massive (end-game)           |
| **Battle of Hogwarts**              | High (multi-stage multiball) |
| **Stacked Multiball + Playfield X** | 50M+ per jackpot             |
| **Potter Frenzy during Multiball**  | 50-100k per switch × chaos   |
| **Borgin & Burkes (built up)**      | Accumulated multiball value  |
| **Quidditch MVP Bonus**             | Up to 40M in multiplayer     |

## 📝 Additional Information

### Extra Ball Opportunities

- **Defeat first Death Eater** (once per game) <kcite ref="28"/>
- Additional EBs available through operator settings

### Mystery Awards

### Setup Recommendations

| Setting               | Recommendation                                                                     |
| --------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Pitch**             | 6.5° standard; steeper (7°+) for faster, more challenging play                     |
| **Level**             | Ensure side-to-side level for fair outlane behavior                                |

| **Competition Mode**  | Enable for tournament play                                                         |

### Easter Eggs & Special Features

- **Potter Frenzy:** All switches score 50k-100k and play "Potter" voice clips from movies <kcite ref="28"/>
- **Quidditch MVP:** In multiplayer, highest Quidditch scorer gets 10M × number of players
- **Staircase Toy:** Ball paths are unpredictable - "one of the best toys ever in a pinball machine" <kcite ref="66"/>
- **Magnets:** Cool ball diverter effects throughout playfield
- **Music:** John Williams score intensifies with combos <kcite ref="66"/>

### Does Completing Modes Change Gameplay?

- **Yes:** Completing lessons unlocks Final Exam and advances toward Deathly Hallows
- **Yes:** Playing all 4 multiballs unlocks Battle of Hogwarts
- **Yes:** Collecting horcruxes unlocks The Boy Who Lived
- **Skill shots evolve** as you progress through the game
