# 🦈 JAWS - Strategy Guide

*Stern Pinball, 2024 — Designed by Keith Elwin*

> **"You're gonna need a bigger boat."**

JAWS, Keith Elwin's sixth Stern design, builds toward its signature multiball through chumming the water, harpooning the shark, and attaching barrels — alongside a separate Bounty Hunt system and a Beachgoer collection track. Our goal: trigger Jaws Multiball repeatedly (your 3rd start unlocks a mini-wizard mode with endless ball save), and build Quickshot and Beachgoer progress for extra balls along the way.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Chum the water first**: shoot the chum bucket captive ball, then the red shots that spawn
- [ ] **Shoot any yellow shot to load the harpoon**, attaching a barrel to the shark at the fin drop target
- [ ] **Make any flashing yellow shot to start Jaws Multiball** — your main scoring objective
- [ ] **Your 3rd Jaws Multiball start unlocks a mini-wizard mode** with the same rules as standard, but with **endless ball save** for its duration
- [ ] **Collect Beachgoers** (spawn at all 5 major shots via surrounding yellow targets) — 8 gets you an extra ball, 15 lights Search or Rescue Multiball
- [ ] **Shoot the harpoon lane when lit for Quickshots** — chain them for a growing multiplier (resets on a miss); 3 Quickshots = extra ball

## 🦈 Core Mechanics

### Jaws Multiball (Your Main Objective)

1. **Chum the water**: shoot the **chum bucket captive ball**, then the **red shots** that spawn from it
2. **Load the harpoon**: shoot **any yellow shot** to attach a barrel to the shark at the **fin drop target**
3. **Start multiball**: make **any flashing yellow shot**

> 🎯 **Pro Tip:** Your **3rd Jaws Multiball** start is special — it unlocks a **mini-wizard mode** with the same core rules as the standard mode, but adds an **endless ball save** for its full duration, making it dramatically safer to push for big scoring during that run.

### Bounty Hunt Multiball

1. Shoot the **fishing reel or right ramp** to light "Start Bounty Hunt" at the right ramp
2. **Select a bounty** using the flippers and action button
3. Complete the **shots indicated on the display** to find the shark
4. Shoot the **fishing reel spinner** to start Bounty Hunt Multiball
5. **Reel it in** by alternating between **flashing green shots** and the **center captive ball/shark**

### Beachgoers

- Spawn at **all 5 major shots** by shooting the **yellow targets surrounding them**
- **Collecting all 3 at one shot** triggers a **timed hurry-up** — its scored value is added to each subsequent shot there while lit during a mode or multiball
- **8 Beachgoers** = Extra Ball
- **15 Beachgoers** = Search or Rescue Multiball

### Quickshots

- Shoot the **harpoon lane when lit** to set up a shot to the **fin drop target**
- Each successful chain **multiplies the award further** — but the **multiplier resets on a missed Quickshot**
- **3 Quickshots** = Extra Ball
- Also the **fastest way to light Gear** for collection at the right ramp, unlocking various perks

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **Chasing Quickshot chains too aggressively** | A single miss resets your entire multiplier progress | Only attempt the next Quickshot in the chain when you have a controlled, confident shot |
| **Fin drop target / harpoon sequencing** | Multi-step setup (chum → yellow shot → flashing yellow) means a missed step wastes earlier progress | Work through the sequence deliberately rather than rushing toward multiball |
| **Bounty Hunt's alternating shot requirement** | Switching between green shots and the center captive ball/shark demands precision | Stay deliberate rather than rushing the reel-in sequence |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Game (Ball 1)

1. Start chumming the water and loading the harpoon toward your first Jaws Multiball
2. Begin collecting Beachgoers across the 5 major shots
3. Try a Quickshot or two when the harpoon lane is lit

**Target Score:** 30M–80M

### Mid Game (Ball 2)

1. Run Jaws Multiball, working toward your 2nd and 3rd starts
2. Push Beachgoers toward 8 for the extra ball
3. Build a Bounty Hunt selection and work the reel-in sequence

**Target Score:** 80M–200M

### Late Game (Ball 3+)

1. If you reach your 3rd Jaws Multiball, take full advantage of the endless ball save mini-wizard mode
2. Push Beachgoers toward 15 for Search or Rescue Multiball
3. Chain Quickshots carefully for growing multiplied value

**Target Score per ball:** 100M–400M+
