# 🎰 The Bally Game Show - Strategy Guide

*Bally, 1990 — WPC Era*

> **"Come on down! The price of this jackpot is right."**

The Bally Game Show (1990) is a WPC-era machine themed around the classic American television game show genre — featuring three mini-games (Price is Right, Wheel of Fortune, and Let's Make a Deal style mechanics), a spinning wheel, multiball, and a progressive jackpot. Our goal: **100M–300M** by completing game show mini-games and maximizing the Showcase Showdown multiball.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Shoot the left ramp** to spin the Showcase Wheel and collect prizes
- [ ] **Complete the mini-game sequences** to advance toward multiball
- [ ] **Hit the center prize area** when lit for the highest single-shot values
- [ ] **Lock balls in the game show booth** to start Showcase Showdown multiball
- [ ] **Build your prize total** through ramp and orbit sequences
- [ ] **Bonus multiplier advance** via the rollover lanes at the top

## 📺 Core Mechanics

### Skill Shot

| Skill Shot | How | Reward |
|---|---|---|
| **Wheel shot** | Time the plunge to hit the spinning wheel section | Prize award + mode advance |
| **Standard** | Full plunge | Ball to upper area |

### Showcase Wheel (Left Ramp)

Shooting the left ramp spins the Showcase Wheel display on the DMD:
- The wheel lands on a prize value (500K–5M) or a special (extra ball, multiball advance)
- Higher-speed ramp shots spin the wheel faster (longer spin = randomized result)
- The wheel can land on "Bankrupt" which removes your current accumulated prize total

### Mini-Game Sequences

Three mini-game modes cycle through the game:

**Price is Right:** Shoot the standup target at the correct value reading. Too high or too low scores less.

**Deal or No Deal:** Shoot the center scoop to take increasing offers, or hold out for the final jackpot.

**Spin the Wheel:** Wheel-based — collect the segments via orbit shots. Higher total = higher mode jackpot.

### Prize Total (Accumulating Jackpot)

Throughout the game, you accumulate a prize total:
- Each mini-game completion adds to the prize pot
- The prize pot is collected during Showcase Showdown multiball
- "Bankrupt" on the wheel resets it — risky shots to the wheel carry this danger

### Ball Lock / Showcase Showdown

Lock 2 balls in the game show booth (right scoop, lit after mini-game progression) to start multiball.

---

## 🎳 Multiball

### Showcase Showdown Multiball

**How to start:** Lock 2 balls in the game show booth (lit after completing 2 mini-games).

**Jackpot structure:**
- 3-ball multiball
- Jackpots via left ramp + center scoop
- **Showcase Jackpot:** Collect all mini-game prizes during multiball → center scoop → accumulated prize pot × 3

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Ball 1
1. Left ramp × 3 (wheel spins for prizes)
2. Mini-game 1 completion
3. Center scoop for prize collection

### Ball 2
1. Mini-game 2 completion
2. Lock first ball (right booth)
3. Second lock → Showcase Showdown

### Ball 3
1. Multiball — left ramp + scoop jackpots
2. Showcase Jackpot (prize pot × 3)
3. Post-multiball wheel farming

### Score Targets

| Performance | Score |
|---|---|
| Beginner | 20M–60M |
| Solid | 70M–150M |
| Strong (MB + Showcase) | 150M–300M |
| Elite | 300M+ |

## 💡 Pro Tips

> **Avoid Bankrupt.** Don't randomly shoot the wheel when it shows Bankrupt positioning on the current spin state. Watch the DMD and time shots when the wheel indicator is in favorable territory.

> **Prize pot is your long-game asset.** Every mini-game completion adds to it. The Showcase Jackpot at maximum prize pot is the machine's peak scoring event.
