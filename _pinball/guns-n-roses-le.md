# 🎸 Guns N' Roses - Strategy Guide

*Jersey Jack Pinball, 2020 — Designed by Eric Meunier & Slash*

> **"Welcome to the jungle. Now play every album."**

Guns N' Roses packs 21 playable songs across 4 albums (Appetite for Destruction, Use Your Illusion I & II, Chinese Democracy), each playable as its own multiball with up to 6 locked balls, plus a timed Slash Solo mode and a four-album wizard mode chain culminating in **Not In This Lifetime**. Our goal: qualify all 4 Album Modes, complete Slash Solo, and trigger Tour Multiball toward the final wizard mode.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Pick a song to play** from the 21 available, spanning all 4 studio albums
- [ ] **Lock up to 6 balls** to turn any song mode into a full multiball
- [ ] **Play 3 minutes' worth of songs from a given album** to qualify that album's **Album Mode** — you need this from all 4 albums to progress toward the final wizard mode
- [ ] **Shoot the guitar pick spinners and the spinning record** to build progress toward **Slash Solo** — first qualified after **2,000 spins**
- [ ] **Slash Solo is a timed mode** and is **required** to reach the final wizard mode — don't skip it
- [ ] **Activate Slash Solo + Tour Multiball** together to unlock **Not In This Lifetime**, the final wizard mode

---

## 🎤 Core Mechanics

### Song Modes (21 Total)

- 20 of the 21 songs come from **Appetite for Destruction**, **Use Your Illusion I**, **Use Your Illusion II**, and **Chinese Democracy**
- Each song is playable as its own mode, **and** as a multiball if you lock balls into it
- **Lock up to 6 balls** to turn any given song mode into a full multiball — the more locked, the bigger the event

### Album Modes (4 Total)

- **Qualify an Album Mode** by playing **3 minutes' worth of songs from that album**
- You need **all 4 Album Modes** completed as a prerequisite for the final wizard mode
- This means your song selection across the game should deliberately spread across all four albums rather than replaying favorites from just one

> 🎯 **Pro Tip — Core Strategy:** Since Album Modes require *cumulative time* spent on songs from that album (not a single perfect run), track which albums you're behind on and intentionally select songs from those albums on later balls.

### Slash Solo

- Built from spinning the **guitar pick spinners** and the **spinning record**
- **First qualifies after 2,000 spins**
- **Timed mode**, and **mandatory** for reaching the final wizard mode — you cannot skip this step

### Tour Multiball

A dedicated multiball mode that, combined with Slash Solo completion, unlocks the endgame.

---

## 🏆 Wizard Mode: Not In This Lifetime

**Requirements:**
1. Play a song from **each of the 4 albums** (qualifying all 4 Album Modes)
2. **Activate Slash Solo**
3. **Activate Tour Multiball**

Completing all three prerequisites unlocks **Not In This Lifetime** — the game's ultimate wizard mode, named for the band's famous 2016 reunion tour.

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **Kinetic diverters (4 total)** | Modern, mechanically active feature that can redirect the ball unexpectedly | Stay ready on the flippers any time a diverter activates |
| **Up-posts on the elevated mini-playfield** | Adds complexity to ball control on the upper section | Practice mini-playfield shots individually before relying on them under pressure |
| **Grinding spinners for Slash Solo too aggressively** | Repetitive spinner shots under fatigue can lead to a careless miss | Maintain consistent, controlled spinner technique rather than rushing toward 2,000 spins |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Game (Ball 1)

1. Pick a song and start building toward its Album Mode threshold
2. Begin spinning toward Slash Solo qualification
3. Lock a ball or two into your current song if convenient

**Target Score:** Build steadily — this is a deep, time-and-completion-driven modern ruleset

### Mid Game (Ball 2)

1. Switch songs deliberately to cover albums you haven't qualified yet
2. Continue building spinner progress toward Slash Solo's 2,000-spin threshold
3. Lock more balls for bigger song multiballs

### Late Game (Ball 3+)

1. Finish qualifying any remaining Album Modes
2. Complete Slash Solo if not already done
3. Trigger Tour Multiball and push for Not In This Lifetime
