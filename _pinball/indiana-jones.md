# 🗿 Indiana Jones - Strategy Guide

*Stern Pinball, 2008 — Designed by John Borg*

> **"It belongs in a multiball."**

This Stern release coincided with *Kingdom of the Crystal Skull*, dividing the playfield into four zones — one per film: Raiders of the Lost Ark, Temple of Doom, Last Crusade, and Crystal Skull. The headline feature is the 8-ball **Ark Multiball**, widely considered the most compelling reason to play this machine. Our goal: work all four movie modes, trigger Ark Multiball, and grind bonus multipliers (which are unlimited) toward Final Adventure.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Take the plunger skill shot** to start your game with early points
- [ ] **Work all four movie-themed zones**: Raiders of the Lost Ark, Temple of Doom, Last Crusade, and Kingdom of the Crystal Skull — each has its own dedicated playfield area and mode
- [ ] **Trigger the Ark Multiball** — an 8-ball event capping off the Raiders scene, and the single most exciting moment in the game
- [ ] **Grind bonus multipliers relentlessly** — they're unlimited, and this is described as the only legitimate scoring strategy for reaching the wizard mode
- [ ] **Set realistic expectations for Final Adventure** — reaching it requires hitting each major shot roughly **28 times**, making this a genuine grind rather than a quick combo

---

## 🗿 Core Mechanics

### Four Movie Zones

The playfield is divided into dedicated areas for each film:

| Zone | Film |
|---|---|
| Raiders area | *Raiders of the Lost Ark* |
| Temple area | *Temple of Doom* |
| Crusade area | *Last Crusade* |
| Skull area | *Kingdom of the Crystal Skull* |

Each zone has its own mode tied to scenes from that film.

### Ark Multiball (The Headline Feature)

Capping off the Raiders zone, **Ark Multiball is an 8-ball event** — a genuine standout feature, well-integrated with the film's iconic Ark-opening scene. This is widely regarded as the most compelling reason to play the machine, so prioritize working toward it whenever you're in the Raiders zone.

### Bonus Multipliers (Unlimited)

Unlike many games with a hard multiplier cap, **bonus multipliers here are unlimited**. Since the wizard mode grind is steep (see below), continuously building multiplier value is described as **the only legitimate strategy** for meaningfully progressing your score over a long game.

---

## 🏆 Final Adventure (Wizard Mode)

**Requirement:** Hit each of the major shots roughly **28 times**.

> ⚠️ **Honest assessment:** This is a genuine grind, not a quick achievement. Don't expect to reach Final Adventure through a single great ball — it requires sustained play across an entire game (or several), repeatedly hitting the same major shots.

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **Captive ball areas (3 total) with slingshots** | The upper playfield's captive ball area paired with slingshots can produce unpredictable ball behavior | Approach with controlled shots rather than full-power attempts |
| **Newton balls (2)** | Unique physics elements that can surprise players unfamiliar with their behavior | Learn their specific rebound patterns before relying on them for precision shots |
| **Stop magnet** | Can hold or release the ball in ways that affect timing | Stay aware of magnet state during relevant shot sequences |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Game (Ball 1)

1. Take the skill shot for early points
2. Start working whichever movie zone feels most accessible
3. Begin building your bonus multiplier — remember it's unlimited, so there's no rush to "max it" and move on

**Target Score:** Build steadily through multiplier accumulation

### Mid Game (Ball 2)

1. Work toward triggering Ark Multiball if you're progressing through the Raiders zone
2. Continue building multiplier value across all zones
3. Track your major-shot hit counts if you're seriously pursuing Final Adventure

### Late Game (Ball 3+)

1. Cash in Ark Multiball for the game's most exciting scoring event
2. Keep grinding bonus multiplier — it remains your most reliable path to a high score
3. If you're a dedicated player, continue accumulating major-shot hits toward the 28-per-shot Final Adventure threshold across multiple games

## 📋 Quick Reference: Strategic Priorities

**Ball 1:** Skill shot, start a movie zone, begin building unlimited bonus multiplier.
**Ball 2:** Work toward Ark Multiball, keep grinding multiplier across zones.
**Ball 3+:** Cash Ark Multiball, continue the long-term grind toward Final Adventure if pursuing it seriously.

**Target High-Score Ballpark:** A strong single-game score comes mostly from disciplined multiplier-building and triggering Ark Multiball at least once — Final Adventure itself is a multi-session grind goal rather than a single-game target for most players.
