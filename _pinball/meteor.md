# 🚀 METEOR Pinball Machine - Strategy Guide

*Stern Electronics, 1979 — Classic Early Solid-State*

> **Spinner-heavy action with escalating letter combos.**

Meteor is a foundational solid-state machine that emphasizes the spinner as its primary scoring mechanic. With its M-E-T-E-O-R letter targets, memory drop-target bank, and progressive bonus multipliers, it rewards focused play and ball control. Goal: reach **1.5–2 million points**, a realistic target on well-maintained machines.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Learn the six-letter sequence:** M-E-T-E-O-R (letters boost spinner value)
- [ ] **Master the spinner:** From the left flipper, rip it hard; spinner value scales with completed letters
- [ ] **Complete the numbered 1, 2, 3 targets:** Each hit = 1,000 bonus points (max 7,000 base)
- [ ] **Manage your bonus multiplier:** Each Meteor completion raises it (max 7×)
- [ ] **Watch for return-lane letter spots:** Left or right return lane hits automatically spot one letter
- [ ] **Avoid the left/right drain drains:** Around-the-world play on the spinner creates high drain risk

## 🎱 Core Mechanics

### M-E-T-E-O-R Drop Target Bank (6 Targets)

This is the heart of Meteor. Each target is worth:
- **E, T, O:** 200 points (or 600 when spinner is lit and high)
- **M, R:** 600 points each (premium letters)

**Spinner Value Builder:**
- Base spinner value: 200 points per spin
- **+200 points** for each E, T, or O down (×3 = 600 additional)
- **+600 points** for each M or R down (×2 = 1,200 additional)
- **All six down = 2,000 points per spin**

**Completing "METEOR":**
When all six letters are knocked down, the entire bank resets to the up position, and your **bonus multiplier increases by 1× (maximum 7×)**. This creates a natural progression: early completions lock in your multiplier foundation, later ones provide incremental increases.

### Numbered Drop Targets (1, 2, 3)

Three separate 3-bank target groups award bonus:
- **Each hit:** 1,000 bonus points
- **Maximum bonus from targets:** 7,000 points (hit 7 targets total across the banks)

This bonus is held at end-of-ball and collected at the start of the next ball, multiplied by your current multiplier.

### Return Lanes (Left & Right)

**Special feature:** Return lane hits **automatically spot one letter in the M-E-T-E-O-R bank**, advancing your spinner value by accident. This means you can complete the set without deliberately targeting each letter.

### Three Star Rollovers

Three star targets on the playfield; minimal scoring but used strategically to navigate the ball or advance bonus.

### Passive Bumpers & Pop Bumper

- **2 passive bumpers:** Unpredictable bounces; avoid them if controlling the ball
- **1 pop bumper:** Located below the letter targets; creates chaos and bounces but scores minimally

### Spinning Target

Single spinner, the primary income stream once letters are collected.

## 💰 Scoring System & Bonus

**Bonus Calculation:**
- **Base Bonus:** 1,000 points per numbered target hit (max 7,000)
- **Multiplier:** 1× → 7× (increases with each Meteor completion)
- **End-of-ball payout:** Base Bonus × Multiplier
- **Example:** 7,000 base × 5× multiplier = 35,000 bonus points

**Multiplier Carryover:**
- Multipliers of **1×–6×** carry over to the next ball
- Multiplier of **7×** resets to 1× on the next ball
- Completing Meteor again during play raises it back up

**Maximum End-of-Ball Bonus:** 147,000 points (7,000 base × 7× multiplier, achieved on subsequent balls after reaching 7× on a prior ball)

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

### High-Risk Areas

| Shot/Area | Risk | Mitigation |
|-----------|------|-----------|
| **Spinner (around-the-world)** | Ball often ricochets left or right into outlanes | Rip it hard and catch the return cleanly; use upper flipper trap |
| **Return lane to outlane misfire** | Alley pass goes too high, rolls down opposite outlane | Controlled passes only; practice the angle |
| **Pop bumper chaos** | Unpredictable bounces to center or outlanes | Avoid triggering it when you have control |
| **Center drain path** | Direct center shots feed to upper flipper, risky return** | Trap on upper flipper before re-attempting |

### Safe Control Positions

- **Right flipper cradle:** Best for planning next spinner shot
- **Upper flipper trap:** Secure position after spinner returns
- **Left flipper:** Setup for controlled left-ramp or letter-bank shots

## 🎯 Shot Strategy & Sequencing

### The Spinner (Primary Income)

**Why:** Highest per-shot value once letters are down. A 2,000-point spinner at 5× multiplier carries 10,000 points to your bonus pool on average.

**How to Execute:**
1. Trap the ball on the right flipper
2. Pass cleanly to the left flipper
3. Rip the spinner hard from the left flipper
4. Catch the return on the upper flipper
5. Repeat

### The M-E-T-E-O-R Target Bank

**Why:** Completing the set escalates your spinner value and advances your bonus multiplier.

**How to Execute:**
1. Prioritize knocking down all six letters early in Ball 1
2. Use left flipper shots toward the letter targets
3. Take advantage of accidental return-lane spots
4. Respect the around-the-world risk; don't hunt letters if your multiplier is high (risk outweighs reward)

### The Numbered Target Banks (1, 2, 3)

**Why:** Direct bonus builders, especially once you've completed Meteor.

**How to Execute:**
- After completing Meteor once, begin hitting the numbered banks to build your bonus base
- Each hit = 1,000 bonus points, so hitting all 7 targets = 7,000 bonus
- Best done on Ball 2+ when your multiplier is solid (3×+)

## 💰 Scoring Strategy by Ball

### Early Ball (Ball 1)

| Priority | Action | Why |
|----------|--------|-----|
| 1 | Complete M-E-T-E-O-R once | Lock in 1× multiplier for next ball |
| 2 | Rip spinner a few times | Establish rhythm; spin value is 200–2,000 |
| 3 | Hit numbered targets (1, 2, 3) cautiously | Build base bonus without drain risk |
| 4 | Play defense if unsure | Preserve Ball 1; losing it early limits multiplier growth |

**Target Score:** 200K–500K

### Mid Ball (Ball 2)

| Priority | Action | Why |
|----------|--------|-----|
| 1 | Complete M-E-T-E-O-R again (if safe) | Advance multiplier to 2× or 3× |
| 2 | Build bonus aggressively | Hit numbered targets and spinners |
| 3 | Rip the spinner repeatedly | Higher multiplier × 2,000-point spins = big points |
| 4 | Collect bonus at ball end | Multiplied bonus carries to next ball |

**Target Score:** 500K–1.2M

### Late Ball (Ball 3+)

| Priority | Action | Why |
|----------|--------|-----|
| 1 | Build toward 7,000 bonus if not maxed | One final bonus-builder push |
| 2 | Rip spinner constantly | 2,000 points × 4× or 5× multiplier accrues quickly in bonus |
| 3 | Complete one more Meteor if reachable | Push multiplier to 5×+ for maximum endgame impact |
| 4 | Play for safety | Protect your accumulated score |

**Target Score per ball:** 800K–2M

## 🏆 Extra Ball & Match Opportunities

**Research Note:** Silverball Mania rulesheet does not document explicit extra ball conditions for Meteor <kcite ref="1"/>. Early solid-state machines often awarded extra balls via:
- Replay score (match or exceed HSH)
- Built-in EB lights (if enabled in ROM)
- Operator settings

**Recommendation:** Check your machine's attract-mode display or manual for ROM settings. If EBs are available, they typically light via the return lanes or a dedicated EB target. If not, focus on maximizing multiplier and bonus on your four balls.

## 🎮 Ball Control Tips

1. **Cradle trapping:** Master the right-flipper cradle to plan your next spinner shot
2. **Soft passes:** Left-to-right flipper passes must be smooth to avoid outlane drains
3. **Spinner timing:** Rip the spinner when you're confident of the return path
4. **Return-lane play:** Use return lane hits to advance letters without risking the spinner
5. **Center avoidance:** Minimize pop-bumper triggering; stay on the flipper loops

## 📈 Scoring Path to 1.5–2M+

Based on published Pinside high scores (2M+ achievable) <kcite ref="2"/>, here's the progression:

### Phase 1: Foundation (Target: 300K)
1. ✅ Complete Meteor once (1× multiplier set)
2. ✅ Rip spinner 10–15 times (200–400K from spins)
3. ✅ Build bonus to 3,000–5,000 (via numbered targets)
4. ✅ Advance to Ball 2

### Phase 2: Escalation (Target: 700K–1M)
1. ✅ Complete Meteor a second time (2× multiplier)
2. ✅ Build bonus base to 6,000–7,000
3. ✅ Rip spinner 20+ times at 2,000 value
4. ✅ Collect bonus at ball end (1,000–2,000 × 2× = 2,000–4,000)

### Phase 3: Peak Multiplier (Target: 1.2M–1.6M)
1. ✅ Reach Ball 3 with 3×–5× multiplier in place
2. ✅ Build bonus to 7,000 if starting low
3. ✅ Rip spinner 25–30 times (rapid succession at max value)
4. ✅ Collect bonus at ball end (7,000 × 4× = 28,000+)

### Phase 4: Final Push (Target: 1.5M–2M+)
1. ✅ Ball 4 or extra ball (if available)
2. ✅ Complete Meteor one more time if safe (6× or 7× multiplier)
3. ✅ Maximize spinner time (last spins worth most)
4. ✅ Finish strong on bonus collection
