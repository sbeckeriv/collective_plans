# 🎡 Fun Land - Strategy Guide

*Gottlieb, 1968 — Designed by Ed Krynski*

> **"Chase the arrow. Light the number."**

Fun Land is a classic Gottlieb Wedgehead built around a boardwalk amusement-park theme, with a rotating arrow offering 20 possible positions to score Special. The catch: that arrow keeps moving every time you hit a pop bumper or duck spinner, making this a genuine game of "chase the moving target." Our goal: corral all 10 shooting-gallery rollovers, control the arrow's position, and score Special.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Understand the rotating arrow moves on its own** — striking any of the **3 pop bumpers** or the **2 duck spinners** shifts it to a new position among **20 possible spots**
- [ ] **Hit all 10 shooting-gallery rollovers/targets** to re-light one of the 10 numbers for Special score
- [ ] **The two Duck spinners light up different numbers** as you spin them — pay attention to which number each spin is currently targeting
- [ ] **Be patient about the arrow** — since bumper and spinner action constantly shifts its position, treat lining up Special as an opportunistic goal rather than something to force
- [ ] **Watch the "Last Ball in Play" light** in the backbox — a useful indicator of how much game you have left to capitalize on accumulated progress

## 🎪 Core Mechanics

### The Rotating Arrow & Special

A lighted arrow can point to any of **20 positions** on the playfield, each representing a potential **Special** score. The challenge: the arrow's position **shifts whenever the ball strikes one of the 3 pop bumpers or the 2 duck spinners** — meaning normal incidental play constantly disrupts your aim at Special.

> 🎯 **Pro Tip:** Because the arrow moves with nearly every bumper/spinner hit, don't fixate on lining up a specific Special position. Instead, stay aware of where it currently points and take the shot opportunistically when it happens to align with where your ball naturally is.

### Shooting Gallery Rollovers (10 Targets)

Hitting **all 10** shooting-gallery rollovers/targets **re-lights one of the 10 numbers** for Special scoring — a methodical, achievable goal that doesn't depend on the arrow's current chaotic position.

### Duck Spinners

The **two Duck spinners** light up **different numbers** as you spin them, triggering bonuses tied to those specific numbers. Track which spinner is currently lighting which number to plan your next shot.

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Ball

1. Start working the 10 shooting-gallery rollovers methodically — this is a controllable, achievable goal
2. Spin the Duck spinners to begin lighting their associated numbers
3. Stay aware of the arrow's current position without forcing shots toward it

### Mid Ball

1. Continue completing rollovers toward re-lighting Special numbers
2. Take Special when the arrow happens to align with a natural shot
3. Keep working both Duck spinners for their number-lighting bonuses

### Late Ball

1. Watch the "Last Ball in Play" indicator to gauge how much game remains
2. Capitalize on any remaining Special opportunities as they arise
3. Finish any incomplete shooting-gallery rollover progress if still achievable
