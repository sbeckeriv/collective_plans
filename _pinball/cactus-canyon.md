# 🤠 Cactus Canyon - Strategy Guide

*Bally, 1998 — Designed by Tom Kopera & Matt Coriale*

> **"Lock the mine. Light the showdown. Survive the stampede."**

Cactus Canyon is a Wild West showdown built around three stackable systems — Motherlode Multiball, Stampede, and the Quick Draw/Showdown chain — all layered on top of a huge Bonus X that rewards feeding the upper-left rollovers. Our goal: stack Motherlode or Stampede together with Showdown for maximum simultaneous scoring, and bank a massive end-of-ball bonus.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Take the skill shot** — Lock, Quick Draw, or 1M are all good awards to aim for
- [ ] **Feed the upper-left rollovers for Bonus X** — it gets very big, and this is one of the most valuable scoring systems in the game
- [ ] **Lock 3 balls at the mine** for Motherlode Multiball
- [ ] **Stack Gold Mine/Stampede with Showdown** by starting them while your last Quick Draw is active, then hitting the drop target to trigger Showdown on top
- [ ] **In multiball, the diverter is always closed** — meaning the left orbit always feeds the Bonus X rollovers, so multiball is your best window to build bonus
- [ ] **Don't get greedy with Polly Peril** — you can't make progress on anything else while it's active, so only start it when you're ready to commit

## ⛏️ Core Mechanics

### Motherlode Multiball

1. Hit the mine to light the lock
2. Shoot the mine again to lock a ball
3. **Lock 3 balls** to start Motherlode Multiball
4. All 5 shots are lit for **500K+ jackpots**, each adding to the Mother Lode value
5. Completing all 5 shots **relights them and adds a multiplier** to the Mother Lode
6. Shoot the mine to **score the Mother Lode** (value resets after collection)

> 💰 **Pro Tip:** Don't rush to cash the Mother Lode — completing the 5-shot cycle repeatedly before collecting stacks multipliers and can turn a modest jackpot into a massive one.

### Stampede

1. Shoot all shots **3 times** to start
2. All shots score **250K jackpots**
3. One **roving shot flashes for 500K**
4. *(Tournament ROM variant: hitting the roving shot repeatedly without touching other shots multiplies its value up to 6x, or 3M)*

### Showdown (via Quick Draw)

1. Light **Quick Draw** on an inlane by hitting the side spot targets — or light it from the skill shot or a Bounty award
2. Roll through the inlane to **start** Quick Draw
3. Shoot the **raised drop target** to complete each Quick Draw
4. Complete Quick Draw for **all 4 drop targets** to start **Showdown**
5. In Showdown, hit drop targets for **300K jackpots**
6. **Complete all 4 drop targets for an Add-A-Ball** — uncapped on standard ROM (Tournament ROM limits this to 2)

> 🔗 **Stacking trick:** You can stack Gold Mine or Stampede with Showdown by starting either while your last Quick Draw is still active, then hitting the drop target to trigger Showdown on top of the already-running multiball.

### Polly Peril

1. Starts automatically when you complete the left ramp, right ramp, or center ramp
2. You must hit the **left or right ramp 5 times**, each worth **500K**
3. Completing it adds **5x bonus**
4. ⚠️ **You cannot make progress on anything else while Polly Peril is active** — and you can simply let it time out if you'd rather not commit to it

### Bonus X (Major Scoring System)

- Complete the **two upper-left rollovers** to advance Bonus X — it scales very high
- The left orbit feeds these rollovers for your **first 3 shots**; after that, a diverter opens and the shot feeds all the way around instead
- **In multiball, the diverter is always closed**, so the left orbit *always* feeds the rollovers — making multiball your best window to build Bonus X aggressively

### Bounty Award Sequence

Cycles through: 500K → Advance rank → Light Quick Draw → +5x bonus → Light left outlane ball save → 1M bonus → Light Gunfight → Light Lock → 250K

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **Getting stuck mid-Polly Peril** | No progress on anything else is possible during this mode | Only start it deliberately, or let it time out if you'd rather keep working other objectives |
| **Forcing Quick Draw drop target shots** | Missed attempts waste the Quick Draw window | Wait for a controlled shot rather than rushing |
| **Popup "bad guy" drop targets** | Can produce unpredictable rebounds | Approach with deliberate, centered shots |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Game (Ball 1)

1. Take the skill shot, ideally toward Lock or Quick Draw
2. Start feeding the upper-left rollovers for Bonus X
3. Begin working toward your first mine lock

**Target Score:** 5M–15M

### Mid Game (Ball 2)

1. Start Motherlode or Stampede multiball
2. Stack Showdown on top by timing your last Quick Draw completion against the multiball start
3. Lean into Bonus X hard during multiball, since the diverter stays closed

**Target Score:** 15M–40M

### Late Game (Ball 3+)

1. Cycle the Mother Lode's 5-shot loop multiple times before cashing in for a big multiplied jackpot
2. Stack Showdown's uncapped Add-A-Balls to extend your ball
3. Bank your now-large Bonus X at end of ball

**Target Score per ball:** 20M–60M+

## 📋 Quick Reference: Strategic Priorities

**Ball 1:** Skill shot, start feeding Bonus X rollovers, work toward a mine lock.
**Ball 2:** Start Motherlode/Stampede, stack Showdown on top, build Bonus X during multiball.
**Ball 3+:** Cycle Mother Lode for multipliers, stack uncapped Showdown Add-A-Balls, bank Bonus X.

**Target High-Score Ballpark:** **40–80 million points** is a strong, competitive game — built mostly on disciplined Mother Lode cycling and an aggressively built Bonus X.
