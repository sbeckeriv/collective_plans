# 🐉 Paragon - Strategy Guide

*Bally, 1979 — Designed by Greg Kmiec*

> **"Four flippers. Three kick-outs. One spinning target. Pure widebody chaos."**

Paragon is a fantasy-themed Bally widebody solid-state machine with four flippers, four pop bumpers, three kick-out holes, and a rich bonus multiplier system. The extra width spreads shots across a sprawling playfield — it rewards deliberate aim over fast reflexes. Our goal: **200K–350K** (5-digit scoring era) through disciplined multiplier building and kick-out hole collections.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Complete the upper right chicane lane** on every ball — it lights key features including extra ball and bonus collect
- [ ] **Hit the 4-in-line drop targets** to advance your bonus multiplier
- [ ] **Use the upper flippers** to direct the ball toward the chicane lane and top kick-out holes
- [ ] **Collect the lit kick-out holes** for bonus payouts
- [ ] **Spin the spinner** early to build base bonus quickly
- [ ] **Avoid random pop bumper sessions** — redirect toward drop targets instead

## 🐲 Core Mechanics

### Bonus Multiplier

| Action | Effect |
|---|---|
| Complete 4-in-line drop targets | +1× bonus multiplier |
| Complete 3-bank drop targets | Advances features (not direct multiplier) |
| Upper chicane lane completion | Lights bonus collect + features |

**Maximum multiplier:** 5× on this era Bally hardware.

### Kick-Out Holes (3 total)

Three saucers around the playfield each award points and advance the bonus base:
- **Top left** — feeds back to upper flipper
- **Top right** — primary bonus collection point when lit
- **Center** — lights randomly after other events

When the top right kick-out is lit (via the chicane lane), it **collects end-of-ball bonus mid-ball** — a huge advantage.

### Upper Right Chicane Lane

The most important repeatable sequence in the game:
1. Feed the ball with the **upper right flipper**
2. Thread through the chicane (narrow lane) to the exit hole
3. Awards: lights bonus collect, advances features, increments star rollover values

### Spinning Target

The spinner on the right orbit builds bonus base rapidly — hit it whenever you're passing through that area. 5–10 spins add significant base value.

### Star Rollovers (4 total)

Four star rollovers scattered across the playfield each add small point increments. They self-advance through pop bumper hits — don't specifically chase them.

## 📊 Shot Analysis

| Shot | Value | Risk |
|---|---|---|
| Upper right chicane | Feature advance + bonus collect | Medium — tight lane |
| 4-in-line drop targets | Multiplier advance | Low |
| 3-bank drop targets | Feature advance | Low |
| Top left kick-out | Points + bonus build | Low |
| Spinner | Bonus base build | Low |
| Pop bumpers (4) | Incidental scoring | None |

### Dangerous Areas

- **Left outlane** — wide playfield means the ball carries further; flippers need a clean transfer
- **Gap between upper and lower flippers** — the four-flipper layout means missed upper shots can fall between flipper pairs if poorly timed
- **Post-drop-target drain** — resets sometimes kick the ball toward the left drain; be ready

### Ball Control on a Widebody

- Use **upper flippers** to direct the ball toward the chicane rather than letting it drift to the lower playfield
- Hold upper flipper to cradle briefly and reassess
- On the lower playfield, trap on left flipper before shooting right-side targets

## 🔄 Progression Systems

Paragon doesn't have named modes but features advance sequentially:
- Star rollovers light in sequence (bumper hits progress them)
- Standup targets light the center kick-out
- The chicane lane resets and relights features each pass

### Extra Ball

Lit by completing the upper chicane lane a second time per ball (operator-configured). Collect via the lit kick-out hole.

2. Chicane lane for bonus collect mid-ball

## 💡 Pro Tips

> **The upper flippers are your best friends.** Most Paragon players underuse them — the chicane lane is much easier from the upper flipper than trying to thread it from below.

> **Don't let the widebody intimidate you.** The extra playfield width means more time to react; use it to trap and reassess rather than flailing at every pass.

> **5× with a large base is your win condition.** One good chicane-lane bonus collect at 5× multiplier can match several balls of casual play.
