# 🏴‍☠️ Black Rose - Strategy Guide

*Bally, 1992 — Designed by John Trudeau & Brian Eddy*

> **"Loop the Whirlpool. Sink the ships."**

Black Rose is a pure score-attack pirate machine, and almost the entire game boils down to one devastatingly simple loop: the Whirlpool Ramp pays escalating millions per shot, while a separate LOCK → multiball → SINK SHIP → cannon chain provides the explosive payoff. Our goal: loop relentlessly, light multiball, and sink as many ships as possible.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Take the skill shot** into the yellow target bank — and control the ball quickly afterward, since there's minimal ball saver protection
- [ ] **Loop the Whirlpool Ramp repeatedly** — it escalates from 100K → 400K → a flat 1M per shot, and is the single best scoring strategy in the game
- [ ] **Build L-O-C-K via the inlanes/outlanes** while looping, then shoot **Pirate's Cove** to start 2-ball multiball
- [ ] **During multiball, trap one ball on the right flipper** and keep looping ramps with the left for 1M + a SINK SHIP letter per shot
- [ ] **Spell S-I-N-K S-H-I-P** to open the cannon for a ship-sinking broadside shot — worth 20M for the first ship, +10M each thereafter
- [ ] **Respect the broadside cannon shot** — it's described by veteran players as the hardest cannon shot in the game by a wide margin

---

## ⚓ Core Mechanics

### Whirlpool Ramp (Your Bread and Butter)

The top-right ramp, looped repeatedly with the **left flipper**, escalates in value:

| Shot # | Value |
|---|---|
| 1st | 100K |
| 2nd | 400K |
| 3rd+ | **1,000,000 flat, every shot** |

> 💰 **Pro Tip:** This single loop, done consistently, can carry an entire game's score on its own. When in doubt, default to looping the Whirlpool rather than chasing anything riskier.

### L-O-C-K Letters & Pirate's Cove Multiball

1. Spell **L-O-C-K** via the inlanes/outlanes (these toggle as you alternate flippers)
2. Once lit, shoot **Pirate's Cove** (far left lane) to start a **2-ball multiball**
3. **Additional LOCK completions add bonus multipliers** for subsequent multiballs

### Multiball Strategy: Trap-and-Loop

Once in multiball, **trap one ball on the right flipper** and keep looping ramps with the left flipper. Every ramp shot during multiball is worth **1M points plus a letter toward S-I-N-K S-H-I-P**.

> 🎯 **Pro Tip:** This is the exact technique top players use to "demolish" the game — control one ball passively while the other does all the scoring work via repeated ramp loops.

### SINK SHIP Letters & The Cannon

Spelling **S-I-N-K S-H-I-P** opens **Davy Jones' Locker** (the below-playfield cannon) for a **broadside shot** at the center hole, sinking a ship.

| Ship | Value |
|---|---|
| 1st ship | 20,000,000 |
| Each subsequent ship | +10,000,000 |

⚠️ **The broadside shot itself is widely considered the hardest cannon shot in the game** — don't expect to land it casually; treat every successful sink as a major accomplishment.

### Lighting the Cannon (Alternative Routes)

Besides spelling SINK SHIP, you can light the cannon by:
- Succeeding on the skill shot
- Clearing all standup target banks
- Looping the side ramp **3 times**
- Hitting all banks in a single shot

### Broadside Saucer Awards

The Broadside VUK awards semi-random prizes, including a timed **"Millions" mode**, video mini-games, and **Instant Multiball**. Collecting all **7 unique awards** lights a **15M point jackpot**.

### Double Broadside

A timed mode from the cannon that **doubles your points, up to a cap of 32M** — a strong bonus window if you trigger it at the right moment.

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Shot | Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| **Post-skill-shot control** | Minimal ball saver protection means an uncontrolled first shot can drain immediately | Get the ball under flipper control as quickly as possible after the skill shot |
| **Broadside cannon shot** | Notoriously the hardest single shot in the game | Don't force it under pressure — wait for a clean, deliberate attempt |
| **Whirlpool Ramp overshoot** | Repeated looping under fatigue can lead to a careless miss | Maintain consistent flipper timing rather than rushing each loop |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Ball (Ball 1)

1. Take the skill shot and get the ball under control immediately
2. Start looping the Whirlpool Ramp to build an early base score
3. Begin building L-O-C-K via inlane/outlane alternation

**Target Score:** 5M–15M

### Mid Ball (Ball 2)

1. Light and start Pirate's Cove multiball once LOCK is complete
2. Trap one ball, loop ramps with the other for 1M + SINK SHIP letters per shot
3. Watch for alternative cannon-lighting opportunities (target banks, side ramp loops)

**Target Score:** 15M–40M

### Late Ball (Ball 3+)

1. If SINK SHIP is spelled, attempt the broadside cannon shot for a ship sink
2. Keep grinding the Whirlpool Ramp between cannon attempts — it never stops paying
3. Chase the Broadside Saucer's 7-award jackpot if you've been hitting it consistently

**Target Score per ball:** 15M–50M+

## ⚙️ Setup Recommendations

| Setting | Recommendation | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| **Playfield pitch** | 6.5°–7.0° | Standard for early-90s Bally machines |
| **Cannon mechanism** | Verify smooth oscillation and firing | Central to the game's signature feature — a sticky cannon ruins ship-sinking attempts |
| **Ball saver timing** | Check factory settings | Skill shot has minimal protection by design; confirm this matches intended difficulty |

---

## 📋 Quick Reference: Strategic Priorities

**Ball 1:** Skill shot, start Whirlpool looping, build LOCK letters.
**Ball 2:** Light multiball, trap-and-loop for SINK SHIP letters.
**Ball 3+:** Attempt the broadside cannon shot, keep grinding Whirlpool between attempts.

**Target High-Score Ballpark:** **40–80 million points** is a strong, competitive game — almost entirely built on disciplined Whirlpool Ramp looping punctuated by successful ship sinks.
