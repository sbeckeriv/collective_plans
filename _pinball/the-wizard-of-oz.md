# 🌈 The Wizard of Oz - Strategy Guide

*Jersey Jack Pinball, 2013 — Designed by Joe Balcer*

> **"Follow the yellow brick road to the jackpot."**

The Wizard of Oz (Jersey Jack, 2013) was Jersey Jack's debut machine — a widebody with 5 flippers, 4 pop bumpers, 5 magnets, 2 VUKs, a 26-inch HD LCD backbox display, and one of the most visually stunning playfields ever made. The game follows Dorothy's journey through Oz with modes for each major film event. Our goal: **500M–2B** by completing the Yellow Brick Road (all modes), triggering Emerald City Multiball, and reaching Somewhere Over the Rainbow wizard mode.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Follow the Yellow Brick Road** — shoot the lit shots in the correct sequence (the game guides you on the LCD)
- [ ] **Collect companions** (Scarecrow, Tin Man, Cowardly Lion) via their dedicated shots
- [ ] **Hit the Wicked Witch** targets to advance her modes and weaken her
- [ ] **Lock balls in the poppy field** for Emerald City Multiball
- [ ] **Use the 5th flipper (upper)** to access the upper playfield targets
- [ ] **Exploit the 5 magnets** — they activate during specific modes to redirect or hold the ball

## 🐈 Core Mechanics

### Skill Shot

| Skill Shot | How | Reward |
|---|---|---|
| **Munchkinland** | Short plunge to top rollover cluster | Yellow Brick Road advance + companion |
| **Full plunge** | Standard | Ball to pop bumpers |

### Yellow Brick Road (Main Progression)

The Yellow Brick Road is the primary progression arc — a sequence of shots displayed on the LCD that must be made in order:
- Each lit shot represents a step on the road to Oz
- Completing segments awards mode bonuses and companion tokens
- Reaching the Emerald City gates unlocks the multiball

### Companions (3)

Three companions must be collected for maximum wizard mode access:

| Companion | Shot | Ability When Collected |
|---|---|---|
| **Scarecrow** | Left orbit | Doubles all Yellow Brick Road shot values for 1 road segment |
| **Tin Man** | Right ramp | Adds a free ball save (heart) when collected |
| **Cowardly Lion** | Center ramp | Extends all timed mode clocks by 15 seconds |

### Wicked Witch Modes

The Wicked Witch has her own progressive sequence:
- Hit the Witch target (lower right standup set) to advance Witch modes
- Each Witch mode is a timed shot sequence with escalating jackpots
- Defeating the Witch (completing all her modes) awards a massive bonus and unlocks the "Surrender Dorothy" sequence

### Five Magnets

Five magnets are embedded across the playfield:
- **Yellow Brick Road magnets (center):** Activate during specific road segments to guide the ball toward the lit shot
- **Edge magnets (4):** Prevent near-drain balls from going out during certain modes
- The 5th flipper accesses upper targets that activate the edge magnets

### Five Flippers

The 5th flipper is the upper-right mini-flipper:
- Accesses the upper playfield (Glinda's area)
- Upper targets award companion bonuses and multiplier steps
- Must be deliberately triggered with the right flipper button while the ball is in position

## 🎳 Multiball

### Emerald City Multiball (Primary)

**How to start:** Reach the Emerald City on the Yellow Brick Road (typically requires 8–12 road segments) → lock 2 balls in the poppy field (left side scoop/VUK).

**Jackpot structure:**
- 3-ball multiball with all 5 magnets active
- Yellow Brick Road shots = jackpots (carried-over road segment values × 2)
- **Super Jackpot (Over the Rainbow):** Complete all road jackpot shots during multiball → center ramp → 100M–400M+
- Companions active during multiball provide their abilities passively

### Flying Monkeys Multiball (Mini)

Triggered by the upper playfield monkey target sequence. 2-ball brief multiball with elevated upper flipper values.

- **Witch defeat** unlocks the Surrender Dorothy bonus (cumulative game-long award)
- **Companion mastery:** Using all three companions in a single multiball = Companion Bonus (100M+)

## 🧙 Wizard Mode

### Somewhere Over the Rainbow

**Requirement:** Complete all Yellow Brick Road segments AND defeat the Wicked Witch.

**Gameplay:** The LCD shows Dorothy's full Oz journey. All shots lit at maximum values. The final "Home" shot awards the Over the Rainbow jackpot — scaled by how many road segments and witch modes were completed.

## 📊 Shot Analysis

| Shot | Value | Risk |
|---|---|---|
| Yellow Brick Road shots (lit) | Road advance + jackpot | Varies by shot |
| Left orbit (Scarecrow) | Companion + road | Low |
| Right ramp (Tin Man) | Companion + road | Low |
| Center ramp (Cowardly Lion / super jackpot) | Companion + jackpot | Medium |
| Witch target (lower right) | Witch mode advance | Low |
| Upper flipper → Glinda's area | Companion bonus + multiplier | Low (positioning required) |

### Dangerous Areas

- **Widebody outlanes** — the wider playfield means a ball moving laterally carries farther before the flipper can catch it
- **Post-magnet redirect** — 5 magnets can send the ball anywhere; always be ready on both lower flippers when magnets are active
- **Upper flipper timing** — the 5th flipper only works when the ball is in the exact upper position; early or late activation misses

## 💡 Pro Tips

> **The LCD is your guide.** Unlike most pinball machines where you must memorize rules, WOZ shows you what shot is next on the road. Follow it — the LCD is the rulebook made visible.

> **Tin Man first.** The heart ball save is the most universally valuable companion ability. Collect Tin Man early so the save is available during road progression.

> **Magnets are allies, not enemies.** Once you understand they're guiding the ball toward lit shots during active road segments, trust them. Don't fight the redirect.
