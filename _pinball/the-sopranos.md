# 🍕 The Sopranos - Strategy Guide

*Stern Pinball, 2005 — Designed by George Gomez, Software by Dwight Sullivan & Lyman Sheats Jr.*

> **"Leave the gun. Take the cannoli. Collect the jackpot."**

The Sopranos (Stern, 2005) is a Whitestar machine featuring the HBO mob drama license with Tony, Christopher, Paulie, Silvio, and the Soprano family. The game revolves around collecting "hits" (whacking targets), advancing the crime family, running mob operations, and triggering Mob War Multiball. Our goal: **300M–800M** by completing hits, running operations, and stacking Mob War with an active operation.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Shoot the left ramp** to start and advance mob operations (your primary progression)
- [ ] **Hit the targets** when lit for "Whack" bonuses (standup targets)
- [ ] **Complete operations** (left ramp + right ramp sequences) for operation jackpots
- [ ] **Lock balls** in Tony's car trunk (right scoop) to start Mob War Multiball
- [ ] **Collect mystery awards** from the center saucer when lit
- [ ] **Build your crime family** by completing character hit sequences

---

## 🤌 Core Mechanics

### Skill Shot

| Skill Shot | How | Reward |
|---|---|---|
| **Operation start** | Time the plunge to lit lane | Operation advance + points |
| **Standard** | Full plunge | Ball to pops |

### Mob Operations (Main Progression)

Mob operations are timed shot sequences started via the left ramp:

| Operation | Required Shots |
|---|---|
| Numbers Racket | Left orbit × 3 |
| Protection Scheme | Right ramp × 2 + standup |
| Stolen Cars | Left ramp loop combo |
| Drug Deal | Pop bumpers + right orbit |
| Tony's Big Score | All major shots once |

Completing an operation awards an operation jackpot (20M–100M) and advances the crime family.

### "Whack" Targets (Hit Sequences)

Five standup targets represent mob hits. When a target is lit:
- Hit it to complete the "Whack" (assassination)
- Each Whack awards 5M–25M + advances the operation
- Complete all 5 targets for the "Boss of Bosses" bonus

### Tony's Car Trunk (Right Scoop — Ball Lock)

The right scoop represents Tony's car trunk. Lock 2 balls inside to start Mob War Multiball.

### Bada Bing Saucer (Mystery)

The center saucer when lit awards mystery prizes:
- Extra time on active operations
- Character bonuses
- Whack target lighting
- Multiball advance

---

## 🎳 Multiball

### Mob War Multiball

**How to start:** Complete 2 operations → lock 2 balls in Tony's trunk.

**Jackpot structure:**
- 3-ball multiball
- Left ramp = operation jackpot (operation value carried into multiball)
- **Super Jackpot (Boss Jackpot):** Complete all 5 Whack targets during multiball → right scoop → 50M–250M+
- Active operation during multiball = operation completion jackpot PLUS Mob War jackpot

---

## 🔄 Progression Systems

- **Crime family ranking:** Each operation completed raises your rank (Associate → Soldier → Capo → Underboss → Boss)
- **Boss rank:** Maximum jackpot ceiling; required for wizard mode access
- **Extra balls:** At Capo rank and by completing specific Whack sequences

---

## 🔀 Stacking & Combos

| Stack | Effect |
|---|---|
| Active operation + Mob War | Operation shots count AND score multiball jackpots |
| All 5 Whacks + Boss Jackpot | Highest single-shot in the game |
| Mystery during operation | Time extension keeps the operation running longer |

## 📊 Shot Analysis

| Shot | Value | Risk |
|---|---|---|
| Left ramp | Operation advance (primary) | Low |
| Right ramp | Operation progress | Low |
| Left orbit | Racket operation | Low |
| Standup targets (Whacks) | Hit bonuses | Low |
| Right scoop (trunk) | Ball lock / boss jackpot | Medium |
| Center saucer | Mystery | Low |

### Dangerous Areas

- **Right outlane** — fast after right orbit; no right-side kickback
- **Right scoop approach** — undershooting can deflect toward right drain

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Ball 1
1. Left ramp × 3 (Numbers Racket operation)
2. Right ramp × 2 (Protection Scheme)
3. 3 Whack targets

### Ball 2
1. Two more operations → Capo rank
2. Lock first ball (right scoop)
3. All 5 Whacks → Boss Jackpot ready

### Ball 3
1. Second lock → Mob War Multiball
2. Boss Jackpot during multiball (5 Whacks + scoop)
3. Push to Boss rank for wizard mode

### Score Targets

| Performance | Score |
|---|---|
| Beginner | 50M–150M |
| Solid | 200M–400M |
| Strong (Boss rank + MB) | 400M–800M |
| Elite | 800M+ |

---

## 💡 Pro Tips

> **Operations are your income.** The left ramp is your primary shot because most operations start there. Master it and you control the game's pacing.

> **Complete all 5 Whacks before the Boss Jackpot.** The Boss Jackpot requires all 5 Whacks done during multiball — set them up before locking balls.

> **Mob War stacked with an active operation is the peak moment.** Every shot does double duty. Set up an operation in the last few shots before triggering multiball.
