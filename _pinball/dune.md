# 🏜️ DUNE Pinball Machine - Complete Strategy Guide
## *Path to 1 Billion Points*

> **"Power Over Spice... Is Power Over Everything"**

Based on Denis Villeneuve's Academy Award-winning Dune films (2021 & 2024), this guide will help you master Arrakis and reach the coveted **1 billion point** milestone <kcite ref="61"/>.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Ball 1:** Focus on collecting resources (water during day, spice at night)
- [ ] **Activate spinners** via left inlane to spread resources
- [ ] **Collect items** from drop targets (Sand Snork first for ball save!)
- [ ] **Start a Harvester Battle** during day - destroy 2nd harvester for Extra Ball
- [ ] **Start a Prophecy Mode** at night - choose "Continue" for DUNE letter
- [ ] **Lock balls** for Desert Power Multiball at orbits
- [ ] **Spell DUNE** to reach mini-wizard modes
- [ ] **Stack multiballs** with modes for maximum scoring

## 🌅 Day & Night Cycle

The game alternates between **Day** (orange HAL lighting) and **Night** (blue HAL lighting). Each cycle lasts approximately **200 switch hits** <kcite ref="61"/>.

| Time Period | Resource Generated | Mode Available   |
| ----------- | ------------------ | ---------------- |
| **Day** ☀️   | Spice              | Harvester Battle |
| **Night** 🌙 | Water              | Prophecy Mode    |

### Time Progression Rules
- **2x Speed:** Time progresses faster once you've started a mode that cycle
- **Hold Point:** At 80% of cycle, progress pauses during active modes
- **Pending Modifiers:** Some Bene Gesserit awards activate at next cycle start

<kcite ref="61"/>

## 💧 Resources

### Water (Blue) 💧
- Generated at **night**
- Consumed during **Harvester Battles** as timer
- Each collected: **50,000 pts**
- Bonus value: **13,000 per water**
- Increases sacrifice jackpot value

### Spice (Orange) 🟠
- Generated during **day**
- Consumed at Prophecy mode cash-out
- Each collected: **50,000 pts**
- Bonus value: **22,000 per spice**
- Jackpot boost: **1,000,000 per spice** (minimum 5,000,000)

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Hit the **left inlane** when lit to activate spinners and spread resources. Also lights 2 random standup targets with the opposite resource type. <kcite ref="61"/>

## 🔧 Items (Drop Targets)

Collect items by hitting the 3 drop targets near the upper flipper. Cycle selection with **right flipper** <kcite ref="61"/>.

### Sand Snork 🐛
- **Function:** Left outlane ball save
- **Activation:** Load at yellow-lit left ramp after collecting
- **Stack Limit:** Up to 3 in inventory
- **Carryover:** Unused snork carries to next ball

### Lasgun 🔫
- **Function:** Massive damage during Harvester Battles
- **Starting Inventory:** 1 Lasgun
- **Shot Location:** Side ramp (or both ramps on EASY setting)

### Thumper 📳
- **Function:** Playfield multiplier + Worm jackpot
- **Activation:** Press **ACTION BUTTON** to deploy all at once
- **Multipliers:** 1 Thumper = 2x, 2 = 3x, 3 = 4x

#### Worm Distance Jackpot Table

| Segments | Multiplier |
| -------- | ---------- |
| 0-2      | 1x         |
| 3-4      | 2x         |
| 5-6      | 3x         |
| 7-8      | 4x         |
| 9-10     | 5x         |
| 11-12    | 6x         |
| 13-14    | 7x         |
| 15-16    | 8x         |
| 17-18    | 9x         |
| 19-22    | 20x        |
| 23       | 30x        |

**Jackpot Formula:** 500,000 × distance multiplier × (active thumpers + 1) <kcite ref="61"/>

> ⚠️ **Warning:** Cannot use Thumpers during Prophecy modes!

## ⚔️ Harvester Battle (Day Mode)

### Starting a Battle
- Available during **day cycle** only
- 6 total Harvester inserts on playfield
- One battle per day cycle

### Battle Scaling

| Battle # | Soldiers | Thopters | Harvester Health |
| -------- | -------- | -------- | ---------------- |
| 1        | 1        | 1        | 8                |
| 2        | 2        | 2        | 16               |
| 3        | 3        | 2        | 20               |
| 4        | 4        | 3        | 28               |
| 5        | 5        | 3        | 32               |
| 6+       | 6        | 4        | 40               |

<kcite ref="61"/>

### Shot Types During Battle

| Shot Color          | Target                  | Points                     |
| ------------------- | ----------------------- | -------------------------- |
| **Worm Area**       | Harvester damage        | 500,000 per hit            |
| **Red**             | Harkonnen soldiers      | 750,000 per kill + 5 water |
| **Yellow (Roving)** | Ornithopters            | 5,000,000 per kill         |
| **Blue**            | Lasgun (massive damage) | Uses 1 charge              |

### Completing the Battle
1. Destroy all defenders
2. Make final yellow shot (jump into Harvester area) OR use Lasgun
3. Harvester destruction: **7,500,000 pts**
4. Worm arrives - collect slain soldiers (blue shots) for water
5. Sacrifice jackpot: **1,000,000 per water**

> 🏆 **Extra Ball:** Destroying the **2nd Harvester** awards an Extra Ball! <kcite ref="61"/>

### Battle Retry Mechanic
If battle times out, progress is saved and resumes next day cycle.

## 🔮 Prophecy Modes (Night Modes)

### Starting Prophecy Modes
- Available during **night cycle** only
- Start at **front scoop** (or rear scoop on EASY setting)
- Change mode selection: **Right orbit** (forward) or **Left orbit** (backward)
- **Ball save:** 10 seconds at mode start

### Mode Order
**Train → Survive → Ride → Rescue → Fight → Awaken**

### Cash-Out Decision
Each mode has a **mid-point cash-out**:
- **Cash Out:** Take jackpot points, end mode (insert turns **Green**)
- **Continue:** Risk it all for **5x reward** hurry-up + permanent **Perk** (insert turns **Yellow**)

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Always choose **"Continue"** - it awards a DUNE letter AND a permanent perk! <kcite ref="61"/>

## 🎪 Side Quests

### ⚔️ Feyd Rautha's Arena Battle

**Qualification:** 4th Bene Gesserit award

**Mechanics:**
- Single-ball mode with **defense system** (starts at 10)
- Display switches to greyscale
- 3-shot combo system

| Action                | Effect                 |
| --------------------- | ---------------------- |
| Complete 3-shot combo | Progress to next phase |
| Target/bumper hit     | -1 defense, 37,000 pts |
| Drain                 | -3 defense             |
| Defense = 0           | Mode ends in defeat    |

**Scoring:**
- Base shot value: **10,000,000** (increases by 10M per shot)
- Points multiplied by (defense / 10)
- **Flawless Victory (defense = 10):** +100,000,000 bonus!

**Completion:** Awards a **DUNE letter** <kcite ref="61"/>

## 🎰 Multiballs

### 🏜️ Desert Power Multiball

**Qualification:**
1. Hit Thumper bumper (hits needed = multiballs played × 2 + 2)
2. Lock balls at left & right orbits (2 locks needed)
3. Start at Worm

> 💡 **Auto-Qualify:** If not played by Ball 3, automatically qualified! <kcite ref="61"/>

#### Build Phase (Before Start)
- Send ball into desert power field (pit)
- **Base jackpot:** 5,000,000
- Pit wall hits: +650,000 each
- Spinner hits: +100,000 each
- Thumper hits: 250,000 each
- **Duration:** ~10 seconds, then value decreases

#### Active Phase
- Jackpot shots lit orange
- Move jackpots using Worm area switch
- **Position Multipliers:** 2x, 3x, 5x (further from center = higher)

#### Super Jackpot
1. Collect 3 jackpots
2. Send ball to desert power field
3. Bash captive ball to build value (20 seconds)
4. Collect at spinner (2x if entering from front)
5. **Value:** Sum of 3 jackpots collected

**First Super Jackpot:** Awards a **DUNE letter** <kcite ref="61"/>

---

### 🗡️ Fedaykin Multiball

**Qualification:**
1. Hit Sand Trap drop target (hits needed increases each time)
2. Lock ball in Sand Trap
3. Hit Sand Trap 2 more times
4. Start by hitting Sand Trap again

**Ball Count:** 2 balls (increases with harvesters destroyed, up to 6)

#### Scoring

| Action           | Points     |
| ---------------- | ---------- |
| Ball caught      | 1,500,000  |
| Ball added       | 2,500,000  |
| Base jackpot     | 5,000,000  |
| Per ball in play | +3,000,000 |

**Add-a-Ball:** Shoot Sand Trap while lit green (up to 3 added, 12-second timeout)

**First Jackpot:** Awards a **DUNE letter** <kcite ref="61"/>

### 📦 Pain Box

**Availability:** Automatically lit at start of Ball 2 (configurable)

**Mechanics:**
1. Drain into left outlane (if Sand Snork not loaded)
2. Ball captured in apron
3. **Hold ACTION BUTTON** until proven worthy
4. New ball auto-plunges
5. Make left ramp shot to save original ball
6. Failure = ball ends

> ⚠️ **Cheat Detection:** Flipping both flippers within 120ms = instant failure! <kcite ref="61"/>

**Completion:** Awards a **DUNE letter**

## 📈 Stacking & Combos

### ✅ What Stacks Well

| Combination                       | Benefit                                      |
| --------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| **Thumpers + Multiball**          | Massive playfield multiplier during jackpots |
| **High Spice + Prophecy Mode**    | 1M per spice at cash-out                     |

| **Rescue Perk + Long Battles**    | Slower water drain = more time               |

### ❌ What NOT to Stack

| Avoid                           | Reason                                             |
| ------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| **Thumpers during Prophecy**    | Cannot be activated                                |
| **Run from Shai-Hulud + Modes** | Prevents Battles, Prophecy, Fedaykin, Desert Power |
| **Fedaykin + Prophecy**         | Sand Trap locked out during Prophecy               |

### ⏰ Optimal Timing

1. **Build resources** before starting modes
2. **Collect items** early (especially Sand Snork)
3. **Start Harvester Battle** early in day cycle
4. **Start Prophecy Mode** early in night cycle
5. **Deploy Thumpers** during multiball for maximum multiplier effect
