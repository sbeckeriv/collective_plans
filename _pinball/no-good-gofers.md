# ⛳ No Good Gofers - Strategy Guide

*Williams, 1997 — Designed by Pat Lawlor*

> **"Drive. Putt. Don't let Bud and Buzz ruin your round."**

Pat Lawlor's final Williams table casts you as a golfer fending off two course-claiming gophers, Bud and Buzz. Play through 9 holes via drive shots and the Putting Green, lock the gophers for multiball, and spin a rotating wheel for hole-completion rewards. Our goal: complete holes efficiently, secure multiball early, build your Cart Path bonus multiplier to 5x, and reach the Hole-In-One Challenge after hole 9.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Choose your skill shot**: plunge hard up the left ramp for a possible Hole-In-One, or plunge lightly to hit skill shot targets for 750K+250K each and light the Putting Green
- [ ] **Shoot the 5 "drive" shots** (left/center/right ramps, left/right orbits) to advance toward completing the current hole
- [ ] **Hit the Putting Green** (back-left) to finish the hole and collect your wheel-determined reward
- [ ] **Secure multiball early** — lock Bud and Buzz via the center ramp sequence, then finish at the Putting Green or Hole-In-One
- [ ] **Build your Cart Path bonus multiplier toward 5x** — the final Cart Path award is "Bonus 5x + Light Extra Ball"
- [ ] **Leave the captive ball alone outside of multiball** — Ripoff isn't worth much on its own, and only hit gophers when going for locks

## ⛳ Core Mechanics

### Golf Strokes (Per Hole)

- Each hole has a par determining drives needed before putting out
- **Gofer hits**: -1 stroke
- **Outlanes/Sand Trap**: +1 stroke
- **Hole-In-One**: finishes in 1 stroke (otherwise minimum 2, maximum 7)
- **Bonus by stroke count**: 250,000 (1 stroke) down to 10,000 (7 strokes), multiplied by your bonus multiplier

### Drive Shots & Putting Green

- **5 designated drives**: left/center/right ramps, left/right orbits
- Completing drives **lights the Putting Green**
- Shoot the **Putting Green** to finish the hole and collect the **wheel-determined reward**
- Shooting the Putting Green **while unlit** scores a "Water Hazard" (10–99,990 points) instead

### Gopher Locks & Multiball

1. Shoot the **center ramp** to raise **Bud**, then hit him
2. **Lock a ball** under the center ramp
3. Shoot the **center ramp** to raise **Buzz**, then hit him
4. **Lock a ball** under the **right ramp**
5. Shoot the **Putting Green or Hole-In-One** to **start multiball**

**During multiball:** the left ramp scores a **Jackpot worth 3.5M+1M** (this value is held throughout the game). Making it lights the **Super Jackpot at Hole-In-One**, worth **double the last-collected Jackpot**. The **3rd Super Jackpot** collected awards an **Extra Ball** instead.

### Wheel Awards (Hole Completion Rewards)

| Award | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Hole-In-One** | Finishes the hole as a 1-stroke score |
| **Bad Shot** | Adds 1 stroke to current hole (or starts next hole at 7) |
| **Cart Attack** | Each drive scores 250K + 100K |
| **Pop-a-Gofer** | Each Gopher hit worth 1M (20-second mode) |
| **Speed Golf** | 3-ball multiball; every inlane triggers the Driving Range ramp |
| **Ripoff** | 2-ball multiball; each ramp scores 100K + 25K/captive ball hit |
| **Q Jackpot** | Left ramp lit for 1M per hole completed |

> 🎯 **Pro Tip:** Modes like Cart Attack **hold their value throughout the game** — accumulate points in them early and cash in later for compounding value.

### Cart Path Bonus Multiplier

The **right inlane lights the left orbit for Cart Path awards**, which cycle through bonus multipliers up to **5x**. The final Cart Path award is **"Bonus 5x + Light Extra Ball."**

## 🏆 Hole-In-One Challenge (Wizard Mode)

**Requirement:** Complete the 9th hole.

A **4-ball multiball** featuring:
- Instant award of **1M per Hole-In-One you've already made**
- **Super Jets and Big Spinners**
- **Ripoff Multiball running simultaneously**
- **"Million Plus" (1M+1M) on the left ramp**
- **Hole-In-One lit for Special**

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **Left outlane** | Has a kickback, lit by completing **KICK targets** (each of 5 standups hit twice) or wheel awards — and you can **stack multiple kickback saves** | Build kickback charges proactively before risky stretches of play |
| **Right outlane** | Awards a Special when lit, but still a drain risk otherwise | Be aware of lit status before relying on it |
| **Captive ball outside multiball** | Ripoff isn't worth much on its own | Leave the captive ball alone unless you're specifically in Ripoff or Hole-In-One Challenge |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Game (Ball 1)

1. Choose your skill shot and start working drives toward your first hole
2. Begin locking Bud and Buzz toward your first multiball
3. Build kickback charges via KICK targets for safety

**Target Score:** 10M–30M

### Mid Game (Ball 2)

1. Secure multiball and ride the Jackpot → Super Jackpot loop on the left ramp and Hole-In-One
2. Build your Cart Path multiplier toward 5x
3. Bank value in held-value modes like Cart Attack for later collection

**Target Score:** 30M–80M

### Late Game (Ball 3+)

1. Push through remaining holes toward hole 9
2. During multiball phases (Ripoff, Hole-In-One Challenge), "wail on the captive ball" repeatedly — completing all 5 hits extends your ball count to the maximum
3. If hole 9 is complete, run the Hole-In-One Challenge wizard mode

**Target Score per ball:** 40M–150M+

## 📋 Quick Reference: Strategic Priorities

**Ball 1:** Skill shot, start drives toward your first hole, begin Bud/Buzz locks.
**Ball 2:** Secure multiball, ride the Jackpot/Super Jackpot loop, build Cart Path multiplier.
**Ball 3+:** Push toward hole 9, extend multiball via captive ball hits, run Hole-In-One Challenge.

**Target High-Score Ballpark:** **80–200 million points** is a strong, competitive game; reaching the Hole-In-One Challenge after completing all 9 holes represents full mastery of this beloved golf-themed classic.
