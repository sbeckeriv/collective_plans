# 🦎 GODZILLA (Pro) Pinball Machine - Complete Strategy Guide
## *Path to 1 Billion Points*

> **"Godzilla: King of the Monsters Fights the Xiliens Across Four Cities"**

Based on Stern Pinball's 2021 release designed by Keith Elwin, this guide will help you master the battles across Tokyo, New York, London, and Paris to reach the coveted **1 billion point** milestone. <kcite ref="1"/>

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Ball 1:** Focus on 2 ramps + scoop to start battles safely
- [ ] **Choose Gigan:** Safest first battle; ramp-based combo shots
- [ ] **Build ramp damage:** Progress toward Godzilla Multiball unlock
- [ ] **Collect spinners carefully:** High risk but build destruction jackpot
- [ ] **Choose Mothra ally:** Left outlane ball save protection
- [ ] **Progress through cities:** Complete Tier 1 battles to unlock Tier 2
- [ ] **Start multiballs:** Tank Attack, Bridge Attack, Godzilla MB
- [ ] **Reach Planet X:** Complete all Tier 2 battles for mini-wizard mode
- [ ] **Unlock King of the Monsters:** Complete Monster Zero, Terror, Planet X

## 🎱 Skill Shots

Godzilla offers multiple opening skill shot options, all achieved from the plunger alone. Making any skill shot awards points and adds ball save time <kcite ref="2"/>.

| Shot                                       | Points     | Method                                           |
| ------------------------------------------ | ---------- | ------------------------------------------------ |
| **Lower Switch Only (Mecha Lane)**         | 250,000    | Soft plunge hitting lower switch below bumper    |
| **Bumper Hit First**                       | 750,000    | Plunge to hit pop bumper before ramps            |
| **Behind Upper Flipper (Scoop)**           | 5,000,000  | Plunge to lane behind upper flipper, trap there |
| **Upper Loop**                             | 4,000,000  | Plunge for upper loop shot (alternates)          |
| **Left Inlane Secret**                     | 1,000,000  | Drain-like shot down left inlane                 |
| **Super Secret Scoop**                     | 10,000,000 | Behind flipper → directly into scoop             |

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** The upper loop and left inlane are safer alternates when the main scoop is hard to reach. Experiment with ball speed to find your reliable skill shot. <kcite ref="2"/>

## 🏙️ City System & Progression

Godzilla features four major cities that unlock progressively: **Tokyo** (starting city) → **New York** → **London** → **Paris**. Each city has unique Tier 1 (single-ball) and Tier 2 (multiball) battles. Completing objectives in a city provides permanent bonuses for Godzilla's damage throughout the remaining game. <kcite ref="3"/>

| City    | Tier 1 Battles Available        | Tier 2 Battles Available    |
| ------- | ------------------------------- | --------------------------- |
| Tokyo   | Gigan, Ebirah, Titanosaurus     | Tank Attack, Bridge Attack  |
| New York| King Ghidorah, Megalon + Gigan  | Saucer Attack, Bridge Attack |
| London  | Godzilla vs. Tier 1 variants     | Multiple multiball options  |
| Paris   | Tier 2 variants unlock          | Final city progression      |

## ⚔️ Tier 1 Battles (Single-Ball Modes)

**Starting a Tier 1 Battle:** Hit 2 ramps (left or right), then shoot the scoop. Select your opponent via flipper control. <kcite ref="2"/>

### Godzilla vs. Gigan (Recommended Early Choice)

**Objective:** Combo ramps to deal damage; defeat Gigan before time expires

| Shot Sequence | Points            |
| ------------- | ----------------- |
| Ramp hit      | 500,000           |
| Complete combo| 2,000,000 base    |
| Each shot     | +500,000 escalate |

**Victory:** Jackpot awarded; progresses building damage toward Godzilla Multiball <kcite ref="2"/>

> **Strategy:** Ramps are safest for ball control. Aim left ramp consistently to build combos without high spinout risk.

### Godzilla vs. Ebirah

**Objective:** Hit spinner shots, then Maser Cannon (captive ball)

| Target Type | Points    | Notes           |
| ----------- | --------- | --------------- |
| Spinner     | 200,000   | Multiple spins  |
| Maser       | 3,000,000 | Final target    |
| Victory     | 5,000,000 | Mode complete   |

**Challenge:** Spinners are risky; ball can drain easily. Save for later balls when confident.

### Godzilla vs. Titanosaurus

**Objective:** Hit blue targets (left-to-right sequence), then Maser Cannon and captive ball

**Points:** 6,000,000+ per victory; highest individual battle score <kcite ref="2"/>

**Difficulty:** More complex shot sequence than Gigan, but very rewarding.

### Godzilla vs. King Ghidorah (Unlocks Tier 2)

**Availability:** Unlocks after ~50% building damage <kcite ref="2"/>

**Objective:** Shoot building for jackpots; defeat multi-headed monster

**Reward:** Tier 2 Battles become available; access to major multiballs

## 🎰 Tier 2 Battles (Multiball Modes)

Tier 2 battles are activated after completing Tier 1 battles and reaching ~50% building damage. These 2-3 ball multiballs offer significantly higher scoring.

### Tank Attack Multiball (2-Ball)

**Start:** Light the scoop by completing Tank Attack objective in a Tier 1 battle

**Mechanics:**
- Green tanks move around the playfield
- Shoot tanks before they reach Godzilla (captive ball)
- Defeat all tanks to light Maser Cannon (target) for **Super Jackpot**
- Each tank that escapes reduces Super Jackpot value

**Scoring:**
- Per tank defeated: 3,000,000
- Super Jackpot: 8,000,000+ (fewer tank hits = higher value)

**Strategy:** Tank positions are not visible until you play; defensive play wins here. Ball saves are active during multiball start. <kcite ref="3"/>

### Bridge Attack Multiball (2-Ball)

**Start:** Captive ball when lit to destroy bridge; enough hits light right ramp for multiball start <kcite ref="3"/>

**Mechanics:**
- All lit shots score jackpots
- Flashing captive ball scores 2x jackpots
- Super Jackpot available at right ramp (timed, 20-second window)
- Add-a-ball possible during timed super jackpot phase

**Scoring:**
- Regular jackpot: 4,000,000+
- Super jackpot: 12,000,000+ (escalates)
- Each add-a-ball: +2,000,000

**Strategy:** Right ramp is the critical shot for super jackpots. Practice magna-grab magnet control here to ensure clean catches. <kcite ref="3"/>

### Saucer Attack Multiball (Escalating Balls)

**Start:** Complete Saucer Attack objective; ball count increases with earlier completions

**Mechanics:**
- Pop bumper and building shots light jackpots
- Progressive difficulty; more balls = faster action
- Can reach 6-ball multiball on high Power Up levels

**Scoring:** Highly variable based on ball count; bumpers add 500K+ per hit during multiball

## 🔋 Godzilla Multiball (3-Ball)

**Qualification:** Accumulate ~100% building damage by completing Tier 1 & 2 battles <kcite ref="2"/>

**Start:** Shoot the scoop once building is destroyed

### Godzilla Multiball Mechanics

| Phase    | Action                        | Points                |
| -------- | ----------------------------- | --------------------- |
| Jackpot  | Shoot lit shots (6 total)     | 10,000,000 base       |
| Super    | After 6 jackpots, captive ball| Sum of 6 jackpots × 2 |
| Continue | Add-a-ball possible           | +3,000,000 per add    |

**Strategy:** This is your first major multiball milestone. Prioritize hitting all 6 jackpot positions before attempting super jackpot. The building and ramps alternate as jackpot shots. <kcite ref="2"/>

> **Pro Tip:** If a ball drains during Godzilla Multiball, you lose all collected jackpots. Ball saves are NOT active here—play conservatively!

---

## ⚙️ Mechagodzilla Multiball (3-Ball)

**Qualification:** Power up Mechagodzilla by hitting right spinner repeatedly and blue target bank <kcite ref="2"/>

**Start:** Shoot right spinner again once powered

### Scoring Structure

| Action              | Points         |
| ------------------- | -------------- |
| Regular jackpot     | 8,000,000      |
| Shield break hit    | 4,000,000      |
| Super jackpot       | 16,000,000+    |
| 20-second timed SJ  | Hurry-up mode  |

**Strategy:** Spinner hits build the power; ramp shots break shields. Combine both for maximum efficiency. The super jackpot is a hurry-up, so speed matters.

## 💪 Power-Ups System (Godzilla Powerup Levels)

Each time you complete a Tier 1 battle, your Power Up level increases (max 11+). At certain levels, you unlock new mode selections and permanent bonuses. <kcite ref="3"/>

| Level | Unlock Type                    | Example Awards                             |
| ----- | ------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------ |
| 1-3   | Common bonuses                 | Jackpot multipliers, timer extensions      |
| 4     | Monster Zero unlocked          | Advanced single-ball mode                  |
| 5-7   | Multiplier progression         | 2x, 3x, 4x destruction bonuses             |
| 8     | Terror of Mechagodzilla unlock | Advanced multiball mode                    |
| 9-11  | Ultimate bonuses               | Heat Ray charge, extra ball potential      |

**Strategy:** Higher Power Up levels unlock harder but more lucrative modes. Balance difficulty with your skill level.

---

## 🔥 Heat Ray (Action Button Feature)

**Qualification:** Spin center spinner repeatedly to charge Heat Ray; hold **ACTION BUTTON** to deploy <kcite ref="2"/>

**Effect:** Instantly completes ALL currently lit shots on the playfield and collects available points

**Usage:**
- Charge during modes with multiple lit shots
- Deploy when you need to cash out without hitting risky shots
- Collect everything lit at once, avoiding shot-specific drains

**Points:** 25,000,000+ depending on mode; variable based on what was lit

> ⚠️ **Warning:** Heat Ray is powerful but not infinite—spinners are risky shots. Spin carefully and plan your rotation to safe shots first.

## 🦅 Allies System

Select allies to gain permanent bonuses for the rest of the ball/game. Allies can stack up to 3 simultaneously. <kcite ref="2"/>

| Ally     | Primary Benefit              | Secondary Benefit          |
| -------- | ---------------------------- | -------------------------- |
| **Mothra** | Left outlane ball save       | 1 per ball; stacks to 3    |
| **Rodan**  | 2x scoring multiplier        | Applies to battle jackpots |
| **Anguirus**| Add-a-ball during multiball | Up to 3 per multiball      |

**Recommendation:** Prioritize **Mothra** first (ball save = safety), then **Rodan** (2x multiplier accelerates scoring). Anguirus is valuable during Godzilla Multiball for add-a-balls.

## 🌍 Destruction Jackpot & Carnage Bonuses

**Destruction Jackpot** is a progressive meter that builds throughout the game via contributions from modes and shots.

- **Base value:** 5,000,000
- **Build rate:** Modes contribute 2-5% of their award value
- **Resets:** When advancing to a new city (but multiplier persists)
- **City Multiplier:** Each city controlled adds a permanent bonus

**Annihilation Bonuses:** When leaving a city, earn a percentage of that city's total completions:
- Tank Attack completed: +2%
- Bridge Attack completed: +4%
- Power modes completed: +2%

**City Carnage Bonus:** Awarded upon city change; multiplied by number of running Annihilation modes and completed objectives

**Strategy:** Cities provide cumulative bonuses. Complete all Tier 1 and Tier 2 objectives in each city before moving on to maximize final Earth Bonus. <kcite ref="3"/>

**Qualification:** Power Up Level 4+

**Objective:** Lock 3 balls, build jackpots, collect super jackpot at reverse shot

| Phase | Action                        | Points           |
| ----- | ----------------------------- | ---------------- |
| 1     | Build jackpot via shots       | 15,000,000 base  |
| 2     | Hit super jackpot reverse     | 30,000,000+      |

**Difficulty:** Medium; reverse shot (left orbit) requires practice but is learnable.

## 😈 Terror of Mechagodzilla (Mini-Wizard 2)

**Qualification:** Power Up Level 8+

**Objective:** Flip-count based multiball with 4 stages; up to 4 balls in play

| Stage | Mechanic                   | Reward                |
| ----- | -------------------------- | --------------------- |
| 1     | Hit 20 flips to progress   | 20,000,000            |
| 2     | Hit 30 flips               | 40,000,000            |
| 3     | Hit 40 flips               | 70,000,000            |
| 4     | Hit 50 flips; final battle | 100,000,000+          |

**Challenge:** Flip constantly to progress; every 10 flips = advancement. This mode is time-based with 60+ second timer.

## 👽 Planet X Multiball (Mini-Wizard 3)

**Qualification:** Complete all four Tier 2 battles (Tank, Bridge, Saucer, plus one more per city rules)

**Objective:** Defeat Xiliens across multiple phases; victory challenge unlocks King of the Monsters

| Phase         | Action                          | Points           |
| ------------- | ------------------------------- | ---------------- |
| 1 - Approach  | Hit ramp shots to build time    | 8,000,000        |
| 2 - Defense   | Shoot building for jack pots    | 20,000,000 ea    |
| 3 - Victory   | Complete sequence; SJ available | 50,000,000+      |
| Challenge     | Extended play if all objectives | Unlock KOTM       |

**Strategy:** This is the gateway to the true final wizard mode. Completing the victory challenge (hitting all required shots) qualifies King of the Monsters.

## 👑 King of the Monsters (Main Wizard Mode)

**Qualification:** Complete Monster Zero, Terror of Mechagodzilla, and Planet X (any outcome) <kcite ref="1"/>

**Part 1 - Single Ball:** Defeat Tier 1 monsters in sequence (60+ second timer)
- Targets include Gigan, Ebirah, Titanosaurus, and others
- Each victory: 30,000,000+

**Part 2 - 4-Ball Multiball:** 22 shots to light final bounty
- Jackpots escalate per shot sequence
- Super jackpot: 100,000,000+
- Victory awards massive completion bonus

**Difficulty:** This is legitimately challenging; expect 50M+ points minimum if you complete part 1, but part 2 requires precise shot control.

---

## 🗻 Monster Island Madness (Secret Final Wizard Mode)

**Qualification:** Complete King of the Monsters AND complete Planet X victory challenge

**Description:** The ultimate secret 6-ball multiball with extended time and escalating shot sequences <kcite ref="1"/>

**Objective:** Defeat all kaiju simultaneously; switch-building mechanics for maximum damage

**Scoring:** 200,000,000+ easily achievable; can approach 500M+

**Challenge:** Extremely difficult; 6-ball action is chaotic. Most players will not see this mode in a casual session.

## 📈 Stacking & Combos

### ✅ What Stacks Well

| Combination                  | Benefit                                              |
| ---------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------- |
| **Rodan + Multiballs**       | 2x scoring applies to all multiball jackpots         |
| **Anguirus + Godzilla MB**   | Add-a-balls extend multiball time for super jackpots |
| **Heat Ray + Mode**          | Completes all lit shots without shot skill required  |
| **Power Up 8 + Terror MB**   | Flip-count mode synergizes with extended modes      |

### ❌ What NOT to Stack

| Avoid                     | Reason                                           |
| ------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------ |
| **Multiple Add-a-Balls**  | Balls drain too fast; limits multiplier benefit |
| **Risky Shots + No Saves**| Ball drains lose collected multiball progress   |
| **Spinner Focus Early**   | High-risk shots; Gigan battles are safer        |

### ⏰ Optimal Ball Progression

**Ball 1:** Build ramp damage safely → Godzilla Multiball
**Ball 2:** Complete Tier 2 battles → Progress to new cities
**Ball 3+:** Qualify mini-wizard modes → Reach King of the Monsters

## 🎮 Shot Analysis

### ⚠️ Dangerous Shots

| Shot                   | Risk                              | Mitigation                    |
| ---------------------- | --------------------------------- | ----------------------------- |
| **Spinner (either)**   | SDTM (sudden death) drains likely | Use sparingly; setup first     |
| **Center Ramp Missed** | Unpredictable bounceback to drain | Trap after shot successfully   |
| **Pop Bumper Area**    | Chaotic exit paths to outlanes    | Avoid during critical moments  |
| **Right Loop Missed**  | Can deflect toward right outlane  | Ensure clean shot aim          |

### ✅ Safe Shots

| Shot              | Benefit                         |
| ----------------- | ------------------------------- |
| **Left Ramp**     | Feeds back to left flipper      |
| **Right Ramp**    | Predictable return to right     |
| **Scoop**         | Ball held; time to prepare      |
| **Captive Ball**  | Controlled shot; no ball loss   |

### 🎮 Ball Control Positions

- **Left Flipper Trap:** Best after right ramp, center shots
- **Right Flipper Trap:** Best after left ramp, orbits
- **Magna Grab Control:** Magnet catches ball mid-shot; release via flippers for control
- **Post Pass:** Transfer ball safely between flippers

## 🏆 Path to 1 Billion Points

Reaching 1 billion on Godzilla requires a combination of efficient city progression, multiball stacking, and wizard mode completion.

### Phase 1: Foundation (Target: 150M)
1. ✅ Complete Gigan battles across multiple cities (safe ramp strategy)
2. ✅ Unlock and collect Godzilla Multiball at least once
3. ✅ Select Mothra + Rodan allies for multipliers and ball saves
4. ✅ Progress to New York city; build cumulative destruction jackpots

### Phase 2: Tier 2 Mastery (Target: 400M)
1. ✅ Complete all Tank Attack multiballs (reduce tank escapes)
2. ✅ Master Bridge Attack Multiball and land super jackpots
3. ✅ Unlock Saucer Attack Multiball (6-ball potential)
4. ✅ Advance Power Up to Level 4+; unlock Monster Zero

### Phase 3: Mini-Wizard Modes (Target: 750M)
1. ✅ Complete Monster Zero (30-50M points)
2. ✅ Advance to Power Up Level 8; unlock Terror of Mechagodzilla
3. ✅ Complete Terror of Mechagodzilla (70-100M points)
4. ✅ Complete all Tier 2 battles to unlock Planet X

### Phase 4: Final Push to 1B (Target: 1B+)
1. ✅ Complete Planet X mini-wizard (50-70M)
2. ✅ Reach King of the Monsters main wizard mode
3. ✅ Complete King of the Monsters Part 2 (100M+)
4. ✅ Qualify Monster Island Madness if possible (500M+ ceiling)
5. 🏆 **VICTORY!** Cumulative destruction jackpots + city bonuses push past 1 billion
