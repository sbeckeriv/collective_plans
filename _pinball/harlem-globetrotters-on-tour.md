# 🏀 Harlem Globetrotters On Tour - Strategy Guide

*Bally, 1978/79 — Designed by Greg Kmiec*

> **"Spell GLOBE. Spin all day."**

This Bally classic is all about building and carrying bonus through a single, efficient combo: the right inline drop targets simultaneously raise your bonus multiplier, boost your spinner value, and spell GLOBE. Master that one sequence and the rest of the game follows naturally. Our goal: max the inline drops for 5x bonus and a 2,000-point spinner, then grind the spinner relentlessly.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Hit the right standup target to open the gate**, then work the **four right inline drop targets** — this is the single most important sequence in the game
- [ ] **Each inline drop target (worth 5K each) does three things simultaneously**: raises your bonus multiplier (2X→3X→5X), boosts your spinner value (100→200→1000→2000), and spots a letter in GLOBE
- [ ] **Once maxed, spin the lit spinner relentlessly** — it's now worth up to 2,000 per spin
- [ ] **The top saucer adds 3K to bonus** — a reliable secondary objective
- [ ] **Hit all five Globetrotters to advance the Slam Dunk Shot**, then slam it in for a **50,000-point Special**
- [ ] **Take a "Fast Break" up the left side into the upper saucer** as an alternative way to spell GLOBE

---

## 🏀 Core Mechanics

### The Core Combo: Right Inline Drop Targets

This is the heart of the entire strategy. The four right inline drop targets (5K each) simultaneously:

| Effect | Progression |
|---|---|
| **Bonus multiplier** | 2X → 3X → 5X |
| **Spinner value** | 100 → 200 → 1000 → 2000 |
| **GLOBE letters** | Spots one letter per drop |

> 🎯 **Pro Tip — Core Strategy:** Open the gate via the right standup target first, then clear the four inline drops. This single sequence maximizes three different systems at once, making it by far the most efficient use of your shots on this machine.

### Spelling GLOBE (Two Routes)

1. **Primary route:** Knock down the four inline drop targets in the right lane (also handles bonus/spinner as above)
2. **Alternative route:** Take a **"Fast Break"** up the left side into the **upper saucer**

### Spinner (Your Endgame Scoring Engine)

Once your inline drops are maxed, the spinner pays up to **2,000 per spin** — by far your best repeatable shot. "Spinner all day" is the literal community-recommended strategy once you're set up.

### Top Saucer

Adds **3K to bonus** with each hit — a steady, reliable secondary objective that doesn't require any setup.

### Slam Dunk Shot

Hit **all five Globetrotters** (standup targets) to advance the Slam Dunk Shot, then **slam it in for a 50,000-point Special** — one of the biggest single awards available in the game.

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **Right outlane detour gate** | A specific drain risk feature on this layout | Be aware of the gate's current state before committing to risky right-side shots |
| **Pop bumper unpredictability** | Standard late-70s bumper chaos | Treat as incidental scoring, not a shot to actively chase |
| **Forcing the Fast Break route** | An alternative, but potentially riskier path to spelling GLOBE | Default to the inline drops unless the Fast Break shot is genuinely safer in the moment |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Ball

1. Hit the right standup to open the gate
2. Start clearing the inline drop targets toward your first multiplier and spinner boost
3. Pick up top saucer hits for steady bonus along the way

### Mid Ball

1. Finish maxing the inline drops (5X bonus, 2,000 spinner)
2. Settle into grinding the lit spinner as your primary scoring loop
3. Work on the five Globetrotters targets toward the Slam Dunk Shot

### Late Ball

1. Continue spinner grinding for steady accumulation
2. Slam in the Slam Dunk Shot for 50,000 if you've completed all five Globetrotters
3. Bank your maxed bonus (multiplied by 5X) at end of ball

## 📋 Quick Reference: Strategic Priorities

**Early:** Open the gate, start clearing inline drops.
**Mid:** Max the inline drops (5X bonus, 2K spinner), start grinding the spinner.
**Late:** Keep spinning, slam in the Slam Dunk Shot if available, bank maxed bonus.

**Target Outcome:** Maxing the inline drop combo (5X bonus + 2,000 spinner) and landing the 50,000-point Slam Dunk Special represents a strong, well-played game on this basketball-themed classic.
