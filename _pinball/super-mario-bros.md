# 🍄 Super Mario Bros. - Strategy Guide

*Gottlieb, 1992 — Designed by Jon Norris*

> **"It's-a me, jackpot!"**

Super Mario Bros. (Gottlieb, 1992) is a System 3 era Gottlieb featuring 3 flippers, an elevated mini-playfield, two ramps, two vertical up-kickers, and characters from the original Mario universe (Mario, Luigi, Princess, Bowser). The mini-playfield on the upper level is a distinctive feature where the third flipper operates. Our goal: **200M–500M** through World completions, the Bowser Battle multiball, and mini-playfield jackpots.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Shoot the left ramp** to access the mini-playfield (upper level)
- [ ] **Use the third flipper** (upper right) to work the mini-playfield targets
- [ ] **Complete World shots** (numbered 1–8 shot sequences) to advance the game
- [ ] **Hit the Bowser target** to start the Bowser Battle multiball
- [ ] **Collect coins** (standup target + pop bumpers) for bonus accumulation
- [ ] **Use VUKs** (vertical up-kickers) to launch the ball from lower to upper playfield areas

## 🌍 Core Mechanics

### Skill Shot

| Skill Shot | How | Reward |
|---|---|---|
| **Mini-playfield** | Plunge and hold left flipper to enter the left ramp immediately | Mini-playfield access + World 1 start |
| **Standard** | Full plunge | Ball to lower playfield |

### World Progression (Worlds 1–8)

The game progresses through 8 Worlds (mimicking the NES game structure). Each World requires completing a specific shot sequence:

| World | Theme | Key Shots |
|---|---|---|
| 1 (Grassland) | Left ramp + pop bumpers | Basic route |
| 2 (Desert) | Right ramp + standup | Mid difficulty |
| 3 (Water) | VUK sequences | Requires timing |
| 4 (Giant) | Mini-playfield | Upper level play |
| 5–7 (Sky/Ice/Pipe) | Mixed sequences | Increasing complexity |
| 8 (Bowser's Castle) | All major shots | Final world |

Completing each World awards a World bonus (scaling from 5M for World 1 to 50M+ for World 8) and advances toward the Bowser Battle.

### Mini-Playfield (Upper Level)

Accessed via the left ramp. The third flipper (upper right) controls the ball on the elevated mini-playfield:
- Mini-targets score points and collect World objectives
- Extended mini-playfield sessions can complete entire World segments without returning to the lower level
- VUK #2 returns the ball from mini-playfield back to lower level

### VUKs (Vertical Up-Kickers, 2)

Two VUKs on the playfield:
- **VUK #1:** Left side — launches ball to mini-playfield entry
- **VUK #2:** Right side — returns ball from a lower catch point back up to the right flipper area

Both VUKs can be used deliberately by directing the ball toward their catch points.

### Bowser Target

A dedicated target near the right side. Hit it 3 times (or complete specified Worlds) to activate the Bowser Battle sequence.

## 🎳 Multiball

### Bowser Battle Multiball

**How to start:** Hit the Bowser target 3 times OR complete 5+ Worlds.

**Jackpot structure:**
- 3-ball multiball (Mario, Luigi, and a Toad ball)
- Each ball color corresponds to a character — hitting character-specific shots scores a character jackpot
- **Bowser Super Jackpot:** Hit the Bowser target during multiball after collecting all character jackpots — 50M–150M+
- Mini-playfield access during multiball doubles mini-playfield jackpot values

### World 4 Multiball (Mini)

Complete World 4 (mini-playfield heavy) to trigger a brief 2-ball mini-playfield multiball. Lower value but quick to trigger.

## 💡 Pro Tips

> **The mini-playfield is the game's heart.** Players who ignore it score 30–40% less than players who exploit it. Access it every ball.

> **Third flipper timing.** The upper flipper on the mini-playfield fires differently than standard flippers — it's smaller and the ball is faster. Adjust your timing.

> **Bowser target = power move.** Hit it 3 times early rather than waiting for World completion — you can trigger the multiball with minimal World progress and still harvest good jackpots.
