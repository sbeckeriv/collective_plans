# 🏖️ Baywatch Pinball Machine - Strategy Guide

*Sega Pinball, 1995 — Designed by Joe Kaminkow & Joe Balcer*

> **"Don't fear the reef — fear the wizard mode chain."**

Baywatch is an underrated mid-90s Sega machine packed with shots — a shark-fin mini-flipper, a lifeguard tower lock for 5-ball multiball, and a three-tier wizard mode chain (Lifesaver Millions → Tourist Season → Earthquake) that rewards completing all five rescue modes. Our goal: complete all five modes, climb the wizard mode ladder, and reach the 6-ball **Earthquake** finale.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Pick a skill shot**: top SPF rollover lanes, the Shark Hole, or the Ironman side ramp are your three standard choices
- [ ] **Watch for the Secret Skill Shot**: shoot the lit Guard Tower for 25 million points if it's available
- [ ] **Lock 3 balls in the Lifeguard Tower** to start 5-ball multiball
- [ ] **Work all five rescue modes** (CPR, Helicopter Rescue, Trapped Diver, Shark Cove, Tidal Wave) rather than repeating one — completing all five is the gateway to Earthquake
- [ ] **Spell S-H-A-R-K** for its standalone feature along the way
- [ ] **Don't ignore the jet ski and swimmer-rescue video modes** on the DMD — they're a change of pace and contribute bonus scoring

---

## 🦈 Core Mechanics

### Skill Shot

Choose between three standard options at the plunge:
- **Top SPF rollover lanes**
- **Shark Hole**
- **Ironman side ramp**

**Secret Skill Shot:** shoot the **Guard Tower** when it's lit for **25 million points** — the biggest skill shot payout in the game if you can time it.

### Shark Flips (Mini-Flipper)

A unique **game-controlled mini-flipper** with a plastic shark fin on top activates at specific moments to redirect the ball — part of the game's playful "shark attack" theme rather than a player-controlled flipper.

### S-H-A-R-K Spelling

Complete the S-H-A-R-K standup target sequence for its own standalone feature/award, separate from the five main rescue modes.

### Lifeguard Tower Lock & Multiball

1. Lock balls in the **Baywatch Lifeguard Tower**
2. Lock all **3 balls** to start **5-ball multiball** with a ball saver active
3. During multiball, all **5 mode shots are lit for Swimmer Jackpots**
4. **Swimmer Jackpot value:** 15M + (5M × Balls-In-Play) + 10M — meaning the more balls still in play when you collect, the bigger each jackpot

> 🎯 **Pro Tip:** Try to delay your first jackpot collection until you have as many balls in play as possible — the per-ball multiplier on each Swimmer Jackpot makes early balls disproportionately valuable to preserve.

## 🌊 The Five Rescue Modes

| Mode | Theme |
|---|---|
| **CPR** | Resuscitation-themed shot sequence |
| **Helicopter Rescue** | Aerial rescue shot sequence |
| **Trapped Diver** | Underwater rescue shot sequence |
| **Shark Cove** | Shark-avoidance shot sequence |
| **Tidal Wave** | Wave-survival shot sequence |

Completing all five of these (each at least once) is the requirement to light the final wizard mode, **Earthquake**.

### Video Modes

Two distinct video modes play out on the large DMD as a change of pace from physical shots:
- **Jet Ski navigation** sequence
- **Swimmer rescue** sequence

These break up the pinball-side action and contribute their own scoring on top of physical shots.

## 🏆 Wizard Mode Ladder

Baywatch has a genuine three-tier progressive wizard mode chain:

### 1. Lifesaver Millions (Sub-Wizard Mode)

- **Requirement:** Spell GUARD
- **Gameplay:** A jackpot counts down from **50 million**; shoot any of the five main mode shots to score the current countdown value before it runs out

### 2. Tourist Season (Sub-Wizard Mode)

- **Requirement:** Hit all five major shots **twice**
- **Gameplay:** 2-ball multiball with a **25 million jackpot** at the right ramp

### 3. Earthquake (Final Wizard Mode)

- **Requirement:** Complete **CPR, Helicopter Rescue, Trapped Diver, Shark Cove, and Tidal Wave**
- **Gameplay:** **6-ball multiball** with a **60-second timer** — the biggest, most chaotic event in the game

> 🌊 **Pro Tip:** Don't rush straight at Earthquake — Lifesaver Millions and Tourist Season are both achievable mid-game milestones that pad your score significantly before you're ready for the six-ball finale.

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Shot | Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| **Shark Flips redirect** | The game-controlled mini-flipper can send the ball somewhere unexpected | Stay ready on the main flippers any time the Shark Flips activates |
| **6-ball Earthquake chaos** | Extremely difficult to track and control that many balls at once | Focus on making any lit shot rather than trying to control every ball — volume matters more than precision here |
| **Forcing Tourist Season's right ramp** | A missed ramp shot under time pressure risks a drain | Only commit to the ramp when you have a clean, trapped-ball setup |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Game (Ball 1)

1. Take a skill shot, watching for the 25M Secret Skill Shot opportunity
2. Start working on one or two of the five rescue modes
3. Begin building toward your first Lifeguard Tower lock

**Target Score:** 20M–60M

### Mid Game (Ball 2)

1. Cash in 5-ball multiball once locks are complete, delaying your first jackpot for maximum balls-in-play value
2. Work toward spelling GUARD for Lifesaver Millions
3. Continue rotating through the five rescue modes rather than over-focusing on one

**Target Score:** 60M–150M

### Late Game (Ball 3+)

1. Push for Tourist Season if you've hit all five majors twice
2. If all five rescue modes are complete, light and run **Earthquake**
3. Use video modes (jet ski, swimmer rescue) as scoring filler between physical shot attempts

**Target Score per ball:** 80M–250M+

## ⚙️ Setup Recommendations

| Setting | Recommendation | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| **Playfield pitch** | 6.5°–7.0° | Sega factory standard for mid-90s DMD-era machines |
| **Shark Flips mechanism** | Verify clean, reliable activation | Central to the game's signature gimmick — a sticky mechanism disrupts ball flow significantly |
| **Trap door (left plastic ramp)** | Check for proper function | Affects ball routing on one of the main ramp shots |

---

## 📋 Quick Reference: Strategic Priorities

**Ball 1:** Skill shot, start a rescue mode, work toward Lifeguard Tower locks.
**Ball 2:** Cash 5-ball multiball with delayed jackpot collection, chase GUARD for Lifesaver Millions.
**Ball 3+:** Push for Tourist Season, complete all five rescue modes, finish with Earthquake.

**Target High-Score Ballpark:** **150–300 million points** is a strong, competitive game; reaching Earthquake marks full mastery of this underrated machine.
