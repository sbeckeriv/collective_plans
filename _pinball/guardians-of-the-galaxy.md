# 🚀 Guardians of the Galaxy - Strategy Guide

*Stern Pinball, 2017 — Designed by John Borg*

> **"Oh yeah!"**

Guardians of the Galaxy packs 8 selectable modes, Groot and Orb multiballs, and a three-tier wizard mode chain (Cherry Bomb → Immolation Initiative → Save Xandar) into a 2-flipper layout. The critical mechanic to learn early: the mode clock **never stops**, so canceling video clips with both flippers is essential to finishing modes within a single ball. Our goal: complete modes efficiently, charge and spend Hadron Enforcer uses wisely, and climb the full wizard mode ladder.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Select a mode before plunging** — at the start of the game (and after completing modes), you choose from up to 8: Quill's Quest, Knowhere, Yaka Arrow, Antiquities Shop, Sibling Rivalry, Pod Chase, Rocket, and Sanctuary
- [ ] **Cancel video clips and animations immediately by pressing both flippers** — the mode clock never stops, so every second spent watching unskipped animation is time you can't get back
- [ ] **Hit the 5 yellow standup targets to charge the Hadron Enforcer** with 3 uses — press the action button to instantly collect the left-most lit mode/multiball/wizard shot
- [ ] **Starting 3 modes lights an extra ball**; completing 6 modes lights a **second** extra ball
- [ ] **Hit Groot to open his mouth, then shoot in** to lock balls toward Groot Multiball (3 locks for the 1st, 4 for the 2nd, double-hits + 4 locks for the 3rd)
- [ ] **Hit the Orb drop target, then the standup behind it**, 3 times to start Orb Multiball

---

## 🌌 Core Mechanics

### Mode Selection & The Mode Clock

Choose from **8 modes** before each plunge: Quill's Quest, Knowhere, Yaka Arrow, Antiquities Shop, Sibling Rivalry, Pod Chase, Rocket, and Sanctuary. Each has a **timer and shot requirements** shown in the upper-left display.

> ⏱️ **Critical Mechanic — The Mode Clock Never Stops:** Unlike many Stern titles, video clips and animations don't pause your mode timer. **Press both flippers to cancel them immediately** — this single habit is often the difference between completing a mode in one ball and timing out.

### Hadron Enforcer

- Hit all **5 yellow standup targets** to charge it with **3 uses**
- Press the **action button** to instantly collect the **left-most lit mode/multiball/wizard mode shot** (with a few exceptions)
- Best used to: finish a mode you're close to completing, make a difficult shot you're struggling with, or collect a Super Jackpot

> 🎯 **Pro Tip:** Save Hadron charges for finishing modes specifically — it's the highest-value use since it directly converts a charge into mode-completion bonus rather than just a single shot's worth of points.

### Groot Multiball

1. Hit **Groot** to open his mouth
2. Shoot **into his mouth** to lock a ball
3. **1st multiball:** 3 locks. **2nd:** 4 locks. **3rd:** double-hits to open + 4 locks
4. **Completed mode shots score 5x value during Groot Multiball** — a major incentive to finish modes before triggering this

### Orb Multiball

1. Hit the **Orb drop target**, then the **standup behind it**
2. **Collect 3 orbs** to start multiball
3. During Orb Multiball, the **mystery award at the scoop becomes an Add-A-Ball**

## 🏆 Wizard Mode Ladder

| Wizard Mode | Requirement |
|---|---|
| **Cherry Bomb Multiball** (mini-wizard) | Complete **4 modes** |
| **Immolation Initiative** | Progress **halfway or more in all 8 modes** |
| **Save Xandar** (final wizard mode) | The ultimate goal — culmination of the full mode/multiball progression |

**Cherry Bomb Multiball** is a timed multiball; when the timer runs out and balls drain, you're shown your total points collected before returning to single-ball play.

> 💡 **Scoring tip:** Completing a mode adds **half that mode's points to your end-of-ball bonus** — so even an incomplete mode attempt that scored well still pays off at the end of the ball.

---

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **Letting video clips play out** | Wastes mode-clock time you can never recover | Press both flippers immediately to skip any animation |
| **Diverter magnet unpredictability** | Can redirect the ball in ways that surprise new players | Stay ready on the flippers any time the diverter activates |
| **Spending Hadron charges on minor shots** | Wastes your most valuable resource on low-impact plays | Reserve charges for mode completion or Super Jackpot collection |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Game (Ball 1)

1. Select a mode and immediately start canceling animations to preserve clock time
2. Build toward 3 modes started for your first extra ball
3. Start charging the Hadron Enforcer via yellow standups

**Target Score:** 15M–40M

### Mid Game (Ball 2)

1. Push toward 6 modes started for your second extra ball
2. Lock balls toward Groot Multiball, ideally with completed modes banked for the 5x shot bonus
3. Build Orb Multiball in parallel via the drop target/standup combo

**Target Score:** 40M–100M

### Late Game (Ball 3+)

1. Complete 4 modes for Cherry Bomb Multiball if not already done
2. Push all 8 modes to at least halfway progress for Immolation Initiative
3. If you've built enough progress, chase Save Xandar as the ultimate finale

**Target Score per ball:** 50M–200M+

## ⚙️ Setup Recommendations

| Setting | Recommendation | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| **Playfield pitch** | 6.5°–7.0° | Stern factory standard |
| **Groot mouth mechanism** | Verify smooth open/lock action | Central to Groot Multiball, one of the two main multiball systems |
| **Diverter magnet calibration** | Check consistent, predictable behavior | Affects ball routing on several mode shots |

---

## 📋 Quick Reference: Strategic Priorities

**Ball 1:** Select a mode, cancel every animation, charge Hadron, start toward 3-mode extra ball.
**Ball 2:** Push toward 6 modes, build Groot and Orb Multiball in parallel.
**Ball 3+:** Complete Cherry Bomb, push all 8 modes halfway for Immolation Initiative, chase Save Xandar.

**Target High-Score Ballpark:** **100–300 million points** is a strong, competitive game; reaching Save Xandar represents full mastery of this mode-clock-driven ruleset.
