# ⚔️ Star Wars: Fall of the Empire (Premium) - Strategy Guide

*Stern Pinball, 2025 — Premium Edition*

> **"The Empire falls. The jackpots don't."**

Star Wars: Fall of the Empire (Stern, 2025) is a late-era Stern Spike-3 machine set during the events of Return of the Jedi and later Legends canon. The Premium edition adds a Death Star II interactive toy, a moving ramp, and an upper playfield section with additional targets. The game features character-based progression (Luke, Han, Leia, Lando), faction multiball, and a campaign mode that mirrors the fall of the Galactic Empire. Our goal: **500M–2B** by completing campaign missions, stacking Endor Multiball with character modes, and triggering the Emperor Showdown wizard mode.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Choose your character** at ball start — each character (Luke, Han, Leia, Lando) unlocks a unique ability and shot priority
- [ ] **Shoot the character's priority shot** to advance their mission sequence
- [ ] **Complete missions** (timed shot sequences) to earn campaign progress
- [ ] **Lock balls in the Death Star II** (Premium) for Endor Multiball
- [ ] **Use your character ability** (action button) at high-value moments for score multipliers or ball saves
- [ ] **Advance the Empire Fall meter** by completing missions — each step increases all jackpot values

## 🌌 Core Mechanics

### Skill Shot

| Skill Shot | How | Reward |
|---|---|---|
| **Targeted plunge** | Time the plunge to hit the lit top lane | Bonus multiplier + mission start credit |
| **Character plunge** | Hold action button during plunge to activate character passive | Character-specific bonus |

### Character Selection

At ball start, select from four Rebel heroes:

| Character | Priority Shot | Ability |
|---|---|---|
| **Luke Skywalker** | Left ramp | Force — doubles next jackpot value |
| **Han Solo** | Right orbit | Blaster — ball save after outlane drain |
| **Princess Leia** | Center scoop | Hope — all active modes +30 seconds |
| **Lando Calrissian** | Left orbit | Gamble — mystery award (random 2×–8× bonus) |

Your character determines which shot scores 2× during the entire ball — prioritize that shot.

### Campaign Missions (Empire Fall Meter)

Missions are triggered by the center scoop or completing shot sequences. Each mission is a timed objectives sequence:

| Mission Phase | Theme | Objectives |
|---|---|---|
| Battle of Endor | Fleet engagement | Shoot left + right ramps alternating |
| Ewok Village | Ground battle | Pop bumpers + standup targets |
| Shield Generator | Sabotage | 3-shot rapid combo |
| Death Star Interior | Luke vs. Emperor | Scoop + orbit sequence |

Completing a mission phase advances the Empire Fall meter. Higher meter = higher all-game jackpot multiplier.

### Empire Fall Meter

A persistent multiplier that increases as you complete missions. At maximum (10), all jackpots are worth 5× their base value. This is the game's long-arc scoring lever — every mission completed matters.

### Death Star II Interactive Toy (Premium)

The Death Star II toy in the center:
- Rotates to reveal different shot paths
- Ball locks inside the superstructure for Endor Multiball qualification
- During multiball, the Death Star fires (light/sound event) when the super jackpot fires

## 🎳 Multiballs

### Endor Multiball (Primary)

**How to start:** Lock 2 balls inside the Death Star II (lit after completing 1 mission phase and shooting the Death Star scoop twice).

**Jackpot structure:**
- 3-ball multiball
- Character priority shot = jackpot (scaled by Empire Fall meter + ball multiplier)
- **Super Jackpot:** Complete all four character shots in one multiball → shoot the Death Star → Emperor fires → 100M–400M+
- Empire Fall meter at 8+ makes this the single highest-value event in the game

### Rebel Fleet Multiball (Mini)

Triggered by completing the Battle of Endor mission. 2-ball brief multiball with elevated left/right ramp values. Easier to trigger but lower ceiling than Endor.

## 🔄 Progression Systems

- **Empire Fall Meter:** Permanent within game; each mission completion +1 step; at 10, all jackpots ×5
- **Character mastery:** Complete 3 missions with the same character = character mastery bonus (25M + ability upgrade)
- **Rebel Medal system:** Collecting all 4 character medals (one per completed mission arc) lights the Emperor Showdown

## 🧙 Wizard Mode

### Emperor Showdown

**Requirement:** Collect all 4 Rebel Medals (complete one mission arc per character).

**Gameplay:** The Emperor activates as the ultimate adversary. All shots lit simultaneously at 10× values. The Death Star fires its superlaser — you must hit the lit reactor shot within 30 seconds to destroy it. Successfully destroying the Death Star awards the Showdown Jackpot (typically 500M–1B based on Empire Fall meter).

## 💡 Pro Tips

> **Empire Fall Meter is everything.** Every mission you complete before Endor Multiball directly multiplies the jackpots. Never rush Endor Multiball before completing at least 4 missions.

> **Han on ball 1, Luke on ball 3.** Han's ball save is most valuable when your score base is low (ball 1). Luke's Force doubler is most valuable when jackpots are at peak (ball 3 with high meter).

> **The Death Star toy is Stern's centerpiece — learn it.** The lock shots have a specific timing window as the toy rotates. Miss it and the ball deflects to the slingshots.
