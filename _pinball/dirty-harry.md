# 🔫 DIRTY HARRY Pinball Machine - Complete Strategy Guide
## *Path to 100+ Million Points*

> **"You've got to ask yourself one question: Do you feel lucky, punk?"**

Based on the 1995 Williams license of Clint Eastwood's iconic detective, this guide will help you master San Francisco and climb toward the **100+ million point** milestone <kcite ref="1"/>.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Ball 1:** Master the skill shot at upper left flipper (start at 5M, +5M each hit)
- [ ] **Light and enter HQ hole** to start the first shotgun shell mode
- [ ] **Aim for left ramp** repeatedly (worth 2x the right ramp)
- [ ] **Complete modes** in order: Barroom Brawl → Car Chase → Warehouse Raid → Letter Bomb → Precinct Attack → City Hall
- [ ] **Advance rank** by shooting left ramp to enable Playfield Promotion (up to 5x multiplier)
- [ ] **Build towards 6-ball multiball** once all six modes are completed
- [ ] **Stack modes with Playfield Promotion** when your rank is highest for maximum scoring
- [ ] **Watch for Midnight Madness** (if the internal clock reaches midnight during play)

## 🎱 Skill Shots

### Standard Skill Shot

The Magna-force automatically activates on plunge, feeding a slow ball to the upper flipper.

| Shot           | Points      | Notes                           |
| -------------- | ----------- | ------------------------------- |
| **Left Ramp**  | 5M + 5M×n   | Increases 5M per successful hit |
| Starting Value | 5,000,000   | First successful shot           |
| Progressive   | +5,000,000  | Each successful repeat          |

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Focus on smooth, controlled left ramp shots to build early points and advance rank. <kcite ref="1"/>

## 🏛️ Core Mechanics

### Playfield Promotion & Rank System

All scores on the playfield can be multiplied by up to **5x** during a Playfield Promotion window, with the multiplier based on your current RANK.

| Rank    | Multiplier | How to Earn          |
| ------- | ---------- | -------------------- |
| Rank 0  | 1x         | Starting state       |
| Rank 1  | 1x         | After 1st left ramp  |
| Rank 2  | 2x         | After 2nd left ramp  |
| Rank 3  | 3x         | After 3rd left ramp  |
| Rank 4  | 4x         | After 4th left ramp  |
| Rank 5  | 5x (Max)   | After 5th+ left ramp |

**Advancement:** Multiple shots up the left ramp increase your rank. You can also **relight Safehouse** by shooting side ramps to advance rank.

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Build rank early, then trigger modes when your rank is highest for maximum multiplied scoring. <kcite ref="1"/>

## 🎖️ Shotgun Shell Modes

Access by lighting the HQ hole (outer loops light it) and shooting it when lit. Modes occur in fixed order, regardless of which one is lit at the sinkhole.

### Mode Order
1. **Barroom Brawl**
2. **Car Chase**
3. **Warehouse Raid**
4. **Letter Bomb**
5. **Precinct Attack**
6. **City Hall**

Once all 6 modes are completed, a **6-ball multiball** starts automatically.

---

### 🥊 Barroom Brawl

**Duration:** 25 seconds  
**Objective:** Shoot the ramps repeatedly for points

| Shot       | Points |
| ---------- | ------ |
| Left Ramp  | 20M    |
| Right Ramp | 10M    |

The left ramp is worth twice as much as the right ramp. Strategy is to alternate ramps or focus on left if you have good ball control.

<kcite ref="1"/>

---

### 🚗 Car Chase

**Duration:** 20 seconds  
**Objective:** Hit both loops and the left ramp, complete combos

| Shot                      | Effect                   |
| ------------------------- | ------------------------ |
| Left Loop                 | 1 hit toward 4-shot combo |
| Right Loop                | 1 hit toward 4-shot combo |
| Left Ramp                 | 1 hit toward 4-shot combo |
| Complete 4 shots (any)    | Finishing Bonus (large)   |

If you make **4 shots in any combination** before the timer runs out, you receive an **added finishing bonus**.

<kcite ref="1"/>

---

### 📦 Warehouse Raid

**Duration:** 25 seconds  
**Objective:** Shoot the warehouse for escalating value and collect contraband items

| Shot                  | Points |
| --------------------- | ------ |
| Warehouse (1st time)  | 10M    |
| Warehouse (repeat)    | +5M    |
| Contraband Items      | Bonus  |

Repeatedly hitting the warehouse increases the award. Collecting contraband items during this mode adds bonus value.

<kcite ref="1"/>

---

### 💣 Letter Bomb

**Duration:** 25 seconds  
**Objective:** Shoot the Safehouse 4 times within the time limit

| Target         | Effect              |
| -------------- | ------------------- |
| Safehouse Shot | Progress counter    |
| Complete 4     | Mode completion     |

Hitting the safehouse four times completes the mode. Each successful hit counts toward your total.

<kcite ref="1"/>

---

### ⚔️ Precinct Attack

**Duration:** Varies  
**Objective:** Defend against attackers by shooting ramps and orbits

Hit the lit shots to defend. This mode varies in difficulty based on game state and multiplier availability.

---

### 🏛️ City Hall

**Duration:** Varies  
**Objective:** Combined ramp and orbit shots to complete

Final mode in the sequence. Completing this triggers the transition to 6-ball multiball.

---

## 🎰 Multiballs

### 🎪 6-Ball Multiball

**Qualification:** Complete all 6 shotgun shell modes in order

**Start:** Automatically begins after City Hall mode is completed

**Mechanics:**
- 6 balls in play
- All standard shots available
- Ramp and orbit shots award jackpots
- Sink 5 balls before draining all to carry to next ball

This is the primary multiball of the game. Stack it with high rank for maximum scoring.

<kcite ref="1"/>

---

### 🌙 Midnight Madness Multiball

**Qualification:** Triggered by internal clock reaching midnight during play

**Duration:** 90 seconds with ball save

**Mechanics:**
- 6 balls in play
- All shots lit and award **5x base value**
- Unlimited jackpots at the sinkhole
- High-scoring opportunity if triggered

This is a time-based special multiball. If your game runs long enough to reach midnight on the internal clock, this activates automatically with massive scoring potential.

<kcite ref="1"/>

| Shot        | Value        | Strategic Use                      |
| ----------- | ------------ | ---------------------------------- |
| **Left Ramp**  | 20M (base)   | **Primary**: Advances rank + base mode points |
| **Right Ramp** | 10M (base)   | Alternative when left blocked      |
| **Left Loop**  | Varies       | Car Chase, orbit collection        |
| **Right Loop** | Varies       | Lights HQ hole, orbit collection   |
| **Sinkhole**   | 5M+ (modes)  | Mode start, 6-ball MB jackpot      |
| **Warehouse**  | 10M+5M       | Warehouse Raid, contraband         |
| **Safehouse**  | Bonus varies | Letter Bomb, Extra Ball light      |

### ✅ Safe Shots

| Shot       | Why Safe                           |
| ---------- | ---------------------------------- |
| Left Loop  | Clean return to left flipper       |
| Left Ramp  | Feeds back to left flipper         |
| Sinkhole   | Ball held; time to set up next shot |

### ⚠️ Dangerous Shots

| Shot           | Risk                                | Mitigation                 |
| -------------- | ----------------------------------- | -------------------------- |
| Right Loop     | Magnet can cause odd ball behavior | Trap after if possible     |
| Pop Bumpers    | Chaotic bounces to outlanes        | Avoid during critical mode |
| Center Targets | Fast return to flippers            | Trap up immediately        |

## 🎁 Extra Balls & Mystery Awards

### Extra Ball Awards

| Method                      | How to Trigger                       |
| --------------------------- | ------------------------------------ |
| Safehouse Extra Ball        | Complete "Light Extra Ball" award    |
| Warehouse Contraband        | Repeatedly hit warehouse target      |
| Operator Configuration      | May be set in game options           |

**Safehouse Mode** can award "Light Extra Ball" which lights the Extra Ball at the HQ hole for subsequent shooting.

> ⚠️ **Warning:** Repeatedly hitting the warehouse when "Contraband" is lit can award extra balls but also game credits if continued too long.

## ⚙️ Setup & Configuration

### Recommended Settings

| Setting                      | Recommended                    |
| ---------------------------- | ------------------------------ |
| **Pitch at Flippers**        | 6.5–7° (starting point)        |
| **Extra Balls**              | Enable for casual play         |
| **Ball Save**                | Enable (helps learning)        |
| **Playfield Promotion Timing** | Standard                       |
| **Internal Clock**           | Enable (for Midnight Madness) |
| **Mode Difficulty**          | Medium (balanced challenge)    |

### Key Features to Understand

- **Magna-Force Magnet**: Right loop uses magnet to guide ball; can affect trajectories
- **Vertical Up-kicker**: Propels ball to upper playfield; part of mode progression
- **Pop Bumpers**: 3 pop bumpers in center; useful for advancing progress but risky for ball control
- **Slingshots**: Standard left and right slingshots; increase scoring during modes
