# 🍔 Diner - Strategy Guide

*Williams, 1990 — Designed by Mark Ritchie*

> **"Serve the order. Beat the clock. Collect Dine-Time."**

Diner casts you as a waitress serving five hungry customers (Haji, Babs, Boris, Pépé, and Buck), with every served customer pushing you closer to the **Dine-Time Jackpot** — a backbox-clock-based award that's the real engine of a big score. Our goal: serve all five customers, max the Cup Shot, and stack multiball to get there faster.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **When a customer calls out an order, hit the drop targets for their dish and sides**, then collect the **Grill bonus before it gets cold**
- [ ] **Serve all five customers** to light the **Dine-Time Jackpot** — based on the backbox clock, this is your main scoring target
- [ ] **Watch the DINER lights and time your Cup Shot** — each 2 ball revolutions advances a letter, and the Cup Shot pays 10x the current light's value
- [ ] **Steer the rollover lanes with the flippers** to stack 2x/3x/4x/5x bonus
- [ ] **Spell C-U-P** to raise your bonus multiplier and light the inlanes; spell **D-I-N-E-R** to enable Stirring the Cup
- [ ] **Lock a ball and nail the left ramp repeatedly** to increase the Dine-Time value and light an extra ball

---

## 🍳 Core Mechanics

### Serving Customers

Each of the five customers (Haji, Babs, Boris, Pépé, Buck) calls out an order:
1. Hit the **drop targets** matching their dish and sides
2. Collect the **Grill bonus before it gets cold** — don't delay once an order is ready

**Serving all five customers** lights the **Dine-Time Jackpot**, an award scaled to the game's backbox clock — this is the central scoring objective of the entire machine. Serving customers also raises your **end-of-ball bonus tip percentage**, scaling with how many customers you've served (up to 10).

### The Cup Shot

- Every **2 ball revolutions** advances one of the **DINER** lights in the middle of the playfield
- The Cup Shot itself pays **10x the current light's value**:

| Letter | Value | Cup Shot Payout (10x) |
|---|---|---|
| D | 25K | 250K |
| I | 50K | 500K |
| N | 75K | 750K |
| E | 100K | 1M |
| R | 250K | 2.5M |

**After R is lit, each successive revolution adds another 10x100K step**, up to a maximum of **10x350K = 3.5 Million** at the cup shot.

> 🎯 **Pro Tip — Core Combo:** Hit the right ramp, then the cup shot for up to 3.5M, then lock a ball. This is the single highest-leverage repeatable sequence in the game.

### Rollover Lanes & Bonus Multiplier

- Steerable with the flippers, these lanes award **2x, 3x, 4x, and 5x bonus**
- After reaching 5x, **every subsequent full lighting of all three lanes awards a flat 100,000 points**

### Spelling Bonuses

- **C-U-P**: raises your bonus multiplier and lights the inlanes
- **D-I-N-E-R**: enables **Stirring the Cup**

---

## 🎁 Multiball

A 2-ball multiball is available — locking a ball and repeatedly hitting the **left ramp** increases the **Dine-Time value** and lights an **extra ball**. Multiball play also raises your chances of completing customer orders faster, accelerating your path to Dine-Time.

> 💰 **Pro Tip:** Multiball isn't just for raw jackpot scoring here — its real value is helping you complete customer orders faster, since Dine-Time (not multiball jackpots themselves) is the dominant point source in the game.

---

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **Delaying the Grill bonus collection** | The bonus "gets cold" (loses value) if you wait too long after an order is ready | Collect promptly once you've completed a customer's order |
| **Drop target rebounds** | Standard risk when working customer orders | Approach with controlled, deliberate shots |
| **Over-focusing on Cup Shot at the expense of customer orders** | Cup Shot is valuable, but Dine-Time is the bigger overall prize | Balance both — don't neglect customer service for cup shot grinding |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Ball

1. Start serving your first customer's order via the drop targets
2. Begin tracking DINER letter progress for Cup Shot value
3. Steer rollover lanes for early bonus multiplier gains

### Mid Ball

1. Continue serving customers, collecting Grill bonuses promptly
2. Hit right ramp → cup shot → lock ball as your core repeatable combo
3. Spell C-U-P for additional multiplier and inlane lighting

### Late Ball

1. Push to serve your final customers and light Dine-Time
2. Use multiball (left ramp grinding) to boost the Dine-Time value and secure an extra ball
3. Collect Dine-Time Jackpot as your biggest scoring opportunity of the ball

## ⚙️ Setup Recommendations

| Setting | Recommendation | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| **Playfield pitch** | 6.5°–7.0° | Standard for early-90s Williams solid-state machines |
| **Drop target reset** | Verify clean, reliable resets | Central to serving customer orders correctly |
| **Backbox clock mechanism** | Check accurate timekeeping | Directly determines your Dine-Time Jackpot value |

## 📋 Quick Reference: Strategic Priorities

**Early:** Serve your first customer, track DINER letters, steer rollover lanes for multiplier.
**Mid:** Keep serving customers promptly, run the right ramp → cup shot → lock combo.
**Late:** Finish remaining customers, boost Dine-Time via multiball, collect the Jackpot.

**Target High-Score Ballpark:** Serving all five customers and collecting a strong Dine-Time Jackpot, combined with at least one maxed 3.5M Cup Shot, represents a strong, well-played game on this beloved diner-themed machine.
