# ⚔️ VIKING Pinball Machine - Strategy Guide

*Bally, 1979 — Electromechanical Classic*

> **Viking legend meets precision pinball.**

Viking is a straightforward electromechanical pinball experience that rewards skillful control and strategic bonus building. With its dual-strategy approach—either chase the drop targets for multiplier or camp the collect lane with bonus stacked—this game challenges both accuracy and ball-control discipline. Goal: reach **200K–300K** on a well-maintained machine.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Complete the left drop target bank** to build bonus multiplier (up to 5X)
- [ ] **Hit the stand-up target** behind drops for 50K per shot (active after drops)
- [ ] **Build base bonus** via spinner and right 3-bank drops to 40K+
- [ ] **Shoot right orbit to light collect bonus** at the left saucer
- [ ] **Execute the collect shot** (left saucer) when your multiplier is high
- [ ] **Avoid bumper chaos** after orbits; plan your next shot
- [ ] **Use bounce-back outlanes** to recover from drain threats via nudging
- [ ] **Chase Extra Ball lights** at drop completion

## 🎱 Core Mechanics

### Left Inline Drop Target Bank (Bonus Multiplier)

| Hit # | Effect | Bonus Multiplier |
|-------|--------|------------------|
| 1–4   | Individual hits + bonus base advance | Progressive to 5X |
| Complete all 5 | Lights Extra Ball rollover + outlanes (25K) | Up to 5X |

**Key Rule:** Completing the 5-target bank in any order advances your bonus multiplier. First completion = 1X, second = 2X, etc., up to 5X maximum <kcite ref="1"/>.

### Stand-Up Target (Behind Drop Bank)

- **Award:** 50K per shot
- **Activation:** Active only after drop bank is complete
- **Permanence:** Stays lit for entire ball
- **Strategy:** High-value shots after you've cleared the drops <kcite ref="1"/>

### Right Orbit & Collect Bonus

| Condition | Effect |
|-----------|--------|
| **Shot made** | Lights top-left saucer for bonus collection |
| **Base bonus reached** | Up to 59K maximum |
| **With 5X multiplier** | 59K × 5 = **295,000 points** |
| **Collection method** | Shoot top-left saucer (narrow shot) |

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** A strong right orbit shot will carry above the bumpers directly into the collect saucer if aimed cleanly <kcite ref="1"/>.

### Bonus Base Advancement

| Source | Bonus Advance |
|--------|---------------|
| Right 3-bank drop hits | +5K per hit |
| Spinner hits | +1K per hit |
| Outlanes (when lit) | +25K per drain |
| Every 10th spinner spin | +2K bonus |

**Super Bonus Hold:** Reaching 20K or 40K base bonus is preserved across future balls <kcite ref="1"/>.

### Spinner (Center)

**Scoring Progression:**
- **1st good rip:** 10 points → increases to 100 points
- **2nd rip (soon after):** Increases to **1,000 points per spin** (max)
- **Every 10th spin:** Adds 2K to your base bonus
- **Timer resets:** Spinner value drops back to 10 if too much time passes

> ⚠️ **Risk:** Spinner shots can lead to center drain or risky bumper bounces. Use only when you're safe on an inlane or can trap cleanly <kcite ref="1"/>.

### Upper Right 3-Bank Drop Targets

| Shot Pattern | Base Value | Max Value | Sequence Multiplier |
|--------------|------------|-----------|---------------------|
| Random hits | 2K per hit | 10K | 1X |
| 1-2-3 sequence | 20K start | 100K | 10X (sequence bonus) |

**Strategy:** Shooting in order (1, 2, 3) multiplies each value by 10, making this a secondary high-value target if you can control the sequence <kcite ref="1"/>.

### Extra Ball Opportunities

| Feature | Condition |
|---------|-----------|
| **Left drop bank completion** | Lights EB rollover at left saucer |
| **Outlane lanes (when lit)** | 25K + potentially EB award |

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

### High-Risk Areas

| Zone | Hazard | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-----------|
| **Center spinner** | Direct drain path; bumper chaos | Trap on inlane after; avoid when pressured |
| **Right orbit (missed)** | Can deflect into right outlane | Execute clean, controlled shot only |
| **Bumper exits** | Balls exiting upper bumpers are unpredictable | Anticipate bounces; keep flippers ready |
| **Left bumper near drops** | Kicks toward drop targets erratically | Account for slow roll after bumper hits |

### Safe Control Positions

- **Lower flipper cradle:** Best for planning orbit shots
- **Upper inlane traps:** Safe holding area after orbits
- **Post-catch positioning:** Transfer between flippers to set up next shot
- **Bounce-back nudge lanes:** Use these to recover draining balls via nudge (unique feature!)

> 💡 **Unique EM Feature:** The bounce-back outlanes let you nudge a draining ball through gates back to the playfield—use this mechanic to extend rallies <kcite ref="1"/>.

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Ball (Ball 1)

**Priorities:**
1. **Complete the left drop target bank** (bonus multiplier builder)
2. **Build base bonus** via right 3-bank and spinner (safer targets)
3. **Start understanding the right orbit** but don't risk drains yet
4. **Avoid aggressive center play** (bumper chaos = drain risk)

**Target Score:** 50K–150K

**Why:** Ball 1 is about setup. Completing the drops locks in your multiplier for all future balls.

### Mid Ball (Ball 2)

**Priorities:**
1. **Build base bonus aggressively** (goal: reach 40K+ for super-bonus hold)
2. **Attempt right orbit** if you have control; light the collect bonus saucer
3. **Execute the collect shot** (left saucer) if your multiplier is 2X+ and bonus is 30K+
4. **Chase EB opportunities** if extra ball is lit

**Target Score:** 100K–250K per ball

**Strategy:** This ball determines your final score. Stacking 40K+ bonus at 4X–5X multiplier = 160K–200K per collection.

### Late Ball (Ball 3+)

**Priorities:**
1. **Light and execute collect bonus** repeatedly (if ROM allows multi-collect per ball)
2. **Protect bonus multiplier** (avoid risky shots; prioritize orbits only)
3. **Hit right 3-bank** for value if orbit is too dangerous
4. **Go for Extra Ball** if conditions are lit

**Target Score per ball:** 150K–300K (with high multiplier)

## 🎯 Shot Analysis

### The Collect Bonus Saucer (Top-Left) — Your Primary Target

**Why it's critical:** With base bonus at 59K and multiplier at 5X, a single collection = 295,000 points. This is the peak scoring shot.

**How to execute:**
1. Build base bonus via right 3-bank and spinner (20K minimum)
2. Complete left drop bank (to reach 5X multiplier)
3. Shoot right orbit cleanly (lights collect at top-left saucer)
4. Trap ball on lower flipper after orbit return
5. Execute controlled left-saucer shot (narrow target)
6. Repeat if ROM allows multi-collect

**Ball-control tip:** Right orbit naturally feeds back toward your left flipper—use this to set up the narrow collect shot <kcite ref="1"/>.

### The Left Drop Target Bank — Multiplier Gate

**Why it matters:** Every completion increases your multiplier by 1X, directly scaling all future collect-bonus points.

**How to execute:**
1. Hit drops in any order initially (there's no sequence requirement)
2. Aim for the center of the bank to ensure hitting a target
3. Complete all 5 targets on Ball 1 to lock in 1X multiplier
4. Don't re-attempt this unless it reset (safer to move on)

### The Right 3-Bank Drops — Secondary Bonus Builder

**Why it matters:** Each hit advances base bonus by 5K (solid, safe value).

**How to execute:**
- Upper-right ramp shots
- Controlled flipper strikes when ball is trapped
- Best used early in the ball to build bonus safely
- Sequence shots (1-2-3) award 10× base value if you can control the sequence

### The Spinner — Risky but Valuable Bonus

**Why it matters:** Builds base bonus and escalates from 10 to 1,000 points per spin. Every 10th spin awards 2K bonus.

**How to execute:**
- Hit only from safe inlane positions
- Avoid when high drain pressure is present
- Useful for building bonus on Ball 1 when multiplier is low
- Stop spinning once you reach 1,000-point ramp (focus on collect instead)

> ⚠️ **Warning:** Spinner shots frequently lead to center drain or chaotic bumper bounces. Use defensively, not aggressively <kcite ref="1"/>.

## ⚙️ Setup Recommendations

| Setting | Recommendation | Reason |
|---------|---|---|
| **Playfield pitch** | 6.0°–6.5° | Standard EM; prevents uncontrolled center drains |
| **Flipper strength** | Medium (default) | Balanced for precision; high strength = uncontrollable bounces |
| **Bonus carry-over** | Check operator settings | Critical: does bonus carry to next ball at 20K/40K thresholds? |
| **Drop target reset** | Verify per machine | Some machines reset after ball; others hold state |
| **Outlane EB light** | Confirm in attract mode | Does completing drops light EB in outlanes, or is it a direct award? |
| **Nudge sensitivity** | Conservative | EM machines punish aggressive nudging; use only for outlane saves |

## 📖 Strategic Summary

**Two Paths to High Scores:**

1. **Drop Target Path (Ball 1):** Focus on completing the left drop bank to lock in your multiplier (1X–5X). This is the foundational strategy.

2. **Bonus Collection Path (Balls 2–3):** Once multiplier is locked, build base bonus via safe right 3-bank and spinner shots. Then execute the right orbit to light, and collect at the top-left saucer repeatedly.

**The Winning Formula:**
- Ball 1: Complete drops (50K–100K)
- Ball 2: Build bonus to 40K+ and collect once at 3X multiplier (120K–180K)
- Ball 3: Collect again at 4X–5X multiplier (160K–295K)
- **Total: 200K–575K possible**

The machine rewards **patience and ball control** over aggressive play. Plan two shots ahead, avoid bumper zones, and treat the right orbit as your money shot once bonuses are primed.

## ⚠️ Dangerous Drain Patterns

**Center Drain from Spinner:** Spinner shots often lead directly to center drain or chaotic pop-bumper bounces. Use only defensively.

**Right Orbit Miss:** A failed orbit shot can deflect hard into the right outlane, ending the ball unexpectedly.

**Bumper Exit Chaos:** Pop bumpers near the upper playfield kick erratically; account for wild bounces when planning your next shot.

**Left Bumper to Drops:** The left bumper's kick angle can send the ball unpredictably toward drop targets. Factor this into shot planning.

> 💡 **Recovery:** Remember the bounce-back outlane feature—nudge a draining ball back into play if you see it heading out the sides <kcite ref="1"/>.
