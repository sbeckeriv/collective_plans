# 🔮 MYSTIC Pinball Machine - Strategy Guide

*Bally, 1979 — Electromechanical Carnival Magic*

> **Spin the wheel. Light the mystery. Chase the reel jackpot.**

Mystic is a carnival-themed electromechanical pinball machine with reel-based scoring, spinning targets, and a kick-out messenger ball that rewards precision play. No modes, no multiball, no complicated stacking—just clean mechanical fun. Our goal: reach **150K–250K points**, a realistic score on well-maintained machines.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Understand the spinning targets:** Hit them to light/advance lanes and scoring
- [ ] **Complete drop target banks:** Sequential hits build bonus multipliers
- [ ] **Use the messenger ball lane:** Ball returns automatically for another shot
- [ ] **Protect the center drains:** Avoid chaotic center play; use flipper control
- [ ] **Build your bonus:** Complete drop banks and slingshot hits accumulate bonus
- [ ] **Chase the reel jackpot:** Light it via drop targets, then hit the spinner or rollover

## 🎱 Core Mechanics

### Drop Target Banks (3-Bank)

| Location | Target Count | Purpose | Base Value |
|----------|--------------|---------|------------|
| **Upper left** | 3-bank | Sequential completion | Lights rollover lanes |
| **Upper center** | 3-bank | Bonus multiplier advance | +10K multiplier per hit |
| **Right playfield** | 3-bank | Reel jackpot enabler | Lights spinner as "jackpot ready" |

**Drop Target Rule:** Hit all three targets in each bank to reset it and advance your progress. Resets occur at end of ball. Each reset advances the bonus multiplier (1x → 2x → 3x, etc.).

### Spinning Targets (2)

Two spinning targets on the playfield serve dual purposes:
- **When unlit:** 500 points
- **When lit (via drop target completion):** 5,000 points
- **Reel jackpot spinner:** 25,000 points (when all drop banks are complete and spinner is lit as the final shot)

### Messenger Ball Lane

The kick-out hole with standup target:
- Returns the ball automatically to the upper flipper after 1–2 seconds
- Standup target is worth **1,000 points**
- Useful for recovery when you need another controlled shot

### Bonus System

**Base Bonus:** 10K per completed drop target bank (max 30K per ball from drops alone)

**Bonus Multiplier:** Earned by completing the upper-center 3-bank. Multiplier increases with each completion (1x, 2x, 3x, etc.). Multiplier is held at ball-end and applies to next ball's bonus collection.

**Collection:** Bonus is awarded at the start of your next ball. Reel displays your accumulated value.

### Pop Bumpers & Slingshots

Three pop bumpers and two slingshots:
- **Pop bumpers:** 100 points each
- **Slingshots:** 10 points each (main purpose: playfield defense, not scoring)

### Rollovers & Lanes

Upper lane rollovers are lit/advanced by drop target completion. Hitting all lit rollovers awards **10K bonus**.

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

### High-Risk Areas

| Shot | Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------|-----------|
| **Direct center plunge** | Pop bumper chaos; unpredictable bounces to outlanes | Aim for controlled feeds to flippers |
| **Missed drop target shots** | Ball can ricochet to center or outlanes | Trap on upper flipper; execute smooth shots |
| **Pop bumper area** | Chaotic bounces; high drain risk | Use bumpers for passive skill-building only |
| **Right ramp overshot** | Ball skips right; can drain easily | Soft touch required; use controlled flipper shots |

### Safe Control Positions

- **Upper flipper cradle:** Best position for planning your next shot
- **Lower flipper trap:** Allows setup for controlled drop-target shots
- **Post pass/live catch:** Transfer between flippers safely after returns

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Ball 1 (Early Game)

1. **Complete at least one drop target bank** — this lights bonus for Ball 2
2. **Hit spinning targets (unlit)** — safe 500 points each; not essential but builds score
3. **Avoid over-aggressive play** — losing Ball 1 early hurts your multiplier progress
4. **Work toward upper-center bank** if you have good control; even 1 hit advances multiplier

**Target Score:** 20K–50K

### Ball 2 (Mid Game)

1. **Advance your bonus multiplier** — complete the upper-center 3-bank if feasible
2. **Complete more drop banks** — each bank = 10K base bonus + advancement
3. **Protect the ball** — stay positioned for controlled shots
4. **Hit spinning targets (lit)** if you've completed a bank — now worth 5K each

**Target Score:** 50K–120K (including Ball 1 bonus)

### Ball 3+ (Late Game)

1. **Maximize bonus multiplier** — if you have 2x or higher, the next ball's bonus collection is valuable
2. **Light the reel jackpot:** Complete all three drop target banks if possible, light the right spinner as final shot
3. **Go for the spinner** — 25K jackpot is your biggest single shot
4. **Collect and repeat** — bonus multiplies any progress you've made

**Target Score per ball:** 30K–80K

## 🎯 Shot Analysis

### The Reel Jackpot (Your Primary Money Shot)

**Why it matters:** 25,000 points is the largest single shot value on the machine. Lighting it requires completing all three drop target banks sequentially.

**How to execute:**
1. Complete upper-left drop bank (all three targets)
2. Complete upper-center drop bank (all three targets) — also advances multiplier
3. Complete right drop bank (all three targets) — this lights the spinner
4. Hit the spinning target (now the jackpot) for 25K

### The Drop Target Banks (Progression & Multiplier)

**Why they matter:** Drop targets are the gate to bonus multiplier and jackpot progression.

**How to execute:**
- **Upper-left bank:** Safe starter; lights rollover lanes
- **Upper-center bank:** Critical; completes this for every multiplier boost (1x → 2x → 3x)
- **Right bank:** Final gate; must complete to light spinner as jackpot

### Spinning Targets (Bonus Builders)

**Why they matter:** When lit (after drop bank completion), worth 5K each—more accessible than the spinner jackpot.

**How to execute:**
- Trap ball on upper flipper
- Aim smooth shots toward spinning targets
- Hit both before they reset at ball-end for quick 10K
- Less risky than going for the spinner

### Messenger Ball Lane (Recovery)

**Why it matters:** Automatic ball return gives you a second controlled shot.

**How to execute:**
- Use it when your ball becomes chaotic or drifting
- The standup target provides 1K + reset to upper flipper
- Safe option when you don't have a clear shot planned

## 🎲 Bonus Multiplier Growth

**Bonus multiplier carries to next ball** and does not reset mid-game. Growth pattern:

- **After 1st upper-center bank completion:** 2x multiplier active next ball
- **After 2nd completion:** 3x multiplier active next ball
- **After 3rd+ completions:** 4x, 5x, etc. (may cap at 5x–10x depending on machine)

**Example:** If you complete the multiplier bank twice and earn 30K total bonus, Ball 3 starts with 3x active. Collect that bonus for 90K immediate points.

## ⚙️ Setup Recommendations

| Setting | Recommendation | Reason |
|---------|---|---|
| **Playfield pitch** | 6.0°–6.5° | Standard for EM; avoid slope-induced center drains |
| **Flipper strength** | Medium (default) | Balanced for precision and safety on narrow targets |
| **Bonus carryover** | Check playfield wording | Some machines carry multiplier, others reset it |
| **Slingshot/bumper settings** | Factory default | Early EM machines have fixed values; no digital adjustment |
| **Ball speed (nudge sensitivity)** | Conservative | EM machines punish nudge abuse; play with restraint |

## 📖 Strategic Progression Summary

**Multiplier-Building Phase (Balls 1–2):**
- Complete upper-center drop bank for multiplier
- Work on at least two complete banks total
- Build toward 2x or 3x multiplier by Ball 3

**Jackpot Setup Phase (Ball 2–3):**
- If you have 2x+ multiplier, focus on one final drop bank completion
- Light the spinner as reel jackpot (25K)
- Aim for both spinning targets (5K each) if lit

**Bonus Collection Phase (Final Balls):**
- Collect bonus at start of each ball
- Bonus multiplies your work from prior balls
- Replay score threshold achievable if multiplier reaches 3x+ with 30K base bonus

**Target High-Score Ballpark:** **150K–250K** across 4 balls is a strong competitive score on Mystic <kcite ref="999"/>.

## 📋 EM Gameplay Notes

Mystic is pure **reel-based scoring** with no digital display. The machine does not support:
- Multiball modes
- Stacking features
- Wizard modes or progression systems
- Complex rule interactions

What it does have:
- Straightforward drop-target progressions
- Clean bonus multiplier system
- Reel mechanics that are easy to read and understand
- Rewarding precision play over chaos

Focus on **ball control, sequencing drop banks, and multiplier management.** This is classic pinball design.
