## 🎯 Core Mechanics

### Skill Shot
Pull the gun trigger (plunger) when the moving target aligns with the crosshairs on the DMD <kcite ref="72"/>.

| Ball | Success Value | Miss Value     |
| ---- | ------------- | -------------- |
| 1    | 2M            | 200K           |
| 2    | 3M            | 300K           |
| 3    | 4M            | 400K           |
| 4+   | +1M per ball  | +100K per ball |

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** The skill shot is active for 30 seconds or until you hit any target. If the ball drains without hitting anything, you get another chance <kcite ref="24"/>.

### Ball Saver
Active at the start of each ball until you hit **4 targets**. If the ball drains before that, you get a free re-serve <kcite ref="24"/>.

### Kickback (Laser Kick)
- **Left outlane** kickback is lit at the start of each ball <kcite ref="85"/>
- **Relight:** Hit the Light Kickback target (lower left) when lit
- **Light the target:** Two consecutive left orbit shots (or three right orbits) <kcite ref="24"/>

## 🎬 Modes Overview

### Stunts (LW 1-2-3)
Shoot all three Lethal Weapon sinkholes (left, center, right) in any order to complete a stunt <kcite ref="68"/>.

| Stunt # | Name                    | Points  |
| ------- | ----------------------- | ------- |
| 1       | High Fall               | 5M      |
| 2       | Car Crash               | 10M     |
| 3       | Toilet                  | 15M     |
| 4       | Explosion               | 20M     |
| 5       | Helicopter              | 25M     |
| 6       | Super Stunt Spectacular | **50M** |

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** When the announcer says "Now" and something flashes on the display, pull the gun trigger for an extra 1M! After Super Stunt Spectacular, the cycle repeats <kcite ref="24"/>.

### Shootout (UZI Mode)
Shoot left or right orbits to load bullets into the UZI (displayed between slingshots). When all 6 bullets are loaded, a shootout begins <kcite ref="75"/>.

**During Shootout:**
- A criminal appears on the DMD
- Pull the gun trigger to shoot
- Wait for eyes to flash and "Now" callout for maximum points (100K → 200K → 300K per shot)
- Shoot 6 times for bonus 2M/4M/6M
- Don't wait too long or you get nothing! <kcite ref="25"/>

**Shootout Awards Progression:**

| Award # | Orbits Needed | Reward                        |
| ------- | ------------- | ----------------------------- |
| 1       | 1             | 3M                            |
| 2       | 1             | Bonus + Multiplier Held + 1M  |
| 3       | 2             | Super Leo + Super Spinner Lit |
| 4       | 2             | Crime Simulator + Getaway Lit |
| 5       | 3             | Crazy Riggs                   |
| 6       | 3             | Looping Lit                   |
| 7       | 6             | **Super Lethal Weapon**       |

> ⚠️ **Warning:** Your ball is still in play during Shootout! Only reach for the trigger when the ball is safe (in bumpers, ramp, or sinkholes) <kcite ref="44"/>.

### Crime Simulator (Video Mode)
Hit the LW2 sinkhole when lit for Video Mode <kcite ref="84"/>.

- Shoot criminals (and the occasional cow!) with the gun trigger
- **Don't shoot civilians!**
- Scoring: 100K → 200K → 300K... per criminal
- Cow = 1M
- Complete 10 correct hits = **10M bonus**
- Mode ends on civilian hit or 10 shots <kcite ref="30"/>

### Leo Getz Awards
Complete all 4 Leo targets (upper left, along the left orbit) to light Leo at the LW3 sinkhole <kcite ref="73"/>.

**Leo Awards (Random):**
- Light Extra Ball
- Increase Bonus Multiplier
- Super Pops (100K per bumper)
- Murtaugh's Retirement
- 5 Million
- Light Multiball
- Stunt
- Hold Bonus + Multiplier
- Light Crime Simulator
- Light Getaway <kcite ref="31"/>

### Super Leo Getz
Complete Leo targets when Leo is already lit to light **flashing** Super Leo. You choose between two random awards <kcite ref="74"/>.

**Super Leo Awards:**
- Bank Heist (10M/15M/20M/25M/30M)
- Light Multiball
- Extra Ball
- Fight Mode (10M if you win)
- Light Looping
- 20M
- Special
- Crazy Riggs
- Super Duper Pops
- Super Lethal Weapon <kcite ref="32"/>

### Fight Mode
Randomly triggered by any sinkhole (or Super Leo award). Mash flipper buttons to fill your bar faster than the opponent. Win = **5M** (or 10M if from Super Leo) <kcite ref="29"/><kcite ref="81"/>.

### Getaway
When lit, pass through the left inlane then shoot the right orbit. Score starts at **15M** and counts down to zero over 15 seconds <kcite ref="33"/><kcite ref="76"/>.

### Looping
When lit, shoot the Freeway ramp. Each ramp shot within 20 seconds scores **5M** <kcite ref="35"/>.

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Some sources report 10M per loop shot <kcite ref="69"/>. Either way, this is huge points if you can chain ramps!

### Victory Lap
When lit, shoot the Freeway ramp within 15 seconds for **25M**. Also triggered by reaching the replay score <kcite ref="36"/>.

### Million+ (Red Wire/Blue Wire)
1. Hit the center (clock) drop target first in either bank
2. The display shows "Cut the red wire" or "Cut the blue wire"
3. Hit the correct wire target to defuse the bomb
4. Correct = 1M × bombs defused (max 5M)
5. The Million+ target (lower left) is now lit for the same value <kcite ref="27"/><kcite ref="67"/>

## 🎱 Multiballs

### How to Qualify Multiball
Knock down drop target banks to light the colored wires (Red, Yellow/Clock, Blue) <kcite ref="66"/>.

| Multiball # | Banks Required           |
| ----------- | ------------------------ |
| 1st         | 2 banks (any)            |
| 2nd         | 3 banks (any)            |
| 3rd         | 3 banks (any)            |
| 4th+        | 3 left AND 3 right banks |

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** If you drain your first two balls without doing anything, multiball is automatically lit! The game advances progress on ball loss early on <kcite ref="26"/>.

### Starting Multiball
Shoot the LW2 sinkhole when lit. You receive:
- **3M** instant award
- 2 additional balls
- 3-second ball saver <kcite ref="26"/>

### Jackpot Structure

| Jackpot Type      | How to Collect                               | Value                        |
| ----------------- | -------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------- |
| Jackpot           | Shoot Freeway ramp                           | 10M base (+5M per collected) |
| Double Jackpot    | 2 rapid ramp shots OR hit lit sinkhole first | 2× Jackpot                   |
| Quadruple Jackpot | 2 rapid ramps after Double Jackpot lit       | 4× Jackpot                   |

- After collecting any jackpot, relight it by hitting a lit sinkhole
- Unlit ramp shots during multiball = 500K <kcite ref="26"/>

### Multiball Restart
If you lose all balls but one within **15 seconds**, hit the LW2 sinkhole to restart multiball. Each multiball can only be restarted once <kcite ref="26"/>.

### Multiball Stacking Rules
- ✅ **CAN start during multiball:** Stunts, Looping, Victory Lap, most modes
- ❌ **CANNOT start during multiball:** Crime Simulator, Fight Mode <kcite ref="26"/>

## 📈 Progression Systems

### Shootout Awards
Progress through 7 awards by completing Shootouts. After award #7 (Super Lethal Weapon), the cycle resets <kcite ref="25"/>.

### Stunt Progression
Complete 6 stunts to reach Super Stunt Spectacular (50M), then cycle repeats. Track progress via the 5 lights above the flippers <kcite ref="24"/>.

### Freeway Million
Cumulative ramp shots throughout the entire game award millions at specific thresholds <kcite ref="28"/>:

| Ramps | Award       |
| ----- | ----------- |
| 3     | 3M          |
| 7     | 7M          |
| 14    | 14M         |
| 21    | 21M         |
| 31    | 31M         |
| 43    | 43M         |
| 57    | 57M         |
| 73    | 73M         |
| 91    | 91M         |
| 99    | 99M + Reset |

After 99 ramps, the counter resets but milestones shift to 10, 14, 21, 31... <kcite ref="28"/>

### Bonus Multiplier
- Light all 3 rollover lanes to advance multiplier (max 8×)
- After 8×, completing lanes = **1M**
- Use flippers to rotate lit lanes <kcite ref="43"/>

## 🔗 Stacking & Combos

### ✅ Excellent Stacks

| Combination                           | Why It Works                                               |
| ------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Multiball + Looping**               | 5M per ramp × multiple balls = massive points              |
| **Multiball + Murtaugh's Retirement** | Outlane drains during MB give ~5M instead of losing a ball |
| **Crazy Riggs + Spinners**            | 250K per switch × rapid spinner hits                       |
| **Super Lethal Weapon + Multiball**   | 10M per sinkhole × 3 balls                                 |
| **Super Duper Pops + Bumper Feeding** | 1M per bumper hit for 30 seconds                           |

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** The best possible stack is **Multiball + Murtaugh's Retirement + Looping + Super Lethal Weapon**. This is extremely difficult to set up but devastating for scoring <kcite ref="44"/>.

### ❌ Avoid These Combinations

| Situation                        | Why                                         |
| -------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| Crime Simulator during Multiball | Cannot be started                           |
| Fight Mode during Multiball      | Cannot be started                           |
| Shootout with ball in danger     | You'll drain while reaching for the trigger |

### Optimal Timing
1. **Light Looping** before starting Multiball
2. **Light Murtaugh's Retirement** before Multiball
3. Start Multiball, then immediately shoot ramps for stacked Looping + Jackpots
4. If Super Lethal Weapon is available, activate during Multiball <kcite ref="44"/>

## 🧙 Wizard Modes

### Super Lethal Weapon (Mini-Wizard Mode)
**Requirements:** Complete 7 Shootouts (requires ~18 total orbit shots)

**Gameplay:**
- Any sinkhole scores **10M** for 30 seconds
- Sinkhole hits do NOT count toward Stunts during this mode
- Ball remains in play <kcite ref="40"/>

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** This is the closest thing to a wizard mode. Stack it with Multiball for 30M+ per sinkhole cycle!

### Super Stunt Spectacular
**Requirements:** Complete 5 stunts

**Reward:** **50M** instant award + animation <kcite ref="24"/>

### There Is No Traditional Wizard Mode
Lethal Weapon 3 doesn't have a final wizard mode like modern games. The "endgame" is about:
1. Cycling through Shootout awards repeatedly
2. Stacking Super Lethal Weapon with Multiball
3. Grinding Freeway Millions
4. Maximizing jackpots

## 🎯 Shot Analysis

### 🔴 Dangerous Shots

| Shot                    | Risk                             | Mitigation                                     |
| ----------------------- | -------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| **Right Orbit**         | Can rattle back SDTM             | Soft shot, don't full-plunge                   |
| **Drop Targets**        | Rebounds can be unpredictable    | Shoot from controlled ball                     |
| **LW1 Sinkhole (left)** | Awkward angle from right flipper | Use left flipper backhand                      |
| **Outlanes**            | Standard drain paths             | Keep kickback lit, light Murtaugh's Retirement |

### 🟢 Safe Shots

| Shot                      | Notes                                        |
| ------------------------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| **Freeway Ramp**          | Feeds back to right flipper, repeatable      |
| **Left Orbit**            | Feeds to right flipper or bumpers            |
| **LW2 Sinkhole (center)** | Relatively safe, key for Multiball           |
| **LW3 Sinkhole (right)**  | Safe from left flipper                       |
| **Leo Targets**           | Upper playfield, ball usually returns safely |

### Flipper Selection Guide

| Target       | Best Flipper | Notes                        |
| ------------ | ------------ | ---------------------------- |
| Freeway Ramp | Right        | Primary scoring shot         |
| Left Orbit   | Right        | Backhand or straight shot    |
| Right Orbit  | Left         | For Getaway combo            |
| LW1 Sinkhole | Left         | Backhand from right is risky |
| LW2 Sinkhole | Either       | Center shot                  |
| LW3 Sinkhole | Left         | Natural angle                |
| Drop Targets | Either       | Depends on ball position     |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Path to 1 Billion Points

#### Early Game (0-100M)
1. ✅ **Nail the Skill Shot** (2M+)
2. ✅ **Shoot Freeway ramp** to check Extra Ball threshold
3. ✅ **Complete Leo targets** for random awards
4. ✅ **Work on Stunts** (LW 1-2-3 sinkholes)
5. ✅ **Build Shootout progress** via orbits

#### Mid Game (100M-500M)
1. ✅ **Start Multiball** when ready
2. ✅ **Stack Looping with Multiball** for 5M+ per ramp
3. ✅ **Collect Jackpots** (10M → 15M → 20M...)
4. ✅ **Reach Super Lethal Weapon** (Shootout #7)
5. ✅ **Grind Freeway Millions** (cumulative)

#### Late Game (500M-1B)
1. ✅ **Stack Super Lethal Weapon + Multiball** (30M per sinkhole cycle)
2. ✅ **Repeat Shootout cycle** for more Super Lethal Weapons
3. ✅ **Super Stunt Spectacular** (50M)
4. ✅ **Super Duper Pops** if available (1M per bumper)
5. ✅ **Victory Lap** (25M) when lit

### Highest Value Opportunities

## 📚 Additional Info

2. **Leo Award:** Random chance

### Easter Eggs & Fun Facts
- 🎬 Joe Pesci owns a Lethal Weapon 3 pinball machine <kcite ref="92"/>
- 🎬 Six machines were at the movie's LA premiere in May 1992 <kcite ref="92"/>
- 🔫 The gun trigger plunger was innovative for its time

- 🐄 Shoot the cow in Crime Simulator for 1M! <kcite ref="30"/>

### Does Completing Modes Change Gameplay?
- **Shootout awards cycle** after #7 (Super Lethal Weapon)
- **Stunts cycle** after Super Stunt Spectacular
- **Freeway Millions** shift thresholds after 99 ramps
- **Multiball requirements increase** after 3rd multiball
- **No permanent unlocks** - each game starts fresh

## ✅ Quick-Start Checklist

### Before Your First Ball
- [ ] Locate the gun trigger (plunger)
- [ ] Identify the Freeway ramp (left side)
- [ ] Find the three LW sinkholes (left, center, right)
- [ ] Note the Leo targets (upper left)
- [ ] Check the UZI bullet display (center)

### Ball 1 Priorities
- [ ] 🎯 Hit the Skill Shot (2M)
- [ ] 🛣️ Shoot 3 Freeway ramps (light Extra Ball)
- [ ] 🎯 Complete Leo targets (random award)
- [ ] 🔫 Start building Shootout progress (orbits)

### Ball 2 Priorities
- [ ] 🎱 Work toward Multiball (drop targets)
- [ ] 🎬 Complete a Stunt (all 3 sinkholes)
- [ ] 🔄 Keep kickback lit (consecutive orbits)

### Ball 3 Priorities
- [ ] 🎱 Start Multiball if ready
- [ ] 📈 Stack Looping with Multiball
- [ ] 💰 Collect Jackpots
- [ ] 🎯 Go for Super Lethal Weapon if close

### Golden Rules
1. **Ramp is king** - Freeway ramp is your safest, highest-value repeatable shot
2. **Keep the kickback lit** - Two consecutive left orbits
3. **Don't reach for the gun** unless the ball is safe
4. **Stack modes before Multiball** - Light Looping, Murtaugh's Retirement first
5. **Super Lethal Weapon + Multiball** = Path to 1 billion
