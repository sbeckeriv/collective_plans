# 🤠 FRONTIER Pinball Machine - Strategy Guide

*Bally, 1980 — Early Solid-State Widebody*

> **A mean machine that punishes hesitation.**

Frontier is a lean, demanding early-SS experience. With dangerous target shots, a dual-bonus system, and punishing drains, it rewards precision and patience. This guide targets **500K–680K points**, a realistic competitive milestone on well-maintained machines.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Understand the two bonuses:** Frontier (lane-based) + Den of Predators (animal matrix)
- [ ] **Complete A-B-C lanes twice:** First time lights EB; second time awards it
- [ ] **Hit left drop targets carefully:** First opens top-right gate; 2nd and 3rd advance multipliers
- [ ] **Spinner after first drop:** ~30K for a quality shot
- [ ] **Right 3-bank advances Predators:** Up to 4× multiplier on 45K-90K base
- [ ] **Avoid the drains:** This machine is notoriously treacherous; play defensively
- [ ] **End-of-ball Predators bonus:** Collect Den bonus at ball end; Frontier bonus carries

## 🎱 Core Mechanics

### Frontier Bonus (Lane-Based)

**Building:**
- Complete **A-B-C lane rollover sequence:** 10,000 points per set (A = 10K, B = 10K, C = 10K)
- Maximum base: **60,000 points** (after 6 complete sets)
- **Multiplier:** Built via left inline drop targets (up to 4×)
  - 1st drop target: Opens Frontier Falls gate; no multiplier advance
  - 2nd drop target: 2× multiplier
  - 3rd drop target: 3× multiplier
  - Up-left standup target: 4× multiplier (if reached)

**Maximum Potential:** 60,000 × 4× = **240,000 points**

**Collection:** Via top-right saucer (gate opens after first drop target lights it). Bonus persists ball-to-ball; collecting via gate closes it but retains multiplier.

### Den of Predators Bonus (Animal Matrix)

**Building:**
- **First set:** Light 15 animals via lane rollovers (A-B-C) = 15 × 3,000 = **45,000 base**
- **Second set:** Light 15 more animals = **90,000 base** (maximum)
- **Multiplier:** Advanced by hitting right drop target 3-bank (up to 4×)

**Maximum Potential:** 90,000 × 4× = **360,000 points** (end-of-ball only)

**Collection Mechanics:** Collected at end of ball; carries forward ball-to-ball unless reset.

### The Spinner

**Location:** Middle-right of playfield

**Activation:** First left inline drop target lights it

**Scoring:**
- Base: 1,000 points per spin
- Quality shot: ~30,000 points (accumulated from multiple spins during strong feed)

**Strategy:** Best on Ball 1 to build early points; less critical later.

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

### High-Risk Areas

| Shot | Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------|-----------|
| **Left inline drop targets** | Sweeping orbits; miss = direct drain | Aim low and straight; only when in control |
| **Right drop targets** | High rebound to center; unpredictable | Plan for chaotic returns; trap immediately |
| **Spinner orbit** | Fast ball return; SDTM if not positioned | Trap after spinner shots on upper flipper |
| **Center shots** | Pop bumper chaos; ends in drain | Avoid unless ball is safely fed from orbit |

### Known Drain Paths

Frontier is documented as a **"mean drainer"** with sweeping target shots and treacherous rebounds. The machine has a reputation for punishing missed targets and imprecise flipper work.

**Safe Control Positions:**
- **Upper flipper trap:** Best for planning next shot
- **Lower flipper cradle:** Setup for gentle shots to right side
- **Post pass/live catch:** Transfer between flippers safely after orbit returns

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Ball (Ball 1)

1. **Plunge safely:** Target right-side feeds or gentle left orbit to avoid center chaos
2. **Build A-B-C set:** Complete lane rollovers carefully (1st set = 30K at base)
3. **Hit first left drop target:** Opens gate; lights spinner
4. **Spinner shot:** Accumulate ~30K while lit
5. **Conservative play:** Don't chase risky drop targets yet

**Target Score:** 50K–150K

### Mid Ball (Ball 2)

1. **Hit second left drop target:** Advances Frontier multiplier to 2×
2. **Complete A-B-C again:** Now worth 30K × 2× = 60K
3. **Light right 3-bank:** Start advancing Predators multiplier
4. **Build Den of Predators:** Aim for 2–3× Predators multiplier
5. **Collect Frontier bonus** if safe: 60K × 2× = 120K via top-right saucer

**Target Score:** 150K–350K

### Late Ball (Ball 3+)

1. **Hit third left drop target:** Advances Frontier to 3×
2. **Maximize right 3-bank:** Reach 3–4× Predators multiplier
3. **Complete second A-B-C set** if possible: For Den of Predators second-set animals
4. **End-of-ball bonus strategy:**
   - Collect Frontier if multiplier ≥ 2× (at top-right saucer)
   - Predators bonus auto-collects at end (no action needed); maximize its multiplier

**Target Score per ball:** 100K–250K

**Cumulative Target:** **500K–680K across 4 balls**

## 🎯 Shot Analysis

### The A-B-C Lane Sequence (Core Money Shot)

**Why it matters:** Each complete set (A → B → C) advances both Frontier bonus and Den of Predators bonus. Do this twice early.

**How to execute:**
- Lane rollovers are located in standard left, center, right positions
- Hit left lane → center lane → right lane in sequence
- Resets if you drain or if you hit them out of order
- Repeat as many times as game allows (up to 6 sets for max Frontier base of 60K)

### The Left Inline Drop Targets (Multiplier Gatekeepers)

**Why they matter:** Only way to build Frontier multiplier (2×, 3×, 4×). Essential for high scores.

**How to execute:**
- 1st drop: Lights spinner + opens Frontier Falls gate
- 2nd drop: Advances to 2× multiplier
- 3rd drop: Advances to 3× multiplier
- Aim low and straight; expect fast returns
- Trap carefully after hitting

### The Right Drop Target 3-Bank (Predators Multiplier)

**Why it matters:** Advances Den of Predators multiplier (up to 4×). Maximum potential: 90K × 4× = 360K end-of-ball.

**How to execute:**
- Right-side targets require controlled flipper shots
- Hit all 3 to advance multiplier one level
- Repeat 3 more times to reach 4× (if safe)
- Each completion increases value and multiplier step

### The Spinner (Secondary Money Shot)

**Why it matters:** Quick 30K when lit by first left drop target.

**How to execute:**
- After hitting first left drop target, spinner lights
- Feed ball smoothly into spinner orbit
- Quality shot accumulates ~30K total
- Trap on upper flipper after returns

### Frontier Falls Gate (Top-Right Saucer)

**Why it matters:** Only way to collect Frontier bonus during play. Gate stays open until you collect (then closes until first left drop lights it again).

**How to execute:**
- Hit first left drop target to open gate
- Build Frontier bonus and multiplier
- Aim ball into top-right saucer to collect
- Strategy: Wait until 2× or 3× multiplier before collecting (60K base × 2× = 120K)

## 💡 Bonus System Details

### Frontier Bonus Multiplier Growth

- **Default:** 1× (base 60K max = 60K collected)
- **After 2nd drop:** 2× multiplier (60K × 2 = 120K potential)
- **After 3rd drop:** 3× multiplier (60K × 3 = 180K potential)
- **After up-left standup:** 4× multiplier (60K × 4 = 240K potential)

**Multiplier persists** across balls until reset; use this to your advantage.

### Den of Predators Bonus Multiplier Growth

- **Default:** 1× (first 45K base)
- **After right 3-bank hits (1–3):** 2× (45K × 2 = 90K)
- **After right 3-bank hits (4–6):** 3× (90K × 3 = 270K)
- **After right 3-bank hits (7–9):** 4× (90K × 4 = 360K)

**Collected at end-of-ball only.** Multiplier also carries forward.

## 📋 Key Setup Notes

Frontier's reputation as a **"mean drainer"** comes from its sweeping target orbits and unpredictable rebounds. Success requires:

- **Precise flipper control:** Not aggressive, but accurate
- **Ball trapping discipline:** Cradle on upper flipper between shots
- **Conservative shot selection:** Only attempt risky targets when multiplier is secured
- **Lane rollover focus:** A-B-C sequences are your safest, most consistent money

The machine rewards **patience over aggression**. Build multipliers early; collect bonuses late.

---

## 📖 Strategic Principles

1. **Frontier bonus is collectible; Den bonus is automatic:** Don't panic if you miss collecting Frontier — it carries forward. Den of Predators auto-pays at end of ball.

2. **Multipliers are persistent:** Earn them early, let them compound across balls.

3. **A-B-C lanes are your foundation:** Complete them at least twice for extra ball. They feed both bonuses.

4. **Right drop targets are secondary but valuable:** Only pursue them if you're not under drain pressure; they advance Predators multiplier.

5. **Play defense first:** Frontier punishes mistakes. A 500K safe score beats a 300K attempt with a drain.
