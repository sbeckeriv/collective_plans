# 🎡 Wheel of Fortune (Stern, 2007) - Strategy Guide

*Stern Pinball, 2007 — SAM Platform*

> **"Spin the wheel. Buy a vowel. Don't go bankrupt before multiball."**

Wheel of Fortune (Stern, 2007) is a SAM-platform machine based on the long-running TV game show, featuring an actual spinning wheel toy on the playfield, letter-reveal progression driven by ramp and orbit shots, Vanna White and Pat Sajak voice call-outs, and multiple puzzle-themed multiballs. The game maps the TV show format onto pinball mechanics: spin the wheel for point categories, complete letter shots to reveal the puzzle, and collect the bonus when you "solve." Our goal: **200M–700M** by spinning the wheel efficiently, solving letter puzzles quickly, and triggering the Bonus Round Multiball.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Shoot the right ramp** to spin the Wheel of Fortune toy — this determines the letter value category
- [ ] **Hit the letter shots** (left ramp, orbits, standup targets) to reveal puzzle letters
- [ ] **Collect revealed letters** by shooting the center scoop (the "solve" shot)
- [ ] **Avoid BANKRUPT** — certain wheel positions wipe out your accumulated letter value
- [ ] **Lock balls** in the puzzle board lock for Bonus Round Multiball
- [ ] **Listen to Pat Sajak's prompts** — they announce the active category and spin result

## 🎡 Core Mechanics

### Skill Shot

| Skill Shot | How | Reward |
|---|---|---|
| **Wheel spin** | Time plunge to right ramp entrance | Wheel spin + bonus multiplier |
| **Letter lane** | Partial plunge to top lane | Letter reveal |

### Spinning Wheel (Right Ramp)

The physical wheel toy on the playfield spins when the right ramp is shot:
- Each right ramp shot = wheel spin
- Wheel lands on a dollar value category (1,000 / 2,500 / 5,000 / 10,000 / FREE SPIN / BANKRUPT / LOSE A TURN)
- **Dollar value:** Sets the per-letter score for the current reveal sequence
- **BANKRUPT:** Resets accumulated letter value pool to zero
- **FREE SPIN:** Extra spin without penalty; wheel spins again
- **LOSE A TURN:** Minor penalty; next shot doesn't count for letters
- Aim to avoid BANKRUPT sections — right ramp power affects wheel landing zone

### Letter Shots (Puzzle Reveal)

After the wheel spins, letter shots reveal puzzle board letters:
- Each letter shot: reveals one letter at the current dollar value
- **Shot types:** Left ramp, left orbit, right orbit, standup target banks
- Letters accumulate value based on wheel position when revealed
- **Consonant shots:** Left orbit, right orbit, standup targets
- **Vowel shots:** Left ramp (vowels cost the accumulated value pool — timing matters)

### Center Scoop (Solve)

The center scoop represents solving the puzzle:
- When the puzzle board shows enough letters to solve: center scoop = SOLVE award
- Solve award = all revealed letter values × current bonus multiplier
- Solving quickly (fewer reveals needed) increases the solve bonus
- **Wrong solve attempt:** Penalty — brief ball saver, mode reset

### BANKRUPT Avoidance

BANKRUPT is the game's primary risk mechanic:
- Lost accumulated letter value is gone
- **Wheel spin control:** Softer right ramp shots tend to land the wheel at lower-value but safer sections; harder shots spin more and risk the BANKRUPT/LOSE A TURN zones
- Many players learn a consistent ramp power that lands reliably on the higher-dollar zones

### Bonus Multiplier (Inlanes)

Standard inlane completion builds the bonus multiplier:
- Left/right inlane shots advance the multiplier
- Multiplier applies to the solve bonus — max multiplier before solving is critical

## 🎳 Multiball

### Bonus Round Multiball

**How to start:** Lock 2 balls in the puzzle board lock (left scoop VUK lock shots) → 3-ball multiball.

**Jackpot structure:**
- 3-ball multiball with all letter shots lit
- Each letter shot during multiball = elevated letter value (wheel position before multiball × 2)
- **Super Jackpot (Big Win):** Solve the puzzle during multiball → center scoop → 50M–300M+
- BANKRUPT is still active during multiball — wheel spin risk remains

### Toss Up Multiball (Mini)

A brief 2-ball multiball triggered by completing the TOSS UP letter sequence (right orbit × 3):
- Lower value but fast to trigger
- Good for a quick score bump mid-game

## 🔄 Progression Systems

- **Puzzle solving:** Each solve unlocks a new puzzle category (BEFORE & AFTER, PHRASE, PROPER NAME, etc.) with different letter distributions
- **Round advancement:** Solving 3 puzzles in sequence advances the round bonus (scales from 10M → 100M)
- **Extra balls:** At round 3 completion and by hitting specific wheel positions consecutively

## 💡 Pro Tips

> **Control your wheel spin power.** Experienced players develop a consistent right ramp power that places the wheel in the 5,000+ section reliably, avoiding the BANKRUPT and LOSE A TURN zones. Practice this specific power level.

> **Solve early and often.** The solve bonus multiplier matters more than accumulating many letters. Solving at 5 letters revealed × 3× multiplier often beats solving at 12 letters × 1× multiplier. Solve when you can.

> **BANKRUPT stings but isn't fatal.** The game continues after BANKRUPT — you lose the pool, not the ball. Immediately go back to the wheel spin and restart the accumulation cycle.

> **Vowels cost your pool.** Shooting the left ramp (vowel shot) deducts from your accumulated value pool just like on the show. Don't shoot vowels when your pool is low — use consonant shots first.
