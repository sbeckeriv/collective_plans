# 🕵️‍♀️ Mata Hari - Strategy Guide

*Bally, 1977/78 — Designed by Jim Patla*

> **"No super bonus. No collect saucer. Just build it and bank it."**

Mata Hari was the 9th most-produced pinball machine ever made (16,260 units), and its appeal lies in clean, elegant simplicity: there's no super bonus or mid-game bonus collect — you build your bonus all game and it pays out automatically when the ball ends. The top kickout hole is your central engine, progressively unlocking outlane safety and your bonus multiplier. Our goal: max the kickout hole's 3-stage sequence for 5X bonus, then choose your best repeatable shot.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Understand there's no bonus collect** — you build bonus all game and it pays out automatically at end of ball
- [ ] **Shoot up through the center to the top kickout hole** repeatedly — this is your primary early-game engine
- [ ] **1st kickout hit:** 3,000 points, +3 bonus places, lights **2X multiplier**, lights **left outlane for 50,000**
- [ ] **2nd kickout hit:** lights **right outlane for 50,000** and **3X multiplier**
- [ ] **3rd kickout hit:** lights **5X multiplier**
- [ ] **Once at 5X, switch strategy**: choose between drop targets or the A-B orbits — favor orbits if your shots often go all the way around (scoring both sides)
- [ ] **Knocking down all drop targets** scores **50,000** and lights the **target special light**

## 🕵️ Core Mechanics

### Top Kickout Hole (Your Bonus Engine)

| Hit # | Reward |
|---|---|
| 1st | 3,000 points + 3 bonus places + lights **2X multiplier** + lights **left outlane for 50,000** |
| 2nd | Lights **right outlane for 50,000** + **3X multiplier** |
| 3rd | Lights **5X multiplier (max)** |

> 🎯 **Pro Tip — Core Strategy:** Shoot up through the center to the top saucer **repeatedly until your bonus multiplier hits 5X** — as long as the kickout feed is consistently safe on your specific machine, this is unambiguously your best early-game shot.

### Drop Targets

Two 4-bank drop target sets. **Knocking down all drop targets** scores **50,000 points** and lights the **target Special light**.

### A-B Orbits

Once your multiplier is maxed, the **A-B orbits** become a strong alternative — particularly valuable if your shots tend to go **all the way around**, since that scores **both** orbit sides in a single shot.

> 💰 **Pro Tip — Post-5X Decision:** After maxing your multiplier, choose between drop targets and A-B orbits based on which shot is more naturally reliable on your specific machine. If orbits commonly complete the full loop for you, they're generally the better choice since you're effectively getting two scoring events per shot.

### Bonus System

- **Base bonus** climbs to **29,000**
- **Maximum overall bonus**: 29,000 × 5X = **145,000**
- **No super bonus, no mid-game collect** — everything banks automatically at end of ball

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **Pop bumper unpredictability** (4 bumpers) | Standard late-70s bumper chaos | Treat as incidental scoring |
| **Unreliable kickout feed on some machines** | If your specific machine's kickout doesn't feed safely, repeated shots become riskier | Adjust your shot frequency based on how consistently safe your machine's kickout return is |
| **Drop target rebounds** | Standard EM/early-SS-era unpredictability | Approach with controlled, deliberate shots |

---

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Ball

1. Shoot the top kickout hole repeatedly to progress through 2X → 3X → 5X
2. Pick up drop target value incidentally along the way
3. Take advantage of newly-lit outlane safety as you progress

### Mid Ball

1. Once at 5X, switch to whichever of drop targets or A-B orbits is more reliable on your machine
2. Continue building toward clearing both drop target banks for the 50,000 Special light
3. Keep working orbits if they consistently complete the full loop

### Late Ball

1. Maximize whichever repeatable shot you settled on post-5X
2. Clear remaining drop targets for the Special light if not already done
3. Let your built bonus bank automatically at end of ball — no need to seek out a collect shot

## ⚙️ Setup Recommendations

| Setting | Recommendation | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| **Playfield pitch** | 6.0°–6.5° | Standard for late-70s Bally solid-state machines |
| **Top kickout hole feed** | Verify safe, consistent return | Central to the entire early-game multiplier-building strategy |
| **Drop target reset (both banks)** | Check for clean, reliable resets | Needed for the all-targets-down Special light |

---

## 📋 Quick Reference: Strategic Priorities

**Early:** Repeatedly shoot the top kickout hole through 2X → 3X → 5X.
**Mid:** Switch to drop targets or A-B orbits based on which is more reliable.
**Late:** Maximize your chosen repeatable shot, clear drop targets, let bonus bank automatically.

**Target Outcome:** Reaching 5X bonus multiplier and maxing base bonus toward 29,000 (145,000 total) represents a strong, well-played game on this historically significant, hugely popular classic.
