# 🛼 Rollergames - Strategy Guide

*Williams, 1990 — Designed by Steve Ritchie*

> **"Survive the rink. Hit the magnet. Keep rolling."**

Rollergames is a roller derby-themed Williams WPC machine with 3 flippers, a ball-catching magnet near the upper flipper, and a unique two-stage kickback in the left outlane. The game is built around lighting multiball, exploiting the magnet for trick shots, and the outlane rescue system that turns would-be drains into scoring opportunities. Our goal: **100M–250M** through disciplined multiball play and smart magnet use.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Light the left outlane kickback** early — it fires a drained ball back up a mini-ramp into play
- [ ] **Shoot the upper right shots** toward the magnet — it catches the ball and you choose where to redirect
- [ ] **Build your multiball lock** by completing the required sequences
- [ ] **Use the third (upper left) flipper** to access upper-playfield shots
- [ ] **Exploit the magnet strategically** — caught balls can be held then released for precision shots
- [ ] **Combo ramp shots** for escalating combo bonuses

## 🛹 Core Mechanics

### Skill Shot

| Skill Shot | How | Reward |
|---|---|---|
| **Standard** | Full plunge to upper lanes | Lane completion + bonus |
| **Controlled plunge** | Short plunge to hit lit lane | Specific feature light |

### Three Flippers

Rollergames has 3 flippers:
- **Lower left + lower right** — standard play
- **Upper left** — accesses upper playfield shots including the magnet zone

The upper left flipper fires the ball toward the upper right area where the magnet sits.

### Ball-Catching Magnet (Upper Right)

When a ball enters the magnet zone (upper right), the magnet catches it:
- The ball hovers at the magnet
- **Press the upper flipper button** to release it toward the left scoop / lock area
- **Press no button** and the magnet releases after a timeout, returning the ball to the playfield

The magnet is the game's signature mechanic — it effectively gives you a precision "second shot" mid-ball.

### Left Outlane Kickback (Two-Stage)

The left outlane has a unique two-stage rescue:
1. Ball drains into left outlane
2. Kickback fires ball up a short mini-ramp
3. A second kicker (not otherwise in play) catches it and sends it around the left orbit
4. Ball arrives at the upper right flipper for a fresh shot

**Light it early** — the kickback requires activation (via specific shots or rollover completion) and can rescue multiple balls per ball.

### Combo Shots

Consecutive ramp shots (left orbit → right ramp, or vice versa) trigger combo bonuses:
- 2-combo: 2× shot value
- 3-combo: 3× shot value
- 4+ combo: escalating multiplier

The combo multiplier resets if you break the sequence.

## 🎳 Multiball

### Rollergames Multiball

**How to start:** Complete the ball lock sequence (shoot the lit scoop/target set to lock balls).

**Jackpot structure:**
- 3-ball multiball
- Jackpots via the primary ramp + scoop shots
- **Super Jackpot:** Complete all jackpot shots in sequence → shoot the magnet zone → magnet releases into the super jackpot scoop — worth 3–5× a regular jackpot
- Combo chains during multiball compound all jackpots

> 🏆 **Magnet in multiball:** The magnet catches one of your multiball balls and holds it — you can delay its release while the other two balls score, then time the release for a clean super jackpot shot.

## 💡 Pro Tips

> **The magnet is a time-buying tool.** Catch a ball when two others are running hot — then release it cleanly toward the super jackpot shot instead of just letting all three drain chaotically.

> **The left outlane kickback is worth lighting every ball.** It requires effort to activate but the rescue can save a ball mid-high-value multiball — huge ROI.

> **Combo chains beat raw shot count.** Four consecutive shots at 4× beats eight uncomboed shots at 1× — plan your routes.
