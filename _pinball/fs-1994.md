# 🦴 The Flintstones - Strategy Guide

*Williams, 1994 — WPC Era*

> **"Yabba Dabba Doo! Now hit those drop targets."**

The Flintstones (Williams, 1994) is a WPC machine based on the classic animated series featuring Fred, Wilma, Barney, Betty, Pebbles, and Bamm-Bamm. The game has ramps, a Dino toy, character modes, and a multiball centered around Bedrock's big events. Our goal: **300M–700M** through character mode completion, Bedrock Multiball, and efficient ramp/loop combos.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Shoot the left ramp** to advance the Bedrock storyline (primary progression)
- [ ] **Hit the Dino toy** for Dino bonuses and mode advance
- [ ] **Start character modes** via the center scoop (Fred, Barney, Wilma, Betty)
- [ ] **Complete a full Bedrock story** to light multiball qualification
- [ ] **Lock balls** to start Bedrock Party Multiball
- [ ] **Use the right orbit** for back-door character progression

---

## 🏠 Core Mechanics

### Skill Shot

| Skill Shot | How | Reward |
|---|---|---|
| **Left ramp** | Time plunge for left ramp entry | Story advance + points |
| **Standard** | Full plunge | Ball to pop bumpers |

### Bedrock Story (Left Ramp)

The left ramp is the primary story advance shot. Each left ramp completion:
- Moves the story forward (6–8 shots per story arc)
- Lights characters for their modes
- Eventually qualifies the Bedrock Party multiball

### Character Modes (4)

Each character has a mode accessed via the center scoop when the character is lit:

| Character | Mode Theme | Key Mechanic |
|---|---|---|
| **Fred Flintstone** | Bowling — hit the bowling lane targets | Left + right standup combo |
| **Barney Rubble** | Water buffalo lodge — timed targets | Center standups |
| **Wilma** | Shopping — ramp completion combo | Left ramp × 3 |
| **Betty** | Knitting — orbit shots | Right orbit × 3 |

Each completed character mode: 20M–75M + story advance.

### Dino Toy

The Dino toy on the right side activates when lit:
- Hit Dino for bonus points and story advance
- During modes, Dino awards a character-specific bonus
- Dino chases the ball when activated (visual effect, not physical chase)

### Right Orbit (Backdoor)

The right orbit is a fast lane that:
- Advances Betty's mode
- Feeds the bumper cluster for passive scoring
- Loops into the left side for combo potential

---

## 🎳 Multiball

### Bedrock Party Multiball

**How to start:** Complete 2 story arcs via the left ramp → lock 2 balls in the center hole.

**Jackpot structure:**
- 3-ball multiball
- Jackpots via left ramp + Dino toy
- **Super Jackpot:** Complete all 4 character jackpots during multiball → center scoop → 50M–150M+

### Pebbles & Bamm-Bamm Multiball (Mini)

Triggered by completing Pebbles + Bamm-Bamm inserts (pop bumper accumulation). Brief 2-ball frenzy.

## 📊 Shot Analysis

| Shot | Value | Risk |
|---|---|---|
| Left ramp | Story advance (primary) | Low |
| Center scoop | Character mode + lock | Low |
| Dino toy (right) | Bonus + story | Low |
| Right orbit | Betty + combo | Low |
| Left standup | Fred's bowling | Low |
| Pop bumpers | Passive + P&B multiball | None |

### Dangerous Areas

- **Right outlane** — fast after Dino approach; the primary drain risk
- **Post-bumper exit** — the bumper cluster redirects unpredictably

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Ball 1
1. Left ramp × 4 (story advance to character lights)
2. Center scoop → Fred's bowling mode
3. Dino toy bonus

### Ball 2
1. Continue story (left ramp × 3 more)
2. Wilma shopping mode (left ramp combo)
3. Lock ball 1

### Ball 3
1. Lock ball 2 → Bedrock Party Multiball
2. Left ramp jackpots during multiball
3. All 4 character jackpots → super jackpot

### Score Targets

| Performance | Score |
|---|---|
| Beginner | 40M–120M |
| Solid | 150M–350M |
| Strong (all 4 modes + MB) | 400M–700M |
| Elite | 700M+ |

---

## 💡 Pro Tips

> **Left ramp is king.** It advances the story, qualifies multiball, feeds Wilma's mode, and is the safest primary shot on the machine. Shoot it constantly.

> **Character modes stack progress.** Start a character mode before shooting the left ramp — ramp shots during an active mode count toward both the mode AND story progression.

> **Dino is easy money.** The toy activates frequently and is a simple direct shot. Hit it every time it's lit — it's one of the highest value-per-risk shots on the machine.
