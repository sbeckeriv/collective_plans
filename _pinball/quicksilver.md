# ⚡ QUICKSILVER Pinball Machine - Strategy Guide

*Stern Electronics, 1980 — Early Solid-State Fast-Play*

> **Ball time is money. Keep the flipper busy.**

Quicksilver is a "chop wood" machine where your score correlates directly with playtime and flipper activity. No modes, no multiballs, no complexity—just straightforward drop targets, spinners, and bonus multipliers. Our goal: reach **2–4 million points**, a realistic competitive score on a well-set machine.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Prioritize the right 3-bank:** Hitting it advances your bonus multiplier toward 5X
- [ ] **Manage spinners carefully:** Return lane lights them, but they time out in seconds
- [ ] **Build bonus first:** Top lanes and standup targets advance base bonus to 75K max
- [ ] **Avoid center target rebounds:** They bounce unpredictably—control your angle
- [ ] **Watch left spinner angles:** Balls only halfway up fall back and drain left
- [ ] **Chase the saucer:** Center 4-bank raises its value and spots letters

## 🎱 Core Mechanics

### Drop Target Banks (3 Total)

| Location | Count | Purpose | Base Value |
|----------|-------|---------|------------|
| **Right side** | 3-bank | Bonus multiplier advancement | 25K per completion |
| **Center** | 4-bank | Saucer value builder + letter spotter | Varies |
| **Playfield** | 7 standup targets | Bonus advancement | 5K–10K (varies by position) |

**Right 3-Bank Rule:** Completing this bank is your top priority. Each completion scores **25,000 points** and advances your bonus multiplier toward the maximum of 5X. This is where your best shot value comes from.

### Bonus System

**Base Bonus:** Starts at 0K.
- Top lanes: Advance bonus 5K each
- Standup targets: Advance bonus 5K–10K each
- Maximum base bonus: **75K**

**Bonus Multiplier:** Earned by completing the right side 3-bank drop targets.
- 1st completion: 2X multiplier
- 2nd completion: 3X multiplier
- 3rd completion: 4X multiplier
- 4th completion: 5X multiplier (max)
- **Total possible bonus:** 75K × 5X = **375,000 points** <kcite ref="1"/>

**Bonus Collection:** Bonus is awarded at end of ball.

### Spinners (Time-Limited)

Three spinners on playfield:
- **Left spinner** (activated by right return lane)
- **Right spinner** (activated by left return lane)
- **Center spinner** (general playfield)

**Critical mechanic:** Return lanes light the *opposite* spinner, but it times out after a few seconds. You must shoot the lit spinner quickly or it goes dark again <kcite ref="2"/>.

### Center 4-Bank & Saucer

The center target 4-bank raises the value of the saucer (located left of center). Hitting targets here also spots you a letter toward QUICKSILVER. The saucer is your ball-control kickout—use it to reset position and catch the ball safely.

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

### High-Risk Areas

| Shot | Drain Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|-----------|
| **Right spinner entry** | Fast balls center drain; slow drain via center targets | Aim for upper flipper control; trap before shooting |
| **Left spinner (partial ascent)** | Ball only halfway up falls back and drains left | Soft touch; only shoot when you have clean control |
| **Center 4-bank rebounds** | Unpredictable bounces off tight target spacing | Play defensively; use saucer for reset |
| **Post hits on lower bank** | Poorly timed shot at lower drop targets drains immediately | Perfect contact needed; sweeping motion is safer |

### Safe Ball Control

- **Trap on flippers:** Hold ball on upper flipper when possible to plan next shot
- **Saucer kickout:** Use center saucer to safely regain control and reset
- **Gentle top lane shots:** Move ball up top rather than attacking center

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Ball (Ball 1)

1. **Build base bonus first** — hit top lanes and standup targets (goal: 50K+)
2. **Attempt right 3-bank** if you have control — this lights multiplier advancement
3. **Avoid risky center play** — early drain costs you multiplier progress
4. **Light spinners opportunistically** — if return lane shot is safe, use it

**Target Score:** 300K–800K

### Mid Ball (Ball 2)

1. **Complete the right 3-bank if not done** — activate 2X multiplier
2. **Continue building bonus** via top lanes and standup targets (goal: 75K)
3. **Advance multiplier again** if ball control is solid (aim for 3X or higher)
4. **Prioritize spinner shots** when lanes light them — quick execution before timeout

**Target Score:** 800K–2M

### Late Ball (Ball 3+)

1. **Maximize multiplier** — push for 4X or 5X via additional right 3-bank hits
2. **Build bonus to maximum** (75K) if you haven't already
3. **Collect bonus at end of ball** — with 5X multiplier, this yields 375K automatically
4. **Chase extra balls** if available (game-dependent)

**Target Score per ball:** 500K–1.5M

## 🎯 Shot Analysis

### The Right 3-Bank (Your Primary Target)

**Why it matters:** Each completion advances bonus multiplier and scores 25K directly. This is where your escalating score comes from.

**How to execute:**
- Shoot from left flipper toward right side drop targets
- Sweeping motion can take down multiple targets in one shot
- Control ball trajectory to avoid center bounces
- Prioritize this on Balls 2–3 when multiplier impact is greatest

### Top Lanes & Spinners (Bonus Builders)

**Why they matter:** Safe way to advance bonus without center risk. Spinners are valuable but time-limited.

**How to execute:**
- Right return lane lights left spinner → shoot it immediately
- Left return lane lights right spinner → shoot it immediately
- Only attempt spinner shots when you have solid ball control
- Once spinners time out, move to next target

### The Saucer (Control & Setup)

**Why it matters:** Ball-control feature that raises its own value and spots letters. Allows you to reset position safely.

**How to execute:**
- Use saucer kickout to regain control between risky shots
- Hit center 4-bank targets to raise saucer value before collecting
- Don't over-shoot saucer; it's for positioning, not scoring

### Standup Targets (Secondary Bonus)

**Why they matter:** Advance bonus safely without targeting specific banks. Good defensive play.

**How to execute:**
- Hit them opportunistically during general playfield play
- Not a primary target, but each hit adds to bonus
- Safe compared to center target bank bounces

## 📈 Strategic Progression Summary

**Low-Multiplier Balls (1–2X):**
- Build base bonus aggressively
- Complete right 3-bank for first multiplier
- Avoid risky center shots

**Mid-Multiplier Balls (2–3X):**
- Aim for 3X or 4X multiplier
- Continue bonus building toward 75K
- Execute spinner shots when lanes light them

**High-Multiplier Balls (4–5X):**
- Maintain 5X multiplier if achieved
- Collect full 375K bonus at ball end
- Play safely to avoid losing high multiplier via drain
