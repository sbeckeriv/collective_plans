# 💍 Lord of the Rings - Strategy Guide

*Stern Pinball, 2003 — Designed by George Gomez*

> **"One does not simply walk into Valinor."**

Lord of the Rings is widely regarded as having one of the hardest wizard modes Stern ever shipped — Valinor Multiball is reportedly second only to The Simpsons Pinball Party in difficulty. Getting there requires completing all three movie multiballs, all six Ring Modes (There and Back Again), every Gift of the Elves, AND Destroying the Ring — in that specific order. Our goal: build toward this full checklist methodically, since no single shortcut exists.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Collect rings from each character's specific shot**: Pippin (left loop), Merry (right loop), Sam (left spot target), Aragorn (right ramp), Frodo (center ramp), Gandalf (center loop), Legolas (left ramp), Gimli (kickout hole under pop bumpers), Boromir (pop bumpers)
- [ ] **Start all three movie multiballs** — Fellowship of the Ring, Two Towers, and Return of the King — to light **Destroy the Ring**
- [ ] **Destroy the Ring requires 6 shots**: the first 4 (Legolas, Gandalf, Aragorn, Merry, in any order) followed by **2 shots to the Ring itself**
- [ ] **Destroy the Ring must be completed LAST**, after everything else on the Valinor checklist
- [ ] **Don't expect a quick wizard mode run** — this game's design intentionally makes Valinor one of the hardest achievements in pinball

---

## 💍 Core Mechanics

### Character Ring Collection

Each character has a dedicated shot that awards their ring:

| Character | Shot |
|---|---|
| Pippin | Left loop |
| Merry | Right loop |
| Sam | Left spot target |
| Aragorn | Right ramp |
| Frodo | Center ramp |
| Gandalf | Center loop |
| Legolas | Left ramp |
| Gimli | Kickout hole under pop bumpers |
| Boromir | Pop bumpers |

### Three Movie Multiballs

| Multiball | Theme |
|---|---|
| **Fellowship Multiball** | The Fellowship of the Ring |
| **Two Towers Multiball** | The Two Towers |
| **Return of the King Multiball** | Return of the King |

There's also a separate multiball mode themed around **Gollum**.

### Destroy the Ring (Mode)

1. **Requirement:** Start all three movie multiballs
2. **Sequence:** Shoot Legolas, Gandalf, Aragorn, and Merry (any order)
3. **Finish:** 2 shots to the Ring itself

---

## 🏆 The Valinor Checklist (Genuinely One of Pinball's Hardest Wizard Modes)

To unlock **Valinor**, you must complete ALL of the following:

1. ✅ Complete all **three movie multiballs**
2. ✅ Achieve **"There and Back Again"** (a sub-wizard mode earned by playing through all **six Ring Modes**)
3. ✅ Collect **all the Gifts of the Elves**
4. ✅ **Destroy the Ring** — and this must be done **after** everything else above

> ⚠️ **Honest assessment:** This checklist is explicitly designed to be one of the hardest in pinball, second only to The Simpsons Pinball Party's wizard mode by most accounts. Don't expect to reach Valinor through casual play — it requires sustained mastery across the entire ruleset, likely across many games before your first success.

---

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **Forcing the full Destroy the Ring sequence under pressure** | A 6-shot sequence with a specific structure — rushing increases miss risk | Take it methodically; there's no rush once you've lit it |
| **Pop bumper-routed character shots (Gimli, Boromir)** | Less predictable than direct ramp/loop shots | Treat these as semi-passive collection rather than forcing them |
| **General difficulty of reaching late-game objectives** | The further you get, the more precision is demanded | Pace yourself — build toward the checklist over multiple balls rather than rushing |

---

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Game (Ball 1)

1. Start collecting character rings from their respective shots
2. Begin building toward your first movie multiball
3. Work on early Ring Modes as they become available

**Target Score:** Build steadily — this is a deep, checklist-driven ruleset

### Mid Game (Ball 2)

1. Complete additional movie multiballs
2. Continue working through the six Ring Modes toward "There and Back Again"
3. Collect Gifts of the Elves as they appear

### Late Game (Ball 3+)

1. If all three movie multiballs are complete, light and run Destroy the Ring
2. Complete Destroy the Ring only after the rest of the Valinor checklist is satisfied
3. If you've genuinely completed everything, push for Valinor itself

---

## ⚙️ Setup Recommendations

| Setting | Recommendation | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| **Playfield pitch** | 6.5°–7.0° | Stern factory standard for early-2000s Whitestar-era machines |
| **Balrog LED update** | Verify factory-installed or apply if missing | A known manufacturer update for later production runs |
| **Pop bumper calibration** | Check consistent registration | Affects Gimli/Boromir ring collection reliability |

---

## 📋 Quick Reference: Strategic Priorities

**Ball 1:** Start collecting character rings, begin a movie multiball.
**Ball 2:** Complete more multiballs, work through Ring Modes toward There and Back Again.
**Ball 3+:** Finish the Valinor checklist in order, save Destroy the Ring for last.

**Target High-Score Ballpark:** Simply completing one movie multiball and a few Ring Modes represents solid progress; reaching Valinor itself is a genuine, rare achievement most players will work toward across many games.
