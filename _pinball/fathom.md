# 🧜 Fathom - Strategy Guide

*Bally, 1981 — Designed by Ward Pemberton*

> **"Free the mermaids. Spin for points."**

Fathom's central conceit is capturing two mermaids (the balls) in two caves, then freeing them via in-line drop targets to build separate blue and green bonus tracks — each maxing at 55,000 with a 5x multiplier, for 550,000 combined. But the real point engine is the spinner, not the bonus. Our goal: light the spinner for 5,000/spin and grind it relentlessly, while building both bonus colors toward their multiplied maximum.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Capture mermaids in the two cave holes**, then free them by shooting the **in-line drop targets** in front of each hole
- [ ] **Green drop targets raise the green bonus multiplier; blue drop targets raise the blue bonus multiplier** — keep them separate in your mind
- [ ] **The spinner is the biggest point source in the game** — far more than bonus — so prioritize lighting and grinding it
- [ ] **Unlit spinner = 500/spin; lit spinner = 5,000/spin** — a 10x difference, so always check whether it's lit before deciding your shot priority
- [ ] **Push both bonus colors toward their 55,000 cap with a 5x multiplier** for up to 550,000 combined — a meaningful secondary scoring source
- [ ] **Skilled players can reach 5x bonus and a 2/3-ball mode** — this is the ceiling to aim for

---

## 🧜‍♀️ Core Mechanics

### Mermaid Capture & Release

1. Capture a mermaid (ball) in one of the **two cave kick-out holes**
2. Free it by shooting the **3-in-line drop targets** positioned in front of that hole
3. Each color's drop target bank raises that **color's specific bonus multiplier**

| Drop Target Bank | Affects |
|---|---|
| Green | Green bonus multiplier |
| Blue | Blue bonus multiplier |

### Bonus System (Two Separate Colors)

- **Base bonus** for both blue and green climbs to **55,000** each — built cumulatively from **1K through 10K lights**, all of which stack together
- Each color can be multiplied up to **5x**
- **Maximum possible:** 55,000 × 5 = **275,000 per color**, or **550,000 combined** across both

### The Spinner (Your Real Scoring Engine)

| State | Value |
|---|---|
| Unlit | 500 per spin |
| **Lit** | **5,000 per spin** |

> 💰 **Pro Tip — Core Strategy:** The spinner outpaces bonus as the dominant point source in this game. Once it's lit, grinding the spinner repeatedly is almost always your best use of a shot — don't let bonus-building distract you from keeping it lit and spinning.

### Multiball

A **3-ball multiball** is available — tied into the cave/mermaid capture system. Skilled players who can manage both bonus colors and drop target banks efficiently can work toward a 5x bonus state alongside a 2- or 3-ball mode.

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **Crossover return lanes** | Left and right crossovers can feed the ball unpredictably | Stay ready on the flippers immediately after any crossover feed |
| **Pop bumper unpredictability** | Standard early-80s bumper chaos | Treat as incidental scoring, not a shot to actively chase |
| **Over-prioritizing bonus over the spinner** | Bonus is valuable but secondary — chasing it exclusively leaves points on the table | Always check spinner lit-status before committing to a bonus-building shot instead |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Ball

1. Start capturing mermaids and freeing them via the in-line drop targets
2. Build both green and blue bonus multipliers as you go
3. Look for opportunities to light the spinner early

### Mid Ball

1. Once the spinner is lit, prioritize grinding it over almost everything else
2. Continue building both bonus colors toward their 55K cap
3. Work toward the 3-ball multiball via continued mermaid capture/release cycles

### Late Ball

1. Maximize spinner grinding time while it remains lit
2. Push for 5x on both bonus colors if you haven't already
3. Bank your combined bonus (up to 550,000) at end of ball

## ⚙️ Setup Recommendations

| Setting | Recommendation | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| **Playfield pitch** | 6.0°–6.5° | Standard for early-80s Bally solid-state machines |
| **Spinner mechanism** | Verify smooth, free spinning | This is the dominant scoring shot in the entire game |
| **Drop target reset (both banks)** | Check for clean, reliable resets | Central to both the mermaid release mechanic and bonus multiplier building |

---

## 📋 Quick Reference: Strategic Priorities

**Early:** Capture/release mermaids, build both bonus colors, look for the lit spinner.
**Mid:** Grind the spinner once lit, keep building bonus, work toward multiball.
**Late:** Maximize spinner time, push for 5x on both colors, bank combined bonus.

**Target High-Score Ballpark:** A game built around heavy spinner grinding combined with maxed 5x bonus on both colors (550,000 combined) represents a strong, well-played game on this classic underwater-themed machine.
