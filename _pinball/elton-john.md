# 🎹 ELTON JOHN Pinball Machine - Complete Strategy Guide
## *Path to 500+ Million Points*

> **"I'm Still Standing"**

Celebrating Elton John's 50+ year music career, this guide will help you master the stage and reach the coveted **500+ million point** milestone. With a lightning-fast layout and streamlined rules, Elton John rewards focused progression through songs, albums, and multiballs. <kcite ref="1"/>

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Ball 1:** Select a song at game start and focus on collecting wardrobe items
- [ ] **Shoot the right ramp** consistently to reach backstage and collect glasses/shoes/outfit
- [ ] **Collect albums** by hitting the center drop targets (rocka-box)
- [ ] **Build Rocket Fuel** by hitting spinner shots throughout gameplay
- [ ] **Trigger Crocodile Rock Multiball** by locking balls at the Tiny Dancer VUK
- [ ] **Collect at least 5 albums** to unlock encore modes
- [ ] **Spell ELTON JOHN** via stars or spot via skill shots to access Signature Stage multiballs
- [ ] **Stack multiballs** (Croc + Rocket = 2x scoring) for maximum points
- [ ] **Complete four milestones** to light Final Tour wizard mode

## 🎱 Skill Shots

Plunge timing is critical on Elton John. The skill shot target rotates downward from top to bottom and back, requiring a well-timed shot.

### Regular Skill Shots (5 Rotating Options)

| Skill Shot                | Points  | Award                                |
| ------------------------- | ------- | ------------------------------------ |
| **Big Points**            | 200,000 | Immediate points, base award         |
| **Bonus X**               | Varies  | Multiplier award for end-of-ball     |
| **Spot Star**             | Varies  | Progress toward ELTON JOHN spelling  |
| **Rocket Fuel**           | Varies  | 50 units toward Rocket Man Multiball |
| **Advance Crocodile MB**  | Varies  | Light or progress crocodile lock     |

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Choose "Spot Star" to gradually unlock Signature Stage multiballs without manually spelling the full ELTON JOHN letter set. <kcite ref="2"/>

### Super Skill Shots

Short plunges to either **backstage shot** (right ramp path) or **side ramp** that then hit a lit upper flipper target award enhanced bonuses with 2x+ value multipliers.

**Advanced Technique:** Full plunges that hit the center drop targets instantly award the next album without triggering skill shot—useful for certain strategic situations.

## 🎵 Songs System

At game start, select one of **15 available songs**. This is your only song selection opportunity per game. Each song features three difficulty levels and unique mechanics.

### Song Mechanics

| Element          | Details                                        |
| ---------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| **Difficulties** | Yellow (easiest) → Orange (medium) → Red      |
| **Note Shots**   | Make flashing note shots at the backstage      |
| **Wardrobe**     | Collect 3 items: glasses, shoes, outfit        |
| **Victory Lap**  | All shots worth 3M+ per shot after 3rd item   |

### Wardrobe Progression

Collecting wardrobe items activates cumulative bonuses:

1. **First Item (Glasses):** Activates **Double Song** (30 seconds, 2x all note shot values)
2. **Second Item (Shoes):** Grants **Double Notes** (2x progress toward completion for next 5 shots)
3. **Third Item (Outfit):** Unlocks **Victory Laps** (all backstage shots = 3M+ each, songs much easier to complete)

> 🎯 **Strategy Tip:** Complete wardrobe collection during songs to unlock maximum scoring potential. The second item's 2x progress is especially valuable. <kcite ref="2"/>

### Song List (15 available)
Includes: "Tiny Dancer," "Rocket Man," "Saturday Night's Alright (for Fighting)," "Honky Cat," and 11 others, each with distinct note-collection mechanics.

## 💿 Albums & Rocka-Box

Hit the **center drop target grid (nine targets)** to collect albums. Hitting the complete grid awards one album and resets.

### Album Progression

- **Albums 1-5:** Unlock the five Encore Modes (after collecting 5 albums)
- **Album #9:** Spots progress for any unlocked encore mode
- **Album #17:** Resets combo counter
- **Albums #6, #12, #18:** Activate timed **Frenzy Modes** (Party, Shuffle, Puzzle)

### Frenzy Modes
- **Duration:** ~30 seconds each
- **Mechanics:** Light yellow targets after hitting red targets (wrong targets)
- **Reward:** Extra Ball for completing lit target sequence

## 🎪 Encore Modes

After collecting **5 albums**, five specialized encore modes become available. These are 30-second challenges with distinct objectives.

| Encore Mode            | Qualification          | Objective                      | Notes                        |
| ---------------------- | ---------------------- | ------------------------------ | ---------------------------- |
| **Tiny Targets**       | 8 target hits          | Hit 8 standup targets          | Straightforward scoring      |
| **Super Bennie & Jets**| 6 orbit shots          | Complete 6 orbit combos        | Requires flipper control     |
| **Backstage Frenzy**   | 5 backstage shots      | Shoot backstage 5 times        | Single-shot focus            |
| **Super Spinners**     | 1000 spins cumulative  | Accumulate spinner rotations   | Builds throughout game       |
| **Piano-Matic**        | 5 piano ramp shots     | Hit piano ramp 5 times         | Orbital control              |

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Completing all five Encore Modes unlocks **Champ Multiball**, the highest-scoring standard multiball on the machine. <kcite ref="1"/>

## 🎰 Multiball Modes

Elton John features three primary multiballs that can stack together, plus Champ Multiball.

### Crocodile Rock Multiball

**Start:** Lock balls at the **Crocodile VUK** (Tiny Dancer target lock mechanism)

**Lock Requirements:**
- First multiball: 2 locks
- Subsequent multiballs: 3 locks per start

**Scoring:**
- **Jackpots:** Shoot flashing shots for base jackpot value
- **Super Jackpots:** Shoot the crocodile to collect (based on jackpots earned)
- **Quick Relocks:** Drain and relock within 10 seconds = 2x super jackpot value

**Milestone:** Collect **two super jackpots** (or one 2x super jackpot) to progress toward wizard modes.

> 🏆 **Strategic Note:** Crocodile can run simultaneously with Rocket Man Multiball for 2x jackpot scoring on both modes. <kcite ref="1"/>

---

### Rocket Man Multiball

**Start:** Accumulate **fuel** (shown on LED rail), then start at the rocket kicker

**Fuel Mechanics:**
- **First tank:** Requires 500 units
- **Subsequent tanks:** Requires 100 additional units per tank
- **Generation:** Spinner hits throughout normal gameplay add fuel units
- **Auto-qualify:** Starting with all fuel tanks loaded is a milestone achievement

**Fuel Perks (Unlock at thresholds):**
- **100 units:** Extra ball saver
- **200 units:** Easier super jackpot qualification
- **300 units:** +1 ball to Rocket Man Multiball
- **400 units:** Permanently light all Rocket jackpots

**Multiball Scoring:**
- Shoot 7 unique shots to light super jackpot at rocket kicker
- Subsequent super jackpots require additional jackpot collections
- Playfield multiplier scales with ball count

> 💡 **Stacking Note:** Rocket Man (2+ balls) + Crocodile Rock = 2x all jackpot values on both modes. Stack early and often. <kcite ref="1"/>

---

### Signature Stage Multiball

Start after spelling **ELTON JOHN** (via nine stars at major shots) or by spotting stars via skill shots. Lock balls at the **piano ramp**, then select one of four chronological tour variations via action button.

#### Tour Variations

| Tour              | Mechanics                                 | Strategy                     |
| ----------------- | ----------------------------------------- | ---------------------------- |
| **1970 Hollywood**| Combo-multiplied jackpots; 4M+ super      | Easier, more consistent EB   |
| **1975 LA**       | Time-decay super jackpots; limited lights | Moderate difficulty          |
| **2009 Las Vegas**| Shoot drop targets for jackpots           | Color-matching bonus element |
| **2023 London**   | Single lit shot rotating q10 sec; 10M SJ  | Most challenging, high risk  |

> 🎯 **Tour Selection:** Beginners should choose 1970 Hollywood or 1975 LA for guaranteed scoring. 2023 London offers higher risk/reward. <kcite ref="2"/>

**Milestone Progression:**
- Achieve **3x super jackpot combos** per tour for milestone progress
- Spell **SUPERSTAR!** by collecting letters across Signature Stage plays
- Play all four tours to unlock Super Stardom wizard mode

---

### Champ Multiball

**Start:** Automatically lit after completing all five Encore Modes

**Mechanics:**
- Shots from each encore mode retain their earned jackpot values throughout
- Playfield multiplier scales with ball count:
  - 5 balls = 2.5x multiplier
  - 4 balls = 2x multiplier
  - 3 balls = 1.5x multiplier
  - 2 balls = 1x multiplier

**Super Jackpots:**
- Escalate from 20M to 30M+ base values after qualifying via jackpot grid completion
- Combine encore-mode rules for massive scoring opportunities

## ⭐ Combos

Twenty-seven recognized **three-shot combos** are tracked. Examples include "The Classic" (N→J→T) and others based on playfield layout.

**Combo Mechanics:**
- First shot of combo lights the next-shot star in **yellow**
- Repeating shots after certain combos earn extended value that increases per repetition
- Completing unique combos unlocks progressive bonuses

**Milestones:**
- First unique combo completion
- Five unique combos light an **Extra Ball**

## 🔤 Stars & ELTON JOHN Spelling

Collect **stars** by shooting major shots (orbits, ramps, scoop, backstage, spinners, etc.) to progress toward spelling **ELTON JOHN**. This unlocks access to all four Signature Stage multiballs.

**Alternative Path:** Use skill shot "Spot Star" option to gradually spell the full name without manual shot completion.

**Star Bonus:** Completing the full ELTON JOHN set awards a **time-based bonus**—faster completion = larger point award.

---

## ⏱️ Honky Cat Hurry-Up & Kickback

**Activation:** Shoot the **left eject target** to light a hurry-up

**Mechanics:**
- Complete the lit shot from the left flipper within the time limit
- Qualification awards a **kickback** (outlane save)
- Up to **three kickbacks** can be reserved
- Failure to complete a hurry-up triggers a **compensation ball save**

**Milestone:** Collect three hurry-ups for milestone progress.

**Bonus Multiplier:** Increases by completing star sets. Can be held via certain album awards.

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Albums often award bonus multiplier holds, making accumulation strategic. <kcite ref="1"/>

## 🧙 Wizard Modes

### Super Stardom (Tour-Based Wizard Mode)

**Requirement:** Play all four Signature Stage multiballs

**Mechanics:**
- **Layout:** Nine color-coded star shots in a 3×3 grid
- **Objective:** Create tic-tac-toe patterns by shooting stars
- **Lock:** Hit drop targets to lock patterns via action button collection
- **Bonuses:** Match patterns for multipliers or meter perks

**Scoring:** Substantial, scaling with pattern complexity.

---

### Final Tour (Primary Wizard Mode)

**Requirement:** Complete four or more milestones (enables with any four)

**Mechanics:**
- Single-ball mode with rotating **regional shot groupings**
- Regions: Europe, Australia, Baltic, North America
- Values escalate every **15 seconds** or via rocket ramp shots
- **Cash Out:** Individually per region via action button to collect totals
- **Complete All Regions:** Shoot side ramp to cash out all regions simultaneously for full accumulated value

**Scaling by Milestone Count:**
- **4 milestones:** 4M base / 400K increment
- **5 milestones:** Increased values
- **10 milestones:** Maximum scaling (25M base / unknown increment)

> 🎯 **Path to 500M+:** Final Tour with 8-10 milestones + Champ Multiball stacks provide the highest-value opportunities on Elton John. <kcite ref="1"/>

## 📈 Stacking & Combos

### ✅ What Stacks Well

| Combination                        | Benefit                                  |
| ---------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| **Croc + Rocket Man Multiball**    | 2x all jackpots on both modes            |
| **Champ + Other Modes**            | Escalating playfield multipliers         |

| **Fuel Tank Perks + Croc Locks**   | Extra ball savers + cascading jackpots   |

### ❌ What NOT to Stack

| Avoid                              | Reason                                           |
| ---------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------ |
| **Signature Stage + Other Modes**  | Only one Signature Stage can run at a time       |
| **Frenzy + Critical Shots**        | Rapid target sequences reduce ball control       |

### ⏰ Optimal Timing

1. **Build Rocket Fuel** before starting Rocket Man (100+ units minimum)
2. **Collect Wardrobe** early to unlock Victory Lap mode
3. **Prioritize Crocodile Locks** early for multiball stacking
4. **Trigger both Croc + Rocket** simultaneously for 2x multiplier effect
5. **Collect Bonus X Skill Shot** to maximize end-of-ball bonus multiplier
6. **Build Milestones** to enable high-value Final Tour plays

## 🎯 Shot Analysis

### ⚠️ Dangerous Shots

| Shot                    | Risk                                    | Mitigation                      |
| ----------------------- | --------------------------------------- | ------------------------------- |
| **Left Ramp**           | Unpredictable exits, potential outlane  | Aim for control, avoid during   |
|                         | drain on missed orbits                  | low-ball-save situations        |
| **Center Drop Targets** | Fast returns create SDTM risk           | Trap up on right flipper after  |
| **Spinner Shots**       | Chaotic bounces, ball speed loss        | Use during multiball instead    |
| **Piano Ramp (missed)** | Deflects to outlane zones               | Ensure clean lock entry         |

### ✅ Safe Shots & Ball Control

| Position                | Benefit                         |
| ----------------------- | ------------------------------- |
| **Right Ramp (normal)** | Direct flipper feed on backstage|
| **Crocodile Lock (VUK)**| Ball held, controlled release   |
| **Spinner (controlled)**| Predictable exit paths          |
| **Backstage (upper)**   | Early upper flipper = trap      |

**Ball Control Positions:**
- **Left Flipper Trap:** Best after right orbit shots for control
- **Right Flipper Trap:** Natural position after left ramp
- **Post Pass:** Safe flipper-to-flipper transfer
- **Cradle:** Hold left flipper for setup between shots

## 🎮 Player Tips from Experts

**Noah Crable's Strategy Priorities:**

1. **Choose "Spot Star" skill shot** to gradually unlock Signature Stage multiballs without manual ELTON JOHN spelling
2. **Prioritize backstage wardrobe collection** when lit—access via right ramp into early upper flipper flip
3. **Use multiballs to tackle drop targets** rather than targeting them during single-ball play
4. **Select easier Signature Stage tours (1970/1975)** to guarantee scoring while building milestone progress

**General Tactics:**
- **Fast gameplay encouraged:** The lightning-fast layout rewards quick decisions and consistent ramp shots
- **Spinners are fuel factories:** Use them to accumulate Rocket Fuel throughout the game without commitment
- **Victory Lap is king:** Once activated via 3 wardrobe items, Victory Lap (3M+ per shot) dominates early scoring
- **Multiball stacking provides 2x boost:** Plan lock sequences to trigger both Croc + Rocket simultaneously
