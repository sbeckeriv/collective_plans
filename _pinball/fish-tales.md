# 🎣 Fish Tales - Strategy Guide

*Williams, 1992 — Designed by Mark Ritchie*

> **"Lock the Caster's Club. Land the Super Jackpot."**

One of Williams' best-selling machines (13,640 units), Fish Tales centers on locking balls at the Caster's Club for multiball, then climbing through three escalating tackle box jackpots before unlocking unlimited 100-million-point Super Jackpots. Our goal: lock 3 balls, collect all three tackle box jackpots in order, then repeat the Super Jackpot for the rest of multiball.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Shoot the Caster's Club** (right side) to lock balls — **3 locks starts multiball**, releasing them from the reel in a chaotic burst
- [ ] **During multiball, relock a ball (or two, for a double jackpot)**, then shoot the **spinner to collect**
- [ ] **Collect the three tackle box jackpots in order**: Tropical (20M) → Fresh Water (30M) → Deep Sea (40M)
- [ ] **After all three, the captive ball lights for a 100,000,000-point Super Jackpot** — collectible **repeatedly** for the rest of multiball
- [ ] **Make 6 shots to the lit center ramp** for the big-value **Monster Fish** award
- [ ] **Shoot the right orbit when lit** to advance the captive ball award

---

## 🎣 Core Mechanics

### Caster's Club Locks & Multiball

1. Shoot the **Caster's Club** (right side) to lock a ball
2. **Lock 3 balls** to start multiball — they're released from the rotary reel in a chaotic burst
3. Once in multiball, **relock a ball (or two for a double jackpot)** to set up your next collection
4. Shoot the **spinner** to collect the jackpot

### Tackle Box Jackpot Ladder

| Jackpot | Value |
|---|---|
| **Tropical** (1st) | 20,000,000 |
| **Fresh Water** (2nd) | 30,000,000 |
| **Deep Sea** (3rd) | 40,000,000 |

> 🎯 **Pro Tip — Core Strategy:** These three jackpots must be collected **in order**. Once all three are done, the **captive ball lights for a 100,000,000-point Super Jackpot**, which can be **collected repeatedly for the rest of multiball** — this is by far the biggest sustained scoring opportunity in the game, so the entire point of running through the tackle box ladder is to unlock this repeatable Super Jackpot phase.

### Monster Fish

Make **6 shots to the lit center ramp** to land the big-value **Monster Fish** award — a strong secondary objective alongside the multiball ladder.

### Captive Ball Award (Right Orbit)

Shoot the **right orbit when lit** to advance the captive ball award, building toward additional value before you even reach the Super Jackpot phase.

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **Chaotic multiball ball release** | All three locked balls release from the reel simultaneously, creating immediate multi-ball chaos | Be ready on both flippers the instant multiball starts |
| **Slightly shorter flippers** | Reportedly 1/8 inch shorter than standard Williams flippers (a European distributor request) | Adjust your shot timing expectations slightly versus other Williams machines |
| **Forcing center ramp shots toward Monster Fish** | Repeated ramp attempts under pressure risk a drain | Only chase the 6-shot sequence when you have consistent ramp control |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Game (Ball 1)

1. Start locking balls at the Caster's Club toward your first multiball
2. Work the center ramp toward Monster Fish progress
3. Shoot the right orbit when lit to build captive ball value

**Target Score:** 20M–50M

### Mid Game (Ball 2)

1. Run multiball and push through the Tropical → Fresh Water → Deep Sea jackpot ladder
2. Relock balls during multiball for double jackpot opportunities
3. Land Monster Fish if you're close to the 6-shot requirement

**Target Score:** 50M–150M

### Late Game (Ball 3+)

1. Once all three tackle box jackpots are collected, grind the repeatable 100M Super Jackpot for as long as multiball lasts
2. Relock aggressively to extend multiball and maximize Super Jackpot repeats
3. Bank Monster Fish and captive ball value alongside your multiball scoring

**Target Score per ball:** 50M–250M+

## ⚙️ Setup Recommendations

| Setting | Recommendation | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| **Playfield pitch** | 6.5°–7.0° | Standard for early-90s Williams solid-state machines |
| **Rotary reel lock mechanism** | Verify smooth, reliable ball release | Central to the multiball-start experience and overall flow |
| **Spinner mechanism** | Check smooth, free spinning | Core jackpot-collection shot throughout multiball |

---

## 📋 Quick Reference: Strategic Priorities

**Ball 1:** Lock toward first multiball, build Monster Fish and captive ball progress.
**Ball 2:** Run the tackle box jackpot ladder during multiball, relock for doubles.
**Ball 3+:** Grind the repeatable 100M Super Jackpot, relock aggressively to extend multiball.

**Target High-Score Ballpark:** **150–400 million points** is a strong, competitive game; reaching and sustaining the repeatable Super Jackpot phase is the true measure of mastery on this beloved fishing classic.
