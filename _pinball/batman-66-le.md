# 🦇 Batman 66 Pinball Machine - Strategy Guide

*Stern Pinball, 2016 — Designed by George Gomez*

> **"Same Bat-time, same Bat-channel!"**

Batman 66 is one of the deepest rulesheets Stern has ever produced — 8 major villain mode variants, 14 minor villain episodes across three TV seasons, three selectable wizard modes, and a final Villain Escape mode. This guide focuses on the highest-leverage strategic decisions rather than every individual episode's shot sequence. Our goal: **500M–1B points**, built on smart minor-villain perk stacking and Villain Multiball jackpots.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Take the Super Skill Shot** (hold left flipper during plunge, then hit any flashing major shot within 5 seconds) — it qualifies a villain or grants a Gadget for free
- [ ] **Catch Bookworm first if you can** — its perk (minor villain awards last the **entire game**) is the single most game-breaking effect available
- [ ] **Catch Mr. Freeze early too** — freezing all game timers lets you run Bat Turn and the playfield multiplier indefinitely
- [ ] **Complete one minor villain from each of the 3 TV seasons** to unlock your choice of wizard mode
- [ ] **Use the Batcave Combo** (scoop → left orbit before any mode shot) to double your entire mode's shot values
- [ ] **Hit Commissioner Gordon's target twice before starting a major villain mode** for +2x bonus multiplier and +1x Villain Multiball multiplier

---

## 📞 Bat-Phone & Major Villain Selection

Hit any of the four major villain shots repeatedly until the turntable reveals the **Bat-Phone hurry-up**. The longer you wait (qualifying more villains) before cashing it, the bigger it gets — from **1.5M up to 12M** — and it boosts in-mode scoring too.

| Villain | Qualify By |
|---|---|
| **Catwoman** | Both ramps (once each) |
| **Joker** | Villain-Vision standup targets |
| **Penguin** | The scoop (bumper entries don't count) |
| **Riddler** | Either orbit |

At the start of a ball with no active mode, **hold the right flipper** to toggle between which villain mode you want to start.

> 🎯 **Pro Tip:** Each major villain has 2 episode variants (8 total modes), each with 3-4 escalating stages culminating in an "unlimited scoring" final stage where any 5 shots re-light the rest. Don't worry about memorizing every stage — the core loop is always **"hit the lit shots, then cash the Super Jackpot at the scoop (or push on for +1x more)."**

### In-Mode Scoring Essentials

- **Each lit shot:** 500K base + 50K per shot made + 50K per Bat-Phone increment, accumulating into the Super Jackpot
- **Batcave Combo:** Scoop → left orbit (lit orange) *before* any mode shot = **2x value for the entire mode**
- **Commissioner Gordon target (2x before any mode shot):** +2x bonus multiplier and +1x Villain Multiball multiplier (repeatable across modes for stacking VB multiplier)

---

## 🎁 Villain Multiball

1. During any major villain mode, hit the **two targets flanking the turntable** to light 3 locks at the right orbit
2. Lock 3 balls (alternative: use a Gadget when a ball exits the left orbit saucer with Lock lit)
3. **Jackpots:** all major shots score green jackpots (~300K + 50K per jackpot)
4. After 12 jackpots, the turntable rotates to Batcave and the **Atomic Pile lights for Super Jackpot** (each subsequent Super Jackpot needs 2 more shots, max 20)
5. **Mystery always awards Add-A-Ball** during Villain Multiball
6. **Shame's minor villain perk** adds +1 ball to Villain Multiball and extends the ball saver by 10 seconds

---

## 🃏 Minor Villains (The Real Strategic Core)

Complete 3 TV standup targets, then shoot the left orbit to start a minor villain mode (45 seconds each). These are spread across all 3 TV seasons and **each carries a persistent perk** — picking the right ones matters far more than individual episode completion.

| Villain | Best Perk | Why It Matters |
|---|---|---|
| **Bookworm** | Minor villain awards last **until end of game** | Game-breaking — catch this as early as possible |
| **Mr. Freeze** | **Freezes all game timers** (Bat-Phone, modes, playfield multiplier, Bat Turn, Villain Escape phase 1) | Lets you milk Bat Turn / playfield multiplier indefinitely |
| **King Tut** (4 variants) | **+1x main channel multiplier per completion**, persists for the ball | Stack multiple Tut completions for a huge multiplier |
| **Egghead** (3 variants) | Points awarded in end-of-ball bonus, accumulate across modes; later variants are **immune to gadgets** | Reliable bonus padding |
| **Shame** | +1 ball to multiballs, +10 sec ball saver | Strong utility, especially before Villain Multiball |
| **Mad Hatter** (2 variants) | Replay failed minor villain modes | Useful insurance, less impactful than Bookworm/Freeze |

> 🏆 **Pro Tip:** If you only remember one piece of minor-villain strategy, remember this priority order: **Bookworm > Mr. Freeze > King Tut stacking > everything else.**

## ⚡ Atomic Pile & Playfield Multiplier

Hit the two standup targets flanking the Batmobile spinner to light the **Atomic Pile** for a **2x playfield multiplier** (30 seconds). Hit the targets again while Atomic Pile is active for **3x multiplier** + timer reset.

> ❄️ With Mr. Freeze's timer-freeze active, you can potentially hold a **3x playfield multiplier for the rest of the ball**.

### Bat Turn (40-second timed mode)

Spin the Batmobile 10x to light Atomic Pile. All major shots light blue — each is worth a hurry-up value (min 250K), un-lighting after being hit; spinning the Batmobile again re-lights everything and resets the hurry-up. **Villain Vision variant:** hurry-up freezes, all shots score 2x, and shots re-light the previous one after being hit.

## 🏆 Wizard Mode Selection

**Qualify by completing one minor villain from each of the 3 TV seasons.** Your next left orbit shot brings up a selection screen for one of three 6-ball multiballs:

| Wizard Mode | Core Loop |
|---|---|
| **Batusi Multiball** | Hit all lit shots for jackpots; 10 pop bumper hits re-light everything; any 5 shots light left orbit for a Super Jackpot that re-lights with increased value |
| **Robin's Holy Multiball** | 2 random shots lit at a time; first jackpot 4M, +250K each subsequent; complete all lit shots → turntable to Batcave → Super Jackpot (sum of all jackpots) |
| **Gas Attack Multiball** | Turntable hurry-up starts at 5M + 2.5M per mode already played; shoot turntable 5x → roving yellow shot lights the Super Jackpot |

All three add balls 1-3 back in and restart with one more shot required (capped at 6-7), so a good run can chain several Super Jackpots.

---

## 🦇 Villain Escape (Final Wizard Mode)

**Requirement:** Capture all four major villains (complete each at least once).

1. **Build Phase** (30 sec): every switch hit adds 10K to your "Villain Value" (used in Phase 2); ends on drain or timer (Mr. Freeze freezes this timer, so it only ends on drain if active)
2. **Multiball Phase** (4-ball): defeat all 4 villains in order by hitting all their lit shots once each (Penguin requires 5 crane hits + scoop), then turntable to Batcave for the **Super Jackpot** at left orbit
3. Each Super Jackpot adds a ball back and requires +1 shot per villain (max 5-6, except Penguin which is always 5 crane + scoop)
4. **Mode ends on drain to 1 ball** — this is the true endgame, so treat every ball save as precious

## ⚠️ Dangerous Shots & Drain Patterns

| Shot | Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| **Penguin Scoop** | Requires *direct* entry — bumper entries don't count toward mode progress | Aim cleanly rather than relying on incidental bumper feeds |
| **Turntable Magnet** | Unpredictable ball behavior, heavily used in Penguin stages 2-3 | Stay ready on the flippers any time the turntable is active |
| **Crane Wrecking Ball positioning** | Miss the lit crane position and lose progress | Wait for a controlled, trapped shot rather than rushing the crane |

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Game (Ball 1)

1. Super Skill Shot to qualify your first villain for free
2. Hit TV targets and the left orbit to unlock minor villains — prioritize finding Bookworm and Mr. Freeze
3. Build toward your first Bat-Phone qualification

**Target Score:** 30M–80M

### Mid Game (Ball 2)

1. Stack King Tut completions if available for main channel multiplier
2. Build Villain Multiball locks during a major villain mode, use the Batcave Combo to double mode values
3. Complete one minor villain per season to unlock wizard mode selection

**Target Score:** 80M–250M

### Late Game (Ball 3+)

1. Cash in your chosen wizard mode (Batusi, Robin's Holy, or Gas Attack) and chain Super Jackpots
2. If you've beaten all 4 major villains, push into **Villain Escape** for the true final challenge
3. Lean on any frozen timers from Mr. Freeze to maximize Bat Turn / playfield multiplier scoring

**Target Score per ball:** 100M–500M+

## 📋 Quick Reference: Strategic Priorities

**Ball 1:** Super Skill Shot, hunt for Bookworm/Mr. Freeze among minor villains.
**Ball 2:** Stack King Tut multipliers, build Villain Multiball, unlock wizard mode selection.
**Ball 3+:** Chain wizard mode Super Jackpots, push for Villain Escape if all 4 majors are beaten.

**Target High-Score Ballpark:** **500M–1 billion points** is a strong, competitive game on this deep ruleset — Bookworm + Mr. Freeze in the same game is the biggest swing factor toward a personal best.
