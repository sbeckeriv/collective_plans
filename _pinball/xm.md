# ⚡ X-Men (Stern, 2012) - Strategy Guide

*Stern Pinball, 2012 — Pro Edition — Designed by John Borg*

> **"Heroes vs Villains. Use your mutant power. Don't let Magneto win."**

X-Men (Stern, 2012) is a SAM-platform machine with a Heroes vs. Villains dual-progression mechanic: you play as the X-Men team defeating Marvel villains through shot sequences, while simultaneously unlocking X-Men heroes as playfield abilities. The Pro edition features the Magneto electromagnet, Wolverine bash toy, Cyclops scoop, ramps for Rogue and Storm, and the Danger Room multiball. Our goal: **200M–800M** by defeating villains efficiently, activating hero abilities strategically, and triggering X-Men Multiball and Danger Room Multiball.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Shoot the right ramp (Wolverine)** to attack the active villain — primary progression shot
- [ ] **Hit the Wolverine bash toy** (center) for villain damage — each hit counts toward defeating them
- [ ] **Shoot the Cyclops scoop** (left side) to start hero modes and charge Cyclops' optic blast
- [ ] **Use the left ramp (Rogue)** to absorb villain powers — unlocks special shot abilities
- [ ] **Complete villain defeat** (via bash toy + ramps) to progress toward X-Men Multiball
- [ ] **Lock balls in the Danger Room** (right VUK) for Danger Room Multiball

## 🦸 Core Mechanics

### Skill Shot

| Skill Shot | How | Reward |
|---|---|---|
| **Cyclops shot** | Time plunge to left scoop | Hero mode start + optic blast charge |
| **Right orbit** | Power plunge | Villain damage + hero advance |

### Heroes vs. Villains (Dual Progression)

The game tracks two parallel progressions:

**Villains (defeat them):**
- Active villain shown on DMD (Magneto, Mystique, Juggernaut, Sabretooth, etc.)
- Each villain requires a specific number of hits to defeat:
  - Bash toy (Wolverine): primary damage source
  - Right ramp (Wolverine): secondary damage
  - Specific shots lit during villain mode

**Heroes (unlock and use):**
- X-Men heroes unlock via the Cyclops scoop and left ramp
- Each hero provides a playfield ability when activated:

| Hero | Unlock Shot | Ability |
|---|---|---|
| **Cyclops** | Cyclops scoop × 3 | Optic blast: hold right flipper → scoop shot scores 2× |
| **Wolverine** | Right ramp × 3 | Regeneration: bash toy ball save active for 20 sec |
| **Storm** | Right orbit × 3 | Lightning: all ramp shots score 2× for 30 sec |
| **Rogue** | Left ramp × 3 | Power absorption: current villain's bonus applies to you |
| **Jean Grey** | Left orbit × 2 | Phoenix force: extra ball at next right scoop |

### Wolverine Bash Toy (Center)

The Wolverine figure in the center of the playfield:
- Each bash: villain damage (variable by villain)
- During villain modes: bash toy = primary damage source
- **3 consecutive bash hits:** Triggers "Berserker Rage" — bash toy scores 3× for 10 seconds
- The bash toy can be hit from either flipper at moderate power

### Right Ramp (Wolverine Attack)

The right ramp is the primary progression shot:
- Scores villain damage + hero advance (Wolverine hero meter)
- Fast loop combo: 2 consecutive right ramps = combo bonus (50M+)
- During X-Men Multiball: right ramp = primary jackpot shot

### Cyclops Scoop (Left Side)

The Cyclops scoop starts hero modes:
- Each scoop shot: advances Cyclops' optic blast charge
- 3 scoop shots: Cyclops ability ready for activation
- Also starts specific timed hero modes (each hero has a 30-second mode)

### Left Ramp (Rogue / Storm)

The left ramp serves both Rogue and Storm:
- Alternate left ramp shots: Rogue meter (odd shots) / Storm meter (even shots)
- Completing Rogue: Power absorption ability ready
- Completing Storm: Lightning ability ready

### Danger Room VUK (Right Side — Ball Lock)

The Danger Room VUK on the right locks balls for Danger Room Multiball:
- Lock 2 balls → Danger Room Multiball begins
- The Danger Room is the X-Men's training facility — the mode represents a training simulation

### Magneto Magnet (Pro)

An electromagnet on the lower playfield:
- Activates during Magneto villain mode and multiball phases
- Not player-controlled; it redirects the ball toward or away from specific shots
- During Magneto mode: the magnet "fights" you by redirecting well-aimed shots

## 🎳 Multiball

### X-Men Multiball

**How to start:** Defeat 3 villains → right ramp lock × 2 → multiball begins.

**Jackpot structure:**
- 3-ball multiball
- Right ramp = hero jackpots (active hero abilities multiply)
- Bash toy = villain super jackpot (villain count × 25M)
- **Grand Jackpot (X-Men Assemble):** All heroes activated during multiball → Cyclops scoop → 150M–500M+
- Magneto magnet active during multiball (adds unpredictability)

### Danger Room Multiball (2-ball)

**How to start:** Lock 2 balls in the Danger Room VUK.

**Jackpot structure:**
- 2-ball multiball with Danger Room training mode shots
- All hero abilities temporarily active simultaneously
- Bash toy during Danger Room = elevated training jackpot
- Good mid-game score push; faster to trigger than X-Men Multiball

## 🔄 Progression Systems

- **Villain progression:** 5 villains → Boss Villain (Magneto) → X-Men Multiball qualifier
- **Hero roster:** 5 heroes → full team → Grand Jackpot eligible during multiball
- **Cerebro meter:** Accumulates passively via orbit shots; full Cerebro = bonus multiplier step

### Dangerous Areas

- **Magneto magnet during his mode** — the magnet can redirect a direct right ramp shot toward the right outlane
- **Post-bash toy** — a hard center bash deflects the ball at irregular angles; be ready on both flippers
- **Right outlane** — fast after right ramp exit

## 💡 Pro Tips

> **Right ramp first, always.** It's the safest primary shot and does villain damage while building Wolverine's hero. It's John Borg's bread-and-butter shot on this machine.

> **Activate Storm before multiball.** The 2× ramp multiplier during Storm's lightning ability stacks multiplicatively with multiball jackpots. Storm active + X-Men Multiball + right ramp loop = the game's highest repeating shot value.

> **Magneto is the hardest villain.** His electromagnet actively deflects your shots during his mode. For Magneto: use the left ramp and bash toy as your primary damage — they're less affected by the magnet's redirect zone.

> **Danger Room Multiball first on Ball 2.** It's faster to trigger than X-Men Multiball and provides a meaningful score injection. Use it to build the scoreboard while continuing villain progression.
