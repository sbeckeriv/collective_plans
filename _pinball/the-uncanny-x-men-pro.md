## 🚪 Danger Room (The Mini-Flipper Area)

Located to the left of the main flippers with a mini-flipper, three targets, a spinner, and a ramp to Cerebro. This is **where most advanced scoring happens** <kcite ref="2"/>.

### Leveling System (Critical for Scoring)

| Level | Color | Effect |
| ----- | ----- | ------ |
| **Level 1** | Green | Double all mission shot values |
| **Level 2** | Yellow | One fewer sentinel required to escape the future (-1 difficulty) |
| **Level 3** | Red | Double sentinels killed; double corresponding future awards |

**How to Progress:** Hit spinner, targets, ramp, and bumper to fill level bar

**Ball Save:** "Abort" save available at game start; requalifiable by hitting X-Jet 3 times. Saves balls draining from Danger Room or main playfield for 5 seconds.

**During Multiballs:** Leveling Danger Room increases jackpot and super jackpot values significantly

**Bonus Calculation:** Danger Room bonus = 10% of level completion scored at end of ball

**Strategic Tip:** Prioritize reaching Level 3 (red) early. The 2x sentinel multiplier accelerates progress toward Berserker multiball and future mode difficulty drops by one sentinel per escape.

## 🎰 The Four Multiballs

**Note:** Multiballs stack with missions but NOT with each other. Berserker has priority start.

### 🔴 **Sentinel Multiball**

**Start Requirements:**
- Hit giant sentinel head **3 times** (then 2 additional hits for each subsequent multiball played)
- Defeat it by hitting until mouth opens

**During Multiball (3-ball standard):**
- All major shots lit yellow for 1x jackpots (~5M base)
- Danger room exit ramp relights shots: orange (2x), red (3x)
- Unlit shots kill 1 sentinel toward Berserker multiball progress
- After 4 jackpots collected: **Super jackpot** at sentinel head = (sum of 4 jackpots × 2)
- Maximum 6 super jackpots possible per Sentinel multiball session

**Perks Affecting This Mode:**
- **Storm perk:** Bonus damage to sentinels; extended multiball duration
- **Wolverine perk:** Bonus damage to first sentinel hit

**Scoring Example:** 4 jackpots of 6M, 7M, 8M, 9M → Super = (6+7+8+9) × 2 = 60M

### 🏙️ **Save the City Multiball**

**Start Requirements:**
- Hit each of 6 primary shots (X-Jet, Downtown, Uptown, Midtown, Subway, Highway) **3 times each**
- Progress shown in upper right corner (tracks completion toward multiball qualification)

**During Multiball (3-ball):**
- Five jackpot areas light via corresponding shots:
  - **Downtown** (left loop): Loops shots advance value
  - **Uptown** (left ramp): Ramp shots advance value
  - **Midtown** (center ramp): Target shots advance value
  - **Subway** (right loop): Spinner hits advance value
  - **Highway** (right ramp): Bumper hits advance value
- Each hit in zone increases that jackpot's value (+500k per hit typical)
- After all 5 zone jackpots collected: X-Jet lights danger room spinner for **Super jackpot** = 1M per spinner spin; 15-second timer
- Kitty Pryde lane bounces add +10 seconds to super jackpot timer

**Scoring Example:** Build 5 zones to 8M-10M each → Super at 8-12 spins = 80M-120M

### 👥 **To Me, My X-Men Multiball**

**Start Requirements:**
- Hit Cerebro skill shots OR strike Nightcrawler targets **5 times**
- Collect Cerebro **3 times** via uptown or highway ramps (when lit yellow)

**During Multiball (2-ball):**
- Hit 6 blue lit shots to summon X-Men and score jackpots
- **Cannot hit same shot twice in succession** (forces shot variety)
- Each X-Men summoned = ~3M-5M jackpot
- After 6 jackpots: **Super jackpot** at left ramp (minimum 5M-8M, increases per ball)
- 2x super jackpot available at danger room exit ramp
- **Bonus:** Summoning all 12 X-Men (across multiple multiballs) unlocks permanent scoring boost

**Scoring:** 6 jackpots × (average 4M) = 24M + Super (5M-10M) = 29M-34M per ball

### ⚡ **Berserker Multiball** (The Rare, High-Value Mode)

**Activation Requirement:** Light at uptown ramp after **35 sentinels defeated** across entire game (no other modes active to prevent blocking)

**Sentinel Count Sources:**
- Sentinel Facility Raid mission: Up to 7 sentinels per play
- The Future (any escape): All lit shots defeat sentinels (2x if danger room level 3)

**During Multiball (3-ball):**
- All shots lit yellow for 5M+ jackpots
- Cannot repeat shots until another is made (forces shot variety across playfield)
- After 5 jackpots collected: X-Jet lights super jackpot (total = sum of 5 × 1M increase per jackpot)
- Danger room exit ramp available for 2x super jackpot value
- Subsequent super jackpots require incremental jackpot rebuilds and increase in base value each time

**Scoring Potential:** 5 jackpots (6M-10M) = 35M-50M + Super (40M-60M) = **75M-110M per Berserker multiball session** (highest single-mode scoring opportunity)

## 🎁 Bonus Calculation & End-of-Ball

**Formula:**
```
(125k × X-Mansion ramps) + (250k × Kitty Pryde lanes) + 
Danger room bonus + (100k × sentinels destroyed) × Bonus X
```

### Bonus X Multiplier (Built via Cerebro targets, Gambit bumper, mission starts)

| Bonus X Value | How to Build | Points Multiplier |
| ------------- | ------------ | ----------------- |
| **1x** | Game start | 100% (base) |
| **2x** | Hit 1 Cerebro target + 1 Gambit bumper | 200% |
| **3x** | X-Jet skill shot (+3 X) | 300% |
| **5x+** | Combine multiple sources (Gambit 3x + Cerebro 2x) | 500%+ |

**Bonus Hold:** Hit target below Danger Room mini-flipper (cannot be intentional — must be random bounce). If held on last ball, bonus **doubles instead of multiplies** by X (huge swing).

## 🥚 Easter Eggs & Hidden Secrets

### **"Professor X's Whisper"**
During multiball, hit the Danger Room mini-flipper ramp consecutively (3x) without hitting another playfield feature. A special callout triggers and grants a temporary +1M per shot bonus for 10 seconds.

### **Magneto's Easter Egg**
If all 8 missions are completed in one game AND a Berserker multiball is started, hitting the center ramp during Berserker triggers a secret "Magneto Steal" sequence where 1 locked ball is stolen and bonus points awarded.

### **"To Me, My X-Men" All Summons**
Unlock all 12 X-Men across multiple "To Me, My X-Men" multiball sessions in a single game. On final unlock, a secret award lights: +50M bonus points.
