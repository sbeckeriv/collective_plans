# 🚀 Mars Trek - Strategy Guide

*Sonic (Segasa), 1977 — Spanish Manufacturing*

> **"Light the orbit. Mind the E/K rebound."**

Mars Trek is a rare Spanish EM built around spelling MARS and TREK for points, with a center E/K standup target controlling Double Bonus — a high-risk, high-reward shot that veteran players approach carefully. Our goal: spell MARS TREK for Special, build bonus toward the 50,000-point extra ball threshold, and only take the E/K shot when the payoff justifies the rebound risk.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Hit the left orbit shot to advance bonus** — and hope it also falls into the top saucer for additional bonus value
- [ ] **Complete MARS** for 10,000 points; complete **TREK** (center target) for another 10,000
- [ ] **Complete the full MARS TREK** to light **Special**
- [ ] **Hit the E and K targets to light Double Bonus** — but only when it's worth the risk (see below)
- [ ] **Reach 50,000 in bonus to light Extra Ball**
- [ ] **Pass the ball through the semi-circular lane when lit** for another Extra Ball opportunity

## 🚀 Core Mechanics

### Spelling MARS and TREK

| Completion | Value |
|---|---|
| **MARS** | 10,000 points |
| **TREK** (center target) | 10,000 points |
| **Full MARS TREK** | Lights **Special** |

### Double Bonus via E/K Targets

Hitting the **E and K** standup targets lights **Double Bonus** — but the **center E/K target carries a real risk of rebounding into the left outlane**.

> ⚠️ **Pro Tip — Core Risk Decision:** Only shoot the center E/K target when **making the shot will give you Double Bonus with an already-high base bonus value**. If your bonus is low, the rebound risk isn't worth it — wait until you have meaningful bonus banked before attempting this shot.

### Bonus & Extra Ball

- **Left orbit shot** advances bonus, and can also feed the **top saucer** for additional bonus value — this is your primary, safer bonus-building shot
- **Reaching 50,000 in bonus** lights **Extra Ball**
- **Passing through the lit semi-circular lane** also awards an **Extra Ball**

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Early Ball

1. Work the left orbit shot to start building bonus safely
2. Begin spelling MARS and TREK opportunistically
3. Avoid the E/K target until your bonus is substantial

### Mid Ball

1. Continue building bonus toward the 50,000 Extra Ball threshold
2. Take the E/K Double Bonus shot once your bonus is high enough to justify the risk
3. Work toward completing the full MARS TREK spelling for Special

### Late Ball

1. Cash in any remaining Extra Ball opportunities (50K bonus, semi-circular lane)
2. Take Special if MARS TREK is complete
3. Bank your (possibly doubled) bonus before the ball ends
