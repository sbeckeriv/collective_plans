# 🌊 White Water (Williams, 1993) - Strategy Guide

*Williams Electronics, 1993 — Designed by Dennis Nordman*

> **"VACATION spells jackpots. Bigfoot spells danger. Wet Willie just wants your ball."**

White Water (WH2O, Williams 1993) is a WPC machine about a whitewater rafting vacation gone very wrong, complete with Bigfoot, a monster called Wet Willie, and a snack bar run by someone who really doesn't care. The game features three ramps, a Bigfoot toy with a ball-swallowing mouth, a Wet Willie ball lock, the VACATION jackpot letter system, and the Wet Willie Multiball. Our goal: **300M–1B** by completing the VACATION jackpot, triggering Wet Willie Multiball, and cycling through Bigfoot encounters.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Shoot the left ramp (Whirlpool)** repeatedly to spell VACATION for the Vacation Jackpot
- [ ] **Feed Bigfoot's mouth** (upper right) to collect Bigfoot bonuses and charge the Bigfoot encounter
- [ ] **Lock balls in Wet Willie's** (left scoop VUK) for Wet Willie Multiball
- [ ] **Hit the right ramp** for bonus advancement and multiball jackpot access
- [ ] **Shoot the Snack Bar** (right scoop) for mystery awards — often extra ball or mode advance
- [ ] **Spell HYDROPLANE** (center lane shots) for a secondary jackpot path

## 🚣 Core Mechanics

### Skill Shot

| Skill Shot | How | Reward |
|---|---|---|
| **VACATION letter** | Time plunge to lit top rollover | VACATION letter advance + points |
| **Bigfoot mouth** | Right orbit power plunge | Bigfoot encounter start |

### Left Ramp (Whirlpool) — VACATION Jackpot

The left ramp is the game's primary shot:
- Each completion advances one VACATION letter (V-A-C-A-T-I-O-N)
- **Completing VACATION:** Awards the Vacation Jackpot (scales up with each cycle)
- VACATION resets and the jackpot value increases for each subsequent completion
- **The Vacation Jackpot is the core scoring engine.** Prioritize this above all else.

### Bigfoot (Upper Right Mouth)

The Bigfoot toy has an animatronic mouth that opens during Bigfoot modes:
- Shoot the ball into Bigfoot's open mouth for:
  - Bigfoot bonus (first hit)
  - Bigfoot encounter mode (subsequent hits during mode)
  - Progressive jackpot during Wet Willie Multiball
- Bigfoot's mouth closes if you miss — wait for it to reopen

### Left Scoop (Wet Willie's — Ball Lock)

Wet Willie's is the ball lock location:
- Each left scoop shot with the lock lit: one ball locked
- Lock 2 balls → Wet Willie Multiball begins
- When not lit: collects a bonus or mystery award

### Right Ramp (River Run)

The right ramp:
- Awards right ramp bonus (advances multiball jackpot value)
- During Wet Willie Multiball: right ramp = primary jackpot shot
- **Second most important shot after the left ramp**

### Snack Bar (Right Scoop — Mystery)

The Snack Bar scoop on the right:
- Mystery award machine: awards are random but valuable
- Frequent mystery awards: extra ball, VACATION letter advance, mode start, kickback light
- Shoot it whenever lit but don't deviate from left ramp cycles for it

### HYDROPLANE (Center Lanes)

Completing the HYDROPLANE lanes (upper rollover set):
- Advances the HYDROPLANE jackpot (secondary jackpot independent of VACATION)
- HYDROPLANE completion + Wet Willie Multiball = both jackpots available simultaneously

## 🎳 Multiball

### Wet Willie Multiball (3-ball)

**How to start:** Lock 2 balls in Wet Willie's (left scoop) → 3-ball multiball.

**Jackpot structure:**
- 3-ball multiball
- Right ramp = base jackpot (200M–500M scaling)
- Bigfoot mouth = super jackpot (Bigfoot encounter value × multiball multiplier)
- **Double Jackpot:** Right ramp × 2 → Bigfoot mouth → 2× the jackpot value
- VACATION letters collected during multiball count toward the next single-ball cycle

- **VACATION jackpot escalation:** Each VACATION cycle raises the jackpot ceiling permanently per ball
- **Bigfoot encounters:** Progressive — each Bigfoot mouth shot raises encounter value
- **Extra balls:** VACATION cycle 2 + Bigfoot encounter 3 = extra ball at specific milestones

### Dangerous Areas

- **Left outlane** — after left ramp eject; ball can kick toward left drain
- **Bigfoot mouth miss** — an off-center shot at Bigfoot can deflect to either outlane
- **Post-multiball** — Wet Willie re-opens after multiball; the ball ejects at speed

## 💡 Pro Tips

> **VACATION is the answer to every question.** Not sure what to shoot? Left ramp. Waiting for a mode? Left ramp. Recovering from a near-drain? Left ramp. The left ramp is safe, consistent, and builds the game's primary jackpot.

> **Bigfoot's mouth timing is key.** The mouth opens and closes on a pattern — wait for it to be fully open before shooting. An off-center hit with the mouth partially closed deflects badly.

> **Wet Willie Multiball + VACATION in progress is the peak.** If you're mid-VACATION and lock two balls, continue the left ramp cycle during multiball — VACATION letters count even with multiple balls in play.

> **The Snack Bar mystery is often extra ball.** If you're at a natural pause (just collected a jackpot, waiting for lock to re-light), the Snack Bar is worth a low-risk shot.
