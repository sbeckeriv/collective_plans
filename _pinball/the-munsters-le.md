# 🧟 The Munsters (LE) - Strategy Guide

*Stern Pinball, 2019 — Limited Edition — Designed by John Borg, Software by Dwight Sullivan*

> **"They're creepy and they're kooky... and the jackpots are mysterious."**

The Munsters LE (Stern, 2019) is a Spike-2 machine featuring 4 flippers, a recessed mini-playfield with its own dedicated buttons and two captive balls, a left ramp with a moving section that reveals the Herman bash toy, dual left inlanes, a stop magnet, and a shaker motor. The LE adds the mini-playfield as a major mechanic. Our goal: **500M–2B** by completing character modes, stacking The Dragon Wagon multiball with active modes, and exploiting the mini-playfield captive ball combo.

---

## 🎃 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Shoot the left ramp** to advance Herman's Munster Masher (bash toy reveal)
- [ ] **Activate the mini-playfield** (LE feature) with dedicated buttons to score captive ball combos
- [ ] **Hit character shots** for each Munster family member to start their mode
- [ ] **Collect Grandpa's Lab** (center scoop) to advance the main story
- [ ] **Lock balls** in the Dragon Wagon via the right orbit for Dragon Wagon Multiball
- [ ] **Use the stop magnet** strategically — it can hold a ball mid-lane to set up shots

## 🏚️ Core Mechanics

### Skill Shot

| Skill Shot | How | Reward |
|---|---|---|
| **Munster skill shot** | Short plunge to lit lane | Character mode start |
| **Full plunge** | Standard | Ball to left flipper |

### Character Modes (5 Munsters)

Each family member has a mode triggered by their dedicated shot:

| Character | Mode Shot | Mode Theme |
|---|---|---|
| **Herman** | Left ramp (bash toy) | Power meter — bash Herman's toy repeatedly |
| **Lily** | Left orbit | Haunted house — timed mystery shots |
| **Grandpa** | Center scoop | Lab experiment — chemical shot sequence |
| **Eddie** | Right ramp | Monster truck — ramp loops |
| **Marilyn** | Right orbit | Normalcy — all shots lit briefly |

Each completed mode awards a character bonus (25M–100M) and advances toward Dragon Wagon Multiball.

### Herman Bash Toy (LE Left Ramp)

The left ramp has a moving section that slides away to reveal the Herman bash toy. The toy opens:
- During Herman's mode
- At specific story progression points

Hit Herman multiple times in the power meter window for escalating combo hits (1M → 5M → 15M per hit in sequence).

### Mini-Playfield (LE Exclusive)

The LE has a recessed mini-playfield on the right side with:
- Two captive balls
- A VUK (vertical up-kicker)
- A digital score display
- Dedicated button controls (separate from the main flipper buttons)

**How to activate:** Press the mini-playfield buttons when the mini-playfield inserts are lit (triggered by completing character shots). The mini-playfield captive balls chain for combo jackpots when hit in sequence.

### Stop Magnet

A magnet in the center playfield area can hold the ball stationary:
- Activates during Grandpa's Lab mode to "freeze" ingredients
- During multiball, can hold one ball for a precision shot
- Player cannot manually control the magnet — it activates on game cue

### Dual Left Inlanes

Two left inlanes:
- Outer left inlane: standard feed to left flipper
- Inner left inlane: feeds to the up-post / alternate routing
- Tracking which inlane the ball exits changes your shot priority

## 🎳 Multiball

### Dragon Wagon Multiball

**How to start:** Complete 3 character modes → right orbit lock × 2.

**Jackpot structure:**
- 4-ball multiball (LE) with all four flippers active
- Character shots = jackpots (scaled by how many modes completed before multiball)
- **Super Jackpot:** Complete all 5 character jackpots during multiball → center scoop → 100M–500M+
- Mini-playfield captive ball chain during multiball = bonus jackpot

### Herman Mode (Mini-Multiball within a Mode)

During Herman's power meter mode, the game enters a 2-ball brief mode where bashing the Herman toy with both balls active doubles each hit's value.

## 💡 Pro Tips

> **The mini-playfield is the LE's premium feature.** It's separate from the main game — practice activating it and chaining the captive balls. Players who ignore it miss 20–30% of available scoring.

> **Herman's bash window has audio cues.** The shaker motor coincides with the peak bash value window. When the cabinet shakes, you're in the money zone.

> **Complete 5 characters before locking.** Same principle as all character-based Stern machines — jackpot ceiling scales with mode completion count. Don't lock until you're maxed.
