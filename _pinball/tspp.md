# 🍩 The Simpsons Pinball Party - Strategy Guide

*Stern Pinball, 2003 — Whitestar Platform*

> **"D'oh! Now do it again for more points."**

The Simpsons Pinball Party (TSPP, Stern 2003) is widely considered one of the best pinball machines ever made — a Whitestar machine with deep rules, 9 playfield characters (each with their own mode), a couch gag progression, and the legendary Mr. Burns Jackpot. Every mode in TSPP is distinct and themed to specific show episodes. Our goal: **300M–1.5B** by completing as many character modes as possible and stacking them with Treehouse of Horror Multiball.

---

## 🎯 Quick-Start Checklist for New Players

- [ ] **Shoot the TV (center scoop)** to start the first character mode
- [ ] **Complete character modes** by hitting the lit shots during each timed mode
- [ ] **Advance the couch gag** (right ramp × 3) to light multiball qualification
- [ ] **Hit the Couch** (left loop) to collect end-of-mode bonuses
- [ ] **Start Treehouse of Horror Multiball** by locking balls in the chest freezer
- [ ] **Collect Mr. Burns Jackpot** — the highest single-shot available on the machine

## 📺 Core Mechanics

### Skill Shot

| Skill Shot | How | Reward |
|---|---|---|
| **TV channel** | Time the plunge to the lit lane | Character mode start + first mode shot |
| **Right orbit** | Hold right flipper at launch | Right orbit value + mode advance |

### Character Modes (9 Characters)

TSPP has 9 characters, each with a unique mode started via the center TV scoop:

| Character | Episode Theme | Key Shots |
|---|---|---|
| Homer | "Homer at Bat" | Left ramp combos |
| Marge | "A Streetcar Named Marge" | Left orbit × 3 |
| Bart | "Bart Gets Famous" | Center standup targets |
| Lisa | "Lisa's Substitute" | Right ramp + left ramp |
| Ned Flanders | "Homer Loves Flanders" | Right orbit shots |
| Mr. Burns | "Homer the Smithers" | Burns target (left standup) |
| Krusty | "Homie the Clown" | Left loop × 3 |
| Sideshow Bob | "Cape Fear" | All shots — timed frenzy |
| Itchy & Scratchy | "Itchy & Scratchy & Marge" | Standup target patterns |

Completing a character mode awards 25M–100M and advances the TV progression.

### Mr. Burns Jackpot

Burns mode, when triggered, lights the Mr. Burns Jackpot:
- Available via the left standup target when lit
- Value accumulates throughout the game based on shots made
- **At game peak:** Can reach 150M–500M per collection
- One of the highest single-shot values in all of pinball

### Couch Gag (Right Ramp)

The right ramp advances the couch gag animation:
- Each right ramp completion = one couch gag step
- After 3 couch gag steps, the couch lock is lit for Treehouse multiball qualification
- Also builds the "sofa" jackpot for multiball

### Chest Freezer (Left Ramp — Ball Lock)

The left ramp with the chest freezer qualifies ball locks for Treehouse of Horror Multiball:
- Lock 2 balls in the freezer → 3-ball multiball starts

## 🎳 Multiball

### Treehouse of Horror Multiball

**How to start:** Complete the couch gag (3 right ramp shots) → lock 2 balls in the chest freezer.

**Jackpot structure:**
- 3-ball multiball
- Jackpots via TV (center scoop) + right ramp
- **Super Jackpot (Couch Jackpot):** Complete all three major jackpot shots → center scoop → 75M–300M+
- Active character mode during multiball compounds: mode shots count AND score jackpots

### Itchy & Scratchy Mini-Multiball

Brief 2-ball multiball triggered by completing I&S mode. Lower value but fast to trigger.

## 🔄 Progression Systems

- **TV progression:** All 9 characters completed fills the "All Characters" bonus (100M+ single award)
- **Couch gag:** Repeatable — each cycle into multiball raises the couch jackpot value
- **Extra balls:** Earned by completing 3 characters and at specific TV milestones

## 🧙 Wizard Mode

### All 9 Characters Complete

**Requirement:** Start and complete all 9 character modes.

**Gameplay:** The "All Characters" animation plays, and a massive all-characters jackpot is awarded. The machine's scoring peak in a single ball.

## 💰 Scoring Strategy

### Ball 1
1. TV → Homer mode (left ramp combos)
2. Right ramp × 3 → couch gag complete
3. Burns mode → Burns Jackpot collection

### Ball 2

## 💡 Pro Tips

> **Mr. Burns Jackpot is the highest single-shot in the game.** Trigger Burns mode early and collect the jackpot when it's at max value — it can be the difference between a 400M and 800M game.

> **Sideshow Bob frenzy is chaotic but lucrative.** When it activates, all shots score simultaneously — don't aim, just shoot. Fast-twitch play is rewarded here.

> **Character modes that align with your dominant shot are free points.** Homer (left ramp) and Ned (right orbit) are the most player-friendly. Prioritize modes that match your natural shot selection.
